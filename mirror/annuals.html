NOTES

Attrition began it's mirror in late 1998, and was not consistent until 1999. Therefore, we only tally the defacements per day, and the chart above after 1998. The totals don't all add up. That's because in some cases we care more about getting a full count than an accurate date. However, when we do date-based calculations, we kick out incomplete dates (most of the incomplete dates are from before 1999).
The chart above is a cute proportional chart, with the largest single monthly total defacements set at 100% of your browser's width. All other monthly totals are proportional to that scale. Enjoy -- munge.
