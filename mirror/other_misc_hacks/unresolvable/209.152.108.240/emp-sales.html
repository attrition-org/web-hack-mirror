Sales and Marketing Positions

Regional Sales Manager - Contractor Supply 
(Posted 10/26/99)

Responsible for hiring, training and motivating sales representatives for our construction contractor supply market channel. This position requires a self-motivated, confident, and persuasive individual who is energized by an opportunity to build a sales team. Sales recruiting experience will be helpful, although a successful sales professional who wants to move into management may have what it takes. This segment of our business has real growth potential, and we are offering a ground-floor opportunity.

National Accounts Manager 
(Posted 10/26/99)

This position is responsible for the continuing development of our relationships with large national accounts. To promote our products and assure proper product mix in their stores, this manager will work with store managers and owners, detail groups, sales representatives and buyers. Experience with large home center chains, both at the buyer level and with in-store service is necessary. You will travel widely, being away as much as 35% of the time.

In our company this is a high-profile position. It is a "people position," and requires attention to detail, self-motivation and an organized approach to major account responsibility.

Product Manager
(Posted 10/16/99)

Product Managers are charged with planning, organizing and controlling assigned product lines through the entire product life cycle, from the concept stages through market introduction and support. They manage progress toward product growth objectives, conduct market and competitor analysis and suggest strategies for optimizing profit.

Requirements include a bachelors degree and a minimum of 5 years of experience in a related field. Contemporary market research and product development techniques should be part of that experience. This is a leadership position requiring good presentation and writing skills, ability to work under time pressure, and an organized approach to tasks.
