Product Line Expands

Through the years the company's MARSHALLTOWN product line has greatly expanded. Originally supplying hand tools for only the masonry trades, now hand tools for the drywall, plastering, and tiling professions can be found in the MARSHALLTOWN line as well.

In 1982, the company tapped a new market by introducing a do-it-yourself line for the homeowner. This new line, called NU-PRIDE, consists of less expensive tools, ideal for homeowner use. Later in 1992, a third line, Quality Line Tools or QLT by MARSHALLTOWN, was established to offer value-priced quality for professionals.  The company's three lines, MARSHALLTOWN, QLT, and NU-PRIDE, provide price and quality ranges to meet market expectations.
