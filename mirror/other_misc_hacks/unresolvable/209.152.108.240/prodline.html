Through the years, the MARSHALLTOWN product line has greatly expanded. Originally supplying hand tools for only the masonry trades, tools for the drywall, plastering, and tiling professions are now available.

  

The Marshalltown Premier Line represents our top of the line tools. These tools are intended for the true professional who works in his/her particular trade all day, everyday. His/her tools have to stand up to heavy, daily use, and Marshalltown's tools do just that and more!
In 1992, a third line, Quality Line Tools or QLT, was established to offer value-priced quality for professionals.
In 1982, the company expanded into a new market by introducing a do-it-yourself line for the homeowner. This new line, called NU-PRIDE, consists of less expensive tools, ideal for homeowner use. An expansion of the NU-PRIDE line is made up of disposable BLT tools.
