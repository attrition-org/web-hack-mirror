Plant and Production Positions

Maintenance Mechanic – 2nd or 3rd Shift.
(Posted 10/22/99)

Maintenance mechanics support our production efforts by performing electrical, mechanical and machining tasks on new or older plant equipment. They must be able to operate lathes, milling machines, drill presses and the related hand tools and measuring instruments. Knowledge of pneumatics, hydraulics and welding practices are highly desirable. This is a skilled position, and requires the ability to perform complex tasks without close supervision.
