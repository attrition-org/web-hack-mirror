Satellite communication services include uplinks and downlinks. Uplink services are original broadcasts to a statewide or nationwide audience. Past clients include the VA Hospitals, The Illinois Geriatric Education Council, the NAACP and Morgan Stanley Dean Witter. Programs originate in our studios and are beamed to a ground station via our microwave link to the John Hancock Building.

Downlink services are simply receiving someone else's progam via satellite and distributing it to an on-campus location. From our studio control room we can distribute satellite programming to both Campus Unions, the Eye and Ear Infirmary and via the network video server and videoconferencing systems in the Academic Computing and Communications Center. We have a satellite viewing room that will hold up to 25 people that is available free of charge during regular business hours.

We can provide viewing of PBS Adult Learning Service events. Check out PBS'sschedule and give us a call to schedule a downlink of any program that suits your interests.

All consultations are free of charge. To inquire about our satellite communication services, call Joseph Faust at (312) 996-7912.
