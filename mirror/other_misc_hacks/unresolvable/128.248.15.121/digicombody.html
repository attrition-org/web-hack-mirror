Digital communications are expanding the reach of educators to learners in nonsynchronous distance learning formats. These include web-based courses, CD-ROMs, two-way compressed video transmission, and digital satellite broadcasts. We provide faculty support for adapting traditional lecture presentations and visual content to a variety of live and pre-recorded transmission formats. Whether it's via satellite, the web, a CD or a hybrid application of digital and analog technology, we can assist you in developing your material and maximizing your confidence and effectiveness.
