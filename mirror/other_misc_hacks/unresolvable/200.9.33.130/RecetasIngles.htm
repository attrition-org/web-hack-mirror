PALM HEART COCKTAIL

Make 4 servings

Ingredients:

2     cups palm heart in pieces
12   cooked shrimps
2/3  cups dressing
1/3  cup tomato sauce
1     tablespoon Spanish red pepper (paprika)
1-2  tablespoon sweet cream
2     tablespoons brandy or cognac
1/8  black pepper
       Lettuce leafs
       Salt

Method:

Settle the lettuce in 4 cups of cocktail. Distribute the palm heart and shrimps. Serve with sauce.

Sauce:

In a glass pot mix the tomato sauce, dressing, Spanish pepper, sweet cream, brandy or cognac, black pepper and salt.

PALM HEART QUICHE

Make 4 servings

Ingredients:

Paste:

1/4 pound butter
1 teaspoon sugar
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 1/4 cups wheat flour
4 tablespoons ice water

Method:

Cremate the butter with salt and sugar. Add the wheat flour until form crumbs. Add the ice water. Stretch the paste forming a circle. Cover Pyrex with the quiche and decorate the border. Stuff and cook at 350ºC for 40 minutes

Stuffing:

2 ounces butter
1/3 cup pricked onion
1/8 teaspoon pepper
1/8 teaspoon hickory nut
1 cup sweet cream
3 eggs
1 tablespoon wheat flour
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 cups lengths of palm heart
1 cup greater Swiss cheese
2 tablespoon grater Parmesan cheese

Crystallize the onion with butter, pepper and hickory nut. Add the sweet cream liquated with the eggs and the wheat flour. Place the palm heart on the paste, dust with the Swiss cheese, bath with the mixed sweet cream and dust with the Parmesan cheese.

PALM HEART CANAPES

Ingredients:

36 round bread with butter, baker on low heat until crisp. Not burned.

Stuffed:

2 ounces butter
1/2 cup pricked onion
1/8 teaspoon pepper
1/8 teaspoon hickory nut
2 cups palm heart in pieces
8 ounces cream cheese
1 tablespoon pricked capers
2 tablespoons grater Parmesan cheese
36 black olives in slices
Salt

Method:

Crystallize the onion with butter, hickory nut, and pepper. Add the palm heart and pricked capers. Cook it for one minute. Let Cool well to mix with the cream cheese beated. Add to the round bread, dust with the Parmesan cheese and decorate with black olive slices.

To serve, put it in pre heat oven at 350ºC for 4-5 minutes. Serve hot.

RICE WITH PALM HEART

Make 4-6 servings

Ingredients:

11/2 cup rice
1/2 k. palm heart in gross slices
3 cups palm heart or chicken tea
1 medium onion
3 garlic
2 tablespoons butter oil
Salt and pricked parsley

Method:

Cook the palm heart with little water during 20 minutes ads salt. Crystallize the onion and garlic, add the palm heart, rice and palm heart or chicken tea.

Boil it over high heat. When the rice is drain turn the heat to minimum heat until the rice is cooked. Before serve decorate with pricked parsley.

PALM HEART IN MUSHROOM SAUCE

Make 4-6 servings

Ingredients:

1 natural canned palm heart in pieces
1 cup milk
1 cup sweet cream
2 tablespoons wheat flour
2 tablespoons pricked parsley
2 tablespoons butter
1/4 dried thyme
1/2 k. mushrooms in slices
1/2 pricked onion
Salt and pepper

Method:

Fuse the butter and add the onion until crystallized. Add the mushrooms and thyme. Cook during 10 minutes. Add the wheat flour and mix well. Add the milk and cream, stirring often. Bath the palm heart with the mixed. Decorate with parsley. Serve immediately.

CHEESE AND PALM HEART FONDUE

Ingredients:

1 natural canned palm heart
1 cup sweet cream
250 gram great Gouda cheese
1 garlic
2 teaspoon hominy
Hot pepper, pepper, dust onion, salt, benne cookie or toast bread with benne

Method:

Cut the palm heart in pieces. Scrub the onion in a ceramic pot. Add the sweet cream, hominy dissolved with water or milk, salt, pepper, onion, palm heart. Cook over medium heat about 5 minutes. Add the cheese stir with wood spoon. Introduce the palm heart pieces on the cheese and place on the bread or cookies and eat.

PALM HEART SOUFLE

Ingredients:

1 cup thick white sauce
1 cup grater ripe cheese
4 egg yolks
1/8 teaspoon powder mustard
4 snow bright eggs
1 big canned natural palm heart
salt

Method:

Cut the palm heart in 1 inch pieces. Mix the white sauce with cheese, egg yolks, palm heart and mustard. Involved with the snow bright eggs. Bake at 180ºC during 20 minutes and eat inmediately.

PALM HEART WITH CHICKEN

Make 8 servings

Ingredients:

1 chicken
250 grass. Canned palm heart
1/2 bar butter
1/3 cup wheat flour
2 cups chicken soup
1/2 cup sweet cream
1/4 cup dries sherry wine
1 onion
1 carrot
3 egg yolks
Salt and pepper

Method:

Cook the chicken over medium heat with 4 caps of water, onion, carrot and salt.

Fuse the butter and add the wheat flour, chicken soup, sweet cream, salt and pepper. Finally, add the yolks mixed with sauce until get thick, the sherry wine and set aside.

Place in a Pyrex chicken pieces add palm heart pieces. Bath with sauce and sherry wine. Place it in the oven at 175ºC or 350ºF during 10 minutes.

PALM HEART TWISTS

Ingredients:

1 cup thick white sauce
1 cup grater ripe cheese
4 egg yolks
1/8 teaspoon powder mustard
4 snow bright eggs
1 big canned natural palm heart
Salt

Method:

Place a palm heart piece in a ham slice and twist. Place each twist in a small tray and flow the white sauce on the twists. Baker at 175ºC during 15 minutes.

GRATINADO PALM HEART

Make 6 servings

Ingredients:

2 cups sour cream
1 tablespoon dijon mustard
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon pepper
2 egg yolks
2 tablespoons palm heart cut into 1-inch pieces
2 ounces butter
2/3-cup bread crumbs
2 tablespoons Parmesan cheese into pieces

Method:

Combine sour cream, Dijon mustard, salt and pepper. Place it in a double boiler, stirring often about 15 minutes. Add the yolks dissolved with 2 tablespoon of cold water. Still cooking and stirring until becomes thicker. Add the palm heart and stir for 5 more minutes. Make 6 dish servings dust with the covering and place it in the oven for 350º C for 10 minutes and serve.

Covering:

Fuse the butter add dust bread. Crisp 2 to 3 minutes stirring often.

Let Cool and add Parmesan cheese.
