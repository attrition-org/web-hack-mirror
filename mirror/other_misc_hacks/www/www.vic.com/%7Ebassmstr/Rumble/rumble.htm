The Bassmasters of the Tennessee Valley (BMTV), Nichols Marine, and SunTrust Bank are proud to announce the Second Annual Rumble at the River (formerly the Federation Club Clash-for-Cash).  BMTV and other local B.A.S.S. Federation clubs wish to challenge all Federated clubs to a rumble for bragging rites, to see who can carry home the trophy.   This tournament is a tournament within a tournament, with club against club, and fisherman against fisherman.  One or two lucky participants will even tow home a brand new 18½ foot Hawk Super 1800 bass boat and/or an aluminum brush boat.

 

When: October 9, 1998
Where: Fort Loudon/Tellico Lakes
Lenoir City Park
Lenoir City Tennessee
