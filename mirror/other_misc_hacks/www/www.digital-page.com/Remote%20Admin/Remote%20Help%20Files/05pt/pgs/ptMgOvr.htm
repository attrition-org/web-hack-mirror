Managing print service 

    You can control how and when print jobs are printed, how print queues function, and which printers are enabled or disabled. You can also control where print jobs are stored on the server.

    This section tells you how to

      Manage print queues
    Enable or disable secure printing, change default queue settings, edit the attached printers list, enable or disable queue protocols, detach printers from a queue, delete a queue
    Manage print jobs
    Move a job to the top of a queue, put a job on or off hold, assign or remove a printer assignment, and delete a job
    Change the print jobs folder
    The location where print jobs are stored
