AppleTalk resources  

    These and many books on AppleTalk are available through most computer bookstores.
    • "AppleTalk Network System Overview" is a condensed version of "Inside AppleTalk."
    • "Inside AppleTalk" (second edition) is a comprehensive look at the AppleTalk protocol architecture.
    • "Planning and Managing AppleTalk Networks" provides an in-depth discussion of administering a small- to medium-sized AppleTalk network.
    • "Understanding Computer Networks" is an introduction to the how, what, and why of networking.
    • "AppleShare IP," Tom Dell. Academic Press, 1998.
    • "Managing AppleShare Workgroup Servers," Dorian J. Couglias and Tom Dell. Network Frontiers, 1995.

World Wide Web resources  

    These and many books on the World Wide Web are available through most computer bookstores.
    • "Elements of Web Design" Darcy Dinucci (with Lynne Stiles and Maria Giudice). Peach Pit Press.
    • "HTML: The Definitive Guide," Chuck Musciano and Bill Kennedy. O'Reilly & Associates, 1996.
    • "Webmaster Macintosh," Bob LeVitus and Jeff Evans.

Internet and TCP/IP resources  

    These and many books on the Internet are available through most computer bookstores.
    • "Internetworking with TCP/IP," Douglas Comer. Prentice-Hall, Inc., 1988.
    • "The Internet Connection," John S. Quaterman and S. Carl-Mitchell. Addison-Wesley, 1994.
    • "TCP/IP Network Administration," Craig Hunt. O'Reilly & Associates, Inc., 1992.
    • "Using Internet E-Mail," Will Sadler. QUE Corp., 1995.
    • "How the Internet Works," Preston Gralla. Ziff-Davis Press, 1997.
