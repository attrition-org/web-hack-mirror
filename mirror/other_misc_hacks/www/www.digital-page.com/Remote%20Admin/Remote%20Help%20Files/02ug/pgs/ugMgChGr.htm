Changing group memberships

    To add or remove users from a group, follow these steps:
    1 Log on to AppleShare IP Remote Admin.
    2

    Click the Users button.


       
    3 Click the Group List button.
    4 Click the name of the group you want to edit.
    5 In the Edit Group window that appears, click Group Memberships.
    6 To add a user to the group, click a user in the Users list and click Add. To remove a user from the group, click a user in the Group Members list and click Remove.
    7 When you're done adding and removing users, click Done.
