Print Server specifications

    • Supported printers: AppleTalk LaserWriter printers, third-party PostScript printers that support the LaserWriter 8.0 or later printer driver, and LPR/LPD-compatible printers
    • Maximum number of attached printers: 30
    • Maximum number of queues: 10
    • Maximum number of concurrent connections: 32
    • Maximum total number of jobs: 500
    • Maximum log size: 64K
