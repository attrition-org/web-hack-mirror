Journey to the Center of the Earth
Based on the novel by

Alien Voices' production of "Journey to the Center of the Earth" is the fascinating story of three explorers who descend to the center of the earth and live to tell the tale. What they find will amaze you. Leonard Nimoy, John de Lancie, and cast of Star Trek actors take you on this incredible journey.
