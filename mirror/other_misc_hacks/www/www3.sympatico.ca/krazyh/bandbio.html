KrazY HousE

... is a group of dedicated individuals who have embarked on a musical quest: to promote awareness of, and compassion for, self and others through the creation and performance of original music. The band plays an original concoction of musical styles that has most recently been described as "CONTEMPORARY FOLK ROCK"

Strong songwriting is the cornerstone of this acoustic-based band. The songs are driven by three female vocalists. HARMONIES ABOUND!!! The lyrics cover a myriad of topics and emotions from introspective examinations and piercing social criticisms to fun-lovin', feel good romps and of course, a love song or two.

KH has had the good fortune to work with producer Rick Hutt (Tom Cochrane, Northern Pikes, Jamie Warren) of Cedartree Studio in Kitchener, Ont., over the last four years. This has lead to the production of our first four-song cd: "4 Now" and our latest full-length cd: "In The Beginning". We are currently in the process of recording our next cd project which we hope will be released this fall.
