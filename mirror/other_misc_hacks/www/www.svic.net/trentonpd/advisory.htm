Gilchrist County Law Enforcement Council

The Gilchrist County Law Enforcement Council is made up of business owners and citizens.  It meets the last Friday of every month.  this Council has lunch together with the Police Officers and they give us input as to what their needs are.   The Police Department puts on an interesting program and has a guest speaker.   If you are in the general area and would like to attend one of these meetings, please join us.  The director of the council is Dr. Robert L. Vaughan, Jr. and he can be reached at 463-8120
