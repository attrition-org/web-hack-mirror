US Wings Signature Series™
Our US Wings-labeled line of flight jackets. Authentic and unique jackets, available exclusively from US Wings! Be sure to check out our newest A-2: the A-2 2000! go there!
B-3 Bomber Jackets
The B-3 Sheepskin Bomber Jacket kept our bomber crews warm during WWII and will keep you equally warm today! Very stylish... the perfect winter jacket. go there!
Avirex Jackets
We offer a wide selection of popular Avirex flight jackets, including A-2s, G-1s and B-3s. go there!
Snoopy Cap, Goggles, & Scarf
Our popular "Snoopy" Leather Flying Cap and Goggles recall the glorious days of open cockpit flying! Complete the authentic look with our 100% Silk Aviator's Scarf. go there!
Indy Jones Jackets
One of our most popular jackets, and the perfect jacket for your next adventure! Available in a variety of leathers, including kangaroo, lambskin, goatskin, and cowhide. go there!
Signature Series A-2 2000
Our A-2 2000 jacket is the same as our USAF-issue goatskin A-2, but features side-entry pockets (which the military version lacks). Check out our wide selection of other A-2s as well. go there!
US Wings Signature Series G-1
Our Signature Series Goatskin G-1 is featured in the movie "The 6th Day" -- we also have a variety of other G-1 jackets, including antique lambskin and Top Gun versions. go there!
Lil' Indy Jacket - For Kids!
We have a great selection of authentic military jackets, scaled-down for your pint-sized aviators and adventurers! go there!
Limited-Edition Flying Tigers A-2
We are offering this outstanding A-2, hand-painted by Rosemary Dery, for a limited time only. Be sure to check out our other Flying Tigers items, too. go there!

Cowhide VIP Indy Jones Jacket (added 1/18/01): We are once again offering our VIP Indy Jones jacket... for a limited time. We recently received a small re-supply of French Cowhide, so these jackets are only available in limited quantities. Get yours while they're still available!
Limited Edition Hand-Painted A-2s (added 1/13/01): We are now offering A-2s hand-painted by Rosemary Dery. These are exclusive designs, and we will feature a new design every quarter (3 months). This quarter: the AVG Flying Tigers.
Hand-Painted Leather Art (added 1/8/01): Due to the popularity of our hand-painted jackets, we now offer hand-painted leather artwork suitable for framing.
Bud Anderson Collection (added 12/23/00): A collection of items paying tribute to Bud Anderson and "Old Crow", his P-51 Mustang. Anderson became a triple ace with the 357th FG, with 16 air victories. We feature his book "To Fly and Fight" as well as a hand-painted vintage A-2 jacket (available soon).
Custom Leather Nametags (added 12/5/00): This is the ultimate personal touch you can add to your flight jacket! Choose from USN/USMC, USAF, or US Army wings, and add up to two lines of text. 100% military-issue!
