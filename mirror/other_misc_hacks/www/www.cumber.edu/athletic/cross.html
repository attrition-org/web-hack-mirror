Staff Directory

After an outstanding 3rd place finish in the NAIA National Meet, the Indians are looking forward to another great season.  The 1998 Indians captured the Mid-South Regional Championship with the first seven places going to Cumberland runners who earned All Conference honors.  In the National Meet, three Cumberland runners earned All American honors last year: Will Hodges, who placed 14th, Jackson Ochaka 18th, and Leroy Thomas 19th. 

     With Hodges and Ochaka returning this year, we are sure to see another appearance at Nationals for Cumberland.

     The Lady Indians finished the 1998 season with a 2nd place finish in the Mid-South Regional Meet.  Cumberland's Brittany Riggins snatched 1st place in the meet.  Riggins also grabbed a 4th place finish at the NCCAA National Meet.

     The Lady Indians hope to improve this year with a very strong recruiting class.  Coach Stroud expects this season will be the  best ever for the Lady
Indians.
