Cumberland College Wrestling

Visit the New Women's Wrestling Web Page!!!

Welcome to the web site of Cumberland College Wrestling Indians. The season is under way and the intensity is heating up.  This is the wrestling team's 4th season and they have improved every year finishing 8th at last year's National Tournament.  We look forward to the team moving up several more notches this year.  Our Indians started the season by defeating Anderson College.  Kevin Case from Cynthiana, Kentucky, and Michael Irving from Pompano Beach, Florida, are leading the team thus far in what looks to be another successful season for the team.

Juan Bell, the team captain, could be lost for the season with an injury.  This is a huge loss, so several of the freshmen will have to step up and assume the role.  Some of the freshmen that will have an immediate impact for the Indians are Florida High School State Champ Andy Medders and two time Indiana State Champ Robert Rosbottom.  "Overall this team is young but very talented.  Success may not be instant but it will come real soon.  The guys work really hard and improve daily.  By the season's end, we will be a tough squad to beat," stated Coach Wilder.
