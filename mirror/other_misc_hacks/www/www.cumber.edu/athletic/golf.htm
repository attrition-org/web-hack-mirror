Golf

MEN'S

     The Cumberland College Indians have a great tradition in golf.   The Indians were KIAC champions 1990 through 1996.  Cumberland is now in a newly formed Mid-South Conference.  The Mid-South Conference is a very competitive conference.  The conference features North Greenville, Cumberland College, Cumberland University, Lambuth University, Georgetown College, Campbellsville University, and Union College.  Cumberland College finished second in 1997 and 1998.  North Greenville has won the first two conference championships.  The conference tournament is held in Hopkinsville, KY, at Western Hills Country Club.  The Indians are coached by head coach Bill Sergent and assistant Kevin Lawson.

WOMEN'S

     The Lady Indians are a new but extremely competitive golf team.   Last year in their first ever NAIA national tournament, the Lady Indians finished 15th in the tournament.  This year in the pre-season poll, the Lady Indians were ranked 9th in the country.  Even though the Lady Indians are young, they figure to be very competitive in the NAIA this year.  The Lady Indians have 2 juniors, 2 sophomores, and 2 freshmen.  The Lady Indians are part of the Mid-South Conference which features seven schools.  The schools are North Greenville, Cumberland College, Cumberland University, Lambuth University, Georgetown College, Campbellsville University, and Union College.  The conference tournament will be held in Hopkinsville, KY at Western Hills Country Club.  The Lady Indians are coached by head coach Bill Sergent and assistant Kevin Lawson.
