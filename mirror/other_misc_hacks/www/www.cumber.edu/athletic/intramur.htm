Intramural Activities

The school year has four major intramural sports seasons: football, basketball, volleyball, and softball. Teams are placed in A-league or B-league play according to skill level. Students who participate in intercollegiate athletics may only participate on intramural teams other than their intercollegiate sport(s). Because team points are accrued for entries, wins, and losses, teams are encouraged to participate in many different activities. Winners of an intramural activity will receive T-shirts in recognition of their accomplishments.

The intramural programs provide an outlet for study breaks, fun, fellowship, and healthy recreation. So get involved and join a team!
