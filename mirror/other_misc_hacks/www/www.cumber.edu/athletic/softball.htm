SOFTBALL
1999

     The 1998 season proved to be an exciting one for the Lady Indians Softball Team!  Finishing tied for 3rd place after Conference play, the Lady Indians got "RED HOT" during the Mid-South Conference Tournament.  Posting impressive wins over Conference regular-season champ, Lambuth University, and runner-up, Cumberland University, the lady Indians became 1998 Conference Tournament Champions.  The momentum carried over to the Regional Tournament held in Frankfort, KY.  The Lady Indians started off with a win over Tennessee Wesleyan and then beat Lipscomb University, who had been ranked in the top ten nationally.   Unfortunately, when the rain moved in the momentum was lost, and the Lady Indians lost 3-4 in the semi-final game in 9 innings to Lee University.  They also lost to Lambuth University 3-4 in 9 inning, and placed 3rd in the Region.  The 1998-99 season looks to be even better; six seniors and all of the starting infield and outfield return.  Key returnees are:
          Shannon Evans - All-Conference MVP 1996
                                    All-Conference MVP 1997
                                    NCCAA All-American 1997
                                    All-MSC 1998
          Lynn Tallent - All-Conference Utility Player 1996
                                All-MSC Honorable Mention 1998
          Amy Long
          Bekkah Massaro
          Emily Vought
          Shannon Farris
          Faun Whitteaker
          Amanda Walton
          Cathy Duty - All-MSC Honorable Mention
     Cathy Duty, Emily Vought, Amy Long and Amy Anelli were also Academic MSC 1998. 
     Key recruits are:
          Jessica Woodward from Springfield, OH
          Sunny Bowden from Allardt, TN
          Heather Branum from Heiskell, TN
          Heather Kenyon from Castlewood, VA
