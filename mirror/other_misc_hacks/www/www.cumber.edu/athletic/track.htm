Track

Indoor and outdoor track have also been added recently to the athletic agenda in Indian country. Both men and women compete on the state-of-the-art facility at the new athletic complex. Gareth Wilford competed in the 1995 outdoor nationals and made the All-American team in the 1500 meters. In the 1997 season, Gareth won the individual championship at the Mid-South Conference Championship held in Lexington, Kentucky. Head Coach Floyd Stroud was named conference Coach-of-the-year.
