     Indian Sports News

The Sports Information Department, headed by Bill Sergent, is in charge of all media relations for Cumberland's Athletic Teams.  Bill is assisted this year by Shannon Evans and Mike Abbott.  Sports Information gathers statistics from the teams and provides the local media with information about teams' and athletes' performances and accomplishments.  Sports Information also reports to athletes' home town media concerning personal accomplishments.  The Sports Information staff is composed of students in the work study program.
