Baseball

     Winning baseball has been a Cumberland tradition for over thirty years. Former Indian Coach Walt Mathes was inducted into the NAIA Baseball Hall of Fame in 1988, the result of his 21-year 396-202 record. Under the tutelage of current Coach Terry Stigall, the Indians are 373-198 over the past thirteen seasons. Eight of his players have signed professional contracts.
