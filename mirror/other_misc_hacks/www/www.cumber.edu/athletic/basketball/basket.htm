Men's Basketball
     The winning tradition at Cumberland College is unprecedented.  In the past two decades we have held a regular seat in the NAIA national rankings with twelve trips to the National Tournament in Tulsa, Oklahoma.  Cumberland is a two time NAIA National Tournament Quarterfinalist and reached the NAIA Final Four in 1996-97.  Cumberland basketball is proud to have had four NAIA All Americans.
     This season will bring in a lot of new faces, but goals will remain the same.  Cumberland is entering its fifth year in the Mid-South Conference.  Prior to that, Cumberland was a member of the KIAC.  From 1979 until it disbanded in 1994 we earned seven KIAC Regular Season titles and eight NAIA District 32 Championship titles.

Women's Basketball
     The Lady Indians enter their third year under the guidance of head coach Melissa Irvin.  This year's team is young with only one senior, Beverly Mutter.  With two Division I transfers and six returning players the team hopes to fair well.
     The Lady Indians' strength this year lies in their versatility.  The Indians are two and three deep in every position.  The team will be traveling to Orlando, Florida in December to participate at the Disney Wide World of Sports.  This year's team looks to finish strong in a very tough Mid-South Conference.
