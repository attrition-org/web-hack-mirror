     The 1999-2000 season looks great for the Indian cheerleaders.  In addition to cheering at all home games for football and basketball, the cheerleading squad competes with other squads in the conference as well as at the UCA National Competition.  This year the squad hopes to move up to Division II in the UCA Nationals and to finish in the top four.  They will also compete in the Ameri-Cheer competition.  With a strong core of 14 returning cheerleaders and a large recruiting class of 15 members, the Indians hope to regain the Mid-South Conference title.

     Students with the spirit and talent to cheer at the collegiate level will love being at Cumberland College.  Since Cumberland considers cheerleading a sport, we can offer scholarships to athletes who prove themselves worthy.
