Cumberland Inn
General Information



Located in Williamsburg, Kentucky, the Cumberland Inn, a property of Cumberland College, is just off Interstate I-75 at Exit 11.

Whether your needs involve a business meeting, a banquet, a wedding or just a nice get away, please consider the Inn for your future plans.  To learn more about the Cumberland Inn, just click on one of the maps below.

The staff at the Inn wants to make your visit a pleasant and enjoyable experience.  If you have any questions or concerns, please feel free to contact us at (606)539-4100 or 1-800-315-0286.

The property consists of three buildings -  the Allison Templeton Adams Center, the Juie Jackson Templeton Hall and the Reese Templeton Adams Hall.
