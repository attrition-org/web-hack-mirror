Welcome to Cumberland College CyberChat

We are glad that you are joining us today. Please feel free to explore our web site as well as our chat rooms presented in CyberChat.

Here are helpful hints that you may want to keep in mind as you enter CyberChat:
1. The first page is a participant information page. This lets us know a little about you. Please fill this out completely. *click submit*

2. *Click here to enter Cyberchat* (this takes just a moment to load)

3. You will now be at the log-in for CyberChat. Please enter your "nick" (the name you will be known by in the chat rooms) as well as your E-mail address. Go directly into the lobby (stated #lobby). From this point there will be someone to help you initially, and you can also find other rooms you may be interested in here. Just double-click on the room you want to go into and it will take you there. *Note: There may be more rooms than can be seen; be sure to scroll down the list to see what is available.

4. To chat: type in the bottom line and press enter when you are ready for the words to be posted. DON'T LIMIT YOURSELF TO ONE AREA-use this to visit all areas you may be interested in.

5. If you have any questions while you are in CyberChat, just press the HELP button on the top right of the screen.

6. To QUIT, type /quit.

THANK YOU FOR YOUR INTEREST IN CUMBERLAND COLLEGE AND WE INVITE YOU TO VISIT US IN PERSON SOON!
