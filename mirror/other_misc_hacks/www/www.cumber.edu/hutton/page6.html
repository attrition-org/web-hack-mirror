CUMBERLAND COLLEGE

A COMMITMENT TO SERVICE

Cumberland College's Leadership and Community Service Program is founded on the conviction that by equipping students for lives of leadership and service we are making an investment not only in their lives but also in the future of America.  One program that uniquely illustrates Cumberland's commitment to these ideals is Mountain Outreach, a volunteer home-building program created by two Cumberland College students in 1981.  This student-staffed program has since built 75 homes and collected and distributed clothing for needy Appalachian families who were previously without the luxury of running water, electricity, sufficient clothing, and/or adequate shelter.  In August of 1990, President George Bush named Cumberland College's Mountain Outreach program the 220th Point of Light.
