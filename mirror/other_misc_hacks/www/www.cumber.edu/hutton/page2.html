ABOUT DR. EDWARD L. HUTTON

Dr. Edward L. Hutton has distinguished himself in the business world and has become known throughout the world for his business acumen.  He began his career in 1946 as Deputy Director of the Joint Export/Import Agency, United States, United Kingdom, and Berlin before becoming Vice President-Director of the World Commerce Cooperation.  He then went on to become a consultant in international trade and finance, and executive Vice President and Financial Vice President of Ward Industries.  He has held numerous positions with W.R. Grace and Company, including Assistant Vice President and Executive Vice President.  He has also served as Chairman of DeBois Chemical Company.  Currently he is Chairman of OmniCare, Inc., and Roto-Rooter, Inc., and is President and Executive Officer of the Chemed Coperation.
In addition, Dr. Hutton currently serves as a Director of Chemed Corporation; Omnicare, Inc.; Roto-Rooter, Inc.; National Sanitary Supply; Grade Energy Corporation; and Sonic Corporation.  He has held many other directorships and trusteeships, including the Veratex Corporation, the Oppenheimer Fund, the International Bank of Washington, D.C., the International Trust Company of Liberia, Bestal Laboratories, and several other insurance companies.
In addition to the Hutton Center for Leadership Studies and the Hutton Townhouses, Dr. Hutton has provided vision and support for Cumberland's Leadership/Community Service program and the Hutton Woods Community.
Dr. Hutton's distinguished accomplishments in business and community service provide an outstanding example for the Cumberland College community as to what can be accomplished through hard work, dedication, and service.
