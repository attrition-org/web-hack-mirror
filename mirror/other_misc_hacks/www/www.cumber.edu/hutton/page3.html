Cumberland College has a long history of dedication to preparing its students for success both in and out of the classroom.  Believing that the most meaningful and rewarding life is one spent in service to others, Cumberland has implemented a unique and innovative Leadership and Community Service program designed to empower and equip students to become compassionate agents of change, to use their talents and abilities to assist in meeting the needs of others, and to inspire and instill the conviction that service to others is a life-long responsibility.

Cumberland College recognizes the importance of providing students both practical, hands-on experience as well as academic exposure to many different types and areas of leadership and community service.  Consequently, Cumberland has taken steps to ensure that its facilities are suitable to accommodate such ventures.  Dedicated in the spring of 1995, the Hutton Center for Leadership Studies houses resources for leadership training and service seminars, complimenting Cumberland's rich liberal arts curriculum.
