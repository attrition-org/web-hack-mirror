Welcome to

AWACS !

This project supplies both students and teachers with resources, tools, and information to assist in a variety of writing tasks.  AWACS is coordinated jointly by the Cumberland College Department of English and the Academic Resource Center.   Here you will find materials gathered by academic departments on campus to aid in writing tasks appropriate to their respective professional activities. Here you will also find information about and access to resources on campus, as well as to resources available on the Internet. These materials include student samples, documentation guidelines, editing tools, and writing exercises.  Should you need assistance in using these materials or like to make any suggestions to improve this project, please contact the appropriate person(s) listed on the Email Contacts page.
