Academic Resource Center

Before you drown in the sea of knowledge, board the ARC!

hours:
Monday-Thursday 8-5, 6-9
Friday 8-4

email:  arc@cc.cumber.edu

phone: 539-4312

Located on the main floor of the Grey Brick Building, the ARC is a place designed with a variety of services to help bridge the gap between the skills you possess and the skills you need.

Tutoring -- Tutors are available for one-on-one study and review sessions.  We try to provide tutors for all academic fields (such as English, math, science, and history).
Study Groups -- The ARC can also provide support and leadership for group study sessions.
Computers -- Equipped with a lab, the ARC provides computerized tutorials in various disciplines.  Our lab has student workers who can assist you with getting started on the tutorial program of your choice.
Test Preparation --  Upon request, the ARC can also assist with preparation for exit examinations such as the NTE and GRE.
Other Resources -- In addition, the ARC has study guides, English information sheets, math video tutorals, and other resources . . . just ask!
