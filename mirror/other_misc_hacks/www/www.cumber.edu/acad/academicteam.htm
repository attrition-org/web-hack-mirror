Cumberland College Academic Team

     Cumberland College has an active and successful academic team that competes in the Kentucky College Quick Recall League.  The team has enough members to field both Division I and Division II teams.  We participate in every tournament the league holds.

     Division I is open to freshmen through seniors.   Participation as a Division II player is restricted to freshmen and sophomores.

     This year's schedule sees us having to take four overnight trips.
