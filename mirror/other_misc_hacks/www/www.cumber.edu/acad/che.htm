The Department of Chemistry

If you're interested in attending graduate school in chemistry, going directly into employment as an industrial chemist, or planning a career in environmental studies, especially research, entering a preprofessional medically related area (medicine, dentistry, optometry, pharmacy, etc.), becoming a secondary education teacher, or combining your studies in chemistry with another field (e.g., chemistry/business or chemistry/English), then you'll find the educational training you need at Cumberland.

You'll benefit from access to top-notch equipment in our classrooms and laboratories, and you'll work closely with experienced professors, who care about you and what you learn.

In addition to course work, our chemistry focus is also on undergraduate research, where students work with a professor on a one-to-one basis on an actual research project.
