College Cumberland Academic Departments

The Business Administration Department of Cumberland College offers B.S. degrees with several separate majors. 

Each must include general education, business core, and specialized upper level courses.   

Candidates for this degree must successfully complete the specified hours in the major plus 45 hours of general education requirements, and either 15 hours of restricted electives, another major, or a minor in combination with a sufficient number of course hours to meet the Cumberland College overall requirements of 128 semester hours

Accounting majors have an additional option of either a 128 semester hour B.S. degree or a 150 hour B.S. degree for those who want to sit for the CPA exam without obtaining a masters degree.

Business Administration majors take advanced courses in finance, marketing, management, or hotel management depending on their emphasis area.  A minor in Business Administration is a very desirable option for students planning on specific professional careers that will be operated as small businesses.

Business and Marketing Education majors are preparing to teach these topics in secondary schools.  They not only need knowledge of the business subject material, but also need preparation for the teaching profession.

Office Administration majors are preparing to organize and direct efficient office environments.  A minor in Office Administration is also available.

Transfer students in Business must complete at least 12 hours in the minor or 18 hours in the major at Cumberland College.

Junior college and community college courses may be accepted as meeting lower division (100-299 course numbers) Business Administration Department requirements, but may not satisfy more than three hours of upper division (courses numbered 300 or above) Business Administration Department requirements.

REQUIREMENTS FOR THE BACHELOR OF ARTS - Completion of the requirements for the B.S. plus 12 semester hours of the same modern foreign language.
