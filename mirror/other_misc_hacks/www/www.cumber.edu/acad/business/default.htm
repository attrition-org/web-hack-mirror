Welcome to the Business Administration Department of Cumberland College.

We invite you to get to know us and see what we have to offer. 

This site is organized into the topics of :

About us - explains the goals of this department, discusses changing opportunities in business, and provides a convenient means for you to contact us.

Catalog - shows the degree requirements, course descriptions and class schedules.

Faculty - lists the faculty.  Separate pages show each faculty member's detail teaching fields, contact information, qualifications, and current schedule.

Interaction - offers involvement in the business student organizations, web links useful to those with an interest in business, and a feedback survey that lets us know how to help you succeed!
