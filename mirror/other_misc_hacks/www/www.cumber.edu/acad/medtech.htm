Medical Technology

After your educational training at Cumberland College, you may become responsible for the accurate performance of laboratory tests that determine the presence, absence, or causes of disease. A medical technologist must be able to conduct a full range of laboratory tests, from the most routine to the most complex, with little or no supervision. Medical technologists are also responsible for the operation of computers, precision instruments, and other complex electronic equipment. A medical technologist may also function as a supervisor, educator, or researcher.
