In the field of history, you’ll be able to take courses from the great cultures of ancient times to Twentieth Century America. Whatever your interests, you’ll be able to explore them to your heart’s content. At the same time, you’ll be able to investigate other areas of history and learn to make connections and draw conclusions based on what you learn.

The history curriculum also allows you to earn a second major by taking electives in another field of your choice. For example, with a double major in history and business administration, you would be able to seek a job in business and industry and, at the same time, demonstrate a strong ability to reflect, reason, and interpret information.

George Washington crossing the Delaware
