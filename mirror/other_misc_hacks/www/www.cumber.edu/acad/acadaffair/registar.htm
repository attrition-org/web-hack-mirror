E-mail and faxed transcript requests will not be accepted.

The following information is required to process transcripts:

*  Name (maiden and married)

*  Social Security Number

*  Date of Birth

*  Dates of attendance at Cumberland College

*  List the number of transcripts you want sent and whether they are Graduate or Undergraduate

*  Graduate and Undergraduate transcripts are separate transcripts and are charged individually

*  Include your current address and phone number where you can be reached

*  Include the address where the transcript is to be mailed

*  Include the transcript fee:  ($5.00 each official : $2.00 each unofficial)

*  Student's signature must be on the request

The student must have a zero balance with the Bursar's Office and be current with any student loan.

Students of SUE BENNETT COLLEGE should follow the above procedure when requesting transcripts.  Indicate on the request that you attended Sue Bennett College.
