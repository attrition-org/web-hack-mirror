The Communication Arts program at Cumberland College includes courses in public speaking, television production, performance studies, argumentation and debate, media studies, and a wide variety of other communication-related subjects. The Theatre Arts program includes training in acting, directing, technical production, and many other aspects of the dramatic arts.
