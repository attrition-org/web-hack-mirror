Pre-Physical Therapy

Students interested in physical therapy as a career are strongly urged to earn a bachelor's degree prior to enrolling in physical therapy school. It does not matter to the physical therapy school what your major is as long as you have completed the necessary prerequisites. However, many pre-physical therapy students major in biology because of the science courses required for admission to physical therapy school. Your academic advisor will work with you to plan a program of study individualized to meet your needs.
