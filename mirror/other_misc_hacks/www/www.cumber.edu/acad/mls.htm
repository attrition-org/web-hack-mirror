Movement and Leisure Studies

The field of physical education is one of activity and service with many job opportunities available to those who are qualified. After you graduate, you will be qualified to teach physical education in elementary, middle, or senior high schools, pursue graduate study, or be considered for many other careers, including athletic coach, sports official, fitness program administrator, director of athletics, director of intramurals, camp director, and armed forces physical specialist.
