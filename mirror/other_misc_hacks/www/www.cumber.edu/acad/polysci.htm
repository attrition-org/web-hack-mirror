POLITICAL SCIENCE

Plato

By studying political science at Cumberland College, you'll be able to explore everything from introductory courses that survey the major concepts, issues, and controversies in political science to more advanced courses in public policy, constitutional law, political theory, comparative government, American foreign policy, the Presidency, and Congress.

If you want to prepare yourself for law school and a law career, consider that political science is probably the academic discipline that comes closest to focusing on what law does in society, how laws are made, the role of the courts, and other related topics. Although you may major in almost any field and still apply to law school, you can get a good feel for studying law by taking political science courses.
