Pre-Law

There is no preferred major for admission to law school, however, political science is probably the academic discipline that comes closest to focusing on what law does in society, how laws are made, the role of the courts, and other related topics. In order to be admitted to law school you must successfully complete the Law School Aptitude Test (LSAT), and your Cumberland advisor will work with you to identify the background courses needed to prepare you for the LSAT and your future success.
