IT'S ALL ABOUT DISCOVERY!   

       

 

The Cumberland College Department of Biology offers a wide variety of courses in the life sciences.  The biology faculty strive to teach majors and non-majors alike how to make informed decisions about contemporary scientific issues.  We believe that an education in the sciences should involve an active pursuit of knowledge, using a contemporary, relevant approach to teaching.

After graduation, our students pursue a wide variety of careers, including medicine, dentistry, veterinary medicine, teaching, as well many others. The Biology Program offers a Biology Major, a Biology Minor and a Teaching Biology Major.
