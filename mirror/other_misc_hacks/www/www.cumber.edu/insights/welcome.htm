Through a mixture of fun and helpful activities, INSIGHTS is designed to assist you in making the transition to college by preparing you for and educating you about all facets of college life. 

Many issues will be addressed in your INSIGHTS group:

getting around on campus
adjusting to new freedom and responsibility
meeting new people
balancing studying and extracurricular activities
developing more effective study skills and classroom behaviors

understanding the impact of a liberal arts education.

Your concerns will shape the issues that will be addressed.   For more information and INSIGHTS, select one of the menu items below.
