February 13 - February 26, 1999

The Cumberland College Art Gallery presents Faculty Photos of the West now through February 19th. The exhibit features photographs of the American West by faculty members Geraldine Housman and Kenneth Weedman. In addition, prints by Hilde Messner Pendick, a painter and graphic artist of international note, have been loaned to the Gallery by Dr. David Lockwood, retired C.C. art professor and long time friend of Ms. Pendick. The Gallery, located in the Andersen Building, is open weekdays.

STUDENT EMPLOYEES: 1998 K-2 and W-2 Forms, Wage and Tax Statements, are now available at the Bursar’s Office.

Resident Assistant Applications for Summer ’99, Fall ’99, and Spring 2000 semesters may be picked up in the Office of Student Services. Application and references are due in the Office of Student Services on February 1, 1999.

ALL STUDENTS: If you missed the mandatory housing meetings, be sure to attend the make-up opportunity Monday, February 1, at 10:00 a.m. in the Gray Brick Auditorium. Speakers are from the Cumberland River Rape Victim’s Services Office. Commuters are welcome.

The C.C. Bookstore Valentine’s Day Sale is February 1-12. Get 20% off all clothing and souvenirs!

February is BSU Penny Wars Month! Make your dorm a winner and help raise money for summer missions. Pennies count as positive points and everything else is a negative point.

The Cumberland College Music Department presents Andrea Anderson in concert, Tuesday, February 2, at 8:00 p.m. in the Recital Hall. Ms. Anderson, a concert pianist, has performed in the United States, China, Sweden, Finland, and Lithuania and has also played on radio and television. She received the Harold Bauer Award by the Manhattan School and a Rockefeller Foundation grant for further study and performance at the Aspen Music Festival.

The College Republicans will meet Wednesday, February 3, at 7:00 p.m. in Gray Brick 236.

The CAB Gameroom Tournament will be held Friday, February 5, and Saturday, February 6. Signups are 6:00 p.m. Friday in the Gameroom. Play begins at 7:00 p.m. with a pizza party following in the Grill at 10:00 p.m. Pool, spades, ping pong, and more!

Send a singing telegram and balloons to someone special. The Chamber Choir will deliver Valentine singing telegrams Thursday, Friday and Saturday, February 11, 12 and 13. Call ext. 4332 to reserve a time. Donations are accepted.

The SGA invites everyone to a Valentine’s Dance on Friday, February 12, from 9:00 p.m. to midnight in the I.M. gym. The dance will be semiformal and pictures will be offered from 8-9:30 p.m. Faculty and staff interested in chaperoning the dance should contact the SGA Office, ext. 4353.
