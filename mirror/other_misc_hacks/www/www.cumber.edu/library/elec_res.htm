CC Cat

 Library catalog of books, video tapes, music scores, CD-ROMs and other materials.

ProQuest

 General magazine index which includes many full-text articles.  Three databases cover general works, newspapers, and business journals.

EBSCO Indexes

 Searchable databases hosted by EBSCO.

Newsbank

 Index and full-text of newspaper articles from around the world.  Also includes current news Awareness.

First Search

 A collection of indexes to books, magazines and even conference proceedings.   Highlights include: "Worldcat", an index to over 30,000,000 books; "ERIC", an index to Education journals and submitted materials; "Contents First", index to 12,500 journals; and "Medline", index and abstract of medical articles.

Galenet

 A collection of four databases from Gale including "Contemporary Literary Criticism Select" and the full "Dictionary of Literary Biography."   Also includes "Gale's Business Resource" and "Peterson's GradSearch."

Britannica Online

 The on-line version of this famous encyclopedia.

Well Connected

 "WELL-CONNECTED covers over 90 common health problems and diseases -- nearly 2,000 pages of the latest in-depth information."

JSTOR

 A collection of academic journals all available in full-text.  Many of the journals are the standards in their field but are limited to journals more than four years old.

College Source

 A collection of college catalogs (full-text).

Congressional Quarterly

 On-line version of title of the same name.  Also includes a very useful Congressional Researcher.  "Your complete source on the most current and controversial issues of the day with complete summaries, all the pros and cons, bibliographies and more."

Mayo Clinic Health Oasis

 Health related topics and information.
