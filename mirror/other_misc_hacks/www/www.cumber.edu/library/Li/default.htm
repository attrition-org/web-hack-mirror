The Hagan Memorial Library offers instruction to Cumberland College
Students, Faculty and Staff

Scheduled sessions can cover CC Cat, FirstSearch, ProQuest Direct and includes a tour of the library.  Sessions can be tailored to meet the specific needs of a class.  Often a bibliography on relevant reference materials is included in the orientation session.  In addition, Internet Research classes for specific disciplines can be scheduled in one of the numerous campus labs.

 

English Comp I

 English Comp I orientation features training on the use of CC Cat, ProQuest Direct, a tour of the library, and a library assignment.

Subject Specific

 Subject Specific orientations provide research assistance for specific academic disciplines.  Materials covered include CC Cat, electronic databases, paper indexes, and reference materials pertaining to specific assignments.

Internet Research

 Internet Research orientations offer the opportunity for students to learn to conduct research via the World Wide Web.   Subjects covered include web page evaluation, search techniques and subject specific web sites.  This orientation provides the student with a hands-on learning experience.
