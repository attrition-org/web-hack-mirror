CC Cat Tutorial

CC Cat is a listing of all the books in the Hagan Memorial Library

There are numerous ways to find information in the Hagan Memorial Library electronic catalog. Using the
WORDS OR PHRASE, SUBJECT, AUTHOR or TITLE functions will provide you with relevant
information on your topic.

  • Words or Phrase - Searches all records for any match using the keywords which describe your topic.

  • Subject - Searches all records to identify books which address a specific topic using standardized subject
    headings.

  • Author - Provides books written by a particular author.

  • Title - Search for a specific book title.

  • Browsing - Search using author, titles, subject, series, or call number.

  • Combination - Searches, in combination, any or all of the keyword searches.

  • Author with Title - Searches for both author and title of a book.

  • Term - Locates books by author, titles, subject, or series.

  • Periodical Title - Listing of journal titles in the Hagan Memorial Library.

If you have questions about using the CC Cat, please ask a librarian.

 

FirstSearch Tutorial

FirstSearch is an electronic reference system designed to provide journal citations and some full-text articles

Use FirstSearch to find information from any of the following databases:

  • WorldCat - Books and other materials in libraries worldwide

  • Article1st - Articles from nearly 12,500 journals

  • Contents1st - Table of contents of nearly 12,500 journals

  • ECO - Electronic Collections Online.  Full text of scholarly journals

  • ERIC - Journal articles and reports in the field of education

  • FastDoc - Index of articles with text available online or by e-mail

  • GPO - U.S. Government Publications

  • MEDLINE - Abstracted articles from medical journals

  • NetFirst - Database of Internet resources

  • PapersFirst - Index of papers presented at conferences

  • PerAbs - Periodical Abstracts with full text.

  • Proceedings - Index of conference publications and papers

  • World Almanac - The World Almanacs.

For assistance with FirstSeach, please contact a librarian.

 

ProQuest Direct Tutorial

ProQuest Direct offers the learner access to numerous journals, newspapers, magazines, periodicals and other sources.

ProQuest Direct offers two ways to search for information:

  • Search by Word - Searching capabilities by words and phrases in the abstract and text of all documents
    available through ProQuest Direct.
  • Search by Publication - Find information using the title of a publication.

Selected journals on ProQuest Direct are available full-text.

For assistance with ProQuest Direct, please ask a librarian.
