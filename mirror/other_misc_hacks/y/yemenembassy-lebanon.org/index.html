Yemeni - Lebanese Relations

Yemen Lebanese relations dates back to 1949 when the first Yemen Legation opened in Beirut in 1949 headed by H.E. Dr. Adnan Tersisi. In 1964 the Legation was upgraded to an Embassy.


Of the oldest agreements between Yemen and Lebanon Agreement on extradition of criminals, Friendship Agreement (signed in February 15, 1949) and Cultural & Commercial Agreement (signed in February 17, 1949)


The first high official visit from Yemen to Lebanon was paid in the 1970's by H.E. Qadi Abdulrahman Al-Iryani, the then President of the Presidential Council of the Yemen Arab Republic (North Yemen). In the 1980's H.E. Mr. Amin Al-Jimail, ex-President of Lebanon paid a visit to Yemen.

Lately Yemen has signed the following agreements with Lebanon. They are:

  1. Agreement on Establishment of Joint High Yemeni - Lebanese Commision, signed in 1999.
  2. Agreement on Economic, Technical and Scientific Co-operation, signed in 1999.
  3. Agreement on Commercial Exchange, signed in 1999.
  4. Agreement on Promotion and Protection of Investment, signed in 1999.
  5. Agreement on Co-operation between Foreign Ministries, signed in 1999.
  6. Agreement on Co-operation in Health Field, signed in 2000.
