: I have an offer to trade Webber for Shareef Abdur-Rahim and Shawn Marion, should I do it? Thanks.

: Jim

Jim,

Thats a tough one. Abdur-Rahim is one of the most consistent fantasy players out there. Marion is showing he could be the SF of the future, putting up great numbers for Phoenix this year. Although I think Marion's number's will tapper off now with Gugliotta back and Anfernee Hardaway returning soon, he is still a solid fantasy player and his future is bright if you are in a keeper league.

Webber tends to be inconsistent and also is a bit of a head case and injury prone. But, he can put up MONSTER numbers (and has been much of the season).

There is certainly risk in both cases, but it comes down to your plans for the future. In a keeper league, I'd have to say no unless you are primed for a run at the championship this season. If you are looking at just this season, and have a deep enough bench to make up for losing both forwards, you are going to get a great threat that could put you over the top!

Good Luck!
