For Your Information

Don't you just hate it when you link to a page and have to wait to see anything? I do, which is why I coded everything here to load fast. I purposely avoid java, bulky graphics, drop-down boxes and anything else which might slow down communications. These web pages are powered by GNU/Linux, Perl, PHP, and an Apache server. Many web pages are driven by server side includes (SSI) or other scripting languages for efficiency and speed. All directory pages and articles are XHTML or being converted from HTML to XHTML and CSS as a transition to XML to conform to the latest W3C standards and also to load faster. And, there are NO frames or software cookies here. I hate frames and I hate cookies. Enjoy your stay.


The WEB SERVER here uses two back to back 550mhz computers plus SSH2 and SSL secure-site Security Encyrption. I also use GNU/Linux (Red Hat 6.2) operating systems and everything is behind a firewall for additional security. (For encrypted mail, my PGP Public Key is: 0xB7EC1B4A)
