Search Instructions

The database used in contract search is updated nightly from AGPS. The main search screen is divided into two parts:

Direct Inquiry (located on the left of the search page) - If specific information is known, a directory inquiry will yield only the results requested.

Contract Number - The number assigned to a contract in AGPS. The contract number always begins with 4 and has six digits. If multiple vendors are awarded items from a solicitation, each will have distinct contract numbers. The contract number will be different each time it is awarded. This field requires an exact match.

T-Number - The number that ties contract groups together (example: copiers, office supplies). If a solicitation results in multiple contracts, the contracts will all have the same T-Number. The T-contract number remains the same year after year. It always begins with 9 and has five digits. This field requires an exact match.

Vendor Number - This number is comprised of the Federal Employer Identification Number (FEIN) plus a two digit location code assigned in AGPS. It must be eleven digits without a space or hyphen and requires an exact match.

Brand - This field is alphanumeric. If used alone, it will yield all contract items where the Brand includes your search criteria (example: criteria = cross results include Cross, Redcross, Crossroads).

Model - Can be used in conjunction with Brand to search for specific brand and model. A partial match can be used in this field as in Brand. It may also be used alone with Brand field blank.

General Search (located on the right of the search page) - This provides a search of the entire database when specifics are not known. This field is not case sensitive and is alphanumeric. You may inquire by contract number, product name, commodity code, vendor name or anything contained in the contract text. Limited search criteria may yield unneeded information. Criteria may be refined to limit search results. Hints for using search criteria may be obtained by clicking Help on the search results page.
