Cooperative Purchasing. In accordance with LRS 39:1701, local public and private procurement units of the State of Louisiana may be permitted to purchase from contracts administered by the Office of State Purchasing. The bidder may, at its option, amend the bid so that any contract awarded will not apply to public or private procurement units.
