Material Safety Data Sheet Request Center Has New Phone Number and E-Mail Address

The FSS Hardware SuperStore Material Safety Data Sheet (MSDS) Request Center has a new toll-free telephone number and e-mail address. A Material Safety Data Sheet provides workers and emergency personnel with the proper procedures for handling or working with a particular substance. MSDS will include information such as physical data (melting point, boiling point, flash point etc.), toxicity, health effects, first aid, reactivity, storage, disposal, protective equipment, and spill/leak procedures. These are of particular use if a spill or other accident occurs. Customers can request any GSA MSDS by calling toll-free: 866-588-7659, or (816) 926-5097, or by sending an e-mail to MSDS@gsa.gov.  Please include your name, voice telephone number, return fax number, and/or e-mail, along with the National Stock Number, manufacturer, product name and/or part number and the date of the MSDS that you are requesting. Requests will be answered by the close of business of the next business day after the request is sent.  Contact: Randall Schober, (816) 926-2429.
