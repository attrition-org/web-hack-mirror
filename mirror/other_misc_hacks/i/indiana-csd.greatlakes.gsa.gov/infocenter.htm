GSA, opening the door to a world of products & services

The Great Lakes Region, Federal Supply Service, opened the first Information Center on September 21st, 1999 at the Major General Emmett J. Bean Center (DFAS) in Indianapolis.

We've Moved

This Center is now co-located with the PBS Buildings Management office located on the first floor NW just inside the North entrance (outside of security). We are available to assist all federal customers in locating any of our over four million products and services offered through our GSA Schedule Contracts by our commercial partners and our supply distribution program.
