Microstructures and Microfabricated Systems Laboratory

Research Activities


Microstructures and microsystems, microfabrication techniques, chemical sensors and microfluidic devices and sensors; Immunobiosensors based on impedance measurements; Micropumps, microvalves and integration into miniature chemical analysis systems for microdialysis; Microfabrication processes; bulk silicon micromachining, surface/bulk micromachining, computer aided design tools for the fabrication of 3-dimensional microstructures, anodic etch stop techniques for silicon; physical vapor deposition; rapid thermal processing: Microstructures; etching high aspect ratio structures for x-ray optics; microfabrication of biocompatible polymer membranes.
