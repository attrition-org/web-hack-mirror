Fundamental Studies of a Micromachining Technique
Pending funding
Dr. Davorin Babic (PI), Dr. Peter Hesketh (Co-PI)

The proposed work is fundamentally important to distinguish the electrochemical and chemical mechanisms involved in semiconductor dissolution processes. This study is technologically important for two reasons:(1) it will provide us with a detailed understanding of the silicon anisotropic etching process which will allow better control of the process for the fabrication of high aspect ratio structures; and (2) it will prove invaluable for the development of models for Computer Aided Design tools for bulk micromachining.
