Query Language
The text search engine allows queries to be formed from arbitrary Boolean expressions containing the keywords AND, OR, and NOT, and grouped with parentheses. For example:

» microfabrication optics; finds documents containing 'microfabrication' or 'optics'
» microfabrication or optics: same as above
» microfabrication and optics: finds documents containing both 'microfabrication' and 'optics'
» microfabrication not optics: finds documents containing 'microfabrication' but not 'optics'
» (microfabrication not optics) and microfluidics: finds documents containing 'microfluidics', plus 'microfabrication' but not 'optics'
» micro*: finds documents containing words starting with 'micro'
