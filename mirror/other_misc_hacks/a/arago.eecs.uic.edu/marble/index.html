The UIC Measurement Request Broker is a prototype system to allow multi-user access to a library of sophisticated test equipment for measurements on a library of devices, in real time via the World Wide Web. The first online laboratory that utilizes this technology is an Interactive Electronics Laboratory.

TIP OF THE DAY: The voltage probes are of two kinds. The ones labelled "Voltmeter Probe" and that have the [V] icon are used for d.c. sweep measurements, wheras the ones labelled "Oscilloscope Probe" and that have the [O] icon are used for transient measurements using a.c. or pulsed signals.
