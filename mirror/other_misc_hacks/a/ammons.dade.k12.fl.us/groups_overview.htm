GROUPS OVERVIEW

 


These groups have been developed to help you child to be successful in school and at home. There are many problems that our children face, but not enough interventions to help [them cope with these problems. As a parent sometimes our children are reluctant to talk to us, but maybe a school counselor may be able to get the youth to open their feelings up so that they can begin to work on these issues.


The different groups are listed on flyer, but here is a summary of the different groups:

Anger management: This group will focus on helping the individual handle their anger in a constructive manner. The student will gain understand of the things that make them
become angry.

Communication: This group will focus on how express yourself in a clear order. This will help students gain clearer thoughts on what they are trying to say in school or
at home.

Conflict Resolution: This group will focus on how to handle problems without fighting. The students will to taught how to talk their problems out.

Grief: This group will focus on the cycles of grief and how grief affects their lives.

Self-Esteem: This group focuses on developing confidence in their perception of themselves physically and mentally.

Substance Abuse: This groups focuses on prevention.   The group will look at how drugs affect the body and the family. The group will look at if family members are using drugs and the affects that it has
on the family.

Divorce: This group will focus on the dynamics of divorce and how it affects the family as well as the person.
