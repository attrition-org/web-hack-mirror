Tricks to Wining the Grading Game

    Your work will be graded in four ways:

1. Completion of your assignments

2. Creativity

3. Composition

4. Craftsmanship

 

How to get an "A"

If you do all four things, you will get an "A"; do three and you get a 'B". Some artists take longer than others to finish their work so you may turn your work in up to one week late but your grade will drop one letter grade.

You will start out in this class with a "C" which is average.

Grades go up and down and your grade can go up very quickly.

Everyone has a bad day once and a while, so if you are having a bad day please let me know. You may turn in work for extra credit, which will help especially if the grade is borderline.

 

YOU HOLD ALL THE CARDS. THE GAME YOU PLAY IS UP TO YOU!!!!
