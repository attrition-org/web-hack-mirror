Ben Tillman's Syllabus

 

Summary of Social Studies Competencies

Grades 6-9

 

At the completion of MIJ Civics, the student will be able to:

Illustrate the Five Fundamental Themes of Geography as they relate to Dade County and Florida.
Apply key map skills and physical geography concepts to the study of the state of Florida.
Analyze key democratic concepts and principles and evaluate their application in contemporary society.
Describe how the process of judicial review and landmark Supreme Court decisions have impacted life in the United States.
Describe the legislative process at the national level in the United States.
Describe their personal involvement in community service to solve local problems.
Explain the criminal justice process from arrest to sentencing and identify the rights of those accused of crimes.
Discuss key aspects of Florida's economy.
Create a personal budget.
Describe the multicultural nature of Dade County.
Apply the five-step decision-making process to a problem which affects the local, state, national, and/or international community.
