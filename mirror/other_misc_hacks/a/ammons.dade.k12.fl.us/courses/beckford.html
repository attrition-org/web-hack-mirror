Language Arts

6th Grade Course Syllabus

Teacher: Mrs. D. Beckford Room 301

 

Course Description: The program is filled with Thematic Units and delightful Literature. The goals are to nurture the love for reading, writing, listening and speaking. Literature will be the main focus, therefore grammar, vocabulary, spelling, and writing will, be related to the content.

 

Required:

Textbooks issued by teacher must be covered and left at home for homework assignments. Agenda books are mandatory daily.

Assignments:

Students are responsible for all material presented in class, including homework and other means of verbal and written assessment. Appearance/neatness will be graded along with content.

Evaluation:

Test: There will be several tests for Language Arts.

Test dates will be announced in class at least one week before the test.

Projects: There will be several projects.

Homework assignments: Students will have frequent assignments involving course material. Homework is assigned Monday through Friday.

In-class quizzes: Announced and unannounced quizzes will be given on an irregular basis.

All tests, projects, and homework must be completed, regardless of lateness.

Unexcused late course work will lose grade or extra credit.

Arrangements for "excused lateness" must be a note from parent/guardian and approved by the teacher.

Course grades will be assigned as follows:

A = (100-94) Excellent

B = (93-85) Good

C = (84-77) Average

D = (76-70) Unsatisfactory

                F= (69-0) Failure

 

*Field Trips: A minimum of two field trips will be planned for this school year. Destination and cost information will be available at a later time.

 

*It is recommended that students record names and phone numbers of at least (2) class members.
