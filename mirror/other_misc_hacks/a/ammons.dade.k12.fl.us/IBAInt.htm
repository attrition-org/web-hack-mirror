I. B. AREAS of INTERACTION

 

Ammons' curriculum, based on the International Baccalaureate Middle Years Program  stresses theme - based learning through five Areas of Interaction.  These Areas of Interaction focus on study skills, the environment, community service, health and social education, and the inventions of man.
