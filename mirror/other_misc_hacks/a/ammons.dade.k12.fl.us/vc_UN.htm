Sixth grade students in Mr. Viana and Mr. Mullin’s  World Geography classes participated in an international video conference on Tuesday, October 24, 2000.  In celebration of United Nations Day, Nova University sponsored WORLDLINK 2000 which brought together students from over 80 countries.  Ammons students joined Coral Gables High International Baccalaureate students in the conference that linked Miami to the Geneva, Switzerland United Nations panel.  The focus of the conference was human rights and the influence of UNICEF on the lives of children throughout the world.
