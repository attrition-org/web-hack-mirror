Institute of Microelectronics (IME) is propelling forward industries in the electronics sector through a combination of diverse talents and state-of-the-art facilities that enables us to create cutting-edge technologies for our industrial customers. IME has developed :

  • RFICs in CMOS technology for wireless communication applications such as Bluetooth, CDMA and WCDMA
  • System-on-Chip solutions for wireless communications
  • high-power RFIC devices
  • silicon-based sensors for better control in automotives; high-quality and low-cost silicon microphones and Bio-sensors including those for DNA research
  • deep submicron high-performance RFIC technologies with low-k dielectrics and copper interconnects built on 0.18 micron geometry; now planning for 0.13 micron technology
  • world-class capabilities in advanced packaging technologies such as flip-chip and 3-D packaging
  • advanced FAR services for wafer fabrication companies
