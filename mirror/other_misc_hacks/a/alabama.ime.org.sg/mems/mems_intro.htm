MEMS

Introduction:

Micro Electro Mechanical Systems (MEMS) R&D in IME has matured into a vibrant effort. We have notable successes in developing state of the art MEMS products with innovative designs and processes, which are close to commercialisation. Some of our success stories include :

    MEMS Products
  • Silicon Accelerometer
  • Electrostatic Micro Relay
  • Silicon Microphone
  • IR Array
  • Biochip
  • Optical MEMS
    One Stop shop for MEMS
  • MEMS Design
  • MEMS Fabrication
  • MEMS Signal Conditioning
  • MEMS packaging
  • MEMS Test, Measurement and Reliability
    •

The MEMS effort has blossomed into a major activity in IME encompassing various departments. IME's initial vision for R&D in all aspects of MEMS devices is being realised by vigorous efforts in MEMS design, Signal conditioning ASIC development, MEMS process technology module development and MEMS speciality package development. Our customers who have sponsored several advanced long-term projects in IME involving all the different aspects of MEMS product development have soundly acknowledged this effort. This activity is also reflected in IME's capability in tackling complex internal projects. IME specializes on Silicon based MEMS devices of industrial relevance, which are capable of mass production at a low cost. These devices are used extensively in automotive, medical, process control, data storage, optical networking and military applications. IME has developed a unique set of capabilities in a total solution for MEMS from concept to manufacturing and be able to provide a "One stop shop for MEMS products". IME has a core MEMS applications group. This group interacts with customers and with the Microelectronics Process Technology, VLSI, Packaging and Failure Analysis departments in IME. IME's engineers design, fabricate, package and test MEMS devices and signal conditioning ASICs .
