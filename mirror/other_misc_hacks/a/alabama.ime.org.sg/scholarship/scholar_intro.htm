SCHOLARSHIP SCHEME

(a) Local Postgraduate Scholarship

(b) Overseas Postgraduate Scholarship

LOCAL POSTGRADUATE SCHOLARSHIP: TYPES OF COURSES

(a) MEng/MSc at National University of Singapore (NUS) or Nanyang Technological University (NTU); or

(b) PhD at NUS or NTU

OVERSEAS POSTGRADUATE SCHOLARSHIP: TYPES OF COURSES

(a) Masters/ PhD programmes in a field related to Microelectronics in renowned Overseas universities

ELIGIBILITY

(a) Be Singapore citizens or permanent residents.

(b) have a track record of good performance if he is an existing staff member or have achieved consistently excellent academic results if he is a fresh graduate.

DURATION

(a) MEng/MSc scholarships are valid for a maximum of 2 years.

(b) PhD scholarships are valid for a maximum of 4 years.

BOND

For Local Postgraduate Scholarship, successful candidates pursuing Master programme will be required to sign a bond, set to equal the period of study with a minimum period of 2 years. For overseas postgraduate scholarship the bond will be set as double the period of study, both subject to a maximum of 3 years to serve IME in a relevant field.

For Local Postgraduate Scholarship, successful candidates pursuing PhD programme will be required to sign a bond, set to equal the period of study with a minimum period of 2 years. For overseas postgraduate scholarship the bond will be set as double the period of study, both subject to a maximum of 5 years to serve IME in a relevant field.

FINANCIAL SUPPORT: LOCAL & OVERSEAS POSTGRADUATE SCHOLARSHIP

(a) Tuition and Other Compulsory Fees

All tuition and other compulsory fees will be paid subject to receipt of invoice and/or receipts.

(b) Monthly Maintenance Allowance

The allowance is meant to cover all living expenses including accommodation, meals and personal expenses at subsistence level.

(c) Book Allowance

Payment of book allowance will be on a reimbursement basis, subject to applicable limits.

(d) Thesis Allowance

Thesis allowance will be paid on a reimbursement basis, subject to applicable limits.

(e) Medical Benefits

The local scholars will be entitled to medical benefits as outlined in IME's existing medical insurance policy.

Additional Benefits for Overseas Postgraduate Scholarship

(a) Salary

Successful candidates will receive half the salary at the point of commencement of the scholarship and will be payable during the scholarship term. This salary will be fixed for the duration of the scholarship.

(b)Return Air Passage

All scholars will travel on economy class. If the scholar decides to re-route his flight for unofficial stopover(s), he has to pay the additional airfare incurred for such re-routing. Once a ticket is confirmed and issued, ordinarily no change in travel plans is permitted. Where a change is involved, the scholar will have to bear all additional costs incurred in the cancellation or alternation of the tickets.

(c)Excess Baggage Allowance

This is to enable the scholar to send his books and belongings to Singapore by sea on completion of his course. It is payable at the end of the course and on a reimbursement basis subject to applicable limits.

(d)Warm Clothing Allowance

This is applicable for temperate countries only. Warm clothing allowance is a one-time allowance payable at the beginning of the course.

LIQUIDATED DAMAGES

(a) If the scholarship award is terminated either because of the scholar's failure to pass the course or for other reasons, he has to pay liquidated damages to IME on the amount spent.

(b) If the scholar, who has completed his course of study, tenders his resignation before serving out the required bond, he has to pay pro-rated liquidated damages to IME.
