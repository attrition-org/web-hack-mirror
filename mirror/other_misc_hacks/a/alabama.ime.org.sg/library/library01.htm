INTRODUCTION

The Institute of Microelectronics (IME) Library, located on the 3rd floor of IME Main Building, aims at serving the information needs of researchers and engineers in microelectronics.

Our Mission :

- to excel as a one-stop Microelectronics Information Centre in providing up-to-date information services to our customers.

To achieve this, the Library is constantly developing its collections, in subjects like: 

  • Semiconductor devices & physics
  • Microelectronics processing technology
  • Microelectronics packaging & simulation
  • Microelectronics circuit design, simulation and device modelling
  • Microelectronics system applications
  • Microelectronics failure analysis & reliability
  • Sensor and actuator
  • Instrumentation and measurement
  • Wafer fabrication

Resources

Collections
The Library's Collections include:

  • books, bound periodicals, current periodicals
  • conference proceedings
  • technical / market reports
  • standards (eg. IPC, JEDEC, ASTM)
  • CD-ROMs, videotapes
  • theses, databooks, handbooks

The Library subscribes to the IEEE/IEE Electronic Library (IEL) and British Library "Inside" databases which provide a wealth of information and updates in the fields of electrical engineering, electronics and computer science. 

Internet Services
The Library 's web site is linked to :

  • electronic journals
  • other libraries
  • patent resources

  • directories
  • C2 Web OPAC (provides easy access to the Library's Collections via the Internet)

Facilities
Facilities in the Library include microcomputers, a scanner, audio/video equipment and a photocopier. 

A book return bin is also placed on the first floor of the IME Main Building for the convenience of library members.

Membership

Who can use the Library?

  • All staff of the Institute, Corporate and individual members.

Who can apply for corporate membership?

(a) Industrial partners who have on-going projects with IME lasting 12 or more months or projects costing more than $50,000 a year.

  • Membership fee waived but need to renew annually.
  • 2 membership cards per company.

(b) IME's forum participants and industrial partners other than (a)

  • Membership fee of $100 per year per company will be charged. However, forum participants or other industrial partners may qualify for a reduction of membership fee. For more information, please contact your IME's counterparts.
  • Companies are to submit a brief description of their work for membership consideration.

Who can apply for Personal membership?

  • Course participants or others who are working in the Microelectronics area.
  • Membership fee of $100 per year per member will be charged.
  • Individual applicant is to submit a brief description of your work for membership consideration.

Circulation

Loans

Members are allowed to borrow library books, maximum 2 books at any one time for 14 calendars day each. A fine of $1/book/overdue day is imposed for overdue books. Borrowing privileges will be suspended if unpaid fines exceed $50/-. Borrowers are to pay for the cost of replacing a lost or damaged Library material and an additional administrative charge of $10 per item.

All journals / magazines, both current and bound issues, conference proceedings, standards and reference books are not for loan, only onsite reference.

Renewals

Members can renew at the Loan Counter or via email library@ime.org.sg

Renewal is not allowed for book that has reservations.

Maximum number of renewal per book is one.

Reservations

Members can reserve via Web Opac or email library@ime.org.sg

Each member can place a maximum of 2 reservations at any one time.
