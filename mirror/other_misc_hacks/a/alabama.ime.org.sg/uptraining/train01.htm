INTRODUCTION

IME supports manpower development through its industrial and student attachment schemes. Industrial attachments are aimed at training existing engineers on relevant microelectronics technologies while student attachment equips final year and post-graduate students with capabilities sought after by industry. IME works closely with Economic Development Board and the National Science and Technology Board to develop the manpower needed by the electronic industry.
One of the training courses conducted at IME
