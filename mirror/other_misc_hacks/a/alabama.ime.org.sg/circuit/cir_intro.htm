I.C. DESIGN & MODELLING

Introduction


The VLSI Design and test department specializes in mixed-signal and RF circuit design. In addition to supporting our internal circuit design efforts, our modeling and electrical characterization group also provides modeling and electrical characterization services to semiconductor foundries and circuit design houses. We specialize in BSIM3 model extraction and RF CMOS modelling.

The Alpha-Service which runs five times a year offers low cost IC prototyping service to circuit designers. We are currently providing 0.35um single poly and four metal process.

IME has designed several successful RFIC chips both in GaAs and in CMOS technologies. Our strength lies in providing integrated RFIC Design and modeling capabilities. We have designed PHS transceiver in GaAs, Bluetooth Transceiver and Mod./Demod. for CDMA in 0.35um CMOS. RF LDMOS power devices have been developed for RF power amplifier application.

The Multichip module technology has been identified as a useful platform for the integration of different IC technologies for microwave and RF applications. MCM technology enables design of integrated RF subsystems using high performance and low cost passive components.

In the area of mixed signal design IME has demonstrated several sensor electronics solutions for pressure and acceleration sensing applications. A/D and D/A for audio as well broadband applications has been developed. A low cost poly/metal fuse calibration circuit has been successfully demonstrated in these applications.
