Littleton Tragedy

April 20, 1999

in Littleton, CO, armed with shotguns, TEC 9 semiautomatic, pipe bombs and a 9mm

killing 13 innocents. The massacre was over around 4pm, when police found the two
boys in the school library, dead from their own hands.

Townsend (18), and Kyle Velasquez were lost.
