Management

Mark E. Kahn
President & Chief Executive Officer

Mr. Kahn has worked for many large and successful Media and New Media companies. He began at NBC as Assistant Producer at The Today Show's Seoul Olympics Division and then moved to CBS News where he was Associate Producer for Weekend News and Sunday Morning with Charles Kuralt. From there, Mr. Kahn moved to The News Corporation and served in many business management position including, Director, Service Development for News Corp/MCI Joint Venture and Director of Business Development for American Sky Broadcasting. In addition, he held the position of VP, Business Development for Internet community based service, iVillage Inc. Mr. Kahn's last position before founding Zero-G Commerce was as Vice President, Marketing & Distribution for News Corporation's Kesmai Corporation, the leading multiplayer game service on the Internet. Mr. Kahn graduated with Honors from Connecticut College in 1990 with a BA in English. Contact me: mark@zero-g-commerce.com

Jay P. Hirschson
Chief Operating Officer & Chief Financial Officer

Mr. Hirschson was Director, Business Development in the Advanced Media Group of News America Digital Publishing (NADP), the News Corporation holding company which oversees and manages all electronic and Internet-related businesses. Prior to joining NADP, Mr. Hirschson was successfully self-employed as an Internet Business Development Consultant and held the position of Director of Production for iVillage, a well-regarded developer of online community-based content. Before that, he had been at the MCI/News Corp. Joint Venture, where he was Senior Producer of Multiplayer Games. In 1995, Jay received an MBA from Columbia Business School. He has held positions as Story Editor at New Line Cinema, Production Coordinator at Miramax Films, and Promotions Director at the National Association of College Broadcasters. He graduated Magna Cum Laude from Brown University in 1989. Contact me: jay@zero-g-commerce.com

Steven L. Nelson
Chief Technology Officer

Mr. Nelson is a member of Team Allaire which is a group of internationally-recognized experts in Allaire's ColdFusion Web Application Language. Steve Nelson is also the Chairman of the Fusebox Organization designed to create an application framework for complex ColdFusion applications. Steve Nelson is the lead author of these standards. He is a senior developer for Zero-G Commerce, Corp, and continues to push the envelope in web application development. Contact me: snelson@zero-g-commerce.com

John A. Londoner
VP, Merchandising

Mr. Londoner most recently spearheaded the creation of the Advertising Sales and Sponsorship department for News Corporation's Kesmai Corporation, the leading supplier of multiplayer games to the Internet. Upon the creation of that department, he held the title of Senior Account Executive and met ad sales goals of over $200,000 in just six months of operation. Prior to working at Kesmai, Mr. Londoner started a successful consulting business in the Electronic Commerce and Online Marketing Arena. Mr. Londoner graduated with Honors from Fordham University with a BA in History in 1993. Contact me: johnny@zero-g-commerce.com
