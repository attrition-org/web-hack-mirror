Mission Statement

Through its portfolio of e-commerce storefronts Zero-G Commerce focuses its efforts on exploiting an e-commerce paradigm and business model whose result is an optimal and profitable method of creating differentiated online stores and consumer shopping experiences.

Zero-G's strategy is two-fold: (i) Integrate 1,000's of product SKU's from wholesale supplier/shippers from discrete consumer categories into the Company's scaleable database, catalog publishing, and online order processing system; and (ii) Create and market multiple online stores and commercial services to meet the needs of distinctive, highly-focused and custom-tailored market segments and opportunities.
