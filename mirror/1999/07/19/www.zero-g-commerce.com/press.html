ZERO-G COMMERCE CORP. CONTINUES TO BRING HIGH QUALITY
ELECTRONIC COMMERCE SERVICES TO THE INTERNET COMMUNITY

New York, NY – April 13, 1999 – Zero-G Commerce Corporation, the creators of the gourmet food superstore Caesar’s Palate: The Epicurean Empire (http://www.caesarspalate.com), today announced the launch of its newest Internet e-commerce service, Nature’s Aide® – The Internet Source for Premium Health Supplements (http://www.naturesaide.com). This latest e-commerce entry from Zero-G Commerce Corporation marks its foray into another major consumer retail category, namely the $2.8 billion market of vitamins and nutritional supplements.

Nature's Aide® has been providing health-conscious Americans with the most popular and highest quality natural health supplements and vitamins since 1997. Nature's Aide® buys nutritional compounds from only the most reputable laboratories and then rigorously tests them for impurities before tabletting the vitamins in its FDA-inspected, "clean-room-level" facilities. This process guarantees freshness, safety and contaminate-free health supplements for our many satisfied customers.

And now Nature's Aide® offers this quality and satisfaction guarantee to the health-conscious Internet shopper, with the efficiency, price satisfaction, value-added features and personal customer service you have come to expect from Zero-G Commerce Corp.

Come to http://www.naturesaide.com to learn more about the health benefits of such popular supplements as Oral Chelation, Ginkgo Biloba, St. John’s Wort, Echinacea, Green Tea and many others or to subscribe to one of Nature's Aide's regular newsletters, including the acclaimed "Natural Medical Quarterly."

ABOUT ZERO-G COMMERCE CORPORATION

Zero-G Commerce Corporation, The Way E-Commerce Works™, a privately held company, is known for creating, developing and operating electronic commerce stores on the global Internet. The Company launched its first Internet-based store, a gourmet food emporium called Caesar’s Palate – The Epicurean Empire (http://www.caesarspalate.com) in July 1998, and launched its second online store, a vitamin service called Nature’s Aide ® (http://www.naturesaide.com) in April 1999. The Company anticipates launching at least three more e-commerce superstores in distinct consumer catgeories over the next twelve months.
