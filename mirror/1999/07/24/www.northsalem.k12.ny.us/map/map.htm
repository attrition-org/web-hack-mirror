North Salem Schools are located in in upper Westchester County, New York about 60 miles north of New York City. The town of North Salem is near the towns of Brewster, Carmel, and Croton Falls, NY. Our address is 230 June Road, North Salem, NY 10560. Our phone number is (914) 669-5414 and you may reach us by FAX at: (914) 669-8753.

From the South

Take I-684 North to Exit 8, Hardscrabble Road. Turn right at the end of the ramp. Continue to the end of Hardscrabble Road, where it joins Route 124 (June Road). The Middle/High Schools are on your left. PQ is on your right 1/8 mile South on Route 124.

From the North

Take Route 22 south and exit at Brewster just before the start of I-684. Turn left at the end of the ramp. Continue to the next traffic light (near ATI gas station) and turn right onto Starr Ridge Road. Continue to the end of Starr Ridge Road, and turn right onto Bloomer Road. Turn left at the intersection (Route 124, June Road). The Middle/High Schools are on your right. Continue 1/8 mile to PQ which is on your left.

From the East

Take I-84 West and take the first exit after crossing the New York State line, Exit 22. Turn left at the end of the ramp. Continue to the intersection with Bloomer Road and turn right. Take Bloomer Road to the end. Turn left at the intersection (Route 124, June Road). The Middle/High Schools are on your right. Continue 1/8 mile to PQ which is on your left.

From the West

Take I-84 East to the Brewster exit. At the end of the ramp, go straight through the intersection onto Starr Ridge Road. Continue to the end of Starr Ridge Road, and turn right onto Bloomer Road. Turn left at the intersection (Route 124, June Road). The Middle/High Schools are on your right. Continue 1/8 mile to PQ which is on your left.

From Above

Land your aircraft at nearby Danbury Airport (General Avaition) which is only 7 miles away. Follow the directions for 'From the East', above. Alternately, you may land at Westchester County Airport which is only 22 miles away, and is served by several major airlines. From this airport, follow directions for 'From the South', above.
