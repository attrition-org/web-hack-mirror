Back to School...
 
 
     
  The school year will begin for students on August 17, 1999.  Walkertown Principal, Mr. John M. Quillen, looks forward to seeing students in Preschool through third grade.  Ms. Sandra Muncy, Principal of Roy G. Eversole Middle School will welcome students in grades four through eight.  Hazard High School Principal, Mr. Don Pratt, looks forward to seeing high school students, grades nine through twelve. 

If your child has not attended  Hazard Independent Schools  before, please  note that your child must have a physical, updated immunization records, and a copy of his/her last report card.  Please bring the address and phone number of the school your child last attended.  Any other  information that may be needed will be requested by the school.  If you would like to register your child (ren)  in Hazard Independent Schools, please contact the schools at the following numbers:
Walkertown Primary School 436-4421,
Roy G. Eversole Intermediate and Middle School at 436-4721,
and Hazard High School at 436-3711. 
        A complete school calendar is now available..  School will begin for teachers on August 12, 1999 and August 16, 1999  with Professional Development .  Students will report to school on August 17, 1999. 
School Day Hours will  be  as follows:
Walkertown - 8:00-2:45

R.G. Eversole- 7:55-2:50

Hazard High - 7:55-3:00
Breakfast served at 7:30 a.m.
