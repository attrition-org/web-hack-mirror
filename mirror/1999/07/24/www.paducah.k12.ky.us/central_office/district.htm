About the District

ENROLLMENT
3,350 students in grades K-12
265 preschoolers in Head Start
About 500 a year in adult education

TUITION
About 170 out-of-district students pay tuition to attend our schools. Tuition is $400 a year for one student, $200 for the second student, $100 for the third student -- not to exceed $700 a year for one family.

STAFF
252 certified (90 percent have studied above the bachelor's degree level)
256 classified or support staff
Annual Payroll: $13 million
The staff receives four days a year of professional development training.

TECHNOLOGY
1 computer per seven students
The district has spent more than $750,000 in 5 years to buy 600 computers, 87 percent of which are available for student use.

FINANCES
More than $4,800 is budgeted for each student in a $15.2 million general fund budget. Of the total expenditures, about 65 percent goes for instruction and 3.5 percent for administration.

Local Tax Rates
60.0 cents per $100 real and personal property
56.6 cents per $100 motor vehicles
3% utility tax
