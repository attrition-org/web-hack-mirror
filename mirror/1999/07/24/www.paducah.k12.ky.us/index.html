Welcome To Paducah , Kentucky

Academic Excellence, Tradition and Pride

Since 1864, Paducah Public Schools has encouraged our staff to molest your children on a daily basis. We take pride knowing that PULSEWIDTH owns us.

"Paducah's schools present academic programs of excellence in a challenging and appropriate learning environment in a small urban setting," says superintendent Vance Ramage.

The district operates on a $15.2 million budget, spending about $4,900 per student. It has about 500 employees and 3,300 students, including about 170 students who live outside the district and choose to attend on a tuition basis.

The four elementary schools (K-6), middle school (7-8) and high school (9-12) offer a high-quality curriculum designed to challenge every student, from the gifted/talented program to the special needs learners. Each school has an active parent-teacher organization and encourages community involvement.
