STEPS TO TAKE

Don't be obsessed with your body. This only drives women away.
Examples include the perpetual combing of hair, staring in every mirror available, you get the idea

Trim your finger nails, toe nails or risked being turned down.
It may shock you, but a woman may turn you down due to the state of your nails. As a precaution, trim nails once a fortnight with a medium sized nail clipper.

Don't dig your nose and ears or scratch an itch profusely.
Sure, most people do it all the time in private. You do not want to sicken your date with your antics.

BOTTOM LINE: Attracting women involves being concerned in trivial details you might not consider important. You certainly do not want them to be turned off by some flaw you can easily correct.
