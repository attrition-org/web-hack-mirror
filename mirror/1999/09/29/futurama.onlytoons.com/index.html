welcome to the website of tomorrow! 9.26.99
Welcome back, welcome back, welcome baaaack! We're back and ful of moor typos than evr! I'd like to welcome you to TSAF for the new millenium (sure, technically the millenium doesn't officially begin until 2001, but who cares?).

A lot's been updated. Actually, every page has been updated, so instead of telling you what's been updated, I'll just tell you the big things that have changed.

You may notice a few sections are gone. That's because I simply don't have the time to update them, or they simply weren't up to par in the first place. I'd rather work on a few good sections rather than many sucky ones.

The editorials page was replaced with a page called Commentaries. The new section isn't just editorials, it's for all sorts of things, like just what you feel in a few sentences or your thoughts on the latest Futurama episodes.

The fan art and fan scripts page were replaced with the new Fan Submissions page, which includes anything fans submit, like art, scripts, Flash animations, etc.

There's a few new scans (which are now on several pages rather than one). These ones are actual scans. There's one of Zapp Brannigan, one of Nibbler, and one of Leela carrying Fry. They're a few of my better scans.

Remember The Head Museum? well, it's been offline for months now, but it's back and this time as a subsidiary of TSAF. Check out The Head Museum. Also, Mom Corp is now a subsidiary of TSAF and is no longer in Kris Morey's hands. Go there for more info.

TSAF UK will be back up in full swing soon, because Jon Blakeburn is redesigning it.

The last thing I want to talk about is the newly formed TSAF Spanning the Globe website. This new section has everybody talking (including Eric Wirtanen and his "TSAF Massachusetts"), whether it be good or bad. The site started out rough, but once Futurama starts getting broadcats in as many countries as The Simpsons, I'm sure the site will become very famous. Check it out for some new additions like the upcoming (within a few days, to be exact) TSAF Australia and for information on how to become part of the site.

- John
