9.28.3000

Uberraschen!!! That's German for "amazing," and what I will use to describe the season premier. I loved it! More than that, I loved seeing Leela in that bathing suit!

Which is why I made some wallpaper fearuring the aesthetically pleasing Leela in her swimwear. Check it out! I also made a scan of the same thing here.

Crowd pleasers we aren't. Sorry that we didn't update exactly after the premier like most sites did, but since this is a character site, and not a definitive Futurama information source, I don't think it was necessary. But I apologize to any who hoped it would be.

As far as updates are concerned, we do have another opinion submitted. Thanks! And if you, like me, loved seeing Leela in that bathing suit, you'll marvel the very large, high quality framegrabs donated by Matt Riley of TSAF. Fun in the sun, bay-bee! Click here

Speaking of framegrabs, you will be happy to know that I am going to work on completing that area soon (by soon, I mean within the next two weeks). I'm not going to kill myself getting tons of them, but I'll only pick out a few of the nicest ones of Leela from each episode.

9.23.3000

We're back! A little early too, which is great. I know you were probably expecting some major changes, and additions, content and design-wise. Unfortunately, that is not the reason I shut down.

You can now rest assured that TLZ is back, and running well in some form or another. There are going to be a few updates soon, but probably about as slow as before. I am working hard in school, and also working on another web project.

We do have a new opinions section. There are only a few submissions, but you can still send them in to me if you have any thoughts on the subject.

We will bring up another subject for opinion submissions in the near future. If you have any topics you'd like to bring up, we'd love to see them too. Remember, this site is fan based.

Matt and I are thinking about more things to add, but we'd also like your ideas. Send them!! No idea will be overlooked! I want to thank Jason from AYNIF for being a strong supporter too! =)

TLZ looks forward to enjoying the new season with you all!!
