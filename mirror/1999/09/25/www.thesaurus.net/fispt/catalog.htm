The Catalogue of the Best 100 Outdoor Sports Activities and Adventure Sports in Europe

Enric Truñó
UESpT President

Barcelona, 31th October 1996

Dear friends,

In the last meeting of the European Union Sport for All (USEpT) Board of Directors the creation of “The best hundred outdoor sport activities and adventure sports in Europe Catalogue” was established with a jury for the selection of these.

The idea behind the project is to annually produce a catalogue of the best projects, wich we hope in subsequent years will also include prizes with support of private or public sponsors.

The possibility of holding a meeting or Fair under the reading “Adventure Sports” is also being looked into, as well as the production of a database.

We should be obliged if you would forward fuller details of your best outdoor sport activities and adventure sports (see the attached guidelines).

In February 1997, a booklet will be published, and also in Internet pages, “1996 best hundred outdoor sport activities and adventure sports in Europe catalogue”.

I hope you are interested in our offer, as it would be a chance to make your region and town activities known.

I look forward to hearing from you.

Yours sincerely

Enric Truñó
UESpT President
