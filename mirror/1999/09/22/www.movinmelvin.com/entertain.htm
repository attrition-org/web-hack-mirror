Movin' Melvin Brown has been singing and dancing most of his life. At the age of forty-six, he took up rhythm tap. And now, at the age of fifty-two, he feels that he is healthier and happier (physically and mentally) than ever.

From a very early age he began to look for answers to health happiness and spirituality, and he still does investigate and write about the conditions of life as effected by philosophy, psychology and spirituality.

Now, Movin' Melvin Brown offers you the opportunity to share in his joy and to improve your health through his music, books and health video. Sale of these products also helps support the "Change This World Project". Which is an on going project to help homeless people in Austin, Texas.
