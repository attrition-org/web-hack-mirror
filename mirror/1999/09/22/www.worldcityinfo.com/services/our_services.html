If you are an international or domestic business traveler, ex-patriot, or tourist, the information being developed for this internet site, and products being produced by MetroGlobal will be of interest.

"Honey, I’ve Been Transferred"

A detailed guide to moving to and living in cities around the world. "Honey, I’ve Been Transferred" will guide you and your family, if your spouse and children accompany you, from your arrival to your departure.

"Honey, I’ve Been Transferred" helps reduces stress and anxiety for you and your family. It features tips on how to navigate the airport, where to find a place to live, how to survive culture shock. It identifies people ready to help you, places to shop, good schools, great restaurants, hotels, and fun things to do while you are on assignment.

"Honey, I’ve Been Transferred" is your comprehensive guide to understanding your new city. From Singapore to Seattle, the Honey, I’ve Been Transferred guides will give you insights that it would take years to learn. Now you have the information right at your fingertips.

One of the most stressful moments in life is when you're told you are being transferred to a new city, particularly to a city in another country where everything from language to customs to geography is alien. Ex patriots, people who have been transferred overseas, will tell you that an overseas assignment can be the most exciting and rewarding time in their lives. But it is also very stressful.

Quote from the Harvard Business Review….

"...while expats generally enjoy working overseas, more than two thirds believe that their companies are not doing a satisfactory job of providing a clear preview of what to expect from the assignment."

To help you succeed in your new assignment, MetroGlobal has developed a comprehensive guide to cities around the world. It is designed to help you and your family, if your family members accompany you, to enjoy and learn from the new assignment. It helps reduce the stress by guiding you through your new city.

Offered to you on a compact disc or in a printed manual, the guide is called, "Honey, I’ve Been Transferred".
