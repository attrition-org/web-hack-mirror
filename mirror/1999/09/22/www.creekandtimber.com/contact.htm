Cagle's Creek & Timber was created by experienced hunters and fishermen who grew tired of spending countless hours each season looking for specific items which were never found. We found that items were limited in both variety and number and that many name-brand products were being sold at unreasonably high prices. Our goal at Cagle's Creek & Timber addresses each of these issues. We provide a large variety of high-quality, reasonably priced products as well as a product locating service. We believe in providing sportsmen with choices, consideration and care.
Whether you know what you're looking for or would like some help locating the product, Cagle's Creek & Timber is the place to shop.
