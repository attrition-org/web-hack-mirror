The Solana Ranch is a 9000 acre Ranch located fifty miles north of Austin near the historic village of Salado, Texas. The Ranch is accessible from I-35 at exit 280 three miles south of Salado,Texas. The Ranch is over thirteen miles long and has a wide variety of habitat. The eastern part of the ranch consists of open pasture and other areas are in cultivation - ideal for dove hunting. The western part of the ranch changes to rolling terrain and rough canyons with a wide variety of trees, brush, grasses, and forbes - perfect for deer, turkey, and hog hunting.
