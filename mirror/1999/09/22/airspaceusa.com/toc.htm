AIRSPACE

The purpose of AIRSPACE® is to locate all public airports near a proposed construction site. It will compute effects upon imaginary surfaces surrounding an airport, identify those electronic air navigation and air to ground communication facilities that may be adversely affected by your construction project. It provides alternatives to construction of a new site by locating existing tall surveyed obstacles where you could quickly establish your facility. Finally AIRSPACE® will assist in the completion of a FAA Form 7460-1, "Notice Of Proposed Construction Or Alteration" and other mandatory FAA Forms.

TERPS® Professional was developed to analyze the instrument procedures used by aircraft landing at an airport. These instrument procedures are built upon the electronic navigation facilities assigned to the airport. TERPS® Professional specifies the maximum allowable height, above mean sea level (AMSL), the instrument procedure will permit at the coordinates under study. New TERPS® Version due soon!
