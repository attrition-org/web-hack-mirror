Apply Notice Criteria Correctly

Federal Aviation Regulations specify how the FAA conducts their analysis, learn how to do it yourself. Use the latest software to quickly search for all public use landing facilities near your site and apply the rules of FAR 77.13 to determine if filling is even necessary. Do this yourself, save yourself time and your company money.

FAR Part 77, Subpart C Regulations
Learn to draw and identifies airport imaginary surfaces. Learn to interpret AIRSPACE® site analysis data.  Understand horizontal, conical, transitional, and approach obstacle surface structure to determined what you should do next. Know your height limits, file and build accordingly.  Save Time! Save Money!

 

Instrument Approach Procedures

Use Terps® Professional to display an airport layout on the screen. Display your study site, obstacles, air navigation facilities, and airport on the screen for further study. Learn how to construct instrument approach procedures for various electronic air navigation facilities. Learn to use Remote Altimeter Setting Source to find additional obstacle clearance requirements. Know how far and in what direction an instrument approach procedure extends. Use Terps® Professional to click and drag an instrument approach on the screen to determine if you are locating in a instrument procedure area.
