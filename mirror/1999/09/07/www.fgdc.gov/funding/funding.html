Welcome to the partnerships resource page. No one organization can build the NSDI. Collaborative efforts are essential for its success. The FGDC encourages federal, state, local, and tribal governments, academia, the private sector, and non-profit organizations to work together within a geographic area to make geospatial data available to all. The FGDC provides seed money to regional consortia through three funding programs: the Cooperative Agreements Program (CAP), the Framework Demonstration Projects Program (FDPP), and the NSDI Benefits Program.
