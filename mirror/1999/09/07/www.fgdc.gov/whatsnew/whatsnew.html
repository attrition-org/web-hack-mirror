June 30, 1999
Southeast Regional Framework Workshop

Location:
Holiday Inn Conference Plaza
130 Clairemont Ave.
Decatur, Georgia 30030
Tel: 404.371.0204
Fax: 404.377.2726

On 14-15 September 1999, the Natural Resource Leadership Group (a regional consortium) in conjunction with the Federal Geographic Data Committee will sponsor the first Southeast Regional Framework Workshop. This workshop is meant to expand dialogue for the continued development and implementation of the National Spatial Data Infrastructure(NSDI) with a focus on regional initiatives and the need of organizations for framework data.

Principal topics of discussion will include:

  • Accessing spatial information using clearinghouse and metadata.
  • Building partnerships to leverage and coordinate the development of spatial data.
  • Standards to insure compatibility and share-ability of spatial data.
  • Developing a framework of commonly used and widely useful geo-spatial data

Special emphasis will be placed on stimulating further participation of regional Federal agencies in NSDI activities thus enhancing regional benefits.

Who should attend?

Representatives of regional organizations in the Southeast actively involved in collection, use, and dissemination of geospatial data.

Cost: No registration fee is required

January 15, 1999
Preliminary Results of NSGIC/FGDC framework survey

The National States Geographic Information Council (NSGIC) and the Federal Geographic Data Committee (FGDC) recently concluded a survey of over 5000 data users throughout the United States. This survey provides a snapshot of data being produced and used in state, regional, and local governments that could contribute to the building of a framework of locally-produced, locally maintained data themes available to all. Preliminary data are available.
