SBLI

Savings Bank Life Insurance, or SBLI, was founded in 1942 and has maintained one of the highest ratings attainable from the industry's leading independent authority, A.M. Best. Through savings banks like People's, SBLI offers a wide range of low-cost Term and Whole Life plans, as well as tax-deferred annuities. High in quality and low in cost, SBLI's policies can meet your protection needs for today and cash accumulation needs for tomorrow.

Affordable Term Life Insurance Coverage

Term insurance is low-cost, pure insurance which is automatically renewed periodically and ends at age 70. You pay only for the life insurance protection you need. Need more information?

Low-Cost Depositor's Group Term Life Insurance

Now you can provide the extra protection you want for your family but thought you couldn't afford. If you're a People's Bank customer between the ages of 15 and 60, you may apply for $15,000 to $300,000 of Depositor's Group Life coverage, which offers the lowest-cost coverage available from SBLI. Need more information?

Unlike other term policies, Depositor's Group automatically includes a waiver of premium provision which will pay your premium should you become disabled and cannot work. Plus, you have the option to convert your Term policy to SBLI permanent Whole Life and begin building tax-deferred cash value and dividends which can be used for any purpose. Need more information?

Single Premium Children's Term

For a one-time premium as low as $80, you can start building a secure future for your children or grandchildren. From 15 days to 15 years old, any child is eligible to apply, and once the policy is approved, it will protect the child up to age 22. At age 22, the child is guaranteed to convert their policy to any SBLI policy for up to five times the original amount. That could mean up to $50,000 of life insurance with no health questions to answer, at an age when premiums are still low. Need more information?


*Death benefit is 1/2 before child's fifth birthday.

 

Enjoy the Lifetime Benefits of Whole Life Coverage

Whole Life is permanent insurance, available in amounts up to $300,000. Anyone age 15 days to age 70 (Straight Life) may apply. Though the premium is higher than Term, your premium remains level and does not increase with age. Your coverage continues for life and earns cash value and dividends which can be used for any purpose such as retirement, education, etc. Need more information?

Straight Whole Life

Straight Life is low cost, permanent insurance for those individuals who believe they'll need life insurance for many years to come. Over time, Straight Life, with its tax-deferred cash value build-up and dividends, can be very economical for those with long-term insurance needs.

You decide how much coverage you need and what you can afford. Your death benefit will never decrease and your premium will never increase. The premium you pay in the first year is the same you pay during the entire period of coverage. Need more information?

Single Premium Whole Life

SBLI's Single Premium Whole Life allows you to purchase the insurance you need with one single premium payment. You'll be insured for life and have accumulating cash value and dividends while providing your family with the added security they need. Need more information?

20 Payment Whole Life

This plan provides the same permanent protection as Straight Life, but you pay a fixed premium for only 20 years. After that, the policy is paid up and coverage will continue without additional premiums. Your premiums are slightly higher because of the shorter payment schedule, but you save money overall because you make premium payments for fewer years and build cash value faster. Need more information?

Whole Life Paid Up at Age 65

An economical way to complete your premium payments before retirement, this plan builds a nest egg for you and your family while assuring you protection throughout your lifetime. You pay level premiums that end when you turn 65, but your coverage and cash values will continue for the rest of your life. Need more information?

Guaranteed Golden

Guaranteed Golden is an outstanding value with low, affordable rates. Forget about physical exams or lengthy questionnaires. We guarantee that we won't turn you down. That's a promise. You automatically qualify if you're a female between the ages of 55 and 75 or a male 55 to 72. The premium you pay will never go up and the value of your insurance will never go down. Plus, you build cash value at the same time.

You can buy it to restore the value of your existing insurance eaten away by inflation, or to give as a gift to someone you love, or more practically, to help pay final bills and tax expenses. As long as you pay your premiums, Guaranteed Golden will protect you for life. Our policy provides more coverage for your premium dollar than others on the market. Need more information?

Tax-Deferred Annuities

SBLI's Tax-Deferred Annuities allow you to put aside cash for your retirement and pay no income tax on the interest you earn until the funds are withdrawn. SBLI offers you the choice of either a flexible savings plan or single premium savings plan.

The TaxSaver I Annuity is our flexible premium savings plan. You may open your account with as little as $5,000 and each year on the anniversary date of your Annuity you can add $1,000 or up to a total combined amount of $500,000 per Annuitant in principal for SBLI Annuities. Each year you'll receive a new competitive interest rate. Your money accumulates interest on a tax-deferred basis, and there are no administrative fees to erode your account value. In later years, it provides a secure lifetime income you cannot outlive.

SBLI's TaxSaver II Annuity is a single premium savings plan. You may open your account with as little as $10,000. Your competitive interest rate is fixed for two years with a new rate declared on your policy anniversary every two years thereafter. Like TaxSaver I, there are no administrative fees and your money accumulates interest on a tax-deferred basis. TaxSaver II can also accept a rollover or transfer from your existing IRA, 401(k) or qualified retirement plan. Need more information?

SBLI's tax-deferred annuities are not federally insured deposits and are not obligations of, nor guaranteed by, any federally insured bank.
