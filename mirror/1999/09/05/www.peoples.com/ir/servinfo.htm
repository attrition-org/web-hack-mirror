Dividend Reinvestment and Stock Purchase Plan

Owners of People's Bank common stock may participate in the Dividend Reinvestment and Stock Purchase Plan. Dividends are reinvested to purchase additonal full and fractional shares without fees or other charges.

For participants in the dividend reinvestment plan, there is also a cash option plan which provides the opportunity to purchase between $100 and $10,000 of People's Bank common stock ($10,000 maximum per month) with a check or money order sent to the plan's, transfer agent, ChaseMellon Shareholder Services. For further information on the dividend reinvestment and stock purchase plan, plus an enrollment card and a description of the plan, contact: ChaseMellon Shareholder Services
