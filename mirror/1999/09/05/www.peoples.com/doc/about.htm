Providing financial services to Connecticut...and the world

People's Bank of Bridgeport, Conn., is a multi-service financial organization internationally recognized for offering credit cards with low, fixed rates. With more than $12 billion in managed assets and 127 branches, People's is the state's leading mortgage originator and top issuer of Savings Bank Life Insurance. Its subsidiaries, People's Securities, Inc., People's Capital and Leasing Corp., Olson Mobeck & Associates, Inc., and R.C. Knox and Company, Inc., offer discount brokerage, equipment leasing and asset management and insurance services, respectively.
