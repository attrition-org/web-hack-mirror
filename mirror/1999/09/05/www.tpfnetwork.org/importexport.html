TPF is a worldwide network of INDEPENDENT freight forwarding firms organized to provide shippers with a better alternative. TPF offers shippers the superior service of local firms operated by hands-on entrepreneurs combined with the global reach of an international network.

The goals and mission of the TPF Network
TPF Association was formed to operate as an international trade association of independent freight forwarding firms. The goal of TPF is to improve the business environment for the independent freight forwarder by providing services that promote international carriage of cargo. The association’s mission is to raise awareness of the independent freight forwarding business, educate members on issues that impact the freight movement industry, enable effective communication and processing for freight movement.
