TPF Central

The TPF Association was formed to operate as an international trade association of independent freight forwarding firms. The goal of TPF is to improve the business environment for the independent freight forwarder by providing services that promote international carriage of cargo. The association’s mission is to raise awareness of the independent freight forwarding business, educate members on issues that impact the freight movement industry, enable effective communication and processing for freight movement.
