Officers and Directors: The board of directors consists of six (6) representatives - with no more than two from the same geographic region of the world. Officers are elected to serve as President/CEO, Secretary and Treasurer.

Member Involvement: Work Groups have been established to address areas of importance to the members. The work groups evaluate the needs of the members, collect information, research options and discuss potential programs to address the group’s issues. The work groups are advisory groups to the Board of Directors and are an opportunity for members to become involved in the decision making process and to shape the future of TPF.
