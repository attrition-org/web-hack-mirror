What Is OrderPal?

OrderPal is an order form processing system. It allows companies with a small amount of products to be able to sell them online. This is perfect for a company who cannot afford to pay the high prices for a full blown shopping cart. The biggest benefit is the flexibility of the system. You can have a page where customers enter the item numbers descriptions and prices of the products. Or you can have a page that contains your product. It is all a one step system for you and your customer! Plus OrderPal takes only 10 Minutes to Setup. We include FREE email support with all orders also.
