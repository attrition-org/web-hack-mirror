eShowcase Pricing

eShowcase is available in two versions:

  • eShowcase Pro - this is the complete shopping mall creation and management software package. Once installed on your site, you can create an unlimited number of stores in your mall. This product is fully automatic, and nothing else is needed (except a web site to host in on).

    The price is $825 and this includes us doing the installation on the site for you, creation of your mal, and testing of all features.

  • eShowcase Standard - if you just want a single internet store installed on your site, without the ability to create other stores or a shopping mall, then you want eShowcase standard. It has all the features including the fully automatic order form and admin menu, but it can only be used to create one estore. The price is $425 .
