The Clock Collector's Guild's prime goal is the preservation of antique and collectible clocks, as well as the craftsman ship required to build and maintain classic time pieces.

We have a large museum of clocks open to the public adjactent to our headquarters in Mainspring, Iowa, which is open to the public 9-5 six days a week.
