About our Company

eShowcase was designed and developed by Hamilton New Media, a company specializing in creating the tools of ecommerce for the 'rest of us'.

Hamilton New Media was formed by author and software developer Bill Myers, with the goal of developing books, newsletters, software applications and training materials for entrepreneurs and small businesses wanting to join in the computer revolution.

Since it's formation in 1987, Hamilton New Media has received world-wide attention and recognition for breakthroughs in desktop video production, CD ROM creation tools, and lately, tools of ecommerce.

Hamilton New Media has been responsible for products such as

  • eShowcase automated business web site builder
  • ezOrderForm automated internet order form system
  • ezContact automated internet contact system
  • Order Desk Pro off-line order tracking system
  • InfoBoss automatic CD ROM creator
  • Helptalk information systems.

Bill Myers is still the chief designer/developer for Hamilton New Media, and if you call us, it's pretty likely, you can get him on the phone. That's one of the benefits of dealing with a small company - you can actually talk to the people who design the products.

You can find out more about Bill Myers, his Product Development Newsletter, his training videos, and his various other projects at his main site.
