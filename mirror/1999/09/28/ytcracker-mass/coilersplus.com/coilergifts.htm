 COILERS are shoelaces permanently coiled with elastic, allowing the wearer to slip in and out of their shoes quickly, without tying or untying. 

They fit any shoe and look great on everyone. The laces can be loosened or tightened, but never have to be tied. COILERS come with a lifetime guarantee!
