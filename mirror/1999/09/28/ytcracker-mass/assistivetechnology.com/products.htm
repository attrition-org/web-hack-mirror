Key Benefits

Has Technology Been Considered? A Guide for IEP Teams.  This publication compiles information essential to have a working knowledge of assistive technology; what it is, why it may be needed, information on the laws which direct it, and a framework to guide the IEP team as they consider the assistive technology needs of a child with exceptional education needs.  This guide is an informative, easy to use,   useful tool for anyone concerned about meeting the needs of children with exceptional education needs:  parents/guardians, teachers, administrators, therapists, consultants, advocates.
