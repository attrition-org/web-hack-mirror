How can we serve your needs?

Crash / Dent Repairs
The basic and most popular duties of an auto body facility. These range from just a simple dent or scratch to the collision encounters. At Creative Cars, not only do we provide an excellent color match to the factory paint, but a quality finish equal to or better than original. We can also handle most insurance claims.
Minor Restorations
Got an older car that looks.......old? Paint fading, peeling, or cracking? Or just tired of the outdated color? Sure lime green was a favorite in the 70's, but this is the 90's! And the price of those new cars, Yikes!! Let us restore the appearance of that used car! We can refinish to the original color or give it a new updated color!.
Major Restorations
Have an antique or classic car? Is yours not worth its potential value because of faded paint or body damage? Creative Cars is an excellent choice for restoring your classic! This process involves stripping the cars old paint completely off, down to the metal or fiberglass. Then carefully preparing the body, block sanding, and applying the color coats. This also involves wet sanding and buffing for that glossy wet, award winning mirror finish !
Custom Creations
Do you want to stand out from the crowd? Are you tired of pulling up to the stop light with another car just like yours in the next lane? Well, If you can imagine it and afford it, we can build it! All vehicles are stripped down to the bare metal or fiberglass. Custom ground effects or body panels can be molded in for a smooth fit. The bodies are then prepped and block sanded. After the color coats, stripes, or any other custom designs are applied, each vehicle is then clear coated for that deep glossy finish. With a final wet sanding and buffing, you're ready for the show circuit!
