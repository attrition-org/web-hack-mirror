About IRA

Investigative Resources Agency is a full service investigations firm. From a simple background check to capital murder cases, we have the skills and experience you demand.

We realize how difficult it can be to gather information and how easy it can be to miss certain details. Today, many cases are decided on those details.

At IRA, we're specialists in gathering information. We know where to look. Our investigators are skilled at tying into today's computer systems and databases. We can find hidden assets, missing persons, and even locate delinquent debtors.

So whether you're an attorney with a case pending or a new parent trying to find the right child care provider, give us a call and leave the details to us.

All of our investigators are licensed in Florida, and skilled in many areas of investigative work. With over 25 years of investigative experience, our team has earned a reputation of excellent service at a reasonable cost.
