The CRS Designation - The Mark of a Professional

The Certified Residential Specialist (CRS) is the highest designation awarded to sales associates in the residential sales field and recognizes their professional accomplishments in both experience and education. They must have first completed the educational requirements for their GRI designation, and an additional number of board certified courses.

Successful completion of the courses lead to the Certified Residential Specialist designation - one of the most successful residential real estate educational programs in the country. Every CRS is a real estate professional equipped with special expertise, thanks to advanced study in listing, selling, investment, taxes and more.

 


Graduate Realtor Institute - A rigorous educational program, requiring the real estate professional to successfully complete courses in marketing, finance, laws, technology, and other programs that will prepare them to help the public in all real estate transactions.

Because buying and selling property has become increasingly complex, you need to rely on an agent with a keen understanding of new technology, laws, procedures involved with real estate transactions - those who make the investment of time and money to achieve the GRI designation, they are committed to professionalism.
