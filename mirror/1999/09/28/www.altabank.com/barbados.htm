The Island of Barbados

Welcome to the sophisticated business environment in Barbados. The financial infrastructure and legislation of this independent Caribbean island are designed to provide you with Safety and Security of your assets. Barbados... The Choice of Discreet Investors

Barbados, only 166 square miles in size is the most easterly of the Caribbean islands with a population of approximately 260,000. It is a former British colony and a member of the Commonwealth, which became independent in 1966.

The island's politically stable government has established an environment conducive to attracting foreign investors and businesses. Following a global search to find a jurisdiction that would benefit our conservative client base, Altamira identified Barbados, as fulfilling the stringent requirements of its investors.

Among the list of criteria which Barbados meets are:
  • Recognition as a major financial centre.
  • Corporate law and legal process are based on common law and the legal system of Great Britain and enhanced by local statutes which have been drawn on modern laws from within the Commonwealth.
