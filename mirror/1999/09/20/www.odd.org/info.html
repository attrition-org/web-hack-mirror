Its quite simple, accounts are free.
All we need to know is what you want for a username and what you want as your password.
Nice huh?
The reason for this in case you are curious, is that we think that ALL information MUST be free. Along with access to it and the ability to distribute it, information in all of its forms can be useful, helpful, and crucial for a healthy society to continue being healthy. We object to the selling, hoarding and withholding of information and are doing what we can to encourage people to "cut the bullshit"
