Consulting Services

Our consulting services cover nearly the entire spectrum of the oil and gas business. All of our consultants have at least one graduate degree (half of our consultants have at least two graduate degrees in the three earth sciences disciplines) with a minimum of 8 years experience with one of the majors. Our experience base covers all continents, most international oil companies and state oil companies. You can contact us in our Denver, Houston or London offices for consulting services.
