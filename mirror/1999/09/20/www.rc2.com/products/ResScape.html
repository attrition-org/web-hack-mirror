ResScape: 3-D Visualization Tool

This program provides a 3D visualization tool based on the OpenInventor graphics toolkit. It provides the capability to generate fence diagrams, rotate the model, see through certain ranges of values, display well paths and their logs, animate the model in the X,Y or Z directions. ResScape is provided with any ResMod license. If you have a fast connection click here for a gallery of ResScape output (ResScape Gallery). Support or questions can be addressed through email resscape@rc2.com or through our Denver office.

Features:

  • Fence diagrams
  • Arbitrary cross sections
  • Data masking
  • Interactively zoom, rotate, and translate
  • Animate in X, Y, or Z planes
  • Show well trajectories and streamlines
  • Unlimited lighting capabilities
