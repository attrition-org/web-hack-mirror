Investment Information

Reservoir Characterization Research and Consulting, Inc., otherwise known as RC-Squared, was incorporated as a Colorado Corporation in 1993. We are a privately held corporation. A wholly owned subsidiary is Reservoir Characterization, Research and Consulting, (UK) Ltd.

Our headquarters office is in Denver, with additional offices in Houston, London and Aberdeen, Scotland.

Besides employee ownership, our two major investors are the Altira Group LLC and First Reserve Corporation. Our listing under the portfolio companies of First Reserve is at the following link First Reserve Portfolio.
