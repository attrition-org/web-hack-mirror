journyx is committed to respecting your privacy. We recognize your need for appropriate protection and management of personally identifiable information you share with us. That is why journyx has established this privacy policy. We want you to understand the care with which we treat your personal information.

In general, you can visit journyx.com without telling us who you are or revealing any personal information about yourself. We track the Internet address of the domains from which people visit us and analyze this data for trends and statistics, but the individual user remains anonymous.

Our demo utilizes "cookies" so that we can better serve you. Cookies are identifiers which a Web site can send to your browser to keep on your computer to facilitate your next visit to our site. You can set your browser to notify you when you are sent a cookie, giving you the chance to decide whether or not to accept it. The information we collect in our demo is used to better serve you. The product is set-up to use cookies to allow you to better track who is accessing the software.

We will take appropriate steps to protect the information you share with us from unauthorized access or disclosure. None of your information will be sold. Ever.
