Nassau Broadcasting Partners is grouped into three core areas covering the state of New Jersey and parts of Pennsylvania and New York State.

Nassau North
Including the counties of Morris, Sussex and Warren, NJ; Monroe and Pike, PA; Orange, NY
 
Nassau Central
Including the counties of Mercer, Middlesex, Somerset, Burlington and Hunterdon, NJ; and Bucks county, PA.
 
Nassau Shore
Including the counties of Monmouth and Ocean, NJ.
