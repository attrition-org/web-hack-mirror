Haltech IG5
Ignition-Only Management System
Last Updated : 15th April, 1999
The Haltech IG5 is the perfect solution for those wishing to upgrade their fuel-only system at a later date to control ignition timing as well , or for those who want to convert their distributor based system to that of a direct-fire ignition system.
 


The Haltech IG5 ECU


 

Features of the IG5 include up to 22 ignition timing MAPS, with one every 500rpm, and 32 adjustment points per MAP for load adjustment. A choice of MAP sensors is available to suit the type of engine you are using. 1-BAR for aspirated engines , 2-BAR for supercharged or turbocharged engines up to 14psi boost , and the 3-BAR sensor for engines up to 28psi boost.
Along with this there are correction graphs, with 32 adjustment points, each for coolant temp , air temp , cranking , and rotary trailing split timing adjustment for engine load and rpm.
Basically it has the same adjustments as the ignition-side of the E6 systems , along with the ability to control a number of programmable output options listed under the E6 and F9 sections.
