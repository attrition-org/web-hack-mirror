Nellis AFB - Home of the Fighter Pilot

Welcome to Nellis Air Force Base, "Home of the Fighter Pilot".   Nellis is a proud member of the United States Air Force's Air Combat Command.  It is home to the largest and most demanding advanced air combat training in the world. 

Spend a few days on the ramp and you will see every combat aircraft in the Air Force, Navy and Marine Corps inventories:   fighters, bombers, helicopters, airlifters, and command and control - they're all here.

Nellis provides training for composite strike forces which include every type of aircraft in the U.S. Air Force inventory.  Training is also conducted in conjunction with air and ground units of the Army, Navy and Marine Corps as well as air forces from our allied nations. 

The crews do not come to learn how to fly, but instead how to be the best combat aviators in the world.   In the process, the aircrews go beyond the four-ship formations used at their home bases, to become part of a huge aerial armada of 80 aircraft or more.
