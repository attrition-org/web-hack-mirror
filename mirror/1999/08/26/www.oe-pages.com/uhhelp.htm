Q : How do I make a web page?

A : Just go to http://www.oe-pages.com  Click on Create uh Page now. The
automator will walk you through step by step. When done creating a page click
on save changes and exit located in the navigation bar. That will take you to a
registration form. Fill it out. Make sure you give us your real e-mail address. Then
you will be given your web page and address.
Note :  Your web page consists of the community, district, and username you
choose when filling out your registration form.

Q :  How do I edit my web page?

A :  Go to http://www.oe-pages.com  Click on edit uh page.  Then click on
free member pages, or premium member pages which ever you are.  Type in
your username and password.  Remember your username is always lowercase
your password can be uppercase if you typed it in that way when you registered
your web page.  Do not type in your email address in the box that says NONE.
That is only their if you forgot your username and password.  Hit the submit
button to enter your editor.  In here you can edit any step on your web page.

Q : Why when I look for my page in a search engine it doesn't show up?

A : Search engines don't automatically know when a new web page enters the web.
You have to tell them. There are hundreds of search engines, but only about 7 or so
that really matter. You can announce your web page to the search engines two ways;
one is to go to each search engine and fill out their forms or you can hire someone to
announce your page to all the engines easily. With Create uh Page, you can announce 
your page to the top engines, and put in meta tags (keywords.) This only costs you 
$25 and is part of our premium account. You can sign up for a premium account after
making a page, or when editing your existing page. Please note that the time it takes for
your site to appear in the search engine varies from one to the next, be patient , it can 
sometimes take up to a month.
