Our Process




Our process starts with the careful selection of images that will eventually grace the face of the switch plate. Then we get the copyright to reproduce these 36" by 48" gorgeous paintings into various smaller sizes to fit the different models of the switch plates.

Then we use some nifty machines to uniformly coat the print surface with a high gloss finish. This brings out the brilliant colors but conversely locks them in for protection.

Each piece of reproduced artwork is then carefully placed onto the switch plate with a strong adhesive. The appropriate holes are painstakingly and accurately cut.

At the end of the process, something special is created. A business opportunity with good margins. A compact product that doesn't take up too much space but is eye catching enough to present your clients a unique choice for gifts ... one that is just right... with a touch of class and elegance...
