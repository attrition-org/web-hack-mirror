Company History




How We Started

We are a home grown company in North Vancouver, Canada. We started out making handcrafted switch plates for sale at craft fairs. Because of the quality of our work, demand for our product grew. However, we found it increasingly hard to satisfy client demand for mass reproduction, specially if it was of the same design. The availability of materials was always limited. This prompted us to find alternative methods and our research led us to our new production process.

The Switch Plates Redesigned

Uncompromising in our standard to maintain or surpass the quality of our product, we experimented with various methods. Technological advances and hard work resulted in numerous improvements. And the use of paintings by talented West Coast artists, made it possible to turn a simple switch plate into a small work of art adorning the wall.

Our Commitment

We, at Miller Concepts are unwavering in our commitment to you, our clients and your needs. Our mission statement more than exemplifies this value:

"To exceed our clients' needs by providing the highest quality products at the lowest possible price."
