It's easy to send a free digital postcard to a friend!

Instructions:
Just complete the form below, following each step along the way. When you've finished click the PREVIEW button. You will be shown a preview. If you don't like your card, you may edit it and then send it, or just cancel the request. The recipient will receive email advising where to find the card. the cards are stored in a non-indexed directory so only you and the recipient will know how to find the card. Have fun!
