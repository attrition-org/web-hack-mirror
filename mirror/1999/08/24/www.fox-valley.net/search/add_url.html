How to Get Listed in the Directory
This Directory is organized by subject and most sites in it are suggested to us by users. Sites are placed in categories by our content team, who visit and evaluate your suggestions and decide where they best belong. We do this to ensure that the Directory is organized in the best possible way, is easy to use, intuitive, helpful, and fair to everyone.

With this in mind, we ask that you follow three simple steps if you have a site (URL) you'd like to suggest:

1. Check to see if your site is already listed.
An important first step, because if your site is already listed, you do not need to submit it again.  If you would like to edit your listing, please use the Change URL form instead.

Check by searching for your site by title, or by browsing appropriate categories. (You can do this from most pages within the directory using the search box near the top of the screen).

2: Find the appropriate category in the Directory.
Finding an appropriate category for your site is at the heart of this process. Remember that our content team visit each site suggested and proper categorization on your part helps us process suggestions quickly.

3. Spider your site from the appropriate category.
First browse to the category in the Directory that you think is most appropriate for your site.

Once in the appropriate category, enter the complete URL in the Spider It form at the bottom of the page and press the Spider it button:

The page will be verified, spidered and any meta tags in the page will be indexed and this information will be returned immediately in a submission form for you to review, correct and complete before submitting.

Please be prepared to provide your e-mail address, your site's title, URL, a brief description and up to 35 key search words to associate with your entry. Please ensure that you use the full URL (http://.....) in your entry. Also, we ask that you do not use any non-alphanumeric characters in the description or keywords. Separate your keywords with spaces, not commas! Please note that final placement of your site is determined by our content team.

We update our database on average every two days and sites following the guidelines above can expect to be added in this time. Please help us to help you by providing your information as outlined above.

If you have any problems spidering a site please email the information to Webmaster@fox-valley.net.
