About the TOP 10 SITES Voting Feature

When your site is accepted in the Directory you are automatically assigned a unique Site ID number and an email is sent to you which includes the html code you'll need to add to your page(s) to create a voting link and begin the voting process.

Sample Link:
Once this html is inserted, the following link will appear on your page:

Vote for this site

FVID Top 10 Sites
Sample Code:

This code may be altered to your own tastes, but the link must remain intact and your Site ID number must be included for the votes to be properly recorded for your site.
How the Voting Process Works:
Each time a new visitor to your page clicks on your voting link a vote is recorded in our search engine for your site. Naturally, the more votes your site gets - the higher it will rank in our TOP 10 SITES list.

Each subcategory in the Directory has a link to the TOP 10 sites for that specific subcategory. There is also a link on the Main page to the TOP 10 list for the entire Directory. When visitors click these links they'll be presented with a hypertext linked list of the Top 10 sites.

If you have any questions or problems regarding the set up of your voting link please contact us. We'll be happy to help you.
