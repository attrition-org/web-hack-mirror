Dansie
JukeBox

Features:
  • Dansie JukeBox will play random music selections or you can choose from a list.
  • A large selection of MIDIs to choose from.
  • Multiple categories of music, so you can listen to the type of music you like.
  • You can upload your own MIDI files to the Dansie JukeBox website in the category of your choice.
  • It floats around on your desktop, so you can take it with you when you surf the web.
Minimum requirements to use Dansie JukeBox:
  • A sound card.
  • Netscape Navigator 3.0+ or Internet Explorer 3.2+
  • A good plugin that can play MIDIs, such as Crescendo!
  • Make sure your browser's JavaScript is turned on.

Frequently Asked Questions:
  • What's a MIDI? What does it stand for?
Musical Instrument Digital Interface. It's the most common and quickest way to send portable music files over the net.
  • Do I need to download and install Dansie JukeBox in order to listen to it?
No. If you already have a recent version of Netscape or Internet Explorer and a MIDI plugin, you are ready to go. Just click on the "Start Dansie JukeBox" button.
