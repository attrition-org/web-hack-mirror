Dansie Webspace Detector

How much disk space is your website using?
Find out at the click of a mouse.
Dansie Webspace Detector Features:
  • Easy installation. Only three variables to configure.
  • Counts and displays in alphabetical order all directories with clickable links to deeper directories.
  • Counts and displays in alphabetical order all file types and quantities in the current directory and on your entire website.
  • Counts and displays total files and directories.
  • Counts and displays total space used on your web site.
  • Runs on Unix and Windows NT with Perl 4 or Perl 5.
