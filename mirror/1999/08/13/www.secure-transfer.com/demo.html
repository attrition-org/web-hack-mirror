Demonstration of
easy access
to shopping cart:

Always place an easy to find graphical link back to the shopping cart somewhere on each page of your website. This link should say something like "View Cart" or "Checkout". This makes it easy for your customers to continue shopping while knowing they can easily find their way back to the shopping cart at any time to complete their purchase. Go ahead, click on the image. The shopping cart comes with this standard image, or you can make your own.
