Welcome to DownTown Web Graphics. As our name implies, we provide web graphics as well as web site development services, but we won't subject you to the details so soon after your arrival. If you're really interested, the links above will probably tell you more than you ever wanted to know.

When we first undertook creating this site, we were tempted to start it off with a flashy show of all we can do. But we killed that idea real quick when we realized that we would be making the same mistake we caution our clients to avoid - graphics overkill. We decided instead to use our own design philosophy, to get our message to you in the quickest, most efficient way possible while still pleasing to the eye and let you decide what you want to know about us. But if it will take flash and glitz to impress you, don't worry, we do strut some of our stuff on other pages and if we don't show it here and you feel you need it for your project, we'll send you examples or links that we feel will convince you that we know what we're doing.

If we've succeeded so far in getting your attention and interest, we hope you'll follow one of the links above to learn more about us and what we have to offer. In the process, we hope to convince you that we are competent, competitive and most important, capable of fulfilling your web site design and graphics needs.
