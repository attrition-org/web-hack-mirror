Opening Remarks from
Major General Adams
 

Mahalo, to the PACMEDTek attendees, sponsors, speakers, chairs and symposium planners! The PACMEDTek Symposium was a tremendous success thanks to the efforts of many staff members and the high caliber curriculum that was offered. As you know, Conference Proceedings will be published this Fall, which will include all the presentations, plus this conference website link has been established to further enhance the distribution of conference information. It is my hope that the lectures and information that were provided at the Symposium will be quoted and shared throughout the Military Health System and the private sector health care community for years to come. Please visit the web link frequently to view the presentation materials, and/or be "hot linked" to other sites covering similar topics. I wish each of you success in your upcoming FY99 clinical initiatives, and hope you consult and/or partner with the Pacific Regional Program Office staff on a frequent basis! Thanks again for making the PACMEDTek Symposium a success!

Nancy Adams
Major General, United States Army
Tripler Army Medical Center
