PRPO & TAMC Staff Visit the Pohakuloa Training Area, Hawaii

Jan. 7, 1999 -- In preparation for a potential new Akamai telemedicine research project, Cynthia Kohuth, R.N., PRPO Telemedicine Clinical Director and Dr. Michael Brumage, Principal Investigator, conducted a site visit at the Pohakuloa Training Area on the Big Island of Hawaii.
