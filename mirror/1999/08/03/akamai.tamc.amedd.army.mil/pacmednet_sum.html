Background:

PACMEDNET is a congressionally directed, automation and telecommunications project focused in the Pacific Region. PACMEDNET focuses on creating information sharing between and among heterogeneous databases as well as identifying the location of the information unique to each patient. Through creation of an interface engine, PACMEDNET has successfully shared information between multiple Composite Health Care System sites and the Department of Veteran's Affairs Vista System (formerly Defense Health Care Program (DHCP)). Through creation of a Master Subject Index (MSI), the project has provided electronic pointers to patient information through use of a Master Patient Locator. To insure appropriate protection of patient information the MSI also tracks provider access privileges to virtual data through the Master Provider Index. These initiatives are done for the purpose of transferring patient medical data (e.g. text, image, video, sound, wave form data, etc.) among military and DVA medical treatment facilities and all necessary theater locations for readiness posture/peacetime, military operations other than war, lesser regional conflict, and major regional conflicts.
