Alumni

This is a consolidated feedback of the 57 students of the ‘batch of 1996’. In brackets is the percentage of students who shared that feeling.

Things they learnt

To take on responsibility (90%)
To work under stress and meet deadlines (90%)
To manage time effectively (80%)
To initiate and complete tasks (75%)
A sense of conviction (80%)
That team-work is vital (70%)
That self-motivation is the way to reach the top (70%)
A professional outlook towards life (70%)
Stamina to work non-stop in timelessness till task is complete (70%)
Effective communication and good interpersonal relations (65%)
To interact with people of a diverse cultural background (60%)

How they transformed

Developed responsibility and accountability (90%)
Became more open-minded (90%)
Shunned the casual attitude (85%)
Ego hassles were solved (80%)
Matured (75%)
Started respecting simplicity (60%)
Quit smoking (35%)

What they had to say

Introspection helps to realize potential and develops confidence (90%)
A directed experiential learning (65%)
"SCMHRD helps one discover oneself" (70%)
