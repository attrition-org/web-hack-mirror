  • Time, budget and quality driven co-curricular activities help students to learn by doing, practical time management rather than knowing conceptually. A drill driven approach to grooming develops healthy habits for excellence.

  • Professional psychological counselling and mentoring to guide students.

  • Weekly open house of students, faculty and staff for problem solving, process improvement and to instill the value of transparency in everyone.

  • Constant contact with parents to understand personal issues affecting students progress.

  • 100 % attendance policy with ‘voluntary absenteeism reporting by students themselves’ to promote honesty and self discipline.

  • Suggestion schemes & instant individual ‘Hurt Resolution’ process through ‘Hurt’ box system.

  • An academic evaluation system of ‘self examination & self correction’ & a 360° developmental appraisal.

  • A credit course of ‘Contemporary Business Environment’ and viva-voce to promote reading habits.

  • An atmosphere where the students can assert, dissent and question the Director, staff & process.

  • Absolute transparency in all actions and decisions including the selection of students.
