BMG, The Cash and Information Management Bank


A history of being different...

Bank Mendes Gans nv (BMG), a member of the ING Group, was founded in Amsterdam, The Netherlands, in 1883. BMG is known world-wide as a niche bank for cash and information management, providing customized services for multinationals. BMG has developed and perfected a full range of banking and information services required by its corporate clients whom it serves with a high level of professionalism and personal service.

A strategy that works...

BMG positions itself as "your in-house bank". Being an extension of corporate treasuries, BMG offers out-sourced systems and services aimed at setting up global integrated cash and information management structures. BMG recognizes the importance of activities for which special expertise and speed of information is required. The ongoing consolidation within corporate treasuries is increasing the need for the out-sourced services which BMG can supply.

Setting directions for a future growth...

BMG has been supporting corporate treasuries world-wide since the sixties. Early entry into electronic banking has become a signature of the bank, demonstrating its ongoing commitment to technology as a means of enhancing both productivity and service. Providing innovative cash and information management solutions, BMG has stretched the boundaries of development in information technology.

True to its own tradition and talent...

BMG has built an excellent reputation as a lead bank in the field of cash and information management, providing highly flexible and customized solutions for corporate treasuries around the world. We believe in hands-on relationships with our customers, innovation in techniques and systems, and stringent internal controls. BMG has persisted in being different from other financial institutions but remains true to its own traditions and talents. "BMG - your key to success in the next millennium."

Mission statement...

"Bank Mendes Gans aims to be a leading bank in the field of international cash and information management for multinationals and large organizations."
