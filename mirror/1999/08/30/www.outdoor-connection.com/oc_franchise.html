    Questions & Answers   top

    Q. What are the advantages in owning an Outdoor Connection Franchise?
    A. The business requires a relatively low initial investment. The value of the business can be increased as business increases with minimal added capital. You have no inventory; it is a service business and it's fun!

    Q. Are there any travel benefits to the franchise owners?
    A. Yes, there are inspection trips that are scheduled throughout the year. These may afford you the opportunity to experience the lodges first hand.

    Q. How does the Franchisee earn income?
    A. You receive commissions on bookings from lodges, resorts, outfitters and other related fields. As the Outdoor Connection franchisor, we can help increase those commissions substantially over the normal commissions.

    Q. What is the future of the fishing & hunting travel industry?
    A. For the well run franchise using effective marketing approaches, offering unique services to its clients and taking advantage of the corporate, vacation, incentive and group market, the growth and potential is fantastic.

    Q. Why Outdoor Connection?
    A. When you buy any franchise, you purchase a name, system, and advertising. By being a part of Outdoor Connection, you are assured of being competitive in the marketplace.

    Q. Is any financng available?
    A. In many cases, Outdoor Connection will finance 50% of the initial franchise fee.
