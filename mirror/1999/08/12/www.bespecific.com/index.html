Welcome to BeSpecific.com!

Our community is in constant change. This is a place where we can tie together the many resources that the Internet and the BeOS provides us. We hope that BeSpecific will become that for you, with sections on a variety of important subjects:

BeSpecific CD Adamation's shareware CD featuring a compilation of the latest freeware and shareware apps from around the Net conveniently organized on a CDROM.
BeDevTalk Be's discussion list covering developing software on and for the BeOS. Archived here for your convenience.
BeCodeTalk Be's discussion list covering coding issues for the BeOS. Archived here for your convenience.
BeUserTalk Be's discussion list covering using the BeOS and BeOS software. Archived here for your convenience.
Usenet A tremendously valuable resource, we have several months of Usenet news archived, and are collecting more daily. We've put some of the archive here, sorted by thread.
FTP Server Adamation's FTP Server makes the  contents of the latest BeSpecific CD available online for those that are only interested in one or two apps.
Match Play Adamation's email games match making service. Owners of Adam 2.0 featuring Chess, Shogi and Go can find other players by signing up here.
