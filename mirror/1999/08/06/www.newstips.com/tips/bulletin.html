Electronic Editorial Bulletin Archive


  • This page contains an archive of the weekly Electronic Editorial Bulletins mailed out by Newstips Inc. The tips are listed by the four digits of the year, followed by the two digits of the month, and then the letter of the week. For example, the first issue of July 2009 would be listed as 2009-07A.
  • The issues stored in this archive first appear three weeks after they have been sent to reporters, and will remain on the server until they are 6 months old. If you have any questions on the Bulletins, please contact marty@newstips.com.
