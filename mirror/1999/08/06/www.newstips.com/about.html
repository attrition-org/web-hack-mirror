About Newstips

Newstips, Inc. provides unique PR services for companies in and around the computer industry. Its flagship is the weekly Newstips Electronic Editorial Bulletin, which reaches only those journalists who cover computer industry companies, products or services. Thousands of journalists receive each weekly issue, entirely via fax or electronic mail. Each issue is a collection of brief, newsy tidbits about Newstips client companies, intended as news hooks or story starters for reporters.

Marty Winston, the man behind Newstips, has
been putting them out since 1983. His own computer
industry PR experience reaches back to 1974.

 

This Web site marks the first time that the Newstips Bulletins are available to individuals other than those journalists covering the computer industry. The bulletins available to the public are delayed by three weeks, and only carry back six months, in respect of the interests of the journalists who are their primary audience.
The bulletins are available in the following manners:
Anonymous FTP on ftp.newstips.com - in the /pub/newstips/tips directory
On the WWW at the URL http://www.newstips.com/tips/bulletin.html
