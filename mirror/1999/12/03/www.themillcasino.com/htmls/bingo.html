  • You must be 18 years of age to play Bingo.

  • Alcholic beverages and drugs - and those under the influence of either of them - are strictly prohibited.
  • No cameras or video cameras allowed without management approval.
  • It is the player's responsibility to call BINGO loud enough to stop the caller before the next number is called.
  • You must BINGO on the last number called for a valid bingo.
  • Bingo cards are valid only during the session in which they are purchased.
  • Each person must have his/her own admission slip with their full signature in full view at all times. Each person must be playing the number of packs on their receipt. No ticket sharing or pack splitting allowed. Bingos will not be paid when pack splitting or ticket sharing is happening.
  • Once the caller closes the game, only those cards declared to be valid Bingos - and no other Bingo thereafter - will be honored from that game.
