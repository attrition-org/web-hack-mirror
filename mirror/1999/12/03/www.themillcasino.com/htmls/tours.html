The Mill Resort and Casino offers three (3) distinct and separate types of group touring accomodations.

  • The "Free Eugene Fun Bus & Eugene Express Shuttle" travels between Eugene and The Mill Casino every Tuesday, Thursday & Saturday for our Eugene customer's convenience.
  • The "Free Roseburg Fun Bus" travels between Roseburg and The Mill Casino every Wednesday for our Roseburg customer's convenience.
  • The "Free Brookings Fun Bus" travels between Brookings and The Mill Casino the 1st and 3rd Thursday of each month for our Brookings customer's convenience.
  • The "Free Casino Shuttle" stops at various hotel, RV parks and campgrounds in the area.
  • The Mill Resort and Casino can accommodate all motorcoach group tours. Tour planning assistance is available. Email us today and our Group Tours Manager will get back to you. We provide hosting on board or "Meet and Greet" for your guests. Free casino Funbooks, Free motor parking and easy access to the casino are available to all group tours.
