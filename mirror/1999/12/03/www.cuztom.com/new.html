What's New at Cuztom

 We are pleased to announce the arrival of the newly released Encad 500 & 700 inkjet printers. We are seeing improved ouput quality and printing speed from these new printers.

And we're just as pleased to disclose that Cuztom has, for the last 6 months, been a beta test site for the recently announced ZBE Chromira digital photo printer. We have been working closely with our friends at ZBE on the development of this printer, offering suggestions and advice. You can learn all about this new printer at their website, www.zbe.com.

Adobe has announced Photoshop 5.5. It's been available since mid-July. From what we've read, there are no significant new features that will impact the kind of work we and most of our clients do. If you do a lot of web design work, there may be new features you'll like.
