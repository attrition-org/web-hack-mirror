TASKS On-line Registration System

Welcome to the on-line registration system for Discovery, Focus,
Visions and Youth seminars. In addition to parents who are registering at no fee
for Discovery, others may now register here and charge applicable seminar fees
to Visa or MasterCard charge cards.

If you wish to pay with a personal check, please use the mail-in registration form.
If you reached this page in error, please return to our Home Page.

  On-line registration consists of the following steps:
     1. Select training type below (schedules on previous page)
     2. Determine your fee (if applicable)
     3. Complete on-line registration form (1 page)
     4. Submit your credit card information for approval
     5. You will receive your training information packet via email to
         complete your registration. The packet includes:
          a. General training information
          b. Training hours
          c. Hotel information
 
Select the training for which you wish to register.
