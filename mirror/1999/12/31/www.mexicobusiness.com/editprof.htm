Editorial Profile

We have permanent sections each month as follows:

CHISMES- A rundown of things heard and overheard in business and political circles.

MARKETPLACE- A briefing of recent deals, joint ventures and economic news.

REPORTER- Trends in business, politics and society.

FARTHER SOUTH- A focus on what's happening in other parts of Latin America.

FIRST PERSON- Interviews with movers and shakers, from business people to politicians to analysts to experts on select industries in Mexico.

FEATURES- In-depth pieces on specific topics related to the economic, financial, political or social world of Mexico.

INDUSTRY FOCUS- A profile of an industry in Mexico, including current business activity, players and opportunities.

TRAVELER'S GUIDE- What you need to know to travel around Mexico for business, including hotel and restaurant reviews.

RESOURCE GUIDE- A directory of companies and events of relevance to executives active in the Nafta marketplace.

HASTA LUEGO- A different dimension on living, traveling and doing business in Mexico.
