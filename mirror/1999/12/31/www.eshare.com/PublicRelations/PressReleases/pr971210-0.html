AT&T Worldnet Service Chooses eShare Expressions' Core Technology

Largest Direct Internet Service in the United States Provides Real-Time Interaction and Community with eShare Technologies

COMMACK, NY — December 10, 1997 — eShare Technologies, Inc., a leading provider of software technology and tools for online community building, announced today that AT&T WorldNet Service is offering eShare Technologies' core interactive and community technology to its customers. eShare Expressions will first be available to users on the AT&T WorldNet Beta Site page on December 15, 1997.

The addition of eShare Technologies' real-time interaction and community software will provide AT&T WorldNet Service members with the ability to create and build personalized communities of interest through embedded chat and threaded discussion forums.

"Our customers have told us they are eager for tools that will enable them to create communities of interest on the Internet," said Thomas J. Shoemaker, Vice President of Product Marketing and Management at AT&T WorldNet Service. "We're now extending community-building capabilities through eShare Technologies, which enables AT&T WorldNet Service subscribers to build their own interactive communities on their own personal Web pages."

"Adding real-time interaction will definitely enhance the overall user experience for AT&T WorldNet Service customers," said James Tito, president and co-founder of eShare Technologies. "AT&T WorldNet Service is a powerful addition to the current line-up of industry-leading customers using eShare Technologies to deliver more value to their customers."

eShare Technologies, Inc. (www.eshare.com) delivers total business solutions for building real-time interaction and community on Web sites, extranets and intranets. With advanced technology, tools and services to meet the growing demand for online community building products and solutions, eShare Technologies empowers companies to offer a wide range of real-time services to customers, suppliers, employees and users. Current eShare Technologies customers include Computer Associates, Geocities, Lycos, Merrill Lynch, UUNet and 1-800-FLOWERS.

AT&T WorldNet Service software for Windows 95, Windows 3.1, Windows 3.11 for Workgroups and Macintosh is available by download free of charge from the public AT&T WorldNet Web site (http://www.att.net) and from the AT&T Corporate Web site (http://www.att.com) or by calling 1-800-WORLDNET. AT&T WorldNet Service was selected as the top Internet service provider by Smart Money (5/97) magazine and PCWorld (7/97), with both publications citing its outstanding reliability.
