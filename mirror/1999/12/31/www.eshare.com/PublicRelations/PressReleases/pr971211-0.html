eShare Technologies Announces Partnership with BSDI

BSDI to License eShare Expressions Core Technology to Deliver Chat Hosting Solutions to BSDI's ISP and Reseller Customer Base

COMMACK, NY — December 11, 1997 — eShare Technologies, Inc., a leading provider of software technology and tools for online community building, announced today an agreement with Berkeley Software Design, Inc. that enables BSDI to resell eShare Expressions core technology to their installed base of 6,500 resellers and ISPs.

The addition of eShare Technologies' real-time interaction and community software will provide BSDI ISPs and resellers with the opportunity to enhance their service offering to their customers. eShare Expressions for BSDI will begin shipping on February 1, 1998.

"eShare Technologies clearly has the right technology at the right time," said Mark Trimue, vice president of marketing at BSDI. "It's powerful chat and discussion forum features deliver an across-the-board solution for our customers and in turn, their customers."

"Adding real-time interaction definitely creates more opportunities for ISPs and resellers to add value to their services," said James Tito, president and co-founder of eShare Technologies. He continued, "And partnering with BSDI is exciting because it enables us to expand our platform offering."

eShare Technologies, Inc. (www.eshare.com) delivers total business solutions for building real-time interaction and community on Web sites, extranets and intranets. With advanced technology, tools and services to meet the growing demand for online community building products and solutions, eShare Technologies empowers companies to offer a wide range of real-time services to customers, suppliers, employees and users. Current eShare Technologies customers include Computer Associates, Geocities, Lycos, Merrill Lynch, UUNet and 1-800-FLOWERS.

Berkeley Software Design, Inc. (www.bsdi.com) is the commercial supplier of the high-performance BSD Internet and networking software originally developed at the University of California, Berkeley. BSDI is privately held and headquartered in Colorado Springs, Colorado.
