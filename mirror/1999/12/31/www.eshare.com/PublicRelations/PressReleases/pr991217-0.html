OneSoft Selects eShare Technologies' Expressions Solution to Enhance Community Offering

MCLEAN, Va. -- (December, 17, 1999) - OneSoft Corporation, a leading provider of Internet commerce solutions, today announced that it has entered into an agreement with eShare Technologies (NASDAQ: ESHR) to incorporate eShare's Expressions™ as part of its OneCommerce™ product offering. eShare, a leading provider of web and telephony customer communication solutions, will provide OneSoft with its award-winning, turnkey solution for adding chat, threaded discussion forums and online presentations to web sites.

OneSoft will incorporate eShare's Expressions into its OneCommerce solution to further enhance its community-building capabilities. OneCommerce is OneSoft's award-winning Internet commerce software, offering a future-ready solution that delivers the extensibility needed to quickly respond to market and technology changes. OneCommerce delivers a seamless combination of content, commerce, and community, enabling companies to deliver valuable and compelling customer experiences resulting in increased customer conversion rates and larger numbers of repeat buyers.

"OneSoft is committed to help our clients achieve success by building customer loyalty, and we recognize the importance of customer interaction," said Jim MacIntyre, chairman and CEO, Onesoft Corporation. "By joining forces with eShare, we will be offering our clients industry-leading customer interaction capabilities, enabling them to build loyal communities of buyers."

"Internet commerce is a strategic resource and competitive asset," said Neil Toomb, executive vice president of business development, eShare Technologies. "OneSoft is the Company choice for a growing number of large- and mid-sized enterprises around the world. In bundling Expressions into their product offering, OneSoft will have state-of-the-art applications which include virtual meetings, live training and conferencing, distance learning, moderated events and social chat."

OneCommerce is the first 100% XML-enabled and component-based complete Internet commerce solution available in the industry. Its extensible patent-pending architecture enables functionality to be flexibly added to the solution. OneCommerce's extensibility enables easy integration with eShare Expressions through open interfaces to deliver robust community building capabilities that will allow companies to track and target community interactions. The combination of OneCommerce and Expressions is taking on-line customer interaction to a new level, enabling companies to create, catalog, target, and track community capabilities including chat, conferences, and bulletin boards.

"The eShare open interfaces and platform commonality made eShare Expressions the logical choice for the OneCommerce solution," said Eric Waller, vice president of development, OneSoft Corporation. "OneCommerce clients will benefit from a tight integration of two industry-leading offerings by delivering the ability to build communities though customer interaction."
