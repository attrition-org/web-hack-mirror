UUNET Adds Discussion and Chat Capabilities to Hosting Services

Service Based on eShare Expressions

FAIRFAX, Va., COMMACK, N.Y., June 17, 1997 — UUNET Technologies Inc., the world's largest Internet service provider and a subsidiary of WorldCom Inc., in conjunction with eShare Technologies Inc., the leader in live online communications, announced today that UUNET will offer its customers the ability to host electronic chat and threaded discussion group forums. The addition of the eShare Expressions' chat server and threaded bulletin board system continues to expand UUNET's comprehensive set of web hosting solutions.

"Discussion and chat forums are increasingly being used as business applications on Web sites, Intranets and Extranets," said Dave Foster, general manager of UUNET's Web Hosting Business. "We believe our customers want to build tighter links with their clientele and other business partners. eShare Expressions provides us with a robust platform to offer this solution."

The capabilities provided by this software enable companies to improve communication, service and support among suppliers, distributors, customers and employees. Specifically, Web chat improves several business communication functions including: customer service/call center inquiries; integration of geographically disparate groups of people into working teams and meetings; and real-time exchanges of information among customers and/or employees.

eShare's Expressions software allows UUNET's web hosting customers to add real-time communications capabilities to their web site, providing access to live conversations in "chat rooms" set up by the hosting site. eShare Expressions can be installed in minutes and users require no installs or plug-ins.

The eShare Expressions chat client operates behind most firewalls--a critical feature for corporate acceptance. Additionally, the chat component of the software supports in-line banner advertising so users can run ads in their chat rooms or put up corporate information schedules or other messages.

The software's bulletin board service gives UUNET's hosting customers threaded discussion group capabilities, allowing users to participate in discussions on topics in a format where messages can be exchanged without the need to have participants on-line at the same time. UUNET's hosting customers can remotely administer all functions of their eShare Expressions service, allowing them to moderate and customize their chat and discussion group forums.

"We believe the power of our software joined with the reach and the depth of UUNET's offerings deliver Internet users a high degree of usability and function," said James Tito, CEO, eShare Technologies. "Live, text-based chat will fulfill a number of group communication needs in the Internet, Extranet and corporate Intranet marketplace. Our products have been created to meet those needs with a focus on ease of use and affordability."

UUNET's web hosting services allow companies to outsource the operation of their web sites, yielding performance and reliability levels that can be difficult and expensive to achieve in-house. UUNET's Web hosting services reduce the complexity and cost of implementing and supporting a high-performance web site. It is a comprehensive solution including hardware, software, high-speed Internet connectivity, ongoing management support and reporting. UUNET's computer facilities are directly connected to UUNET's Internet backbone. UUNET's web hosting customers include USA Networks, Marriott Corp., and The Motley Fool.

Pricing and Availability
The eShare Expressions hosting service is now available for extended beta to all UUNET web hosting customers. Pricing starts at $125 per month for chat forums with 25 simultaneous participants.
