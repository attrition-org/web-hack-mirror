eShare Captures More Than 100 New Customers in Response to its iChat Transition Program

Special Offer to Abandoned iChat Customers Results in Key Customer Wins for eShare

Commack, NY — Feb. 22, 1999 — eShare Technologies, the expert in human interaction on the Internet, today announced that it is signing on more than 100 new customers since the company announced its transition program for abandoned customers of Acuity's iChat products just over a month ago. eShare offered the transition in response to Acuity's drastic move to discontinue its iChat product. Last week Acuity announced the sale of its iChat product line to KOZ.com, an organization with a local community-building focus, further weakening support for the iChat customer. eShare's program has saved many iChat customers from potential lost revenue and operation time due to the lack of support from their software vendor. Because of this program, former iChat customers now have the assurance that they will be able to maintain their chat applications in the future.

"eShare's expertise in the chat marketplace and our continued dedication to our customer base has enabled us to sign former iChat customers," said Jim Tito, founder, chairman and CEO of eShare Technologies. "As a pioneer in the chat market, we are prepared to offer continued support and services to our customers. eShare is dedicated to our Expressions customers and we believe in the future of chat. We see chat as a key core technology and are leveraging it as a component of our customer relationship management products."

"Major League Soccer is pleased to offer chat software that does not require any software downloads or other obstacles in order for users to access our chat room at MLSNET.com," said Jeff Diecks, Web site manager for Major League Soccer. "Software such as eShare's Expressions helps us provide an entertaining Web site that is easy for MLS fans to use."

eShare Expressions can be utilized for virtual meetings, live training and conferencing, distance learning, moderated events and social chat. The product line features four key components Chat, a server-based software solution for adding live real time interaction; Forums, a conferencing tool; Web Tours which provides the ability to lead a group on a tour from page to page and site to site; Moderator, for hosting and managing live discussion and Integrator Pro which integrates the software with existing databases.
