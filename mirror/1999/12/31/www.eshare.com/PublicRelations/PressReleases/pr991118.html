nutrisystem.com Selects eShare Technologies to Provide Online Support for Web Visitors

ATLANTA, GA - (November 18,1999) -- eShare Technologies (NASDAQ: ESHR), the leading provider of unified web and telephony customer communication solutions, announced today that nutrisystem.com (OTC Bulletin Board: NSCI) has selected eShare's NetAgent solution to provide live customer service to nutrisystem.com's web visitors.

By implementing eShare's NetAgent, the industry's first Internet customer contact solution that allows eBusinesses to communicate with multiple web visitors via e-mail or live interaction, nutrisystem.com is supporting its objective of becoming the leading e-commerce provider of weight loss services and products. On the nutrisystem.com online weight loss program, clients can lose weight in the comfort of their own home by using the effective NutriSystem meal plan and participating in the exclusive online counseling sessions.

Brian D. Haveson, president and chief executive officer, nutrisystem.com, stated, "eShare's NetAgent solution is an invaluable part of our web site, since the convenience and privacy of nutrisystem.com are important factors to everyone interested in weight loss and on-going weight control. NetAgent allows our web visitors to have private online discussions with our experts, all from the convenience of their home computers."

"eShare is excited to be a large part of nutrisystem.com's e-commerce strategy," said James Tito, vice chairman and president, eShare Technologies. "By selecting NetAgent as their online customer service tool of choice, nutrisystem.com has validated our philosophy that real-time, live interaction with customer service agents is key to becoming a leading eBusiness on the Internet."
