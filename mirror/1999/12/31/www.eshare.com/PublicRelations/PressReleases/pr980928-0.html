Sage Networks and eShare Technologies enter into strategic alliance to offer best-of-class Hosted Interactive Communications

Cambridge, MA, and Commack, NY, September 28, 1998 — Sage Networks, Inc., a company focused on becoming the industry benchmark in Web hosting today announced a strategic partnership with eShare Technologies, a leading developer of powerful enterprise-class interaction software and services for the Internet. Under the terms of the agreement, eShare will use Sage's state-of-the-art hosting facilities to provide its customers with a new high-performance chat hosting product, and Sage will market eShare's Expressions Interaction Suite and NetAgent™ products as hosted solutions to its customers and reseller community.

Sage will host eShare's new hosted chat offering that utilizes its leading product, Expressions. eShare's Expressions interaction software transforms users' sites into thriving communities with dynamic chat, discussion forums, and Web tours for meetings, conferences, guest speaker events, distance learning and training. NetAgent Customer Interaction Center allows businesses to assist customers quickly and efficiently over the Internet. Sage's world-class data center and eceptional customer care delivers a high quality hosted chat solution, currently available through eShare (www.eshare.com).

"We're delighted with Sage's innovative hosting strategy and our partnership to provide hosted chat solutions," stated Jim Tito, President and CEO of eShare, "Our hosted chat solution will play well with Sage's large reseller and web development community."

Rajat Bhargava, Chief Operating Officer of Sage Networks, added, "We are excited about working with eShare, a leader in the enterprise chat market. Sage's hosting services will provide a reliable hosting environment, while eShare will offer stable, scalable web-based interactive products to our customers and developer community."

About Sage Networks
Sage Networks was founded in 1997 with the goal of becoming the industry benchmark in Web hosting. Sage's strategy is to capitalize on the explosive Web hosting market opportunity by growing its business through acquisition. Backed by a seasoned management team with extensive experience in Internet technology and growth through acquisition strategy, Sage is also differentiated by delivering exceptional customer care and building a reliable, high-performance network. Sage is able to offer access to a broader range of hosting solutions and thereby extends the value customers realize from their corporate Web sites by enabling them to migrate from static sites, to interactive sites, to transactional sites, which incorporate electronic commerce. Sage offers its Web hosting solutions for Internet business applications through a growing worldwide network of resellers.

Sage Networks is headquartered in Cambridge, MA and funded by New York City-based Charterhouse Group International, Inc., a leading private equity firm that recently closed an investment fund totaling $1 billion. For more information about Sage Networks, please visit the Sage Networks' Web site (www.sagenetworks.com).

About eShare
eShare Technologies, Inc. (www.eshare.com) delivers powerful enterprise-class interaction software and services that are crucial for eBusiness. These innovative technologies include web-based customer service and support, customer self-service, live conferencing and events, Community chat and threaded discussion forums, and custom integration tools. Current eShare Technologies clients include AT&T WorldNet Service, Computer Associates NetHaven, GeoCities, Lycos, networkMCI, UUNet, the University of Florida, Pfizer, Bertelsmann, Columbia University, Hewlett Packard, Xerox and 1-800-FLOWERS.
