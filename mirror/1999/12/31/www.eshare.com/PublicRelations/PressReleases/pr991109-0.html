ClientLogic Corporation, a leading provider of infrastructure solutions to technology and Internet companies, announced today a partnership with eShare Technologies

NASHVILLE, Tenn. -- (Nov 9, 1999) -- ClientLogic Corporation, a leading provider of infrastructure solutions to technology and Internet companies, announced today a partnership with eShare Technologies (NASDAQ: ESHR), a total-solution provider of unified Web and telephony customer communication solutions for customer-contact centers, e-commerce and online communities.

ClientLogic leverages eShare's NetAgent(TM) software, the first integrated Web customer interaction solution that combines e-mail management with live interaction for eBusinesses, in support of ClientLogic's Internet customer-care solutions.

"NetAgent enhances our existing Internet customer-care solutions, allowing ClientLogic to better manage e-mail traffic and engage in instant dialogs with visitors to clients' Web sites," said Bob Fetter, chief marketing officer at ClientLogic. "ClientLogic associates will manage customer e-mail histories, push Web pages and files, and conduct real-time text chat. In addition, ClientLogic will provide customer self-service tools and automated-response services to answer common e-care questions on behalf of clients."

ClientLogic incorporates NetAgent into its existing Primus(R) knowledge-base and Vantive customer relationship management (CRM) applications, adding significant value for its clients. Primus, a leader in Web-based problem resolution and knowledge-management software, provides solutions that enable businesses to deliver superior customer service and support to maintain a competitive advantage in the e-business marketplace. ClientLogic uses Primus software and services to create, capture, reuse and share up-to-the-minute knowledge that can be easily accessed to solve problems, answer questions or find options for customers on the spot. ClientLogic's tentative timeline for full implementation of the NetAgent e-care tool is slated for the end of the year.
