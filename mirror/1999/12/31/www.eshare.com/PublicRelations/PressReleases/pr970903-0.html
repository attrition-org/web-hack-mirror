eShare Technologies Enters New Strategic Partnership

CLIQNOW! To Distribute eShare Expressions to Channel clients

Commack, NY (September 3) — eShare Technologies has signed a partnership agreement to provide multiple copies of eShare Expressions to CLIQNOW!, who will in turn offer enhanced live Web communication services to its customers.

CLIQNOW! (www.cliqnow.com) will distribute 50 copies of eShare Expressions to the sites in its network. Adding live discussion to Web sites has been proven to increase the amount of time people actually spend there, increasing the value of advertising that can be sold both on the site and within eShare Expressions. This results in increased revenue and enhanced customer loyalty for Web sites.

CLIQNOW! has developed its networks based on this single, powerful principle: Create an environment that joins related Web sites under important content types. Users gain access to a valuable neighborhood of the best sites covering their specific interests. With this in mind, CLIQNOW! realized that Web-based live text and threaded BBS communications services are quickly becoming a "value-added" commodity that most sites consider a necessity to maintain client relationships.

With networks including CLIQCOLLEGE!, CLIQGOLF!, CLIQFINANCIAL!, CLIQTECH!, CLIQHOME!, CLIQTRAVEL & CLIQKIDS!, the provider has created target audiences that benefit surfers and advertisers alike. CLIQNOW! will re-distribute eShare Expressions on over 50 client sites.

Scott Paternoster, Executive Vice President at CLIQNOW! remarked, "Live Web communication will add a vital element that will allow the affiliates within our networks to become more diversified, which in turn will allow our ad sales offerings to become more desirable."

He continued, "We chose eShare Expressions because of its versatility, ease of use, and the overall superiority of the product."

Interactive ad sales networks such as CLIQNOW! are using the eShare Expressions Communications Suite to provide live communications and discussion forums on their web sites. Others partnering with eShare Technologies include: FOX News, Mecklermedia's Webdeveloper.com, UUNET, Sony Music, CUC International, and MCI.

The eShare Expressions software allows companies to improve communication, service and support among suppliers, customers and employees. Web communications improves the customer service/call center inquiry process; integrates geographically disparate groups of people into working teams; and facilitates the exchange of information among customers and/or employees.

eShare Expressions can be installed in minutes and users require no plug-ins. The eShare Expressions client interface operates behind most firewalls-a critical feature for corporate requirements. In addition, the chat component of the software supports in-line banner advertising so users can run ads in their chat rooms or display corporate information schedules or other messages.

The software's bulletin board system provides threaded discussion group capabilities. Users can participate in discussions by exchanging messages at their convenience; they don't have to be on-line at the same time.
