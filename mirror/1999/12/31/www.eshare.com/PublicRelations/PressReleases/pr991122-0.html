MindSpring Enterprises Selects eShare Technologies as its Customer Contact Management Solution Provider

-- Leading Internet Service Provider Selects eShare's XChange Solution to Enhance its Customer Service Initiatives --

ATLANTA, GA -- (November 22, 1999) - eShare Technologies (NASDAQ:ESHR), a leading provider of integrated customer contact management solutions, announced today that MindSpring Enterprises, Inc. (NASDAQ: MSPG), one of the nation's leading Internet service providers (ISP), has selected eShare Technologies as its contact management solution provider to enhance communication between the company and its customers.

MindSpring will install eShare's Xchange, an interaction-driven contact management solution specifically designed to be user-friendly, enabling MindSpring's customer service representatives to proactively contact customers and provide them with the information they need in an expedited manner. The Xchange is a full-featured teleservice application based on open architecture. Features of the solution include intelligent predictive dialing, inbound call processing, call blending, unlimited scripting, transaction recording and playback, data management utilities and reports.

"MindSpring is a customer-driven company, and we select partners that can enhance our customer service operations," said MindSpring director of call center technology, Matt Brown. "eShare's XChange solution provides us with a sophisticated methodology to enhance our on-going customer loyalty and retention efforts."

Recently, MindSpring ranked highest in overall customer satisfaction among the seven largest national Internet Service Providers (ISPs), according to the JD Power and Associates 1999 National ISP Online Residential Customer Satisfaction Study(SM).

"We feel this partnership reflects the philosophy that both companies recognize; only by implementing solutions that enhance customer satisfaction and improve business operations can one help define tomorrow's standards for integrating technology and business leadership," said Aleksander Szlam, chairman and chief executive officer for eShare Technologies. "It's exciting to develop a relationship with a company whose core values are so close to those of our own."
