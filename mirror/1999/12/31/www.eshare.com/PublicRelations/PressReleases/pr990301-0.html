eShare Technologies Inc. Partners With Line4 to Provide Total CTI Business Solution

LOS ANGELES — March 1, 1999 — eShare Technologies Inc., the expert in human interaction on the Internet, today announced at CTExpo, a strategic partnership with Line4, a pioneer in the field of computer telephony integration (CTI). As a result of the partnership, eShare will integrate its NetAgent™ customer interaction solution with Line4's LiveTouch product which links a firm's World Wide Web site to its call center, applications and databases. The combined technologies enable companies to integrate all of their information resources to produce the maximum efficiency from their online businesses.

Line4's LiveTouch solution enables customers on the Web to initiate phone contact with a call center representative. It also provides companies with tools for utilizing information gathered from the Web for telemarketing, datamining and customer service purposes. With eShare's NetAgent™, customers gain real-time access to a company's customer service help desk using cutting edge human interaction technology. Through the integration of the two products, LiveTouch users will gain the benefits of real-time customer service, including increased customer satisfaction, a reduction in expensive telephone charges and the ability to handle a high volume of customer calls using just one agent.

"We are delighted to be partnering with a proven business technology leader like eShare Technologies," said Russ Blake, director of marketing for Line4. "With eShare's NetAgent™, our customer service offering is a total solution. Our users can now offer their clients the same quality of interactive assistance online as they would receive over the telephone."

About Line4
Line4 Inc., formerly Aristacom International, has been in the business of linking an enterprise's existing computer and communications resources to create more powerful business systems since 1988. A leading vendor of computer telephone integration products and services, Line4's customers include First Card, Sallie Mae Kawasaki, MBNA, Bank of America, Whirlpool, Victoria's Secret and the Internal Revenue Service. About eShare NetAgent™
eShare NetAgent™ is a customer interaction solution that delivers multiple integrated interaction technologies. eShare NetAgent™ enables users to conduct e-commerce support, send files, deliver tutorials and give pre-scripted assistance at the click of a button. With NetAgent, agents can provide personalized, real-time assistance, which allows organization to successfully conduct business on the Web.
