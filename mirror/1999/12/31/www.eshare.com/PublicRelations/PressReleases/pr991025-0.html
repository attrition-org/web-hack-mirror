eShare Technologies Partners With iWon.com, a New Destination Portal Featuring the Internet's Largest Guaranteed Cash Giveaway

-- New Portal Leverages eShare's Interactive Customer Technology to
Provide Web Visitors with Chat, Discussion Forums and More --

ATLANTA, October 25, 1999 - eShare Technologies (NASDAQ:ESHR), the leading provider of mission-critical Internet customer communications, has partnered with iWon.com (www.iwon.com), a new CBS-backed destination Internet portal combining world-class search, content and functionality with the Internet's largest guaranteed cash giveaway. Launched this month, iWon.com is giving away to its users $10,000 every day, $1 million every month and $10 million on Tax Day (April 17, 2000).

"iWon.com chose eShare to increase visitor traffic, loyalty and satisfaction, and manage online communications," said James Tito, vice chairman, eShare Technologies. "eShare provides this exciting new site with award-winning, turnkey solutions for adding chat, threaded discussion forums and online presentations. iWon.com, by utilizing eShare's technology, provides its visitors with community chat, live training and conferencing, distance learning, moderated events, and more."

"Portals are the starting point for tens of millions of consumers on the Internet, and they demand the very best," said iWon.com Co-CEO Jonas Steinman. "Through our strategic partnership with eShare, we're able to provide consumers with access to a host of services from a name they know and trust."

"iWon.com is very proud to be associated with eShare Technologies and looks forward to a long and mutually beneficial relationship," added iWon.com Co-CEO Bill Daugherty.

iWon.com was designed and developed in collaboration with Sapient Corporation, a leading e- services consultancy. The site features an innovative and patent-pending entry tracking system that appears at the top of each page and provides users with a real-time tally of the entries they have accumulated toward the Daily, Monthly, and Tax Day giveaways. The site also features search, Web directory, and shopping engine functionality from Inktomi Corporation, a leader in Internet infrastructure applications.

About iWon.com
iWon.com, based in Irvington, NY, is a CBS-backed destination Internet portal combining world- class search, content and functionality with the Internet's largest guaranteed cash giveaway. Located at www.iwon.com, iWon.com is giving away, to its users, $10,000 every day, $1 million every month and $10 million on Tax Day (April 17, 2000). Users collect entries into the Daily, Monthly and Tax Day giveaways for practically everything they do on the site - from searching the Web and reading the news to checking their emails and tracking their stocks. The more you use iWon.com, the more entries you collect and the greater your chances of winning. Participation is free.

About eShare Technologies
eShare Technologies, Inc. (www.eshare.com) delivers powerful enterprise-class interaction software and services that are crucial for eBusiness. These innovative technologies include web-based customer service and support, customer self-service, live conferencing and events, distance learning, Community chat, threaded discussion forums, and custom integration tools. Current eShare Technologies clients include AOL, AT&T WorldNet Service, Computer Associates, GeoCities, Lycos, networkMCI, UUNet, the University of Florida, Pfizer, Bertelsmann, Columbia University, Hewlett Packard, Xerox and 1-800-FLOWERS.
