Directions to eShare Technologies, Inc.

Here are Directions to eShare Technologies' Corporate Headquarters in Commack, NY. Have a safe trip!

Via Northern State Parkway
Take Northern State Parkway East to Exit 46, New Highway. Turn right at first stop sign onto Moreland Road. Make first right onto Mall Drive. Take to 51 Mall Drive (on right) and follow signs around right side of building to eShare Technologies.

Via Long Island Expressway
Eastbound:
Long Island Expressway East (495) to Exit 53, Wicks Road, make a left, cross over Motor Parkway to Moreland Road (pass Howard Johnson's on left) Take 3rd left onto Mall Drive. Take to 51 Mall Drive (on right) follow signs around right side of building to eShare Technologies.

Westbound:
Long Island Expressway West(495) to Exit 53, Wicks Road, make a left onto Motor Parkway and then a right onto Moreland Road. Take 3rd left onto Mall Drive. Take to 51 Mall Drive (on right) follow signs around right side of building to eShare Technologies.

From Airports
From JFK Airport take Belt Parkway East. Exit off Belt Parkway to Cross Island Parkway North. Take Cross Island Parkway and exit onto Long Island Expressway East (495) to Exit 53. Follow directions to Corporate Headquarters via the Long Island Expressway Eastbound, above.

From LaGuardia Airport take Grand Central Parkway Eastern Long Island. The Grand Central turns into the Northern State Parkway. (See Northern State Parkway directions above.)

From MacArthur Airport take Veterans Highway North. Exit off Veterans Highway onto the Long Island Expressway West (495) to exit 53. Follow, Directions to eShare Technologies Corporate Headquarters via the Long Island Expressway Westbound, above.
