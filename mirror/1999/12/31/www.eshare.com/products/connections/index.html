eShare Connections™ gives you the power to customize your own instant messenger and offer it to your site's community of users. Faster and less cumbersome than email, eShare Connections™ comes loaded with handy user features. Online users can search for and communicate with each other in real-time, bookmark their favorite URLs, store conversation notes - even enable sounds to alert them when friends have logged on.

A compact client rides on users' desktops and becomes active the moment they turn on their PC. This critical starting point is the ideal time to deliver ad messages using the software's built-in banner capabilities.

Build community and increase traffic

  • Enable users to instantly connect with others within your community.
  • Boost user base with a proven traffic-building tool.
  • Provide users with an incentive to return to your site.
Encourage users to stay loyal to your community
  • Stimulate interaction at your site to increase membership.
  • Recapture users lost to 3rd party instant messengers.
  • "Persistent" messenger lets you put your ads in front of users first.
Empower users with a must-have "sticky" tool
  • Users can send instant messages to family, friends and business associates.
  • Powerful search feature lets users locate contacts quickly.
  • "Peer" alert feature alerts users when friends are online.
Feature-rich and easy-to-use
  • Compact client rides on user's desktop.
  • Works through HTTP firewalls
  • Users may share files and create a library of favorite URLs
