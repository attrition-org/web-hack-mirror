How to Configure Outlook Express to Use a HotMail Account

 The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Outlook Express version 5 for Windows 98
  • Microsoft Outlook Express version 5 for Windows 95

MORE INFORMATION

To configure Outlook Express to use a HotMail account:

  1. If you already have a HotMail account, skip to step 3. If you do not have a HotMail account, start Outlook Express, point to New Account Signup on the Tools menu, and then click HotMail.
    2.  
  2. Follow the instructions on the screen to finish setting up your HotMail account.
    3.  
  3. On the Tools menu, click Accounts, click Add, and then click Mail.
    4.  
  4. Type your name, and then click Next.
    5.  
  5. Click I already have an e-mail address that I'd like to use, type the e-mail address of your HotMail account, for example, myname@hotmail.com, where myname is your HotMail user name, and then click Next.
    6.  
  6. In the My incoming mail server is box, click HTTP.
    7.  
  7. In the My http mail service provider is box, click HotMail, and then click Next.
    8.  
  8. In the Account name box, type your HotMail account name, in the Password box, type your HotMail password, and then click Next.

  9. Click Finish.

Hotmail server URL:

http://services.msn.com/svcs/hotmail/httpmail.asp
