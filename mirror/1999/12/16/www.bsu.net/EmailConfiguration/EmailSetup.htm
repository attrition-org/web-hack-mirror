Having problems with your e-mail configuration? Please first check to make sure you have these basic e-mail settings in place. Then if you still have a problem, you will find specific instructions below for all of the most popular e-mail clients.

Basic E-mail Setting Rules:

Outgoing mail(SMTP) Server: mail.bsu.net
Incoming mail(POP3) Server: mail.bsu.net
E-mail address: username@bsu.net         or  username@mail.bsu.net
Username: Unique ID used in BSU.NET
Password: Password to access BSU.NET
Maximum File Attachment Size:
2.0 MB
Individual E-mail Box Size: 10 MB
