We at BSU.NET have recently installed a new mail server.  The new server has many more capabilities then did our previous version of e-mail.  You now have a number of options when it comes to sending and receiving e-mail.

  •     Check your mail via the world wide web!  You can check your mail on the go by simply going to any web browser on any machine anywhere in the country.   How?  You ask?  Using either Netscape or Internet Explorer,  you can simply go to www.bsu.net and click on the Check Your Email (left side of the screen).  Follow the links to check your e-mail.  To get a peek at what the web screens will look like once you open to the page click on the thumbnails below.

                                                                       

                Logon Screen                                        Opening Screen

  •    Administer your own e-mail account via the web! You can change your personal information, set vacation messages and other tasks with the administration option.  You have essentially the same functions as you do with the web messaging on this portion.  You can however view the status of the mail server from this screen as well. Have a peak at those screens by clicking on the thumbs below.

                                                                    

                    Logon Screen                                   Opening Screen

  • Use 2 different types of e-mail clients!  You may not have heard of POP3 or IMAP4 but you have heard of Eudora Light or Netscape Communicator.   Eudora Light is a POP3 client.  With POP3 you log into the mail server and download your e-mail to your local PC and then manage it from there.  With Netscape Communicator you can use either POP3 or what is known as  an IMAP4 client.  IMAP4 gives you the capability to manage your e-mail while it is still on the server.  You do not need to download your e-mail in order to place it in folders.  You can create e-mail folders on the server!  To find out more about IMAP4 and POP3 click on the links below!  You can download Eudora Light or Netscape Communicator by clicking on the highlighted words above.

  • You do not need to make any changes to your existing setup!  Your current settings are still good.  There is no need to make any changes to your existing e-mail settings.  You of course have more options and can only enhance what you already have.
