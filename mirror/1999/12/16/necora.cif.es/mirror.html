From Birth till Death, A person's Manifesto

      Literally forced by a society unequivocally obsessed with efficiency we rise and scurry through the sub-terrainian tunnels and wheels created by our makers, predestined to endlessly strive towards the illusion of happiness in the commercial promised land.

           Discontent and unfulfilled the sun ascends and with a “new” day it brings another artificial cycle of momentary self-gratification at the mercy of our supposed peers. With no end in sight we fuel our product addicted egos until the purchase of our final resting place.

           From the instant of conception we are trained to work towards a comfortable life, and to what avail? A comfortable death.

           There is a buffer in your head where your speech is halted and stored temporarily only to be cut up and censored before being spat out as acceptable, conditioned, "civilized" conversation. Words that are descent, sensible, meaningless.

           There is a buffer in your head where your actions are halted and stored temporarily only to be cut up and censored by the heavy hand of the founding books and the swift strikes of the blinded female that wields her blade and quotes these books. Actions that are "thought-out", restricted, meaningless.

          There is a buffer in your head where your thoughts are halted and so utterly polluted that the limitations of the forms of expression imposed upon me cannot demonstrate the level.

        The world I live in is one of absolute freedom. A world where we are not governed by a violent partnership of oppression but by mutual respect and endless compassion. There are no bubbly tar coloured beverages or cow upholstered status symbols. You don't have to be silent, you don't have to be scared. The barrier between your mind, itself, your body, and the world is struck down by the willingness of others to listen and actually hear. The flood gates of expression are flung wide open.
