Welcome to the Laredo Independent School District's Web Site.  We are located in Laredo, Texas right on the Texas-Mexico Border.  We have three High Schools, one Magnet School, four Middle Schools, twenty-one Elementaries, and one Alternative School scattered throughout Central-South-West Laredo.  We currently have 24,000 students enrolled in the District.

Please feel free to browse and visit often  as we are continuously updating and revising our site.

The Mission of the Laredo Independent School District is to provide a quality education to the students of LISD.   The students and teachers are the District's important assets, and the District is committed towards the enhancement of academic achievement, the provision of a safe environment, and the   search for excellence at every level of the District.
