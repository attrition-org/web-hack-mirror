HITACHI MULTISTANDARD TVs.

Please note that you must have a Multistandard TV as well as a Multistandard VCR in order to view all standards of tapes (PAL-SECAM-NTSC).   If you need to convert from one standard to another you need a Multistandards Converter or a Converter/VCR Combo.  For more information on video conversions please see the Converters page.

All the multistandard electronics we sell (TVs and VCRs) are Auto-Voltage/Auto-Hertz (90-260 Volts, 50-60 Hertz), no switching or transformer needed. These are perfect for use with the electricity of every country on earth though in some cases the plug that fits in the wall may need an inexpensive adapter to simply change the plug pin shape, US$2.99.  In case the antenna COAX plug is the wrong fitting shape a small adapter for that costs US$2.99.  In the US any RADIO SHACK has these.  Ask for EURO-TO-F-TYPE adapter.
