Wood Furniture

 

Bell'Oggetti, a leader in innovative European home theater designs emerges with a new look - the natural beauty and warmth of solid wood. Utilizing its renowned cable management system, cords and cables blend into the clean lines of classically styled furniture.

 

Bell'Oggetti ® - the name means "beautiful objects" - offers the finest in furnishings for the home audio/video system. Unique, contemporary, innovative, Bell'Oggetti ® furniture is the crowning touch for any premium-quality component, and a complement to virtually any decor.

The wood series from Bell'Oggetti ® offers unsurpassed quality and design and they perfectly integrate your television and audio and video components in your modern environment.  As with the entire collection of audio and video furniture,  Bell'Oggetti solid wood furniture line is made only from select material - in the wood furniture's case, of top-choice, hand-selected wood obtained only from the world's best suppliers.  The beauty of this wood does not fully transpire on the below images or other images on our web site.   With the patented Cable Management System, unsightly wires and cables will be out of view yet may be easily accessed in case of necessity.  If you have any questions regarding our Bell'Oggetti furniture or need assistance selecting the proper audio/video furniture system or television stand or would like to order, please call or e-mail us.
