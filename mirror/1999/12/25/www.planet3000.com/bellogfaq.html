Frequently Asked Questions

1. Where can I purchase Bell'Oggetti® products?

Bell'Oggetti® products are available for purchase, and can be seen at leading retailers throughout the United States and Canada. Bell'Oggetti® is also available in Australia, Bermuda, The Bahamas, Japan, Israel, Italy, Germany, Greece, Norway, Oman, Panama, The Philippines, Puerto Rico, Russia, Thailand, and Venezuela.   However, if you don't want to pay retailer prices then you should purchase Bell'Oggetti® products from Planet 3000.

2. Can I purchase direct from Bell'Oggetti®?

Bell'Oggetti®'s products are not available only through the manufacturer.

3. Can any model be custom fit to my specifications?

Bell'Oggetti® products are made at our Italian manufacturing facilities, and designed to fit most combinations of TV/Audio/Home Theatre, and Home Office requirements. Contact your Bell'Oggetti® dealer for assistance in matching Bell'Oggetti® component furniture to your equipment needs. Bell'Oggetti® products are not available built to individual specifications.

4. What Is the pricing for Bell'Oggetti® furniture?

Bell'Oggetti® products are available at retailers in a price range starting at SRP's of $249.95 and higher. Your retailer will be pleased to quote you competitive pricing to meet your requirements.  Through Planet 3000 you will get extremely competitive pricing.

5. What Is CMS®?

CMS® "Cable Management System"™ is a Bell'Oggetti® exclusive design element which hides unsightly wiring so your equipment is handsomely shown and enhances your home décor. The photographs of installed products on our web site are real products, and as you can see, show no unsightly wires to take away from home décor.

6. What tools do I need to assemble Bell'Oggetti®?

All Bell'Oggetti® products include complete assembly instructions, and include all fasteners and tools needed. Average do-it-yourself skill is all that is required.

7. If I move, or want to change the location of my equipment, can I disassemble and reassemble Bell'Oggetti® without buying new parts?

Since Bell'Oggetti® structures are made of steel they can be easily disassembled and reassembled with no damage. Unlike much composition or traditional "KD" wood furniture, Bell'Oggetti® can be disassembled and reassembled countless times without suffering any damage, or weakening of its structure. Parts replacements are available from your dealer, in the unlikely event of loss or damage.

8. What type of glass shelving is used in Bell'Oggetti®?

Bell'Oggetti® glass shelves are made of tempered safety glass, equal in quality and strength, to the finest museum showcase standards. The standard finish includes polished and beveled edges with rounded corners to provide the utmost in décor and safety.

10. How much weight will the top and the shelves hold?

The top shelf will safely hold the specified TV's as shown in the chart above. Middle shelving will safely hold your DVD, VCR, Compact Disk Player, or similar electronics. Middle shelving is limited to fifty(50) lbs. per shelf.

11. Are the Shelves adjustable?

Depending on the model, shelves are adjustable. Please see specific model specifications for the model of your choice.

12. Can Bell'Oggetti® be made in a different color or custom painted.

Bell'Oggetti® is only available in standard factory finishes It can be painted over by you to match your décor with acrylic paint, however, since Bell'Oggetti® is a powder coating factory baked surface material, we cannot recommend any recoloring or the quality of any new non-factory applied finish.

13. Why should I buy Bell'Oggetti®?

Join the thousands of satisfied Bell'Oggetti® owners worldwide who chose Bell'Oggetti® for their Home Entertainment and Home Office décor furniture. Bell'Oggetti® is very affordable since you can add to your initial purchase to accommodate the growth or expansion of your equipment. The color and design remain consistent and you are not faced with obsolesce as you add components to your entertainment or home office location. Easily moved due to its' modular design and "KD" disassembly and reassembly, Bell'Oggetti® uniquely combines the strength of steel with tempered glass in exclusive designs that are timeless and enhance your home décor.
