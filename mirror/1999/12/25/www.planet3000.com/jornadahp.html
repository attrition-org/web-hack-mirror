Hewlett-Packard Jornada 820 Palmtop Computers and Accessories.


The Hewlett-Packard Jornada is one of the few mobile devices truly designed to meet the needs of the busy mobile professional. Weighing in at just 2.5 pounds (1.1 kg), you can slip the HP Jornada into a briefcase or purse and carry it with you anywhere.

The HP Jornada powers up instantly for on-the-spot access to your information. Its high-performance 190MHz StrongArm RISC processor manages power so efficiently you can use it up to 10 hours without recharging. Or pop in the optional extended battery for up to 15 hours of uninterrupted use.

The Hewlett-Packard Jornada comes with dial-up software and a built-in 56Kbps modem, and, Microsoft Pocket Inbox, Microsoft Pocket Internet Explorer, and bFAX Pro allow you to easily access e-mail, surf the Web, or send faxes without the hassle of carrying an external modem. The HP Jornada comes complete with familiar pocket versions of Microsoft Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Access, and Outlook. One-step connectivity software and included sync cable let you easily synchronize e-mail, documents, and personal information with your desktop PC. You can do all this on the HP Jornada - and more.

The Hewlett-Packard Jornada is the perfect tool for a successful meeting. With its wide, full-color screen, touch-typeable keyboard, and integrated touch pad, it's completely comfortable for taking notes and accessing information. Plus, the handy built-in VGA-Out port allows you to deliver presentations using any standard monitor or projector.
