What Multi Standard Converter do you need?

 

First of all, what is the difference between a MULTI STANDARD VCR and a STANDARDS CONVERTER? What do you need to do what you want?

A Multi Standard VCR will play PAL/SECAM/NTSC tapes ONLY on a TV of the VHS tape standard you have, therefore requiring the purchase of a TV as well as a Multi Standard VCR.
A Standards Converter allows you to PLAY any standard tape on any standard TV and also, to convert the standard of the original tape allowing a copy to be made in a NEW TV standard. These are either combination VCR/Converters or Stand-Alone Converters. All of ours are Digital and are TRUE Converters. We don't sell "transcodifiers" or analog converters and we strongly advise against them.
[Please don't be fooled by pseudo-converters! Ask for our FAQ file explaining the TRUTH about this before buying from anyone.]
This means you can watch any TV STANDARD VHS tape in the world (PAL/SECAM/NTSC) on any TV in the world, whether PAL, SECAM or NTSC TV or make an actual copy of any standard tape to any other standard, literally altering the electronic information and send it to a friend for him to watch on his own local TV.
