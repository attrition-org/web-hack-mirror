Planet 3000 is proud to present to you our fine line of Italian design audio/video, office and home furnishings.


Bell'Oggetti ® - the name means "beautiful objects" - offers the finest in furnishings for the home audio/video system. Unique, contemporary, innovative, Bell'Oggetti ® furniture is the crowning touch for any premium-quality component, and a complement to virtually any decor. Showing a distinct flair of Italian design, Bell'Oggetti ® offers a full line of home-entertainment furnishings, centering around furniture for large-screen televisions and extending through speaker stands, audio racks, and complete entertainment centers.   This exclusive line of furniture compliments to a perfection Planet 3000's wide range of televisions, VCRs and audio equipment.

Bell'Oggetti ® also features a cable management system (CMS) for securely hiding power cords and interconnect cables.
