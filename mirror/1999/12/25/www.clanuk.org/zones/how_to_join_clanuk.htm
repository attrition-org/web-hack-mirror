Instructions for joining ClanUK via MMG

1.  First go here!

- A Full Member asks someone if they would like to join ClanUK. (or they ask A Full Member and A Full Member thinks about inviting them)

- A Full Member  has gamed with them and know they are honorable and adhere to the Charter

- A Full Member introduces them to another full member, and the other full member evaluates them also

- A Full Member sends in ALL of their information, i.e. email address, icq number, full name, game name, games they play etc.. to BRUTUS the trial membership person. A Full Member has just made this person a trial member, effective now.

- The PNM is now a trial member of ClanUK

(extra advice)
- The Trial Member coordinator should send these details to the GM or Head Person in charge of that game. (So all the people know what is going on and can arrange for the below)

-----------------------------

Now to join the relative Clan Team in whatever zone the PNM wishes to play, the PNM and Full member approach the GM or Head Honcho to get on the guild stone, get the password to the server, or whatever.

The GM or Head Honcho may have additional requirements/rules to understand to play this game with the fellow Clan mates. Example, sharing the loot, don't shoot me, team rules.

--------------------------------
If you need help, here is the Contact list of ClanUK persons.
Make sure you read all the  www.clanuk.com Info & Help and you will understand the process.
