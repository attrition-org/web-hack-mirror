Lets go to Leicester! 
(and get rather tipsy)

Well apart from floods and a large godzilla type thing rampaging across the hotels Im trying to contact buuuuut anyway......

Then we play games, swop warez errr I didn't say that!, make many backup copies of various games and gawp at my immense mp3 collection :o) So then we get to the evening and we go and get fed, I should be able to find some sort of restaurant for this in the meantime though we could always eat at the hotel. Now comes the drinking and debauchery!!! (woo hoo!)

Anyway there's allot of places that we can go to and Im sure we can ask the hotels for a VERY VERY late bar :o)

Lets just say this is the morning where you wake up and wander why you feel like the phantom of the opera before realising that one side of your face is resting on a painful corner of the door handle outside someone elses room, or somet like that!!!. So then we all say our goodbyes wandering how much w'eve all spent and go home only to jump onto ICQ and talk about what we got up to!!!
