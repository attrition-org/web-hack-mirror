Trade and investment has always been an important part of the UK-Australia relationship. Overall, the United Kingdom is Australia’s third-largest trading partner. British investment, valued at $A111.6 billion at June 1997, ranges across manufacturing, financial services, resources and many other sectors. The UK is the second-largest destination for Australian foreign investment - $A27.1 billion in 1995-96
