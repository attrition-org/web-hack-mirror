Many people, especially parents and educators, are concerned about inappropriate material on the Internet being accessible to young people. All links available here have been checked to ensure that their content is suitable for children. We hope you find them useful and appropriate.

The nature of World Wide Web is such that the British High Commission cannot guarantee that a user of the BISA site will not be able to access inappropriate material despite our rigorous efforts to minimise that risk. To alleviate the problem, we suggest that parents and educators install commercially available Internet filters.

These links are provided for your convenience. This listing does not indicate any recommendation or endorsement.

The British High Commission hopes that these links provide a valuable resource for educators and help you understand a lot more about modern Britain. You will find many links listed more than once, e.g. you'll find the English Civil War Society under both education and history.
