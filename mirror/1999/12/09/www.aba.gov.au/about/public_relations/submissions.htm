ABA public relations
submissions

  One of the ABA’s corporate objectives is to promote the Authority’s established role as a principal source of expert advice and information on broadcasting and related issues. In this context, the ABA contributes to public inquiries by making submissions on issues that may have an impact on the Authority’s regulatory functions.

A list of submissions that the ABA has made in 1999 is provided below.

Public Consultations on Australia’s Approach to Further Multilateral Trade Negotiations, Department of Foreign Affairs and Trade, May 1999.
