Materials to Help You Order Form

"Now that I have information about my disease, I am not so filled with fear and despair." --Chantal, Illinois

Information is truly one of the most potent weapons a woman can carry. That's why the Association is pleased and proud to offer members the materials in this catalog--books, tapes, newsletters, and other resources, all based on the experiences of women with the disease and our research. Nowhere else can you find the accurate, highly informative literature about endometriosis that we offer here.  But where do you begin?

Hints for choosing the materials that will be most valuable to you:

Too much to choose from? Start with our books, The Endometriosis Sourcebook and Overcoming Endometriosis.  After you've absorbed those, turn to our collections, special articles, and newsletters.

Looking for a good doctor? Order a contact/registry list from the order form and call other members in your area for background on local physicians.  You can also contact the nearest Association chapter or support group for information on physicians and on group meetings.

Need to inform your family or others about endo so they're more understanding and supportive? Our videotape "You're Not Alone...Understanding Endometriosis" has been used with great results.

Wish you could hear the leading experts discuss the most up-to-date treatments of the disease? Order our taped cassettes of speeches given by leading doctors and others at the Association's 15th Anniversary Conference in November 1995. Listen while driving, getting dressed, cooking, or at any other time your hands are busy but your mind could be taking in important information on endometriosis.

Don't Forget! Become a Member and Stay a Member!

The Endometriosis Association's work is made possible by members, primarily women with endometriosis, who support the Association through dues and volunteer efforts.  Without them, we would be unable to maintain the staff and organization to generate our programs and materials and keep prices low. Therefore we think it is only fair to ask that you join when you take advantage of our materials by ordering from this catalog. You can become a member by sending a check and filling out the membership form, or add your membership to your order by filling out the appropriate section of the order form. We look forward to having you join us!
