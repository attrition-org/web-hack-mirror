End Endo Now Club

Your small monthly gift really adds up when you use electronic fund transfer.  Your gift of just $15 per month is $180 per year!   And your dollars go even farther to provide support, education and research for women and girls with endo and their families when you use electronic fund transfer, because you're knocking out administrative costs.

Fight back at endometriosis the way it often attacks us -- monthly!  It's easy to do.  Just print out this page, sign and date it, and send it in with your first month's check.  Your bank will do the rest.

I want to give $_______ per month.  I'll enclose my first month check.  Beginning next month, that amount will be automatically transferred to the Endometriosis Association.  I understand that I can change or discontinue this arrangement at any time by contacting the Endometriosis Association.   The amount will be transferred from my account on the 10th of each month.

_______________________          _______________
                Signature                                         Date
