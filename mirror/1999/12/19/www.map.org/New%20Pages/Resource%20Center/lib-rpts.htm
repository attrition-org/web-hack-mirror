Key Global Health Reports

The following are links to important reports on world health topics available on the Internet. If you know of reports that should be added, send us E-MAIL to let us know.

 

Global HIV/AIDS - 1997
The Joint United Nations Program on HIV/AIDS (UNAIDS) and the World Health Organization report that over 30 million adults and children are now believed to be living with HIV infection.

World Health Report -- 1997
The World Health Organization's annual report on health around the globe. The 1997 report -- "Conquering suffering, enriching humanity" -- focuses on chronic diseases. The 1996 and 1995 reports are also available.

State of the World's Vaccines and Immunizations
The World Health Organization and UNICEF team up to provide the first ever report monitoring vaccinization and immunization efforts around the world.

Weekly Epidemiological Report
The World Health Organization's weekly review of disease reports.

The State of the World's Children-- 1998
UNICEF's annual report on children, including health indicators.

Progress of Nations 1997
UNICEF's annual report ranksing nations according to their achievements in child health, nutrition, education, family planning, and progress for women.

Morbidity and Mortality Weekly Report
The U.S. Centers for Disease Control and Prevention's weekly statistical look at disease outbreaks, trends and other information.
