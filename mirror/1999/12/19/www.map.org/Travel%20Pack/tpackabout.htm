Travel Pack ®

About Our Pre-Packaged Program:
This program offers and assortment of the most critically-needed medicines and medical supplies ideal for short-term teams, doctors, nurses and/or church mission group. It is designed to relieve the time-consuming and lengthy process of identifying diseases common to developing countries, choosing appropriate medicines to take and attending to other details to insure safe passage of the valuable life-saving cargo. Medical Packs are developed every 30-45 days, depending on product expiration dates and availability. All medicines are in-date and first-quality. Some of the medicines may have a short expiry date. Please call for the most current offer.

Packaging:
The Travel Pack is pre-packaged in two 1.6 cubic ft. boxes, each measuring 17" x 11" x 14". The Travel Pack weight varies between 64 and 69 lbs and is designed to meet international airline travel requirements. The two cartons of supplies, when strapped together, meet size and weight requirements as one package for check-in baggage. Travel Pack cartons are affixed with handles for your carrying convenience.

Service Fee:
MAP's donated medicines and medical supplies are free of charge. Handling charges assists with one-third of the costs of MAP's computerized inventory system, highly trained staff, extensive storage space and distribution tracking system. Plus, 2/3 of the costs of processing, packing and shipping are subsidized through the generosity of donations from individuals, churches and foundations.

Payment:
Prepayment of the service fee must accompany your order and may be made by using check or credit card. Medical Packs may not be returned for a refund.

Customs Information:
Customs documents; a notarized Gift Certificate and a Packing List are attached directly to the pack.

Shipping Information:
Shipping charges are included in the service fee. Requests can be shipped with in two days of your confirmed order, if needed quickly, and shipped to you by UPS ground transport. Multiple orders may require additional delivery time. Medical Packs may not be shipped by container. They are for hand-carry or air freight shipment only.

IMPORTANT!
Contents of Packs change every 30-45 days; therefore, the list of medicines you review might be different than the medicines you actually receive unless you request an updated list prior to departure. We ship the newest offer according to the scheduled departure date. If a packing list of the medicines is needed before the medicines are scheduled to arrive, please contact MAP either by phone or by fax.
