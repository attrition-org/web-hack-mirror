 A MAP International TRAVEL PACK® is  a pre-packed assortment of the most critically-needed medicines in the developing world.

The Travel Pack consists of two boxes with handles for easy carrying which equal two carry-on, or one piece of check-in baggage.

HOW TO ORDER A TRAVEL PACK

Call us at 1-800-225-8550
or email: lcrosby@map.org

  • All applicants for a travel Pack must be approved through MAP's eligibility process.
  • A physician's signature is required.
  • Contents of packs may change according to products available.
  • All travel packs are for hand-carry or air freight shipment only.
  • Travel Packs must be used outside of the United States for the treatment of the ill, needy and infants and must be given free of charge.
