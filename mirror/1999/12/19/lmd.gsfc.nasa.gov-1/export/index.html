NASA  Privacy Statement

This site was designed to provide the GSFC community with information related to the export process. Readers are cautioned, however, that it is the responsibility of the GSFC Export Control Office to interpret export regulations and to determine the proper export authority. ALL EXPORTS MUST BE APPROVED BY THE GSFC EXPORT CONTROL OFFICE. EXPORT LAWS AND REGULATIONS ARE BINDING. PENALTIES FOR VIOLATIONS WILL BE ASSESSED TO THE PERSON RESPONSIBLE AND NOT TO NASA.
