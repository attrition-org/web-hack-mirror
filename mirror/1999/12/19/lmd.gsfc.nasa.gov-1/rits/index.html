RITS
The GSFC Receiving Inspection and Test System (RITS) is a web-based system, maintained by the Logistics Management Division (Code 230), employed by acquisition initiators to establish and document Receiving Inspection Instructions on a Work Order Authorization (WOA) and compile a history of supplier performance based upon the results of receiving inspection and test for each acquisition. This system is also used by acquisition initiators and procurement personnel to check the performance history of a supplier prior to purchasing products. GPG 4520.2, "Incoming Inspection and Test," is the governing directive detailing the requirements for RITS use. To access the current version of GPG 4520.2, access the Goddard Directives Management System (GDMS).
