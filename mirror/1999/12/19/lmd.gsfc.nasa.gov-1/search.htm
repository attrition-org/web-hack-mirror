Query Language

The text search engine allows queries to be formed from arbitrary Boolean expressions containing the keywords AND, OR, and NOT, and grouped with parentheses. For example:

logistics services logistics and services
finds documents containing 'logistics' or 'services' finds documents containing both 'logistics' and 'services'
   
logistics or services logistics not services
same as above finds documents containing 'logistics' but not 'services'
