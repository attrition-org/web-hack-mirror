Vehicle Reservations

The Motor Pool Dispatch Office provides vehicles for employees to use while on official business. Vehicles may be used within a 200-mile radius. All requests to travel greater than 50 miles require travel orders. Employees must possess a valid state driver’s license.

Customer Process:
  • Vehicle Requests

Requests for vehicles must be made to the Motor Pool Dispatch Office.

Special vehicle needs must be submitted in writing to Code 234 at Greenbelt or Code 231.W at Wallops. The request must be signed by a Branch Head or higher.

  • Reporting Accidents

In the event of an accident, drivers must:

Fill out the Motor Vehicle Accident Report (SF 91) and the Statement of Witness Form (SF 94). The GSFC Form 23-14B, Accident Reporting Instructions, provides instructions. The forms are located in the glove box.

Turn in all forms to the dispatcher upon return or drop them in the after-hours drop box.
