2000 CALENDAR ORDERING INFORMATION

 

Calendars for 2000 are now available and orders may be placed if you have funds for ordering stores stock items. To obtain your calendars, please place an on-line electronic requisition through the AMMS Web Order Entry System at Greenbelt or Wallops. Also, you may forward a GSFC Form 20-7, Stores Stock Requisition, (for Greenbelt customers) or a Form WI-929, Stores Stock Requisition, (for Wallops customers). Requisitions should be sent to Stores Stock, Code 239, Building 16W (for Greenbelt customers), or to Building F-19, Code 239, (for Wallops customers). We ask that you consolidate your requirements at the division or branch level, if possible, instead of submitting a separate requisition for each calendar.

Following is a list of the 2000 calendars that are available from stores stock. If you'd like any other calendar, please prepare a GSFC Form 20-19, Item Review and Stockage Request, for the desired item and send it to Supply Operations, Code 239 (Building 16W for Greenbelt customers, or Building F-7 for Wallops customers). You may also forward a completed Stores Stock Requisition along with the stockage request.
