UNCF Scholarship Applications due Fall 1999

The United Negro College Fund has provided, for your convenience, applications on-line for UNCF scholarship programs due during the fall 1999-00 academic year. Deadline dates for these programs are between the months of September 1999 and December 1999.

Programs below are administered on a competitive basis and require the following materials be attached and forwarded to UNCF with the completed applications.

  • Official Transcript
  • Letters of Recommendation
  • Essay
  • Photograph

Download applications for complete information on eligibility criteria and the selection process.

All applications are in Adobe Reader format.  You must use the Adobe Reader 3.0 or higher to view and print these applications. You can download Adobe Reader for free at: http://www.adobe.com/prodindex/acrobat/readstep.html
