NAC/NPAC-UNCF Annual Conference 

 

Each year the NAC/NPAC-UNCF hosts its annual conference in a city that has an established Inter-Alumni Council (IAC). The NAC and the local host committee (comprised of local IAC members) work diligently throughout the year to bring together UNCF staff, college students, college presidents and administrators, alumni, high school students and friends and family of UNCF. The conference presents an opportunity for all of the constituents to network, discuss pertinent educational and political issues, attend workshops and seminars and plan the annual fundraising activities to raise funds for The College Fund.


2000 NAC/NPAC-UNCF Conference Highlights
