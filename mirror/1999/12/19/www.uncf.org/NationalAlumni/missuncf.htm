1999 Miss National UNCF Tracy Lynn Samuel is our 1999 Miss National UNCF. Ms. Samuel is a junior at Wiley College majoring in Biology, with a minor in Zoology. She is a native of Beaumont, TX and graduated from Central Senior High School. Tracy is also a proud member of Alpha Kappa Alpha Sorority and the Pre-Alumni Council.

Her plans after graduating from Wiley are to attend graduate school and gain a master’s degree in marine biology and pursue a career as a marine biologist. Tracy’s motto is "If you ever want to succeed in life, you have to change the impossible to the possible".

Miss Samuel is available to make guest appearances. For more information, please contact Monise Quidley, National Director for Alumni Affairs and Organizations, United Negro College Fund, (703) 205-3463 or MQuidley@uncf.org.
