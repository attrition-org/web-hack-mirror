Columbus Torah Academy

Children's Art Gallery

We have established a children's art gallery at CTA in memory of Harriet Weltz z"l. Each month, the artwork in the hallways will be changed and three outstanding pieces will be selected by local artists for display in the permanent gallery in the CTA library. All of the artwork will be photographed and stored on computer for the enjoyment of others for many years to come.
