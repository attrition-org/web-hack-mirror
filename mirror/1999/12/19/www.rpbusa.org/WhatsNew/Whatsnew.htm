RESEARCH TO PREVENT BLINDNESS SCIENCE WRITERS SEMINAR

Research to Prevent Blindness (RPB) will hold its 15th Biennial National Science Writers Seminar in Eye Research in Universal City, CA September 26-29, 1999. The Seminar features reports from 32 of the nation's leading ophthalmic scientists concerning the latest advances in eye research, including investigations into macular degeneration, cataract, glaucoma, diabetic retinopathy, corneal disease, eye movement disorders, color blindness, dry eye and pterygia.

To obtain a compilation of papers presented at the 14th seminar, and/or to request a copy of the 15th compilation upon publication, contact Tom Furlong, Director of Public Information.
