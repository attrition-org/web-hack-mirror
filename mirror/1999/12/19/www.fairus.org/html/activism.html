This section provides access to several resources that will assist your efforts to influence immigration policy at the local or national level. Action Alerts contain information on the most important, fast-breaking issues. The News Summary page will keep you abreast of what the media are saying about immigration. The Stein Report also highlights news developments and includes information that may be in tomorrow's news.

Four legislative resource connections allow you to check on Key Legislation and send Messages to Congress. Additional legislative resources may be found in the Legislation section.

Finally, you may want to refer to past Immigration Report stories, FAIR Press Releases and to local facts that you will find on the State Factsheets. Don't forget that you can always turn to the Search function to help you find that elusive tidbit of information you are seeking.
