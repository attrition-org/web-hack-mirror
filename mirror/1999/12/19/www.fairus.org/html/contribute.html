WHY YOUR HELP IS NEEDED
FAIR receives no government funding and depends on the financial support of individual donors to do its work. FAIR is a tax-exempt 501(c)(3) non-profit organization and all contributions to FAIR are 100 percent tax-deductible to the donor. We need your help!

IT'S EASY! Click on one of the following ways to make your tax-deductible contribution to FAIR:

  • Contribution form to send in with your check, money order, or credit card authorization via snail mail.

  • Make a contribution via a Visa or MasterCard on-line.

  • Become a FAIR Cornerstone Contributor by authorizing a monthly charge to your Visa, MasterCard, or regular bank account.

FAIR TOP RATED The National Charities Information Bureau (NCIB) awards FAIR its top rating for meeting or exceeding all its standards for charitable giving. NCIB's website is www.give.org.

WANT MORE INFORMATION? You can also request that a packet of membership information be sent to you by e-mailing a request to: memberservices@fairus.org.
