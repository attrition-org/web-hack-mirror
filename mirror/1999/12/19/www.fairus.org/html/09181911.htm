More on the Immigration Billboard Campaign

At latest count the coast-to-coast immigration awareness billboard campaign now numbers 41 billboards, and the latest location to be targetted is South Carolina. The billboard campaign, launched earlier this year in New York City, has now arrived in Charleston with eight new billboards.

In California there are now 21 signs, and their location may be found on the Project USA website. Minnesota is another billboard location featured on the ProjectUSA.org website.
