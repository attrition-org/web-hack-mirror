This section provides information and resources for following and influencing the congressional process that sets our nation's immigration laws.

FAIR's current legislative priorities are on the Legislative Update page. Key Legislation, Scorecards and Hearing reports are to help you influence your representatives.

Messages to Congress are themes your represen- tatives should hear, and the State Factsheets provide local data to strengthen your messages.

To find you your representatives or senators and contact them, click here.

Updates on breaking legislative developments are also available by calling (202) 745-2959.
