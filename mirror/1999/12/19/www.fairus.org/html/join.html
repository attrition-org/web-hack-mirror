MEMBERSHIP BENEFITS

FAIR members get ten issues each year of The FAIR Immigration Report -- the nation's most widely read newsletter with immigration reform facts and news, special bulletins on immigration legislation and happenings in Congress, and invitations to attend FAIR sponsored membership and national events, meetings, and conferences. Because FAIR is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization, contributions to FAIR are 100 percent tax-deductible to the donor.

FAIR TOP RATED

The National Charities Information Bureau (NCIB) awards FAIR its top rating for meeting or exceeding all its standards for charitable giving. NCIB's website is www.give.org.
