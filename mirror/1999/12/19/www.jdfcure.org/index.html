JDF-Funded Research Discovery Offers Hope of Diabetes Vaccine

May 14, 1999—Today, in the journal Science a team of researchers led by Ji-Won Yoon, Ph.D., and funded in part by the Juvenile Diabetes Foundation International (JDF) published successful results in preventing the development of Type 1, or juvenile, diabetes in mice—a step closer toward the discovery of a vaccine for this deadly disease in humans.—See full story.

  
Jan. 99—The new JDF public service announcement (PSA) campaign features the Foundation’s International Chairman, Mary Tyler Moore. Mary, who has had diabetes for over 30 years, filmed the spot with her friends Joey, 7, Samantha, 9, Jennifer, 10, and Will, 12. Video, storyboard, print ads, and a new series of Web banners underscore the central message: “Insulin is not a cure”—Go to JDF PSAs.
