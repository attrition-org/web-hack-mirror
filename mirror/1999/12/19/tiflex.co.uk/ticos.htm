TICO S is a high performance machinery mounting material. It matches today's fast changing environment, where ease of machine mounting and flexibility of plant layout are prime factors.

TICO S is manufactured from a blend of carefully selected cork particles and polychloroprene / acrylonitrile elastomers.

 

Key features of TICO S include:
 

Rapid installation.
Enables flexibility of machinery layout.
Helps reduce vibration.
When used in conjunction with TICO adhesive, virtually eliminates use of anchor bolts and associated floor damage, providing major savings.
Retains properties throughout long maintenance free life.
Operational temperature between -40° and +100°C (-40°F and 212°F).
Can reduce transmitted noise.
Wide range of standard sheet sizes and thicknesses.
Maximum static stress 0.35 MN/m² (35,000 Kg/m²)
Compatible with all types of machine base.
Can be supplied cut to size.  

 
TICO ADJUSTAMOUNTS

Where the accuracy of plant height, gradient or level is important, TICO Adjustamounts provide efficient precision mounting. Swift screw adjustment, plus the numerous advantages of TICO resilient inserts, make them the ideal choice for mounting any free standing equipment.

Key features of TICO Adjustamounts include:
 

Accurate and speedy height adjustment and levelling.
TICO resilient insert virtually eliminates floor damage.
Low profile for minimum additional height.
Will also fit many existing levelling studs on plant.
Wide range to suit many applications and loads. 

 

TICO ADHESIVES & SEALANTS

A range of top quality products specially formulated for TICO pads. These are the only adhesives and sealants we recommend as others may degrade the TICO pad or provide insufficient bonding.

TICO A/GE
A synthetic elastomer based adhesive for bonding TICO S/PA and TICO LF/PA pads to most floor materials and machine structures e.g. concrete, metal, brick, ceramics and timber.

TICO A/VF Void-filler Adhesive
A special polychloroprene based adhesive for bonding TICO VF/PA/1 void filler to concrete, brick or ceramic surfaces.

TICO A/WB Wet Bond Adhesive
A three part mortar type adhesive for bonding TICO S/PA and TICO LF/PA pads to damp or pitted surfaces of concrete, brick or ceramic. This system comprises an epoxy resin, hardener and granular filler.

TICO A/ST Sealing Tape
A fabric reinforced tape with self adhesive surface for covering and sealing joins between TICO pads and/or void filler.

TICO A/SU/1 Sealant
A liquid two-part polysulphide compound which sets with permanent elastic properties for sealing pit installations against liquids and other foreign materials.

TICO A/SU/3
An elastomer based primer for applying to concrete surfaces before the use of TICO A/SU/1 sealant.
