Reliability

Tiflex Ltd has been manufacturing quality polymer compounds for industry for over forty years.

A part of the international James Walker Group of Companies; we specialise in the design, development and production of compound to meet the needs of roller manufacturers, extruders and other quality polymer processors throughout the UK.   

Manufacturing Expertise

Tiflex use only the highest quality raw materials specially selected for property, performance and process behaviour. We understand the importance of consistency for our customers. Our compounds are mixed on open mills under clean and tightly controlled conditions in order to deliver the highest standards of reliability, ingredient dispersion and product performance.

Support

Advice and support from experienced polymer, technologists within our fully equipped materials laboratories.
Rheology and basic property information is available for every batch supplied. More in-depth testing to international specifications or to meet specific performance criteria can be undertaken for larger projects.
Wherever you are situated in the UK or overseas, our experienced staff are on hand to provide on-site assistance and advice.

Our Continuous Improvement Programme means that we strive constantly to optimise every aspect of our process - from purchasing to packaging. At regular intervals, we gather and analyse data on quality, efficiency and service performance. This procedure - coupled with market research - enables us to target and focus effectively on improvement areas.

We aim to deliver what you want, when you want it, at a price which represents genuine value.
In short, we aim to provide trouble-free compounds that match up to the expectations of today's quality polymer processors.
