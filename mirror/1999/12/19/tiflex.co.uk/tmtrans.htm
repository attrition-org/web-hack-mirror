Tiflex Ltd believes in putting the customer first at all times and to this end we offer an extensive range of products that are both practical and attractive.

The company boasts an impressive portfolio of customers in the UK, Europe and the Far East. We pride ourselves on the levels of service and technical ability ensuring that the specification of the products is compatible with customer requirements.

Tiflex has established a position as one of the leading suppliers of specialist flooring in the market place today. We are continually extending our product ranges and upgrading our product specifications to meet with the ever increasing demands placed upon them.

Constant research, development, testing and a commitment to excellence has put Treadmaster where it is today, at the forefront of the flooring industry. We are proud of our leading position and we will strive to maintain it.

Treadmaster was first introduced over 30 years ago and is widely used in buildings such as airports and hospitals, railway stations, military installations and public transport vehicles.

All grades are available in a wide selection of bright colours and a broad range of sizes.

Treadmaster combines the natural individual features of granulated cork and rubber elastomers, which produce an ideal resilient flooring material. This provides the pedestrian with secure footing in a sound absorbing environment. It has excellent resistance to slip, even in wet conditions.

 

TREADMASTER TM7 AND TM5

Treadmaster TM7 and Treadmaster TM5 are exciting, innovative ranges of fire resistant floorings designed for the passenger transport industry.

They have been developed to be competitively priced in this specialist area, whist meeting the stringent demands of a safety conscious market. Continual research and development ensures that they remain in tune with the very latest production methods and design requirements.

TM7 conforms to low smoke and high fire retardant requirements and is currently being used by train manufacturers and operators throughout Europe and in the Far East.

TM5 conforms to strict low smoke and high flame retardancy specifications and the cork composition gives extremely good anti-slip properties in both wet and dry conditions.

Bright and colourful, TM5 can be produced to meet specific corporate colour schemes. Its cleanability means that TM5 will keep its fresh, stylish appearance longer than competitive products.

 

TREADMASTER TM3, TM4 AND TM6

Treadmaster TM3 is a synthetic rubber bonded cork material, with a high degree of resistance to oil and grease and suitable for most outdoor uses. It is designed to give Class 2 fire resistance when tested to BS476 Part 7.

Treadmaster TM4 is a natural rubber bonded cork material exhibiting high coefficient of friction properties, specify designed for the transport industry and high usage areas.

Also available in the Treadmaster range is Treadmaster TM6 - a unique blend of non-PVC elastomer bonded cork with mineral fillers which makes it flexible and hard wearing. TM6 is available in a wide range of bright colours. 

 

SOFT STEP

Soft Step mats are the ideal answer to wet applications such as swimming pools, shower rooms and saunas. They have a safe non-slip base, a hygienic antibacterial treatment and are comfortable, soft and warm under foot.

Soft Step mats are made from recycled PE plastic which can be recycled; are hardwearing and resistant to wide temperature fluctuations (-40°C to +50°C).

Soft Step mats are available in a range of attractive standard colours.
