Many years of involvement with the off-shore engineering and production industries has led to the development of a range of elastomer based components providing unique solutions for the support of off-shore pipework.

These include the TICO Pipe Grip, rubber covered U-bolts, TICO Clip Strip, strap clamp lining and TICO Bondslip, providing a resilient, low friction sliding bearing unit. In addition TICO Bellows and Compensators are extensively used off-shore for the jointing of a variety of ductwork.

In this demanding environment, Tiflex has provided and continues to provide an extensive design and problem solving service.

 

TICO Pipe Grips

Deep Water Exploration and production in hostile environments demands the highest quality equipment. That equipment is only as good as the component parts from which it is built. When Tiflex worked with leading offshore specifiers to develop an isolated pipe handler this philosophy was our starting point. Every part of the TICO Pipe Grip has been designed to perform its job safely, economically and reliably.

Key Advantages of the TICO Pipe Grip include:

Rubber parts moulded from fire retardant material - will not support the surface spread of flame; low evolution of toxic gases.
Serrated inner face on grip-type Pipe Grip provides an unrivalled level of cushioning and support.
Operational temperature range from -50°C to +120° continuous, 150° intermittent.
High quality zinc plated or stainless steel metal parts.
Accurate threads mean easy fitting.
A design which fully isolates the pipe from its support helping to prevent electrolytic corrosion.
A wide range of standard sizes.    
 

TICO Clip Strip

Utilising our well known TICO S material and drawing on many years of experience in the design of load bearing elastomeric components, we have designed this range of products to provide the highest level of shock and vibration isolation between pipework and hanger. Available in a comprehensive range of sizes TICO Clip Strip provides an effective and economical answer to the problem of pipe isolation.

Key Advantages of the TICO Clip Strip include:

Manufactured from TICO S material, proven in industrial applications world-wide.
Raised shoulder design for positive location.
Sizes to suit most common strap widths.
Quick, easy installation.
Reduction and absorption of shock, noise and vibration.
Pliable and easy to cut.
Prevents electrolytic action between dissimilar metals.
S/CL grade suitable for continuous temperatures of up to 100°C.
HT/CL grade suitable for continuous temperatures of up to 150°C.    
 

TICO Bond Slip Units

TICO Bond Slip Units provide a simple, economical, easy to install solution to the problem of providing movement and support. They can be used in mechanical systems and pipework installations.

TICO Bond Slip Units have been developed over our many years of involvement with petrochemical and industrial clients. Utilising sate-of-the-art plastic materials in conjunction with unique TICO resilient substrates, they have been proven in thousands of applications world-wide.

Key Advantages of the TICO Bond Slip Units include:

Simplicity of design and installation.
Maintenance free.
Very low coefficient of friction. Normally less than 0.1.
Smooth movement without the occurrence of 'stick slip'.
High quality materials used throughout.
All bonding carried out under controlled conditions.
Provides noise and vibration attenuation.
Full technical support at all phases of design and installation.
Proven over many applications.
