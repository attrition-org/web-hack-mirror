The Treadmaster Original range has been the benchmark for anti-slip deck coverings for over thirty years. It's performance benefits have been enjoyed by professional seamen and yachtsmen the world over.

Manufactured from natural cellular ingredients blended with carefully selected polymers, Treadmaster provides excellent traction underfoot - wet or dry. Treadmaster's durability is legendary with many years of service life assured.

Treadmaster Original is available in two surface finishes: the widely recognised diamond pattern (DP), providing the ultimate in grip through a wide range of operating conditions, and the smooth pattern (SP) specifically designed to give a combination of non-slip properties and comfort. 

 

Treadmaster Sport

The Treadmaster Sport range has been introduced to address the needs of the sport boat and personal water craft market. A range of vibrant colours compliments the decor which is now typically used on this type of craft.
Treadmaster Sport is manufactured from high quality synthetic polymers providing the legendary Treadmaster grip together with good looks which will last for many years.

 

Treadmaster Step Pads

Treadmaster Step Pads have been introduced to provide a quick and easy method of covering small areas of deck. They use materials from the Original and Sport ranges, fitted with a high performance waterproof self- adhesive backing, simply peel and stick!   

 

Treadmaster Engine Room Grade (ER)

This grade is specifically designed for below deck application in machinery spaces. it has an enhanced level of resistance to mineral oils and is coloured black to minimise the effects of staining.
