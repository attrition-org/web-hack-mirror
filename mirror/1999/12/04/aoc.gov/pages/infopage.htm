The United States Capitol

For Further Information

On-line information now available at this site includes

  • Information about visiting the Capitol.
  • A brief bibliography of information about the Capitol and its works of art.
  • Answers to frequently asked questions (FAQs) about the Capitol.

Additional information about the Congress and images of the Capitol are available at the WWW pages of the U.S. Senate and the U.S. House of Representatives and at the Library of Congress on-line exhibit "Temple of Liberty: Building the Capitol for a New Nation".

Another useful on-line resource is the virtual tour of the Capitol on the Web site of the United States Senate.

You may also wish to visit THOMAS, the Library of Congress's legislative information resource.

For information about opportunities for employment with the Architect of the Capitol, visit the Office of Personnel Management's USAJobs agency search page and enter "Architect of the Capitol" in the Keyword box.
