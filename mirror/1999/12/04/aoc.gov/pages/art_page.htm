The United States Capitol

Art in the U.S. Capitol

The works of art in the Capitol reflect the development of the United States and the Congress. They range from bronze and marble statues to oil portraits and frescoed murals. Their subjects include prominent Americans, important moments in history, and allegorical representations of the nation's ideals.
