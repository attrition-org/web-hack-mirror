The United States Capitol

The Congressional Office Buildings

The Capitol Complex includes six major office buildings for the House and Senate:

  • House Office Buildings
    • Joseph G. Cannon House Office Building
    • Nicholas Longworth House Office Building
    • Sam Rayburn House Office Building
  • Senate Office Buildings
    • Richard B. Russell Senate Office Building
    • Everett M. Dirksen Senate Office Building
    • Philip A. Hart Senate Office Building

A map of the U.S. Capitol complex (110k .jpg) showing the location of the congressional office buildings is also available.
