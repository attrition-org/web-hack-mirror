Please read and print the full instructions before installing the wallpaper.

 To install the following image as your computer's wallpaper you must first find out  your screen resolution.  To find out your screen resolution-

1) minimize all windows until your main desktop is showing  
2) right mouse click anywhere on your main desktop 
3) choose properties and go to your settings tab 
4) your screen resolution will be noted in the display/desktop area .  
5) close your settings window and return to this page

Now click on the proper image size on the right.  After the image appears, right mouse click on the image and choose...set as wallpaper.  You have now installed official Colts wallpaper.
