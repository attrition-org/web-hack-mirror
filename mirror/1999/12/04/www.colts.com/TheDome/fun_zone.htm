ATA Colts Fun Zone

Come out to Pan Am Plaza before every Colts home game and experience the ATA Colts Fun Zone.  Show off your skills at the Quarterback Challenge or the Field Goal Challenge.   Bring the kids and let them jump in the Helmet Bouncer, climb the Over the Top mountain slide or run through the Obstacle Course.  Don’t miss your opportunity to visit the Colts Pro Shop to buy jerseys, T-shirts, sweatshirts and more.  A variety of merchandise is available for you to purchase on the plaza.

Also enjoy the food, beverages and live band*.  The Fun Zone opens three hours before kickoff so you have plenty of time to eat, drink, play, shop, and listen to great music.  In addition, raffle tickets may be purchased to win lots of great Colts merchandise, Jiffy Lube oil changes for one year, or airfare for two on ATA.   And, at the end of the season one lucky person will win 2 tickets to the Super Bowl! 

All of the proceeds from the raffle and Fun Zone benefit Noble of Indiana.  Noble of Indiana’s Children’s Services helps children with or at risk of developmental delays develop successfully.  Noble’s Adult Services aids adults with disabilities in becoming independent members of the community.  Last year, Noble of Indiana served more than 2,000 children and adults with disabilities.

Through a variety of innovative programs, Noble of Indiana has served children and adults with disabilities and their families for nearly 50 years.

Noble of Indiana inspires the community to recognize, embrace and include the contributions of individuals with disabilities.    

Come check out the fun on Pan Am Plaza before every Colts home game!!!
