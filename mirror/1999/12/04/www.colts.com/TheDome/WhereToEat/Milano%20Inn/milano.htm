A downtown landmark known for authentic Italian cuisine.   A family owned establishment since 1934 with a menu consisting of favorites like shrimp linguine, lasagna, fettuccine alfredo, along with a variety of chicken and veal.   The Milano Inn is one of  the city's most popular restaurants.
