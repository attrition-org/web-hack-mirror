An exciting place for the whole family to gather before home games.   There is plenty of food and drink, games for the kids, and raffle prizes for Colts merchandise. 

Tailgating is highly encouraged. The South Lot is where you may find a lot of fellow car tailgaters, and where radio stations and entertainment are scheduled to appear.

There are more than 40,000 parking spaces available on game day within a 10-minute walk of the Dome. Of these spaces, about 10,000 are directly adjacent to the stadium. Only 2,500 of these are controlled or managed by the Dome. Stadium parking ranges from $5-10 for most areas.

Keep in mind that Indiana State Law prohibits the sale of alcoholic beverages to persons under age 21. Additionally the sale of alcoholic beverages is prohibited on Sundays by commercial facilities. Beer and other beverages are sold inside the Dome.
