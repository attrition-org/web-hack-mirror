December 2, 1999
What would YOU do to see the BLUE??

Today ten crazy Colts fans competed for the chance to win a trip for two to Miami to cheer on the Colts.  Some of the contestants painted themselves blue and shaved body parts, while another became a human bowling ball.  There was even a contestant who ate nightcrawlers and washed them down with raw eggs to show his true blue.  The winner Jon Colby was pelted with pies, eggs, water balloons, and various other substances while wearing only his boxers.   He then proceeded to sing his own Colts rap song and put on a dancing exhibition.   Congratulations to all the contestants who all showed there true blue.
