November 23, 1999
INDIANAPOLIS COLTS SELL OUT FINAL THREE HOME GAMES
FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

The Indianapolis Colts final three home games against the New York Jets (Nov. 28), the New England Patriots (Dec. 12) and Washington Redskins (Dec. 19) are sold out.

The Jets will be returning a limited number of tickets which will go on sale Friday. This marks the first time since 1985 that the Colts have sold out all eight regular season games.
