December 1, 1999
RUNNING BACK EDGERRIN JAMES WINS NFL MONTHLY AWARDS
FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

Indianapolis Colts RB-Edgerrin James has been named NFL Offensive Rookie of the Month and AFC Offensive Player of the Month for his performance in the Colts’ four November games.  James is the only player to ever win both honors at the same time.

James is the first rookie to win a conference Player of the Month award since it was established in 1988. James also earned the NFL Offensive Rookie of the Month Award for September.  James totaled 82-443, 2 TDs rushing and 24-262, 2 TDs receiving in the Colts’ four November games, including three 100+ rushing outings. 

The Colts posted their first undefeated November since 1975. James opened the month with 20-109 rushing/7-90, 1 TD receiving against Kansas City.  He followed up the performance with his third consecutive 100+ rushing game in the Colts 27-19 win over the New York Giants.  James was 16-108 rushing and 5-72 receiving. 

James earned AFC Offensive Player of the Week for his game against Philadelphia.  He totaled 22-152, 2 TDs rushing/5-47, 1 TD receiving in only three quarters of action.  He had 117 yards rushing in the first quarter.   James was 24-74 rushing/7-53 receiving against the New York Jets last week in the RCA Dome.

James ranks first in the NFL in rushing attempts, first in AFC yards, second in the NFL, T2nd in AFC rushing touchdowns, T4th in the NFL.  His seven 100+ rushing games top the NFL, and the total set new Colts rookie record and ranks as the second highest seasonal total in club history (8, Eric Dickerson, 1988).
