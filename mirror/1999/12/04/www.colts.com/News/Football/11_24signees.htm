November 23, 1999
COLTS SIGN DB-ERIC SMEDLEY AND DL-SHANE BONHAM 

FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

The Indianapolis Colts signed free agent DB-Eric Smedley and DL-Shane Bonham, the club announced today.

Smedley rejoins the Colts after being waived on November 20.  Bonham was terminated by San Francisco on November 9.  He was inactive for the first eight games with the 49ers.
