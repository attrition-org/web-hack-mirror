How can I order season tickets?

Season tickets go on sale to the general public in
mid-March. Call (317) 297-7000 for information. They can
fax or mail a diagram of available seats.

How can I arrange to have a group of people come to the Dome?

Contact our ticket sales office at (317) 299-3663. They will
assist you in the arrangements and tell you about special
incentives for groups. They can also arrange a pre-game
tailgate party or reception.

How and when can I order single game tickets?

Single game tickets go on sale August 2 at the RCA Dome Ticket Office or via Ticketmaster at 10:00 A.M. Their number is (317) 239-5151 or check out
their web site at http://www.Ticketmaster.com. You can also call the Colts ticket office at (317) 297-7000 to request an individual game order form.

Where can I get Colts stuff?

Visit the ColtsPro Shop in downtown Indianapolis, located at CircleCentre Mall. We're downstairs by Nordstrom, at the corner of Georgia and Illinois Streets.  Or visit our Colts Pro Shop online for the phone number to request a catalog.

Can I take a tour of the practice facility?

Tours are offered at the RCA Dome in downtown
Indianapolis. Please call (317) 237-5210 for information.
Currently, tours of the Colts Complex (the practice facility)
are not being offered to the general public.

Do you offer internships?

The sales, marketing, public relations and operations
departments currently offer internships during the season.
Send resumes to Indianapolis Colts, P.O. Box 535000,
Indianapolis, IN 46253. Letters should be directed to the
specific department of interest.

How can I get donations for an auction or charity?

Contact the Community Relations Department at (317)
297-2658. You must submit an application, which must be
received at the Colts office at least one month prior to the
event.
