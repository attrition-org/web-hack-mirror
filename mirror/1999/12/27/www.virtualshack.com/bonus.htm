What time were you born?

Let the hour of your birth determine the real traits behind what makes you tick!!!

MIDNIGHT- 2 AM

THE QUICK LEARNER

Dominant house-third, ruled by Mercury

Knowledge is power. You are an avid learner and extremely curious.

The quick thinker that you are you hate not being up to date on the latest news, current events and gossip. You also possess an incredible energy and young outlooks on life. But be careful your consistent need to be the center of attention may get your friends to think of you as over anxious.

2 AM- 4 AM

THE SPENDER

Dominant house- second, ruled by Venus

Spend, spend, spend, that’s what you seem to do best. This isn’t too much of a problem because you are willing to work long and hard for your money and you reap in the rewards. The best characteristic you possess is the clear focus which allows you to achieve anything you want when you set your mind to it, but be careful this passion can also lead to possessiveness and jealousy. Develop your social skills so that you have a good idea of who you are and your friends worth rather than pure material worth.

4 AM- 6 AM

THE LEADER

Dominant house- First, ruled by Mars

Born to be the boss. A natural charismatic leader, you love your freedom and hate to answer to anyone. Becareful your honesty and directness may rub others the wrong way, because it comes off as self-centeredness and demanding. To live up to your full potential you must learn

Co-operation and harmony with all that’s around you.

 

 

6 AM- 8 AM

THE MYSTERIOUS ONE

Dominant house- 12th, ruled by Neptune

Your mystery lays in your solitude and withdrawal from the rest of the world. Your sensitivity makes you very weary of others and the cruelties that exist in everyday activities. You are extremely intuitive and spiritual; you can easily tune in and feel the undercurrents in situations. You prefer working behind the scenes. The planet Neptune represents self-sacrifice, so becareful not to fall into the trap of being the martyr, extend yourself only so far.

8 AM- 10 AM

THE POLITICIAN

Dominant house- 11th, ruled by Uranus

Your magnetism, confidence and sociable nature makes you the life of every party. Your whole life lays in impressing and being surrounded by people, although you are extremely independent and pride on being unconventional. You stand for and believe in humanitarian causes and will do anything for your voice to be heard.

10 AM- 12 PM

THE CLIMBER

Dominant house- 10th, ruled by Saturn

Self-discipline, ambition, and motivation are the core of your character. Becareful of your quest for power and control, your ruthlessness to be number one in your career may turn your competitors into enemies. Although you may be accused of being power-hungry most people born at this time rise to be extremely successful public figures.

12 PM- 2 PM

THE WANDERER

Dominant house- 9th, ruled by Jupiter

Extreme curiosity always leads you to seek new and wonderful experiences ranging from spiritualism to sensualism; from factual to adrenaline trips. Completely unmaterialistic, you prefer to marry someone to share life experiences rather than financial security.

Although your wanderlust is one of your most positive attributes, sometimes it can lead you to lack in self-discipline.

2 PM- 4 PM

THE PASSIONATE ONE

Dominant house- 8th, ruled by Pluto

Intense and sexually passionate. May lead too much undesired drama and unnecessary upheaval in your life. Due to hard times in life you have become a very strong individual. You are extremely committed to personal relationships, becareful not to scare off you partner with your intensity.

4 PM- 6 PM

THE LOVER

Dominant house- 7th, ruled by Venus

This is also the marriage house; usually people born at this time find it hard not to be in a relationship, they don’t feel alive if they’re alone- this can lead to extreme unhappiness. You try to be in complete harmony with everyone around you hating conflict and tension. People find it a joy to be around you because you’re so flexible and compromising.

6 PM- 8 PM

THE TEAMPLAYER

Dominant house- 6th, ruled by Mercury.

You’re a natural born martyr, always sacrificing yourself for the big picture. You are the friend that everyone can always turn to. You do your best work with your hands and are extremely optimistic in any situation. The most important thing for you is your temple (your body) you need to be fit and healthy. You take every fitness trend to it’s extreme until you get bored with it and move on to the next. You loyalty to your partner may also be a bit extreme, watch out that you’re not taken advantage of.

8 PM- 10 PM

THE ENTERTAINER

Dominant house- 5th, ruled by the Sun.

You have both the leadership skills and the star quality that allows you to shine on and off stage. You are the party animal on one end yet adore being a romantic and a passionate lover. You are the life of the party always fill the room with laughter and joy. Everyone loves to be around you.

10 PM- MIDNIGHT

THE SEEKER

Dominant house- 4th, ruled by the Moon.

Very emotional and longing for security, you seek answers in spiritualism. You are in constant search for inner peace and happiness. You are extremely close with your family and siblings. Once you find your soul mate it’s a bond for life.
