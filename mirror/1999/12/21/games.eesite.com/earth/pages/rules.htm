In order to make everyone's experience here at Echelon enjoyable, we have set up several basic guidelines which we ask that you follow. Violation of any of these rules can and will result in immediate removal of your Earth: 2025 account.

Account Management

Users are allowed to create and maintain one account in each of two Earth: 2025 gaming environments: one in the Standard Game and one in the Tournament. In addition, users are allowed to maintain an unlimited number of accounts in the Free-for-All Server. Players should only use their own account; under no circumstances should accounts be shared, traded or played by anyone other than the original owner.

Contact Information

The information you provide when signing up is used to protect the Earth: 2025 game from fraud. You must provide complete and accurate information to participate in any Echelon Games. Please read our Privacy Policy for your protection.

Game Play

Under no circumstances are users allowed to use the aid of automated processes to improve their performance in the Earth game. Use of automated scripts or other software that accesses the Earth servers for any reason outside of normal gameplay is expressly forbidden.

Fair & Honorable Play

Earth: 2025 maintains the highest standards of ethics within the game. Any instance of inappropriate language, manipulation of bugs, or acts of fraud will result in immediate removal from the game. We rely on our users to help us enforce these rules and provide an enjoyable gaming environment for users of all ages and backgrounds.
