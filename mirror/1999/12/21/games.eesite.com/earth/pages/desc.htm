To help accomodate all of our Earth: 2025 players, Earth: 2025 offers three different styles of Earth Servers for your pleasure. Players are welcome to play an account in each server. Each is described below.

The Standard Server

The Standard Server offers our regular games. Each player may manage one and only one account on the Standard Server. This server is split into several games which each player may choose from: The Intensive Game (Game A) offers the ability to play in teams (known as Clans) and should be played mostly by experienced players. Game B, the Standard Game, is for those players who prefer play in a large group environement, but without extensive teamwork. In addition, there are 8 smaller, random games that are designed for people interested in playing with a smaller group of people.

The Tournament Server

Like the Standard Server, players may manage one and only one account on the Tournament server. Tournament games are shorter -- one month each -- and faster paced than the Standard games. New players begin in a random game at the lowest level of the tournament ladder. Each round, the best players will move up to a higher level game with tougher competition. The top 5 players in the highest level game (Game A) each month will be enshrined in the Hall of Champions. will

Free for All Server

The Free For All server allows players to play an unlimited number of accounts with relatively relaxed rules. While turns accumulate more slowly than the other servers, this game gives players the opportunity to experiment with different strategies and get all the Earth gaming they can handle. Language and behavior rules still apply, however, and users are expected to treat fellow players with proper respect.
