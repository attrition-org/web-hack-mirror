To help accomodate all of our Utopia players, we offer two different styles of Utopia games for your pleasure. Players are welcome to play in both servers. Each is described below.

The Standard Game

The Standard Game is our headline feature -- this is the main Utopia game that everyone should participate in. With over 40,000 players here, the main Utopia game offers an incredible interactive gaming environment like no other. Players are allowed to maintain one and only one account in this game.

The Training Ground

The Training Ground serves as a free-for-all game. While players are still expected to follow the other rules of the Utopian Code, you are allowed to create multiple accounts to practice different strategies and styles of play. Because this game is simply for experimentation, the Forums and Politics sections are disabled. In addition, this game may be used for testing new features, and may be reset or simply removed at any time. This game is not designed for serious interactive play.
