IN TRIBUTE TO THE HUMANITARIAN RELIEF COMMUNITY:

A DECLARATION OF GRATITUDE

 

Whereas humanitarian disasters have occurred over the past two years with unprecedented frequency and magnitude,

Whereas these crises have taken place on every continent, and have included both manmade and natural disasters,

Whereas many of these humanitarian crises continue today, affecting millions of people in some of the world’s poorest countries,

Whereas humanitarian relief professionals continue to save lives and relieve suffering around the globe,

Whereas humanitarian workers have lost their lives in service, from Albania and Chechnya, to Burundi and Somalia, and

Whereas humanitarian workers serve at great personal risk, but continue to serve so that others will be less vulnerable,

We, the undersigned, join Ambassador J. Brady Anderson, the Administration of the U.S. Agency for International Development and the President’s Special Coordinator for International Disaster Assistance, in paying tribute to the humanitarian community. We express our sincere gratitude and admiration for your courage, your commitment, and your professionalism.

MAP International is a 45 year old Christian, non sectarian relief and development agency with offices in Latin America and Africa, and health care programs in over 130 developing countries. MAP is dedicated to the provision of essential medicines, the promotion of community health development and the prevention and eradication of disease. Every $1 donated provides $21 in medicines, medical supplies and programs. Administrative and fundraising expenses total only 1.4% of total revenue.

For more information about MAP International or to make a donation please call 1-800-225-8550.
