You can get involved in MAP's global ministry to bring health and hope to a hurting world. First, we need to know about your interests. Please fill in the form below, provide your e-mail address and click the "submit" button on the bottom of the page to be sure that you receive the information you'll need to Get Involved.

MAP Monthly Letter
A monthly communication from MAP Interim President Michael J. Nyenhuis detailing one of the many MAP projects and opportunities for involvement.

MAP International Report
The report highlights current and ongoing projects, as well as recent accomplishments. Testimonies from recipients of medicines, educational programs, and development efforts. Includes coverage of MAP activities around the world.

Christmas Cards
The annual Christmas Cards project is geared toward lifting up the name of Jesus Christ. Christmas cards are selected to convey the compassion of God that MAP is committed to fulfilling.

Corporate Brochure
MAP's Corporate Brochure includes the most recent Annual Financial Report. Also included are recent updates of activity from MAP's regional offices.

Educational Tours
MAP conducts guided tours to MAP projects in East Africa and Latin America. Tour details are available upon request.

MAP Facts
In-depth fact sheet about MAP International. When was it founded, who have been MAP's president's, etc. Informative data.

Volunteer Opportunities
Opportunities available with MAP locally in our offices or in your own home town.

Medical Supply Updates
Update's from MAP's Medical Supply Program showing. the volume of medicines sent to various countries.

Travel Packs
Medicines for Missions...a pre-packed assortment of the most critically needed medicines in the developing world. The Travel Pack consists of two boxes with handles for easy carrying which equal two carry-on, or one piece of check-in baggage.

Planned Giving
Information on the tax advantages and immediate income opportunities through planned giving. Information includes gift annuities, wills, trusts and estate planning.
