The Environmentalist's Guide to a Sensible Immigration Policy -- $12.50
If immigration is the leading cause of population growth in the United States, and population growth is the leading cause of environmental degradation, why do environmental groups like the Sierra Club shy away from discussing immigration? This publication explores that question and equips concerned environmentalists for addressing the need for immigration reduction. Sections of this 63-page book are:
  • Growing Like Crazy
  • Choice or Consequences
  • Sprawl City,USA
  • The growthmongers
  • What to do

How to Win the Immigration Debate -- $5.00
If you've ever taken lumps in an argument over immigration reform, this is the book for you. It's a primer on how to argue for immigration reform and win! Sections of this 150-page book concern:
  • How to Make the Case for Immigration Reform
  • How to Counter Your Opponents' Arguments
  • Understanding the Immigration System
  • Tips on Presenting Your Arguments

Congress DIRECTORY -- $2.00
