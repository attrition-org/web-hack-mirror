IDOP Material
Now Available
for YOUR USE!

Twenty-three pages of helpful material
relating to the persecuted church worldwide
are now available for you
to view, download, print and distribute.

Two Adobe PDF files are posted here in English. But you need to have the Adobe Acrobat Reader software on your computer in order to view and use them. If you do not have the latest version of the Acrobat Reader you can download it for free from this Adobe website.

Once you have the Acrobat Reader installed, click HERE for the first section of International Day of Prayer material in Adobe pdf format. Please NOTE that this is a large document (nearly 734k) and could therefore take quite a few minutes to load.

For the "Organizations" material in pdf format, click HERE. This file, which is also very large (803k), includes all the material here concerning Christian Solidarity International, Christian Solidarity Worldwide, Friends of the Martyr Church, Open Doors with Brother Andrew, Release International, and The Voice of the Martyrs.

We would also encourage you to print pages from this website for photocopying and sharing with others in your church and circle of friends.  If you do not want to download the PDF file, another way to make this information available easily is to simply print out selected pages from within your browser.  If you have a color printer you could even print a color version.  But even a non-color printer will still produce reasonable results this way.  Either way, the printed pages can then be photocopied for further distribution.

For those with e-mail, the material is available as simple e-mail text. Please send your request to:
