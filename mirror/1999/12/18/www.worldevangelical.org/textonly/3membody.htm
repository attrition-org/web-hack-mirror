World Evangelical Fellowship
Member Organizations

Uniting across geographic and cultural barriers through the World Evangelical Fellowship, God's people have found a framework for fellowship and a forum for wrestling effectively with the full implications of discipleship. Through national and regional evangelical fellowships, the churches have gained much needed visibility and the legitimacy that enables them to have a vital voice in nation building.

This document lists all regional member organizations of the World Evangelical Fellowship (national member bodies are listed under each regional link). Then affiliate organizations and associate members are listed.
