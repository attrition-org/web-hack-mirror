World Evangelical Fellowship
Ministry Departments

WEF's mission is to assist the worldwide church to disciple the nations for Jesus Christ. Some of the services WEF provides to achieve this mission are accomplished through various ministry departments, in addition to the work done by commissions.

Ministry departments at the present time include:

  • Church & Society Department
  • Leadership Development Department
  • Publications Department
