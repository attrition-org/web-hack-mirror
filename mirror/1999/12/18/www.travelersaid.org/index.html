Travelers Aid International is the association of Travelers Aid programs in the United States and Puerto Rico. Travelers Aid International's member agencies serve individuals and families in crisis due to homelessness, mobility, or other disruptive circumstances. In 1997, Travelers Aid International member agencies assisted more than 3.8 million people. In depth services including housing, transportation assistance and case work were provided to more than 150,000.
