Volunteer Connection

In many communities, volunteers are the lifeblood of Travelers Aid programs. Whether assisting stranded persons at an airport, train or bus station, helping operate a shelter, or providing some other form of assistance to persons who are disconnected from their support system, volunteers provide reassurance, helpful information, or referral to essential services.
