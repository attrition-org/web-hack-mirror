Our Stories

For nearly 150 years, Travelers Aid has been helping people - All kinds of people: Those stranded or disrupted on their journey, the homeless, families and individuals in crisis, runaway youth, all of them disconnected from traditional support systems. Their collective story is the story of Travelers Aid. We invite you to share those stories. Whether you are a volunteer, a person helped by Travelers Aid, an agency caseworker who used an innovative problem solving technique to help someone, or maybe just an observer who saw Travelers Aid offer a helping hand, we want to hear from you. Use the form below to submit your story. We will share the best of the stories on this Web site.

Here are a few RULES:

  • We need your name and address, but will not print that information if you so request.

  • Stories submitted become the property of Travelers Aid International.

  • We reserve the right to edit stories, especially for length, and to disguise real names (and even cities) to protect confidentiality.

  • Please limit your story to no more than 300 words.

  • Submission of a story does not guarantee it will be published.
