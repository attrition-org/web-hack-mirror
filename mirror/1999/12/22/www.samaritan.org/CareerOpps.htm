Career Opportunities with
Samaritan's Purse
Assistant Carolinas Field Representative

Location: Boone, North Carolina
Job Description: Opportunity for a degreed person in NC and SC area to assist in the promotion of Operation Christmas Child, a children's ministry of Samaritan’s Purse. Duties include providing administrative support in the recruitment of church participation, volunteers, and prayer and financial support as well as media promotion. Candidate will offer skills in public speaking and networking.  This person will have independent work habits and motivation as well as being multi-task oriented. Occasional travel required.

 
Assistant Director of Information Systems

Location: Boone, North Carolina
Job Description: Opportunity for a developed Information Systems person to play a strong role in day-to-day IS operations including quality control, development of policies and procedures, and review of activities. Successful candidate will be highly organized, be able to thrive in a fast-paced work environment, and possess people skills capable of working in all areas of corporate information systems.

 
Assistant Logistics Coordinator

Location: Boone, North Carolina
Job Description: Opportunity for degreed individual to coordinate and assist in managing nationwide collection network operated by volunteers for Operation Christmas Child. Duties include preparing and disseminating communications, handbooks and project materials. Successful candidate will offer knowledge of logistics, traffic and invoice analysis in addition to interpersonal skills and experience in a high-energy environment.

 
Associate Producer

Location: Boone, North Carolina
Job Description: Producer with broadcast quality writing and creative skills to write and assist in producing programs, promos, PSA's, direct response, features, and corporate videos. Must be willing to travel and be able to direct field shoots. Minimum 3-5 years experience required.

 
Attorney

Location: Boone, North Carolina
Job Description: Opportunity for a qualified staff attorney reporting to the corporate counsel. In addition to appropriate education, candidate will offer a minimum of 3 years experience specializing in corporate clients. International experience a plus.

 
Graphic Designer

Location: Boone, North Carolina
Job Description: Talented degreed person with a minimum of three years experience in print design. Responsibilities include assisting designers in development of brochures, annual reports, newsletters, posters, displays, etc. Must be proficient in PageMaker or QuarkExpress, Illustrator or Freehand, and PhotoShop. Knowledge of print production necessary, MAC experience preferred. Experience with a Christian organization a plus.

 
Producer

Location: Boone, North Carolina
Job Description: Talented producer with strong broadcast quality writing and creative skills to write and produce programs, promos, PSA's, direct response, features, and corporate videos. Must be willing to travel and be able to direct field shoots. Minimum 3-5 years experience required.

 
Secretary

Location: Boone, North Carolina
Job Description: Available position for an experienced secretary in the Projects Department. In addition to appropriate education, candidate will offer a skill set which includes computer literacy, multi-task orientation, experience in a fast paced environment, making travel arrangements as well as an understanding of administrative support.

 
Senior Programmer

Location: Boone, North Carolina
Job Description: Successful candidate will have experience in structured and event-based programming, database design and programming, Microsoft Visual Basic, and SQL-Server.  In addition, this person will have experience using a structured software modeling, design, development, and implementation process.  Also desired is familiarity with the UNIX operating system and utilities.

 
Staff Accountant

Location: Boone, North Carolina
Job Description: Seeking a degreed staff account. Duties include general accounting, reporting, and summarizing as well as specific assignments.

 
Videographer

Location: Boone, North Carolina
Job Description: Videographer with broadcast quality skills. Minimum of 3-5 years EFP/ENG experience needed. Looking for field experience in shooting stories and features. Must show creative abilities and be able to tell a story with camera shots and angles. Must be able and willing to travel extensively with short notice.

 
Volunteer and Church Coordinator

Location: Boone, North Carolina
Job Description: Opportunity for degreed individual with strong interpersonal skills to coordinate volunteer and church relationship strategies and materials nationwide for Operation Christmas Child. Duties include recruiting large numbers of volunteers and developing and implementing church recruitment strategies. Skills in marketing, meeting deadlines, and multi-task orientation a plus.
 

Interested applicants should send resume and cover letter to Tom Dyer, Director of Human Resources, at tdyer@samaritan.org or Samaritan's Purse, P.O. Box 3000, Boone, NC 28607.
