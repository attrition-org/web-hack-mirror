SUBSCRIBER SERVICES
Organizations or individuals who are not IIT, Chicago-Kent College of Law or Stuart School of Business students or faculty may become Information Center subscribers for a fee. Local subscribers may check out library materials under the Information Center's circulation policy. 

Subscriber Services also include the following: 

 eLOISe
Dial-in access to eLOISe, document delivery using imaging technology.  eLOISe is now available through the internet.  Try a search on Internet eLOISe by choosing the General Login button.

 DOCUMENT CENTER
Phone in requests to the Document Center for photocopies/faxes of cited materials (cases, statutes, law review articles, etc.) 

 REFERENCE AND RESEARCH SERVICES
International research, current awareness services, and online research conducted by the professional reference staff of the Information Center 

 CLARK
Access to ClarK, the online catalog for the holdings of the College of Law Library, the Stuart School of Business, and the Library of International Relations.  The telnet version of ClarK is currently being replaced by a web-based catalog in order to enhance its usefulness to all of our clients. 

To become a subscriber, please fill out the online form.  To find out more about the Information Center Subscriber Services Program, contact Fred Barnhart at (312)906-5600 or by email at refreq@kentlaw.edu
