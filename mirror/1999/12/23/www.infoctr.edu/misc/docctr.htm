DOCUMENT CENTER SERVICES


 
Clients of the Information Center are entitled to utilize the document delivery services of the Document Center. Subscribers may phone in requests for photocopying of materials located in the Library's collection. Materials may be picked up locally by messengers or faxed to client offices. 

COPYRIGHT COMPLIANCE
The Information Center, in conjunction with the ExLIBRIS Group, has developed software that makes monitoring copyright, reporting, and compliance with the Copyright Act easy and straightforward. The software, ExMENTIS, allows all replication (whether photocopying, microform copying, facsimile copying, or electronic copying) for Information Center clients to be tracked and appropriate license fees correctly allocated. ExMENTIS tracks direct agreements with publishers and/or rightsholders as well as indirect allocation through a registration agency, such as the Copyright Clearance Center. All appropriate reports to clients, registration agencies, publishers/rightsholders in addition to in-house management tracking reports can be generated with a few keystrokes. 

For further information on Document Center services, contact doccente@kentlaw.edu
