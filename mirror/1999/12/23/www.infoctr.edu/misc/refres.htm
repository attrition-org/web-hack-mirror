REFERENCE & RESEARCH SERVICES

The professional law librarians of the Information Center are trained in library science and have law degrees or other professional degrees. The law librarians have a wide range of expertise - legal, business, and international.

Subscriber Services includes a Research Services component. These services - provided by the professional reference staff - include:

INTERNATIONAL RESEARCH SERVICES
In-depth research, using print and online sources as well as contact with government agencies and subject specialists. Project-oriented requests include analysis and supporting citations and documentation. (Hourly Fee)

CURRENT AWARENESS SERVICES
An information tracking service for legal issues. Subscribers receive updates at scheduled intervals. (Monthly Fee)

ONLINE SEARCH SERVICES
Access to numerous commercial and academic databases. Search results available in print. (Database Costs Plus Handling Fee)

To become a subscriber of the Information Center, please use the online form, or contact the Information Center at 312-906-5600.
