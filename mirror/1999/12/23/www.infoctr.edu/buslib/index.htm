The Stuart School of Business is supported by the uniquely multi-disciplined collection within the Information Center at the Downtown Center of the Illinois Institute of Technology. The Stuart collection has been integrated with the overall Information Center collection, allowing the user to draw easily on the combined resources of the Stuart School, the Law Library, and the Library of International Relations, bringing together their outstanding resources in business, law, international relations, and the social sciences.


The Stuart collection, while wide reaching, is particularly strong in those areas supporting the programs of the Stuart Business School--financial markets and operations management. The collection is constantly being enriched in order to better serve the needs of Stuart students and faculty.


In keeping with the philosophy of the Information Center, the Stuart School of Business Library is a technologically advanced integration of traditional business resources with modern electronic storage and access technologies. CD-ROM based databases provide access to full-text images of periodical articles from over 1300 domestic and international journals, as well as company directories, investment analyses, newspaper articles, and other critical business and financial data.
