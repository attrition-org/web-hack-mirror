M-HIP CUSTOMERS

McDermitt-Humboldt Internet Provider (M-HIP) A non-profit company owned and operated by McDermitt High School students. We have two goals:

  1. To Provide meaningful entrepreneurial learning experiences for students.
  2. To Provide cost effective Internet services to residents of Humboldt County previously excluded from having access to the net.

CUSTOMER SERVICE

We sincerely hope to provide professional service to all our customers. The primary service we provide centers around the actual hookup of your hardware to the NET.

Service we can provide includes:

  • Sending software that will enable you to install the system.
  • Talking you through the installation process.
  • Giving advice concerning the hardware capacities necessary to achieve adequate internet service.

Service we can not provide includes:

  • Assisting with the installation of out-of-date or inadequate equipment. We are not equipped to make old, under-powered equipment perform like new.
  • We will not offer service if the equipment does not have the following minimal specifications:
