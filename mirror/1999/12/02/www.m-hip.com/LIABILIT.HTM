  Definition of Terms

Technician: A Technician is defined as any person instructed by or acting under the authorization of Humboldt Internet Provider to perform an operation or an assessment of a piece of hardware.

Repair: Repair is defined as the elimination of an existing problem or unsatisfactory condition of a piece of hardware.

Configuration: Configuration is defined as any setup, installation or upgrade to a piece of properly-working hardware and/or its systems.

Transport: Transport is defined as pick-up, delivery and/or physical act of transportation to and/or from the present location of a piece of hardware.

Hardware: Hardware is defined as any computer CPU, peripheral device, or accessory.

Incompatible: The term Incompatible is used to describe any hardware or operating system that is too old and/or too irregular to be made to function with our service.

Irreparable: The term Irreparable is used to describe any piece of hardware or operating system that, after assessment, attempted repair or configuration, is determined by a Technician to be beyond our help.
