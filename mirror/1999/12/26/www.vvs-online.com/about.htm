Our vision

We understand how focus drives a company because we have a very clear one - the business customer. We aim all of our products, services, and efforts toward helping our customers succeed in their business through effective management of their communications. With products ranging from analogue to digital dialers,  business telephone systems, complementary services, and other ancillary products, we are sure to be your ‘‘One-Stop Shop’’ for all of your communications needs.

Because of our focus on the business customer, our network strength, and our tradition of excellent customer service, V.V.S. has the vision and resources to become your company’ total communications service provider

Historical overview

This is an historical overview with the main events in the existence of V.V.S. sorted by year .

 1995  

May 1995 : V.V.S. is established By Mr. Vermeulen and Mr. Van der Taelen.

December 1995 : V.V.S. supplies its clients with a good price/quality equipment as well as the service for it. Two of the major audio and safety suppliers in the automotive sector in the Antwerp region decide to outsource its services to V.V.S..

 1996 

April 1996 : V.V.S. gets 2 new contracts for installations.

November 1996 :  V.V.S. Wins B.I.A.S.C.A. with a redesigned audio system for a Toyota .

 1997 

January 1997 : V.V.S. Hires an extra engineer to handle the growing demand for installation of audio and safety equipment  in the automotive sector.

March 1997 : Mr. Van der Taelen sells his chares to Mr. Vermeulen and leaves V.V.S.

June 1997 : V.V.S. experiences heavy difficulty due to a stagnating service market.

October 1997 : Mr. Vermeulen reorganizes V.V.S. and focuses on the remaining contracts. Unfortunately having to fire the personnel. 

 1998 

January 1998 : V.V.S. decides to focus on telecom products and value added services such as diallers, and PBX. Services such as installation and support are added.

February 1998 : V.V.S. Is running full speed again and hires a secretary to handle the communications flow.

August 1998 : V.V.S. hires extra personnel to support growing demand for products and services.

November 1998 : V.V.S. relocates to the present office to be able to ensure service and support for its growing carrier / reseller clientele.

 1999 

February 1999 : V.V.S. Decides to leave automotive sector completely to focus on telecom only !
