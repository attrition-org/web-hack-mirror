TASMAN SEA

TASMAN SEA, portion of the South Pacific Ocean, between the mainland of Australia and the island of Tasmania to the W and the North and South islands of New Zealand to the E. Named after its discoverer, the Dutch Indian Ocean to the W and the Coral Sea to the N. At its widest point, between Sydney, Australia, and the North Island, it extends for about 1930  km (about 1200 mi).
