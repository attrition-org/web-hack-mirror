Our Aims

This site has been put together by Dudley Council in partnership with the Dudley Gateway Project. The aim of the site is to:

  • promote the Borough, the Council and its services
  • facilitate access to Council services
  • provide information to residents, users of Council services, businesses and the general public

Disclaimer

Links to external sites have been added for the convenience of users, but Dudley Council takes no responsibility for their content.

This site has been constructed according to strict guide-lines as regards download times and ease of use. It is best viewed at a resolution of 800 by 600 pixels, but is fully useable at lower resolutions. It has been optimised for Internet Explorer 3 or 4, but works perfectly well with Netscape3 and 4.04, Opera and (if using hardware other than PC or Mac) Lynx, Voyager and AWeb.
