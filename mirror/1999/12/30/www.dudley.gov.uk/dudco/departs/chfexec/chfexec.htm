CHIEF EXECUTIVE’S DEPARTMENT

The Chief Executive is Andrew Sparke. He is head of the Council’s paid service, principal adviser on matters of policy and has overall responsibility for the coordination of the Council’s activities.

  • Contacting the Department
  • The Departments Pages

The Chief Executive’s Department supports both the Chief Executive and the work of the Council as a whole by providing a range of functions as follows.

Personnel & Support  – firstly providing expert support to the Council to enable the effective and fair management of its employees. secondly developing and driving forward Council-wide policies and initiatives, providing a focus for bidding for resources from external bodies and maintaining a plan for dealing with emergencies.

Information – promoting the work of the Council to the media and ensuring that Council Employees are kept well informed.

Committee Services – providing support for the Council’s Elected Members and managing all the meetings of the Council and its Committees.

Administration – providing a range of support services for Council Departments.

Elections & Electoral Registration – managing the electoral and democratic process including running all Local, European and General Elections and preparing the Electoral Register.

Health & Safety – providing advice to departments to ensure that the Council meets its responsibilities under the Health & Safety legislation.

Training and YT – providing a wide range of courses and consultancy advice for staff from all departments and running a Training Scheme for young people involving 60 clerical and administration trainees per year.

Telecommunications – running the Council’s central switch board and managing the telephone network in order to provide a cost-effective service.
