Under the Chief Legal and Property Officer the Department is divided into three divisions: Legal Services which provides a complete legal service to the Committees of the Council and its departments; Estates and Administration comprising valuation and estate management services, Property Client Team, Registration Service, clerical, IT and secretarial support, and local land charges; and the Property Consultancy which is a Direct Service Organisation providing a full range of professional services on property related matters, commissioned through the Client Team in the Estates and Administration Division.
