The Education Department provides educational services to pupils and adults. It is responsible for implementing national legislation, affecting pupils, parents, governors, teachers and others. Education Committee sets the policy framework and directs the work of officers and headteachers in the provision of appropriate services for the local community. The Department has established many partnerships with other Council departments and external agencies, such as Dudley Health, to maximise the resources available to pupils in the Borough. The Department has six divisions, Resource Allocation and Special Needs, Schools and Advice, Inspection, Catering Services DSO, Music Services and Community Development Unit.
