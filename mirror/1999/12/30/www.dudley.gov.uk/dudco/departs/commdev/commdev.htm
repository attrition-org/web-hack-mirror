The Community and Lifelong Learning Unit promotes the involvement of the local community in establishing a comprehensive and varied programme of educational, cultural and social opportunities which will enhance the dignity and quality of life for both young people and adults within the Borough.

It is responsible for youth and community centres, through a team of full and part time staff, and provides support and assistance to voluntary youth and community organisations.
