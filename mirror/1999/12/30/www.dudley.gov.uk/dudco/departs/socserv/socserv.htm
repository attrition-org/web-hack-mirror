Whilst the Department concerns itself with all aspects of social care, its main aim is to arrange and provide support and care services for those people and their carers who, for various reasons, are vulnerable or at risk. In doing this, the objective is to ensure that people in the Borough receive the best services which can be provided within available resources. This includes services for older people, children in need, people with mental health problems, people with a physical or sensory impairment and people with a learning disability.
