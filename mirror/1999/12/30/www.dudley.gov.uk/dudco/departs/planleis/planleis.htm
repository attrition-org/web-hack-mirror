Reporting to the Leisure and Development Committee and the Planning Committee the Department's responsibilities cover many of the services essential to the well-being and quality of life of the Borough's residents, visitors, business, industry and commerce. These services include Planning, Building Control, Economic Development, Building Conservation and Archaeology, Nature Reserves and other green issues, Parks and Public Open Space, Sports Development and Leisure Centres, Libraries and Information provision, Local History and Archives Services, Museum provision, Public Halls, the Performing Arts and Entertainment. In addition to these operational service areas the Department coordinates major projects of a multi-department nature such as the Castle Gate development.
