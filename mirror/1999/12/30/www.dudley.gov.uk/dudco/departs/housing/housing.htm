The client side of the Housing Department has its main office at the above address. The Council has defined the principal aims of its Housing Service as follows: to ensure the supply of good quality affordable homes to meet the current and future needs of the people in the Borough; to make people aware of the range of housing opportunities available; to enhance people's quality of life through the provision of housing, personal and support services; to develop a greater partnership between the Housing Department and the customers it serves; to conduct all activities of the Housing Service with sensitivity, efficiency and respect for the individual.
