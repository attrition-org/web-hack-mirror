The Finance Department is headed by the Chief Finance Officer, Mike S Williams, who has overall responsibility for administering the Council’s financial affairs. He is directly supported in his work by two Assistant Chief Finance Officers and a Management Support Unit.

The Department consists of six divisions which are responsible for a range of functions, as follows.

Audit Services – reviews the Council’s internal controls to ensure proper accountability, and value for money; provides general advice and support to Council officers in conducting their day to day work.

Benefit Services - administers a permanent case load of over 30,000 benefit claimants and pays out over £55m in Housing and Council Tax Benefit payments each year; investigates and detects benefit fraud.

Financial Services – prepares annual budgets and final accounts, assisting Council departments in managing budgets totalling over £300m; manages the Council’s borrowing (including former WMCC debt) totalling £400m; administers the Council’s payroll, risk management, creditor payments and sundry debt billing systems.

I.T. Services – manages the Council’s computer systems including hardware and software installation, providing day to day support and training in the operation of systems.

Purchasing Services – ensures Council departments obtain the best value when purchasing goods and services.

Revenue Services – administers the billing, collection and recovery of over 126,000 Council Tax accounts plus over 10,000 National Non Domestic Rate account. (Business Rates).

The Finance Department is committed to providing its customers with good service. If you have any comments to make about the services you may contact us on the e-mail addresses below.
