A-Z of Useful Telephone Numbers

The phone numbers below will connect you to the services listed without having to go through the main council switchboard first. If you can’t find or are uncertain about which Council service you need, please ring the MAIN SWITCHBOARD on (01384) 818181 – our telephonists will be happy to help.

If you have an EMERGENCY that requires Council Services outside normal office hours, please ring (01384)  818182, or, for Social Services emergencies, (01384) 456111.

If you are deaf or hard-of-hearing, you can MINICOM (text phone) us on (01384) 815273 or, outside normal office hours, on (01384) 812040.

If you are unable to get to a phone during office hours and need to cancel an appointment, ask for an application form or leave some other message, please ring our MESSAGE LINE on (01384) 815220. Your request will then be dealt with as soon as our offices re-open.
