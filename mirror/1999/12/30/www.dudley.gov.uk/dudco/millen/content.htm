The Millennium Festival

Millennium Year will be a unique opportunity to celebrate the last two thousand years and to look forward to the future.

Dudley Council would like to support community projects, celebrations and activities and has decided to do so within a framework of "Making a difference for the new Millennium." The Council is particularly keen to encourage activities which have an emphasis on young people.

The celebrations will start on New Years Eve 1999 and run until the actual start of the third Millennium on 1 January 2001.

If you would like to know more about what is being planned or if you are considering organising an event, please contact Andy Webb (Recreation Manager) on 01384 815579.
