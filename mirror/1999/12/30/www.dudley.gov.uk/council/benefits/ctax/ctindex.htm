Exemptions, Discounts and Disabled Reduction

 

You may be entitled to a:

50% discount (at least) -  for unoccupied properties

25% discount - if you live alone.

Reductions are also available if all or all but one of the people living in your home are:

STUDENTS

STUDENT NURSES

RECEIVING CHILD BENEFIT

YTS TRAINEES

APPRENTICES

SEVERELY MENTALLY IMPAIRED

PRISONERS

CARERS

HOSPITAL/HOME PATIENTS

You may also get a reduction if a person with a physical disability lives in your property* who needs a ROOM, ADDITIONAL BATHROOM or KITCHEN, due to the nature of the disability, or uses a WHEELCHAIR indoors.

* applies only to properties in band B and above

For further details please see your bill or contact:
Revenue Services Division, Dudley MBC, PO Box 3, Priory Road, Dudley, DY1 1HG
Telephone: 01384 818000 (8 lines)  or 814000.  E-mail to revenue.finance@mbc.dudley.gov.uk

Any discount or exemption entitlement is shown on your bill.  If it does not appear to be correct, please complete the on-line notification.

Direct Debits

Compared to other payment methods, monthly direct debiting offers you the following advantages:

IT SAVES YOU TIME - no more queuing to pay, no more cheques to write!

PAYMENT FOR SOMEONE ELSE - eg: an elderly relative.

GIVES YOU PEACE OF MIND - Your bank/building society make sure your bill is paid on time.

IT SAVES YOU MONEY - No more stamps to buy or envelopes to find!

LOWER ADMINISTRATION COSTS - Direct debit payers help to keep the council tax as low as possible by reducing handling costs.

FULL GUARANTEE AGAINST ERRORS - See the Direct Debit Guarantee below.

In order to pay your council tax in monthly instalments by direct debit, please complete and return the instruction on the bottom of your bill or contact the Council as soon as possible, asking for a direct debit instruction.

You will be notified of the amounts to be debited at least 14 days before the first payment date.

If you have any queries about the direct debit scheme, please telephone 01384 814000 (4 lines).

The Direct Debit Guarantee

This guarantee is offered by all the banks and building societies that take part in the direct debit scheme. The efficiency and security of the scheme is monitored and protected by your own bank/building society.

If the amounts to be paid or the payment dates change, you will be told of this in advance by at least 14 days as agreed.

If an error is made by Dudley MBC or your bank or building society, you are guaranteed a full and immediate refund from your branch.

You can cancel a direct debit at any time by writing to your bank/building society. Please also send a copy of you letter to us.

This guarantee is offered by all the banks and building societies that take part in the direct debit scheme.

If you require help or further information, please telephone our Help Line on 01384 818000 (8 lines) or 814000.  You can also e-mail Revenue Services on revenue.finance@mbc.dudley.gov.uk
