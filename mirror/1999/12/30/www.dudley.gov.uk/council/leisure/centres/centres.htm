DUDLEY'S LEISURE CENTRES

This page has information on the following centres:

  • Brierley Hill Leisure Centre
  • Coseley Swimming Pool & Supachute
  • Crystal Leisure Centre
  • Dudley Leisure Centre
  • Halesowen Leisure Centre

Dudley's Leisure Centres are based in Brierley Hill, Coseley, Dudley, Halesowen and Stourbridge.

All five centres have swimming pools, run numerous keep fit and aerobic classes, provide a wide range of activities for children both during and outside of school holidays and have a variety of rooms available for private hire.

The Crystal Leisure Centre is the newest and largest in the group. Its main attraction is its leisure pool with wave machine, flumes, rapids and bubble seat. The centre also has an indoor climbing wall and a licensed bar.

Dudley Leisure Centre has the greatest provision for squash and runs an active squash league, it is also the only centre with an indoor artificial pitch ideal for five-a-side football.
