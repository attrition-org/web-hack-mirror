DISCOVER THE KEY TO LEISURE

If you live in the Dudley Borough area and are:

A registered Jobseeker and in receipt of Jobseekers Allowance
In receipt of Family Credit, Income Support, Housing Benefit, Council Tax Benefit, Disability Working Allowance
A Youth Trainee/Training for Work trainee / Project 2000 nurse
A full time Student (over 16 years)


You may join the KEY TO LEISURE MEMBERSHIP SCHEME
which offers you and your dependants discounts on activities and events in the Borough of Dudley.

YOUR KEY TO LEISURE IN DUDLEY
Sets you free to enjoy a whole range of leisure activities and saves you money.

HOW TO JOIN
Call at your local Leisure Centre, Swimming Pool or any main Library, Monday-Friday with an up-to-date passport type photograph for yourself and each dependant, and proof of address and eligibility.   Then fill in the Key To Leisure Application Form, pay your joining fee(s) of just £1 per member (full or dependant) and start straight away.

CATEGORY PROOF OF ELIGIBILITY REQUIRED
Registered Jobseeker Giro Cheque / Letter
Family Credit Benefit Book
Income Support Benefit Book or Giro Cheque letter
Housing Benefit Council notification letter (HB 94)
Council Tax Benefit Council notification letter (HB 94)
Disability Working Allowance Benefit book or Giro Cheque letter
Youth Trainee Employer's letter
Training for Work trainee Employer's letter
Project 2000 nurse Employer's letter
A full time Student (over 16 years) 'Full time' Student Union Card or College Enrolment letter

THE DISCOUNTS OF OFFER
Dudley Libraries Half price reservations, compact disc, cassette and video hire
Sports Development 10 - 25% reduction on Dudley Sports Development coaching courses
Events Half price adult & child admission to Dudley Show & Dudley Fireworks
Arts & Entertainment Concessionary price for Key to Leisure members for advance tickets.
'On the day' tickets (subject to availability) at £2.00 per ticket
Parks Half price Tennis, Bowls and Croquet
Leisure Centres Half price Swimming, Badminton, Squash/Racketball, Weights Room/Fitness Room, Bowls, Snooker, Roller-skating, Table Tennis, Climbing and Spectators on a casual 'no-prior booking' basis.   KTL is accepted at the following: Brierley Hill, Coseley, Crystal, Dudley, & Halesowen Leisure Centres, Coseley Swimming Pool, Cradley High School, Thorns, Hillcrest & Leasowes School & Community Colleges
Adult Education Cheaper fees on some adult education courses at many local schools and colleges.
Leisure Goods Discounts at some local leisure goods retailers
