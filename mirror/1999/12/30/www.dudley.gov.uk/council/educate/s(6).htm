Admissions to all of the following schools (with the exception of Bishop Milner) are administered by Dudley LEA. You may express a preference for any one of the listed secondary schools in the Borough of Dudley.

Please note that:

All secondary schools are comprehensive

All schools are non-denominational unless otherwise stated,

CE = Church of England
RC = Roman Catholic
A county school is a school which is maintained by the LEA. Aided schools and special agreement schools are voluntary schools, i.e. they are maintained by the LEA but not established by them.
The column headed 'Type' refers to the different classifications of school,
C = County
VA = Voluntary Aided
SA = Special Agreement
GM = Grant Maintained
