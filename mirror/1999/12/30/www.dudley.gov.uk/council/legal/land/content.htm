Land Charges

Introduction to the Local Land Charges Service

The Local Land Charges Service is provided for those purchasing property and land within the Dudley Borough boundary. The purpose of the service is to disclose to the prospective purchaser information, which may affect a particular property or plot of land.

The Local Land Charges section maintains a Register that is updated daily in order to provide answers to Search requests. The section also obtains information from relevant parts of the Council such as Planning, Building Inspectors, Environmental Services, Private Sector Housing and Highways. (We have our own Highway Authority and are agents for Severn Trent Water Authority.)

We have a Charter Mark for excellent service that we won in December 1996. We were the first Metropolitan Borough Local Land Charges team to achieve this award.

A Search is in 2 Parts

The first part is called the LLC1, which deals with all registerable charges, for example, financial charges (registered against property/land by the Authority), improvement/renovation grants, tree preservation orders, conservation orders and planning applications, which have been approved with conditions as from 1st August 1977.

The second part, known as the Con 29, is a standard form, which deals with 'Additional Enquiries', relating to highways, sewers, planning and environmental health history which may affect the property/land.

Information provided in response to a Search/Enquiry, which is part of a conveyancing procedure, is set down in legislation and in standard questions agreed between the Law Society and Local Government organisations.

Personal Search Applications

If you wish to make a Personal Search of the Local Land Charges Register/Councils Statutory Registers, we offer a personal appointment system between 9.00am and 4.30pm Monday to Friday. There is a statutory charge for this service.

If a full Personal Search is required, five days notice for an appointment is necessary. You will receive answers to enquiries on the LLC1 form and the Con 29 form.

Submission of Searches

Both the LLC1 and two Con 29 forms should be submitted at the same time if answers to both are required.

Please note that Searches must include the following when submitted to be accepted for processing:-

  • The correct fee made payable to Dudley M.B.C. (see fee list).
  • Give accurate full postal address, including full postcode if possible, of the property to be searched against.
  • Submit two plans of ordnance survey quality showing the boundaries of the property/land to be searched against clearly marked in red. If plans are NOT received we are unable to give our service standard guarantee.
  • If you have a DX number, please ensure you quote it on all Searches submitted. This helps keep costs down and gets the Search back to you more quickly.
