What can I borrow ?

Books

There are over 600,000 books available for loan, ranging from popular fiction for adults and children to postgraduate study material. All holdings are listed in the library catalogue. Books can be borrowed free of charge, but in most cases there is a small charge for audio and video hire.
There are also Asian language books (Punjabi, Urdu, Hindi and Gudjerati) available for loan at Dudley, Halesowen, Long Lane, Lye and Stourbridge, together with a collection of French, Spanish, German and Italian novels at Brierley Hill, Dudley, Halesowen and Stourbridge libraries.

Audio

There are over 30,000 audio items available from selected libraries. These include cassettes, and CDs, ranging from pop music to the classics together with a wide range of books on tape.

Video

Over 3,000 titles are available, from selected libraries. The collections include feature films, instructional videos and videos for children.

 

Loan period

Adults can borrow up to 8 books in one visit together with 4 audio items and 2 videos. Books are loaned for 4 weeks, audio items for 2 weeks and videos for 1 week.

Children can borrow up to 5 books at a time together with up to 3 books on tape; all are loaned for 4 weeks.

Should you wish to extend the loan of any item this can be done by post, ‘phone, fax, e-mail or in person at any library, provided the items have not been reserved by other users. If the items you wish to borrow are not immediately available, you can reserve them on payment of a small fee and they will be obtained for you.
