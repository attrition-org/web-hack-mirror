How to use the library

 

You can use any library in Dudley just by going into it. The enquiry services, study facilities, books, newspapers etc. can be used, completely free of charge.

It also costs nothing to become a member of the library, which enables you to borrow library items for home use.

To join, take two forms of identification to your nearest library, complete the registration form and you will be given a ticket that you can use straight away, and which can be used in any library in Dudley.

(Library users under the age of 18 must have their application endorsed by a parent or guardian).
