PLANNING SERVICES Back to main Social Services Department Page

Each year Dudley Social Services Department and Dudley Health Authority consults with users and carers of services in order to help plan services for the future. Following this, two major planning documents are produced; the Community Care Plan which covers services for adults and the Children's Services Plan which covers services for children.
