Services for Householders

The Council collects almost one tonne of household waste from each of the Borough’s homes each year. It provides over 100 recycling sites and over 3,500 tonnes of waste are recycled each year. The Council also deals with industrial air noise and land contamination and ensures that industrial developments do not harm the environment.

Refuse Collection

We will collect your rubbish on the same day each week and tell you in advice of any changes to collection days. We will collect missed rubbish on the same day if you tell us before 12.00 noon or the next day if you call after 12.00 noon. We also provide free special collection service twice a year to collect bulky waste, including garden rubbish and for a charge will remove larger items of rubbish eg: sofas and fridges within eight working days of your request.

To report missed collections or to request chargeable ‘special collections’ Ring 01384 814633

Pest Control Service

We provide a free pest control service for rats, mice, cockroaches and bed bugs. A chargeable service is available for wasps and fleas.We aim to collect stray dogs within four hours of you contacting us – between 8am and 2pm Monday to Friday.

To request the above services Ring 01384 814786/7

Blocked Drains

We will try to clear blocked drains on the next working day for which a call out charge is made.

To request this service Ring 01384 814633
