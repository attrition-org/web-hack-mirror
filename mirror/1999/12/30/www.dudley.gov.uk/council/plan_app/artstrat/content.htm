DUDLEY PUBLIC ART STRATEGY

                       


The Council has recently published its Public Art Strategy.  The strategy will guide and develop the Council's public art service in the Borough through to 2001.

In producing the strategy, the Council has built upon ten years of experience through 270 commissions in public art mostly carried out through its award winning Borough Public Artist initiative.

Whilst the focus of the Council's public art activity has remained firmly within the Borough, it has also acted as consultant to Councils from Shropshire through to South Wales as well as to other public bodies including the regional Passenger Transport Authority.

The Strategy builds upon this experience and provides a definition for public art, outlines how public art relates to the Council's corporate agenda and sets out the various commissioning and delivery mechanisms used by the Council to pursue and develop its approach to public art in addition to setting objectives for future development.
