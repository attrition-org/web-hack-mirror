1. INTRODUCTION

This document is a strategy for the protection and enhancement of canals in Dudley.

Its overall aim is to raise awareness, set standards, promote renewal, investment and partnerships and to generate discussion on potential funding sources and partnerships and priorities for attention.

The following pages introduces the Draft Strategy and the complete copy has the following contents:

  • Introduction
  • Position Statement
  • Definitions
  • Guidelines for Canalside Development
  • Restoration of Former Canals
  • Corridor Studies
  • Funding and Implementation
