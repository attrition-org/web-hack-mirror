Roads in the Borough

Dudley Council is responsible for the maintenance of 550 miles of roads in the Borough 1240 miles of footpaths and 33,000 street lights and signs.We carry out a programme of regular repair and maintenance work but sometimes problems occur which we do not know about. In these circumstances it is helpful to receive reports from residents.
