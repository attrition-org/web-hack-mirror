I.T. Services Division

"To help the Council meet its business objectives and deliver best value by exploiting Information and Communications Technology."

I.T. Services Division is a Division of the Council’s Finance Department and is based at three sites within the borough. The Division provides a range of services in Information and Communications Technology(I.C.T.). Services are based around a council wide data communications network connecting all council’s desktop equipment and servers. Services are based around an ICL VME mainframe, an number of Unix Servers and an increasing number of Windows NT servers. The Division is split into the following 5 sections:-

  1. Business and Personnel Section
  2. Production Services Section
  3. Information Systems Section
  4. Strategy and Best Value Section
  5. Customer Services Section
Business and Personnel Services Section
  • To allow customers of IT services Division to easily purchase goods and services provided.
  • To ensure efficient and effective business methods are used with the I.T. Services Division which are capable of supporting the internal market and delivering "Best Value".
  • To develop and maintain accurate information records to enable the management and control of the Council’s hardware and software assets.
  • To provide a focus for Staff Development and Training. To ensure that individuals can grow and that the I.T. Services Division and our customers benefit from better trained and more experienced staff.
  • To provide reception and administration services for the I.T. Services Division.
  • To provide training for customers of the I.T. Services Division to ensure that they are able to obtain best value for money from the systems provided.
Production Services Section
  • To provide support for the customers of the I.T. Services Division to ensure that they obtain the best value for money from the equipment and services provided.
  • To ensue that the equipment provided by the I.T. Services Division suffers least possible interruptions in service
  • To provide a support service for queries relating the use of desktop office systems products throughout the authority
  • To ensure that requests for new equipment and changes to existing equipment are carried out quickly and effectively.
  • To manage the Corporate Mainframe, Printing and Data Preparation services to provide high quality low cost services for our customers to agreed customer understandings.
  • To provide and manage the council’s Data Communication Network to allow any terminal attached to it managed access to any corporate or departmental service.
  • To provide a well managed and secure environment for housing our customers servers.
  • To provide technical support for the Operating Systems and networks software on distributed and personal computers
Information Systems Section
  • To ensure that the existing production systems continue to provide high levels of reliability and accuracy.
  • To develop those systems to meet our customers changing needs, and where necessary to develop new systems to schedules agreed, in advance, with our customers
  • To develop standards and techniques to ensure that the methodology used by the I.T. Services Division remains appropriate to the needs and technology employed by the council
  • To ensure that the council’s Geographic Information Systems continue to provide high levels of reliability and accuracy.
  • To develop solutions based on Geographic Information to meet our customers changing needs.
Strategy and Best Value Section
  • To develop a number of corporate strategies to ensure that the council continue to obtain best value from its’ investment in Information and Communications Technology.
  • To develop the council’s Electronic information strategy in the form of Web based services, e’commerce etc.
  • To ensure the council’s Web site is developed in a well structured and co-ordinated manner.
  • To ensure that the services Delivered by the I.T. Services Division are of a high quality and to benchmark our services to prove the delivery of best value
Customer Services Section
  • To provide a single point of contact, through a Help Desk, for customers of the I.T. Services Division with problems or queries relating to any of our services our products.
  • To speedily manage the resolution of problems within time scales set out in the I.T. Services Division’s Service Plan
  • To provide management information relating to the use of the Help Desk service.
  • To identify and understand the needs of customers and to ensure that the goods and services provided by the I.T. Services Division match customer’s expectations.
  • To provide a single point of contact for customers on a strategic level.
  • To build relationships between customers and I.T. Services Division.
  • To provide project co-ordination and consultancy to ensure that any projects undertaken by I.T. Services Division are delivered on time and within budget.
