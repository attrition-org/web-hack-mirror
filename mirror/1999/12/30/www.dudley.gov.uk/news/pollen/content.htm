Pollen Count

The pollen level is counted early each weekday morning during the pollen season in Dudley Town Centre. This season varies slightly each year dependant on weather conditions.
The "Pollen Count" is actually the average number of pollen grains per m3 of air over the previous 24 hours and this is classified as:- HIGH, MEDIUM or LOW.

The count, in conjunction with the local weather forecast is used to give a pollen forecast for the next 24 hours.


The pollen count has now finished.
It will be restarted in Spring 2000.
