Dudley Information Centre welcomes tourists and visitors to Dudley.  We can help with information about places to visit, accomodation, eating out, shopping, tickets for many local events and travel - all the things you need to make your visit enjoyable!

Dudley Information Centre has a wide range of leaflets and information about Council and other public services.  The community information system maintained by Dudley Libraries can also be accessed here.

The Centre is also a source of tourism, travel and holiday information with leaflets to take away and timetables, guidebooks and other information which can be referred to.

Tickets for many local events can be purchased, including telephone sales by credit or debit card. 

Last year the Centre had almost 37,000 visitors, received over 11,000 phone calls and made one and a half thousand accommodation enquiries for visitors.  Why not try it out?
