Trading on the Net

The use of the Internet as a trading medium is an exciting prospect for the Council and we are in the process of making information more easily accessible to both customers and potential suppliers and contractors.

Our future plans include:

  • Providing detailed specifications and descriptions for all contracts.
  • Providing contact points for specific requirements (i.e. the contact name and address for requesting tenders for the supply of photocopiers).
  • Advertising tender dates and deadlines.
  • Enabling suppliers and contractors to request tenders through the e-mail facility.
  • Placing orders and receiving invoices on the net.
