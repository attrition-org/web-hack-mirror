The Black Country at War
16 October - 28 October 2000
Sixty years after the beginning of the Second World War, Dudley Museum and Art Gallery will commemorate the role of the Black Country and its people in the conflict, in a dramatic and moving exhibition.  As well as life on the Home Front, with its rationing, make-do and mend, air raids and the blackout, the exhibition will look at the enormous contribution made by women and local industry to the war effort.  It will also feature the experiences of individuals involved in Civil Defence such as Home Guards and ARP wardens.

Dudley Society of Artists Annual Exhibition
6 November - 11 December
Local artists show an exciting selection of their most recent work in a wide variety of media.  The majority of pictures on display are for sale and this is an ideal opportunity to buy original artwork at a very reasonable price.

Dudley Camera Club 
18 December - 22 January 2000
Dudley Camera Club, which is now entering its 52nd year, aims to promote photography at all levels and is one of the leading clubs in the Midlands.  This annual exhibition of photographs displays a diverse range of topics and will be complemented by an audio visual presentation.
