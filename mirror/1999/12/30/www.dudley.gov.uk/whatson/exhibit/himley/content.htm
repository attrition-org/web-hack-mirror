Strangely Familiar
24 July - 26 September
Have you ever looked at a painting , drawing or sculpture and thought it strangely familiar?  Throughout the centuries artists have borrowed inspiration from fellow artists.  Many paintings, etchings and sculptures have been reworked and interpreted.  Artists have applied new skills and contemporary perspectives to the work of their predecessors.
Recognising that art does not exist in isolation from other art has inspired Nottingham based artist, Philip Cox, to create humorous tributes and interpretations of some of the art world's most famous pieces in the form of life-size paper sculptures.
