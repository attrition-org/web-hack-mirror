Lo-Co Glass
20 November - 16 January 2000


Flame Decanters by C. Hawkins

An exhibition of work by Colin Hawkins and Louise Edwards, winners of the 1999 Broadfield House Studio Scholarship.  Colin trained at Sunderland and the Royal College of Art and Louise at Goldsmiths College and the International Glass Centre, Brierley Hill.  They set up their own business, Lo-Co Glass in 1998, and have been assisting at the Blow Zone studio in Amblecote in return for studio time to make their own work.
Colin and Louise have already marketed several successful ranges such as their decorative fruit scent bottles and colourful flame decanters, but the exhibition will also include new work that they will be developing during their year in the scholarship studio.
