Legacy is a highly specialized prepaid legal program whose exclusive focus is estate planning... Legacy is administered by the Foundation for Financial Education (FFE)...Since 1980 FFE has advocated the use of Living Trusts as an effective estate planning tool to eliminate probate, save estate taxes, and make certain your wealth is distributed according to your wishes...FFE is a nonprofit organization whose mission is to provide individuals and families access to quality estate planning at an affordable price.

Using Legacy to create your living Trust and other documents is one of the most important steps you will ever take. It will literally change your life...Most people don't like to think about their mortality or even the idea of becoming disabled or incompetent...The ability of your trust to bridge the gap between life and death is surely one of its most remarkable characteristics.

Your living trust is a thoughtful and considerate gift for those you love...The time and effort on your part to create your documents will be greatly appreciated by your family.

Your membership in Legacy will be invaluable to help you maintain and keep your documents current and up to date.

The Foundation for Financial Education (FFE) was created in 1980 as a national nonprofit educational organization in response to the need for quality, easily accessible estate planning services.

Under the guidance of a National Advisory Board consisting of leaders from government, industry, education, and consumer groups, FFE developed Legacy Prepaid Legal Program enabling anyone to create and manage their estate plan.
