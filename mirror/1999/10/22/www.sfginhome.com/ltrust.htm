Properly done, the Living Trust will avoid the Probate process, thereby saving TIME and MONEY. Unlike probated Wills, which are readily available for public review, the Living Trust maintains privacy.
  • It allows you to maintain CONTROL over all the assets in the trust during your lifetime.
  • It avoids the problems and risks of joint ownership.
  • It protects minors from court imposed guardianships, protects dependents with special needs; and is almost impossible to contest.
  • It prevents a conservatorship at disability or incompetence.
  • It provides various other benefits and can even be an effective prenuptial agreement.
