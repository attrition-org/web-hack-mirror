California’s fee schedule for attorneys is found at Probate Code Section 10810 and for executors is found at Probate Code Section 10800 it can be a complicated formula. For ease of approximation, a simplified formula can be used. The formula can be applied to determine the probate cost on estates between $100,000 and $1 million.  The formula is determined by multiplying the gross estate by 2% and then adding $1150.  This result would equal the statutory attorney fee.  The executor is also entitled to this same fee; therefore, the formula is written this way:  [(gross estate value x 2%) plus $1150] x 2 = approximate probate cost.
