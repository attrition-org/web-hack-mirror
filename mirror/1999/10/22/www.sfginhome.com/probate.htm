Probate is a process where the courts transfer your assets from your name to your heirs name...The problem with probate in California is that it can take up to 2 years or longer to get your assets out of the probate process and this process can eat up 5% to 10% or more of your gross estate...The only people who win during the probate process is the attorney’s who gets to probate your estate...In California if you own as little as $20,000 of real estate your assets may be subjected to probate.

Is this what you want to happen to your assets for your family?
This can all be avoided with proper estate planning!!
