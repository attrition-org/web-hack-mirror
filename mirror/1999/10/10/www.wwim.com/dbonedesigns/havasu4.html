Havasu Falls  
Havasupai Indian Reservation, Arizona  

This beautiful spot at the west end of the Grand Canyon in Arizona is heaven on earth for me. The people are good hearted, mindful, and respectful of one another. On the 8 mile hike into this lavious area, I "blew" out my big toes being forced to wear sandals the rest of the trip. Let's just say that clinging to large rocks with bare feet while climbing them was a new adventure to me.
