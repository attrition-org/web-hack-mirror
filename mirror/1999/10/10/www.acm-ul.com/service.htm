U.L. Monitoring Station
-Our Services-

 

A professional dispatcher is constantly monitoring your protected premises, 24-hours a day, seven days a week. In the event of an alarm, our fully automated system instantly displays all information to determine the nature of your emergency and respond quickly and efficiently according to your instructions.

Our ultra-modern, U.L. approved central station facility is one of the largest in the country and utilizes some of the fastest Intel P6 mainframes along with fourth generation custom software by Dice Corp., for state-of-the-art monitoring. Highly sophisticated telecommunications equipment and ergonomically designed work stations keep our dispatchers connected to and focused on your protection.
