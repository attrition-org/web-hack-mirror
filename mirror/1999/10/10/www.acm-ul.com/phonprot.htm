-Phone Line Protection-

 

ACM is one of the leaders in redundancy and definitely does not fall short when it comes to phone line protection.

THE PLAN - When we planned our phone system, in addition to redundancy in the switch itself, we provided for full redundancy for the way the phone lines are delivered to our facility.

At ACM, we have several completely separate, independent T-1 phone cables coming into our facility from long distance and local carriers. They approach us from opposite directions with both local and long distance lines and route to separate, independent central offices of the phone company.

In the event that there is ever a problem on a phone line path or with a phone company central office, the calls of ACM automatically switch to the other.

THE RESULT - Whatever the weather or emergency, ACM continues to provide the vital service and communications that you have come to expect and deserve without interruption.
