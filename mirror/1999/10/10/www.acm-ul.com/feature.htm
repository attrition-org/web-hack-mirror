-BACK-UP POWER & SYSTEMS-
We are equipped with three separate sources of back-up power:

1. Five uninterruptable Power Supplies (UPS).

2. Two automatic 50 kW Generators with manual override, in a totally enclosed alarmed area.

3. Three Complete Rechargeable Inverter System with 6000Watts output that will keep us functional (completely functional) for a minimum of 24 hours, as a backup to the generator. All of these back-up power systems are maintained and tested weekly to ensure they are in excellent working order. Our telephone cables feed to us underground directly from the phone company.

We also have two totally redundant computer systems operating simultaneously. With two sub system back-up computers we are a fully automated U.L. Approved Central Station. We have had our U.L. approval for over twelve years.
