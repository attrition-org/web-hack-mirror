U.L. Monitoring Station

 

-Who We Are-
We have been in business at the same location with the same owners for 20 years. We are in our own modern 10,000 square-foot facility. Our building was designed and built to well exceed U.L. requirements. The cement blocks have steel rebarb wire with cement poured into every opening to make a vault-like structure. On our roof, we have lightning rods with thick copper ground cables to protect us against lightning strikes. We also have complete electrical suppression throughout the building to protect against spikes or grounds. Two full building generators, five ups (backup) systems and three invertors for backup power.

"Service & then Some!" Service is our product. Our company management and employees are dedicated to providing service second to none. Total customer satisfaction is our commitment to our customers.

We have a complete automatic water sprinkler system on both floors with a "Class A" fire system consisting of smoke and rate-of-rise detectors and pull stations throughout the building. For added security, we are monitored by another Central Station.
