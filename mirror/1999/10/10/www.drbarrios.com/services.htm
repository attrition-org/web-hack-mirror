Fortunately, surgery can correct many orthopaedic problems, however, in many instances surgery is not always necessary. Orthopaedic examination, X-rays, MRI, and other diagnostic tools allow our physicians to map out the best care and treatment options for our patients. We realize that each patient's injury is unique to that individual; whether it be a general orthopaedic condition such as a broken finger or a traumatic condition such as a fractured hip. Each patient will receive the best orthopaedic care available.
