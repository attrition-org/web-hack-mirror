Your Global Pacific DSL connection is... FAST!

You can access the Internet at speeds ranging from 144 KbPS up to 1.5 MbPS.

 

 

The phone line to your location is often referred to as "the last mile." This "last mile" of copper wiring has long been the bottleneck to providing fast data services to homes and businesses--until now. DSL utilizes a line-coding process that for the first time allows very high-speed, affordable connections over this last mile.

Always Connected: In addition to tremendous speed, DSL connections are "always on" or "always connected". This means that users don't need to dial up each time they want a connection to the network.

Secure Connection: DSL uses dedicated copper lines that are not shared with your neighbors.

Multiple DSL technologies that can be rapidly deployed: Global Pacific Internet provides solutions based on Asymmetric DSL (ADSL), Symmetric DSL (SDSL), and ISDN DSL (IDSL) technologies. This allows Global Pacific Internet to provide a DSL solution regardless of the installation location. Typically, ADSL and SDSL equipment can not be installed on lines greater that 18,000 feet from the central office or on lines that have repeaters or Digital Loop Carriers (DLC). However, IDSL can typically be provisioned on these lines. By supporting multiple DSL technologies, Global Pacific Internet can provide a fast, always connected solution to all of its customers regardless of location or line quality.

IDSL also uses the same equipment as ISDN, making it easy to migrate from ISDN to the always-connected, affordable IDSL service (GPI-IDSL144).
