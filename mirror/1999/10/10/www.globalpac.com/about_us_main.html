Who We Are

Global Pacific Internet was originally a sales and service computer store. In December, 1995, we added a full service Internet division and advertised unlimited Internet access services for well below the national average price. Since we offer superior access and our price was substantially below the $30 average charged by competitors, our growth has been astronomical. We soon were forced to add more skilled personnel and move the Internet division from the retail store into the deluxe office facilities at Nexus City Square. We have added more servers and offer many different Internet and intranet solutions to individuals and companies. Solutions include unlimited Internet access, web design, web hosting, frame relay, routers, CSU/DSU, and excellent technical support. Our clients are not only very happy with the quality of our work and service, they are even more satisfied with our low prices and friendly service.

Why Choose Us?

Global Pacific is a complete Internet and Intranet Provider. Our staff has a wealth of knowledge in Internet and Intranet technology and service. Global Pacific is a well established business, we will be here today, tomorrow, and far in the future. Our prices are fair and simple to understand. Customer satisfaction is our number one concern.
