What is a Co-Location ?

To Co-Locate simply means to put one or more servers directly on a LAN system that has a direct connection to the Internet backbone.

Co-Location is the way to go for many companies these days. This allows your customers to access your Internet site at the fastest rates possible with the highest amount of traffic your machine can handle 24 hours per day.

Global Pacific will let you locate your web servers, your email servers, your commerce servers, your game servers, and almost any other type of server directly on a LAN connected to the Global Pacific Internet backbone. Your company will easily be allowed up to 5 GB of data transfer a month at bursts of 3 mb per second with out any additional charges occurring. This simply means for many companies that they will have easy, reliable, 24 hour internet access at rates well below the cost of running a dedicated access line to your location.

Global Pacific is a flexible diversified Internet Company. We have multiple T1 lines from many different companies to ensure redundancy. This means your system will always be connected to the Internet backbone and people that you allow to connect to your system will be able to do so from any place at any time of the day or night.
