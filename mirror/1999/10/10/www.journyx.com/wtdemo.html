journyx Timesheet Online Demo

This Online Demo of journyx Timesheet is a fully functional version of the product. You can log in as a typical employee, a typical accountant, a typical manager, or as the system administrator. Each has an interface suited to their particular needs.
  • Employees: (Users) can see and report on their own time, and modify it.
  • Accountants: (Reporters) can report on anyone's time but only modify their own.
  • Managers: (Managers) oversee a defined group of users.
  • Administrators: (Admins) can do anything, including adding and deleting users, projects, codes, and subcodes.
