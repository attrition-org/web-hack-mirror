  • FREETIMESHEET.COM Service Launched by Journyx, Powered by IBM & Linux

    AUSTIN, Texas, USA, – August 3, 1999 - Journyx, the marketshare leader in web-based timesheet and project tracking solutions for the enterprise, announces the release of FREETIMESHEET.COM, the world’s first free integrated timesheet and expense report solutions portal.   • journyx names William Leake Vice President of Marketing and Sales

    AUSTIN, Texas, USA, - August 2, 1998 - Journyx, the worldwide leader in web-based time tracking solutions, today announced that William Leake has been named vice president of marketing and sales, where he will oversee product management, marketing communications efforts, strategic development, and product sales. For the past two years, Leake headed both international sales and domestic channel development for internet security vendor Haystack Labs and, following Haystack's acquisition by firewall leader Trusted Information Systems (TIS) in October 1997, directed all business development for TIS in Canada and Latin America (Rest of Americas). At Haystack, he was responsible for more than 65 percent of total corporate revenues, and grew both domestic and international channels more than 10-fold during his tenure, which allowed the company to be acquired on very favorable terms.

  • journyx Timesheet 2.0 is released

    AUSTIN, Texas, USA, July 13, 1998 - Journyx, the worldwide leader in web-based time & project tracking solutions, today announced industry-leading functionality and flexibility in its release of journyx Timesheet 2.0, available immediately. journyx Timesheet 2.0 provides a number of enhancements, including NT support, which extend its lead over other time-tracking products in the marketplace.
