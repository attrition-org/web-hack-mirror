journyx Timesheet Features

Security

  • No part of journyx Timesheet is accessible without a password
  • Four levels of user authority:
    • Users can create and modify their own time
    • Reporters can view and report on any time record, but only modify their own
    • Managers can oversee and manage a defined group of users
    • Admin (System Administrators) can modify anything, add and delete users, projects, codes, and subcodes
  • Configurable user access through the use of groups
  • Administrators can configure password strength and aging to meet your company's security requirements
  • Easily create an Audit Log of all actions made to the journyx Timesheet database

Time Entry, Cost, Expenses, and Billing

  • Customize the journyx Timesheet color scheme for your company.
  • Customize time entry screens for particular users or groups
  • Rename, re-populate, or remove fields as appropriate for your employees or your enterprise
  • Create Memorized Time Entries to make entering time faster
  • Globally create, modify, delete, and commit Time and Expense Records
  • Track Project status, Time and Attendance, Activities, Clients, Billings, and much more
  • Quickly and easily import and export time information between journyx Timesheet and Microsoft Project
  • Enter payroll or billing rates specific to each employee
  • Keep track of all work on a project, plans, etc. using project journals

Reporting

  • Highly configurable internal reports
  • Over 25 standard internal reports
  • Maximum reporting flexibility achieved through the use of Crystal ReportsTM or SQL queries
  • Export reports into most popular accounting, payroll, and project management software
  • Run and email reports at Administrator defined intervals

Integration

  • Easily export information into your existing payroll, project management, and accounting software
  • journyx Timesheet ships with a default database; however, integration is available with all ODBC compliant databases
  • Integration is available with your existing corporate systems

Additional Features

  • Year 2000 Compliant
