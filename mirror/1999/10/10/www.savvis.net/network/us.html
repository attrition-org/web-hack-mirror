A Network Built For Speed

What differentiates SAVVIS from other global internetworking providers? Our unique Internet network architecture. The SAVVIS ProActive NetworkSM speeds your Internet data through a controlled environment and completely bypasses the congested public Internet exchange points. Your data never stops flowing, never even slows down.

  • Greater Redundancy. SAVVIS maintains multiple DS-3/OC-3 circuits to each Point of Presence (POP), and three or more DS-3/OC-3 interconnects and multiple private peers at each of its PrivateNAPsSM.
  • Most Direct Route. You get direct "on net" connectivity to the major backbones of the Internet. With our PrivateNAPs strategically located throughout the country, your data is usually just one or two hops from its destination network.
  • Maximum Reliability. SAVVIS reroutes your traffic if the availability or performance of a POP, interconnect or peering partner connection suffers.
  • Fastest Access. Avoid the clogged public MAEs and Naps SAVVIS speeds your data through our controlled, performance-guaranteed PrivateNAPs to completely bypass congested public Internet exchange points.
