Retention and Transition Division

Mission and METL

 

MISSION STATEMENT:

Increase readiness by employing effective methodology to:

  • retain quality soldiers;
  • transition soldiers into the USAR Selective Reserve;
  • stabilize the force to meet the end strength objective;
  • reduce the outlay of resources to attract, access and train quality soldiers.

 

MISSION ESSENTIAL TASK LIST (METL):

  • Proponent for the Office of the Chief, Army Reserve, Retention and Transition Program
  • Publish,monitor and validate retention, transition and warrant officer mission objectives
  • Provide Office of the Chief, Army Reserve, Retention and Transition Program Regulatory and Policy Guidance
  • Identify and minimize retention and transition distractors
  • Create, implement and sustain retention and transition recognition programs
  • Encourage development and implementation of new and proactive efforts to retain and transition soldiers
