    Q. Can I run cgi scripts on my free site?
    A. You can't install cgi scripts on the server however we do provide links to sites that will host cgi's for you for free so all you need to do is paste some html on your page and you have a working cgi script!
    We are working on a web interface now which will allow you to install certain scripts though - a web board script is already setup for you.

    Q. Can I put pay banners on my free site?
    A. No! We are able to offer free hosting only because we charge for advertising on sites on our servers. If we allowed sites to put loads of other banners up then we would lose money and so not be able to support free hosting.

    Q. Can I have my own domain name?
    A. Yes! We don't charge for this but you will have to register (or transfer) the domain yourself and then let us know so we can map it to our servers.

    Q. How do I get more disk space?
    A. If you have a very busy site we will give you more space for free or you can purchase additional disk space for $10/month for 20Mb.

    Q. Can you remove the banners from my site?
    A. If you upgrade to a Gold Membership for just $10/month we will remove all advertising from your site, grant ftp access and full cgi privileges.

    Q. Can I have an email address aswell?
    A. Of course! Simply sign up for a FREE Wigloo Mail account. This is a private web based email system which also collects mail from all your other accounts!

    If you have any further questions please contact us.
