Welcome to SPOTCHECK Online.

SPOTCHECK® is an electronic insurance benefit verification service developed by Insurance Benefit Spot Check, Inc. At this site you can learn more about the company and its Y2K compliance statement, its provider products and payor services.

Users registered for SPOTCHECK internet services can also use this site to send online eligibility requests to selected payors.

SPOTCHECK is pleased to announce that it now supports eligibility access to Lifeguard. Lifeguard access includes its HMO, PPO, and POS Plans. In addition, through the SPOTCHECK POS or PC products, providers may now access the Blue Shield of California Medicare Plan 65 HMO. These payors greatly enhance our California coverage along with payors such as Health Net, Blue Cross of California, PacifiCare, and others.
