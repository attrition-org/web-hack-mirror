Haiti is one of the worst places in the Western hemisphere to live.
Thanks, in large part, to the unending misapplied intervention 
by the United States political leadership, which has allowed
successive despotic governments of Haiti 
to trample of the basic human rights of Haiti's people,
especially the children.
Again and again the people of Haiti have endured
the robbery of their meager wealth by
corrupt leadership supported by the 
United States government. 
Now Haiti is one of the poorest nations on earth.
In the early days of the AIDS crisis Haiti was maligned
in the U.S. and Haitians endured extreme acts 
of discrimination when the Haitian people were 
misidentified in the U.S. media as carriers of the deadly virus.
Now children are sick, the water is filthy, the
infrastructure is in ruins and the new Haitian government
is once again under the thumb of a directionless
U.S. foreign policy.
We have asked ourselves what can we do to help these people?
After careful consideration we decided that an organization 
called World Vision was the best way.


World Vision has been working in Haiti for years.
Going from village, to city, bringing help to those
children and families who are most in need.
They are firmly established in the country
and are the best hope these children have 
for a future free of starvation,
disease and ignorance.

A percentage of your Membership contribution
will go directly to World Vision
and specifically help the children
whose photos we include here.

Perhaps one day Haiti will be the island paradise it once 
was and it will be in part because of your help.

We are offering our help by sponsoring individual 
children through World Vision. 
We are in no other way associated with World Vision.
We do not necessarily share the opinions or beliefs
of World Vision but we feel that World Vision
is the most effective instrument
for allowing us to directly help the children of Haiti.
