ABOUT MEMBERSHIP
 

    Q: What is "Membership"?

About Profiles/Personals
  • It’s free to submit a Profile and Personals and it’s free to browse them but you must be a Member to respond to any of them.
  • Ideally you may submit one Profile and then continue on to submit Personals in the state or country files where you have an interest. It is preferred that individuals do not submit more than five Personals.
  • Profiles and Personals should not include email addresses or other contact information. Submissions that contain alternate contact information will be deleted.
  • Responses are sent via a special system we call "Shielded Email". The purpose of "Shielded Email is to protect the email addresses in the profiles and personals from spammers and to permit recipients the option of ignoring unwanted responses or revealing their email address unnecessarily.
  • Responses are sent immediately via email, we do not delay delivery.
  • If you would like to delete your Profile just click here. You will be given the option of just deleting your Personal(s) individually or deleting your Profile and all Personals.

    • Deletions will take effect within 12 to 24 hours of your request.

ABOUT DELETIONS

We reserve the right to delete or edit any posting submitted to http://www.gayuniverse.com or it’s related pages. If you would like to request that a posting be deleted just send me an email message and tell me where the posting is located. To determine the location (URL or Address), view the posting with your preferred Internet Browser, select and copy the location and paste it into your email message explaining why you would like the posting deleted.

ABOUT LINKS

Linking your commercial or personal website - If you have your own website, specifically created for the Gay community or contains information pertinent to the Gay community, we will consider your website for a link on our pages if:

  • Your website does not sell pornography or contains excessive links to sites that sell pornography.
  • Your website does not require visitors to pay a fee to verify their age.
  • Your website does not duplicate services provided by GayUniverse.
  • Your website does not exist on a server or host that duplicates services provided by GayUniverse.
