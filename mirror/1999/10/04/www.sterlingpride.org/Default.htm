The Sterling Pride
Division Champions
Hampden Invitational, May 1999

The Sterling Pride is a U-12 girls soccer club, from the Sterling Youth Soccer Association (SYSA) of Sterling, VA, and plays in the  Washington Area Girls Soccer league (WAGS).  Our players are drawn from Sterling, Sterling Park, Ashburn, and other neighborhoods in and around Loudoun County, as well as some from outside the county, such as Reston.  

Have a look around, and please be sure to visit our sponsors page, whose kind and generous support has helped make this team possible.
