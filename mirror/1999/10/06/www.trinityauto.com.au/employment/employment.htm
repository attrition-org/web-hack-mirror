Opportunities
Trinity Auto Group currently employs over 96 full-time and casual staff. From senior management positions, through to junior clerical and vehicle detailers, the positions are many and varied. Please bookmark this page and return often to check for any vacant positions.

Service Department
Our Service Department, on occassion, has vacancies for technicians in the following areas: mechanical, electrical, airconditioning installation and servicing, LP gas installation and servicing, electronic fuel Injection and electronics.

Sales Department
Our Sales Department employs finance and insurance consultants, new vehicle sales consultants, used vehicle sales consultants, vehicle appraisers, and clerical staff.

Administration
Clerks, receptionists and stock controllers are among the positions available with in the administration department.

Parts Department
Our Spare Parts department employs delivery drivers, storepersons, clerical staff, and spare parts interpreters and on-road representatives.
