Trinity Auto Group holds a prominent position in the Cairns city area with a diverse range of new and used vehicle products. Prior to being purchased by the Henry Walker Eltin in 1997, Trinity Auto Group was known as Broadley Auto Group, a business which had been established in Cairns for 16 years.

Henry Walker Eltin is an Australian owned public company with international operations in contract mining, civil engineering, environmental management services and transport services. Its customers include the world's leading mining companies and Australian Government agencies. Operations are conducted in over 30 locations throughout Australia, South East Asia, New Zealand and South America. Henry Walker Eltin is listed on the Australian Stock Exchange and employs over 2000 people.

The dealership holds new vehicle franchises for Ford, Honda, Hyundai, Suzuki, Land Rover and Kia with used vehicles, parts and service available to customers.
With new vehicle sales of around 140 vehicles per month and a staff of 96, Trinity Auto Group is the largest motor vehicle dealership in Cairns. The recently remodeled Ford showroom will ensure the dealership retains the number one position.
