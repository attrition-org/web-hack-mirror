deBenneville Pines Camp and Conference Center
Volunteer Needs for Summer 1999


This message come to your from Janet James, manager of your beautiful UU
camp, de Benneville Pines.

Summer is here, and so goes the crazy schedule of camp. I could sure use
some help with the maintenance end of things. I have Craig's Cabin available
for most of July. I am hoping that there are a few of you who would like to
come up for one, two or three days and help with some important projects.

1) Staining the rails on the decks
2) Painting a fence or two
3) Painting fire hydrants
4) Rebuilding a fence
5) Rescreening a few windows
6) Staining cupboards in cabin #6
7) Sewing covers for the rocking chairs in Craig's Cabin (9 sets of pads to
be covered)
8) Filling potholes in the road

the list can go on.........

With the exception of July 2 - 5 and July 9 - 17, Craig's Cabin is not being
used and is a very nice place to stay. Food service is available Sunday
dinner through Saturday breakfast with the exception of July 5 - 9, when the
kitchen is closed.

Any time you can spare would really help us out. The week of July 5 - 9 are
perfect days for us to have volunteers in camp, but you would need to cook
for yourself in the kitchen of Craig's Cabin since our new staff will be in
training.

Also, I am looking for an adult at least 25 years or older to be a "den mom"
for the week of July 18 - 24. I have 4 teenage girls who have volunteered to
work at camp that week. I would like a responsible adult to stay in Craig's
Cabin with them, just to keep a concerned and watchful eye on them. This
person can volunteer to work odd jobs or can relax all day and night. The
point is to have an adult present in the Cabin for safe keeping of our young
volunteers. The teens range in age from 15 - 17. They are unrelated and
coming from several different churches. Sounds like a great job for someone
looking to read a few good books and get away from the city.

Please contact me by email or call me at 909-794-2928. Because UU youth camp
is in session during most weeks in July, volunteers will be asked to fill out
a volunteer application and references will be checked. Thank you for your
consideration of this opportunity. de Benneville Pines was built by
volunteers--you are its soul!

Janet James
Manager
