DIRECTIONS to  de Benneville Pines

    de Benneville Pines is the Pacific South West District ("PSWD") UU camp and conference center, nestled in the beautiful SAN BERNARDINO MOUNTAINS at an altitude of 6800 feet.  It is about 75 miles east from Los Angeles.
Address: 41750 Jenks Lake Road West, Angelus Oaks CA 92305
Telephone: (909) 794-2928
E-mail:  uucamp@aol.com

HOW TO GET THERE:
From Los Angeles: Take Interstate 10 east to Redlands (about 60 miles)-- Or,
From Highway 91 heading east (Riverside Fwy.) — Take HWY 215 heading northeast for about 6 miles; then take Interstate Hwy 10 heading east for 6.7 miles to Redlands;
>>>>

  • In Redlands, exit Interstate 10 at University. Turn left at the end of the ramp.
  • Go north to Lugonia (CA Highway 38).
  • Turn right (East) and follow Highway 38 into the mountains.
  • After passing through Mentone it's about 15 miles to ANGELUS OAKS.  --When you come to forks in the road, keep left.
  • Pass through the hamlet of Angelus Oaks and continue another 6 miles. Watch for a row of green mail boxes on the left and turnouts on both sides of the road.
  • Turn right at Jenks Lake Road.  (If you see signs for "Barton Flats" you have passed the turnoff.)
  • Drive about one mile to the camp entrance; watch for the "de Benneville Pines" Camp sign on the right.
  • Follow the unpaved road into the camp parking lot and  PARK FACING OUT (Forest Service Rules).
