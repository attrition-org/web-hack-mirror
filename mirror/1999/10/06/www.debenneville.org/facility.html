deBenneville Pines Camp and Conference Center

Facilities

Nestled in a forest of towering pine, cedar, fir and majestic oak, deBenneville Pines has facilities to meet a wide range of program needs. The spacious central meeting and dining Homet Lodge is available for group activities, as are the coffee house and several smaller meeting rooms. Expansive deck andpatio areas invite casual meetings, arts and crafts activities, or games from table tennis to volleyball. Children will enjoy the play area designed especially for them. The swimming pool is popular on warm summer days and the spa is open year round.

Cabins feature rustic comfort in a range of accommodations from semi-private to mini-dorm rooms and a housekeeping cabin with a complete kitchen. The recently completed Craig's Cabin offers more modern accommodations and includes kitchen and meeting area. The housekeeping cabin (#6) will accomodate 14 people and Craig's Cabin sleeps from nine to twenty-five. Both cabins are available for retreats, parties, reunions and meetings. The heated cabins have modern bath facilities and the beds have comfortable mattresses. Camp capacity is 120 persons. For information on how to rent Craig's Cabin and other Camp facilities, please call Janet James, camp operations manager, 909/794-2928 or by e-mail.

The kitchen staff serves hearty, homestyle meals and offers vegetarian and vegan fare if arranged in advance.
