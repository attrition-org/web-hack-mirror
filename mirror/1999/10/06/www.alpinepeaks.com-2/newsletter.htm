August 1999

Changing of the Gaurds in Owner Relations
Sadly, at the end of August, Alpine Peaks will say farewell to a long-time valued emplyee, Laurie Mosier. Laurie and her husband will be moving out of Grand County with their sights on the Denver area. She has truly enjoyed her work here and the opportunity to work for you. Help us to wish her luck in her future endeavors and also in welcoming her replacement.

Your new Owner Relations contact will be Shannon Cormier. Shannon has been with Alpine Peaks since June of 1995. She and her husband moved to the Winter park area from Fort Collins, CO where she had been working towards a Bachelor of Fine Arts degree. Most
of you may already know Shannon, she has 
worked the front desk, reservations, and most recently as Sales Manager.
her vast knowledge of Alpine Peaks will surely assist your ownership needs. Please stop in to say hello during your next visit to your mountain home.

Unit Inventory
We have added a new specialist to help keep your unit inventory up to par. Branda Brown will be leading our quality assurance team by keying in on everything from pillows to teaspoons. Please help us provide guests of your unit with all the comforts of home.

Winter Park Balloon Rally
Back for a second year, the Winter Park
Balloon Rally was held August 14-15 at the Fraser Valley Sports Complex. Over 29 balloons lifted off Saturday morning! Rain
and fog hindered the balloon "glow" which
was to be held Saturday night and the launch 
scheduled for Sunday morning. This event was organized by Sue and Jack Castellion of Grand Adventures Balloon Tours of Fraser.


Activities Director
Added to Alpine Vacations staff is Karen Davis, Activities Director. We have expanded our activities program to offere more activities (some at discounted rates) and make booking more convenient. one call can do it all. Simply call us for yourselves or for your guests and let us assist you with your activity arrangements.
Lift tickets, skier/rider lessons, snow ca tours, snowshoe tours, x-c ski tours, dog sled rides, hot air balloon rides, snowmobliing, sleigh rides and snowscoots. We offer many activities or the summer also.

Base Village Project
Construction continues on the Base Village project.This picture as ofAug.16.

Stores are sceduled to open on November 17, opening day of the resort, while owners can take occupancy of their new condow by mid-March. These new condominiums will add 1000 "pillows" to Winter Park and the Fraser Valley. Another big project at the resort is the addition of another highspeed quad chair, replacing the Apollo and Eskimo ("Eski-slow") lifts. Ride time will go from 9.5 minutes to 4.2. This lift will run from mid-mountain near Snoasis to the 10,700 foot summit at Sunspot
