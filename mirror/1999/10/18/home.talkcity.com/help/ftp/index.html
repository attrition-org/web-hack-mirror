    What is "FTP"?
FTP stands for File Transfer Protocol. When we use "FTP" as a verb, we mean transferring files from one place to another on the Internet. This walkthrough is designed to help you FTP your pages successfully to your home page area on Talk City. If you experience any trouble with the process please feel free to contact JaxCCC@hosts.talkcity.com.
