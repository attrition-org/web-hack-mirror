WHAT IS SWIM-A-THON?

Swim-A-Thon is the major national fundraising project in which all affiliated clubs in Canada participate. It promotes and finances amateur competitive swimming. The purpose of Swim-A-Thon is to raise money for the club through swimmers participation in a marathon swim; having been pledged so much per length swum, or a predetermined amount. Swim-A-Thon is an excellent method of raising funds for your club.
