SWIM ONTARIO AWARDS



Swim Ontario administers an extensive awards program that incorporates its own categories and that of the Ministry of Citizenship, Culture and Recreation and Swimming/Natation Canada. These highly coveted awards express the Association's admiration and gratitude to organizations and individuals who have contributed to the advancement of swimming in Ontario.

Ontario Aquatic Hall of Fame


The Ontario Aquatic Hall of Fame was designed to recognize outstanding achievements and contributions by individuals to swimming in Ontario. Inductees belong to three categories - swimmers, builders and pioneers.

Swim Ontario Awards


This section includes information on the Swim Ontario Awards Program, the Ministry of Citizenship, Culture and Recreation Awards, the Swimming/Natation Canada Awards, the Ontario Swimming Coaches Association Awards and the Ontario Swimming Officials Association Awards, plus other awards.
