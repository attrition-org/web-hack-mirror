Swim Ontario
(Who we are)

"Swim Ontario is a non-profit sports governing body responsible for the organization and regulation of amateur swimming in Ontario (province in Canada). Swim Ontario promotes excellence by structuring, regulating and supporting programs which encourage the maximum development of each individual swimmer. It is directed by a volunteer Board of Directors, and its policies are administered by a full-time professional staff located at the Ontario Sport and Recreation Center in Toronto."

Swim Ontario attempts to create and enhance an image for competitive swimming as a sport that involves fun, strength, attainment of personal excellence, dedication, health, safety and discipline--in short a sport of superior values.

There are over 100 swim clubs, over 9,000 registered swimmer, about 400 coaches, 20,000 officials and other volunteers throughout Ontario.

Swim Ontario divides the province into six regions. If you need more information about swimming in Ontario or you are looking to join a swim club in your area look up the nearest to club to you and contact us and we will put you in touch with that club.
