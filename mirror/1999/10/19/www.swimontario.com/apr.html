Provincial Tour Team

Due to circumstances beyond our control, plans for the tour team had to be changed. The team will now attend the Mel Zajac meet in May, will participate in the SNC training camp in Vancouver, and will travel to the Grand Prix in Gainesville, Fla., to compete.
