1999-2000 Club Affiliation

This is a reminder to clubs that club affiliation information went out on April 1, 1999 to your club address on file with Swim Ontario.

The affiliation fee memo includes Annual Sanction fee, OSCA Membership Fee, Club Affiliation Fee and GST. Please pay the total amount indicated.

Please be sure to complete all sections of the affiliation form.

Affiliation forms and fees must be received at the Swim Ontario office by July 30, 1999.

Swim instructor Required

Greetings, my name is Brian King. I am the coordinator of the Japan Exchange and Teaching Programme at the Embassy of Japan in Ottawa, While most of the participants in the programme are hired as ALT's (Assistant English Teachers), there is presently a demand for one swimming instructor from Canada to participate in the Sports Exchange Assistant component of the programme. I would greatly appreciate it if Swimming Canada could post this position. I can be reached at my e-mail address [infocul@embassyjapancanada.org] or by phone for greater details about this programme at the Japanese Embassy at 613-241-8541 ext. 134.
