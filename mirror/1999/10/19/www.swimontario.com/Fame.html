LEGACY CAMPAIGN

Amateur sport touches the lives of all those around us, youngand old. Participation in sport gives people a sense of well-being and pride.By working together with the Ontario Aquatic Hall of Fame YOU can help providemotivation for young athletes across the province.

Through our exhibits, these young athletes will be given the opportunity to learn about significant role models who exemplify the important values of amateur sport.

Join the excitement.

Do you have aquatic memorabilia that you would like to see preserved and displayed for others to enjoy? Would you like t o donate it? Your contribution will be gratefully accepted and your name displayed with it.

All contributions advance sport in a special way.

The Ontario Aquatic Hall of Fame is a monument for Ontarians, whose achievements in sport at the local, national and international level are a source of pride for all of us. Our history greatly impacts the direction of our future.

This is an ambitious undertaking that will require the support of all who value and wish to preserve our rich aquatic sport heritage.

Help us to create a legacy that will ensure Ontarians are involved in aquatic sport for years to come.
