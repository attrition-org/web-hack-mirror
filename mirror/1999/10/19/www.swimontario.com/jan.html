1999 Ontario Tour Team

Swim Ontario will be selecting a tour team off Spring Nationals, March 17-20, in Victoria. We will select 8 swimmers (4 males and 4 females) and 1 male and 1 female coach to represent Ontario at a meet in Phoenix, Arizona the weekend of May 14-16, to be followed by the Mel Zajac Jr. International Swim Meet in Vancouver the weekend of May 21-23. This will be an excellent opportunity for swimmers to experience quality back-to-back meets. Swimmers will be selected off current long course performance charts. The tour is sponsored and paid for by Swim Ontario.
