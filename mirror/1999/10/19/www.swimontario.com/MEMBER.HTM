For detailed information on swimmer registration and club affiliation please see the Swim Ontario Club Manual.

SWIMMER REGISTRATION FEES


Provincial Fees
$29.00 (+GST) per competitive swimmer
$6.25 (+GST) per entry level swimmer
* National Fees
$36.00 (no GST) per competitive swimmer
$1.00 (no GST) per entry level swimmer
* National fees are collected by Swim Ontario on behalf of Swimming/Natation Canada

Total Competitive Level Registration Fee - $67.03
Total Entry Level Registration Fee - $7.69

CLUB AFFILIATION FEE


$4.00 per swimmer (+GST) based on the total number of swimmers registered with Swim Ontario on August 31st of the previous year. The maximum fee is $600.00 and the minimum fee $100.00.
New Club Affiliation is $100.00 (+GST)
University or Summer (Apr. 1-Aug. 31) club affiliation fee is $100.00 (+GST).
