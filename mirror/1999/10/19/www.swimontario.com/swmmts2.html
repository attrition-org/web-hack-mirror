Entry Limitation

Swimmers may enter any event in which they have met the qualifying time.

Swimmers 17 and over, qualifying in 1 event, will be given 2 "free" swims and, by extension, if they qualify for 2 events, will be allowed 1 "free" swim for a total of 3 events.

Swimmers 16 and younger, qualifying in 1 event, will be given 4 "free" swims and, by extension, if they qualify for 2 events will be allowed 3 "free" swims, etc., for a total of 5 events.

 

 

Meet Rules

SNC rules will apply.

 

 

Relay Entries

2 Relays per event per club.

Relays will be swum in prelims, with the top 8 teams advancing to finals. Although there are no entry standards for relays, please submit Short Course times for seeding purposes.

 

 

800/1500 Freestyle

The 800/1500 Freestyle Events are Time Finals and will be swum fastest to slowest (alternating female and male), during prelims.

 

 

Proof of Times

Proof of time sheets are attached and must be used to prove each event in which a swimmer has qualified. "Free" swims are to be marked as "NT" and will be seeded last. The forms must be completed, signed and mailed with entry grids prior to the entry deadline.

MEMO

 

 

TO: All Clubs Attending Provincial Championships

FROM: Swim Ontario

RE: Failed Swims

 

 

 

The following motion was passed by the Swim Ontario Management Committee at the July 13, 1998 Management Committee Meeting. The motion was ratified by the Swim Ontario Board at the September 12, 1998 Board meeting.

"The Management Committee recommends to the Board of Directors – In order to control the practice of entering swimmers who are not qualified to swim an event in a provincial championship swim meet, Swim Ontario will institute a $50.00 fine for each "failed swim". The following administrative process will apply:

  1. Host club to be responsible for administering process.

  2. Upon being notified of a "failed swim", coach or club will immediately be required to pay the fine. Failure to pay the fine when requested will make the club ineligible for the balance of the meet and future meets.

  3. The club will have 30 days from notification date to provide proof of time to host club.

  4. All fines to be collected and retained by host club."
