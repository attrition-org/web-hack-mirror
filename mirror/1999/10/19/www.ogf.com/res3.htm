The Rhythmic Gymnastic Group Competition Technical Rules & Regulations serve as a complete guide to the rules of competition. Prepared by the RG Sub-Committee, it provides the judge, coach and gymnast with an outline of group competition.
Pre-Competitive Coaching Manual $17.75
This comprehensive and illustrate manual is designed to provide a sound, progressive approach to the instruction of Rhythmic Sportive Gymnastics at the pre-competitive level. It provides an outline of the basic elements which should be covered as well as a complete appendix with suggrestions for talent identification, testing standards as well as lesson plans. Part one describes the process of athlete selection, long range planning for physical development and includes lesson plans. Part two deals with specific physical preparation to include movement categories, illustrations to show correct techniques as well as preparatory exercises for locomotor exercises. Part three includes technique for Ball and Rope.

Judges' and Coaches' Video with Text: 1997-2000 $35.00
The video contains 12 World Class routines performed from the previous three World Championships. This package includes a comprehensive document that is the most current and up to date resource available to coaches, judges and athletes that specifically references the Code of Points. There is a thorough explanation of all elements with detailed descriptions of execution errors. The 12 routines are scripted in detail and includes the complete breakdown of expert scores for artistic, execution and difficulty elements.
Sports Aerobics Workshop Notes and Video $38.25
The manual has compiled the information from two workshops and is meant as an introduction to Sports Aerobics. This fun activity, now recognized by F.I.G., could easily be a welcome addition to any club looking to expand its program with a healthy, positive experience targeting active people. This is a great activity for any age.
Hague Highlight $30.00
This video highlights the top performances from athletes that competed at the 2nd World Championships in the Hague, October, 1996. This video contains excellent examples of routines that can be modified for any level of athlete. It includes excellent performances showing execution, artistic and difficulty skills.

O.G.F. Technical Rules and Regulations $10.75

F.I.T.Handbook $46.50
This handbook is revised and produced annually and contains the definitive outline of member federations and address; presidium and technical committee members and address; regulations for world, intercontinental and continental championships; world age group competition regulations equipment; guide to judges and difficulty table and calculation guide for tumbling.
O.G.F. Trampoline Safety Manual $5.50
An excellent introductory manual on trampoline safety for the gymnastic coach and instructor of physical education. Topics include equipment set-up and maintenance, common but avoidable accidents, preventative measures, spotting principles and proper use and fitting of spotting belts. This comprehensive fully illustrated manual gives explicit and detailed directions for teaching trampoline in a safe and productive environment. 		O.G.F.Trampoline Lesson Plans $10.25
This comprehensive manual includes complete descriptions outlining the standards, techniques required for each item as well as common errors for skills, drills and progressions listed in the 25 lesson "pages". The lesson pages are presented in an easy to follow chart form, so that the progress of each athlete can be monitored and recorded on a daily basis. Sample routines appropriate for each level of achievement are included.
