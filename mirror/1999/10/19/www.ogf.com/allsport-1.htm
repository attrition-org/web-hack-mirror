All Sport Insurance Marketing Ltd.(ASIM) is a central purchasing agency specializing in Sports Liability and Accident Insurance for Amateur Sporting Groups throughout Canada.

The Insurance Program is marketed in each province in conjuction with each Provincial Sports Federation.

The foundation of the program is education on risk management based on many years of acturaral experience and its success is achieved by sports experts and experienced insurance personnel working together to manage risk. In the 10 years that ASIM and its associates have specialized in this field we have developed programs that meet the diverse needs of each of our sporting group clients.

ASIM is acknowledged as the only facility in Canada specializing solely in Amateur Sports Insurance. Our clients include hundreds of Provincial Sports Groups as well as many National Associations.

ASIM conducts loss and risk control programs for all sports. We collect and analyze loss statistics, case histories and guidelines to assist sport governing bodies in reducing the exposure to their participants, volunteers and spectators, which helps to contribute to reduced insurance cost.

In addition to publishing loss control and educational bulletins we host regular seminars for coaches, managers, participants and volunteers. These programs are an educational and evolutional process to develop standards, procedures, checks and evaluations which form part of a safety program with the objective to eliminate fatalities, serious injuries and reduce other accidents.

ASIM has an associate claims handling facility with offices in major cities throughout Canada. The claims offices are able to respond quickly to claim situations wherever they may occur.
