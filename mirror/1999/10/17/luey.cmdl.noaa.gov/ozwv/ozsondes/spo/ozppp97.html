1997 South Pole Ozone Profiles

Return to the South Pole ozone page.

Ozonesonde balloons were launched at the South Pole by Mark Boland and Glenn McConville of NOAA/CMDL approximately every three days starting September 1, 1997 to monitor the development of the vertical profile of the ozone hole. These profiles can be viewed here.
