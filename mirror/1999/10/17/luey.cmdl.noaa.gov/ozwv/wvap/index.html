The water vapor group at NOAA/CMDL has been measuring water vapor in the upper troposphere and lower stratosphere since 1980 using small balloon borne frost-point hygrometers. These hygrometers are built and calibrated in our laboratory. The focus of our research is long term monitoring of stratospheric water vapor and the processes that control stratospheric and upper tropospheric water vapor. Aside from our long term monitoring program at Boulder, we have conducted several intensive programs in Antarctica, the Arctic, and several equatorial locations.
Currently, we are involved in the Observations of the Middle Stratosphere (OMS) research project as well as the Soundings of Ozone and Water Vapor in Equatorial Regions (SOWER) mission.
