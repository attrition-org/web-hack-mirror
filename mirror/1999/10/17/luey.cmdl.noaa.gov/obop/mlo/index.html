Mauna Loa Observatory -- Hawaii

U.S. Dept. of Commerce / NOAA / OAR / CMDL / OBOP

 

The CMDL Mauna Loa Observatory maintains monitoring programs in greenhouse and other trace gases, atmospheric aerosols, solar radiation variability, stratospheric ozone, and meteorological parameters to support NOAA climate and global change programs. The staff consists of 8 federal employees and a large and variable number of visiting researchers that support NOAA and other agency research programs.
