Samoa Observatory -- Cape Matatula, American Samoa

U.S. Dept. of Commerce / NOAA / OAR / CMDL / OBOP

The CMDL American Samoa Observatory maintains monitoring programs in greenhouse and other trace gases, atmospheric aerosols, solar radiation variability and meteorological parameters. The staff consists of 2 federal employees.
