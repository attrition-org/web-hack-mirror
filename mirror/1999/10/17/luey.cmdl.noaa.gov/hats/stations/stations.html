HATS Cooperating Stations

U.S. Dept. of Commerce / NOAA / OAR / CMDL / HATS

Besides the NOAA/CMDL baseline observatories that are already mentioned on the CMDL homepage, we also cooperate with some other sites around the globe, that are not necessarily used by other NOAA or CMDL divisions. Those stations expand our network for flask and in-situ measurements for some of the compounds measured by HATS.

NOAA/CMDL baseline observatories

  • Pt. Barrow, Alaska (BRW)
  • Cape Matatula, American Samoa (SMO)
  • Mauna Loa, Hawaii (MLO)
  • South Pole, Antarctica (SPO)

Cooperating sites

  • Alert, Canada (ALT)
  • Niwot Ridge, Colorado (NWR )
  • Cape Grim Observatory (CGO)
  • Harvard Forest, Massachusetts (HFM )
  • Grifton, North Carolina (ITN)
  • Park Falls, Wisconsin (LEF)
