Carbon Cycle Greenhouse Gases

Cooperative Data Products

U.S. Dept. of Commerce / NOAA / OAR / CMDL / CCGG

  • GLOBALVIEW
    A carbon cycle model validation tool constructed using observations from a cooperative global observing network.

  • MOPITT Validation
    CMDL is participating in the validation of NASA's Measurement Of Pollution In The Troposphere (MOPITT).

  • IDS Data
    Cooperative database of global CO measurement data (read protected)

  • Cooperative Flask Data
    Database of CMDL flask measurement data for Cooperative Investigators (restricted access).
    For access to this database, email a request to Pieter Tans, Group Chief

  • Isotopic measurements
    Isotopic measurements of CO2 and CH4 in flask samples. A Cooperative program with University of Colorado/INSTAAR

  • Software Packages
    Some software packages that may be of interest to the trace gas measurement community.
