INFORMATIONAL PACKETS

Informational Packets can be obtained on any stress or health related issue. This material is culled from both the scientific and lay literature, and is constantly updated. Some of the most frequently requested topics include:

Job Stress (sources, health and fiscal effects, and solutions)
Stress Management Techniques
Post Traumatic Stress Disorder
Stress and Cardiovascular Disease (Heart Attacks, Sudden Death, Hypertension)
Stress and Cancer
Gender Stress
Stress in Children
Stress and Aging
Stress and Subtle Energies (Music, Aromatherapy, Electromagnetic Energy, Acupuncture)
Holiday Stress
Stress Reduction Effects of Social Support

The minimum price for a packet is $35.00 (usually 15-20 pages), with additional material available at a significantly reduced rate depending on the amount required or available. Please be as specific as possible about the topic you are interested in, how much material is required, and your budget. Requests may be sent by fax: (914) 965-6267, e-mail: stress124@earthlink.net or Surface mail to: Director of Communications, The American Institute of Stress, 124 Park Ave., Yonkers NY 10703. Most orders are processed within 10 days, and payment by check or Money Order is required prior to shipping.
