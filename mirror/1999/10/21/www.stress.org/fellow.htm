FELLOWSHIP, MEMBERSHIP, SUBSCRIPTION INFORMATION

FELLOWSHIP in The American Institute of Stress is available to health professionals and other qualified individuals with appropriate evidence of advanced education, training, skills or related experience in a stress related area. Please submit a CV or resumé, with biographical data, reprints of publications or other supportive information for review by our Accreditation Committee. Fellows will receive a complimentary subscription to our monthly Newsletter, and reduced rates for Informational Packets, selected publications and meetings, and a Certificate of Fellowship suitable for framing. The Fellowship fee is $90.00.

MEMBERSHIP in The American Institute of Stress is available to anyone and provides a complimentary subscription to our monthly Newsletter, and reduced rates for Informational Packets, selected publications and meetings. SUSTAINING MEMBERSHIP is available for $100 annually, and includes the above and 2 additional free Newsletter subscriptions.
