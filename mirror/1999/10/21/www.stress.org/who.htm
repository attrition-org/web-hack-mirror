"WHO'S WHO" DIRECTORY
and OTHER NETWORKING LINKS

As a result of numerous requests from the media, corporations, researchers, practitioners, and the public, we are in the process of compiling a "Who's Who in Stress and Behavioral Medicine". This Directory will be cross referenced by areas of interest, services provided, and geographical location, to facilitate networking and access to information. It is designed to include all individuals or organizations offering stress reduction programs and services, alternative medicine stress reduction approaches, and interests and activities in some specific stress related area. This Directory will be useful in supplying accurate lists for those who wish to target an audience with more specific interests or needs. Information has been obtained from publications and recommendations and referrals from various health professionals. Since some of those listed may be unaware of their inclusion, we are attempting to contact them to obtain updated information to conform with our database. Registration electronically facilitates this process. There is no fee or obligation for listing. A periodically updated hard copy being prepared for libraries and other institutions will be available at markedly reduced rates for those who participate, and wish to learn of others with similar interests. For a listing in this Directory, simply supply the information requested in Fellowship, Membership, Subscription Information.

BOOKS, TAPES, PUBLICATIONS, MEETINGS OF INTEREST

Reviews of books, other educational offerings, and Meetings of Interest appear in each monthly Newsletter. To receive an e-mail copy of lists of books, cassettes, videotapes and Meetings of Interest, send us a request by e-mail. Both of these lists are updated quarterly.
