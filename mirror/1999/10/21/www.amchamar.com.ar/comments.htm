 COMMENTS MAGAZINE

AmCham’s monthly business magazine, Comments on Argentine Trade, is the only English-language publication dedicated entirely to covering business in Argentina.

Each month, Comments’ journalists and commentators take an in-depth look at what businesses are doing in Argentina. The magazine provides information and analysis that readers can use to better understand the Argentine economic and business environment.
