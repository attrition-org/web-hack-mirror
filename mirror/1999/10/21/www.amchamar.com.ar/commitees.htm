 The Chamber Encourages Direct Participation in Committee Work

COURSES, SEMINARS AND CONFERENCES

The Chamber and its committees regularly organize and host meetings of general interest to its members and their invited guests. Speakers, both Argentine and foreign, have included government officials, businessmen, consultants and authors.
The Chamber is comprised of nearly 600 members, the executives of which participate actively on Committees that meet regularly, including:

COMMUNICATIONS

President: Patricio Bonta (J. WALTER THOMPSON)

Committee Coordinator: Fernanda Salerno at fsalerno@amchamar.com.ar or 4371-4500

Goals: To centralize and coordinate the diverse  activities of the AmCham. To help the AmCham expess its goals coherenty  to organizing its the presentations and communications in an uniform manner. To improve, maintain and create a positive business image.

Through the fortnightly bulletin “AmCham Newsletter,” members will be updated on the latest events, news as well as services available to them at the Chamber. The monthly magazine “Comments on Argentine Trade” is a forum for important topics and issues of general interest.

This commission supports other commissions in the AmCham on matters related to image, events and communication.

EDUCATION

President: Félix de Barrio (FUNDACION LEER)

Vice-president: José Luiz Bichuetti  (ARTHUR D. LITTLE)

Committee Coordinator: Fernanda Salerno at fsalerno@amchamar.com.ar or 4371-4500

Goals: To develop and organize educational and informational activities regarding international trade, investment, services and the transfer of technology by means of entertainment and new management techniques.

To help all Chamber members reach their business goals.  

ENVIRONMENTAL

President: Julio Torti (ERM ARGENTINA)

Committee Coordinator: Román Bartomeo at rbartomeo@amchamar.com.ar or 4371-4500

Goals: To offer consulting services to associated companies on all matters related to their particular activities and their relation with the environment. To become a forum for public officials on the layout of adequate regulations for those matters.

GAS & ENERGY

President: Héctor Sergio Falzone (CMS OPERATING)

Committee Coordinator: Fernanda Salerno at fsalerno@amchamar.com.ar or 4371-4500

Goals: To be a forum with quick and efficient response capacity in the face of potential regulatory changes in the sector that can affect the principal objective of AmCham regarding energy issues, which is promote competitiveness and freedom in the sector.

To create an environment of discussion from an objective perspective, rather than a sectoral one, and to strive to facilitate the solution of energy problems in Argentina and the Mercosur.

To promote transparency and competition in the oil, gas and electric energy markets.

Finally, to establish policies regarding regulatory entities in the sector.

INDUSTRIAL RELATIONS

President: José María Bossio (TENNECO AUTOMOTIVE)
Vice-president: César G. Ferrante (BRONS & SALAS)

Committee Coordinator: Fernanda Salerno at fsalerno@amchamar.com.ar or 4371-4500

Goals: To promote greater exchange among Human Resources managers of matters of general interest. To develop activities that aim at deregulation and labor flexibility. To organize informative meetings open to all members.

TELECOMMUNICATIONS

President: Roberto Pérez (CRM - MOVICOM)
Vice-president: Luisa D. Cerar (GLOBAL CROSSING INT'L)

Committee Coordinator: Román Bartomeo at rbartomeo@amchamar.com.ar or 4371-4500

Goals: To promote telecommunications and information technology deregulation and freedom for the development and growth of the Argentine economy while defending the concepts of free market and economic liberty.

To determine the factors that can affect the rights of free competition, free market and develop the necessary actions before the government and the general public. To organize activities on these topics for our members.

INSURANCE

Committee Coordinator: Román Bartomeo at rbartomeo@amchamar.com.ar or 4371-4500

Goals: To promote clear standards that will make insurance activity more efficient and transparent, while remaining respectful of the involved parties' legitimate interests.

To strengthen the differences and specific treatments among the different types of insurance. To promote equal tax treatment and facilitate the publicity of all information that will be of help to the insured.

PATENTS AND INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY RIGHTS

President: Daniel Zuccherino (SCHERING - PLOUGH)
Vice-president: Pablo Horvath (PFIZER)

Committee Coordinator: Fernanda Salerno at fsalerno@amchamar.com.ar or 4371-4500

Goals: To foster an adequate legislation regime that will guarantee effective protection for patent rights and offer a legal framework to investors.

The Commission has produced a series of papers on these issues and has submitted then to governmental authorities and to the general public.   

TAX & LEGAL AFFAIRS

President: Alfredo Rovira (BRONS & SALAS)
Vice-president: Rodolfo Amor (ESSO S.A.P.A.)

Committee Coordinator: Román Bartomeo at rbartomeo@amchamar.com.ar or 4371-4500

Goals: To assess present regulations and legal framework regarding foreign investments, industry and commerce, and propose the necessary changes that are important to the interest of all members.

To produce documents on specific topics that will define the Chamber’s position as well as establish direct connections with government authorities and congressmen. The commission will also examine tax reforms and regulations and will serve as a channel of communication to keep government updated on the chamber’s member’s initiatives and interests.

TRADE

President: Hugo Krajnc (CARGILL)
Vice-president: Rodolfo Ceretti (FORD ARGENTINA)

Committee Coordinator: Román Bartomeo at rbartomeo@amchamar.com.ar or 4371-4500

Goals:  To promote commercial relations between Argentina and the United States. To develop programs compatible with the Free Trade Area of the Americas (FTAA), NAFTA, Mercosur and other regional trade blocs.
