Directory of American Business in Argentina

The AmCham Directory offers a complete listing of member companies, with information on directors and officers, type of business activity, addresses and telephone numbers, and other pertinent facts and figures.

The Directory also offers:

  • A list of U.S. companies in Argentina compiled by the AmCham’s staff, with updated information on local representatives, agents and distributors.

  • An index of trademarks and brand names.

  • General information on Argentina compiled by the AmCham’s staff, including financial, economic and trade statistics, as well as information on government and consular offices.

  • General information on United States Embassy and Consular services as well as other private institutions located in Argentina.

The Directory is distributed free of charge to AmCham members and is available for a fee to non-members.
