 AMCHAM´S CORDOBA DELEGATION

..
After eighty years of active service in Argentina, the American Chamber of Commerce this year founded a delegation in Córdoba, which was chosen as the first delegation outside of Buenos Aires due the province’s economic growth and high level of international investment.

The Córdoba delegation is an integral part of the American Chamber of Commerce, and as such, shares the AmCham’s overall objectives of promoting bilateral commerce, free enterprise, direct investment, the transfer of technology, ethical norms and other mutual interests between Argentina and the United States.

The Córdoba delegation of AmCham will have many activities such as: the organization of educational seminars and events with Argentine and American government officials and business leaders, as well as the organization of working committees covering themes of interest for business people.
