 PREMIO CIUDADANIA EMPRESARIA

..
This year, AmCham launched the “Premio Cuidadanía Empresaria” (Corporate Citizenship Prize). The principal objective of the prize is to increase awareness of the importance of the ties between the business sector and the community as a whole by recognizing those companies that have developed programs that enrich the community while stimulating those that have not to become more aware of the mutual benefits to community involvement. The result will be continual improvement of the image of the business community in society as a whole.

The winner of the prize will be announced in November of 1999.
