October 6, 1999
SAVVIS Helps the Financial Market Improve Communication with FinancialNet

SAVVIS announces the introduction of SAVVIS FinancialNet, a closed user group designed to serve the communication needs of the financial marketplace. FinancialNet is an established Community Of Interest Network (COIN) that only authorized subscribers can access. This innovative solution is a fast, efficient and highly secure way to exchange information, provide access to applications and conduct transactions within the financial community. More…

SAVVIS Unveils ProLink MDN, Enables E-Commerce, Online Communities, Multi-Site Communications

SAVVIS announces expansion of its ProLink line of private network products to include ProLink Managed Data Networks (MDN). ProLink MDN is a secure, high quality, affordable solution for businesses that need to deliver content, extend enterprise-wide communications and conduct e-commerce between business locations. More…

SAVVIS Hires Bridge Veteran to Focus on Financial Industry

Michael W. Cattell, is appointed as vice president of financial markets business development. Cattell's prior experience as vice president, private network services for Bridge Information Services is a significant asset to his new role at SAVVIS. As the primary liaison between the SAVVIS sales organization and the sales and marketing team at Bridge, Cattell will focus his efforts on developing opportunities within the financial community for SAVVIS internetworking services. SAVVIS Communications Awarded 'BEST OF SHOW' for VPN Service at N+I

SAVVIS announces its Global ProLink Virtual Private Network (VPN) with Quality of Service (QoS) feature has been named "Best Of Show" for WAN Services at the Networld + INTEROP (N+I) show. SAVVIS' Global ProLink VPN is a suite of Internet and networking next generation services designed to enable efficient and economical electronic communications and commerce.
