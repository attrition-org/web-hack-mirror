We Give You The Support You Need

Your business depends on reliable network connections. But the world of data communications is becoming increasingly complex. Whether you're based in the Kansas City or Kuala Lumpur, you'll get the same high quality SAVVIS support. Prevent Problems With Proactive Service

But your royal treatment doesn't stop there. Our SAVVIS Network Operations Center (NOC) is fully staffed with a team of experienced Network Engineers and Customer Support Advocates who proactively monitor your Internet connections for the life of your service.

On Call, All The Time

In addition to keeping your service up and running 100% of the time, our SAVVIS service and support team is highly skilled at handling the full spectrum of customer support needs, from simple operations to complex problem identification and resolution. We're there when you need us — open 24/7/365 — ready, willing and able to assist you with your Internet connections and concerns and resolve most questions on your initial call.

SAVVIS Promise

Monitoring. SAVVIS continually monitors each backbone and customer circuit from its state-of-the-art Network Operations Center (NOC).

Testing. SAVVIS remotely tests every network and customer router every 90 seconds.

Notification. SAVVIS notifies its customers within 15 minutes — guaranteed — if there is ever a network outage resulting in downtime.

Rerouting. SAVVIS instantly and automatically reroutes traffic if there is ever a backbone failure.

Consulting. SAVVIS notifies customers if they approach or exceed the maximum capacity of their circuit, and proactively recommends Internet access and security solutions appropriate for their business.
