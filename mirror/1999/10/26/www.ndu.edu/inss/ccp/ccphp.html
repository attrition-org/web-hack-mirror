Center for Counter Proliferation and Research

The Center for Counter Proliferation Research (CCP), hosted by INSS, was founded to explore therange of political-military issues relevant to the proliferation of nuclear, biological, and chemicalweapons, collectively known as weapons of mass destruction. Established in June 1994, the CCPsponsors research and discussions on these issues among the Defense Department's policy,operations, acquisition, and intelligence communities. Additionally, the CCP directly supports theprofessional military education programs of NDU's colleges and other components.

Latest publication:  U.S. Nuclear Policy in the 21st Century - Executive Report
