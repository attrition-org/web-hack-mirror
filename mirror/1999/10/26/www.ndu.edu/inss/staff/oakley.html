AMB ROBERT B. OAKLEY (retired)
Visiting Fellow
202-685-2374
oakleyr@ndu.edu

Ambassador Oakley retired from the Department of State in 1991 after 34 years of diplomatic service. His positions included Ambassador to Pakistan, Zaire, and Somalia; Coordinator for Counterterrism; Assistant to Vice President for Middle East and South Asia on National Security Council staff and Deputy Assistant Secretary of State for East Asia Affairs. Ambassador Oakley subsequently served President Bush and Clinton on Special Envoy to Somalia.
