CONFERENCES DIRECTORATE

The Conferences Directorate organizes and presents annual national-and international-level symposia on security, strategic, and foreign policy issues. Each year, four symposia focus on Europe, the Pacific, Joint Operations, and a current topic of national interest. The Directorate also hosts distinguished speakers who address issues of interest to national security policymakers. The objectives of these two programs are to create a major outreach program in which the National Defense University, in conjunction with the national and international security communities, shares ideas, explores alternatives, and defines issues.

Conferences Director: James R. Graham
