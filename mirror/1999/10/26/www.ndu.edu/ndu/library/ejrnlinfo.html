Introduction To Full Text E-Journals

The electronic journal titles in these lists have been selected for their relevancy to the NDU curricula. Titles are taken from two of the subscription services [ProQuest and JSTOR] subscribed to by the NDU Library for the use of NDU students and faculty; the complete range of titles in these two services are not included in these lists. Access to subscription collections is available from the University network [directly or thru the extranet].

In addition, electronic journal titles available freely on the internet have been included for your convenience.

The earliest date any type of full text is available is posted to the Full Text Coverage column. Particularly with ProQuest, the early ('87 +) full text might be in image format only or only text format.

Links are provided for each title included; some titles have more than one link if they are available from multiple sources. For ProQuest titles, Click the link, Then use the NEW SEARCH FOR PUBLICATION option to find the title.

NOTE: JSTOR does not contain current issues of any journal. This archival service runs 3 to 5 years behind and will have no journal issues newer than 1996.

If you are aware of e-journals we have missed in areas of NDU interest, please send a note to the Webmaster [humeh@ndu.edu].
