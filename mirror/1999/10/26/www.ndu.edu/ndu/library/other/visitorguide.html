NDU Library Internet Resources

Bibliographies
Subject bibliographies are prepared by the reference staff in support of the curriculum.
http://www.ndu.edu/ndu/library/bibs/bibs.html

Current Journal Abstracts
An SDI product produced by the NDU reference librarians intended to keep faculty and students up-to-date on the latest journal literature in the NDU Library collection. The journal citations include an abstract and are arranged by LC subject heading.
http://www.ndu.edu/ndu/library/cjas/cja.html

Electronic Journals
Links to selected electronic journals in NDU's subject areas.
http://www.ndu.edu/ndu/library/ejrnlinfo.html

GPO Access
Service of the U.S. Government Printing Office that provides free electronic access to a wealth of important products producted by the Federal Government.
http://www.ndu.edu/ndu/library/onldb/gpoacces.html

Manuscript Collections
Special Collections, Archives and History holds many manuscript collections of contemporary military leaders.
http://www.ndu.edu/ndu/library/other/manuscript.html

Military Journals in Electronic Format
Links to both subscription and free journals relevant to defense. Subscription collections are accessible to NDU only.
http://www.ndu.edu/ndu/library/ejrnl_military.html

Map Resources
Links to new and exceptional sites for digital maps and mapping resources.
http://www.ndu.edu/ndu/library/ntk/maps.html

SCAMPI
