Current Journal Abstracts [CJA]

The Current Journal Abstracts (CJA) is published by the NDU Library as a current awareness service for staff, faculty and students. The citations and abstracts are compiled by the reference staff based on their relevance to the academic programs at the University. Each CJA has its own subject index using Library of Congress Subject Headings.
