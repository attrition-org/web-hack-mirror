Organized in 1982, the Foundation is a non-profit, tax exempt organization classified under paragraph 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code. It establishes private sector support through grants and donations to enhance NDU's teaching and research capabilities in ways not made available through government appropriations.

These enhancements involve rotating research and teaching functions, chairs and fellowships, seminars, conferences and other specialized means to enrich the academic ambience commensurate with what is expected of a prestigious university.

The President and other officials of the University are prohibited by law from soliciting funds from outside sources to meet these special needs. The Foundation was created as an independent, non-military entity to raise funds to support the NDU programs as described above.

The Foundation directors are patriotic citizens who serve without compensation and devote their effort to help the NDU to enhance its level of EXCELLENCE IN NATIONAL SECURITY EDUCATION.
