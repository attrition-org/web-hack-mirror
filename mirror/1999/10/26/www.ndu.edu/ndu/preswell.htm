Welcome to the Home Page of the National Defense University (NDU), our nation's premier joint military education institution. On this page and its component links, you'll find a continually expanding body of information: outlines of curricula at our constituent colleges; research and publications by our faculty and students; and information on upcoming symposia, conferences, seminars, and workshops involving the University. Our NDU Library's link includes both a searchable index of its holdings and connections to a host of other related web sites. On its link, NDU's Institute for National Strategic Studies (INSS) provides online publication of the annual "Strategic Assessment" and periodic "Strategic Forums".
We hope that this home page, and those like it in the Professional Military Education
community, offer both the medium and the message of the information age. There is now little
doubt that the Internet, and its World-Wide Web in particular, has become an important,
worldwide vehicle for the information explosion. As for the message, we will make every
effort to provide a relevant, growing, and elastic information resource on these pages that is of
real value to our students, present and past, and to all others with a genuine interest in
professional military education and research.

Perhaps no single factor has so much potential for changing the nature of how military
organizations function, during peace and war, as the information explosion. By making
unprecedented amounts of information immediately available in easy-to-use forms at
diminishing costs, the emerging "information highway" will certainly alter society, to say nothing
of military conflict. This home page is part of our effort at NDU to keep pace with this
ubiquitous, rapidly evolving phenomena with enormous consequences for the way we live --
and fight.

Richard A. Chilcoat

Lieutenant General, USA
