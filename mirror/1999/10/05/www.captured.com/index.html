WireTap UBB

Hey okay here is the juice about this highly anticipated mod for q3a that will fuse some new gameplay mack-daddy badassness right into your perplexed brainwaves.

We have spent well over six months preparing the concepts and artistic style of WireTap, just to give the project a greater base for good gameplay and excellent creativity. :)

I will release the mod specs as soon as we can get them finished and agreed upon -- almost there!

We still need a full time coder on this project, so all applicants please contact me ASAP via email.

We have a support team with a few coders all set up and ready to go, but we need someone to head up the project's code; basically oversee the whole process and talk to the coders, establish the base and guide the project from beta to v2.0.

We have mappers already, but if you have Model tallent, please contact me too! :)

WireTap is a teamplay mod under construction for q3a.   We have a web board set up for that mod HERE, or you can hit the Dteam web board HERE.

Currently we are looking at some very slick looking gameplay involving maps that will be compatible in CTF mode, as well as our patented ViRuS gamemode.

The object of ViRuS is to get into the enemy base and upload a virus to their computer (MCP) without getting your ass whipped in the process.

Expect new weps and some badass mappies just for your gaming nightmare.

Expect a private beta test to occur when we get the source code to q3a. :)
