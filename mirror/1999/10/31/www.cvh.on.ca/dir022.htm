Program:
Cansurmount

Description:
One-on-one visits with people who have cancer and/or their families by volunteers who have been touched by cancer, either personally or through a family member. Trained volunteers help people with cancer and their families deal with the diagnosis of cancer. (Note: medical advice and professional counselling not provided).

Information & Referrals
Emotional Support

Eligibility:
