Description:
Nurses and home support workers specially trained in palliative care provide services to the terminally ill at home. Staff work with clients, physicians, and other members of the home health care team (For more information on assessment and effective management of symptoms for those facing death, please see Respiron Care Plus' Palliative Care Booklet).
