Program:
Mammography

Description:
Offers breast screening for women age 50 and over. The friendly, easily accessible centres provide mammography (x-ray views of the breast), physical breast examination by a specially trained nurse examiner, and information on breast self-examination.

Services Offered:
Mammography/Breast Examination
Education & Information

Eligibility:
Any woman over age 50 with no previous personal history of breast cancer, no surgical enlargement of the breasts, no acute breast problems, who has not had a mammogram in the past 12 months.

Ontario
