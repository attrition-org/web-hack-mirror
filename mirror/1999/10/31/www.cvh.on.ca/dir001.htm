Program:
Meal Delivery Service

Description:
Brampton Meals-on-Wheels offers hot, nutritious meals to seniors and handicapped persons, who for short or long periods of time, are unable to manage food preparation. This service enables them to remain in their own living environment for as long as possible.

Meals are prepared in Peel Manor under the direction of a fully qualified Dietician. Special dietary needs are available as prescribed by the client's physician.

Services Offered:
Meals

Eligibility:
Any person who, as a result of age or physical disability, is unable to prepare nutritious meals and whose limitations make it difficult to obtain adequate food services.

Area Served:
Brampton

Service Location:
Client's Homes

Wheelchair Access:
N/A

Languages Served:
English

Hours of Service:
Meals can be ordered 8:30 a.m. - 1:00 p.m., Monday to Friday.

Cost:
$3.25/meal, payable weekly or monthly by cheque or cash. Cost partially subsidized by Ministry of Community & Social Services.
