Program:
Look Good...Feel Better

Description:
Look Good...Feel Better offers women undergoing cancer treatment tips on enhancing their appearance with wigs, head coverings and cosmetics. Supported by a number of agencies and companies, the program gives women a self-esteem boost and also gives them a chance to meet other women living with cancer.

Services Offered:
Wigs and Esthetics

Eligibility:
Any woman undergoing cancer treatment; please call to register.
