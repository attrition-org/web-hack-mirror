ANNUAL REPORT OF THE CREDIT VALLEY HOSPITAL ASSOCIATES

The Credit Valley Hospital Associates

The Associates had a banner year. The Gift Shop, HELLP Lottery, Bingo and Bazaar exceeded their expectations which resulted in $245,100 in donations.

The Gift Shops continued to modernize their operations with the addition of "Interac" purchases and a new computerized cash register to allow better and more detailed accounting. The Econo Centre expanded and now boasts brighter lighting, better shelving and wider aisles. This year’s bazaar was also a huge success with more vendors than the Restaurant could handle - some had to be accommodated in the hallways. The Bazaar raised $3,000.00 more than budgeted.
