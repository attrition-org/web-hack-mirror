Program:
Cansurmount

Description:
One-on-one visits with people who have cancer and/or their families by volunteers who have been touched by cancer, either personally or through a family member. Trained volunteers help people with cancer and their families deal with the diagnosis of cancer (Note: Medical advice and professional counselling are not provided.).

Services Offered:
Information & Referrals
Emotional Support

Eligibility:
Individuals diagnosed with cancer and/or their family members.
