Respite Registry (Caregiver Relief)

Description:
Assistance or relief for people who care for an elderly or disabled family member or friend. A respite worker will come to your home to provide security and companionship for your loved one. The respite worker will assist with daily activities while you go out. This is not a nursing or homemaking service.
