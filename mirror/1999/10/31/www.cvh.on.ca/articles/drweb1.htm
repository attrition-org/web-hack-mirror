Why You Need A Family Doctor

T here are a wide range of problems that may affect your health. Family doctors are trained to keep you and your family healthy, and to treat you when you are sick.

Family doctors are also a good source of health information and can help you sort through today's complex health care services. Their training, skills and knowledge help you to keep well and to manage your health or medical problems wisely.

Knowing you and your family helps your doctor to understand your problems and allows trust to grow as times passes. This relationship means better care for you and your family and makes the best use of health care dollars.

How To Find A Family Doctor

T here are several things to consider when looking for a family doctor:

  • If you prefer a specific hospital your family doctor should have priviledges there.
  • It may be important to you to have a family doctor who speaks a language other than English, or who is a member of your culture or gender.
  • The location of the doctor's office should be close to your home or place of work.
Many Ways To Find A Family Doctor

T o find a family doctor you can start by asking a friend or someone in your family.

The Ontario College of Family Physicians can help you by giving you the names of doctors in your area who are members of the College. The number there is (905) 629-1600.

The College of Physicians and Surgeons of Ontario can also help you find a Family Doctor. You can reach them at (416) 967-2626.
