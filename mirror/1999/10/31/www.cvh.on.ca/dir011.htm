Description:
The Canadian Breast Cancer Foundation (CBCF) is a volunteer-driver charitable foundation dedicated to the support and advancement of breast cancer research, education, diagnosis and treatment. Through fundraising, sponsorship, donations and membership, CBCF contributed $2.1 million to research and education in 1996. In 1997, the Foundation launched its first Education Grant review process for research grants.
