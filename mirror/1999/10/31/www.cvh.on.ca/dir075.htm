Program:
Get On Track

Description:
Get On Track is a personalized, individual program to help individuals quit smoking. The Lung Association focuses on prevention of lung disease, and recommends this program to those who want to quit smoking to improve their health. Those already diagnosed with lung cancer are also welcomed in the Get On Track program.

Services Offered:
Smoking Cessation Program

Eligibility:
