Program:
Cancer Information Service 1-888-939-3333

Description:
Questions about Cancer? Call the Canadian Cancer Society's toll free Information Service at 1-888-939-3333. Our information specialists can provide information specific to your needs in clear, understandable terms and in a supportive manner that ensures caller dignity and confidentiality.

Services Offered:
The Cancer Information Service provides information on:

  • prevention and early detection
  • all types of cancer
  • cancer treatment
  • drugs and clinical trials
  • dealing with side effects
  • complementary therapies
  • cancer statistics
  • emotional and financial support
  • nutrition
  • help in your local community

Eligibility:
Anyone can call including cancer patients and their loved ones, the general public and healthcare professionals.
