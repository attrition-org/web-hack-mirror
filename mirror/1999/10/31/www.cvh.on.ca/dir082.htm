Program:
Oncology Program

Description:
Multi disciplinary team provides outpatient care for patient with cancer who require systemic therapy, including chemotherapy and follow-up for bone marrow transplants. Services include supportive treatment, blood transfusions, paracentesis, thoracentesis, vascular access devices assessment, screening, education and care. Patient education, resource library and support programs (e.g., Feeling Your Best) are also provided.
