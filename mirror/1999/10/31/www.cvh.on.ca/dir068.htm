Program:
Cancer Hospice

Description:
This six-bed facility provides palliative care to cancer patients with a prognosis of three months or less. Each patients is provided with a private room, equipped with a wheelchair-accessible washroom. Health care is provided through the Community Care Access Centres, with volunteers and staff providing all other services.

Services Offered:
Accommodation
Palliative Care

Eligibility:
Cancer patients with a prognosis of three months or less; a physician referral is also required.
