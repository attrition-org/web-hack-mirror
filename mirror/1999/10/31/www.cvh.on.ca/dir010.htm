Description:
The Canadian Breast Cancer Foundation (CBCF) is a volunteer-driven foundation dedicated to the support and advancement of breast cancer research, education, diagnosis and treatment. In 1996, CBCF contributed $2.1 million to research and education through its fundraising initiatives.

In addition to providing community and corporate presentations on breast cancer, CBCF also produces a variety of publications and resources, which are available to any member of the community. Everything from Canadian breast cancer statistics to mammogram information to breast self-examination guides are available.
