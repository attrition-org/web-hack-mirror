Program:
Patient Care Programs

Description:
The Toronto-Sunnybrook Regional Cancer Centre provides a range of cancer patient care services in a multidisciplinary environment. These services cover the full continuum from screening to palliative care, and include radiation, surgical and systemic treatment activities. Supportive care services complement these activities. Care is provided in outpatient, inpatient, and outreach settings.
