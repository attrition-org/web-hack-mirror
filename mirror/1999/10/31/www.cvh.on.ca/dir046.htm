Program:
Medical Supplies Management

Description:
Caremark provides an extensive inventory of medical, wound management and urological supplies for individuals in their homes.

Caremark also provides coordination between the patient and their health care team to ensure smooth and uninterrupted care. A clinical consultant can assist the health care provider with product selection and treatment protocols.
