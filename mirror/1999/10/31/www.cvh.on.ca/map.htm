Directions to CVH


The Credit Valley Hospital is located at the intersection
of Eglinton Avenue West and Erin Mills Parkway in Mississauga.

By car:

From the north: Take Hwy. 401 (east or west) and exit at Mississauga Rd. Turn south and stay towards the right at Argentia and turn onto Erin Mills Parkway. Continue driving south for about 10 minutes and CVH will be on your left as you pass Erin Mills Town Centre and Eglinton Avenue West.

From the south: Take the QEW (from east or west) and exit at the Erin Mills Parkway exit. Drive north-west for about 10 minutes. CVH is the first exit past the Hwy. 403 bridge on ramp.

From the east: Take Hwy. 403 and exit at Erin Mills Parkway. Turn right onto Erin Mills Parkway. The entrance to CVH is just past the first set of traffic lights.

From the west: Take Hwy 403 and exit at Erin Mills Parkway. Turn left onto Erin Mills Parkway. The entrance to CVH is just past the first set of traffic lights.
