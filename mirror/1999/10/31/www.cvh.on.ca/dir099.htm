Program:
Dental Oncology

Description:
The only one of its kind in Canada, the Dental Oncology program provides surgical facial reconstruction and prosthetic devices for cancer patients who have undergone major surgery which has drastically altered their appearance. Everything from prosthetic eyes to major bone reconstruction are provided.

Surgical Facial Reconstruction
