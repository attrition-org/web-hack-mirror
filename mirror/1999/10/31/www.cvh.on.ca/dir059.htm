Description:
Therapeutic intervention aimed at cure or, at least, significant prolongation of life for the patients with malignant disease.

Patients are assessed and treatment determined by Oncologists from Credit Valley Hospital who are on site monthly to follow care and assess new referrals (Children are followed by the Hospital for Sick Children). Internists on staff follow-up between visits.
