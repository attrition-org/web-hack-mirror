Directory of Brain Injury Services

Locating relevant information can be among the greatest challenges facing individuals with brain injury and their families. Furthermore, professionals working with survivors of brain injury frequently have difficulty finding other appropriate service providers for their clients. The newly expanded Directory of Brain Injury Services, published by the Head Injury Association of Toronto (HIAT) addresses this critical need for information. It provides a comprehensive index to services available for persons living with the effects of brain injury in the Greater Toronto Area. The cost of the 300-page Directory is $30, plus $5 for shipping and handling and $2.45 GST. It is available directly from HIAT at 45 Sheppard Ave. E., Suite 419, North York, ON M2N 5W9 or by calling (416) 222-7445 or via fax at (416) 222-1911.
