Program:
Reach to Recovery

Description:
One-on-one visits with people who have breast cancer by volunteers who have had breast cancer. Trained volunteers help breast cancer patients deal with the diagnosis and treatment. (Note: Medical advice and professional counselling not provided).

Information & Referrals
Emotional Support
Support Kit
