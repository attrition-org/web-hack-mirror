Program:
Neuro-Oncology Program

Description:
Support service/education/assessment provided on outpatient basis for patients with malignant brain tumors. The multidisciplinary team (neurosurgeon, neurologist, oncologist, registered nurses, social workers, occupational therapists and physiotherapists) helps patients and families with coping mechanisms, symptom management and support, and systemic therapy.
