Career Opportunities

The Credit Valley Hospital is a growing community hospital in the Greater Toronto area, with expanding programs in Cancer Care and Child & Maternal Care dedicated to providing the highest quality health care.

We at The Credit Valley Hospital are proud of our formidable staff and the outstanding physicians whose personal dedication and commitment to quality health care have made our Hospital a progressive health care facility, one of the best in Canada.

Cited as one of the top 10 preferred employers of women in the publication "Canada's Best Employers for Women" by Tema Frank, we are proud to offer a rewarding and challenging place to work which provides continuous learning opportunities for professional and personal growth.

Career Opportunities at The Credit Valley Hospital range widely from the traditional roles in nursing, technical, paramedical, clerical and service support functions, through the more unusual, yet equally important roles, such as video production specialist, quality utilization analysts, back wellness specialist, cytogeneticists and polysomnographers. No matter what the position, each and every staff member at The Credit Valley Hospital is part of an innovative, creative team, dedicated to providing the best available health care to our commmunity.
