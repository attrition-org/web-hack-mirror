Program:
Psychosocial Services

Description:
Princess Margaret Hospital's Psychosocial Program offers patients access to Social Workers, Psychologists, Psychiatrists, and a Cancer Coping Skills program. This program is aimed at helping cancer patients and their families deal with the emotional effects of their illness.
