ATI leads the way in bringing DVD video to your PC.

Together with your PC's DVD drive and any one of ATI's RAGE PRO (AGP 2X versions), RAGE 128 or RAGE 128 PRO-based graphics accelerators, you can get smooth flicker-free DVD on your PC without the need for additional hardware!
