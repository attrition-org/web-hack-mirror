Formerly known as Peripheral Technology International Limited, PERITECH SYSTEMS PLC offers two specialist divisions - Sun Microsystems hardware and Consultancy Services. Established in 1991, we have built a solid reputation based on cost, product availability, technical support and on the close relationships we have with our customers.

Peritech Systems plc is a leading supplier of Sun Microsystems products. We have the infrastructure to deliver and support Sun workstations and servers to meet your business requirements. All our staff are Sun trained, (sales and technical), under the Competency 2000 program.

Services
As a support driven company we offer technical consultancy and systems/network design. At our facility we preconfigure, load software and integrate peripherals prior to delivery, at which point we can install and provide on going support.

Overview
Built on a solid reputation, with its expertise based on 10 years experience with Sun Microsystems products, Peritech Systems Plc is well placed to offer outstanding quality and value to its customers. As an authorised Sunsoft reseller, we are well placed to offer you the vast range of Sun software to meet your business needs. From operating systems to security products we can provide and install the very best on offer.
