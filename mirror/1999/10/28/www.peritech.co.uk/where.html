Directions - Finding us at the Parker Centre
From M1 junction 25
  • Take A52 to Derby city centre.
  • At the first island (traffic lights) move into the right hand lane for the A61.
  • Take the 3rd exit off the A61 to Sir Frank Whittle Road.
  • At the first island turn left.
  • Turn left again for the Parker Centre.
  • Continue for 150 metres and then turn right into the Parker Centre.
  • Follow the road right and then left.
  • We are about 50 metres along on the right-hand side.

From A38 North
  • Exit left at Derby sign posted Nottingham at roundabout
  • Straight over the next two roundabouts.
  • At the third roundabout turn right and then immediately left.
  • Continue for 150 metres and then turn right into the Parker Centre.
  • Follow the road right and then left.
  • We are about 50 metres along on the right-hand side.

From A38 South
  • Follow A38 (ring road) around Derby to the Little Chef.
  • Turn right and follow the route for Nottingham.
  • Straight over the next two roundabouts.
  • At the third roundabout turn right and then immediately left.
  • Continue for 150 metres and then turn right into the Parker Centre.
  • Follow the road right and then left.
  • We are about 50 metres along on the right-hand side.

From A52 Ashbourne, or from A6 North
  • Turn left onto the A38 South (the Derby ring road).
  • Follow A38 (ring road) around Derby to the Little Chef.
  • Turn right and follow the route for Nottingham.
  • Straight over the next two roundabouts.
  • At the third roundabout turn right and then immediately left.
  • Continue for 150 metres and then turn right into the Parker Centre.
  • Follow the road right and then left.
  • We are about 50 metres along on the right-hand side.
