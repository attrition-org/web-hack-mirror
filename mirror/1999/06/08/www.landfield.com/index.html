A Word from Mr. Coggins:

I'm not aware of any crackdown that's been bigger than this. We've had hacker cases before in our office; this is the broadest and most comprehensive one we've had. We're obviously working with Washington on this. No one's been charged with anything. This is an ongoing investigation. Obviously, the crashing of the Web sites complicates the investigation, because it's an additional federal offense that we're now looking at...

We will catch a number of these people, they will be sentenced, and probably sent to jail for this...

If there are people out here listening to this, who think this is a game, and it's cat and mouse, don't throw away your life by getting involved in committing a federal felony.
