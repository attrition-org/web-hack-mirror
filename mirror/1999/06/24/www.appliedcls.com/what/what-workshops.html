ACLS provides a workshop for people preparing for the LSAT exam, using techniques based on our basic principles of individual differences, multi-domains, and computer technology.

We will teach you the fundamentals of different types of LSAT questions, and gradually, effective techniques on tackling the harder ones. At the same time, we'll teach you how to use your learning style to your advantage to achieve mastery of the LSAT.

The LSAT workshop consists of three intensive weekend sessions and support services that span a period of two months. Please contact us for more information.
