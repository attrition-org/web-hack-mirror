Company Information

    Contour Construction Ltd. was founded in 1984 in Edson, Alberta. We are oilfield construction contractors specializing in road, lease, oilfield, and plant construction in the Edson, Whitecourt, Fox Creek, and Hinton regions.

    Lease and road construction, cleanups, reclamation work, line clearing, subcontracting on pipelines and plantsite construction are all a part of our regular operations.

    Contour's office and shop are located in Edson Alberta. We have equipment is based out of our shop in Edson and Whitecourt.

Contour Equipment

    Contour has a well maintained fleet of equipment ranging from Komatsu D37P-5 to Cat D8L, hydraulic excavators, graders, motor scrapers and compaction equipment.
