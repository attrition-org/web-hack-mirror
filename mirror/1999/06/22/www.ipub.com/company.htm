iPUB was formed in San Francisco in 1995 to design and develop sites and promotions for the exploding online world. We are unusual among online developers because we do online design for clients as well as publish our own content. This combined developer/publisher perspective improves the profitability and quality of our work for clients.

Our first project, Guerrilla Marketing Online, for the Web and AOL is an iPUB owned and published property. Guerrilla Marketing Online is the official companion to Jay Levinson's best-selling book series and is used by hundreds of thousands around the world. The Guerrilla site is one of few to boast profitability within the first 3 months and remains profitable today.

iPUB also designs and produces client sites and online promotions for companies such as Wells Fargo, AOL and others. We do everything from commercial shopping sites and online applications, to information and entertainment services.

We believe that great design improves accessibility and understanding of the content but should never test the limits of the users bandwidth or technology.
