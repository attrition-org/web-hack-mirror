Registration Information

HOW TO REGISTER FOR NTC COURSES

Registering online?  Into which of these categories do you fall?

  • You know which class you want to take (including the course code and registration ID number), and you know your NTC training number and have your supervisor's approval.  Click here and register now.
    .
  • You are from outside BLM and need information about application procedures, tuition, and NTC's course cancellation policy. 
    .
  • You need to find the right course and register for it:
  1. Select a course by checking offerings from the DATE matrix or the CATEGORY matrix at the top of this page.  
  2. Jot down the course code and registration ID number of the course. 
  3. Click the name of the course to access the course description.  
  4. If the course description matches your training need, click "Register."
  5. Complete the online registration form.
  6. Send to NTC by clicking the "Submit" button.
    .
