Directions to the BLM National Training Center
9828 North 31st Avenue, Phoenix, AZ  85051

 
.Map #1 - Phoenix Metropolitan Area
If you're driving to NTC from the airport,
(1) exit the airport, drive south on either 44th Street or 32nd Street and connect with I-17 north to Flagstaff.  
Exit Peoria Ave (west);
.
or
.
(2) exit the airport, drive north on 32nd Street and connect with either Route 202 west or I-10 West.   Follow 202 or I-10 to I-17.  Take I-17 north to Flagstaff.   
Exit Peoria Avenue (west).
.Map #2 - Phoenix Metro Center Area
After exiting on Peoria Avenue (west), continue several blocks to 31st Avenue.  Turn left (south) on 31st Avenue. Drive approximately 3 blocks to Cheryl Drive.
The National Training Center occupies the SW corner of 31st Avenue and Cheryl Drive.
