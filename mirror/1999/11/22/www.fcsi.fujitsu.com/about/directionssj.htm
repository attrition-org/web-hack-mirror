Directions to FCSI - San Jose Office

Fujitsu Compound Semiconductor, Inc. (FCSI), is conveniently located in the heart of "Silicon Valley". FCSI is within easy driving distance from all three major area airports; just minutes from San Jose International, Oakland International, and San Francisco International.

  • From the San Jose International, travel north on Highway 101 to the Trimble Road exit. Follow Trimble Road east until Zanker Road, turn right and drive to 2355 Zanker Road.

  • From Oakland International, travel south on Interstate Highway 880 to Montague Expressway. Follow Montague Expressway west, turn left onto Trimble Road, then drive to Zanker Road, turn left and drive to 2355 Zanker Road.

  • From San Francisco International, travel south on Highway 101 to Trimble Road, follow Trimble Road east to Zanker Road, turn right and drive to 2355 Zanker Road.
