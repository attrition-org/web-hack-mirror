PREFACE
For more than twenty years, Fujitsu has been a pioneer in the development of devices for fiber-optic communication systems. In fact, devices developed by Fujitsu have themselves improved and enlarged the scope of what fiber-optic systems can do today.

In 1979, Fujitsu introduced the first commercially available low-noise germanium avalanche photodiodes and long wavelength laser diodes with single-mode operation.

In 1987, Fujitsu introduced the most advanced InGaAs avalanche photodiodes and InGaAsP distributed feed-back lasers in the fiber-optic market.

In 1992, Fujitsu introduced the first commercially available distributed feedback laser with an integrated electro-absorption modulator which dramatically increased the link lengths achievable from 2.5 Gbs systems.

Fujitsu today remains the world leader in laser diodes and photodetectors for the CATV and telecom fiber optic markets. The present offerings include:
  1. Low cost fabry perot lasers and PIN photodiodes for fiber in the loop applications.
  2. Pump lasers.
  3. Electro-absorption modulator integrated, directly modulated, CW source DFB's and optical receivers for WDM and single wavelength long haul applications at bit rates up to 2.5 GB/s.
  4. High power analog and return path laser for CATV applications.
  5. GaAs ICs for optical components.

In short, Fujitsu offers the most complete line-up of lightwave products with the highest standards of quality and reliability in the market today.

For special requirements, Fujitsu is also the right company to call on. Our local technical staff will, on short notice, assist you in achieving the most economical solution for your special requirements.

Every effort has been made to ensure that this catalog contains clear, correct product specifications and descriptions along with recommended applications for Fujitsu products. Whatever your fiber-optic requirements, there is a Fujitsu product to meet your needs.
