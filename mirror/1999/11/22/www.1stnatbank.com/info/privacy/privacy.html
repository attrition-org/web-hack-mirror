Privacy Statement

Visitors to the First National Bank of Homestead (FNBH) public Web site remain anonymous. FNBH does not collect identifying information about visitors to our site.

FNBH uses standard software to collect non-identifying information about our visitors, such as:

  • date and time our site was accessed
  • IP address (A numeric address given to servers connected to the Internet)
  • Web browser used
  • City, state, and country.

FNBH uses this information to create summary statistics and to determine the level of interest in information available on our site.

Visitors may elect to provide us with personal information via E-mail, or our guest book. This information is used internally, as appropriate, to handle the sender's request. It is not disseminated or sold to other organizations.

The Online Banking private section of our Web site uses Secure Socket Layer (SSL) encryption technology with a Verisign Digital Key. User security for Online Banking is controlled through the use of three user-defined identifying codes.
