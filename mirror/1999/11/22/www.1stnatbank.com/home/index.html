The year 2000 bug, also known as the millenium bug, is a major concern to all financial institutions. The bug exists in software or hardware that hold two-digit dates. The concern is that when the year 2000 (aka Y2K) comes around, the software or hardware will roll-over to 00 (which can be interpreted as 1900).
