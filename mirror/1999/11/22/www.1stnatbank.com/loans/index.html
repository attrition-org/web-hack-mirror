Welcome to the Loans Page! This page is an index to all of the loan accounts that we offer. Each type of loan is listed along with a brief description. Please see the individual loan pages for more details.

Agricultural - Agriculture is a major part of our local economy, which is why we specialize in Agricultural lending. We offer agricultural loans to all types of businesses - from farms to nurseries. Our goal is to help you grow and remain profitable in your operation. We are a guaranty lender through the United States Department of Agriculture Farm Service Agency Programs.

Business Loans - Small business plays a major role in the well being of our community. Our loan officers will take time to get to know your business and put a financing package together to meet your credit needs.

Consumer Loans - Loans for the purchase of a car, boat, motor home, special vacation, or that needed home improvement.

Mortgages - A home may be the single most important purchase of a lifetime and we will help you finance the purchase or refinance of that home. From construction to long term lending.
