17 September 1997 Traveling to Fort Rucker, Alabama


We're located in the Southeast corner of Alabama. Take US 231 to Ozark, Alabama and turn west on Andrews Avenue. Follow Andrews Avenue to the center of Fort Rucker (approximately 10 miles) and turn right on Dustoff Street. The hospital building is #301, the last building on the left.

Need a trip map? Try Map Quest, Maps on Us, or one of the many other mapping programs on the web.

We also have a map of Fort Rucker. This is a very large graphic.
