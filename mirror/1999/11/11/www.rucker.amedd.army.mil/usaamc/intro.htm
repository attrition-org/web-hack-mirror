    As a part of our marketing of the hospital and TRICARE,  "A FAMILY PRIME EVENING" is held the second Tuesday of each month in Room V-130 at 6:30pm. This evening introduces newcomers to our military health system and provides information on the services available at Lyster Army Hospital.  Retiree prospects are given the opportunity to gain insight into the TRICARE Program, and  Staff members from the Pharmacy, Preventive Medicine, Managed Care, TRICARE Service Center, and Acute Care provide briefings on subjects related to TRICARE and the Hospital.  The Pharmacy also provides wellness packages to attendees. Point of contact for more information is Martha Frausto at 255-7337.
