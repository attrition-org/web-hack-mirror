Administrative Services conducts the accounting, payments and financial functions for the MDA. It also provides personnel services for the department which include the preparation of forms and reports necessary to recruit, test, hire, promote and terminate employees. Office Management works closely with all divisions to resolve clerical overload, space allocation and utilization, inventory control and related matters. The Safety and Health Section is responsible for the MDA's employee safety program, reviewing all accidents and personal injuries to find causes and eliminate hazards. The officer also conducts work site inspection to ensure OSHA compliance.
