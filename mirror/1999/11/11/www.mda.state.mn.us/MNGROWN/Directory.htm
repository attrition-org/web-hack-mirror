Welcome to your personal guide to fresh, quality, fun Minnesota Grown agricultural products. This directory includes more than 425 locations statewide, including farmers' markets, farm bed and breakfasts, orchards, pick-your-own farms, Christmas tree farms, Community Supported Agriculture farms, and other locations that sell agricultural products direct to you.

Each farm/market listing includes information on location and products offered. Additional information provided by the grower may include: address/directions, hours of operation, phone, e-mail and web links.

Thanks for visiting. Whether you're looking for the hardiest ornamental products, agri-tourism opportunities, or any of the fine food products featured, you can be assured that our state's growers offer the best.
