Welcome...

...to the Army Declassification Activity(ADA) Home Page on the U.S. Army Records Managment and Declassification Agency (RMDA) Web Site. The ADA Home Page describes the Mission and Organization of the ADA.  It also provides an overview of the Army Declassification Plan and the Executive Order 12958 that it supports.   The review for declassification of documents is a records management function and is a mission of the Deputy Chief of Staff for Personnel, US Total Army Personnel Command, and The Adjutant General.
