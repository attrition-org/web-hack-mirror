Welcome...


...to the Army RecordsManagement Division (RMD) Home Page on the Army Records Management and Declassification Agency Web Site.  This site describes the Mission, Organization, and Objectives of the RMD. It also provides operational guidance on the creation, maintenance, and disposition of Army records.  The division is the operational manager for the Army Records Management Program  and is a mission of the Deputy Chief of Staff for Personnel, US Total Army Personnel Command, and The Adjutant General.
