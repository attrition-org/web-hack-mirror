Records Management and Declassification Agency Functions

- Manage the Army's records and files management, Freedom of Information/Privacy Act, Executive Order 12958 Declassification, and Electronic Records programs. Review functional areas to determine operational procedures and recommend new or updated policy.

- Develop plans, program/procedures to improve the Army's Records Management and Declassification Programs including use of advanced technology.

- Review MACOM program effectiveness.

- Maintain liaison with industry on latest recordkeeping hardware/software technology, facilitating migration of applicable technology into Army systems.

- Initiate and conduct studies and prototype tests of technological advances for applicability to Army recordkeeping applications.

- Responsible for Army-wide operational management of Maintenance and Disposition of Records, Duplicate Emergency Files, Privacy Act, Freedom of Information Act, Rulemaking, Correspondence, Official Mail Management, Terminology, Abbreviations and Brevity Codes, Vital Records, Special Records, and Compilation of Army Addressees.

- Assist ODCSPER in development of records management policies.

- Support Office, Special Assistant for Gulf War Illnesses

- Research Army records for information supporting investigations into Agent Orange, Gulf War Illness, and Post Traumatic Stress Disorder Claims.

- Review for declassification all classified Army records identified for automatic declassification under Executive Order 12958

- Maintain and manage the Army's Electronic Reading Room
