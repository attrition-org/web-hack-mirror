Welcome...

...to the US Armed Forces Center for Research of Unit Records (CRUR) Home Page on the U.S. Army Records Management and Declassification Agency (RMDA) Web Site. The CRUR Home Page describes the Mission and Organization of the CRUR.  It also provides an overview of the Center's support for Office, Special Assistant for Gulf War Illness, Agent Orange and Post Traumatic Stress Disorder Claims.  The Center's reseach is a records management function and is a mission of the Deputy Chief of Staff for Personnel, US Total Army Personnel Command, and The Adjutant General.
