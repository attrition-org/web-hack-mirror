If you are looking for military or civilian  personnel records, please go to

www.nara.gov/regional/stlouis.html.

To request records you will need to submit a written request  that

Includes:  Your Name, Serial Number/Social Number, Date of  Birth, Address, City , State, and Zip Code.

 

To assist in helping you search for other Army records please send an electronic mail message to

records@rmpo.belvoir.army.mil

 

Include what type of record you are looking for,

your Name, Address, City, State, and Zip Code.

A daytime telephone number and facsimle number is useful, but not required.
