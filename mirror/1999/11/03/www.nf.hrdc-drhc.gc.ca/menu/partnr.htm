Labour Market Development Agreement (LMDA)

  • LMDA Information - This agreement, effective April 1, 1997, reflects an innovative and creative approach to addressing the unique needs, problems, and challenges of Newfoundland's labour market.   Through a co-management initiative between the Governments of Canada and Newfoundland and Labrador, labour market programs and services will be developed to assist unemployed people to return to work.
