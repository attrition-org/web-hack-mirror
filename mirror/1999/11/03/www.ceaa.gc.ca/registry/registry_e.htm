Public Registry System

Canadian Environmental Assessment Act

The Federal Environmental Assessment Index (FEAI) is a master list of all the environmental assessments carried out under the Canadian Environmental Assessment Act that have been registered by responsible federal authorities. The index also provides contacts with each assessment in order to provide the public with an opportunity to obtain further information.
