Y2K compliance statement

 

Since April 1996, Statistics South Africa has been involved in a Y2K (Year 2000) compliance project. The National Year 2000 Decision Support Centre (NYDSC) and the Office of the Auditor-General are also involved in, or aware of this, project.

New computers and operating systems have been installed, and all software programmes in use are either being rewritten or re-engineered to ensure compliance. Project plans indicate that most, if not all, systems within Stats SA will be Y2K compliant in time.

However, Stats SA is dependent on a large number and variety of sources for statistical input, and - at least at this stage - it is not possible to guarantee that the systems of each and every Stats SA information supplier will be Y2K compliant by the due date.

Pieter Gildenhuys
Director: Information Systems
Statistics South Africa
November 1999
