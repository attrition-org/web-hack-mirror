Databases

ETOH Database
The Alcohol and Alcohol Problems Science Database, commonly referred to as ETOH is an online bibliographic database containing over 100,000 records on alcohol abuse and alcoholism. Access to ETOH requires a browser with frame capabilities. If your browser cannot view frames, please see the download guidelines.
