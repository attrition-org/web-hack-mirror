    • Occupation Summaries
      These give you answers to important questions on hundreds of occupations in your local area and beyond, including work descriptions, employment prospects and list of potential employers.

    • Labour Market News
      Our monthly newsletter keeps you up to date on industry related happenings - locally and across the province.

    • Employer Lists
      This section provides lists of potential employers in your area and across the province based on your occupation.

    • Nova Scotia Wage Information
      This report offers estimates of going wage rates for hundreds of occupations all across Nova Scotia.

    • Nova Scotia Statistics
      Find commonly requested statistics. Included are current and historical Labour Force Survey results (such as unemployment rates), and a monthly overview of Nova Scotia LFS results in the Nova Scotia Labour Force Notes. We offer access to Employment Insurance data, population counts and links to other useful statistics that can help with planning, reporting and analysis.

    • Labour Market Reviews
      An annual review which will help you understand trends and developments in your labour market. Keeping informed of these trends will allow you to adapt to future changes and developments.

    • Community Profiles
      The Community Profile section will give you a snapshot of selected communities in Nova Scotia, providing information on employement, housing, hospitals, government organizations, and schools.

    • Industry Summaries
      Your resource for information on industries across the province including the number of people employed, current related news articles and our five-year provincial forecast.

    • Upcoming Opportunities

      • When a major business announces an expansion or a new company is coming to town, check here for information on what skills will be required, what jobs are being created and how to apply.
