    • HRDC Nova Scotia Job Bank
      The HRDC Nova Scotia Job Listings show selected job openings posted in Human Resource Centres across Nova Scotia, along with many hard-to-fill job orders for the rest of Canada.

    • HRDC National Job Bank
      Search for jobs across Canada by job title or location. Complete inventories of job postings or recent updates in the past 48 hours are available

      These sites can help you find work with government
      Canada's Federal Public Service. (including Career Alert).

      Opportunities with Canadian Municipalities.

      A selection of work opportunities from across our province.

    • Electronic Labour Exchange
      An electronic matching service for workers and employers. Create a profile of your skills and experience, find job opportunities, advertise your skills and save your profile for future use.

    • Worksearch - Work Links
      The Worksearch site deals with all aspects of work search. The Work Links option will do job matching across the country and provide an automatic email posting to inform clients regularly of job opportunities in their field.
