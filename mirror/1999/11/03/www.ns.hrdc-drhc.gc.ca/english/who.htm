Who We Are What We Do

Human Resources Development Canada (HRDC) is the federal government department that helps Canadians prepare for, find and keep work. We also assist Canadians in their efforts to provide security for themselves and their families, and we do our best to promote a fair, safe, healthy, stable, cooperative and productive work environment for all Canadians. Our Programs & Services page will give you more specific information about us and what we do.

This site brings together all of the offices of HRDC that are located within the Nova Scotia Region.
