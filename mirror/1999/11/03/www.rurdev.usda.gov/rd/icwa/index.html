The Second International Conference
on
Women in Agriculture
June 28 - July 2, 1998
Omni Shoreham Hotel
Washington, D.C.

Planning for the New Millennium

Bringing together women from all walks of life in agricultural communities to network with experts, meet their peers, exchange
views with policymakers, and develop strategies and devise recommendations to improve our world.


President Clinton proclaimed October 15, 1997, as International Rural Women's Day. Please read this historic proclamation.

Please visit the Women in Agriculture (WIA) website, on-line since January 20, 1999. The WIA site follows-up on the First and Second Conferences and is part of a worldwide communications network for ag women.

You will exit the Rural Development server when you visit the WIA website.

This Conference is sponsored by the President's Interagency
Council on Women and is hosted by the United States Department of Agriculture.

Please visit our Breakout Sessions site and our Conference Agenda site for details on the Conference schedule, including the speakers for events, and the times and topics for all workshops.
