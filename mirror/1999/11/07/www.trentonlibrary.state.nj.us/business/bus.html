DESCRIPTION:

Our Business and Technology Department has moved to the 2nd floor to join with our Reference Department. All of the many resources in the subject areas of business, technology and the hard sciences are still in the collection including:

  • Over 250 newspapers and periodicals dealing with business and/or technology
  • Some 23 financial newsletters
  • CDROM databases, including Dun's Business Locator and Moody's Company Data and Moody's International Company Data
  • A wide range of business directories, including Dun & Bradstreet's, Consultant's Directory, Ward's Business Directory, Dalton's Philadelphia Metro Directory, Directory of NJ Manufacturers, McFadden American Financial Directory, and others
  • Telephone directories from across the country, plus a comprehensive telephone directory on CDROM
  • Job Information Center for jobseekers
  • Inhouse index of various NJ business magazines and newspapers
  • And many other sources dealing with management, accounting, import/export, franchising, advertising, retailing, real estate, banking, starting and running a small business, personnel management, economics, government auctions, computers, mathematics, etc.

Professional staff members are available to assist you with questions, either in person or by telephone.

For other business information available on the Internet, check out the Business and Finance link on the List of Links.
