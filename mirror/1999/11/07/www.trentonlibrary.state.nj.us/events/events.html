SPECIAL EVENTS AT TRENTON PUBLIC LIBRARY

The Main Library now features a STAR WARS Exhibit.
A wide variety of toys and books are featured in the display which is located in the lobby in front of the Children's Department. Most of the items were loaned to the library by Trenton resident Eric Coumbe.

The theme of the movies at the Main Library in November and December is
LEGENDS OF FILM:

JEZEBEL - November 4 at 6:30pm
REBEL WITHOUT A CAUSE - November 18 at 6:30pm
MATA HARI - December 2 at 6:30pm
HIGH NOON - December 16 at 6:30pm

The films are shown in the meeting room on the first floor, admission is free and secured parking is available in our parking lot.
