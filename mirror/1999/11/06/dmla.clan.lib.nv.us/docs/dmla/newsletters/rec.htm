Nevada Records is a quarterly publication of the Nevada Department of Museums, Library and Arts, Nevada State Library and Archives. It is distributed to all local governmental entities in Nevada, and to other recipients who have expressed an interest. Edited by Bill Bowden. We welcome your ideas, comments and suggestions, and we solicit items of interest from our readers. For information, call the editor at (775) 684-3425.

Interested parties desiring to be placed on our mailing list should contact the editor at the above phone number or by Email at: bbowden@clan.lib.nv.us
