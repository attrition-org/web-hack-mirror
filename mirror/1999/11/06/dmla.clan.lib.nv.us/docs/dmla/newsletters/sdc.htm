The SDC Report is published tri-annually by the Nevada State Data Center (NvSDC), a service of the Nevada State Library and Archives. The NvSDC is part of a national network of state data center lead agencies and statewide affiliate offices. The NvSDC and its Nevada affiliates can provide an abundance of demographic data about Nevada or a specific county, city, congressional district, census tract, voting district or Native American lands within our state.
