Our New Look And Feel

After many weeks of hard work, we have redesigned the Department of Museums, Library and Arts Homepage. This page is all about how to get the most out of your visit to our site.

Let's Go!
If you're in a hurry, want to get right to the content, and skip the rest of this "How To" page, the Site Map has links to all our major areas.
What about shortcuts?
You can select from our "Department of Museums, Library and Archives" menu bar (located at the top of every page) anytime to jump immediately to the beginning of any of our major sections. They provide you with links that take you to Search (Search this site and the WWW), Site Map (a detailed site index), Events Calendar, News Releases, What's New (updates to this site) and Email (a form for questions).
How do I navigate this site?
The navigation buttons on the left will take you to another page within your current section. At the bottom of every page there is a link to the DMLA Homepage as well as the State of Nevada Homepage.
How do I get more information?
We have provided an individual email link for each of our divisions so you can contact them directly. Every page has an email button in the menu bar at the top of the page.
What is the image on the DMLA Homepage?
The image is an architectural drawing for the Carson City Mint (1870-1893), now home to the Nevada State Museum

Your comments and/or suggestions are very much welcomed and appreciated! Thanks for visiting our web site. We hope you have found this brief overview helpful.

Now put your seat in the upright position and get ready for take off. Click on one of the menu topics at the top of this page, or click on Site Map to begin your trip through our site.
