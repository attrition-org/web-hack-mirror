GENERAL ENQUIRES

General information

If you would like more information about a specific responsibility or area of the Australian Broadcasting Authority. contact: info @aba.gov.au

Complaints about programs

If you would like to make a complaint about a television or radio program, you should first make your complaint directly to the station concerned. The station should tell you how it intends to deal with the complaint and what opportunities are available to you to take the matter further. If you are dissatisfied with the station's response or if the station does not answer your complaint within 60 days, you may then complain to the ABA.

Media releases and Publications

If you would like more information about a news release or to purchase a publication, please contact the media and public relations section: info @aba.gov.au
