elcome to the Business Opportunities Office Home Page. It is the policy of the Jet Propulsion Laboratory (JPL) that small businesses, small disadvantaged businesses, and women-owned businesses have an equitable opportunity to compete for contracts. JPL has an aggressive program designed to afford small businesses the maximum opportunity to compete for contracts, and to place the maximum number of contracts for supplies and services with them, without decreasing the performance efficiency of any contract. The responsibility to increase the opportunities for small businesses to compete for contracts lies with the Business Opportunities Office.

his home page provides current information about resources available to businesses seeking contracting opportunities at JPL.
