Tower District
Home of the Tower Theater, the Tower District has become known as our own special community within the city. Offering fine restaurants, dinner theaters, coffee shops and several small shops, the Tower is a great place to spend an afternoon or evening.

The Tower District offers a wide variety of events for the community including:

Halloween in the Tower
Fat Tuesday - Mardi Gras Celebration
Cigar Fest

For more information visit the Tower Districts Website.

The 1999 Hoop-It-Up 3 on 3 Basketball Tournament will be held on October 9 and 10 in downtown Fresno. If you are interested in receiving an entry form for the tournament or volunteering, please call the Parks, Recreation and Community Department at (559) 498-4923.

Whether you're a Top Gun or a rusty gun, we'll do our best to match your team against teams on your playing level. Whether you're 8 or 80, male or female, play every day or haven't played in five years, you'll get fun games, fairly matched. Top Gun Division winners will earn the right to represent this city at the Regional Playoffs, and if they win there, at the National Championships, which will be broadcast on NBC Sports.
