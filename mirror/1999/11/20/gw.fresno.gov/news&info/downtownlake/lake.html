Plans For A Proposed Downtown Lake Unveiled
Councilmember Bredefeld annouces plans for a proposed downtown lake which will include a water feature for the Fulton Mall.  Introduced will be the lake design and color drawings of its features and vision for Fresno.  The 14 acre lake will include a community walk/park which will perimeter the lake as well as 30 acres of prime commercial land bordering the lake front property. 

The unique lake design will provide the pivotal piece  to tie the  
 
downtown area together by connecting the Fulton Mall, Downtown Stadium, Uptown Cultural Center, Convention Center and Freeway 41.  The creation of this downtown lake would ensure that this land in the downtown area would again become prime real estate.
 
Joining Councilmember Bredefeld will be Councilmember Dan Ronquillo and  members of the Downtown Lake Committee.  Large color illustrations of the Downtown Lake and Fulton Mall water feature will be on display.
