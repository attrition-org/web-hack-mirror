Fresno Parks & Recreation Department offers a variety of programs for all ages. Youth activities include baseball, swimming and hockey. Senior programs are offered at most park and recreation facilities and include activities such as exercising, arts and crafts, bingo, ceramics, senior clubs, card clubs, parties and bus trips.
 
Experience a wide range of out door recreation.  A short driving distance to three of the countries finest National Forest: Yosemite, Sierra and Sequoia National; Fresno is an ideal location for the outdoor enthusiast. Enjoy bicycling, camping, canoeing, horseback riding and of course skiing at Sierra Summit. Just 65 miles northeast of Fresno, Sierra Summit Ski Resort offers beginners, intermediates and experts some of the best ski terrain in the world.
