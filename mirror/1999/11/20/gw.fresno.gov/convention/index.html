Located in the heart of California–and in the heart of Fresno–our award winning community complex encompasses over 5 city blocks. It is regarded as one of the most unique and versatile meeting and event centers in the Western United States, averaging over 650 events per year. The Fresno Convention Center is the site of concerts of all sorts–Country, Jazz, Rock, Hispanic, Classical, Christian Contemporary and many more. There are many wonderful plays and musicals performed here each year. The complex consists of four separate, but compatible, structures. What makes this complex so truly versatile is its ability to accommodate a major convention, sporting event, and big box office entertainment performance–simultaneously. 

At capacity, the three main facilities can seat over 19,000 people at one time. Combined parking areas have space for 1,300 cars. Our Fresno Convention & Conference Center can accommodate groups of many different sizes.
