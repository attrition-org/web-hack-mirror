This Study Guide comes from the many questions which arose when I read 'Ways of the Strega' by Raven Grimassi. The Questions are chapter by chapter to help you through the book. Even if you have read the book before I urge you to sit down with it again and answer these questions. Some are easy; some will challenge your thought process and all will help you gain a deeper knowledge of 'La Vecchia Religione'. You may want to keep a journal. The information presented on my site does not even come close to exploring all that is 'Stregheria'.
