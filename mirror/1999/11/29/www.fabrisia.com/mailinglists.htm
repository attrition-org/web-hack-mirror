Mailing Lists are like a private newsgroup. When you join (subscribe) all messages sent by other members are also sent to you via your e-mail. You can reply to the whole group, or privately to a member - or just read all the messages. Mailing Lists rely on input from the members who can sometimes get a little 'off topic' so descriptions of what's discussed (below) are guides only. If you know of any mailing lists suitable to this site (ie: new age, pagan, witchcraft, wiccan etc.) please let me know.

Animal Totems/Familiars majordomo@witchhaven.com - This list if for discussion about Animals within the Craft. to join send an email with the words: subscribe animals in the text of the message.

Celtic Lore - Specifically devoted to the discussion of Celtic folklore and traditions.

Herbs majordomo@witchhaven.com - This list is for discussions about the Magickal/Medical uses of Herbs. To join send email with the words subscribe herbs n the text of the message.

Italian New Age List - In Italian for Italian speakers.

Magickal Theory Mailing List - The purpose of discussing the how and why behind ritual.

Solo listproc@listproc.bgsu.edu - Discussion forum for solitary practitioners of peaceful spiritual paths. To join send email with the words: subscribe solo in the text of the message.

Archangels - A group of people from all religions who enjoy learning about the Angels. Angels belong to all faiths, not just those of one religion.

Tarot tarot-l-request@dragon.org -To join send email with the words: subscribe tarot-l in the text of the message.

Wiccan & Pagan Study Group - The mailing list for Southern Hemisphere Pagans.

Learning the Tarot - This list is for anyone who wants to explore Tarot in depth -- from the beginner who may not even have a deck yet, to the reader with years of experience.

Mediums - A list for discussing mediums and mediumship, spirit communications and after-death communications.

MoonShade - A mailing list for European Pagans; The topics of this list are Paganism, Wicca, magic, tarot, astrology, runes, basically anything pagan you can think of.

GoddessChild Mailing List - allows children (ages 8 to 16) to correspond with other Pagan children
