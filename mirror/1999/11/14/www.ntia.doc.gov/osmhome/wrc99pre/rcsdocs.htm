NTIA Radio Conference Subcommittee (RCS) Documents

 

The proposals and preliminary views contained in the letters below reflect the output of the RCS. Some items were drafted by the RCS and reflect the federal government's perspective. In other cases the proposals or preliminary views were drafted by the FCC’s Informal Work Groups and have been reviewed and modified by the RCS. NTIA and FCC representatives will work together to come to a final agreement on each proposal or preliminary view. In many cases, these proposals and preliminary views have been further modified during negotiations between NTIA and the FCC. For the current versions of the Draft Proposals and Preliminary Views go back to the home page and view the files contained under heading "WRC-2000 Documents".

Do you need to comment on any of these proposals or preliminary views? Federal government representatives should send their comments to the RCS Secretary wmitchell@ntia.doc.gov; persons not associated with the federal government should send their comments to the FCC Advisory Committee for WRC-2000, WRC-99@fcc.gov. Please be sure to provide sufficient information identifying the issue. For more information on the FCC's preparation for WRC -2000, please go to the FCC web page for WRC-2000.

You may send general questions about WRC-2000 activities to wmitchell@ntia.doc.gov at NTIA.or dweiland@fcc.gov at the FCC.
