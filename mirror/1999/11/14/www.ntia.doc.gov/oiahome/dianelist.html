NTIA'S OFFICE OF INTERNATIONAL AFFAIRS

Related International Websites
(Diane's List)

NTIA's Office of International Affairs provides the following list of hypertext links as a service to the general public, without vouching for the veracity of any information contained therein. Please email the webmaster if you have additional useful links. Please note that we try to include sites that are institutional, organizational, or educational in nature; corporate or marketing sites will not be listed unless they provide unusually valuable information regarding international and corporate trends and/or valuable hypertext links. This site is organized by:

(1) GENERAL INTERNATIONAL TELECOMMUNICATIONS: (a) General Links, (b) Telecom & Mass Media News Services, (c) Development Issues, (d) Equipment Licensing Information, (e) Accounting Rates & Settlement Issues, (f) International Standards, (g) Satellite Information, including Orbital Slots, (h) Spectrum, Wireless & PCS,      (i) Submarine Cable Information;

AND

(2) GENERAL INTERNET INTERNATIONAL INFORMATION: (a) General Policy, (b) Internet Surveys, (c) Internet & Interactive News Services, (d) General Associations, (e) Internet Societies, and

(f) Issue Areas: 1)  Governance ,   2)  Information Technology, 3) Intellectual Property Rights, 4) International Electronic Commerce, 5) International Voice Telephony, 6) Network Interconnection/Security/Reliability,             7) Privacy.

Then, covering both Major Topics, by Region, Individual Country/Market, International Organizations, and

Other Subjects: Governmental Administrative Sites, News Services, Embassies, New Applications, Travel Services: Airlines,  Rail ,  Currency Converters, Customs Information Worldwide, Hotel Bookings, Mapping Sites, People Searches/Directories, Travelogues.
