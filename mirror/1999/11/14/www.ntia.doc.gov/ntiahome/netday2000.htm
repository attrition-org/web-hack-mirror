Information on NET DAY 2000




  • Assistant Secretary Larry Irving encourages business leaders to develop public-private partnerships with schools. In an address before organizers and participants in the NetDay movement to wire schools across the nation, Irving proposed a partnership between schools and businesses similar to the Adopt-A-Highway program. (Full text of speech) (2-11-97)
