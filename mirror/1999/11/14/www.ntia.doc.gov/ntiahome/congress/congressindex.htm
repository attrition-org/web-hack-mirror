NTIA Congressional Testimony

  • Acting Associate Administrator Kelly Levy delivered testimony on H.R. 1746, the Schools and Libraries Internet Access Act, before the House Subcommittee on Telecommunications, 9-30-1999.

  • NTIA Administrator Larry Irving testified on "The Digital Divide: Bridging the Technological Gap" before the Empowerment Subcommittee of the House Committee on Small Business, 07-27-99.

  • Testimony of Andrew J. Pincus, General Counsel, Department of Commerce, on progress towards transitioning management of the Internet domain name system ("DNS") to the private sector, delivered before the Subcommittee on Oversight & Investigations, House Commerce Committee, July 22, 1999.

  • Assistant Secretary Larry Irving testified on the reauthorization of NTIA before the Telecommunications Subcommittee of the House Commerce Committee, May 11, 1999.

  • October 7, 1998 Testimony of Becky Burr before the House committee on Science, Subcommittees on Basic Research and Technology on Domain Names.

  • NTIA's testimony on the future of the Internet domain name system (DNS) and issues surrounding its management, before the House Committee on Commerce, Subcommittee on Telecommunications, Trade and Consumer Protection on June 10, 1998.

  • NTIA delivered testimony for the Executive Branch on H.R. 1872, the Communications Satellite Competition and Privatization Act of 1997, before the House Committee on Commerce, Subcommittee on Telecommunications, Trade and Consumer Protection, 09-30-97.

  • Testimony of Larry Irving, Assistant Secretary of Commerce for Communications and Information before the House Committee on Science, Subcommittee on Basic Research on Internet Domain Name policies. An Appendix to the testimony provides a summary of comments received in the NTIA/PTO domain name inquiry, 09-25-97

  • Assistant Secretary Larry Irving testified before the Senate Committee on Commerce, Science and Transportation on the views of the Administration on the transition to digital television, 09-17-97.

  • NTIA testified on International Satellite Reform before the Senate Subcommittee on Communications, July 30, 1997.

  • Assistant Secretary Larry Irving delivered testimony on the reauthorization of NTIA before the House Subcommittee on Telecommunications, Trade, and Consumer Protection of the Committee on Commerce, 04-24-97.
