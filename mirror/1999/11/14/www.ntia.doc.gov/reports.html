A report by NTIA's Spectrum Planning and Policy Advisory Committee (SPAC) summarizes and discusses policy considerations that are relevant to the transfer of radio spectrum from Federal Government to the private sector and/or sharing of Federal Government spectrum with private sector users. This report is the result of the SPAC's efforts to study and advise the NTIA on the problems inherent in recent loss of government spectrum. The report is not a publication of the NTIA, but is intended as advice to the NTIA and other bodies governing the use of the radio communications spectrum.
