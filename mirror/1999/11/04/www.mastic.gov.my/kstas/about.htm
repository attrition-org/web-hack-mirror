About MOSTE

Ministry of Science, Technology and the Environment, Malaysia (MOSTE)

Background

The Ministry of Science, Technology and the Environment Malaysia (MOSTE) was established in 1973 as the Ministry of Technology, Research and Local Government.

In 1976, the Ministry assumed its present name in view of the more targeted role it plays in promoting and developing science and technology in the country.

Mission

To promote science and technology competence for international competitivenes while ensuring environmental conservation and sustainable development.

Objectives

  • To promote understanding, awareness and appreciation of science and technology.
  • To implement science and technology research development.
  • To conserve and monitor the environment.
  • To provide technical and management support services.
