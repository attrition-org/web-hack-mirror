    QUICKTIME allows you to use video with sound, animation, music and text. Quicktime 2.0 and above provides support for: MPEG, searcheable antialiased text, MIDI music and compressed 16-bit audio. Maximum replay quality with no help from hardware is 320x240 at 30 frames per second. Quicktime movies, when assisted by hardware, are fully capable of broadcast-quality video.
