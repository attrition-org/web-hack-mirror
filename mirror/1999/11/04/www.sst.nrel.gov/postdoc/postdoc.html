Applications for Post-Doctoral Positions

NREL's Solid State Theory Group accepts each year 1-2 highly qualified new postdoctoral researchers and research associates for a 2-3 year appointment. The process of hiring is highly competitive. The group performs state-of-the-art theoretical research in the areas of

  • Quantum Nanostructures

  • Alloy Phase Stability (metals, semiconductors, oxides).

  • Photovoltaic Semiconductor Materials

  • Development of Methods for Large Scale Electronic Structure Calculations.

The group has a distinguished record of publication and innovation, which has resulted in a highly successful citation record.

Postdoctoral and research associate positions are for two years, renewable upon mutual agreement to a third year. Salary range is $44,000-53,000 per year. The Solid State Theory Group currently consists of ten Ph.D.'s in condensed matter theory and interacts with a broad range of experimentalists at NREL. (See an online list of current and past personnel in the group.) Furthermore, the group has outstanding computational facilities: remote access to T3E and C90, and a local computing system consisting of a 16 node SGI Origin 2000 and 10 IBM RISC machines. The group also has an excellent basic-research atmosphere, and is located in the beautiful Rocky Mountains (less than one hour from hiking and ski resorts).

  • When to apply: Jan. 1 - Mar. 15
  • Acceptance decisions are made: March-April
  • Work starts: Aug.-Oct.
  • What to submit: Interested candidates should send in writing (no e-mail) a curriculum vitae, list of publications (including preprints of unpublished papers, if possible) and arrange for two references, addressed to:
