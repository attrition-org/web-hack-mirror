Admissions Information

Admissions Procedures for Certificate Programs

Submit completed application with a $15.00 non-refundable application fee. Certain certificate programs may require placement testing and other related documents. Contact the Office of Student Services for more information.

Admissions Procedures for Diploma and Cooperative Degree Programs

Submit completed application with a $15.00 non-refundable application fee. Request the last high school attended to forward an official transcript to the Student Services Office. GED scores may also be accepted. Transcripts from colleges and/or technical schools attended must be submitted. A high school diploma or GED is required for admission into some programs. All students must have a GED or high school diploma prior to graduation. Make qualifying scores on the placement test offered at Lanier Technical Institute. SAT or ACT scores may be used in place of placement testing. Some programs may have additional requirements. Contact the Office of Student Services for detailed information.

Registration

Applicants will receive an official acceptance letter, which will specify a date and time to report for registration. Final acceptance will depend upon completed application and space availability in the program. If an application cannot be processed for the quarter indicated due to the program being filled or not beginning that quarter, the file will be processed for the next available quarter. A $25.00 late fee will be charged at late registration.

Fees

Tuition and fees for full-time students are $298.00 plus books and supplies. These fees are paid each quarter. Fees are $21.00 per credit hour. Additional quarterly fees include a $25.00 activity fee and a $21.00 registration fee. Books and supplies cost extra.

Financial Aid

Students who have applied and have been approved to receive Financial Aid may register by invoice during the registration process. Tuition, activity fees and registration fees may be funded by Financial Aid. Students should be prepared to pay insurance fees and exemption fees. Contact the Office of Financial Aid for more information by calling 770.781.6946.
