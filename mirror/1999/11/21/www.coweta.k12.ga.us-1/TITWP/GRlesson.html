BUSINESS LETTER: COVER LETTER
Eleventh Grade

OBJECTIVE: To write a cover letter to be attached to an application for a job.

ACTIVITIES:

1. Fill out an application.

2. Read the situation.

    Situation: The following advertisement appeared in your local paper on Saturday. You want to be given an interview for the job. (Make up any information to complete the letter.)
      Hospitality Worker/Food Service. Ambitious person with pleasant personality needed.

      Restaurant experience helpful but not necessary.

      Apply to Director, Hospitality Services, Peachtree Regional Hospital,

      Newnan, GA 30263

3. Write a cover letter to the director. When writing this letter, be certain to do the following:
    a. Use block business letter form.

    b. Explain why you are writing.

    c. Point out your interests, experiences, and background.

    d. Proofread for any mechanical errors.

EVALUATION: The teacher will check student letters, especially the achievement of a., b., c., d. on number 3.
