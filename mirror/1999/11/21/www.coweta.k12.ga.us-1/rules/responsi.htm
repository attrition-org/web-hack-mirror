Faculty Responsibilities for Student Internet Use

Students must display their licenses while using the Internet.
If in doubt about a site, go HOME immediately.
A license is good for only the student named, not for group research.
Licenses confiscated for improper use shall be returned to tech support with a written statement.
Students are NEVER to be left unattended while using the Internet.  Teachers are responsible for monitoring their students.
Students should never share their passwords with other students.
NEVER give students a teacher or sysop password.
