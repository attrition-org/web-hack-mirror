African American History

 

Here are some great sites for Black History Month

  • Photo Tour - Take a photo tour of the civil rights movement. This site has a collection of 27 pictures documenting significant events in the struggle for equal rights.
  • United States Postage Stamps - See the many African Americans that have been honored by the Post Office. Included are pictures of the stamps and short biological information about the person being recognized.
  • Martin Luther King, Jr. - Stanford University has a comprehensive listing of MLK,Jr.'s papers and several links to other information about him. If you have audio capabilities, you can hear a portion of his "I Have a Dream" speech.
  • African Americans in the Sciences - Very informative site with a list of the contributions of many African Americans in the sciences. Included are links to other pages, including one on George Washington Carver.
  • Overview of Black History - World Book has designed this site which provides a tour of five different periods in Black American History.
