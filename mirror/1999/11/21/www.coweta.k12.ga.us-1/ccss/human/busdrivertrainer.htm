POSITION TITLE: School Bus Driver Trainer

JOB DESCRIPTION:

Conducts training for all new school bus drivers in accordance with Georgia State Department of Education and Georgia State Patrol requirements and guidelines. Evaluates driver proficiency and makes training assignments based on evaluated weak areas. Plans and conducts bus rider safety training program for elementary students. Schedules and manages school bus evacuation training program. Evaluates new safety and training materials. Reviews and maintains disciplinary report for driver continuation training. Plans and conducts annual driver skills training. Rides with and observes drivers when required. Prepares trainees for CDL evaluation and maintains all associated training records. Maintains MVR records. Conducts classroom training and coordinates additional hands-on training for school bus monitors. Assists in presentations during driver safety meetings.

REQUIRED EDUCATION, CERTIFICATION OR TRAINING:

Minimum high school diploma or GED

REQUIRED SKILLS:

Must have good oral and written communication skills. Must complete Georgia State Patrol School Bus Driver Training Program - Basic and Advanced

REQUIRED EXPERIENCE:

Minimum five (5) years school bus driver experience
