POSITION TITLE: School Clerk - Madras Middle - 205 Day

JOB DESCRIPTION:

Greet visitors courteously, determining their needs and direct or escort them to the proper person. Receive and route incoming calls, answer questions of callers and take messages for staff members. Assist in preparing reports, type from copy, rough draft or detailed instructions. Open and distribute mail. Operate copy machine, Apple and IBM computer equipment and fax machine. Maintain files. Check students in/out of school. Perform such other duties as may be required to assure the smooth and efficient operation of the school.

REQUIRED EDUCATION, CERTIFICATION OR TRAINING:

Minimum high school diploma, appropriate clerical and business training/experience

REQUIRED SKILLS:

Basic knowledge of business English, general vocabulary, spelling, math and a working knowledge of modern office practices and procedures. Ability to maintain records and files; ability to work harmoniously with other employees; ability to meet and deal with the public in an effective and tactful manner.

REQUIRED EXPERIENCE:

Two (2) years acceptable clerical experience preferred
