Department of Human Resources

The Director of of Human Resources is Blake Bass. His office is located on the first floor of the Jackson Street facility. Applications may be picked up at this office or may be downloaded from the web in PDF format (requires Adobe Acrobat Reader).

Instructional applications are available to all persons wishing to apply for vacancies in the Coweta County School System. The issuance of any application does not imply that a vacancy exists or that the applicant will be considered for immediate employment.

Applications should be completed and returned to the Department of Human Resources, P.O.Box 280, Newnan, Georgia 30264, (770)254-2803. Any questions concerning an application or system vacancies may be directed to that office.

The reference forms included with the application should be filled in at the top and given or mailed to your listed references. If you have teaching experience, you should send the forms to the principal or superintendent of your most recent employment. If you are just entering the teaching field, you should send the forms to the principal and teacher where you completed your practice teaching experience. Other forms may be sent to college teachers who have worked with you in your major field. All references must be mailed directly to the Department of Human Resources and will be considered confidential information.

A copy of every college transcript should be included. Unofficial copies will be accepted.

Teachers must hold or be eligible for valid Georgia teaching certificates. Copies of valid teaching certificates should be attached to the application. Information regarding certification may be obtained from the Professional Standards Commission, Twin Towers East, Atlanta, Georgia 30334.

Applications will be screened by the Department of Human Resources. Based upon the application, supporting documents, references, and personnel needs of the Coweta County School System, personal interviews may be scheduled.

Upon receipt of all information requested, the application will be placed in an active file where it will remain through two hiring cycles. Materials submitted in the application package will not be returned.

You will be notified if additional information is needed or if an interview is to be scheduled. The Department of Human Resources should be informed of changes which occur in the information in this application.
