Titanic TeacherNet

A Focus on Non-Fiction Literature

 

The purpose of this web site is to provide students with an overview of the Titanic. Historical accounts, a look at myths and misunderstandings, newspaper clippings, and photographs are all areas that we will look at during this lesson.

This is an introduction to the Titanic and historical information about the voyage.

Chronological History of the Titanic- This is a day by day, hour by hour account of what happened.

Facts and myths about what happened to the Titanic.

Visit this site to learn about the recovery efforts for the Titanic.

Lesson Plan<>

1. Click here to see the worksheet that goes with this lesson.
You may then print it out for use with your class. 

2. Explore the web sites and complete the worksheet of the History of the Titanic.

3. Discuss the difference between historical non- fiction and fiction.

4. Research to find more information about the Titanic.
