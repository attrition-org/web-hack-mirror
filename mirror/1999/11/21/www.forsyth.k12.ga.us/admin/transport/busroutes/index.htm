Transportation
Bus Route Information

At this section of the website, you will find:
  • General Bus Route Information you need to know
  • Instructions for finding specific bus routes

General Information:

  • Elementary routes will start at 7:00 a.m.
  • Middle and High routes will start at 7:30 a.m.
  • Middle and high school students in the Central HS and South HS areas of the county will be transported on separate buses. In the North HS area middle and high school students are transported together.
Instructions for finding specific bus routes:
  1. First, select the appropriate school from the drop-down list above. This will take you to a new page for your specific school.

 

  1. At the next screen, choose the appropriate bus number from the drop-down list. (If you don't know the bus number, simply view each bus number's route until you find the stop closest to your street).
