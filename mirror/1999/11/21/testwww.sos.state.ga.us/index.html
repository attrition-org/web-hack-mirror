Welcome to our website. In the Georgia Secretary of State’s office we are committed to making this the most customer-friendly agency in state government.

The Secretary of State is one of Georgia's oldest Constitutional offices. Our responsibilities are diverse, and range from administering the state's election laws to registering corporations to investigating charities and telemarketing fraud.

Our work touches millions of Georgians each year. At this site you can search for a corporate name, check on a dentist's record, download an application for a CPA's license or interact with us in scores of different ways that once required a phone call, a letter or a personal visit.

If you have questions or suggestions on how we can better serve you, our valued customer, please e-mail me at sosweb@sos.state.ga.us.
