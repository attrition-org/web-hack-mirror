Application Procedure for Teaching Positions

    STEP 1: Applicant should submit a completed employment application.

      STEP 2: Applicant should submit the items required to complete his/her applicant file.

        ---A complete file will contain:

  •             Completed professional employment application.
  •             Transcripts of all work completed.
  •             A minimum of three(3) professional references (college placement files are acceptable for entry level teachers).

      STEP 3: The Personnel Department will notify applicants when their application is complete.

      STEP 4: Applicants will be reviewed by the Personnel Department.

      STEP 5: Selected applicants will receive a post card instructing them to contact the Personnel Office and schedule a screening interview.

      STEP 6: Upon completion of a screening interview, applicants are eligible to be interviewed by principals/department heads for specific positions.

      STEP 7: Before an applicant can be recommended for a position, he/she must provide:

Copy(ies) of the results of content knowledge assessment taken (e.g. ETS Praxis II, Georgia Teacher Certification Test (TCT), etc.).

Copy(ies) of appropriate educational certificate (or proof of eligibility for certificate).
