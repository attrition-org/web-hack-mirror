Network Management
-
Look to Creative Business Concepts for comprehensive services that help manage the development and evolution of your network system. We will design a network management program to meet your specific needs today and tomorrow. Our network management services include:
  • Universal premises wiring maintenance
  • Security planning and management, including strategic planning that balances distributed access with security
  • Resource planning to identify future system hardware and software needs, matching them with emerging technologies, and developing implementation option

A variety of auditing and documentation services, designed to provide the reliable data needed by managers with fiscal and operational responsibilities, are also offered. Creative Business Concepts helps you maintain high-quality system performance through:

  • Identification of system-wide usage patterns
  • Documentation of failure occurrences and resolutions
  • Software distribution and license integrity
  • System performance and monitoring
  • Records, reports, and analyses of user statistics
  • Recommendations for system optimization

Support and Maintenance Services designed to meet any organization’s support requirements. Pre-packaged offerings will fit most requirements, but Creative Business Concepts, Inc. Will tailor a Custom Support Agreement designed to meet the most demanding organizational needs.

  • Hardware support agreements covering Files Servers, Network printers and other critical sub-systems
  • Operating Systems Support agreements covering Microsoft NT and Novell Netware
  • Standard Business day coverage
  • Round the Clock (24 X 7) for Mission Critical environments
  • Prepaid reduced-rate Labor Block Support agreements
