What's New With IHB

At their meeting of July 29, 1999, the Texas Industrialized Building Code Council adopted new code editions.  The adoption of the new code editions is effective February 8, 2000.  The codes include the 1997 edition of the Uniform Building Code; the 1997 edition of the Standard Building Code; the 1997 edition of the International Fuel Gas Code; the 1997 edition of the International Plumbing Code; the 1998 edition of the International Mechanical Code; the 1999 edition of the National Electrical Code; the 1998 edition of the International Energy Conservation Code; and the 1998 edition of the International One and Two Family Dwelling Code

For more information you may contact us at industrialized.buildings@license.state.tx.us or call Katherine Vaughan or Donna Lipke at (512) 463-7353.
