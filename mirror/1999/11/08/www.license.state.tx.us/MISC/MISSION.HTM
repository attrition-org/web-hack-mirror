Mission Statement

The mission of the Texas Department of Licensing and Regulation, is to promote a fair business environment for our regulated community while protecting public safety and welfare.

We believe our mission is accomplished when we actively educate consumers concerning the areas we regulate, ensure the effective, efficient and economical use of public resources, regulate industries we oversee in a fair and impartial manner and recognize the dignity and worth of our employees.
