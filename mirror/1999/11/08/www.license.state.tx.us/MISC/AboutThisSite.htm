About This Site

Most pages on our Web site have our standard Web Site border containing a TDLR logo, our Web site address, the state seal, a link to this page, a means of contacting our Web site team, a hyperlink to a page with additional State Agency Web sites, a copyright hyperlink, a link to our enabling statute, and a 'Last Site Revision' stamp.

The 'Last Site Revision' stamp indicates the latest date any page on our site was modified.

Generally, each page contains a 'Last Page Revision' stamp.  It is located in the upper middle area of the page, below the Web Site border and indicates the last date the specific page being viewed was modified.

Email links, indicated by the mailbox icon, may be sent to a specific licensing groups response team by clicking on the icon on the respective license's home page.  Email may also be sent to TDLR's Web Team, which is concerned primarily with the Web site itself, by clicking on the icon at the top of most pages.
