Washington State County Road Administration Board

        A comprehensive and dynamic, yet easy to use, road inventory and management system that, we hope, makes it easier for counties to preserve and improve their transportation infrastructure.

        History of CRIS (a.k.a., the long version) if you're interested...

        Inventory and maintain the road network and it's features such as:

        • RoadLog, Geometry, Classification, Bridges, Signs, Guardrails, and Striping. Also things like Traffic Volume, Accidents, Culverts and many more...
        • Manage your pavement inventory, maintenance management and bridge programs with flexible tracking, analysis, predictive and budgeting tools.
        • The CRIS future includes Safety Management, Needs Assessment, and Priority Programming with Windows, SQL, GIS and client server compatibility.
        Our commitment to supporting the counties and creating useful tools is as strong as ever. Some elements are:

        • Regular training on all CRIS modules, offered free to all county users.
        • Training as-needed for technical information and assistance.
        • Holding workshops for CRIS module development.
        • Providing consultative help for customizing CRIS to individual county needs.
        • Offer technical expertise in all areas of Information Technology
