Washington State County Road Administration Board

here we are

To get to the County Road Administration Board Office drive to Olympia on Interstate 5 and follow the signs to get on U.S. route 101. You should then take the first exit off Highway 101, turn right at the traffic light, and then turn right again at the next traffic light. Take the first right (just past the AM/PM) and follow the frontage road to Chandler Ct. (the second cul-de-sac on the left). Our office is easily located on the second floor of the building on the right at the end of the road.
