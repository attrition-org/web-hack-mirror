InterNIC

This page includes information on what InterNIC is and how they operate. A lot of people always have questions about InterNIC. So this page will help you with your questions.

InterNIC is the company that sets up the actual domain name (example : www.yourdomain.com). For example if you want to setup a new domain, you have to go to InterNIC and submit a form so they can assign you the domain name. Click Here to visit InterNIC.

This is how InterNIC's payment plan works. First they bill you for 2 years in advance. That comes out to $70. Once the 2 years is up, then they charge you yearly for $35 a year. InterNIC will bill you through e-mail, then they will send you a hard copy through postal mail.
