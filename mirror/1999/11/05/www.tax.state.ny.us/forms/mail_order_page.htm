Forms and Publications by the U.S. Mail

Please read the following before placing your order:

This service allows you to order most New York State Department of Taxation and Finance tax forms and publications for delivery by conventional mail. Please allow one to two weeks for delivery of your forms.

You will be able to order up to five different forms from this service. You will receive two copies of any form that you order and one copy of any publication. Instructions will be included with your form orders.

If you need to order a greater quantity of forms or order items not available through this system call the appropriate number on our How to Reach Us page.

You will first be asked to enter your mailing address as well as your E-mail address   We need your E-mail address in case we have contact to you concerning your order.  You will then be asked to enter the tax year and the form number (e.g., IT-201) of the item you are requesting. If you do not know the form number, refer to the descriptions of each form on our Forms and Instructions or Publications and Technical Bulletins pages. In the event you cannot determine a form's number, we have included an area for you to provide a description of the form. You should also use this area to describe the specific period for forms that are filed more than once in a year (e.g., I need the quarterly sales tax return for the period Sept. 1, 1998, through Nov. 30, 1998.)
