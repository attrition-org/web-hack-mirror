Forms Corrections and Changes

Caution: If you downloaded or printed any of the forms listed below prior to the Date Posted listed in the table, you should download or print them again.   The form numbers will link to the updated PDF form files.

Date Posted Form Number Revision Date Description of Change
7/13/99 NYS-50 7/99 The general information section has had three minor revisions:

1.  On page 10, under Qualified employer, condition (2) of that paragraph should read ``must have filed all contribution reports due for all periods of liability in the three payroll years...''

2.  On page 16 and on the inside front cover (overview), under Supplemental Wage Reporting Requirements for Construction Companies, the text was revised to explain that the construction company reporting requirement has been postponed until the calendar quarter October 1, 1999, through December 31, 1999, with the first return due February 28, 2000.  Construction companies will receive further information.  Disregard any old drafts of Form NYS-45-CC and its instructions.

3.  On page 30, the correct telephone number for ordering forms and publications from areas outside the U.S. and outside Canada is (518) 485-6800.

The city of New York resident tax tables were revised effective July 1, 1999.  The new city tables were printed in a separate booklet, Form NYS-50-SUB, and mailed to employers.  An error that had existed in Table B on page T-38 has been corrected.   Section 3 of NYS-50 (7/99) contains the same information as NYS-50-SUB.
       
3/16/99 CT-3-A-I 1998 There is an error in the instructions, Form CT-3-A-I, on page 11, for Line 58.  The last sentence of item d) should read: "For net operating losses incurred in taxable years beginning on or before August 5, 1997, the ANOL may be carried back 3 years and carried forward 15 years."
