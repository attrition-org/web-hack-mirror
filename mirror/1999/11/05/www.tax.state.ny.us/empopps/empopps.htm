Employment Opportunities and Information

Listed below are current employment opportunities with the New York State Department of Taxation & Finance.  If you have any questions about these opportunities, please call the department’s Office of Human Resources Management at (518) 457-2900.

Senior Attorney

The New York State Department of Taxation and Finance is seeking candidates for Senior Attorney positions in the Department's Office of Counsel in Albany, New York.  The positions will be assigned to various legal functions, including the development of tax legislation and regulations, and tax litigation work.  A background in taxation and/or litigation is preferred.  The entry level salary currently ranges from $33,456 to $40,436, depending upon qualifications, plus an excellent benefit package.  Final salary upon completion of the required training period is currently $50,004.  The minimum requirement for appointment to these positions is graduation from an accredited law school.  Admission to the New York State Bar is also required as a condition for final retention.  To qualify for appointment, candidates will be required to file for the Legal Specialties Examination, which is an evaluation of education and experience.  Resumes may be sent to:  Frank Borysewicz, NYS Department of Taxation and Finance, Building 9, Room 252, W.A. Harriman State Office Bldg. Campus, Albany, NY 12227.  For more information call Frank Borysewicz or Marian Beckett at 518-457-1903.  Further information about the Legal Specialties Examination is available on the Internet at:  http://www.cs.state.ny.us.

Tax Auditor Traineeship & Data Processing Fiscal Systems Auditor Traineeship
Tax Auditor 1 & Data Processing Fiscal Systems Auditor 1

The Department of Taxation & Finance is seeking candidates statewide for two professional career fields.  The traineeships represent excellent opportunities for individuals who have a bachelor’s degree in accounting to start a rewarding and challenging career with an employer committed to developing highly skilled, enriched and motivated professionals in a dynamic and challenging work environment.  Tax Auditor 1 & Data Processing Fiscal Systems Auditor 1 positions provide advanced placement for individuals who, in addition to meeting the educational qualifications, also have two years of full-time professional accounting, auditing and/or computer science experience in a financial environment.  We offer competitive salaries, state-of-the-art technology tools, excellent benefits and opportunities for employment throughout New York State and in Chicago, Illinois.
