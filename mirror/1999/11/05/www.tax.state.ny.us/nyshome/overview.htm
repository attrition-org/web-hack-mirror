Department of Taxation and Finance Home Page

Visitors' Overview
Site Index - Table of Contents - An index of all major links on the website and also categorized links by tax type.

Forms and Instructions - This link provides access to current and prior-year New York State tax forms, instructions, applications, etc., grouped by tax type (income, sales, corporation, etc.).  It also provides information on tax rates, interest rates, forms corrections, plus information of special interest to practitioners.  (A link is also provided to download Adobe Acrobat Reader, which is used to view and print the form files.)

Publications and Technical Bulletins - Popular publications, Memos (TSB-M), Advisory Opinions (TSB-A), and N-Notices.

Personal Income Tax Electronic Filing - Contains information on electronic filing for Individuals as well as Tax Professionals. Individuals can read about the benefits of electronic filing as well as how to file from home or through a tax preparer.  Tax Professionals can obtain general information regarding program participation, publications, technical updates and program highlights.

Frequently Asked Questions - Income Tax for Year 1998 - Answers to frequently asked questions concerning income tax.

Taxpayer Bill of Rights - Information on "Your Rights and Obligations Under the Tax Law ."

 Tax News - News, publications and general information from the Tax Department and other agencies which relate to taxes, including press releases and Quarterly Enforcement Summaries since 1996, and the Department’s Annual Reports.

Tax Policy and Statistical Reports - This link provides a sample of recent publications prepared by the Office of Tax Policy Analysis.   Information includes mandated tax studies, summaries of recent tax legislation, statistical collections and tax liability, and special interest policy reports.

Employer Withholding and Wage Reporting - General information regarding withholding tax, wage reporting, and new hire reporting, including registration information, reporting requirements, filing methods, and forms and publications.

How to Reach Us - Information on how to reach the Tax Department by telephone, Telecommunications Device for the Deaf (TDD), Fax-On-Demand, or by mail, as well as other related contact information.

Links to Related Sites - A list of (and links to) other tax-related resources on the World Wide Web.

New York State Home Page - The Home Page for New York State, including the Governor's page and links to all state agencies.
