How to Reach Us

By Telephone:

   Telephone assistance is available from 8:30 am to 4:25 pm, Monday through Friday.

General Tax Information -- For information or answers to your New York State tax questions, call toll free: 1-800-225-5829

Forms Ordering -- For forms and publications, call toll free: 1-800-462-8100

Business Tax Information Center -- For information or forms for Withholding, Sales, Corporation, Truck Mileage, Motor Fuel, Petroleum Business, Alcohol, Cigarette, Tobacco, Beverage Container or Hazardous Waste Tax and the International Fuel Tax Agreement, call toll free: 1-800-972-1233

Personal Income Tax Refund Information -- To inquire on the status of your refund, call toll free:  1-800-443-3200.  You will need your social security number, the filing status , the number of exemptions and the address listed on your New York State tax return.  Touch tone phone callers can use an automated selection Monday through Friday from 8:30 am to 7:25 pm.

Corporate Dissolution -- To dissolve a domestic corporation, call toll free: 1-800-327-9688

Tax Compliance Division -- For assessment, billing and collection questions, call toll free:  1-800-835-3554

Telephone assistance is available toll free from within the United States and Canada.  If you are calling from outside the United States and Canada, you need to call 518-485-6800.


By TDD:

  
If you have a hearing or speech impairment and have access to a Telecommunications Device for the Deaf (TDD), call toll free from the United States and Canada (8:30 am to 4:25 pm, Monday through Friday): 1-800-634-2110.  If you do not own a TDD, check with independent living centers or community action programs to find out where machines are available for public use.

To Receive Forms by Fax:

  
Fax-on-Demand Forms Ordering System -- From a touch tone phone, call toll free from the United States and Canada: 1-800-748-3676, available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.  Most forms are available.  A fax code is used to identify each form .  An index of fax forms and their codes is available via fax and can also be viewed on this website.
