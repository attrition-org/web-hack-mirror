WHS Mentors Assisting Peers
MAP Program
Kathy Lough, Advisor


  M.A.P. (Mentors Assisting Peers) is a student service organization which provides academic tutors. 25 students are now members and volunteer their time to tutor nightly in the High School ; Tuesdays and Thursdays at the middle school; and Tuesday and Wednesday nights at Pine Street. High school tutoring is provided on a walk in basis between 2:30p and 3:00p in the high school media center. The junior high and Pine Street schedule tutor usage during homework club.
  The mentor group provides academically gifted students with an opportunity to share their knowledge with others and to reach out to their peers. This organization depends totally on the strength of commitment of its members and meets biweekly to discuss tutoring assignments, strategies and have an occasional social event. It is another prime example of the positive activities of W.H.S. students!
