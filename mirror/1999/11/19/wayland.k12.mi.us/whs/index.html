Mission Statement
Wayland Union High School believes that all students will be provided a stimulating and positive environment where optimum learning can be attained. The school and community will provide an environment in which students have the opportunity to develop their full potential.

Students will receive a comprehensive education, build intellectual, social and physical skills which can enable them to be positive, responsible citizens of their respective communities and the world.
