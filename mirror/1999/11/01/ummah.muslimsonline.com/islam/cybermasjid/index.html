What is CyberMasjid?

CyberMasjid is a voluntary project to help the mosque reach out to a wider audience on the internet especially the younger generations and the working adults.

CyberMasjid is totally run by groups of volunteers and we are happy if one can step forward and lend us a helping hand. Anybody can play their part, be it small or big. Anybody can make the difference.
