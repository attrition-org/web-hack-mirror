Citizens Against Communist Chinese Propaganda Homepage

Citizens Against Communist Chinese Propaganda is a group of interested individuals devoted to the non-violent and legal removal of religious and minority exhibits from the Communist Chinese government owned and operated theme park known as Florida Splendid China in Kissimmee, Fla.
We do not believe that violence of any form is a legitimate form of political expression. We believe in the right of free speech, free association, and the right to unobstructed religious practice. The definition of the speech and religious practice is left to the individual.

The World Uyghur Network News is produced by the Eastern Turkistan Information Center (ETIC) and is devoted to the current political, cultural and economic developments in Eastern Turkistan and to the Uyghurs related issues.
Eastern Turkistan is a name used by the majority of the Uyghurs for their Motherland located in what is at present Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region of China.
The World Uyghur Network News is intended to bring information on the situation in Eastern Turkistan from the Uyghur and other sources to attention of the international community.
