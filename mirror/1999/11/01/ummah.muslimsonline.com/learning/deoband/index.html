Soldiers of Islam

(Battalion of the Sahaaba)

(Radhiallaahu Anhum Ajma'een)

The purpose and intent of these pages is to express Islam in its Pristine Purity. At the same time we wish to inform everyone that our aim in life is to try to our level best to uphold the Honor and Dignity of those illustrious luminaries of Islam who have been subject to the criticism of all and sundry.

I am speaking of those great personalities of this beautiful Deen who have given their entire life in its service. Those Luminaries who sacrificed everything they had in their possession for the upliftment of this Deen of Islam.

These people are the Sahaaba (Radhiallaahu Anhum Ajma'een), the Taa'bieen, the Taba' Taabieen, The Fuqahaa, Muhadditheen, and all other great scholars of Islam especially the Ulama of Deoband (Rahmatullaahi Alaihim).

We wish to state very emphatically that SOLDIERS OF ISLAM DO NOT ENDORSE ANYTHING UNISLAMIC FOUND ON THE NET. WE ARE MERELY USING THESE FACILITIES AS A MEANS OF PROPOGATION.
