COMMAND VISION
     
We are the center of Excellence for Readiness, Tactics, Doctrine, Battle Group and Joint Operations. We prepare AEGIS ships to fight and win! Our education and training capabilities define state-of-the-art technologies.
Every member of our culturally diverse work force contributes to their full potential in accomplishing our mission. Trust abides at every level. Team members are excited to come to work because of the way we conduct business. Students are encouraged and enspired to become more capable warfighters. We continually strive to improve ship's performance and reduce the cost of training. We are a functionally integrated Surface Combatant Training and Readiness Center, optimizing the overall combat readiness of the fleet while reducing support costs.
