COMMAND GOALS
     
Continuous Process Improvement. As a team, we will embrace the philosiphy of quality improvement as our normal way of conducting business to maximize the effectiveness of our organization.
Mission Growth. We will integrate ourselves with all activities that provide life-cycle engineering and training, creating a surface combatant training and readiness center which meets the naval goals of today and tomorrow.
Resource Management. We will make effective and efficient use of personel, equipment, monetary and facility resources in order to become the TOTAL Ship Education and Training Center.
Emerging Technologies. We will work together to improve our efficiency by taking advantage of technological innovations in both mission and support areas to facilitate customer satisfaction.
Command Climate. We will provide our people and their families with an environment conductive to pursuing a successful career and living a safe and healthy lifestyle.
