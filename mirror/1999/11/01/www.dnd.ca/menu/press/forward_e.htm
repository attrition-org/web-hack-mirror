The Department of National Defence and the Canadian Forces take the issues of gender integration, sexual harassment and the need to reform the military justice system seriously.  To assist you to identify what measures have been taken or promised, and to set the standards we wish to achieve, you will find an organized list of news releases, backgrounders, speeches and reports.
