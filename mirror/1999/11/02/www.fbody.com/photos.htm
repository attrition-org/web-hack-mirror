I don't ask for much, so please try to include everything I do ask for. I need to know the following information and please format your email to me is this way:
Name: FIRST and LAST NAME
From: City, State
HomePage: http://whatever If you don't have a homepage Make one! or leave it blank.
Car: Year Color Model

If you want to tell me what mods you have or whatever go ahead, but I don't post that info, the list for some people would go on and on. I am willing to do things like John Doe's Blown 97 LT4 30th Aniv SS if you have a blown car. I can also do Mary's N2O Hawk if you so wish...

Photo:
PLEASE PLEASE PLEASE send me your photo in .jpg format!! I have a tendancyto accidently hit delete when I get .bmp or .art formats since they are:
a) very large file formats
b) more work for me

Not to sound lazy, but I have been getitng a lot of photos lately and jsut can't keep up.

So, your photo should be a .jpg image, preferably not more than 25K or so, I have to download these things ya know? ;) the smaller file size the better, BUT that doesn't mean make your image 200x200 pixels! Small file size, but maybe like a 640x480 image.

Non required stuff that helps

NameYearTrimColor.jpg
