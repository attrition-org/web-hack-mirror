Help About Information About EZ/EC Communities

Information About EZ/EC Communities provides detailed information about the communities designated as Empowerment Zones and Enterprise Communities in both rural and urban areas.

Map of Designated EZ/EC Communities

The map indicates states with an Empowerment Zone, Enterprise Community, or both. By clicking on the map, you can reach a list of the EZ and EC communities within the state. Click on the highlighted community name to get detailed information about a particular community, including a map showing the location and status its projects and a summary of its strategic plan.

List of Champion Communities

A list, alphabetical by state, of communities that completed the strategic planning process as were designated as Champion Communities

Community Home Pages

Many Empowerment Zones and Enterprise Communities have established their own home pages. From here, you can jump to their sites.
