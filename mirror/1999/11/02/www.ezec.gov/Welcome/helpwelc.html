Welcome

Click on the Welcome bar to see and hear messages from President Clinton, Vice President Gore, Secretary of Agriculture Glickman, and Secretary of Housing and Urban Development Cisneros.

A brief video sequence can be viewed by clicking on their photos. This requires that your computer is configured for sound and that you have an appropriate video client installed, such as Video for Windows, QuickTime, or MPEG. Several of these clients can be obtained from the EZ/EC FTP site. Click on Internet Software in the Toolbox section.

A short audio may be heard by clicking on the audio graphic. Your computer must be configured for sound in order to hear it.
