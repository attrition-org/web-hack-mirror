Help About Investment in EZ/EC Communities

Investment in EZ/EC Communities provides information about making investments in communities designated as Empowerment Zones and Enterprise Communities in both rural and urban areas.

Guides to Investing in EZ Communities

A guide to investing in Empowerment Zones and Enterprise Communities. Click on the highlighted text in the table of contents to skip to sections of particular interest.

Future Plans

Under consideration as a future addition to this section is a searchable database of products and opportunities for investing in EZ/EC communities, to help EZ/EC's market themselves to businesses and to help businesses in these communities market their products. Please let us know your opinions about this proposed service. You can e-mail your ideas to webmaster@ezec.gov.
