Enhanced 'Logo' Listing

The Suppliers directory is accessed by users that are actively looking for a product source. Clearly there are, in many cases, a number of alternative suppliers. By enhancing your listing in with your company's logo you can stand out from the crowd. Your logo can appear as a maximum of 100 pixels wide by 60 pixels high.

Cost

The cost of "Enhancing" your listing is only £ 60.00 per category per year (plus vat, where applicable). Standard listing is, of course, free. To enhance your listing now simply send us an email giving your full company details, the Company name, Town, Telephone number and contact name that should appear and attach your logo, in GIF or JPEG format- our 'scanning' fee is £40.00 (plus vat), if required. Then tell us the categories (click here to view categories) that you want

Links

Suppliers with WWW sites can link to their sites from the relevant categories within the suppliers directory and from any press releases and/or 'news' items they may have on the site.

Cost

The cost of "Linking" is only £ 50.00 per category per year (plus vat, where applicable). To 'link' from our site simply send us an email giving your full company details, the Company name, Town, Telephone number and contact name. Then tell us the categories (click here to view categories) that you want

Earpieces

The top of every page on this site has a pair of earpieces either side of the page name banner and when users 'click' on the advert that is being shown they are taken to the site or section being promoted, at that moment. These earpieces are sold as pairs and there are typically four earpiece advertisements to a page (see top of page).

Cost

Cost of Earpieces (per pair) is between £40.00 and £85.00 per thousand, plus vat where applicable.

Interlink Pages

Many of our sections have interlinking advertising opportunities. These pages are on screen for a few seconds and bear the logo of the sponsor. They can also have a few 'words' and link to the sponsor's site, if required. These are only available per month.

Cost

Cost of Interlink page promotion varies between £300.00 and £750.00 per calendar month according to section- plus vat, where applicable.
