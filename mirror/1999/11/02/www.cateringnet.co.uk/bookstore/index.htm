Book of the Month

'Your Place or Mine? cooking at home with restaurant style'
- Jean-Christophe Novelli with Sheila Keating

We endured a long wait for Jean-Christophe Novelli's first cookbook but now its here to bring a little Novelli magic into our homes, we should rejoice.

'Your Place or Mine?' (Quadrille Publishing £25- our price £20 +p&p) is sublime. The pages are filled with Novelli's particular style of inspired French country dishes and clever fusions of flavour. Each leaf serves up further inspiration as the Michelin-starred chef demonstrates how simple it is for the home cook to copy his amazing combinations of flavours and decorative presentation.

Any man who creates a single dish with two of my favourite foods - venison and scallops - has to get my vote. But it's his advice, so often omitted in other cook books, on seasoning, seasoning, seasoning, which is invaluable.......more on this
