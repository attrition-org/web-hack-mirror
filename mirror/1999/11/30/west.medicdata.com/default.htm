MedicData.com - Your anonymous, secure and private online Health and Medical records service

Under Construction. Not for Public Use

Ever have the need to access your vital health and medical records rapidly and in complete privacy? Encountered an unexpected medical emergency while travelling abroad or on a business trip away from home? Does your child's new school want to know when the child was immunized? 'www.MedicData.com' is your online medical records database.

Here, you can conveniently save, retrieve, and update your medical records in complete privacy via the Internet. Many travellers will benefit from the exceptional convenience provided by the MedicData online database. The data is not traceable to a name, address or any other identification except the user-selected ID and password. Many of 'www.MedicData.com' services are provided to you free of charge.

Future expansions will include the display of your medical records in many different languages. X-ray records, Pap smears, and other medical images submitted by you or your healthcare professional will be made available online. The storage of medical images will incur a fee. Additional capabilities will allow the user to contact and consult health care providers who have registered with MedicData to check appointments, refill prescriptions, make payments and more!
