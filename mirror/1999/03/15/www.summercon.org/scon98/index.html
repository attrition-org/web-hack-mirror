Welcome to the SummerCon '98 Home Page 

Summercon X is in Atlanta, GA on the weekend of June 5th, 6th, and 7th.

It will be held at the Comfort Inn Downtown in Atlanta.

There will be Summercon tee shirts on sale for $20. They will help pay for the cost
of the conference.

The con will begin on Friday, June 5th, at 7:00 pm, with people convening in front of the hotel. If it is raining, then we should meet in the inside lobby area. Friday is a time to get to know people and just kick back to find out who you will be partying with that night.

The speeches will begin at 12:30 pm, on Saturday, June 6th. X will be the moderator and Clovis will act as his humble backup. The order of speakers and topics will be Announced at the con on Saturday, June 6th. As long as no one breaks any toilets, there may be doughnuts this year. 

A final meeting place and time will be decided upon on Saturday and announced at the con for Sunday's activities. Usually Sunday is a day for winding down, saying good bye, and going home.
