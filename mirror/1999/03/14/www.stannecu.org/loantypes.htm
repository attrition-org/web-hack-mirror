New Auto Loans
St. Anne Credit Union requires a 5% down payment on all new auto loans. Terms range from 2 to 5 years. Before your loan can be finalized, we will need the following:
Main Share Account with $25.00 minimum balance
Proof of income (copy of recent paystub)
$12.00 fee for Single Vendor's Interest Insurance
Insurance Binder
Copy of Purchase Contract*
Copy of RMV-1 form.* We must be listed as:
St. Anne Credit Union
93 Union Street
New Bedford, MA. 02740-6361
Lienholder Code: #1894
* On refinances, we will need a copy of registration and a copy of financial note in place of these forms. We will also need $25.00 for the Registry of Motor Vehicles to add us as lienholder.

Consumer Loans
This is similar to a personal loan with one exception. The payment must be automatically withdrawn from your savings account. By allowing the credit union to do this, you receive a full one percentage point discount. If the funds are not in your account on your due date, the credit union will increase your rate from 9.75% to 10.75%, or 11.50% to 12.50%. You may borrow from $500.00 to $7,500.00 for terms up to four years.

Share Loans
This type of loan is secured by your Main Share Account. You can borrow up to 90% of the balance in your account for 3% over the current share rate. You still earn interest on your savings. The maximum term is 48 months. Monthly payments of principal and interest are required.

Loans secured by Certificates of Deposit accounts are also available at a rate of 3% over the rate you are receiving on your CD. Your loan will mature on the same day the CD securing it does.

Equity Loans
This is a fixed rate loan on your home. St. Anne Credit Union will loan up to 80% of the appraised value of your property less the first mortgage. The terms are five to fifteen years. The same fees apply as for the Equity Line of Credit. The loan is available on owner occupied property only.
