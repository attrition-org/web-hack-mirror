Recommended Browsers

For best results when viewing this site, we recommend the use of Netscape Navigator or Microsoft Internet Explorer, version 3.0 or later. Up-to-date information about winsock applications, including web browsers, may be found in one of the best program evaluation and download site (Stroud's Consummate WinSock Applications).

To take full advantage of the hypertext capabilities of your browser, you may configure it with external viewers. A useful list of helper applications for biologists was made available by Raja Khalifah.

Navigation

The SBS Homepage contains links to all the pages available on this server. You can return to the Homepage by clicking on the SBS Logo, found in the upper left corner of each page. In addition, you can reach the main pages in every section by using the navigation bar at the top of the document. For those using text-only browsers, links to the main pages of various sections are also provided at the bottom of every page.

To find out specific information, you may want to use our Search form.

Contributions

If you would like to contribute material for this site, please contact us by e-mail or by phone (x-2591).

  • If your material should be included in the pages currently maintained by our crew, please provide it in an appropriate form (e-mail or floppy disks prefered) and we will update the hypertext pages.
  • If your have a personal web page and want to have a pointer to it from our Directory pages, let us know the URL of your page (typically, this is "http://cctr.umkc.edu/user/username/index.html"). To create a web page, you need to have an HTML editor (we use HomeSite, although a simple text editor is enough if you know HTML well enough), and a cctr account where to upload your finished page.
