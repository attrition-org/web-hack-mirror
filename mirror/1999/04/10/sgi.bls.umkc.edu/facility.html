SBS Research Facilities

Modern biological research is a complex effort, materially aided by the availability of support facilities to aid in the aquisition of data, construction and maintainance of the laboratory equipment, and analysis and interpretation of the experimental results. Laboratories are equipped for modern research carried out in space asigned to individual faculty members as well as in a large number of central facilities. Research facilities are located primarily in the Biological Sciences and Chemistry building complex on the Volker Campus, and some in the school's laboratories on the Hospital Hill Campus.
