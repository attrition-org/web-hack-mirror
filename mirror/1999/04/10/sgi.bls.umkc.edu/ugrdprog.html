Programs

Students may pursue programs of study leading to the following degrees:

  • Bachelor of Science in Biology
  • Bachelor of Arts in Biology
  • Bachelor of Science in Medical Technology

Admission Policies and Procedures

Undergraduate students are admitted through the University's admission office.
You can apply on-line: Application for Admission.

  • Freshmen are admitted according to General UMKC Undergraduate Admission Policies and Procedures.
  • Transfer students should contact the University admissions office or the School of Biological Sciences for information about transfer admissions.
  • Two-year community college students are encouraged to choose a major in the school as early as possible and seek guidance from University admissions officers and their own advisers so that a smooth transition into SBS programs is possible.

Note: Disability regulations: UMKC and University of Missouri
