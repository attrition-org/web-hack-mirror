Hasbro's Complete Summer 1999 Star Wars Catalog
Thu, Apr 01, 99 02:30:31 AM CST

Adam wrote in to let us know that the Star Wars Collector Union has posted detailed scans of a complete Hasbro Summer 1999 Star Wars Catalog. With so many Hasbro Episode 1 toy pictures already on the net, it's hard for new scans to warrant a "wow"... but these ones do. Head over if you're willing to see spoiler pictures.

Test Shots of New Battledroid Figure
Tue, Mar 30, 99 09:19:31 PM CST

www.theouterrim.com has sent us a report that they just posted new test shot scans of the battledroid that will come with Qui-Gon Jinn in the Naboo F/X playset. This version is quite cool, with gun actually being part of the figure itself, and a nice pose as well. No extending neck, though. Go on over and check it out!
