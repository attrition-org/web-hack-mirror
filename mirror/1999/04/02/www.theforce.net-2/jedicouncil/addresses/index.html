T H E STAR WARS ADDRESS LIST

All of the addresses listed on this page were collected by TheForce.Net through our own resources. However, there are a couple of other Star Wars Address sites in existence where you can verify the validity of the addresses. They are Star Wars Autographs and Autograph Central. We'll let you know if our reader's have had success, but these other sites can also tell you which are reliable.

This list is continually updated and expanded. Please WRITE US and let us know if you have success or failures writing these addresses. It will help us keep it up to date and others get their mail through. Thanks!

It is very important that you read our MAILING TIPS before attempting to send anything to these addresses. Those addresses which have received successful replies are marked with a *.

You can write most of the Star Wars Talents through the Star Wars Insider and they will see that it gets to the person you are writing to.
