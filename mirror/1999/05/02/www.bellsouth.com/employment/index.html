BellSouth Corporation is an international communications company. We provide exciting and varied career opportunities in a dynamic and growing industry. We are headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia, USA, with subsidiaries operating throughout the United States and in various locations in Latin America, Europe, and Asia/Pacific.

Our talented and dedicated people allow us to reach our goals of world-class customer satisfaction, profitable growth, industry leadership, and community involvement. We show our appreciation to our employees by providing attractive compensation, excellent benefits, training and development programs, and a rewarding work environment.

The talents and dedication of our employees achieve success for our company and our communities as well. BellSouth, as a corporation, and our employees actively support education, the arts, community development, and civic involvement. Our School to Work, Job Shadowing, and Pioneering the Future programs, for example, have been widely adopted as models of corporate leadership and employee involvement.
