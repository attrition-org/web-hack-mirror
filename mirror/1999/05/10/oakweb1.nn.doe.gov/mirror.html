Leaky ducts not only waste heating energy but can contribute to other problems in the interior environment. Up until now, fixing these leaks oftentimes has not been practical. Using simple and inexpensive new technology developed at Berkeley Lab, these inaccessible leaks can be sealed by injecting aerosol sealant into the duct system.
