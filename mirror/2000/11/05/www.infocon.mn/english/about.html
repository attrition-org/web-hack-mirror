MISSION STATEMENT

Our mission is to help organizations, companies and individuals to benefit from the use of information and communications technology by providing professional consulting, research, training and technical services.

Our Company is committed to the principle that the ICT is a tool of development for Mongolia and Mongolian people. InfoCon Company cooperates with national and international, governmental and non-governmental organizations, private companies, businesses and individuals on ICT development. We have an extensive computer network, online reference data and more than 10 years experience supporting ICT in Mongolia. These resources allow us to rapidly assemble teams to provide consulting for projects of any size or scope.
