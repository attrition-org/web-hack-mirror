The Meredith Publishing Group includes magazine and book publishing and a variety of resources (such as our Consumer Database) used in the integrated marketing programs we develop for outside clients.

Our publications have two common themes: service journalism and a home-and-family focus. We publish 20 subscription magazines, more than 40 special interest titles and numerous custom publications. We also have more than 300 book titles in print, most of which carry the Better Homes and Gardens trademark.

Fiscal 1999 Publishing Group profits were $119.6 million, 62.3% of the company's total profit.
