Broadcasting
 
Meredith owns and operates 12 television stations with a total reach of nearly 10 percent of U.S. households. Eight of our stations are in the top 35 markets, and many of our stations are located in high-growth areas such as tourist destinations and state capitals. The Broadcasting Group currently includes six FOX affiliates, five CBS affiliates and one NBC affiliate.

Fiscal '99 Highlights
Broadcasting Group profit totalled $72.3 million, 38 percent of the company's total profit. Revenues totalled $262.1 million, 25 percent of the company's total revenues.
