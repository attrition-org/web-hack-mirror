Using the NPMOC Web Site

All of the Naval METOC Operational Support Web pages are structured similarly. If any assistance is required, that guidance is listed below:

The What's New Section is a place to look for important current announcements about the web and the center itself, from product updates to upcoming events. 

The Products Areas are a repository of METOC products served to the Naval METOC community by the center. Regional products can be accessed by clicking on the applicable area. The Exercise and Special Support Sections are established for tailored exercise and special operations support.

The Related Links Section is a collection of links to other METOC-related sites outside of the Naval METOC operational product webs.

The Comments Section provides the chance to ask questions and give feedback about the site and the Naval METOC Web.
