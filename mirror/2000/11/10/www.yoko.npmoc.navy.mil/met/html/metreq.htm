Requesting MET Services

  • Services will be provided upon request, but may be limited due to manning constraints. Please included the following information:
    • Length of services requested
    • Type of support required
    • Berthing limitations by rank/gender
  • Short notice requests are acceptable.
  • Priority of Requests
    1. Warplans and Contingency Operations (Navy/Joint).
    2. Deployments and Workups (Navy/Joint)
    3. Exercises (Navy/Joint)
    4. Other (including support to Allied, Foreign, and Coalitions Armed Forces).
  • See 3140.1K for further information on requesting MET services
