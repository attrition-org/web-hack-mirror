G. A. A. - Gaelic Athletic Association
The Gaelic Athletic Association is dedicated to promoting the games of hurling, football, handball, rounders, and camogie.

The Ladies Gaelic Football Association
The Ladies Gaelic Football Association today is an organisation which endeavours to reach into every Parish in our land to promote Ladies Gaelic football and to support the Irish language and other aspects of Irish culture.
