Humpback

Helping us market and promote Sheldon Jackson College

because all prospective collegians should know about us!

Sheldon Jackson has a program wherein "friends" of the College can help the Admission & Financial Planning Office sensitize prospective students to our distinct school. There are several ways that "Humpbackers" can assist us:
  • Telephoning SJ prospects in your home area.
  • Writing SJ prospects in your home area.
  • Representing SJ at a college/career fair in your home area.
  • Hosting a small Sheldon Jackson College reception in your home for SJ prospects.
  • Circulating a Sheldon Jackson College poster and other SJ materials to schools, libraries, and churches in your home area.
