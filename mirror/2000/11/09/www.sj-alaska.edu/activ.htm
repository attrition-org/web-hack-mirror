Student Activities

Mission

The mission of the activities department of Sheldon Jackson College is to provide, safe, educational and fun programs to develop community and unity on campus within the mission statement of Sheldon Jackson College. The activities department works closely with outdoor recreation classes, campus ministry and the Office of Residence Life to provide a well rounded array of things to do both indoors and outdoors.

Participation in campus activities is always fully encouraged and any suggestions students have are taken seriously and frequently result in new activities. The education which students receive in both academics and activities at Sheldon Jackson College will prepare them for the adventure of a lifetime.

The activities program at Sheldon Jackson College is made up of three primary components: outdoor recreation, intramural sports, and social\educational programs. Typically, the following kinds of activities are offered.


Outdoor Recreation

  • Backpacking and camping are readily available activities, using trails within easy access from campus which lead into the Tongass National Forest, the largest temperate rainforest in North America.
  • Sitka Sound and the surrounding coastal waters are home to myriad wildlife: whales, seals, Stellar sea-lions, otters, and much more.
  • Kayaking or sightseeing trips on the water are a great way to relax and enjoy the beauty of Southeast Alaska.
  • Mountain biking is popular in Sitka, and the Forest Service is currently working on developing some trails in the area.
  • Scuba diving is also a popular activity in the area. Certification is available in Sitka.
  • Sitka's waters are also a great place for deep sea, fly and sport fishing.


Intramural Program

The intramural programs at Sheldon Jackson range from quick pick-up games to elaborate tournaments in a number of sports like basketball and volleyball, and even ping pong.

  • Basketball is a one of the most popular sports on campus, and the college hosts a number of different local tournaments throughout each year.
  • Both indoor and outdoor soccer are played throughout the year at S.J.
  • The Hames Physical Education facility has two racquetball courts.
  • A full length frisbee golf course has been plotted by students and both golf and ultimate frisbee are played regularly on campus.

Social/educational Programs

There are more to the activities at Sheldon Jackson than just fun and games. Student clubs, campus ministry, residence life staff, and the director of activities offer a wide array of activities which provide something for everyone:

  • Quilting and craft work
  • Chess tournaments
  • Casino nights
  • Dances
  • Live musical performances
  • Film festivals
  • Cultural events such as Native carving demonstrations, traditional storytelling techniques, and Alaska Native dancing


Getting involved in outdoor recreation

Students have the potential through student activities to gain on-the-job training in outdoor recreation techniques as a student guide. Eligibility hinges on experience and requires at least one semester as a full-time student at Sheldon Jackson College.

Getting involved in activities

The Associated Student Body of Sheldon Jackson College has a position of Vice President of Student Activities which provides experience in governmental affairs, management and event planning.
