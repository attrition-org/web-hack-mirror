Welcome to the Associated Alumni of Sheldon Jackson

Members of the Associated Alumni of Sheldon Jackson include graduates of any SJ school or college, or attendees who have completed at least 24 credit hours. The mission of the association and its board of directors is to enhance the image of and provide service to the college, its departments, faculty, students and alumni through the development, support and maintenance of a comprehensive program of alumni participation in SJC activities.

Current AASJ Board President: Twila (Wilson) Palmatier, SJHS '60
