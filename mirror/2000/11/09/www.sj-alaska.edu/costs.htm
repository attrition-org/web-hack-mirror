2000-2001 Sheldon Jackson College Costs

Remember two important factors when considering Sheldon Jackson College:

  1. Like most private colleges, each student pays a different cost to attend SJ-depending on your family's finances and your past academic success.
  2. 90% of SJ students receive some amount of financial aid. So only one in 10 actually pays the costs as listed below. (See "Financial Aid at Sheldon Jackson College.")
