Liberal Arts

Some benefits of studying liberal arts at SJ:

  • What is crucial for advancement and success in the world of work is not specialized training but the ability to think critically and judge wisely. A liberal arts major exposes the student to the broad areas of human knowledge: history, philosophy, literature, the arts, religion, journalism, speech, and the social sciences.
  • SJ students have interned with the Alaska Repertory Theater, the local Public Radio station, the Alaska State Museum, the Alaska Court System, the National Park Service, local hospitals and treatment centers.
  • Multicultural student body.
  • Wilderness setting.
  • Independent, interdisciplinary courses.
  • Close faculty contact.
