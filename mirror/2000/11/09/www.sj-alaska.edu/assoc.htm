Associate Degrees and Certificates

SJ offers both associate of arts and associate of science degrees (each is 60 hours). Students may choose an emphasis from the following:

  • Art
  • Business
  • Education
  • English
  • Fisheries
  • History
  • Human Services
  • Humanities
  • Outdoor Recreation
  • Political Science
  • Religion
  • Science
  • Seafood Technology
  • Social Science
  • Theater Arts

SJ certificates (about 48 hours) are offered in Fisheries and Seafood Technology.
