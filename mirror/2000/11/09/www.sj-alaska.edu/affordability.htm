SJ dedicates about $500,000 of our own revenue to scholarships!

Sheldon Jackson College offers four scholarships to new students:

  1. Honor Scholarship (for students not from Alaska)
  2. Alaska Scholarship (for students from Alaska)
  3. Legacy Scholarship (for dependents of SJ alumni)
  4. Referral Scholarship (for students referred by SJ alumni, trustees, volunteers, and Humpbackers)

 

  We do not require a separate application in order to be considered for SJ financial aid. Just be admitted and complete a FAFSA.

 

SJ participates in all federal and Alaska financial aid programs.

Pell grants, SEOG, Work-Study, Perkins loans, Stafford loans, and PLUS loans are awarded at SJC. The Alaska Student Loan, the Winn-Brindle (fisheries) Loan, and the Rural Teacher Loan are all given to Alaskans at SJC.
