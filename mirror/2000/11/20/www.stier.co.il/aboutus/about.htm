The Stier Group is Israel’s leading fair organization. Established in 1974, the company is still managed by original owners, assisted by a team of 18 experienced professionals fully trained in all aspects of exhibition and convention management. The office has computerized facilities and exclusively designed software, which enable the highly efficient operation in both national and international scale.

Stier Group offers full ranges of client services, including stand construction, stand furniture, design consulting, human resources service, publication and publicity. With the assistance of sub-contractors, Stier Group provides, both on the site and off it, the fullest attention to details with which no other local exhibition organizer could compete.

Until 1979, Steir Group activities were wide-ranging, dealing with all types of exhibitions: for trades, industry and the public. During the 1980’s, the focus has changed, with the advent high-tech. Most of the subsequent events, which Stier Group organized then, and continues to do these days, are highly specialized exhibition and conventions or prestigious national events. The exhibitions are in the fields of technology, research, science, medicine and specific industrial topics.

Company Policy – New initiatives & traditional exhibitions Stier Group has initiated and developed a series of exhibition, which are held periodically in Israel. The majority of these exhibition have become extremely popular and well attended events, all of national importance and some with vast international interest. Moreover, Stier Group is expert in organizing tailored specialized events requiring a high level of management and precision conclusion.

Organizing 10-12 Major Exhibitions a Year
The firm organizes between 10-12 major events a year, each being a main exhibition in it’s field giving the maximum attention in a way that ensure that each event receives the special care it deserves.

Congress Management
Stier Group is equipped to prepare, manage and execute national and international conventions either concurrent to related exhibition or independant of any exhibition. Services include Turn-Key project management (selection of convention sites, preparation for publicity and publications, and also congress secretariat management for supervision prior to and during events). From 1974 to 1999 Stier Group organized over 700 meetings of various sizes – from small symposia to large and complex conventions, some of which combine seminars and meetings simultaneously. Stier Group also provides a specialized network of travel agents, furnished with sophisticated communication equipment to provide prompt, reliable and special services to visitors.
