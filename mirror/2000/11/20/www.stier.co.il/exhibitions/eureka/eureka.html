Visitors Information    

For the first time in Israel, a multi-convention that will enable executives in the fields of management, marketing, finance, maintenance, production and information systems to examine at close proximity innovations and solutions needed for the system in question.
For the first time in Israel, presentation of solutions and applications that are based on computer demonstrations will be made possible in the lecture hall, in the atmosphere of an all-encompassing multi convention.
For the first time in Israel, solutions will be presented according to topic.
This is the opportunity for companies to display their solutions and applications to the professional public – developers, project managers, executive managers and professional users.

Where and how?
The convention will take place for 3 days, in all 6-lecture halls at Hilton hotel. The sessions will be held concurrently, so the visitors can choose between the lectures. There are 5 sessions each day, all together 90 sessions, and 3 plenum with Keynote Speaker. The sessions are planned according to the following topics:
for details of the sessions see "congress"
Softweek exhibition will be held concurrently – the first international exhibition of business solutions and applications in Israel.

Who should attend?
This conference is designed for professionals in the fields of high-tech, production, finance, logistic, marketing, industry, and public services.
