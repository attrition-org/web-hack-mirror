Congresses and Meetings:

A congress is planned to run concurrently with the exhibition on NEW TECHNOLOGIES TRADE PRESENTATIONS.    Engineers from exhibiting companies will present papers in a congress organized by a Steering Committee comprised of Ms S Birenboim of Silvan Trading, Mr I Erez of the Feuchtwanger Group, and Mr G Tilman, who represents Contel Control and Instrumentation Engineering Ltd.
The congress will be held in  Hebrew  and English.  Those wishing to present a paper are requested to send an abstract of 100 words to the Congress Secretariat no later than 31 January 2002.
For further information regarding both the exhibition and congress, please contact Mrs Ilana Bart Tatz  at  II Stier Group Ltd.

Various Instructions for Exhibitors:

  • Public entrance: Registration areas are in building 32 and building 27.
  • The height of the stand must not exceed 2.50 meters. Exhibitors wishing a display higher then the maximum height must receive written authorization from the organizers. Note: any such authorization is subject to a surcharge by the organizers.
  • Unconstructed space may be rented in specific areas of the exhibition halls. The minimum size of such space must be 40 sqm.
  • Unconstructed space does not include: partitions, fascia signs, spotlights, furniture, electrical supply.
  • Prior to the setup of Unconstructed space, a final plan/layout must be submitted to the organizers for approval. The recommended period for submission of the final plan is at least 60 days prior to booth set up.
  • Fees/prices listed on the brochure have been calculated as of July 2000. The organizers reserve the right to change these rates without prior notification; however this will not affect those that have already submitted their application and reserved their exhibition space prior to such changes.
