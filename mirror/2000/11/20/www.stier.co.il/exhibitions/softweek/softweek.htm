Introduction:
A new concept for presenting business solution and software applications demonstration to chief executives in managing, marketing, financing, maintenance, and production together with solutions to projects developers and projects managers.
Softweek will expose you to wide clients – some that you are already familiar with, and to new. It will save you expensive time and effort. It will enable you to take part in a prestigious professional event, well publicized and with many participants, at a minimal cost.
The high-tech industry in Israel is one of the most important and interesting enterprises in the world.
The rapid technological development creates a permanent need for updated data for the professional community and for the skilled users.
The target population of Softweek is completely professional. Softweek is intended primarily to the top executive level and decision makers in organizations and industries – president, vice president, production-managers, R&D directors, project managers, marketing managers, accounting managers, and engineers.
It designed to all companies and firms in the following eras: hi-tech, computers, automation, data retrieval, graphic design, manpower, international trading, barcode, information systems, telemarketing, business intelligence, statistics, architecture, etc.
