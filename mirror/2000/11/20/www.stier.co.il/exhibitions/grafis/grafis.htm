  GraFis 2001
The 4th International Exhibition for Digital Design, Printing and Publishing

26-29 November 2001 Tel Aviv Fairgrounds

Visitors Information

  FLOOR PLANS

Grafis is the main exhibition in the field of digital design, print and production. 
The exhibition combines equipment and technologies in all digital media eras: printing, Internet, video editing, animation and multimedia, together with equipment and automation for printing production, pre-print and digital print.

Various Instructions for Exhibitors:

  • Public entrance: Registration area is in building 32., see floor plan.
  • The height of the stand must not exceed 2.50 meters. Exhibitors wishing a display higher then the maximum height must receive written authorization from the organizers. Note: any such authorization is subject to a surcharge by the organizers.
  • Unconstructed space may be rented in specific areas of the exhibition halls. The minimum size of such space must be 40 sqm.
  • Unconstructed space does not include: partitions, fascia signs, spotlights, furniture, electrical supply.
  • Prior to the setup of Unconstructed space, a final plan/layout must be submitted to the organizers for approval. The recommended period for submission of the final plan is at least 60 days prior to booth set up.
