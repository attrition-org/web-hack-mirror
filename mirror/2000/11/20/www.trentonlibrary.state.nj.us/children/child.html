Come visit the recently renovated Children's Room!
Our Children's Department has more than 20,000 books for children to borrow, reference materials for school projects, and a multimedia computer for children to use. We help children with homework, reference questions and papers, and word processing on the computer. An average of 1,414 children a month visit the Children's Room.

November is American Indian Heritage Month

Film and story times are 10AM & 7PM on Mondays and 10AM on Tuesdays. On Wednesdays there is a film at 3PM and on Saturdays there is a story, film or craft activity at 2PM.

The Children's Department is looking for donations of 16mm films for children. If you have films in good condition that you want to give to the Trenton Public Library please contact:
