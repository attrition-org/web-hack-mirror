Federal Government Documents

The Trenton Public Library is a partial government depository and receives about 25% of all documents published by the U.S. Federal Government. Our holdings include the Federal Register, the Congressional Register, and all census materials for the state of New Jersey.

For other Federal Government information available on the World Wide Web, see the Federal Government link on our List of Links.
