Customer Information:

 If you are a consumer and would like more information on Aiwa products, then please call our Aiwa customer information centre on 0870 168 9000 or alternatively e-mail info@london.aiwa.co.uk

To locate your nearest Aiwa Authorised Service Centre, go to our service and support website www.aiwa-euroservice.com, and proceed to the service facility list. The Authorised Service Centre listings are available as a PDF file, to read this you will need the Adobe Acrobat Reader installed on your PC. To obtain a copy of the software, follow this link www.adobe.co.uk

If you are not an Aiwa spares account holder, you can order spare parts for your Aiwa products from our authorised spare parts distributor CPC on 01772 654455.
