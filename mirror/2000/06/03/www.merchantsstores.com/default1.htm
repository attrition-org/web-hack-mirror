Our Privacy Policy:

We will not sell, rent, loan or give away your information to anyone. We do email customers, on a random basis, after a sale to make sure that both the products and service they received were satisfactory. The only other time that you will hear from us is if we have a question about, or problem with, your order.

NOTICE TO WEBTV USERS: You may not be able to place an order on our website because WebTV technology (even the latest versions) doesn't allow the use of "cookies"...this means that the shopping cart feature won't work properly. If you're using WebTV (or just have a "thing" against cookies) and want to place an order with us, just give us a call, or mail, email or fax your order to us.
