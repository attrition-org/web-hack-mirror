Philosophy
    Sitka School District Home School Support Program is in its fourth year of operation.We believe that education may be gained through a wide variety of experiences and situations, which includes the home and the community environment as valid and important resources. We believe it is important to offer choices and the resources and services to support those choices. We are in the position of offering a bridge between people in our community who have chosen an alternative to enrollment in the Sitka Schoools and the traditional school setting. In order to honor the diversity of the population of Sitka, we have developed a program which allows for flexibility and options.
     
Curriculum Choices
    Curriculum options for children kindergarten through eighth grade include choices from prepacked curriculums or the option of creating an individualized program.The Home School Coordinators assist in putting together a balanced curriculum and are available for consultation on a regular basis.

    The majority of our high school and middle school curriculum is ordered from University of North Dakota-Independent Study. Our credit requirements are aligned with Sitka High School. Testing for all high school courses takes place in the home school office.All records and transcripts are kept in the Home School office and a Sitka School District diploma is awarded upon graduation. Contact our office for additional information on how high school classes are handled. Specific responsibilities are outlined for parents and students.

     
Curriculum Support: Lending Library and Workshops
    The Home School Corespondence Program has established a lending library - a collection of materials covering all topic areas and age/grade levels. The resources are available to our participants for check-out. Included, also, are computer software programs, math manipulatives, science material tubs, etc.

    This year's workshops include an art and crafts workshop (parent facilitated), science workshop, and a writer's workshop (Home School Coordinator facilitated). Materials needed for the workshops are provided by the office.
