Middle School Principal Position
POSITION VACANCY

The Sitka School District is accepting applications for a middle school principal position beginning the 2000-2001 school year. Valid Type B Alaska Administrative Certificate required. Closing Deadline:  June 8, 2000 or open until filled.  Applications and information may be obtained from the Superintendent's Office at 300 Kostrometinoff Street, by calling (907)966-1251 or at our web site http://www.ssd.k12.ak.us/SSD/appproc.html.  The Sitka School District is an equal opportunity employer.  Alaska Natives are encouraged to apply.

Preschool Position
POSITION VACANCY

The Sitka School District is accepting applications for a preschool special education position.  Valid Type A Alaska Teaching Certificate with a special education endorsement or Valid Type C Alaska Teaching Certificate with Speech Pathology endorsement required.  Special education major and experience in effective schools teaching techniques preferred.  Closing Deadline: Open until filled.  Applications and information may be obtained from the Superintendent's Office at 300 Kostrometinoff Street, by calling (907)966-1251 or at our web site http://www.ssd.k12.ak.us/SSD/appproc.html.  The Sitka School District is an equal opportunity employer.  Alaska Natives are encouraged to apply.
