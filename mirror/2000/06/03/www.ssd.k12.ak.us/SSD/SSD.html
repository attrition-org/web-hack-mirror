Mission Statement

Reflecting our unique historical, cultural, and environmental diversity, the Sitka School District, through innovative use of educational, vocational, and community resources, educates all students to become competent, literate, responsible citizens capable of making successful life choices within the global community.

"Educating Today's Children To Become Tomorrow's Leaders."
