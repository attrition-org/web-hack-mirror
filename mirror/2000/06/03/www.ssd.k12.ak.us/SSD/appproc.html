Teacher Application Procedures

Back to Sitka Main Page

Thank you for your interest in the Sitka School District.  The following materials may be printed to assist you in completing your application packet.

1.  Look on the Sitka School District's web site to find out what certificated positions are currently available.

2.  In order to apply for any of these vacancies you must complete and print out the Alaska Teacher Placement Statewide Application Form.

3.  Along with this application, the Sitka School District requires that the following items be completed and returned to the Sitka School District, PO Box 179, Sitka, AK  99835 as well:

  •  Information Form
  •     Current Resume
  •    Three (3) References (mailed directly from the reference)
  •     Unofficial Transcripts to apply for position,  Official Transcripts required if hired for a position.
  •     Copy of your Alaska Teacher Certification or your Certification Application
  •     Professional Philosophy
