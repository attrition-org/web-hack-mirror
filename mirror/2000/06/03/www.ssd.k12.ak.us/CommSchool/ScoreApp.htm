Summer Camping Outdoor Recreation Education


This program is open to youth ages 10-14 years old; activities include hands on projects, working on local trails, hikes, a camping trip, field trips and learning about outdoor survival.


The project is sponsored by Community Schools
S.C.O.R.E. Supervisor - Woody Widmark

WHEN: June 1 - July 16, 1999
Mondays and Wednesdays - 11:45-4:00 p.m.
OR
Tuesdays and Thursdays - 11:45-4:00 p.m.

Plus
A camping trip and special activities

WHERE: Local state and national parks and lands.


HOW TO GET INVOLVED: There is no fee for this program but youth must complete an application form and make a commitment to complete the project and attend regularly. There is a limited enrollment.

APPLICATION: An application form is attached or one may be obtained at the Community Schools office. Applications are due back at the Community Schools office ( 601 HPR, Sitka) by May 14th. Be sure to answer the question "Why do you want to be in SCORE?", this is very important to us.

WHAT YOU'LL NEED TO PROVIDE: You will need to bring your own outdoor gear, boots, gloves, rain gear etc. You will also need to provide sack lunches or snacks and a beverage.

All youth who join the S.C.O.R.E. team receive a S.C.O.R.E. shirt, learn about local trails and outdoor survival, enjoy a pool party after project is completed and have FUN!


For more information, please call Community Schools at 747-8670.

PARENT COMMENTS
1994


The following are comments from parent with children involved in the S.C.O.R.E. program.

Team building is one of the best aspects of this program.

My son is learning important information in survival skills as well as having fun with his peers.

David seems to really enjoy S.C.O.R.E. and has enthusiasm for it. It's very important for him to be at each meeting. I think it has helped his self-esteem and increased his willingness to get away from the TV.

This is a good program for the children over the summer months, very educational. It teaches the kids values and how to be productive citizens. Kids think its FUN. A great program for community as a whole.

I would like to see the program extended, i.e.: Winter Outdoor Education/Survival.
