NorthernLight

Search Results are Likely to be:
Educational Research-based

Specific Search Tips: typical web searching with a full +/- system that supports truncation and automatically searches plurals

Comments/Recommendations:

  • Search results are organized into easy to understand catagorized sub folders
  • Clean organization

    • Allows for publication searches


http://www.northernlight.com

Eblast

Search Results are Likely to be:
Educational

Specific Search Tips: basic search techniques, indexes sites reviewed by the editors of Encyclopedia Britannica, all sites are recommended: 1-5 stars

Comments/Recommendations:

  • Provides subject indexes
  • Highlights "Site of the Day"
  • Links to Encyclopedia Britannica on-line
  • Provides "Bookmarks of the Smart and Famous"
  • Allows for simultaneous searching at AltaVista
http://www.eblast.com

AltaVista

Search Results are Likely to be:
Educational Research-based

Specific Search Tips: simple and advanced searching using +/-, wild cards, truncation, and exact phrases

Comments/Recommendations:

  • Allows for a search of terms specific to these fields: URL, host, applet, anchor, images
  • Has ability to limit search to pages written in specified languages
  • Allows speciality searches such as maps, graphics, people finder

    • Has ability to refine searches and translate text to a variety of languages.
http://www.altavista.com

Dogpile

Search Results are Likely to be:
Variety 

Specific Search Tips:

Comments/Recommendations:

  • Allows you to prioritize the search engine you wish to use
  • Supports 25 search engines

    • Results are listed by search engine
http://www.dogpile.com

Excite

Search Results are Likely to be:
Commercial

Specific Search Tips: Simple and advanced searching using +/- and boolean searches of AND, OR, NOT

Comments/Recommendations:

  • Searches for the concept of what you are searching for as well as the key words
  • Allows for extended searching
  • Offers a Search Wizard which is a thesaurus
  • Provides a helpful reference desk

    • Provides a link to note-worthy news articles releated to your search
http://www.excite.com
