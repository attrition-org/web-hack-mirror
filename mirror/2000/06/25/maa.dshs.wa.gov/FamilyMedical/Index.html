Beginning September 1999, MAA established the Family Medical Project to help families who stopped getting medical benefits after they left the cash TANF program.   Following are several pages for the Project.

Family Medical Project - an overview of the project.

Memo to CSOs - describing the project and procedures for CSO workers.

Client Flyer - explains to clients how the project will affect them.
Client Letter - this letter will go out to affected clients in February 2000.  Using returns from this letter, DSHS will update the addresses for clients before they are reinstated.

Frequently Asked Questions - this site, intended for clients and providers, will be coming soon.
