  Our Mission
  The Medical Assistance Administration maximizes opportunities for low-income people to obtain appropriate, quality health
services.

               Our Goals
  To provide quality and timely disability determinations while containing costs in the disability determination process.

  To enhance and maintain customer focused orientation within MAA, utilizing continuous quality improvement principles.
 

  To enhance the quality of, access to, and satisfaction with health services provided to MAA clients.

  To maintain or enhance the performance of operational systems essential to supporting clients’ access to quality health services.
