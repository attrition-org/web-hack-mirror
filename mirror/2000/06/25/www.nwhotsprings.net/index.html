MINDLESS THINKING
We all surrender each and every day,
to mindless thinking,
and a lot of nothing to say.
With glazy stares
over crystal rim,
that no one bares,
to stop,
or cares,
to cease,
because it's the one and only,
true communication,
caused by sound frustration.

Actually we have nothing to say,
and like the music loud,
and cover ourselves in a shroud,
of smoke, dim lights, the crowd.
And I may be the only one,
but what is the fun?
We all sit there and smile,
while all the while,
we know it's fake,
but our rep is at stake.
And I don't know how much more I can take,
before I just break.

it's a crime.

ignite on Jerry,
you're not supposed to be merry.

that stayed longer than expected,
but is dearly wanted,
for what is has projected,
upon our brain,
and corrupted what once was sane.

the commercial god is crowned,
and is wealthier than any man,
whether you're dan, dave or stan,
you all pay your toll, your tax,
for twisted facts.
