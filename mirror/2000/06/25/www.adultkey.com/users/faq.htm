Question 1 How long does it take to get an ID?
Answer 1
Return to top		 You are assigned an ID as soon as you sign-up. Your ID is then activated within 10 minutes or less if your credit card is approved. If you applied longer than 10 minutes ago and have not been able to use your ID, email keymaster@adultkey.com

 
Question 2 Where and how can I use my AdultKey ID?
Answer 2
Return to top		 AdultKey is valid on all Participating AdultKey Websites. When you go to any of these sites look for a yellow banner that displays "AdultKey Owners Enter Here" and click on it. You will then be asked for your User ID and Password.

 
Question 3 How do I find out my AdultKey User ID and Password?
Answer 3
Return to top		 Go to the AdultKey User Information Checker to locate your information. You will be asked for the card number you originally used to apply.

 
Question 4 Why don't some of the links on the Participating Sites work?
Answer 4
Return to top		 Occasionally a Webmaster may forget to update his/her site's information in our database, so an un-usable link may appear. If you find such a link please inform us my sending email to keymaster@adultkey.com

 
Question 5 When I enter my UserID and Password it does not send me to the site I am trying to enter. Why?
Answer 5
Return to top		 After you enter your UserID and Password our system displays an Advertisment or Special Offer screen. If you are not interested in that offer you may click on the "Continue to Site" button and you will be sent to the site.

 
Question 6 How will the charge appear on my credit card bill?
Answer 6
Return to top		 The charge on your credit card bill will appear as "AdultKey" or "N A S Internet".

 
Question 7 How do I cancel my membership?
Answer 7
|Return to top		 AdultKey is a Non-Recurring Membership. If you wish to cancel, you do not need to do anything. Your membership will automatically expire at the end of it's term.

 
Question 8 My UserID and Password are correct but they do not work. Why?
Answer 8
Return to top		 First, make sure your membership has not expired. You can check this at the User Information Checker. Second make sure you are typing your UserID and Password correctly. You may also send e-mail to keymaster@adultkey.com

 
Question 9 Can I upgrade my membership?
Answer 9
Return to top		 Yes, please send your UserID and the term (in years) you wish to upgrade to keymaster@adultkey.com.You will receive a confirmation by e-mail

 
Question10 None of the answers above helped me. What do I do?
Answer 10
Return to top		 Please send e-mail to keymaster@adutkey.com
