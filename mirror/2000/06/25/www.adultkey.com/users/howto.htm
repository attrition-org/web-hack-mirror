Instructions to Use your AdultKey ID:
Step 1 Getting to the AdultKey Sites List (Links Page):
Go to the AdultKey Sites List page at http://www.adultkey.com/sites/   The Sites List page has all the AdultKey participating sites listed in alphabetical order.
Step 2 Find the "AdultKey Members Enter Here" banner:
Once you are in one of the participating sites look for a banner that reads "AdultKey Members Enter Here" and click on it.
Step 3 Enter your AdultKey ID and Password:
You will be prompted to enter your AdultKey ID and Password, please do so, then click on "Enter Site".
Step 4 Advertising Page:
Before you are forwarded to the site you may see an Advertising Page asking you if you want to order.

If you do not wish to order then simply click on the
"No - Continue to Site" button and you will be sent to the site.
