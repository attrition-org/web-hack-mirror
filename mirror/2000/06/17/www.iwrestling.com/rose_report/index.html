    Randy Martson reports that Billy Gunn could be back onto WWF TV in September. If he can come back in as good a shape as before, his return could be just as big as the Undertaker's. Jim Ross often calls Gunn the best pure athlete on the roster, and with work on his mic skills, Gunn could be a major player in the future of the company. When he injured himself, if you remember, he was feuding with DX. There is no word on whether he'll be with or against DX on his return.

    According to http://pwtorch.com...

    Vince Russo is just about at his boiling point with WCW. He is tremendously frustrated with just about everything that is going on within WCW, including the low ratings, Standards and Practices vetoing ideas, and Bischoff and himself arguing over ideas. For example, Bischoff wants to bring back Kimberly and Lex Luger, while Russo is dead set against it. People close to Russo said he was so upset he "quit" from WCW earlier today, but thought it over for a few hours and decided to take a few days off to ponder his future with the company. Reportedly, Hulk Hogan is siding with Russo in this argument. There is a possibility that this is a work, but it isn't likely. As always, we will keep you all abreast of the situation, and pass along any and all information we receive.
