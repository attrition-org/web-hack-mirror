    • HWF Presents Ontario Onslaught
    By William Martinez on June 17, 2000

    The following was sent in to me by Markus Anderson:

    HWF WRESTLING PRESENTS:
    Ontario Onslaught
    Saturday Night July 1st
    The Rinx (65 Orfus RD)
    (416)-410-7469
    Toronto Ontario
    Tickets $15 Dollars
    DOORS OPEN 6:00pm
    BELL TIME 7:30pm

    MATCH #1 Jason Kronan VS G. Rease Crusierweight Sensation "X- Rated" Jason Kronan faces G. Rease with his manager Otto Bahn in a match for the Hardcore Wrestling Federation Crusierweight Championship belt. Jason Kronan a native of good old USA, will be making the trip across the border. This 20 year old ECW Prospect has impressed Paul Heyman so much that he is the talk of the HWF locker room.

    MATCH #2 Tornado VS Tyson Dukes Fan favorite Tornado is set to face Tyson Dukes both of these top Crusierweights have just recently made there return to Hardcore Wrestling after stints down in Florida with Dory Funk. This match will prove to be a high energy matchup.

    MATCH #3 The Anarchist VS Custom Made Man Custom Made Man one of the top performers in the HWF will have a big challenge when he faces The Anarchist in a match to determine the #1 Contender to the Hardcore Wrestling Federations Crusierweight Championship. These men have never met each other in the squared circle before so this will be yet another huge match.

    MATCH #4 The Pitbull VS Jack Damage This is the return for the Pitbull to Toronto, back on September 25th at the Opera House he was involved in the BLOODIEST match in Hardcore History, following the match he needed 20 Stitches, he is set to get HARDCORE on former Heavyweight Champion (and member of Legit Heat) Jack Damage.

    MATCH #5 The Ebony X-Press VS Triple B & Slade The Ebony X-Press has destroyed every tag team in there path over the last 6 months, defending the Tag Team titles in ever major city in Canada along the way. But this will be there "LARGEST" challenge yet as 6'8 400 pound Triple B (Big Black Bastard) and his tag partner Slade get there Tag Team Shots.

    MATCH #6 Bloody Bill Skullion VS Notorious T.I.D. comming off a BARBWIRE MATCH in Guelph Ontario these too HWF Hardcore Legends will be feeling the pain, but no doubt will be able to crank it up for a hardcore TABLES & CHAIRS matchup.

    MATCH #7 (2T) Tommy Twilight VS Magnus This match will be in a 12 foot high steel cage to settle this feud once and for all. Tommy Twilight is being flown in from Grand Prix Wrestling for this BIG TIME Main Event. that will see the current Heavyweight Champion put the belt on the line in a match he promises will be the BIGGEST and BADDEST Match in Toronto Indy History.
