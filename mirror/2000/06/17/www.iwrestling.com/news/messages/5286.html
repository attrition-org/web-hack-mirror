    • Trish Stratus gettin her own nutrition bar
    By Dustin H on June 16, 2000

    Trish Stratus coming out with her own nutrition bar, Hardcore Holly to get a push, & more

  • The odds-on-favorite location for the 2001 edition of "No Way Out" is Providence, Rhode Island, while the rumored city to host Summerslam 2001 is San Jose, California. Of course, Wrestlemania 2001 will be held in Houston Texas' Astrodome but that should go without saying. I will try and get more confirmation on these dates as well as when tickets go on sale for Wrestlemania in the future so keep checking back.

  • Hardcore Holly is all but confirmed to be getting a push later on this summer, perhaps even to the point where he wins the I-C title from Chris Benoit in a few months. The WWF is said to be happy with his work ethic, perserverance as well as the fact that he does a great job of keeping himself out of trouble/controversy (except the BB incident of course). There was some concern that after Vince Russo's departure (Russo was said to be one of Holly's biggest supporters), the WWF would slowly put Hardcore on the backburner however as we have seen in recent weeks, it is quite the opposite.

  • This is just a rumor but I've heard that Trish Stratus will come out with her very own line of supplements or a nutrition bar or other related health product. I'm not sure if it will just be available in Canada, but I'll try and get more information.
