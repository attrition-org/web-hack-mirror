    WWF RAW is WAR results: 6/12

    Welcome everyone, welcome to RAW is WAR! RAW starts off with footage from Judgment Day when the Undertaker made his return and cost The Rock the WWF Championship. The Rock says that he has heard that all weekend long that come Monday night, The Rock is in store for a fight. Come Monday night, Kane and the Undertaker are going to get back at The Rock for hitting them with chairs. The entire Faction will get back at The Rock for making their lives a living hell.
