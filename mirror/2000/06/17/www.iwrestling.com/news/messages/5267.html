    • WWF July RAW Magazine Preview
    By Justin Winslow on June 15, 2000

    Cover: The Dudley Boyz has just put some unknown wrestler through a table. D-Von and Buh-Buh Ray are dressed up as waiters and on the cover it says, "Your Table is waiting..."

    Features:

    Table for Two (Page 14)
    The Dudley Boyz have paid dues and earned respect on their way to Superstar success

    The RAW Interview (Page 22)
    Jim Ross talks about his behind-the-scenes role in the Federation, as well as "Good Ol’ J.R."

    Want to play Doctor? (Page 30)
    Trish Stratus was on her way to medical school before destiny made her a diva

    Inside "The Game" (Page 36)
    Triple H tells the real story behind his rise to the top, on and off camera

    All in the Family (Page 44)
    The Samoan Mat Dynasty Over the Decades, From Afa and Sika to Rikishi and The Rock

    Bull's a Fighter
    Barry Buchanan has fought his way back from Limbo to seize a spot in the Federation.

    The People's Hot Rod? (Page 54)
    Add The Rock and Kane to the Ever-Growing Roster of Federation-Themed Funny Cars.

    Beast from the East (Page 58)
    From Signs and Entrance Music to the Superstars Themselves, Japanese Influences can be seen all over the Federation.

    Click to Win (Page 62)
    How can you buy authentic signed federation collectibles? Point your mouse and click.

    Cover Story:
    The Dudley Boyz tells RAW Magazine exactly how they’re tabling the competition.

    You can buy this WWF RAW Magazine at all newsstands and at WWF Shopzone.
