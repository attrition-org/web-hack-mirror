    By Kevin Gregg on June 17, 2000

    News on the persona of the Undertaker and more!

    The following is a recap of a 4 and a half minute video from undertaker.com:
    The video clip starts off with the Undertaker being revamped with some new music, and that's the music of Kid Rock. The Undertaker says that for years, they did the toning with the bells, and they want something with a lot of energy to express that he is coming back with attitude. The Undertaker says that Kid Rock's American Bad Ass hit it right on the head.

    Chris Jericho: "Kid Rock has really tied into the fan base!"

    Jeff Hardy says that he and Matt were out there watching the sound check and the performance, and Kid Rock rocked the house. The Undertaker says that Kid Rock was rocking the place at the rehearsal. The Undertaker says that Kid Rock is a big time player, and he looked forward to singing his song on World Wrestling Federation T.V. Jericho was sitting in the dressing room, and he got the urgent message that the producer of the show wanted him to introduce Kid Rock. The producer asked where he has been, because he was on to introduce Kid Rock. Jericho says that he can hardly speak today, because of the power and fire that he gave into the introduction.

    The Undertaker says that it's not the match made in heaven, but it's a marriage that is going to work real well. He likes Kid Rock, and he has the ability to strike a nerve, because he knows what fans want. He has the ability to give lyrics that people can identify to.
