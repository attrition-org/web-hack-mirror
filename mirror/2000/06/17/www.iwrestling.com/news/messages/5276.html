    • WWF Smackdown! results: 6/15
    By Kevin Gregg on June 15, 2000

    WWF Smackdown! results: 6/15
    Commentators: Michael Cole and Jerry "The King" Lawler
    Report by: Kevin Gregg of PowerWrestling.com

    WWF Smackdown! starts off with footage from this past Monday night when a match was set for The Rock, The Undertaker and Kane against Triple H, Shane and Vince McMahon for the King of the Ring. Linda McMahon made that match, and she also put Shane and Vince in a tag team tables match against the Dudley Boyz! Chris Jericho also got another shot at the WWF belt against Triple H, and the match was extremely close, but Triple H was able to come out on top. Later in the night, Kane and The Rock were forced to go against each other, because the Regime took pictures of Kane's face, and they were going to show the world, but GTV proved that the pictures weren't able to be developed.

    King of the Ring Qualifying Match
    Grandmaster Sexay vs. Edge
    The Rock and Kane then hammered away on the McMahon/Helmsley Faction. Too Cool's music is playing in the arena, and they are on their way to the ring as the WWF Tag Team Champions. Grandmaster Sexay is going against Edge in this King of the Ring Qualifying match. Christian comes out to the ring with Edge, and to start off the match, Grandmaster Sexay and Edge lock up in the middle of the ring. Grandmaster Sexay gives Edge a side head lock, and then Edge whips him off the ropes. Grandmaster takes Edge down with a shoulder block. Edge goes for a high cross body, but Grandmaster Sexay is able to duck it.

    Grandmaster Sexay slams the head of Edge into the turnbuckle, and then he goes to the second rope and nails Edge with a missle drop kick. Grandmaster Sexay grabs the arm of Edge and works on it, and then Edge rakes Grandmaster in the eyes. Edge whips Grandmaster off the ropes, and he goes for a back body drop, but Grandmaster kicks him in the chest. Grandmaster then clotheslines Edge to the outside of the ring, and Christian is able to help Edge to his feet. Grandmaster Sexay gives Edge and Christian a baseball slide to knock them down. Too Cool then dance together. Edge slams Grandmaster Sexay into the steel steps, and then he slams his head into the steel steps.

    Edge rolls Grandmaster back into the ring and he gives him a double ax handle. Edge slams Grandmaster's head into the turnbuckle, and then he kicks away on him. Edge whips Grandmaster to the corner, and Grandmaster falls down in the corner. Edge picks Grandmaster up and gives him a body slam. Edge goes off the ropes, and he nails him with an elbow drop. Edge covers Grandmaster Sexay for a two count, and then he goes for a suplex, but Grandmaster blocks it and puts Edge on the top rope. Grandmaster gives Edge a number of right hands, and then he whips Edge off the ropes. Grandmaster gives Edge an Enziguri, and then he picks him up and gives him a body slam.

    Grandmaster Sexay goes to the top rope and Edge knocks Grandmaster down before he can do anything. Grandmaster Sexay is able to regain control, and he goes into a sunset flip into a power bomb. Grandmaster pins Edge for a two count, and then he grabs hold of Christian. Edge tries to charge at Grandmaster, but Grandmaster moves out of the way and Edge hits Christian. Grandmaster rolls Edge up for a two count, and then he gives him a neck breaker. Grandmaster Sexay goes to the top rope, and he gets out his goggles.

    Christian goes to knock Grandmaster down, but Scotty is able to stop him. Grandmaster Sexay gives Edge the hip hop drop, and Shane McMahon comes to the ring with a steel chair and he nails Grandmaster Sexay with it. Edge is then able to pin Grandmaster Sexay to get the win to advance in the King of the Ring tournament. The Faction is shown backstage, and Vince says that thanks to Shane, phase one is complete, and then they leave to have some fun!

    Winner - Edge

    Vince McMahon and the rest of the McMahon/Helmsley Faction are all on their way to the ring, and they have got to be frustrated because of Linda McMahon. The fans give the Faction a negative reaction as they get in the ring, and Shane gets on the mic and says "This just in..." For whatever reason, Edge and Christian have challenged the very tag team that Vince McMahon defeated in this very ring last Monday night. So tonight, in this very ring, Edge and Christian will be taking on none other than the Dudley Boyz. By the way, Edge and Christian have been gracious enough prior to their match, so get your cameras ready for a special photo opportunity of Edge and Christian.

    Vince gets on the mic and says that on last Monday night, his charming, darling, CEO wife, Linda came down to the ring, and she decided that she was going to make a match. She made a match up with Stephanie defending her WWF Women's Championship. The fans weren't applauding after the match was over, because Stephanie is still the WWF Women's Champion. The fans cheered when Linda made a match with Vince and Shane in a table match against the Dudleys. They weren't cheering because of the McMahons victory. The fans really thought the CEO accomplished something when she made a match with Triple H defending the WWF title against someone he didn't know.

    The fans weren't too happy with the result, because Triple H is still the Game, and the World Wrestling Federation Champion. The fans probably thought it was a good idea when Linda prevented Kane, the Undertaker and The Rock from tearing each other apart. She decided that they should unify and at the King of the Ring, Kane, the Undertaker and The Rock will compete against the threesome of Shane McMahon, Vince McMahon and Triple H. There are some out there that say that this could win match of the year honors, because the vast majority of the fans are thinking, unbalance. There is no telling who is going to win this match. Both teams have equal abilities. There are some who think The Rock's team has the edge, and there may be an even smaller amount of you that think that Triple H's team doesn't have a chance.

    Triple H has an opinion on the subject, and Triple H gets on the mic and says that he's got an opinion alright. His opinion is that he has never been more confident in a team to be successful than he is that team right there. With Vince McMahon, the Giant Killer, and the Game on one team, they are virtually unstoppable. Triple H says that he is so sure of that, that it's almost not even fair. It is a great match, but it's almost unfair. Triple H says that they are going to take the match that Linda made, as great as it is, and they are going to make it even better. There seems to be some confusion as to who the number one contender is for the WWF Championship.

    Is it Kane? Is it the Undertaker? Is it The Rock? The problem is that you really don't know, so at the King of the Ring, we are going to solve all of that. In one match, they are going to get it all done. They are going to put the number one contendership on the line. In the unlikely event that Linda's team can beat them, the person that gets that victory will then be deemed the number one contender, and he will defend his World Wrestling Federation Championship against that person in July. That seems like a good deal. Can the Undertaker trust that Kane won't stab him in the back for a shot at the belt? Can you really trust that The Rock won't stab the Undertaker and Kane in the back for a shot at the belt?

    On Linda's team, it seems that no one can trust anyone, and Triple H says that he has always heard it said that there is no "I" in team. Triple H says that he has successfully put the "I" in team, because it will be about who wants it more, the Undertaker, Kane or The Rock. In the likely event that Triple H's team is victorious, then out of those three, nobody gets a shot at the WWF belt, he will give the number one contender's slot to the winner of the King of the Ring, and he will defend the World Wrestling Federation Championship against that person. Vince tells the fans to give it up for Triple H!

    Vince then continues talking, and he says that Shane has a surprise for the Dudley Boyz, and in this very ring, Chris Jericho, Y2J is going to have a nice, or not so little surprise when the Undertaker arrives here tonight. The Undertaker will be greeted with a special surprise. Kane will see action here tonight, and Kane will square off against? Vince says that if he told everyone, it wouldn't be a surprise. The Rock will also see WWF action in this very ring tonight, but against who? Vince starts to say, and Triple H tells Vince that the information should be on a need to know basis, and the pieces of crap known as the fans don't need to know!

    A taxi is shown, and Pat Patterson, Michael Chiota and Gerald Brisco are in it. Another taxi comes up from behind, and Crash says that he needs to find someone, because he needs protection. Brisco, Patterson and the referee get out of the limo, and they argue.

    T&A vs. the Hardy Boyz
    T&A are on their way to the ring along with Trish Stratus, and they are going against the Hardy Boyz in tag team action. Jeff and Test start it off in the ring, and Jeff kicks Test in the gut a number of times, and then he goes to whip Test to the corner, but Test reverses it. Test grabs Jeff Hardy, but Jeff is able to get off Test's shoulder. Jeff goes for a suplex, but he is unable to do it. Jeff gives Test a number of gut shots, and then he tags in Matt, and the two give Test a double suplex. Matt nails Test with a number of right hands, and then he gives Albert a right hand. Test grabs hold of Matt and then he slams him down to the mat. Test gets on top of Matt, and he gives him a number of right hands.

    Test tags in Albert and then the two double team Matt. Albert pins Matt for a two count as Jeff breaks up the pin. Albert gives Matt a shot to the throat, and then he gives him a number of kicks and punches in the corner. Albert picks Matt Hardy up and he tosses him across the ring. Albert backs Matt into the corner, and then he whips him to the other corner. Albert charges at Matt, but Matt is able to get his elbow up and stop Albert. Matt goes to the second rope, and he gives Albert a tornado DDT. Matt tags in Jeff as Albert tags in Test. Jeff gives Test some shots to the mid section and then he goes to work on Albert. Jeff tags in Matt, and then the two double team Test with double leg drops. All four men get in the ring and the Hardy Boyz double team Albert as well. The two clothesline Albert to the outside of the ring and then Test goes for a right hand, but he misses.

    Matt gives Test the twist of fate, and then Jeff goes for the swan-ton bomb, but Trish is able to stop him from doing so. Albert splashes Matt in the corner, and then Test gives Matt a boot to the face, and then he pins Matt to get the win. After the contest, Albert holds Jeff and Trish slaps him across the face. Lita comes to the ring and knocks Trish Stratus down from behind and Albert doesn't even realize it!Test grabs hold of Lita and shoves her down to the mat. The Hardy Boyz clear the ring of T&A and then Jeff gives Albert a high cross body over the top rope. Lita grabs hold of Trish and slams her down to the mat. Lita then goes to the second rope and gives Trish a leg drop.

    Winner - T&A

    The Undertaker is shown in the backstage area and he has arrived on Smackdown!. A number of police are there to greet him, and Vince shows up and asks what we have here. It's an American Bad A$$. Bull Buchanan is with him, and Vince welcomes the Undertaker to Chicago. Vince reminds the Undertaker of who he nailed and stole the car of on Monday. The man that he stole the car of went to get a warrant for the arrest of the Undertaker. Vince tells the police to arrest the Undertaker and the police cuff him up. Vince says that the Undertaker is not such a bad a$$ after all.

    The Undertaker tells the police that Bull stole his bike. The police tell the Undertaker if that is the case, they can go after Bull next if he makes a complaint. The Undertaker and Bull get face to face, and the two exchange words. The Undertaker splits on Bull, and then Bull gives the Undertaker a right hand! The police separate the two, and as the Undertaker is hauled off, Vince says that he loves being him!

    King of the Ring Qualifying Match
    Scotty 2 Hotty vs. D'Lo Brown
    Vince and Shane are in the backstage area, and it looks like Vince is leaving and Shane says that he will take care of everything tonight. Vince tells Shane not to have too much fun. D'Lo Brown is on his way to the ring, and he is going against Scotty 2 Hotty in a King of the Ring Qualifying Match. D'Lo Brown starts it off with a kick to the face of Scotty 2 Hotty and he gives him a couple of right hands, then he whips him off the ropes and goes for aback body drop, but Scotty kicks him in the gut. D'Lo tries to throw Scotty to the outside of the ring, but Scotty lands on the apron. Scotty flings himself back into the ring and then Scotty nails D'Lo with a right hand. Scotty whips D'Lo off the ropes, and he catches him with a drop kick.

    Scotty 2 Hotty gives D'Lo Brown an elbow drop, and then he starts doing some moon walking. D'Lo goes for a clothesline, but Scotty ducks it. D'Lo picks up Scotty and he slams him down to the mat. D'Lo nails Scotty with a number of right hands, and then he gives Scotty the D'Lo Leg Drop. D'Lo Brown pin Scotty 2 Hotty for a two count, and then he goes for another slam, but Scotty reverses it into a drop kick. D'Lo goes for a right hand, and then Scotty blocks it and nails D'Lo with some right hands of his own. Scotty whips D'Lo off the ropes, and he gives him an elbow.

    Scotty gives D'Lo Brown a big back body drop, and then Scotty whips D'Lo Brown to the corner, but D'Lo reverses it. D'Lo tries to charge Scotty in the corner, but Scotty moves out of the way and gives D'Lo Brown a bulldog. Scotty 2 Hotty goes for the worm, and he gets it on! Scotty 2 Hotty then goes to the second rope, and goes for a DDT, but D'Lo Brown is able to slam Scotty to the mat. D'Lo Brown then goes to the top rope, and he goes for the frog splash, but Scotty gets out of the way. Scotty then goes to the second rope, and he gives D'Lo Brown a DDT by jumping off.

    Winner - Scotty 2 Hotty

    Patterson, Brisco and Chiota are shown, and Pat tells Gerald that he has to think about his Hardcore Championship, instead of shopping. The referee tells them that he thinks he saw Crash, and Patterson tells him that his job is to count one, two, three. Kane is shown backstage, and he is up next, but who is he facing?

    Non Title Match
    Kane vs. Chris Benoit (c)
    Kane is now headed to the ring, and we are about to find out who he is going against. Kane is going against the WWF Intercontinental Champion, Chris Benoit, but this isn't about the Championship here tonight. Kane gives Benoit a kick to the gut, and then he picks him up and slams him to the corner. Kane charges at Chris Benoit, but Benoit is able to get his elbow up in the way. Benoit backs Kane into the corner, and he stomps, punches and chops away on him. Kane grabs Benoit by the throat and he tosses him into the corner and then gives him a number of right hands. Kane picks Benoit up and he gives him a hip toss to the middle of the ring.

    Kane nails Chris Benoit with a clothesline, and then he picks Benoit up, but Benoit is able to get out of it, and Benoit goes for a back body drop, but Kane gives him a right hand to stop him. Kane whips Chris Benoit off the ropes, and Benoit slide under Kane's leg, and Kane nails Benoit with a big boot. Chris Benoit goes to whip Kane off the ropes, but Kane reverses it, and Benoit gives Kane a drop toe hold. Benoit gives Kane a drop kick to the face, and then he pins him for a two count. Chris whips Kane to the corner, and then he grabs Kane by the waist, and Kane fights out of it with an elbow. Kane gives Benoit a couple of shots to the back, and then he picks him up and gives him a face first suplex.

    Kane picks Chris Benoit up and he gives him a side walk slam. Kane whips Chris Benoit off the ropes, and Benoit rolls under a big boot, and then Benoit goes for the Crippler Cross face, but Kane counters, and goes for a choke slam. Benoit counters and goes for the Cross face again, but he is unable to. Kane nails Chris Benoit with a clothesline, and Benoit rolls to the outside of the ring. He grabs his belt, and Kane follows him out and gives Benoit a right hand. Kane tosses Benoit back into the ring, and then he goes to the top rope and goes to nail Benoit with a clothesline, but Benoit gets the Championship up and hits Kane in the head with it. The referee calls for the bell, and Benoit hits Kane again. Kane is able to get up, and Benoit can't believe it! Chris Benoit gets out of the ring and leaves as Kane's music plays in the arena.

    Winner as a result of a disqualification, Kane

    Christian is shown, and Edge shows up, and Edge says that he talked to their mom, and she is happy that they qualified to the King of the Ring. Edge tells Christian that if they defeat the Dudley Boyz, they'll be on good terms with the Faction, and then they'll probably get a title shot. Christian says that he thought that they were called the Regime, but Edge disagrees. Edge says that they should compromise and call them the Factgime!

    Edge and Christian vs. the Dudley Boyz
    Edge and Christian are now headed to the ring, and they are going against the Dudley Boyz! Christian gets on the mic and says that before they so beat up the Dudley Boyz, he just wants to say greetings to all of their fans in the windy city. Christian says he guesses they call it the windy city, because this town blows! Like so many other cities in this nation, Chicago has a serious case of the uglies! Edge says to think of all the ugly things this city is known for, Roger Ebert, Opera, the Cubs record, which is why, for the benefit for those with Flash Photography, they offer an extra two seconds for a grand total of 7 seconds of Edge and Christian! The two post in the middle of the ring and then the Dudley Boyz come to the ring and the games are over!

    D-Von and Christian start it off in the ring, and Christian gives D-Von a kick to the gut, and then he gives him a right hand. D-Von whips Christian off the ropes, and then he nails him with a clothesline. D-Von tags in Buh Buh Ray Dudley, and the two whip Christian off the ropes as the fans chant "We want table!" Buh Buh gives Christian a tilt a whirl slam, and then D-Von gives him a big splash. Buh Buh gives Christian a number of right hands into the corner, and then he whips Christian to the other corner. Christian gives Buh Buh a kick to the face, and then he tags in Edge. Buh Buh gives Edge a Samoan drop, and then he chops him in the corner.

    Buh Buh gives Christian a clothesline, and then Edge goes to the second rope and gives Buh Buh a missle drop kick. Edge and Christian back Buh Buh into their corner, and they stomp away on him. Christian gives Buh Buh a right hand, and then he tags in Edge. The two whip Buh Buh to the other corner, and then they double team him. Edge nails Buh Buh with some right hands, and then a kick to the gut. Edge tags in Christian, and the two whip Buh Buh off the ropes, and Buh Buh nails Christian with a clothesline, and then he hits Edge with a DDT.

    Buh Buh tags in D-Von and D-Von goes to work on both Edge and Christian. D-Von gives Edge a reverse DDT, and then he pins him for a two count as Christian breaks up the three count. Buh Buh tosses Christian to the outside of the ring and the two teams fight up the ramp. The referee counts both teams out and the match is ruled a double count out, yet the two teams continue to fight. Buh Buh gives Edge a suplex on the stage and Buh Buh sees some tables at the bottom of the stage. D-Von picks up Christian and gives him to Buh Buh, and Buh Buh gets ready to jump off the stage and power bomb him, but DX comes out and stops them from doing so.

    The Road Dogg nails Buh Buh in the head with a steel chair, and then DX gets a dumpster out and puts the Dudley Boyz in the dumpster. Edge, Christian, The Road Dogg and X-Pac put them in and Buh Buh fights out of it, and he nails them with a right hand, but Edge and Christian are able to slam the dumpster shut on the Dudley Boyz. DX ties the dumpster shut, and then they roll it off of the stage onto the tables! A number of WWF officials come out to attend to the Dudley Boyz.

    Winners - Double count out

    Chris Jericho vs. Bull Buchanan
    EMTs are shown helping the Dudley Boyz out of the wreck that they were just in, and then we go to the arena, and Stephanie McMahon-Helmsley is on her way out. Stephanie joins Jerry Lawler and Michael Cole for some commentary, and then the music of Chris Jericho starts playing in the arena. Chris Jericho has a mic with him, and Michael Cole asks Stephanie who his opponent is going to be, but Stephanie says that she can't give it away, because then it wouldn't be a surprise. Chris Jericho gets on the mic and says that week after week, a lot of the Jerichoholics refer to Stephanie as a sl*t, but she is not. She is a filthy disgusting, brutal, bottom feeding... Bull Buchanan's music hits, and he interrupts Jericho's ranting, and he is on his way to the ring to take on Jericho.

    Bull gets in the ring and he shoves Jericho down to the mat, and he knocks him down with a right hand. Chris Jericho gives Bull some chops, and then he gives him a bulldog down to the mat. Jericho pins Bull for a two count, and then Jericho gives Bull a boot to the face, then he goes to the second rope, and nails Bull with a missle drop kick. Bull gets on the apron, and Jericho gives him a drop kick off the apron to knock him down. Jericho grabs a bottle of water and he puts some in his mouth and he spits it at Stephanie! Bull rolls Jericho back into the ring and Jericho catches him with an elbow. Chris Jericho nails Bull with a clothesline, and Stephanie grabs some water and she throws it into the face of Jericho.

    Bull Buchanan then gives Jericho a scissors kick to the back of the head to get the win. After the contest, Triple H comes out and he starts beating on Chris Jericho. WWF officials come to the ring to do their best to stop the fight, but they are unable to do so as Triple H continues to stomp and hammer away on Jericho. Triple H gives Chris Jericho the Pedigree, and then he and Stephanie trash talk him as he's down and out on the mat.

    Winner - Bull Buchanan

    Gerald, Patterson and the referee are all shown, and Pat tells Gerald to get his head out of his a$$, because he is going to get his championship back. The referee tells them that he saw Crash Holly, and Pat tells him to shut up and count the three count. Gerald asks Pat if they can go to a club, and Pat says that they will, after he wins the belt back. Crash Holly is shown in NY, and he is looking for someone, but who is he looking for?

    Footage of what happened last week is shown, and it's of Val Venis taking on Rikishi Phatu after Trish Stratus demanded the match. The two went at it, and then at the end of the contest, Rikishi Phatu and Val fought to the stage, and Rikishi was all bloodied up, but Rikishi kept fighting back. Rikishi gave Val Venis a kick to the face and he knocked him off the stage, and then he gave him a big splash. Val Venis is in the ring, and he says that last Thursday on Smackdown!, he left Rikishi battered, beaten and bloodied in the ring, screaming for mercy. Rikishi then decided that he would kick Val 10 feet off the Smackdown! stage to the concrete floor, and then take his 400 pounds and squash him like a pancake, trying to end his career.

    Val says that only one thing came to his mind after that, and that is pay back. Val calls out Rikishi Phatu, and it looks like we're going to have another match! Rikishi Phatu comes out and he means business! Val doesn't wait for Rikishi to get into the ring, and Val goes after him with some right hands. Rikishi fights back and the two brawl on the outside of the ring for a moment. This isn't an official match as Rikishi tosses some steel steps into the ring. a referee shows up and does his best to break up this fight. Rikishi slams Val Venis into the steel steps on the outside of the ring and then he picks up some television monitors and slams them down to the ground.

    Rikishi gives Val Venis a back body drop onto the announcer's table, and then he slams his head into the table. Rikishi gives Val Venis a body slam on the table, and the announcer's table breaks! Rikishi then gives Val Venis a number of right hands, and WWF officials are trying to break this up. Rikishi gives Val Venis a super kick and then Val grabs a television monitor and he nails Rikishi right in the face with it. Val grabs the monitor one more time, and he slams it right into the head of Rikishi. Val Venis grabs a steel chair and he nails Rikishi with it, right over the head. WWF officials do their match to break this up and they are able to restrain Val Venis.

    The commentators talk about what just went down, and there is blood on the announcer's table. Crash Holly is shown, and he comes across Samuel L. Jackson. Crash talks to Samuel, and he tells him that he's one bad mother. Samuel tells him to shut his mouth, and then he says that he knows Crash, because he's the Hardcore Champion. Crash asks Samuel for some help, and Samuel asks Crash if he tried the APA. Crash says that he has, but he has heat with them. Samuel says that it's his duty to protect Crash Holly, now! Kevin Kelly is shown with The Rock, and we are going to get a word with him in a few moments!

    Kevin now talks to The Rock, and The Rock is going against someone that he doesn't even know tonight. The Rock does his thing with "Finally, The Rock has come back to Chicago!" The Rock says that all night long, they have been talking about a surprise opponent, and for all The Rock knows, it could be anybody. It could even be Kevin Kelly. Kevin says that he can assure The Rock that it's not him. The Rock says that it doesn't matter who the Faction throws at The Rock, and then The Rock says to just bring it, if you smell what The Rock is cooking! Pat, Gerald and the referee are shown, and they come across Samuel L. Jackson. They ask him if he has seen Crash, and Gerald says that is his belt. Samuel stands up and then he knocks Patterson and Brisco down, because they wanted to get at Crash Holly.

    Kurt Angle vs. The Rock

    The Rock is now headed to the ring, and we are going to find out who The Rock is going against in a moment. Triple H and Stephanie are shown watching from the backstage area, and you gotta wonder what they are interested in. It's Kurt Angle, and he's got a mic with him as he makes his way to the ring. Kurt says that the McMahon/Helmsley Faction has hand picked him to be the mystery opponent tonight. The true people's champion! Kurt says that unlike The Rock, he doesn't raise an eyebrow, he raises a standard of excellence that The Rock and the people here tonight can only dream of achieving. Kurt says that reminds him of a story of the Olympic games, and The Rock holds his hand up, and Kurt stops talking!

    The Rock motions his hand saying to just bring it, and Kurt questions The Rock, and then he says that he can bring it, and he's been bringing it since 1982! The Rock gives Kurt a number of right hands, and then Stephanie and Triple H are shown. Stephanie says that they should help him, and Triple H says that he doesn't want to help him. Stephanie gets up and leaves and goes to help Kurt Angle, and it looks like they are not on the best of terms. Triple H gets up and goes after Stephanie as the two go at it in the ring. Kurt Angle gives The rock a number of right hands in the corner, and then The rock whips Kurt to the corner, and Kurt gives The Rock an elbow. Kurt runs at The Rock, and The Rock catches him in a power slam.

    Stephanie and Triple H make their way to the ringside area, and The Rock notices them and that distracts him. Kurt angle gives The Rock a number of right hands, and then The Rock fires back with a clothesline. Kurt whips The Rock off the ropes, and Stephanie grabs The Rock's leg. Kurt Angle is able to get a belly to belly on The Rock and then he pins him for a two count. Kurt Angle chokes The Rock over the second rope, and then behind the back of the referee, Triple H hits The Rock a couple of times. The Rock gives Kurt Angle a number of right hands, and then Kurt picks The Rock up and gives him a back suplex. Kurt pins The Rock for a two count and then Kurt gives The Rock a number of shots to the back of the head.

    The Rock gets in the corner, and Kurt Angle kicks at The Rock and chokes him with his foot. Kurt gives The Rock a couple of right hands, and The Rock is able to fight back with some right hands of his own. Kurt Angle goes off the ropes, and The Rock tosses Kurt to the outside. Stephanie distracts the referee, and Triple H goes to hit The Rock, but The Rock blocks it and nails Triple H. Stephanie nails The Rock with the women's belt, and The Rock goes after her, but Kurt Angle nails The Rock from behind. Kurt rolls The Rock back into the ring and then The Rock gives Kurt Angle the Rock Bottom! Both men are down in the center of the ring as The Rock is unable to capitalize.

    The Rock gets up and gives Kurt a number of right hands, and then he gives Kurt a tilt a whirl power slam. Triple H gets on the apron and The Rock whips Kurt into him and knocks him off the apron. The Rock gives Kurt Angle a DDT, and Shane and Bull Buchanan come out and The Rock takes care of them. The Rock gives Kurt Angle a spine buster, followed by the people's elbow to, and he goes to pin him for a three count, but Triple H interrupts the pin. Bull and Triple H stomp away on The Rock in the middle of the ring and Kurt Angle joins in as well. Kane's music hits, and he is on his way to the ring to save The Rock and he goes to work on everyone with some right hands, and Chris Benoit makes his way to the ring. Chris Benoit goes to work on Kane, and he nails him in the knee to slow him down. Kane picks Chris Benoit up and he gives him a choke slam, and then all hell continues to break loose in the ring as Smackdown! goes off the air!

    Winner as a result of a disqualification, The Rock
