    • Top 4 Wrestling Video Game Sales Update
    By Nick Evanish on June 15, 2000

    The following is from Game Week Magazine

    The article is an update on the status of the top wrestling games on the market today!

    • WWF SMACKDOWN from THQ is the best selling videogame in 2000 for the Playstation. It's fourth best across all console platforms and 8th in the Top 30 interactive software titles.
    • WRESTLEMANIA 2000 for N64 from THQ sold well over 1 million copies in 6 months.
    • WCW MAYHEM for N64 from Electronic Arts sold between 600,000 - 700,000 in 8 months.
    • ECW HARDCORE REVOLUTION for Playstation and N64 sold aprox. 130,000 - 140,000 total.
