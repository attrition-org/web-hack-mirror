    WWF Raw Results From LA

    Opening Segment
    - Shane McMahon made his way to the ring to announce that he is challenging The Rock tonight. Stephanie and Triple H were next to come out. Stephanie complained about her injuries she suffered thanks to The Rock. Triple H challenges the Rock in a non-title match. Next the owner of the WWF, Vince McMahon, paraded out to ringside. McMahon shook hands with his family members. Vince announced that he too would like to fight The Rock tonight.

    - Backstage a live shot is shown of The Rock entering the building is shown.

    Eddy Guerrero vs. Y2J
    - Chris Jericho is joined by Chyna at ringside. Before the match Eddy continues his use of his phrase "Latino Heat!" Chyna is not pleased and is ready for Y2J to crush him. Eddy glances at Chyna throughout the match. Jericho executes two powerbombs and hit a rope moonsault. Jericho can not pin him because the ref is knocked out. Chyna jumped in the ring and counted 3. She raised Jericho's hand and then kicked him right in the mid-section! She then threw Eddy on top of Jericho. When the ref awoke, he counted 3 and Eddy is the new European Champion!
    Winner: Eddy Guerrero

    - Backstage Stephanie has a sit down talk with her father, Vince. Stephanie says it is only right that Triple H gets to fight The Rock tonight. After another minute of sweet talking Vince agrees to think about it.

    X-Pac and Road Dogg vs. T&A
    - Before the match the Road Dogg says Trish has a butt like Rikishi Phatu! X-Pac starts out the match against Prince Albert. Soon he tags in the Road Dogg and he plants Albert with punches. Albert fought back with a scissor kick. Test is tagged in and clotheslined Road Dogg. Test missed a face kick and got caught up on the top rope. X-Pac kicked him back into the ring. The Road Dogg used his patented Shake Raddlin Knee Drop. Test tags in Albert, as Test is worn down. Albert walks in to get hit with the X-Factor from X-Pac, and then pinned by Road Dogg.
    Winners: X-Pac and Road Dogg

    - Backstage Angle is asking Howard Finkel a few questions about last night. Fink replied by telling Angle he wasn't a champion. Angle walks away calmly and then stormed back and executed a choke-out hold on him.

    Tazz vs. Chris Benoit
    - A slow start to this match, as Tazz and Benoit fight back and forth. Later in the match, Benoit applied a SharpShooter on Tazz. Tazz refused to tap out and the match continued. Benoit lashes out and threw punches at Tazz's head. Tazz slipped behind him and applied the Tazzmission. Saturn rushed to the ring to save Benoit. Tazz let go of the hold and suplexed Saturn off the top rope. Benoit then sneaks behind Tazz and executed a German suplex, giving him the win.
    Winner: Chris Benoit

    - Backstage Big Show and Shane meet up and talk about last night. Big Show told Shane that if he ever needed him, he would back him up. Big Show then mentions he is going to go cut loose on Hollywood!

    Edge and Christian Interview
    - Michael Cole brings down the new Tag Team Champions. Once they get to the ring, they dismiss Cole and tell them they can handle it by themselves. Edge and Christian go on to gloat about their victory. After they're done, they welcome the Hardy Boyz out. The Hardy Boyz limp out to the ring as Edge and Christian continue to gloat. The Dudleys come out and attack Edge and Christian. Christian felt 3D before leaving the ring. A fight between the Hardys and Dudleys break out, which is broken up by officials.

    Big Show vs. Rikishi
    - Rikishi is followed by Too Cool to the ring. Big Show starts out with an overwhelming clothesline. After that he does the moonwalk and the worm! Rikishi plants his butt into his face and Too Cool knocks over Big Show from the outside. After the match Rikishi and the boys get down.
    Winner: Big Show by DQ

    - Backstage Triple H and the McMahon's draw straws to see who will face The Rock. Shane wins!

    Big Show Interview
    - Big Show explains to the fans that he often likes to dance. Too Cool's music plays and he began dancing like a fool in the ring! Very funny!

    - Backstage The Rock promises the fans that he will be WWF Champion again. He tells Vince McMahon that he made a very bad decision and will pay greatly for it.

    Crash vs. Hardcore Holly
    - The Acolytes interfere in this match early on and attack Hardcore Holly. After beating the living daylights out of Hardcore, they lay Crash on top. Crash pins Hardcore Holly to become the new Hardcore Champion. After the match he celebrates with the Acolytes.
    Winner: Crash

    - Big Show catches up with GrandMaster Sexay backstage and splashes him through a table after Sexay insults his dancing.

    Kurt Angle vs. Val Venis
    - Kurt Angle walked to the ring with a pair of rubber gloves, telling the fans he is going to wear the gloves while wrestling a former adult video star. Venis got mad and attack Angle. Angle soon switched things around and applied a chokehold on Val on the mat. Val tapped out and Angle had yet another win to celebrate.
    Winner: Kurt Angle

    Kane vs. Bull Buchanon
    - Bossman joined bull at ringside for this match. This match didn't last very long at all. Kane chokeslammed Bull right in the middle of the ring. He then pinned him before Bossman could interfere. After the match Bossman and Bull handcuffed Kane to the turnbuckle.
    Winner: Kane

    Shane McMahon vs. The Rock
    - Shane enters the ring area sporting a Rock T-Shirt. Vince McMahon walked to the ring to meet up with his son, Shane. Rock storms the ring and ripped off Vince's jacket and cussed at him. Shane tried to escape, The Rock chased him up the ramp and grabbed him. Triple H comes out and begins to beat on The Rock. Shane attemtps to pin The Rock, he kicks out. Vince McMahon decides to clothesline The Rock over the announce table. Back in the ring, Shane continues to punish the Rock. Finally the Rock slammed him, but get pedigreed by Triple H. Triple H tries to wake up the ref, The Rock kicked out and the fans go crazy. Vince got in the ring and got slapped by the Rock. Triple H hit The Rock from behind and kicked him in the corner. Triple H shoved the ref down and continued beating The Rock. The Rock, rockbottoms Triple H and pinned him!
    Winner: The Rock!
