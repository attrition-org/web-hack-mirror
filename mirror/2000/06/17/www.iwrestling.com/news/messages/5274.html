    By Robert Pasbani on June 15, 2000

    WWF Smackdown Report for 6/15/00
    Taped from Chicago, IL
    Report by WrestleWomen.net

    Edge with Christian came out to take on Grandmaster Sexay in a KOTR Qualifying Match. This was a very slow paced match. In the end, Sexay went on the top rope and was about to do the hip hop drop. Chrisitian got on the apron but Scotty got him off and they brawled outside. The ref was distracted by this. Sexay then completed the hip hop drop but Shane ran in and hit Sexay with a chair. Edge then got the victory.

    Backstage, Vince McMahon said stage one of their plan is complete.

    The McMahon-Helmsly Regime came out and cut their patented long promos. They say Kane, Rock, and UT all have surprises tonight.

    Footage is shown of Pat Patterson and Gerald Briso looking for Crash Holly in NYC.

    T and A with Trish Stratus took on The Hardy Boys. This was a great match and it went back and forth. In the end, Jeff Hardy went for a senton bomb but Trish tripped him and T and A got the win. Then, Lita came in and attacked Trish which included a legdrop from the top rope.

    Backstage, UT arrived and Vince McMahon, Bull Buchanon and local police officers greeted him. Vince said that because UT stole a car on RAW, that person filed charges and UT is arrested.

    Outside, Vince is shown leaving the arena.

    Scotty Too Hotty faced D-Lo Brown in a KOTR Qualifying Match. The was another slow paced match. In the end, Scotty won with a DDT to qualify.

    The Dudleys took on Edge and Christian. Edge and Christian were fighting the Dudleys as payback to Shane McMahon for helping Edge qualify for the KOTR tourney. This was a great match with many chants of table during the match. In the end, the match was thrown out and both teams were fighting up the stage. Buh Buh saw a table on the floor and picked up Christian and was about to powerbomb him but DX come out and attack the Dudleys. They put the Dudleys in a dumpster and threw them off the stage...great spot!

    Bull Buchanon and Chris Jericho wrestled and Stephanie McMahon did comentary. This was a fast paced match. In the end Stephanie threw water at Chris, which distracted him so that Bull could get his leg drop off for the win. Then Triple H came in and attacked Jericho after the match.

    Val Venis came out and challenged Rikishi. Rikishi came out and the match went on. With all the weapons used it was like a hardcore match. There was monitors and chairs used and the annoucers were left without a table. The match ended abruptly with a commercial break.

    Brisco and Patterson run into Sameul L. Jackson and Jackson shoves Patterson down.

    The Rock comes out to take on his mystery opponent who is Kurt Angle.This was a decent match. In the end, the Rock wins with the People's Elbow. Afterwards, Triple H gets involved. Kane comes out and DX follows. Then, as DX thinks they are done, UT's music hits and he kills DX.
