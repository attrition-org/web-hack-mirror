    By Kevin Gregg on June 14, 2000

    Divas Interview with Chyna: She will be on the WWF Magazine!

    Chyna will be on the cover of a future WWF Magazine, and WWFDivas.com had the chance to talk to Chyna. Here is what she had to say:

    CHYNA TALKS ABOUT HER CHARACTER
    Chyna says that for four years, people have always seen Chyna as being a body guard, and she never considered that one dimensional, because she did so much and broke so many grounds. It's intriguing for people to see that she portrays such a hard image. She wears make up all of the time, and people forget that she has to put it on. When she goes out to dinner on a personal side, she wears make up, dresses and anything to make her look good. That's a lot more of Joanie, and people always want to know more about what is behind the character. From a personal stand point, it's extremely rewarding, because for all of the hard work that she has done, the ultimate in femininity for her is to be able to carry muscle, be smart, athletic, pretty and physically attractive. That is what they are trying to portray, and she says that it's sometimes hard to portray that to today's society, and it's great to get out there today and portray that image.

    CHYNA DISCUSSES THE SHOOT
    Chyna says that they were shooting a cover shot for the WWF magazine, and what they were trying to do is present one of the many sides of Chyna. It just happened to be one of her softer sides. She says that she didn't want to do the in your face, T&A type of shot. That is not very Chyna, and there is a way to display a lot of softness of her with feathers and jewelry.

    CHYNA DESCRIBES HER LOOK
    She says that she thinks they achieved the effect of the make up, and it wound up being a girlie effect. Every girl likes to play with a boa, and it's just a very early item. They tried to match the make up with that, soft pink lips, and more of a natural eye type. It's to show a little bit more of Joanie as well as Chyna. Her look is a lot different when she goes to the ring, and she's much harder edged. It allows her to show a softer side, and they do it through color, hair style and accessories.

    CHYNA AT THE SHOOT
    Chyna says she thinks that she is one of the very few people who have been able to show both sides. For a long time, she was only able to show one side, and it's not a side that was looked at the way it is now. She can show strength, have some muscle, and she can be smart, dress up and be pretty. She can have fun with it, smile, and laugh and whack someone when she's done!

    COVER GIRL
    This is one of the easier photo shoots that she has done, simply because it was a lot more of Joanie. She says that she felt very relaxed, and it was fun. It's fun to be dressed up like a barbie doll, and that's where they were going, with the Chyna Doll. They dressed her up in feathers, and she was sweet as ice cream, and just as cold.

    A DIFFERENT SIDE OF CHYNA
    She worked four different wraps, and one had sort of an eye lash curl to it, and to her, it was very glamorous. Her favorite was probably the traditional feather boa, with all of the feathers popping out. Chyna says that she had a real hairy like boa that was very soft. They all gave a tad bit of a different effect, and a different look. She had a ton of jewelry and it was a lot of fun to wear.

    THE NINTH WONDER OF THE WORLD
    Her favorite look isn't Chyna, because she is very proud of the way Joanie looks. She is a confident woman, and she wears her make up, soft colors as usual. Most of the time, she is casually dressed. She wears a lot of work out outfits, and she's happy the way she looks. It's fun to be able to embellish herself into Chyna, and a glam type of look. Her favorite look is herself.
