    • ECW Living Dangerously Results and 200 Images
    By Robert Pasbani on March 12, 19100 at 22:20:00

    ECW Living Dangerously 2000
    Live from the O'niel Center in Danbury, CT
    Report by Wrestling Arena

    - Steve Corino comes down and runs down the Dusty Rhodes and then notices the wife of the Sandman at rinside. He begins to speak about how she put over more wrestlers than Dusty Rhodes has had matches. She slapped Corino and he dragged her into the ring where Rhino speared her through a table. Sandman came down to save her as Corino called down Dusty for the match.

    - Dusty Rhodes vs. Steve Corino
    Dusty RHodes takes control of the match quickly as he drags Corino around the ring area. Special referee is HC Loc. Dusty quickly drags Corino to the outside of the ring and down the isleway. He keeps nailing Corino with the cowbell as Corino blades himself early. They brawl into the crowd for a few minutes as Corino takes control of the match. Dusty blades as Corino worked over the forehead of Rhodes. They brawl around the arena and soon get back into the ring as Rhodes and Corino brawl in the ring. Jack Victory begins to interfere as Corino places a chair into the corner but Rhodes tosses him into it. Rhodes then drops the elbow but Corino begins to make a comeback with Dusty style punches and elbows. Jack Victory grabs Rhodes who gives him the elbow as Steve Corino gets a second bell. HC ties the bell onto the head of Corino and Dusty nials him with the chair. Dusty Rhodes then drops the elbow to get the pin on Steve Corino.

    - Dangerous Alliance vs. Danny Doring/Roadkill
    Lou E. Dangerously starts by running down the crowd. He then says that he has a announcement to make and that he is the hottest entity in wrestling. Danny Doring and Roadkill then made their way down to the ring with Electra. The match starts with Roadkill and CW Anderson. Anderson takes first control but Roadkill fights back with the Dirt Road Slam for a two count. And then drops a second rope elbow on CW. Anderson tags in Wiles and Roadkill tags in Doring who tags control with a hip drag but Wiles counters with a arm drag and arm bar. He tags in CW as they go for a double clothesline but Roadkill attacks them from behind. Roadkill then nails the Amish Splash on CW. They toss both members of the Alliance outside the ring and Doring nails a pescado on the two. They go in the ring as Wiles gets a slam on Doring and takes control. Anderson tags in and give Doring a chicken wing slam. Anderson chops Doring in the corner but Doring fight back with chops of his own but Anderson keeps him in the corner of the Dangerous Alliance. Wiles tags in and continues the assault on Doring with different offensive moves and tags back CW who works over the arm of Doring. Doring fights back with the G-Spot Sweet and get the tag for Roadkill who takes out both members of the Dangerous Alliance. They pai off as Doring fights Wiles and Roadkill with Anderson. The match breaks down at this point with everyone fighting. Doring and Roadkill begin to double team Wiles and nails a top rope legdrop and Roadkill was about to frog splash him when Electra trips him. CW gives a spinebuster ot Roadkill as the Dangerous Alliance gets the win. Electra then leaves with the Dangerous Alliance.

    - Simon Diamond vs. Kid Cash
    Simon Diamond starts off saying that he has a problem then Jeff Jones come out and says that he does a problem. Then Mike Awesome came out and told everyone to leave the ring or get killed. Kid Kash then attacks Awesome who destroys Cash with a fall away slam, Awesome Splash. He then took him outside the ring where he tossed him into a guardrail twice. Cash tossed him over the guardrail out of desperation and then springboard plancha into the crowd onto Awesome. Cash then gave Jones a spike DDT in the ring. Awesome got back into the ring and dominated Cash. He grabbed a table place Cash on the table. Cash got off and went to punch Awesome but Awesome grabbed him and powerbombed him off the top like he did to Spike at the last ppv for the pin.

    - Gedo/Jado vs. Chetti/Nova
    Jazz after the match between Awesome/Cash came down to help Cash but Diamond came back with Jado/Gedo and attacked her. Nova/Chetti then made their way out and started the tag match. Complete back and forth action with a mixture of power moves and submission style wrestling. Gedo and Jado held the advantage for the most part with them having control over Nova as they kept him in their corner. Nova after a few minutes was able to make the hot tag to Chetti who fought off Gedo and Jado. Nova got back into the match. They tossed Chetti out of the ring and gave Nova a superbomb. They only got a two count as Nova made the save and then Nova/Chetti nailed the tital wave on Gedo for the pinfall victory.

    - Little Guido vs. Super Crazy
    Match starts out quickly with Guido taking the first advantage with a low blow. He tossed in Crazy to the corner. Crazy fights back and tossed Guido to the floor and then into the crowd. Graziano went to splash Crazy who moved and went into the crowd. He then did a springboard moonsault onto the two in the crowd. Guido and Crazy go back in the ring and trade control. And then went back to the outside of the ring. He placed Crazy into a fujiwar armbar. Then Sal attacked Crazy and tossed him back into the ring as Guido controlled the match. Guido went to use a chair on Crazy in the corner but it bounced back in his face as Crazy gave him a chair shot and then a second off the second rope but was only able to get a two count as Guido blades. Crazy then gives him 10 punches in the corner and does the same to Sal. Crazy then stood on the shoulders of Sal for a splash but Guido got his legs up. Guido then whipped the back of Crazy with Sals belt. He went for a pin but only got a two count. Crazy comes back with a tornado DDT and grabs the belt but Guido came up from behind. He drops a rocker dropper from the second on Crazy for a two count. Sal brings in a table and Guido places Crazy on a table. Guido stands on the shoulders of Sal but missed a elbow drop. Crazy then gives a hurricurana. Crazy drives Guido through a table but only gets a two count and then goes for a brain buster on Guido which he nails for the pinfall victory.

    - Kintaro Kanemura vs. Ballz Mahoney
    Kanemura starts with control of the match with kicks and then nails a senton from the top rope for a two count. Mahoney fought back with a spinebuster and then nailed a frog splash for a two count. Kanemura grabbed a chair and nailed him with a hard chair shot which Mahoney no sold. Mahoney then gave Kanemura the nutcracker sweet for the pin.

    - The Baldies then came out to attack Mahoney. They placed him on a table on the outside and Grimes did a summersault plancha onto Mahoney in a huge bump. New Jack then came down and attacked the Baldies. New Jack destroyed the Baldies with a trashcan full of weapons. Weapons were a crutch, Playstation, guitar and much more. Grimes then came in and attacked New Jack from behind with a pizza cutter. New Jack fought back and used the pizza cutter on Grimes and a snow shovel. Grimes battled back as they went to the outside and into the crowd. They went over into the concession area. At this point it was hard to follow the action because of the amount of people in the area as they rushed it so they cut a long shot for a few seconds. Grimes grabbed a table when they got back close and was attacking New Jack and placed him on a table as New Jack was busted open. Grimes then grabbed a second table and placed it on top of New Jack. Grimes climbed to some scaffolding. New Jack got off the table and climbed up the scaffold that looked to the 10 to 12 feet over the ground. At this point it was slow as both gained their balance on the scaffold. Grimes was about to powerbomb New Jack when he gave Grimes a low blow. Then New Jack and Grimes fell to the ground but Grimes missed the table completely in a very scary looking bump. Grimes though was able to kept up with help from security as was New Jack. They ended this as Grimes was carried to the back. He kept motioning to the crowd that he was all right. After the Grime/New Jack bump they cut to Styles/Cyrus as they spoke for a few minutes. Its not clear wether either New Jack or Grimes was seriously injured.

    - Tag Team Titles: Impact Players vs. Awesome/Raven vs. Dreamer/Tanaka
    Raven/Awesome vs. Impac Players began first with Awesome doing a huge plancha onto the Impact Players. Then Dreamer and Tanaka came down. Dreamer nailed a DDT on Raven quickly but Awesome made the save. The match was a 6 man brawl with fighting everywhere. Dreamer and Raven for the most part have been paired off as they really sold the Dreamer/Raven feuds from 95 to 97. Really hard bumps with tables and chairs as camera continually cut from one fight to another. In the ring Raven sent Dreamer through a table like the bump on TNN this week. Then Masato Tanaka did a release German suplex through the table on Awesome and then did his roaring elbow finish for the pin as Raven/Awesome were quickly eliminated. The match went into a traditional tag match with Players vs. Dreamer/Tanaka. Impact Players took first control as Dreamer was down. Dreamer was busted open as the Impact Players kept him the corner. Dreamer fought back after Credible superkicked Storm by mistake. He made the hot tag to Tanaka who destroyed Storm with a ton of attacks. Dreamer was able to join in as they controlled the Impact Players with a variet of tandem moves. Dreamer was able to nail the Spicolli Driver but Credible nialed him with a Singapore Cane and Storm gave him a reverse DDT. Tanaka nailed his Diamond Dust finisher on Credible but couldn't get the pin. Impact Players then gave Dreamer a spike piledriver for the pinfall victory.

    - Rhino vs. Super Crazy
    Cyrus came down to say how happy he was that Rhino would soon be the new tv champion. He ran down ECW till Paul Heyman came out with and cut a swearing laced promo. Gertner then came down and gave a similar promo saying that TNN from tonight on was going down. Match starts out with Crazy taking control with speed but Rhino slowed him and took the match to the floor where Rhino used powermoves. He brought Crazy back into the ring and continued to hold the advantage. Super Crazy was able to get control of the match when Rhino missed a spear and went through a table. Crazy then went for his three moonsaults but Rhino pulled the ref onto him. Tajiri then came down and spit mist into the eyes of Crazy. Then RVD's music played as RVD, Alphonso and Riggs all came down to help. RVD and Rhino fought and RVD was able to nail him with his crutch. Super Crazy was then able to get the pinfall victory to become the new ECW TV champ. The heels after the pin dominated and destroyed all the face. Then Enter Sandman began to play as Sandman came out through the crowd and drove away the heels as the faces celebrated in the ring.
