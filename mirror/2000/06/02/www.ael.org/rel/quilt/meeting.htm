June 18-23, 2000
Ramada Inn and Convention Center • Lexington, Kentucky

The QUILT training-for-trainers approach helps school districts prepare local teachers who then train others in their region. AEL staff have instructed more than 650 trainers who have gone on to present the material to more than 4,500 teachers. (Click here for comments on training.) Mini-grants are available to support Kentucky elementary, middle, and high schools.
