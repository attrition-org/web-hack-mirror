The ITI@ael archives

AEL, Inc., archives digests (collections) of all the messages sent to iti@ael.org since October 1995. You can search the archives by entering a keyword--a name, word, or phrase--from the message you are trying to retrieve. It might take a little digging and clever use of key words to winnow down the right message, but if it was sent to iti@ael.org, it's in the archives. There are two ways to get to the searchable archives:

  1. Go to http://www.ael.org/rel/iti/itilist.htm and click on "searchable archive."

  2. Go directly to http://aelliot.ael.org/~burnsb/
