Join AEL's Interdisciplinary Teamed Instruction (ITI) Discussion List

We operate a listserv for educators who are exploring interdisciplinary teamed instruction. The ITI listserv provides a moderated, electronic forum for sharing questions, insights, experiences, and resources related to ITI.

The ITI listserv offers a searchable archive in digest format, which contains all messages sent to the discussion group since October 1995. Enter keywords or phrases to identify specific digests that contain information of interest to you.

Although you must be a listserv subscriber to participate in the discussion, everyone can use the archive and links.

To subscribe to the list, send an e-mail message to the Majordomo List Manager at this address:

   majordomo@ael.org

with the following as the body of the message

   subscribe iti your_email@somewhere.com

To unsubscribe from the list, send e-mail to the Majordomo List Manager (address above) with the following message

   unsubscribe iti your_email@somewhere.com

Note: Subscription requests MUST be in the body of the message, not in the subject line. If you try to subscribe/unsubscribe otherwise, the request will be ignored.
