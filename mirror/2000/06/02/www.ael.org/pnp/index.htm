About the Catalog

AEL publishes a variety of products on education topics that provide R&D-based information to educators, policy makers, parents, community members, and others. The products result from the work of the Regional Educational Laboratory, the Eisenhower Regional Consortium for Mathematics and Science Education, the ERIC Clearinghouse on Rural Education and Small Schools, the Region IV Comprehensive Technical Assistance Center, and the Regional Technology in Education Consortium.

Products can be accessed by:

  • a key-word search,
  • browsing the product list, organized alphabetically by publication date, or
  • browsing a subject index.

Full-text versions of some products are available on-line and may be downloaded.
