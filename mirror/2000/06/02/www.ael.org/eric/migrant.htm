Brief Articles for Parents
We now have two sets of six articles each (English only or Spanish and English). They are ready to scan or paste into your newsletter, newspaper, or periodical. Clearly and briefly, each article addresses a child development or education topic of concern to parents.

ERIC Digests are brief (1,500-word) summaries of the education literature on specific topics, including reference lists for readers who want to know more. Digests are informative, easy to read, and free (you can download them here or order printed copies by contacting the clearinghouse). Our current list of ERIC Digests related to migrant education is shown below:

  • Assessing LEP Migrant Students for Special Education Services, by José R. Lozano and Jaime A. Castellano (1999), EDO-RC-98-10

  • Child Labor in Agriculture, by Shelley Davis (1997), EDO-RC-96-10

  • Migrant Students Attending College: Facilitating Their Success, by Susan Morse and Patricia Cahape Hammer (1998) EDO-RC-98-10

  • Responding to Undocumented Children in the Schools, by Susan C. Morse and Frank S. Ludovina (1999), EDO-RC-99-1

  • Unschooled Migrant Youth: Characteristics and Strategies to Serve Them, by Susan C. Morse (1997), EDO-RC-97-2
