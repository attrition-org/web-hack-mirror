ERIC Digests are brief (1,500-word) summaries of the education literature on specific topics, including reference lists for readers who want to know more. Digests are informative, easy to read, and free (you can download them here or order printed copies by contacting the clearinghouse). Our current list of ERIC Digests related to small schools is shown below:

  • Affective and Social Benefits of Small-Scale Schooling, by Kathleen Cotton (1996), EDO-RC-96-5

  • Current Literature on Small Schools, by Mary Anne Raywid (1999), EDO-RC-98-8

  • Curriculum Adequacy and Quality in High Schools Enrolling Fewer Than 400 Pupils (9-12), by Christopher Roellke (1996), EDO-RC-96-7

  • Downsizing Schools in Big Cities by Mary Anne Raywid (1996), EDO-UD-96-1

  • Ongoing Dilemmas of School Size: A Short Story, by Craig Howley (1996), EDO-RC-96-6

  • School Size, by Karen Irmsher (1997), EDO-EA-97-5

  • The School-within-a School Model, by Sarah Dewees (1999), EDO-RC-99-2
