Our Newsletter
Subscribe to the ERIC/CRESS Bulletin! The Bulletin announces new books, Digests, and other resources related to our specialties published by us and others from across the nation. Published only three times each year, it won't clutter up your mailbox and it's free. ERIC Digests
Digests are brief (1,500-word) summaries of the education literature on a specific topic, including a reference list of about 10 good sources for more in-depth information. Digests are informative, easy to read, and free. All in-print Digests published by this ERIC Clearinghouse are available by subscribing to the ERIC/CRESS Bulletin. Digests are also ready to view, print, or download from this site. Browse our Digests in the following areas of specialization.

American Indian & Alaska Native education

Mexican American education

Migrant education

Outdoor and Experiential education

Rural education

Small schools




Articles for Parents
We now have two sets of six articles each (English only or Spanish and English). They are ready to scan or paste into your newsletter, newspaper, or periodical. Clearly and briefly, each article addresses a child development or education topic of concern to parents. They are available free, in print (limit one print copy of each set per order) or by downloading them here.
