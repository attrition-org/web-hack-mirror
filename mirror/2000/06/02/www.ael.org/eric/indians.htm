ERIC Digests are brief (1,500-word) summaries of the education literature on specific topics, including reference lists for readers who want to know more. Digests are informative, easy to read, and free (you can download them here or order printed copies by contacting the clearinghouse). Our current list of ERIC Digests related to American Indian and Alaska Native education is shown below:

  • Comprehensive Planning: Guidance for Educators of American
    Indian and Alaska Native Students    ,
    by ORBIS Associates (1996), EDO-RC-96-3

  • Countering Prejudice Against American Indians and Alaska Natives
    Through Antibias Curriculum and Instruction    ,
    by Deirdre A. Almeida (1996), EDO-RC-96-4

  • Drop-Out Rates Among American Indian and Alaska Native
    Students: Beyond Cultural Discontinuity    ,
    by Richard St. Germaine (1995), EDO-RC-96-1

  • Postsecondary Financial Aid for American Indians and Alaska Natives by Deirdre A. Almeida (December 1999), EDO-RC-99-3

  • Schools, Principals, and Teachers Serving American Indian and Alaska Native Students, by D. Michael Pavel (1999), EDO-RC-98-9

  • Teaching Young Children about Native Americans,
    by Debbie Reese (1996), EDO-PS-96-3

  • Teaching American Indian and Alaska Native Languages in the Schools: What Has Been Learned by Thomas D. Peacock & Donald R. Day (December 1999), EDO-RC-99-10
