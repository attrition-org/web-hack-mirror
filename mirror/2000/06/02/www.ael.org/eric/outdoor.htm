    ERIC Digests are brief (1,500-word) summaries of the education literature on specific topics, including reference lists for readers who want to know more. Digests are informative, easy to read, and free (you can download them here or order printed copies by contacting the clearinghouse). Our current list of ERIC Digests related to outdoor and experiential education is shown below:

    • Experiential Learning in Higher Education: Linking Classroom and Community, by Jeffrey A. Cantor (1997), EDO-HE-95-7

    • The Foxfire Approach to Teaching and Learning: John Dewey, Experiential Learning, and the Core Practices, by Bobby Ann Starnes (1999), EDO- RC-98-6

    • Multicultural Issues in Outdoor Education, by Nina S. Roberts and Donald A. Rodriguez (1999), EDO-RC-99-6

    • Outdoor Education and the Development of Civic Responsibility, by Judith A. Boss (1999), EDO-RC-98-5

    • Teaching Science in the Field, by Carol Landis (1996), EDO-SE-96-07
