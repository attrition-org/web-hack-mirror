Paperwork Burden Statement

According to the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995, no persons are required to respond to a collection of information in connection with a federally funded project unless such collection displays a valid OMB control number. The valid OMB control number for this information collection is 1880-0529. The time required to complete this information is estimated to average less than 10 minutes per response, including time to review questions, search existing data resources, gather the data needed, and complete and review the information collection. If you have comments concerning the accuracy of the time estimate or suggestions for improving this form, please write to: U.S. Department of Education, Washington, DC 20202-4651. If you have comments or concerns regarding the status of your individual submission of this form, write directly to: National Library of Education, U.S. Department of Education, 400 Maryland Avenue, S.W., Washington, DC 20202-5523.


PRIVACY STATEMENT We do not collect e-mail addresses of visitors to our Clearinghouse Web pages unless you voluntarily subscribe to one of our services or request information. Any additional information on what pages patrons access or visit and information volunteered by the patron is kept confidential. The information we collect is used for internal review and to improve the content of our Web pages and our service to patrons. It is discarded after use. Individuals' information is not shared with other commercial or governmental organizations. We will only contact you if you request it, if we have a question about your order, or to confirm your satisfaction with our service. If you supply us with your mailing address, you will receive the information for which you gave us your address. Information from survey participants is aggregated to prepare statistical reports for the U.S. Department of Education.
