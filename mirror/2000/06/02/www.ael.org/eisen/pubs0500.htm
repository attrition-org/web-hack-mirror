Focusing on Assessment

Assessment that Informs Practice is the theme of the latest issue of ENC Focus: A Magazine for Classroom Innovators, now available on-line at http://www.enc.org/focus/assessment. ENC Focus features educational news, editorials, essays, classroom stories, and various abstracts of materials from the ENC Collection.
