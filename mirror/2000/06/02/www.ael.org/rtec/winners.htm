AEL >> RTEC >> Teachers

In spring 1999 SEIR*TEC at AEL conducted a survey of software selection and use among teachers in Kentucky, Tennessee, Virginia, and West Virginia. A followup survey was conducted this spring. Results of the survey will be published in the fall.

Respondents were entered automatically in a contest to win a Palm Vx Organizer and a variety of software titles. The drawing was conducted on May 25. Prizes will be mailed in two to six weeks.
