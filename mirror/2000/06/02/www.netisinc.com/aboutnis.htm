About NIS, Inc.

Network Integration Services, Inc., is a premier networking service company in the Midwest. Our staff can provide expertise in local, regional, national & global networking. We have worked for government, small business, large regional corporations & Fortune 500 firms. With this experience we offer:
  • Networking Technical Contract Service
  • Network Installation
  • Network Engineering
  • Service Contract Maintenance / Custom Manufacturing
  • System Integration
  • Training, Sales & Service for Cisco & Telegenix Products

In our experience we have learned to conduct business based on some fundamental principals.

  • Do what is right.
  • Do your best.
  • Treat others as you would like to be treated.

It may sound corny, but these are things that we believe in. We think that the interests of our customers and our company are best served when these basic tenets are kept in mind.

Our staff of highly skilled networking professionals can handle any size job. We are interested in discussing your future network plans and requirements and look forward to the opportunity to serve you.
