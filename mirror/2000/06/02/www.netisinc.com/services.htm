About NIS Services

NIS is a full service provider of networking, telecommunications, and computer-related services. We specialize in the engineering, analysis, installation and ongoing operation of network infrastructure, servers and workstations, video conferencing, and telephony systems.

Following a brief description of our people, engineering, analysis, installation and operations capabilities, there is a listing of many of the categories of services that NIS can provide.

Our People:

At NIS we take pride in the quality of our people. All of our staff members have come from companies where they designed, installed, and managed large enterprise networks. Our senior staff members have over ten years experience in all aspects of computer, video, and telephony networks ranging from small departmental LANs to global enterprise networks. More importantly, they were the visionaries, the instigators, the leaders, the ones who were willing to try something new, the ones everybody turned to with questions, the ones who got it done. The best. They bring a wealth of experience, technical expertise, project management skills, and team leadership abilities. For you, that means confidence in knowing that your needs can be met, your way. There are no square pegs looking for round holes at NIS.

How is your documentation? If you’re like most companies, it’s the last thing to get done, if it gets done at all. There have usually been two or three changes since the last documentation update. And besides that, nobody likes to do it. As a result, most networks are woefully under-documented. NIS can take care of that for you along with staffing your help desk, remotely monitoring your network, isolating and restoring problems. Top
