Since 1980, Acme has been in the business of building software systems. The inital products, both of which were designed to solve complex optimization problems, were Priority Dispatcher (software that generates efficient transportation routes) and DOCS (software that creates equitable schedules for medical staff). Within a few years, the need to provide custom printing features for Platinum Accounting became our third product, Acme Forms Tool.

By 1995, we began providing client consulting services to implement financial and manufacturing software. That same year, Acme became an Internet Service Provider (ISP), providing Internet access to end-users and business.

Today, Acme offers a wide range consulting services, financial and manufacturing packages, network integration, online self-study courses, Internet access, web hosting, innovative design, and custom programming, as well as a list of ready-to-use Acme-developed applications.
