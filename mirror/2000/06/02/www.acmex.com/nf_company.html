Founded in 1980 in Cleveland, Ohio, Acme Express began as a software development company specializing in solving optimization problems. The resulting products were Priority Dispatcher (software designed to calculate efficient transportation routing) and DOCS (a program to track and maintain hospital staff scheduling). In a few years, the need to provide custom printing features to Platinum Accounting became Acme Forms Tool. So started the software development.

In the mid 1990's, the need to provide consulting services to clients in the implementation of financial and manufacturing software became another service offering. Shortly thereafter, Acme began providing Internet access to end-users and business.

Today, Acme offers a wide range consulting services, network integration, Internet access, web hosting, innovative design, and custom programming, as well as a list of ready-to-use applications.
