Visitation Areas and Rules

All visitors must park in the green parking spaces provided for visitors.   Handicapped parking is provided on the south side of the entrance to Alhambra.

Areas:

F-Area Inmates have both inside and outside visitation areas depending on their level of clearance.
Baker Unit All visitation is non-contact only and subject to space availability.
I-J-K Wards Visitation areas are outside, there is no inside visitation.
George Ward Visitation areas are outside, there is no inside visitation.

Rules:

Inclement Weather:

Visitation for all outside areas may be terminated due to inclement weather.   Units that have inside visitation will be utilized during inclement weather.

 

Recreation:

There are a few board games and playing cards for visitors who are interested.   Please take care of games.  You are responsible for the care of the equipment.

 

Vending Machines:

Vending machines are provided in the various visitation areas.  Do not kick, bang on or tip these machines if you lose your money or the item you wish to purchase.   Any money due to you must be obtained from the vendor.  Place all empty soda and trash in the proper receptacle.  Children must be accompanied by an adult when purchasing items from the vending machines.

 

Rest Rooms:

Rest rooms are provided in all visitation areas.  Children must be accompanied by an adult.

 

Tobacco and Tobacco Products:

Smoking is only allowed in authorized areas.  There is no smoking in any indoor area.  Tobacco and tobacco related products are not allowed in the Flamenco Mental Health Center Visitation area.  All Tobacco products must be left outside of the unit during visitation.

 

Dress Code:

All male/female visitors over the age of 8 years will comply with the dress code.   No blue denim pants (blue jeans or stone washed), shorts, skirts or jackets.

Shorts must be mid-thigh in length (no cut-off).
No blue chambray shirts
Clothing that looks like the inmates uniform will be prohibited.

Male: 

  1. Shirts will be worn at all times. 
  2. No muscle shirts, tank tops or bare midriff shirts will be allowed. 
  3. Sheer see-through or open netted clothing will not be worn.

Female:

  1. Tops of the clothing will be no lower than the underarm in the front and back. 
  2. No bare midriff or strapless tops, tube tops, halter tops, body suits or swim suits will be permitted.
  3. Skirts and dresses will be allowed, but no shorter than mid thigh when seated. 
  4. Slits in skirts and dresses will not extend above mid thigh when seated. 
  5. Sheer see-through or open netted clothing will not be worn.

Proper Identification: 

Identification will include the visitor's name, picture and date of birth.

Acceptable identification:

  1. Military ID Cards
  2. Passports
  3. Valid State Driver's License
  4. State or Federal Agency ID Cards
  5. Government Employee ID Cards
  6. Immigration and Naturalization Documentation Cards

Children:  Must have a copy of their birth certificate to enter the visitation area.  If the child is accompanied by someone other than the parent, a notarized letter from the parent authorizing the child to visit with the visitor.


Items Admissible to Visitation:

Personal ID

Prescription Medication in the original container (only quantify required during visitation)

Lighter (1)

Unopened pack of cigarettes (1)

Coinage totaling no more than $10.00 per visitor (no paper money is allowed)

 

Conduct:

All conduct by both inmates and visitors will be quiet, orderly and in good taste.

Physical contact within the limits of common decency will be allowed.

Kissing and embracing will be permitted only at the beginning and end of each visit and will not be prolonged.

All clothing will remain fastened, hands will remain outside the clothing at all times.

Prohibited Conduct:

Lying on the floor or ground, upon seats or tables, under tables or attempting to conceal the visitor and/or inmate from staff.

Sitting on a visitor's lap or holding the visitor on the inmate's lap, with the exception of a young child.

Sitting with ones legs entwined or overlapping with another persons legs.

Use of profanity, making loud noises, disturbing other visitors, creating a mess or otherwise being a nuisance in the visitation area.

Same sex kissing or embracing (with the exception of relatives or immediate family members).

Straddling bench seats.


Searches of Visitors:

All visitors will be required to clear a metal detector and/or a pat search by a staff member of the same gender.

Visitors refusing to be searched will be denied entrance.   In addition, visitation privileges may be suspended or permanently revoked.
