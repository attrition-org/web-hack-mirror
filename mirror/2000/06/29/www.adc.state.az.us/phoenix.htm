Work, Education, and Treatment Programs

Work Programs

Alhambra/ Flamenco: Skilled inmates are selected from the general population inmates throughout the state to perform maintenance on the facility and other functions such as Porters, Yard Mainenance (Flamenco area only).
Globe: DOA; City of Globe; Forestry; ADOT - weekend; Gila Co. Fairgrounds; Maintenance; Kitchen workers; Warehouse; Barber; Tailor; Recreation aide, Town of Miami; Landfill Crew.
ACW: Televerde; Arizona State Hospital Maintenance; ACI; DOA; Department of Health Services; SODEXHO, Community Services and Technical Innovation. 
Aspen/SPU Facility, building and equipment maintenance; porters, yard maintenance and dining hall/ kitchen sanitation crews.

 

Education Programs

Alhambra/ Flamenco: Mandatory Education. Special Education, Chapter 1 literacy (Mental Health Center), GED (Mental Health Center and F-Area)
Aspen/SPU: Mandatory Education; GED; Special Education; Chapter 1 Literacy
Globe: Mandatory Education; GED; Special Education; Chapter 1 Literacy; Rio Salado Vocational Programs; Parenting and Distance Learning
ACW: Mandatory Education; GED; Special Education; Chapter 1 Literacy; Rio Salado Vocational Programs; Parenting and Distance Learning

 

Treatment Programs
All units offer medical, dental and nursing care to meet the essential needs of inmates.

Alhambra/ Flamenco: Operates a forty bed licensed acute care mental health unit and a 125 bed licensed intermediate mental health unit. Group and Individual therapy are provided as part of an integrated daily living program. General medical and dental care.
Aspen/SPU: Operates as the transitional care unit for mental health inmates. Psychiatric assessment, staffing, group and individual therapy, work, recreation, and education are all part of each inmate's treatment plan. General medical and dental care.
Globe: Medical; Dental; Pharmacy; inmate required psychiatric evaluations  are sent to ASPC - Alhambra. 
ACW Medical and Dental.

Directions

To Alhambra:
Take I-10 east to the 24th Street exit. Go north at 24th St. to Van Buren St. Travel east on Van Buren approximately 200 yards. At the entrance, turn left. The prison is located east of the entrance.   Map to Alhambra

To Aspen/SPU:
Take I-10 east if traveling from Phoenix, or I-10 west if traveling from Tucson, proceeding via I-10 to the 24th Street exit. Turn north, traveling until you reach Van Buren Street. Continue north until the first driveway, which is approximately 200 yards. Turn right. The parking lot and prison will be on your right-hand side. Map to Aspen

To ACW:
Take I-10 east if traveling from Phoenix, or I-10 west if traveling from Tucson, proceeding via I-10 to the 24th Street exit. Take 24th Street to Van Buren and head east until you get to 32nd Street. At 32nd Street, there will be a stop light. Turn north/ left. Once you turn, you will need to turn left again into the parking lot, which is approximately 50 yards from the stop light. Map to ACW

To Globe:
If traveling from Phoenix:

Take I-10 east to the Globe/Mesa exit.  Follow this to State Route 60.  Take Route 60 to the city of Globe (passing through Miami and Claypool).   Remain on SR 60 until coming to a stop light (a Taco Bell and Star Mart will be on the right hand side).  Turn left at the light onto Junction 77 (to Show Low).   Take Junction 77, approximately 4 miles to the County Fairgrounds sign (right hand side).  Turn right at the Fairgrounds sign, and remain on the paved road.  Before coming to the big blue Gila County Fairgrounds building there will be a T-junction in the paved road.  Go to the right and follow this road to the facility.

  If traveling from Tucson:

Take Highway 77 to Globe.  At the stop sign turn left onto US Route 70.  At the first stop light (a Taco Bell and Star Mart will be on the left hand side).    Stay in the farthest right hand lane, which will turn into US Route 60/ Highway 77 (to Show Low). Take Junction 77, approximately 4 miles to the County Fairgrounds sign (right hand side).  Turn right at the Fairgrounds sign, and remain on the paved road.  Before coming to the big blue Gila County Fairgrounds building there will be a T-junction in the paved road.  Go to the right and follow this road to the facility.
