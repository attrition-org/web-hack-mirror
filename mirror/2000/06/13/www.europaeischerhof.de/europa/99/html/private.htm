It gives us very great pleasure to present our privately run hotel in Heidelberg, which has been in the family of the present owner-managers for three generations.

For 135 years now, DER EUROPÄISCHE HOF-HOTEL EUROPA has been a home from home for cultivated travelers in search of timeless traditional charm, both in atmosphere and service. The name of our establishment stands for all that is best in European hotel culture. It is this that sets us apart from the anonymity of the big hotel chains.

On the following pages we should like to acquaint you with a variety of offers and arrangements that are quite frankly designed to whet your appetite. We are out to convince you that a visit to Heidelberg is eminently worthwhile and that a stay at our hotel would be exactly what is needed to make that visit a truly unforgettable experience. That is why we are taking this superb new opportunity to give you an impression of what makes DER EUROPÄISCHE HOF so unique and of the people who ensure that it stays that way.
