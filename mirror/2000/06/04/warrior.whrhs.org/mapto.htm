From Route 78 Eastbound:

Take Route 78 to Exit 36, Warrenville Road, Bear Right off exit, go through first light and make first LEFT onto Mountain Avenue.

Take Mountain Avenue East to Stirling Road, Turn Right onto Stirling Road, Watchung Hills is second school on the Left.


From Route 78 Westbound:

Take Route 78 West to Hillcrest Road, Exit 40, Bear right off exit.

Turn Left at 4-way Stop onto Mountain Avenue. Proceed down Mountain Avenue to second light and turn LEFT onto Stirling Road. WHRHS is second school on the left.

From Route 22:

Take Watchung Exit onto Somerset Street heading North.

Take second exit off of Watchung Circle onto Stirling Road. Lake should be on your LEFT.

Follow Stirling Road to school on right, just past Rt. 78 overpass.
