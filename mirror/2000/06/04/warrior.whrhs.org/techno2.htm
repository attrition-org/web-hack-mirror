Technology at the Hills

These past three years has brought an explosion of new technologies at the Hills, from new research labs in the media center, science wing and math lab, Internet access, new courses in PC Technology, and wiring the buildings for data feeds to all classrooms. Many departments are addressing technology with different approaches. Whether it's Technology Education or Educational Technology, this page is designed to showcase some of this new technology used by teachers, departments, clubs and activities.  More information on our technological plans can be obtained by contacting Mr. Donald Momberg, our District Technology Coordinator.
