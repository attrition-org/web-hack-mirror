Delayed Opening and Emergency School Closing Information

Information about school closings and/or delayed openings are broadcast over the following radio and cable TV stations between 6:30-7:30AM
There will also be postings on the Main Web Page!

WCTC 1450AM New Brunswick
WKMB 1070 AM Stirling
WKXW 101.5 FM Trenton
NEWS 12 Channel 12 Edison
Chat Radio 1040 AM Flemington
New Station 1610 AM Watchung

Watchung Hills Regional High School is a separate district from Warren, Watchung, Long Hill and Green Brook schools. The school closings may not be uniform.  Do not call the local police.  Call the high school at (908)647-4800.

School will begin at 9:15 A.M. when a delayed opening is announced and the school schedule will be adjusted accordingly.

Busses will roll and students will be picked up one hour & 30 minutes later than their regular pickup times.

If a delayed opening is called and then the weather worsens, school may be cancelled for the day. Again, these changes will be broadcast before 8:00 A.M. on the above radio and cable TV stations.
