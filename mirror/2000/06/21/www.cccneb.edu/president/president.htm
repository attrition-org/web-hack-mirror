Welcome to Central Community College!


We are pleased that you are interested in and have taken the time to look at our web site.  Central Community College has two major areas of emphasis - students and community.  Students are our number one priority.  Central has developed innovative instructional delivery systems to make instruction convenient for the student.  Individualized instruction allows students to begin classes any day the College is in operation.  The community education system provides credit and non-creditcourses at some 90 locations throughout the service area.   The college has programs and services that address nearly any request you may have.  Give us a chance to serve you.     (JWP)
