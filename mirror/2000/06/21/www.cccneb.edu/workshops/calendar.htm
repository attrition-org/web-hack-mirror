Welcome to the CCC Public Relations calendar. Here you will find a listing of workshops, short-term training programs, conferences and special activities that are being offered by the college throughout its 25-county service area in central Nebraska. It also includes training offered throughout the state by the Nebraska Environmental Training Center. To find out more about what's happening during a particular month, click on the appropriate link. Please note that although all events are listed by their starting dates, some workshops and conferences have earlier enrollment deadlines.
