MAUI - According to Maori legend the North Island of New Zealand is actually the world's largest fish.

Hooked by Maui, a legendary Maori hero, during an expedition to prove his fishing skills.

With the Wellington area being the head,
East Cape the fins and Northland the tail
or - Te Hiku o Te Ika.
