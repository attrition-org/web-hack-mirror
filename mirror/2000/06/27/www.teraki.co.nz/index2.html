TE RAKI, in the Maori language, means 'The North'.

And Northland is one of the most beautiful areas of New Zealand.

From the golden sand of the east coast beaches, north to - the(almost) northern most point of the country - where the Tasman Sea meets the Pacific Ocean, down the rugged west coast with its Ninety Mile beach and breathtaking Kauri forests.

Northland truly earns its name as one of New Zealand's lifestyle capitals.
