Like the Internet, the petroleum industry is in a dynamic state. Events change rapidly and one must continuously stay abreast of developments or risk being left behind. I hope that you will use OPEC Online to keep track of the issues and events which shape this industry; and to build a deeper knowledge and understanding of OPEC and its Member Countries.

OPEC plays a vital role in the world economy and, as you will see from the figures in these Web pages, that role continues to grow.

OPEC remains committed to the principles upon which it was formed in 1960, including fair and stable prices for petroleum producers; an efficient, economic and regular supply of petroleum to consuming nations; and a fair return on capital to those investing in the industry.

Happy browsing.

R.Lukman (signed)

HE Dr. Rilwanu Lukman
OPEC Secretary General
