OPEC is an Organization of eleven oil producing and exporting countries, from Africa (Algeria, the Socialist People's Libyan Arab Jamahiriya and Nigeria); Asia (Indonesia); the Middle East (the Islamic Republic of Iran, Iraq, Kuwait, Qatar, Saudi Arabia and the United Arab Emirates); and Latin America (Venezuela).

Click on the map and see details about each Member Country, including their petroleum reserves and production capacity. You may also view the history of OPEC, which discusses the Founding Member Countries. There are also lists of the  Ministers (Heads of Delegation), the Board of Governors, and the National Representatives to the Economic Commission Board.
