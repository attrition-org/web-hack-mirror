No. 4/2000 29 April 2000

Vienna, Austria

 

 

Resolutions of the

109th Meeting of the OPEC Conference

 

The 109th Meeting of the Conference of the Organization of the Petroleum Exporting Countries, held in Vienna, Austria, from 27-29 March 2000, adopted the following Resolutions, which, in accordance with customary procedures, have been ratified by the Member Countries and are issued herewith:

 

RESOLUTION No. 109.378

The Conference,

upon the recommendation of the Board of Governors,

approves

  1. The Statement of Income and Expenditure for 1999 showing a total expenditure of

ATS 151,732,704;

2. The Statement of Accounts as at 31 December 1999, and the Audit Report submitted thereon by the appointed Auditors, TPA Control Wirtschaftsprüfung G.m.b.H.

 

RESOLUTION No. 109.379

The Conference

resolves

that the next Ordinary Meeting of the Conference shall be convened in Vienna, Austria, on 10 September 2000.

Done in Vienna, Austria, this Twenty-Ninth day of March 2000.
