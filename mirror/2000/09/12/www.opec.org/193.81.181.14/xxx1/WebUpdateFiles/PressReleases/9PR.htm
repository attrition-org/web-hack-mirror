For immediate release

Vienna, 29th March 2000

 

 

Nine OPEC Member Countries’ Press Release

 

Nine OPEC Member Countries (Algeria, Indonesia, Kuwait, S.P. Libyan A.J., Nigeria, Qatar, Saudi Arabia, the United Arab Emirates and Venezuela), meeting in Vienna on the occasion of the 109th OPEC Conference, on 29th March, 2000, decided to restore their production to their pre-March 1999 levels, making their individual Member Country output levels as follows:

Production

(tb/d)

Algeria 788

Indonesia 1,280

Kuwait 1,980

S.P. Libyan A.J. 1,323

Nigeria 2,033

Qatar 640

Saudi Arabia 8,023

United Arab Emirates 2,157

Venezuela 2,845

Total 21,069

 

These nine Member Countries strongly emphasized their firm commitment to this agreement, which is effective from 1st April 2000, and each stressed its intention to ensure full compliance.
