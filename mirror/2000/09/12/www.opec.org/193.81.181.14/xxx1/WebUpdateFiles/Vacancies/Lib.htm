The Organization of the

Petroleum Exporting Countries

 

has a vacancy for an

 

Experienced Librarian

 

in its energy research-oriented Library.

 

 

Prospective candidates should be aged 28-40, and must have a university degree in Library Science or a postgraduate diploma in Librarianship or Information Science. A minimum of five years experience is required, three of which should have been spent in a scientific or technical library. Knowledge of reference petroleum literature and publications is necessary, as well as experience of computer application in library classification.

 

Fluent English is essential and some German and French desirable.

 

Interested candidates should send their application, CV and a recent head-and-shoulders photograph to:

 

Head, Administration and Human Resources Dept.

OPEC Secretariat

Obere Donaustrasse 93

A-1020 Vienna, Austria.

 

The deadline for receiving applications is two weeks from the date of this publication.
