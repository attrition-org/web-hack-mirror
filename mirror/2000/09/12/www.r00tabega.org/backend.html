 Backend Information
If you wish to display the latest files from us on your website, we provide solutions to do so. To start with, your webserver must support SSI, Perl, and be able to modify your crontab.

1/Download r00tabega-latest-files.pl.txt
2/Rename the file extension to .pl and chmod it to mode 755
3/Type ./r00tabega-latest-files.pl to verify if it works for you
4/Add it to your /etc/crontab and set it to execute it every hour
5/That's all. Now just include it on your page with an ssi tag
