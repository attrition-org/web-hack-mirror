Wireless Internet Access

Silicon Desert has partnered with Clearwire Technologies, the developer of a new fixed wireless local access product and service deployed in Albuquerque, to deliver quickly provisioned, fractional T1 speed IP connectivity throughout the metropolitan area. Silicon Desert, an authorized Clearwire reseller, bundles Clearwire's wireless local access services with their secure Internet access and other value added services to provide one stop shop solutions to your IP communications needs. Through this partnership, Silicon Desert further demonstrates its commitment to delivering high quality services at affordable prices to the business end-user.

The Clearwire solution is a fixed wireless IP network that operates in the 2.4 GHz ISM band and delivers local loop connectivity to Silicon Desert’s network at speeds of up to 640 Kbps. The Clearwire product and service are not affected by weather and are highly secure, drawing from the technology’s origins in the military.

Clearwire’s network consists of base station infrastructure equipment and customer premise equipment or CPE. The base station infrastructure equipment, owned and operated by Clearwire, is positioned at existing tower or rooftop sites that afford effective line of sight to the desired area of coverage. The CPE, which functions as an access server, is connected to an end-user's LAN, hub, or PC via a 10 Base-T Ethernet interface; no router is required. Both base station equipment and CPE employ Clearwire's unique T/R Module technology that allows the antenna and indoor unit to be installed up to 250' apart, without degraded performance.

Silicon Desert and Clearwire back the service with a 30-day money back guarantee, and better yet, there is no equipment to buy.
