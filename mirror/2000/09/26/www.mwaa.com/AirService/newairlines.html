New Passenger Air Service
Washington Dulles International Airport is the Gateway to the World, with 247 weekly international departures.

Back to the Air Service main page

INTERNATIONAL

Air Canada began the first nonstop service between Dulles and Vancouver on June 5. Air Canada also increased service to Halifax and Montreal, adding a second and third nonstop to each destination. (Read more about flights to Canada...)

Effective June 27 to August 24, ANA will operate 5 nonstops between Dulles and Narita, Tokyo. Daily service will return August 25.

Korean Air operates nonstop on Monday's between Seoul and Dulles. This nonstop is in addition to Korean Air's 3 weekly direct flights operating on Tuesday, Thursday and Saturday between Dulles and Seoul.

Sabena

Fly nonstop everyday except Monday and Wednesday, between Dulles and Brussels, departing Dulles at 6:20 pm. Sabena offers a network of more than 100 destinations in Europe, Africa, India, the Middle East and Far East.

For more information call Sabena at 1-800-955-2000.

Spanair began daily service March 26, 2000.

United Airlines added a third daily flight to London/Heathrow in April. The new nonstop flight departs Dulles at 7:20 pm.

DOMESTIC

 

American Trans Air, a low fare airline, will launch new nonstop service between Reagan National and Chicago's Midway Airport on April 13. For more information contact American Trans Air (ATA) at 1-800--FLY-ATA (1-800-435-9282).

Frontier Airlines can get you to nearly every major destination in the continental United States. Call Frontier toll-free at 800-432-1359.

Legend Airlines

Legend Airlines began new low-fare service four times daily between Dulles and Dallas Love Field, Texas, April 4. They can be contacted at 1-877-359-5343 (FLYLEGEND).

Spirit Airlines, a low fare specialist, will begin non-stop, daily service from Reagan Washington National Airport to Ft. Lauderdale and Melbourne, Florida on October 26. For information or reservations, please call 1-800-772-7117 or www.spiritair.com.

United Express

United Express began new daily regional jet service four times a day between Dulles and Louisville June 8. 

United Express will began nonstop service six times daily between Dulles and Shenandoah Valley on March 2, 2000.

For more information and reservations call United Express at: 800-241-6522
