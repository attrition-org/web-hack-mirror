Diadora Concept Store was created to offer a complete range of products for sport and leisure in a pleasant environment, specifically designed to satisfy customers' purchasing needs.

Multi-functional island displays and wall modules highlight the range of colours and possible matches that can be made with different clothing items, footwear and accessories.

This particular formula enables Diadora to display its complete product range and thus let customers choose the right item at their leisure, with helpful assistance from the friendly shop personnel
