Diadora is close to the Brazilian people and wants Guga at the Olympics.

Sacrificing its sponsorship rights over Gustavo Kuerten, Diadora decided to accept, like it did already last week, that Guga plays at the Olympic Games in Sidney with a different uniform than the one produced by the Italian company.

With this decision, Diadora wants to show its full commitment to the Olympic spirit and its close feeling with Guga and the Brazilian people which, like Diadora, want to see his hero at the Australian Games.
