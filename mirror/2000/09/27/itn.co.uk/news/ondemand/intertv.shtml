ITN Interactive TV on NTL

Don't wait for the news but go straight to the stories you want.

Available to all customers with the ntl interactive service , ITN interactive lets you take charge. Watch ITN's non-stop news service or call up more features at the touch of a button.

The TV channel stays on your screen so you'll never miss what's going on. See additional pictures, read more about the news of the day or go in-depth with special reports.

Unmissable news without the distraction of phone-ins, features or fillers. It's sharp, fast and personal, putting you in charge of the news you want at the touch of a button.
