Today's Top Stories

Greek ferry disaster

A ferry has sunk off the Greek island of Paros. The number of dead has been rising since the accident. The ferry was taking holiday makers from Athens to some of the islands in the Aegean. Our Europe Correspondent Bill Neely will be reporting live from Paros.

 

 

Small car safety

European safety tests on small cars show they are getting safer - after concerns among drivers. However there has been little improvement in safety for pedestrians. Our Consumer Affairs Correspondent Chris Choi will be looking at the latest findings.

 

Premiere for British movie hope

The new British film "Billy Elliot" is already been described as the next "Full Monty." It is the story of a young boy from a mining village who defies his family and his background to become a ballet dancer. Our Media and Arts Correspondent Katie Derham will be at thepremiere.
