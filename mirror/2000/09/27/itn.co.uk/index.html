Death toll hits 62 in Greek ferry disaster

Greek officials have confirmed that the death toll following the sinking of a ferry off the Aegean island of Paros has risen to 62 with 29 people still missing.

It is believed 443 people had been rescued or accounted for after the ferry Express Samina ran aground and sank in gale-force conditions. An investigation is underway to find the cause of Greece's worst maritime disaster for 35 years.
