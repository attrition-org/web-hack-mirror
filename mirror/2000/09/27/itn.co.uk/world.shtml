Passenger ship disasters worldwide

Some of the worst passenger ship disasters over the last four years include:

 

Year 2000 -

June 29 - INDONESIA - Only about 10 people survive when an Indonesian ferry, the Cahaya Bahari (Light of the Ocean), with 290 fleeing refugees and almost 200 passengers, sinks off the coast of Sulawesi.

June 22- CHINA - At least 130 die when ferry Rong Jian, with about 220 people, capsizes on the Yangtze River near Luzhou in Sichuan.

April 12 - PHILIPPINES - At least 138 drown after the ML Annahada sinks off Jolo island in the south.

 

1999 -

November 25 - CHINA - Only 22 survive when the Dashun, with at least 308 aboard, founders and breaks up in stormy waters off the port city of Yantai on the east coast.

May 8- BANGLADESH - Ferry MV Dwipkanya, carrying some 400 passengers, sinks in a whirlpool during a storm near Lakhsmipur, 175 km (110 miles) from Dhaka. At least 300 are missing.

March 22 - SIERRA LEONE - At least 150 drown when overloaded motorised canoe capsizes off the town of Tasso.

March 6 - SIERRA LEONE - More than 100 traders drown and around 100 go missing after their overloaded boat sinks in rough seas off Freetown.

February 6 - INDONESIA - Only 20 are rescued from ferry Harta Rimba, carrying 325 passengers and seven crew, which sinks in South China Sea near Tambelan islands, north of Jakarta.

 

1998 -

September 18 - PHILIPPINES - Almost 200 die after the 13,935-ton MV Princess of the Orient sinks near Manila. Almost 300 survivors are recovered.

June 12 - GUINEA-BISSAU - About 200 people fleeing fighting in Guinea-Bissau drown when they tried to reach the Bijagos Islands off the coast of the West African state.

 

1997 -

September 8 - HAITI - Between 300 and 400 people believed dead in sinking of packed ferry bound for the island of La Gonave.

September 1 - NIGERIA - Passenger ferry Olodiama collides with tugboat off southeastern Nigeria. At least 130 are missing and presumed drowned.

August 25 - NIGERIA - About 100 drown when two wooden boats collide off Nigeria's Niger delta.

 

1996 -

May 21 - TANZANIA - Tanzanian ferry MV Bukoba capsizes on Lake Victoria near western town of Mwanza. Only 114 survive out of about 1,000 estimated to have been on board.

January 19 - INDONESIA - Up to 338 believed to have died after ferry Gurita sank in heavy seas off Banda Aceh. At least 55 confirmed dead, 238 listed as missing, 40 are rescued.
