Who Are We?



Many people have asked just who it is exactly that runs HNN. In an
effort to answer that question we provide this information.









        




Weld Pond, a past employee of L0pht Heavy Industries and now with @stake, is
the
managing editor of HNN. He has
released
numerous security advisories on everything from Lotus Notes to Windows
NT. He also acts as general webmaster; perl, java, and cgi guru and
graphic designer for HNN. (Picture provided by The NewsHour with Jim
Lehrer)
