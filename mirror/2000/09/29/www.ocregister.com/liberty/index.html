Our credo:
The Opinion pages of the Register are dedicated to furnishing information to our readers so that they can better promote and preserve their own freedom and encourage others to do the same. We believe that only when people are free to control themselves and all that they produce can they develop to their utmost capabilities. Freedom is neither license nor anarchy. It is control and sovereignty of oneself, no more, no less.
