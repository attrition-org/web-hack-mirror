Take a tour of the Register

Tour Guest Requirements

  • Maximum of 15 people per tour
  • Minimum age is eight years old.
  • Minimum number of tour participants is five. Groups with fewer than five participants will be scheduled with other small groups.
  • The Register requests at least one week notice of cancellation.
  • Tours will not rescheduled for groups that fail to notify the Register of cancellation.
  • Tour groups should arrive at the Register 20 minutes before tour begins and check in with the lobby receptionist.
  • All guests are required to wear a temporary security badge while on Register property. One person from each group may sign the entire group in with the lobby receptionist.

    Parking

  • Visitor parking is located in front of the Register and is marked. All groups should park in the area. If guest parking is full, please see guard located off Orange County Register Way.

    Conduct

  • All questions are encouraged. Register tours are designed to teach people about what we do and how we do it.
  • Flash photography is allowed although we ask guests not to take pictures of text displayed on reporters' computer screens. This is for competitive reasons.
  • Do not touch computers or other equipment. particular care should be taken by adults when touring the pressroom. There are lots of moving parts and little hands can be hurt or burned.
  • No screaming, running, jumping or roughhousing allowed during tours.
  • Unruly children will be warned if behavior is out of line. If problems persist, the child will be asked to take a seat in the lobby for the remainder of the tour. One adult will be required to sit with the child until the tour is completed.
  • No food or drinks allowed while taking the tour.
    • Tour hotline: 877/877-4MYREGI
