About OCREGISTER.COM

OCREGISTER.COM is produced by The Orange County Register, a Freedom Communications Inc. newspaper in Orange County, Calif., USA. The Register ranks in the top 25 circulation newspapers in the U.S., selling 356,520 copies daily and 415,638 Sundays.

All information in OCREGISTER.COM and the name and logo are copyright The Orange County Register and all rights are reserved.

Letters to the Editor: If you want to send a letter to the editor to appear in our print edition, write to letters@link.freedom.com. Letters must be signed and give the writer's name and telephone number (addresses and telephone numbers will not be published). Letters of about 200 words will be given preference. Letters will be edited for length, grammar, and clarity. General questions about the Opinion pages should be directed to deputy editor Matt_Leone@link.freedom.com.
