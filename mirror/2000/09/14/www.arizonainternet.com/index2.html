       Arizona Internet Services understands that the absolute performance of your web presence is directly affected by the quality and speed of our network. Instead of utilizing the congested and faulty public peering points as many providers do, we maintain multiple direct T3 and T1 access points to multiple backbone providers. This is a very important differentiation that significantly
reduces the number of hops (routers) your network traffic must traverse before it reaches a national or international Internet backbone. This fundamental network strategy ensures that your connection will always experience the clearest path possible to the Internet. To meet the wide range of
