Feedback

 

Contracting Opportunities On-Line is provided by the Metropolitan Washington Airports Authority as a service to the contracting community.  Its goal is to provide comprehensive project information for construction, architectural and engineering, goods, and services opportunities with the Authority in the most useful format possible.  Since its introduction two years ago, the site's evolution has been based directly on feedback from its users.  If you have any suggestions on how we can improve the site or features that you would like to see in the future, feel free to e-mail me at giardinm@mwaa.com or contact me by phone at 703-417-8672.  Additionally, please take a moment to complete the Contractor Satisfaction Survey to tell us about how you utilize the information provided by the site, as well as to give us your feedback on each of the site's sections.  Thank you for your time, and watch for significant additions to the site during the year 2000!

Sincerely,

Mike Giardina
Procurement Technology Systems Specialist and Plans Coordinator
