Featuring

Dulles In the New Millennium! Construction Opportunities Soar To New Heights At Washington Dulles International Airport! Another Major Capitol Development Project Underway!

Washington Dulles International Airport is expanding beyond the horizon with OVER $3 BILLION worth of construction and design projects expected to be awarded through 2006. This is the opportunity of the millennium! Get informed and be prepared for these upcoming projects. Engage in a panel discussion with Authority Prime and Subcontractors who will inform you how to secure work on Authority contracts.

Goods & Services Opportunities at Washington Dulles International and Ronald Reagan Washington National Airports.

Participate in a panel discussion with Authority Prime and Subcontractors who will inform you on how to secure work on Authority contracts. Learn about the Authority’s LDBE program, recent changes in the LDBE and DBE programs and how these changes will increase opportunities for small businesses in the future.

Concession Opportunities Take Off at Washington Dulles International and Ronald Reagan Washington National Airports.

 Join in a panel discussion on upcoming concession opportunities at the Airports through 2006. The Authority’s developers, Host Marriott and Westfield Corporation, will present information on their procurement process and future concession opportunities at both Airports. Concessionaires will offer perspective on how they got started and their experience as airport concessionaires.

Technical Assistance Workshop

The Small Business Administration (SBA) and the Department of Transportation (DOT) will host an informative workshop on financial and technical assistance programs.

There is no charge to register, but you must register in order to attend the seminar.
