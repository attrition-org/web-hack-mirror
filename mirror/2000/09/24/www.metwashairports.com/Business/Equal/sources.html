C. Additional information the offeror needs to know and do:

  1. The physical location of the Authority's procurement office in Alexandria.
  2. The offeror needs to be thoroughly familiar with the Authority's contract requirements including a clear understanding of the proposed project. This may be accomplished by:
         a. Visiting our office to discuss standard contract formats.
         b. Reviewing solicitations, which have already been issued.

D. Additional Tasks for the Offeror:

The offeror should submit a written request to the Procurement and Contracts Department at the following address, asking that his/her firm be add to the branch's database of prospective offerors:

  1. Metropolitan Washington Airports Authority
    Procurement and Contracts Department, MA-440
    1 Aviation Circle
    Washington, DC 20001-6000
    PLEASE NOTE that a firm's inclusion on this list does not guarantee that it will be notified of any specific work.
  2. Offerors should also ensure they understand the procedures for becoming certified as an LDBE and/or DBE with the Authority.
