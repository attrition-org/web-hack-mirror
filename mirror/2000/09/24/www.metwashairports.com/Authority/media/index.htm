Media Guide

The purpose of the Metropolitan Washington Airports Authority Media Guide is to provide local and national news media organizations with information to work most effectively with the staff at Ronald Reagan Washington National and Washington Dulles International Airports.

  • Air Transport Association ( ATA) - www.air-transport.org  - Association that represents America's leading airlines.
  • American Association of Airport Executives ( AAAE) - www.airportnet.org .
  • Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) - www.faa.gov  - The federal agency responsible for aircraft operations throughout the U.S. The FAA operates the control towers at both airports.
  • National Transportation Safety Board (NTSB) - www.ntsb.gov  - Federal agency responsible for investiging aircraft incidents.
