Directions To Washington Dulles International Airport (IAD)

From Downtown Washington DC:

Go west on I-66 to exit 67. Follow signs to the airport, approximately 16 miles from exit 67.

From Baltimore:

Go south on I-95 to exit 27 (I 495 West - "The Beltway") to exit 12B. Follow signs to the airport, approximately 13 miles from exit 12B.

From Richmond:

Go north on I-95 to exit 170 (I 495 - "The Beltway") to exit 12B. Follow signs to the airport, approximately 13 miles from exit 12B.

From West Virginia:

Go south on I-81 to I-66 East. I-66 East to exit 53 (Rt. 28 North). Follow Route 28 to airport.

From Roanoke, Southwest Virginia:

Go north on I-81 to I-66 East. Follow I-66 East to exit 53 (Rt. 28 North). Follow Route 28 to airport.

For exact door-to-door directions, go to www.mapquest.com and click on Driving Directions. Under Advanced Options be sure to select Airports and enter "Washington DC" in either the Destination or Starting Location boxes.
