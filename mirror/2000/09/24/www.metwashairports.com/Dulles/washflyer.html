Washington Flyer Airport Express Bus
  Washington Flyer Airport Express Bus service links Ronald Reagan Washington National Airport with Washington Dulles International Airport, connects Dulles with the Metrorail subway system, and provides nonstop transportation to downtown Washington DC, stopping twice each hour at nine of the city's most popular hotels and Union Station. 17 buses are handicapped accessible. For additional schedule information, call 1-888-WASHFLY.
