New Pathway Information Packet

This collection of journal articles, bibliographies and curriculum maps describes the history and current state of the New Pathway Problem-Based curriculum at Harvard Medical School. It also includes copies of the MedEd News, "Tutorial Experience: A Survival Guide" and the OED Program Report. To request a packet please call (617) 432-0340. There is a $25 charge.
