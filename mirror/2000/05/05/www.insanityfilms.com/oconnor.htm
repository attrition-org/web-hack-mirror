Damaged Goods marks Colin's debut film performance. Seattle theater credits include Prince Hal in Seattle Shakespeare Festivals Henry IV, Part One and Seattle Repertory Theater's Psychopathia Sexualis. (Other Regional Credits include Richmond in Richard III at the Idaho Shakespeare Festival and Young Scrooge with Nebraska Theater Caravan's Midwest Tour of A Christmas Carol.) He received a BFA in Acting from the University of Illinois' Professional Actor Training Program and completed an acting internship with the Milwaukee Repertory Theater where he made his professional stage debut in Feydeau' .
