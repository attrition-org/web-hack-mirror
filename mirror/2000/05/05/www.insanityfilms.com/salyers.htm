William Salyers

Bill's Seattle performances include such shows as Taming of the Shrew and Tuesday at the AHA!, A Midsummer Night's Dream and Servant of Two Masters at the Bathhouse, the Empty Space's production of Dracula and Intiman's Peter Pan. Television roles include Shakespeare in Northern Exposure and a scalpel-wielding maniac in Medicine Ball. Feature Film credits: NBC's The Marla Hanson Story and Pandora's Clock, Trust Me from 2207 Productions and Crocodile Tears. He holds a bachelor of fine arts degree from the College of Santa Fe in New Mexico.
