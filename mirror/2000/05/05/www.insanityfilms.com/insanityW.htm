Insanity Filmworks, Inc. (I.F.) is an independent, motion-picture production company located in the Seattle area; Tacoma, Washington to be more specific (don't ask). We are dedicated to making good films that are both cinematically fulfilling as well as commercial in appeal which is not what people think when they hear the name, Insanity Filmworks, but anybody that makes films the independent way knows the derivation of the name; as you have to be crazy to do it. Not only are we crazy, but we're crazy enough to love it. As a matter of fact it is a prerequisite if you want to work with Insanity Filmworks and it's creator, Writer-Producer-Director, Jason Francois or any of his associates. No completely sane people allowed, unless you have tons of cash in which case we're willing to teach you.
