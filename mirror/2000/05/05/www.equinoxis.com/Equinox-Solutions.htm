Protector
The Ultimate in Real-Time Fraud Protection
Telephone hackers cost carriers nearly $12 billion a year. With Protector fraud detection software, you can ensure maximum protection for your network with a system designed to identify suspicious network activity quickly and stop fraud in progress. For nearly a decade, Protector has helped hundreds of customers limit losses to fraud using real-time, rules-based technology to distinguish fraud from honest customer usage. A sophisticated alarming system and automatic disabling feature shuts down thieves on-the-spot to prevent future losses to fraud.

Guardian
Real-Time Credit Monitoring
Expand your menu of services with Guardian credit monitoring software. Perfect for the college or university markets, Guardian monitors dollar usage in real-time to prohibit customers from exceeding their credit limits. Guardian can alert when customers are nearing their credit limitations or even disallow calls until customers have cleared their account balances. Once payment is received, Guardian automatically reactivates the customer account.

TeleScope
Real-Time Network and Financial Analysis
Respond immediately to opportunities and risks in the constantly changing wholesale market with TeleScope. This innovative traffic and financial analysis tool allows you to make intelligent network management decisions by providing critical real-time information about both your domestic and international traffic, including cost, revenue, and completion rates. QuickRoute
Least Cost Routing System
Reduce overflow traffic costs by automatically directing calls to your most cost-effective alternative route choice with QuickRoute. Fast and easy to use, Quickroute performs least cost routing based on LATA or Tandem by enabling you to convert your NPA/NXX routing database. One table updates every switch in your network! QuickRoute also streamlines management by managing hundreds of thousands of records in minutes.

ShowCDR
Sophisticated CDR Search System
"I couldn’t run my network without it!" We often hear this statement from our customers using ShowCDR. As the best search utility of its kind on the market, ShowCDR eliminates the need for switch-based searches by offering the most advanced means to view, search, and report call records. With a simple, intuitive user interface, ShowCDR gives you real-time access to call records for fraud investigation, trouble reporting, traffic analysis, and customer support.

AccessCDR
Trouble-Free Customer CDR Access
Eliminate the time-consuming burden of manually responding to your customers’ requests for CDR information. With AccessCDR, you can automatically provide your customers with real-time access to their own call detail records. AccessCDR writes binary call records to one of multiple files based on the originating trunk group, terminating trunk group, or billing number so that you can streamline management and control security access by user.

ExtractCDR
Multi-System Mediation
Effectively mediate your call detail and billing records amidst the constantly changing nature of downstream systems with ExtractCDR. Carriers need an integrated, flexible system that will adapt and expand to keep up with their multiple management systems and varying data formats. ExtractCDR is designed to help carriers overcome the burden of multi-system data processing with a low-cost, user-friendly system that filters and converts raw switch data and billing records into usable formats.
