Equinox Information Systems is approximately 13 miles (20 minutes) from Nashville International Airport. Upon exiting Nashville International Airport, follow signs to I-40.  Go West towards Nashville on I-40 for approximately 3/4 mile. Exit to Briley Parkway.  Go Northwest on Briley Parkway for approximately 12 miles. Exit to Briarville Road. Go North on Briarville Road for approximately 1/4 mile. Equinox Information Systems is located in a colonial brick building on the right hand side.
