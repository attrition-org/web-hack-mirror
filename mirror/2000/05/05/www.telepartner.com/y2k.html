Year 2000 compliance means that neither performance nor functionality is affected by dates prior to, during, and after Year 2000.

Each Telepartner product has undergone comprehensive testing. Test plans consisted of test cases to ensure compliance, accuracy and continued product functionality. With the system and application dates adjusted, testing covered the following time spans:

  • 12/31/1998 - 01/01/1999
  • 09/08/1999 - 09/09/1999
  • 12/30/1999 - 12/31/1999
  • 12/31/1999 - 01/01/2000
  • 02/28/2000 - 02/29/2000
  • 02/29/2000 - 03/01/2000
  • 12/31/2000 - 01/01/2001

Additionally, BIOS testing was performed to determine what impact if any a non-compliant system would have on our software products.
