State-of-the-art Computer Services:

In addition to reliable, on-time delivery of your freight, you can also count on AAA Cooper Transportation for fast and accurate delivery of information concerning every aspect of your shipment.  We utilize a wide variety of the industry's most advanced computer hardware, software, and communications technology to enable us to tell you exactly what you need to know, when you need to know it.

Electronic Data Interchange (EDI)

By transmitting data electronically we have eliminated a mountain of paperwork, and greatly reduced the chance of data entry errors.  To put it simply, EDI makes us more efficient. It saves us money and we can pass those savings on to you. 

EDI can benefit you in several ways.  Statements and invoices, for example, can be electronically transmitted from our computer to yours, thus eliminating time-consuming, error-prone paper shuffling.  Shipment status reports - including locations and condition - can be updated and transmitted electronically for up-to-the minute information.  Time and errors associated with payment processing can be dramatically reduced by electronic transmission of payment information.  And to further improve the accuracy of billing we are using EDI for Bill of Lading information.

Imaged Documents

The ACTION System (AAA Cooper Transportation Images Online Network) has been established to help us meet the informational needs of our customers and employees for imaged data. 

The speed of delivering documents - and the visual quality compared to microfilm - have been vastly improved thanks to the use of the very latest imaging technology.  Signed delivery receipts and Bills of Lading are now scanned and stored on optical media.  These documents can be transmitted to your fax machine within minutes of your request via the internet track option or from our customer service.  Future use of image technology will apply to other critical documents as needed.

Service Guide

AAA Cooper Service Guide Program is a simple and reliable tool for determining service times and distances.  The program consists of four screens: The Guide screen, which displays zip code information regarding our terminal, distance between, and number of service days; The Map Screen, which shows our terminal locations with specific terminal, address and phone info; The Calendar Screen, which displays a calendar showing pick-up and delivery days between specified Origin and Destination points; The Service Days Screen, which allows you to view number of days required to move shipments from one terminal to another. Download it today. Follow the simple instructions for installing the software and you are ready to go.

Fax Tracing

A fax report showing all vital information about your shipments can automatically be sent to you each morning. This fax report can be customized to suit your individual needs.

Computerized Dispatch and Pickup

AAA Cooper's largest terminals are all equipped with state-of-the-art computer systems that enable us to provide you with the most accurate and efficient service available.  Special shipping instructions and scheduled pickups are maintained in these systems.  The computers determine the optimum pickup and delivery routes for shipments.  Trucks with onboard computers can communicate with the local freight terminal to send and receive continuous updates of vital shipment information allowing for real-time reporting of delivery information. This information is updated real-time into AAA Cooper tracking option via the internet.

Service Time Reports

Personalized transit time reports can be generated weekly or monthly -- on magnetic tape or diskette -- to answer all of the questions a shipper normally has about carrier performance. These service reports can also be sent via email for timely delivery. Other reports are available upon request.

Other Reports

If you have unique requirements for information on your shipments, AAA Cooper can provide special reports just for you.  Tariff charts by zip code and class, for example, are available.  We can provide comparison charts among different rate scales.  And we can summarize your statements on a special weekly or bi-weekly basis.
