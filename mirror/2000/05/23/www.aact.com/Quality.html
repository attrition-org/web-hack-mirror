Mission Statement

"Our Company Mission is to provide Superior service by Excellent people using Good equipment at a Fair profit."

Quality Policy

"We will provide error-free services, that conform to our customers' requirements -- on time, every time. Our name will represent quality to ourselves and to our customers."

     
    Since the inception of our formal Quality Improvement Process in 1990, AAA Cooper Transportation has used our Quality Policy as a beacon to direct the efforts of all our employees toward satisfying our customers in each business transaction.

    At AAA Cooper we have used our Quality Improvement Process as an avenue which allows us to form partnerships with our customers. These mutually beneficial partnerships allow all parties to openly discuss areas such as: requirements, schedules, costing and commitment. These partnerships are at the heart of the growth and success AAA Cooper has enjoyed over the past two decades. Using these partnerships, AAA Cooper has been able to grow and expand with our customers, while at the same time attracting new customers through outstanding service and innovative ideas.

Quality Improvement Process: 
    The Quality Improvement Process is directed by the Steering Committee, which consists of Mr. Mack Dove, President & Chief Executive Officer,Mr. J.E. "Pete" Barkley, V.P. & Chief Operating Officer and Mr. Charles Coggins, V.P. and Chief Financial Officer. Implementation is carried forth by Jimmy Barkley, the Director of Quality and the trained and knowledgeable staff in the Quality Department.
Training: 
    To ensure everyone understands their role in the Quality Improvement Process, each employee attends specifically designed courses, conducted by trained instructors from the Quality Department. While attending these courses, employees learn the language and concepts of the Quality Improvement Process. They are also trained in teamwork, measurement and problem-solving techniques. Advance Quality Classes also include training in process analysis, planning productive meetings and recognizing and analyzing paradigms.

    Quality Training is an ongoing and integral part of the Quality Improvement Process at AAA Cooper Transportation.

Implementation:

    Implementation of the Quality Improvement Process is conducted through the Quality Improvement Teams. Quality Improvement Team Members include department heads, terminal managers, supervisors and other key personnel. These teams have the responsibility of implementing the Quality Improvement Process at their local facility. This includes forming quality workgroups, corrective action teams, and sub-committees. These groups are made up of personnel from all segments of our company. Their involvement ensures the Quality Improvement Process is continually moving forward. By using their knowledge to improve all of our work processes, employees can continually improve services to our customers.
Commitment:
    The employees of AAA Cooper Transportation are committed, "to doing our jobs right the first time." By adopting this Zero Defects attitude we ensure the name AAA Cooper Transportation will represent Quality now and in the future.
