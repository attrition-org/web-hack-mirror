Contact Information

The easiest way to contact the Apache Software Foundation is via e-mail. Our address for general correspondence and licensing questions is

apache@apache.org

Normal mail and faxes can be sent to our physical office address at 

     The Apache Software Foundation
     1901 Munsey Drive
     Forest Hill, MD 21050-2747
     U.S.A.

     fax: +1.410.803.2258

Queries from the press (and ONLY queries from the press, NOT support questions or anything else!) should go to our media relations address: 

     press@apache.org

If you are interested in donating funds or equipment to the Apache Software Foundation, please see our information on contributing to the ASF.
