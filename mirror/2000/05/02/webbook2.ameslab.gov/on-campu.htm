On-Campus Laboratories

     The CSET laboratories located in the Metals Development Building on the ISU Campus, together with laboratories in various academic departments provide access to a wide variety of computational and experimental resources.   Analytical services allow elemental, molecular, compositional, and structural analysis on a variety of materials, including feedstocks, products, and by-products, process stream components, and waste materials.  Specific instrumental capabilities include most forms of of chromatography, x-ray analysis, microscopy, and spectroscopy.   Laboratory-scale equipment is available to evaluate combustion, gasification, pyrolysis, anaerobic digestion, and fermentation process.
