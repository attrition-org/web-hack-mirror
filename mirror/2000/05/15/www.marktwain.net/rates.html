Internet Rates

Explanation of   Rate Structure

$9.95/mo for 15 hours; $.30/hour (1/2 cent per minute) for usage over 15 hours up to $18.95/mo.  For Usage between 45 hours and 200 hours per month the rate will be $18.95/month.     Extremely high volume users with usage over 200 hours per month will pay $25.00.   Extremely high volume users are only 1% of our customers. 

There's no need to choose among different rate plans, this is a single plan that fits all sizes.  If you are on vacation one month and only use a few hours  you would pay $9.95.  The next month if you are on the internet 40 hours per week you would pay $18.95.  This way you don't have to lock in to a high end rate that you pay 12 months a year even for months with low usage.  Refer to the table below to see how much you will save with these new rates.

A one time set-up fee of $10 includes the Easy Internet CD which contains the latest Internet Explorer AND Netscape Browsers, plus customized installation instructions.
