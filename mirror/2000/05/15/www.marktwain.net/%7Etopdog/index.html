Shipping

 If delivery is requested we will ship by air freight.  Airfare costs vary.  Price is approximately $100 - $150.  There is a $25 deposit for a reusable dog crate.  Airline arrangements can be made for you if prepaid to a designated location.  Buyer is solely responsible for costs of crate, insurance during shipment, and cost of freight charges.

Care
Our facilities are inspected annually by the USDA to insure we meet federal standards.  However we strive to far surpass these regulations in terms of facilities, health, and personal care.  Our dogs are raised in a loving, humane, and sanitary environment.  Our whelping facility is climate controlled year round.  Our puppies are examined by a veterinarian who specializes in small animals to ensure each puppy meets our high standards.  All puppies are vaccinated and wormed.  We give our dogs a lot of one on one personal attention.  We treat each and every puppy with LOVE & AFFECTION.

Credentials
We are proud members of the Missouri Pet Breeders Association, attend bi-annual conferences, and are a board member of the Professional Pet Association, and attend quarterly seminars.  We are members of the American Kennel Club, licensed by the state of Missouri, and federally licensed with the USDA under the Animal Care Facility Act.  We have 30 years of experience as breeders and love our dogs.

About Us
Our family lives on a 1250 acre farm in the picturesque rolling hills of Northeast Missouri.  Our experience of raising dogs started over 30 years ago when we were given a Saint Bernard puppy as a gift!  Since then we have grown and along with our Soft coated Wheaten Terriers we also raise and sell Bearded Collie, Brussels Griffon, and Cavalier King Charles spaniel puppies.  We also raise Amerifax and Black Angus cattle and we grow corn and soybeans on our farm.  We have three grown children and six wonderful grandchildren.  Our lives on the farm have been blessed and we love what we do for a living.  View more photos of our wonderful landscape.
