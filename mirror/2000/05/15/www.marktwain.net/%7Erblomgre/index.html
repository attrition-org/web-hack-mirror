Welcome to the Blomgren's home page. We are glad that you decided to take a peek at us. We now live in Lancaster, MO and are both semi-retired. Dick retired from the US Navy in 1988 and we moved to Lancaster in 1991. We are members of the Lancaster Christian Church where Dick serves as a Deacon, Past Chairman of the Church Board, and a member of the Worship Committee. Bev is a member of Christian Women's Service (CWS). We have three children, Vernon, a U. S. Navy Veteran, a resident of Lancaster and working in Ottumwa, IA, Diane, a member of the U.S. Air Force and stationed at Whiteman AFB in Missouri, and Chris, who completed two years at Cornell College in Mt. Vernon, IA and his first year of Nursing school at Indian Hills Community College in Ottumwa, IA and is currently serving with the United States Army at Ft. Leonard Wood, MO. We have also been blessed with four granddaughters, ages seven through sixteen. They are (youngest to oldest) Kaitlin, Bridgette, Brittany and Rachael. The two youngest belong to Diane and the two oldest belong to Vernon.
