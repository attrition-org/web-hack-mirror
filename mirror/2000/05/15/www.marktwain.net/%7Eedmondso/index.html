Welcome to our little piece of of the web. We live in Kahoka, Missouri, which is a small town in Clark County. This page is nothing special, it is just a place to let people know a little somthing about us.

My name is Brent Edmondson. I have been married to my wife Jamie for 11 years. We have two children named Tony and Chelsea. We are a typical two income family. I work full time in a manufacturing facility and go to school part time in the evenings to complete my degree in Accounting. Jamie works full time in a nursing home as a Licensed Practical Nurse and is going to school part time to get her Registered Nurse degree.

I spent almost 6 years in the United States Air Force. After active duty, I transfered to the Missouri Air National Guard in St. Louis, Missouri. I enjoy the military very much. As a family, my wife and I have been around the world. We spent a year living in Germany, which was very cool. The time there was very educational and gave us insight and respect for other cultures. Since joining the Air National Guard, I have been to Turkey 3 times and a trip to Denmark. The diversity of the different cultures was enlightening. I enjoyed learning how the people in other countries that I have visited lived, worked, and what they do for recreation.

We are both involved in the Boy Scouting organizations here in Kahoka. I am a Assistant Scoutmaster in Boy Scout Troop 145 and Assistant Cubmaster and assist where needed in Cub Scout Pack 145. Jamie is the Tiger Coach and asssists where needed in Cub Scout Pack 145. Tony has been a member of the Cub Scout Program since he was old enough and is enjoying it very much. Chelsea has been a member of the has been a member of the local Girl Scout since she was old enough and enjoying that very much.
