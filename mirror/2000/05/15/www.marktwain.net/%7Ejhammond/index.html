Welcome to My webpage.  My name is John Hammond.  I live in Kahoka,MO.   This page is nothing special.  More or less it is an outlet for my opinions and political beliefs also for entertainment.  I am into RACING, golf, guns, coins, history, beer, and outdoor activities.  Below you will find my favorite links to GUNS, BEER, OUTDOOR, Honda Odysseys, HUNTING, and RACING, etc.  I listen to Rush and I am basically a conservative politically.  The second Amendment is equal to any other in the Bill of Rights.

I have been married for 7 years and have a son who is 21 months old.  I served 5 years in the U.S. Army.  I received a B.S. degree in Industrial Science from Northeast Missouri State University in Kirksville, Mo. and a Masters degree in Industrial Safety Management from Central Missouri State University in Warrensburg, Mo.  I work as a Plant Engineer/Safety and Environmental.

Very rarely do I meet a beer I don't like.  Pilsners are my favorite such as Pilsner Urquell, Staropramen and Warsteiner.  I homebrew my own sometimes.  It is usually worth the work.  If I buy beer it is usually a microbrew or Rolling Rock.   Miller Genuine Draft is a great commercial beer. 

I like to collect vintage military firearms, being a history buff.  Old  War movies are great especially John Wayne.
