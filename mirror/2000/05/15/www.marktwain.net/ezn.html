Easy Internet is distributed free to all new customers as part of their setup fee.

Existing Customers can purchase Easy Internet for $5 which covers our cost of the CD including shipping and handling.  Send a check for $5.00 to Northeast Missouri Rural Telephone or Marktwain Rural Telephone Company and request the Easy Internet CD and instructions. 

All of the programs on the Easy Internet CD ROM are updated every two weeks to ensure we are distributing only the most current versions.  All program names are the property and trademarks of their respective owners and are used and included with permission.  

Current versions of the browsers on the CD are Internet Explorer 5.0 and Netscape Communicator 4.5. 

While there are many great programs available on the CD, we only offer support for Internet Explorer, Netscape, their associated email programs and your dial-up connection.
