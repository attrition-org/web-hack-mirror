Webwatch offers you two different presence services :

The Standard Banner Campaign:
Your banner can be displayed according to two options :
    • in a general rotation on the site or
    • on specific thematic pages –or section- chosen by you
Your presence booking can be made based on the number of impressions desired and/or specific days/dates of presence. Product category and brand exclusivity options are available as well. You’ll receive a daily campaign reporting with the number of impressions and the clicthrough ratio to the banner’s target link.
 
All bookings must be confirmed 7 days before the beginning of the campaign.
Concerning the technical details, all the campaign banners must be 468x60 pixels, maximum 7K of weight and in a .GIF or .JPG format.
 
Banners must be sent, at the latest, 3 days before the beginning of the camapign. Please send banners to the following e-mail address: advertise@webwatch.be
 
Want to advertise but you don’t have a banner ? No problem, Webwatch remains available to design and program a banner according to your creative brief.
