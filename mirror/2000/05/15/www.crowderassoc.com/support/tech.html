Support - Installed Technology

Our hosting services are provided through RapidSite's advanced network operations center, located in the U.S. It is connected to the world by three high speed DS-3 Internet connections through three separate providers: Sprint, MCI, and UUnet. This high speed connectivity combined with exceptionaly fast and reliable servers ensures your Web site will be fast and responsive, 24 hours a day, every day.

RapidSite's advanced technology features the following:

Unix Web Servers

  • Silicon Graphics Origin 200 and Challenge S Servers
  • 256 to 512 MB of RAM
  • 4 GB System Disk
  • 18 GB Striped Data Storage
  • Dual 180 MHZ RISC CPUs
  • Operating System - IRIX V6.4
  • Web Server Software - Apache V1.2

Cisco 7505 Routers

  • Internet backbone level routing

Internal Backbone

  • 1.2 GB/s Cisco Catalyst 5000 switches as backbone
  • Each server has dedicated 10 MB ports
  • Speed to router is 100 mb/s

Connectivity

  • Three (3) full fiber optic T3's to multiple redundant and diverse backbones (UUnet, MCI, and Sprint), with additional redundancy / diversity from dual building entry points and multi-homed central office provisioning. Systems monitor backbone performance and automatically send IP traffic down the shortest path using the Cisco BGP4 protocol.

Backup Power

  • All servers and communications equipment receive uninterrupted power via Deltec battery backup (UPS).
  • Backup generator can power the entire complex and all critical equipment.

Backup Storage

  • All sites are backed-up daily to a 40 TB (terabyte) robotic tape backup system by Storage Technologies which employs the latest DLT4 technology.
