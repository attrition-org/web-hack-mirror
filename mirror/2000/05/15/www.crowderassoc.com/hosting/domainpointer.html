    Note:
  • The InterNIC (the governing agency in the US) charges a registration fee of $70 for each new domain name (.com, .net, .org), which covers 2 years. After the 2nd year they will charge you $35 per year.
  • If you are requesting a non-U.S. InterNIC domain name as a pointer (not a .com, .net, .org, etc.) you will be required to register the domain name with the local InterNIC.

  • Your domain will point to an "Under Construction" page until you specify the IP address you want your Domain Pointer to point to.
  • You may change where your Domain Pointer points at any time through your online account Control Panel.
