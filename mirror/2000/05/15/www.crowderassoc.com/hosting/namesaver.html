Name Saver - Domain Parking Service

Name Saver domain parking service was created to accommodate customers who need domains registered but are not yet ready for a web site.

$40 plus NIC fees (billed separately) will reserve your domain name for up to two years.
  • Saves your domain name with no monthly hosting fee.
  • “Under Construction” default page included.
  • 1 e-mail forwarding
  • Additional years are $15 per year.
  • If the reserved domain name is upgraded to a hosting plan within two years, the hosting plan setup fee will be waived. (excludes High Volume, E-commerce, and NT plans)
