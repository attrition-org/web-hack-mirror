US Sports Hockey Schools provide young hockey players with the opportunity to get fitter, stronger, and faster. Our schools are geared to improve the skills and increase the hockey passion of each player, in a positive learning environment.

While at camp you become a better all-around player with intense training in the key fundamental hockey skills:

  • POWER AND SPEED SKATING
  • STICKHANDLING, SHOOTING/SCORING
  • OFFENSE/DEFENSE and UNSELFISH TEAM PLAY.

The US Sports Hockey School directors are some of the most renowned coaches in America, who have decades of experience in building first-rate instructional programs. The directors carefully select and train their staff, which includes other professional coaches and college players.

  
 
 

WHAT'S INCLUDED

Resident Camper- Receives a five-hour per day hockey program, 3-4 hours on ice, all meals, housing and activities.
Extended Day Camper- Arrive at 8:30 a.m. and leave at approximately 9:00 p.m. after evening activities and hockey games. The fee includes the resident camper hockey instructional program, lunch, dinner and activities.
Day Camper- Arrive at approximately 8:30 a.m. (daily schedule may vary) and leave after the hockey program has been completed.

All campers receive an official camp practice jersey, workbook/yearbook and personal hockey evaluation.
