The Permissions Department

The Permissions Department looks forward to providing you with the trademark and copyright information you require. We will respond to your specific inquiry as soon as possible, in many instances within 48 hours. Unfortunately we cannot promise a specific turnaround time due to the varied nature of the permission requests we receive.

The Permission Department at Corel encourages you to submit your permission requests via e-mail or fax. Prior to making your inquiry, please review the information contained on this Web site regarding the use of Corel Trademarks and materials protected by Copyright, such as box shots, clipart, photos, screen shots or logos.

When making a request, please provide the following information:

  • Full name
  • Company name
  • Address
  • Phone number
  • Fax number
  • E-mail address
  • Request type (i.e. box shots, clipart, photos, screen shots, logos)
  • Samples of your intended use (when possible)

Please send your request together with your contact information to the Permissions Department by one of the following methods:

  • E-mail: permissions@corel.com
  • Fax: (613) 725-2691
  • Mail: Corel Corporation
