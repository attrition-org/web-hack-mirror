Corel Print Office™ 2000 for Macintosh®
The first-ever Macintosh® version of Corel's easy to use publishing software, Corel Print Office 2000 for Macintosh is an ideal solution for small office and home users that need to create high-quality projects at an affordable price. Corel Print Office 2000 for Macintosh allows users to create eye-catching documents—brochures, business cards and more—for all their business needs. This software package also includes a file conversion utility for translating popular Macintosh word processing file formats not natively supported by Corel Print Office 2000.

Corel® Custom Photo
Give your photos amazing impact with Corel® Custom Photo. This easy-to-use photo-editing software lets you add fun effects to your favorite pictures and personalize your projects. Take advantage of more than 30 special effects—including transparencies, red-eye removal and warping—and over 10,000 striking graphics. Whether you're touching up damaged pictures, e-mailing personalized projects to friends and family, or publishing your creations to the Web, Corel Custom Photo gives you spectacular photos in a snap!

Corel® Custom Photo for Macintosh®
Corel® Custom Photo for Macintosh® provides powerful image-editing and painting tools, while remaining easy enough for novice computer users. Corel Custom Photo for Macintosh allows users to make creative photo projects such as postcards, magazine covers, coasters, sports cards, gift tags and much more. Personalized photo memories such as framed photos, personalized greeting cards and photo projects can also be sent over the Internet, or published to HTML for display on a Web page.
