A brief history of WordPerfect®

WordPerfect® Law Office 2000 (released December 20, 1999)
As the world’s only legal-specific office suite, WordPerfect Law Office 2000 comes equipped with the latest technology from all of Corel’s previous legal suite partners, as well as brand new technology in the form of DEAL PROOF™ SE, a cutting-edge technology developed by Expert Ease Software Inc. DEAL PROOF SE marries artificial intelligence and document and analysis technologies to allow users to edit, proofread and review complex documents. WordPerfect Law Office 2000 also offerd superior compatibility with older versions of WordPerfect and Microsoft® Word; compatibility with electronic filing formats such as PDF and open standards such as XML; the ability to convert to the EDGAR file format in order to electronically file documents with the US Securities and Exchange Commission; and many other features tailored specifically to legal professionals.

WordPerfect® Family Pack (released November 29, 1999)
Just in time for the holidays, Corel has developed an easy-to-use, feature-packed suite specifically tailored to novice computer users who are looking for the ideal home software package.WordPerfect Family Pack helps users create professional-looking letters, reports, newsletters, school projects, greeting cards, party decorations, certificates, keep track of home assets and the family budget, and more.

WordPerfect Family Pack is part of Corel’s vertical market segmentation strategy – building on the foundation of the WordPerfect line, and offering a package that effectively meets the needs of the home user. A study by IDC indicated that the home productivity software market, while already very prominent and growing rapidly, is still under-penetrated with very few vendors successfully meeting the needs of the home user. WordPerfect Family Pack is expressly designed to meet the needs of the home market and will be competing in the same niche as Microsoft’s Works Suite.
