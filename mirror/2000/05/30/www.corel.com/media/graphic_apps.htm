CorelDRAW™ 8 for Power Macintosh®
Designed for the Mac® OS, CorelDRAW 8 for Power Macintosh delivers an incredible collection of software for illustration, photo editing and bitmap creation. Plus, amazing compatibility features let you share files across platforms with ease.
