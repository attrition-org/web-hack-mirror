The Office of Management & Finance:

  • Serves as the management support system for the Department of Social Services.
     
  • Provides fiscal services, information services, human resources, budget, policy, planning, research, and evaluation services, and rate setting services to the Secretary, and the various offices under the Secretary.
     
  • Monitors departmental policies and procedures to assure compliance with state and federal laws.

Programs and Services 

  • Division of Fiscal Services - Provides financial, purchasing, and contracting services to DSS.
     
  • Division of Human Resources - Provides personnel management to DSS.
     
  • Division of Information Services -Provides computer support and data management for the entire department.
     
  • Division of Planning and Budget - Coordinates all activities relative to planning, legislation, and budget for the department.
     
  • Division of Rate Setting - Establishes per diem rates for 24-hour substitute care providers.
