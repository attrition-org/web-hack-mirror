Scope of the Congress

The World Congresses on Intelligent Manufacturing Processes & Systems, the First in Puerto Rico (1995) and the Second in Budapest (1997), organized by Prof. V. R. Milacic, were a continuation of activities which began in 1985 with the first International Seminar on Intelligent Manufacturing Systems organized in Dubrovnik.

The main objective of the Congress is to provide a world forum to exchange knowledge, experience, results and information related to various aspects of factory design for the future as a challenging frontier for manufacturing industry.

The scope of the Congress covers philosophical, scientific, technological and practical concepts concerning research, development and realization of Intelligent Manufacturing Process and Systems, offering a unique opportunity for experts to meet and exchange ideas in this highly relevant and exciting field.

Abstracts and Papers

Abstracts may be submitted by fax or post. They should include the title, author’s full name, affiliation, address, email address, and telephone and fax numbers. Abstracts should not exceed 300 words.

Submission of a paper implies that it has not been submitted or published elsewhere. Authors of accepted papers will be expected to sign a copyright release to allow publication of the proceedings.
You can find guidelines for the formatting of the abstracts and papers here.

At least one author of each paper is required to present the paper at the Congress. Fax submissions should be sent to 617-258-7488. Email submissions should be sent to Ms. Pat Smethurst at smethurs@mit.edu

 

Submitting your Paper

The preferred form of submission for the papers is via e-mail. Please take a look at the guidelines for the format here.

When submitting your Paper electronically, please name the file as follows: name_last.doc or name_last.pdf, where name is replaced with the first name of the corresponding author and last is replaced with his/her last name. The paper from Carlos Tapia should be named carlos_tapia.doc or carlos_tapia.pdf.

At least one author of each paper is required to register and present the paper at the Congress. Email submissions should be sent to Ms. Pat Smethurst at smethurs@mit.edu

 

Production System Design Workshop

The Workshop will focus on the design of "lean" production systems from an integrated engineering and management perspective. The session will highlight machine design for systems and the alignment of performance measures with enterprise objectives. The fee for the one-day Production System Design Workshop is $175.00.
