Evaluation Steps
1. Download Lucy The full production software is available at this web site at no cost.
     
2. Install Lucy You are licensed to install Lucy on as many computers as you like. By involving more people in an evaluation, you get more useful feedback.
     
3. Use Lucy All of the conversion functionality is available for you to use. You can use your own drawings or the sample drawings we provide. There is no limit on time, drawing size, or the number of conversions you can perform during your evaluation of Lucy. In fact, we encourage you to put Lucy to the test in a true production scenario.
     
4. Export your conversion results In order to complete your evaluation of Lucy, you will probably want to export your own conversion results to DXF or IGES. Get your 10 free Conversion Units to enable a limited number of Export and/or Print functions.
     
5. Purchase When you are convinced that Lucy will save you time (and money), you can purchase a Seat License or Conversion Units  by calling us at 1-877-LUCY-CUS. We will give you a unique key code that will enable Lucy to print and/or export conversion results to standard CAD interchange file formats. Any work that you saved to Lucy's proprietary file format during your evaluation can now be printed and exported to IGES or DXF.
