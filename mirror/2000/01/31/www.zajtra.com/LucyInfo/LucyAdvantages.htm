What does "Lucy" mean?

 

Lucy Advantages

 

Accurate Geometry Recognition Lucy automatically recognizes line, circle, circular arc, ellipse, elliptical arc and spline geometric entities, even when crossing other entities.
Intelligent Text Recognition Lucy uses Intelligent Character Recognition to recognize machine generated and hand-lettered text scattered anywhere on a drawing or map and oriented arbitrarily. You can extend character libraries by teaching Lucy new character definitions.
Rectification Lucy mathematically corrects near conditions to CAD quality. You can have Lucy snap directional entities horizontally and vertically along coordinate axes, snap definition points to a grid, intersect entities at a single point, and intersect tangent entities with continuous slope.
Direct Manipulation Lucy eliminates tweaking of entities by trial and error.  You can directly create and edit geometry and text entities by tracing raster data.
Productive Clean-up Tools Lucy's clean-up functions minimize human effort in reviewing and fine-tuning the results from automatic recognition. Clean-up tools target the unique tasks associated with raster to vector conversion. You can change entity types, break, join, conform, offset, trim and rectify entities. Lucy also tracks and facilitates entity validation to eliminate duplicate work.
Two Pricing Options You can choose the plan that best suits your needs.Our Per Seat Plan is typically most cost-effective for dedicated usage of Lucy on one or a limited number of computers. Our Per Drawing Plan is more cost-effective for low volume usage or when installing Lucy on a large number of seats
