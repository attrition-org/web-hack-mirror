Together with Student Wise and Zappa Sumbuca, RSFM and Reds on Campus are to host the Free State regional coinage championships.   Last years winner, Wayne Van Rensburg, is back to defend his title.The winner gets R3000 and a place in the final, which is held in Cape Town.

PLACE : Reds on Campus

WHEN : Friday 21st August 1998

M.C. : RSFM's Brek-farce D.J.  WARREN MANNING.    

FEEDBACK

The night was very successful, it started with the first round (a knockout round ) with 6 to each table we had over over 40 entrants. The first round lasted 20mins. The winner fom each table gained a place in the semmi-final from which 4 finalists came through. The final  lived up to the Zappa motto, GO BIG OR GO HOME, Wayne, last years finalist, was once again there showing how it is done. 3 of the finalists did eventualy decide to call it a night before the time was up!! Leaving a winner for the 1998 Zappa Sambuca coinage regional champs, good-luck to him in the national finals in Cape Town. What made this evening extra big was the crowd participation, especialy in the final.

Winner:  Aldrich Barnard
