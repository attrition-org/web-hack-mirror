TITLEIST AND FOOTJOY WORLDWIDE, celebrating its 11th anniversary as the AJGA's National Sponsor, has been the catalyst and driving force behind the American Junior Golf Association's success. In 1989, after eight years of product sponsorship, Titleist and FootJoy Worldwide signed on as the AJGA's National Sponsor. As a result of Titleist and FootJoy Worldwide's commitment to junior golf, the AJGA has been able to improve its services each year to meet the needs of its growing membership.
