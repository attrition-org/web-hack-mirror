The AJGA Foundation is a 501 (c)(3) nonprofit organization and is the designated fund-raising agency of the American Junior Golf Association.

Since its inception in 1982, grants from the Foundation have provided assistance to numerous grass-roots junior golf programs across the country. Click HERE to see the 1999 Beneficiaries of AJGA Foundation Donations. The Foundation is also dedicated to helping secure the long-term growth and future of the AJGA.

Making this possible are the many individuals and organizations that contribute to the AJGA Foundation. The following programs have played a major role in the Foundation's success:

AJGA 1% Club and Matching Sponsors

Founded in 1988, the AJGA 1% Club offers a proven opportunity for all professional golfers to show their support for junior golf by donating one percent of their official Tour earnings to the AJGA Foundation. Members of the 1% Club give back to the game and help provide opportunities for junior golfers.

As AJGA 1% Club Matching Sponsors, individuals, golf clubs and corporations also contribute by matching the contribution of their favorite Tour Player.

To see the current members of the 1% Club, click HERE.

Giving Program

AJGA alumni, parents, coaches, clubs, associations and corporations make annual contributions to the Foundation through the AJGA's Giving Program. Giving Program members are able to assist the growth of junior golf while keeping up to date on the activities of the AJGA. Please contact AJGA Headquarters about becoming a Giving Program member.
