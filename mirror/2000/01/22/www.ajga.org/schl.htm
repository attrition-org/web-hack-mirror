PING American College Golf Guide
The PING Guide contains vital information on golf programs at over 1,000 colleges and junior colleges, with answers to many of the questions facing college-bound students. There is a step-by-step procedure along with a calendar of what you should do from your freshman through your senior year, and it contains sample letters and recommendations on writing resumes. The Guide also gives complete scores of over 92 college conference championship tournaments plus scores of regional and national college golf championships.

AJGA Player Profile

The AJGA Player Profile is a tool the AJGA is providing to its College Bound Student membership to aid in securing college golf scholarships. The players simply complete a form provided by the AJGA that will allow juniors to highlight their academic and golf achievements. This form will be electronically distributed to college golf coaches selected by the AJGA member.

In addition to highlighting the junior's achievements in the classroom and on the golf course, the players will be able to indicate their upcoming tournament schedule as well as their college preferences.

The AJGA Player Profile will be updated throughout the year and, in September, an up-to-date profile and tournament results will be sent to the college programs in which the junior has expressed interest. Further, if a coach requests information on a player, it will immediately be made available to the coach.

GOLFSTAT College Resource Information

For high school and junior golfers interested in playing at the collegiate level, GOLFSTAT's Prep Report is the most valuable resource currently available. This report provides each university's rating by division; tournaments and rounds played; representation in Regionals and/or Nationals by individual or team; rounds played, scoring average, year in school for the five most utilized players; and a breakdown of players on roster by year in school.
