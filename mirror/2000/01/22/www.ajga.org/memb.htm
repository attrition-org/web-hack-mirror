The AJGA offers memberships to junior golfers and college golf coaches:
Junior Membership

Membership is open to male and female junior golfers ages 18 or younger who are not currently attending college. Juniors may become members before their 13th birthday; however, they must be at least 13 years of age to participate in an AJGA event. Junior membership expires Dec. 31, 2000. High school graduates who are age eligible may participate throughout the calendar year immediately following high school graduation or until the first day of classes at their particular college or university. Coaching Membership

The AJGA Coaching Membership, added in 1997, is a vital tool for college coaches. For its 2000 Coaching Members, the AJGA provides the following:

  • Access to the AJGA Player Profile Database
  • 2000 Membership Roster
  • Results from all tournaments and qualifiers distributed electronically
  • Other important information about the AJGA and its junior members.
