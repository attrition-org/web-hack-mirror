Izzo Cup Rankings

The second Izzo Cup between the United States and Sweden will be held June 26-30, 2000, at Ullna Golf Club in Stockholm, Sweden. The top seven boys and girls will be selected to the U.S. team. Points, which are based on the Golfweek/Titleist Junior Rankings, are accrued from Jan. 1 through Dec. 31, 1999 and determined by top finishes in designated tournaments.
