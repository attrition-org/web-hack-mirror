Background:

   Our clinic was established in 1988 as a Sexual and Reproductive Health Service rendered in a confidential and caring environment. Through specially trained professional staff we provide the best quality or care possible for all our patients. Confidentiality is a prime concern for both patient and staff. Since February 1997 we have been performing termination of pregnancy with compassion understanding and complete confidentiality.

Location:

   Our clinic is conveniently situated just off the Republic and William Nicol Drive intersection in Sandton, Johannesburg.

Hours:

   The clinic is open between 08h00 and 17h00 every day or after hours by special arrangement. We work on an appointment basis only so there is no unnecessary queuing.

Fees:

   Fees vary for different services. Payment is made prior to the procedure. We accept cash and credit cards only.

Services:

   We offer the following services:

  • Family planning
  • Pregnancy crisis counselling
  • Sexual dysfunction counselling
  • Sexually transmitted diseases treatment
  • HIV/AIDS testing and counselling
  • Termination of pregnancy
  • Paternity testing
  • Rape counselling
