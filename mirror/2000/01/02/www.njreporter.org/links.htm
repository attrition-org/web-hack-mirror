Government

New Jersey Home Page: The official -- and most comprehensive -- New Jersey web site. Maintained by New Jersey state government.

http://www.state.nj.us/index.htm

New Jersey State Library's CyberDesk: Particularly useful is link to "New Jersey Government Publications on the Web," which provides an index and links to several hundred state government reports published on the web.

http://atlantic.njsl.tesc.edu/webpac/

New Jersey Legislature Home Page: Bills, legislative agendas, committee lists and other legislative information available on-line.

http://www.njleg.state.nj.us/

New Jersey Public Policy Institutes

New Jersey Policy Perspective: Public policy research organization headed by Jon Shure, adviser to former Governor Florio, that issues reports focused primarily on economic and tax policy. Dedicated to "progressive principles" and an "activist, ambitious public sector."

http://tap.epn.org/njpp/

Public Affairs Research Institute of New Jersey: Founded in 1930, PARI provides comprehensive data and analysis of demographic, business and social trends in New Jersey, with a focus on the interaction of public policies and the private sector. Economist Don Linky heads the institute.

http://www.nj.com/pari/

Other Resources

Governing Magazine: The best magazine on comparative policy trends in state and local government. Major articles from the past year available under "Highlights of Current and Recent Issues."

http://www.governing.com/main.htm

Government Executive Magazine: Updated on-line daily, this magazine and its web site are a leading source of information on the inner policy workings of the federal government. Particularly strong in its coverage of "reinventing government" and procurement and technology issues.

http://www.govexec.com/
