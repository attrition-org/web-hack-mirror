The Domain of Northern Virginia
The Domain of Northern Virginia rose to prominence within the Camarilla shortly after its establishment under the auspices of Prince Genna Xavias.  Though the Domain has faced numerous challenges through out the years, it has remained in the forefront of Camarilla politics for quite some time.
Unlike some of her neighboring and sister Domains, unsavory elements like the Sabbat, or even the Anarchs, have found no home or quarter in NVA.  Though the citizens of  the Domain have fought numerous wars against the Sabbat, Garou, and other assorted enemies, the Domain has remained steadfastly Camarilla.
Throughout the years Northern Virginia began to develop a reputation as a very accepting and tolerant domain, noted for it's large number of Independent residents and relaxed attitude.  However, the Domain is far from perfect.  From spies at the highest level of City Office to internal struggle and strife within the Clans, Northern Virginia has always held its own nervous edge.
With sister domains in Baltimore, Southern Maryland, and Washington DC, Northern Virginia remains the oldest southern stronghold of the Camarilla along the East Coast, serving as the first point of contact for the Sabbat and Garou working up through the South-Eastern United States.
Though it has had it's troubles, Northern Virginia remains much like it was when founded so long ago...
Proud,
Loyal,
Camarilla.
