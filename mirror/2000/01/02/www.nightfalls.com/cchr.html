Character Creation & House Rules
 
All Night...Falls characters must be made in accordance with the Laws of the Night, Third Edition.  Player Characters must be of the seven main clans (brujah, gangrel, malkavian, nosferatu, toreador, tremere, ventrue) unless given Storyteller permission.  Caitiff are also subject to ST approval.  Paths other than humanity must be ST approved.  No PC character may be lower than 8th generation, and generation is also subject to ST approval.  All new characters must be checked out, okayed, and signed by a ST before entering play.  A current and updated copy of your character sheet should also always be on file with the ST's.
