Tired of Managing Multiple Vendors?

If you are like most businesses, you are managing a series of technology vendors each with their own area of expertise. You may have a software supplier, a hardware vendor, another vendor for communications and probably a series of consultants that you call on from time to time to solve unique business problems.

Have you noticed that many vendors rarely plan and implement technology as a team? This leaves you to keep the line of communication open between your vendors. Each vendor may be proficient with his own products and services, but probably knows very little about your total requirements. Should you experience a problem it is often difficult to determine the solution. Most vendors are not willing to accept responsibility for solving the problem and blame the other vendors leaving you with a technology nightmare. And this only gets worse if you have multiple offices with different vendors.

 

So, How Do You Avoid This Situation?

Good You become an expert in technology and dedicate considerable resources to project management and vendor communications.
Better You share project management and technology expertise with CSB Systems, assigning specific projects to CSB Systems you feel are beyond your scope or resources.
Best You let CSB Systems fully manage all your information needs and you concentrate on improving customer service.
