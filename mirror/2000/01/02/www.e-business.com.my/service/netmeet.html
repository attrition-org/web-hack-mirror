Microsoft Netmeeting 2.0 is a product that features all six most-wanted collaboration tools such as audio, video, file transfer, chat, document / application sharing, and whiteboard.  

To you all non-technical persons it means you can chat, talk over the internet using NetMeeting as a internet phone.

Guess what ! You will be able to meet new cyber friends on the net. 

With a whiteboard you could illustrate ideas by pasting graphics files or a screen capture of what's on your computer. 

The best part of NetMeeting is that it is FREE. So if you don't have a copy of it, download it now.
