RICHMOND FIRST CERTIFICATE COURSE
A comprehensive course to prepare students for the Cambridge First Certificate in English examination written specifically for the revised 1996 syllabus.

Unit Monitor: focuses on the topics found in the unit so as to monitor it and brings together vocabulary related to these topic areas

Exam Advise: the 22 detailed sections provide students with comprehensive information about how to approach the different exam tasks

FCE Plus: this integrated workbook provides supplementary exercises based on the grammar from the unit and plenty of replica exam practice exercises for Papers 1, 2 and 3
