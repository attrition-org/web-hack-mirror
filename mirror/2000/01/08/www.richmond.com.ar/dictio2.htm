RICHMOND PICTURE DICTIONARY
The Richmond Picture Dictionary is an ideal resource for children in their first years of learning English. It has been designed to teach vocabulary and the meaning of words through the medium of pictures.

contains some 1,500 everyday words organised around key topic areas and consisting of: over 1,000 nouns, 250 verbs and more than 100 adjectives

features an 82-page illustrated dictionary containing over 1,500 full colour images of excepcional clarity and detail

includes a comprehensive word list with clear explanations of all the vocabulary featured in the illustrated pages.
 

Some key topic areas included in this visually supported material:

  • the body
  • the city
  • plants
  • animals
  • jobs
  • health
  • the Universe and the Earth
