YOUR TURN NEXT
True beginner to Pre-Intermediate
This three-level series for 3rd. Cycle EGB focusses on learners using the language. This improved edition of Your Turn has been tested and student approved.
 
The wide variety of exercises and activities in Your Turn Next caters for different learning styles. All students will find activities which are compatible with their preferred mode of learning based on multi-ability class approach.

To use the language effectively learners need to understand and feel confident of the basic structures of English and have a firm grasp of vocabulary relevant to the task and exercises.

Learners study structures and functions in detail, with an emphasis on the cognitive aspect: completing examples, building up grammar tables and working out rules of form and use.

Activities are complemented with extra reading, writing and listening skilis practice in the sections "Across Cultures", ·Cross Curricular", and "Resource File".

Pair and group work provides a medium to develope the learners' experience of collaboration, mutual respect and support.

There are Hints in the ,teacher's notes which provide procedures to follow and guidance on a variety of classroom management techniques.
