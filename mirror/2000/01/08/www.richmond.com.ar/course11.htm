FREEFORM
1 & 2  Split Edition
Freeform is a course for classes having two or three periods per week or 70 hours a year.
 
Freeform takes students up to intermediate level and is especially designed for mixed level classes. Motivating topics and activities sustain interest and challenge the students to think about the way in which they are learning the language.

it help students to communicate from the beginning by focusing on their own experiences

it provides suitable material for large classes having two or three periods per week

it incorporates activities to the textbook
