FILE
Pre-Intermediate / Intermediate / Upper Intermediate.
File is designed to take learners across the intermediate plateau, at the same time preparing them for a First Certificate Examination course.
 

introduces and practises key areas of grammar, vocabulary and pronunciation in clearly signposted "files"

provides presentation and practice which allows learners to draw on their own experiences and use language in a wide variety of context

encourages learners to systematically check, categorise and memorise new language

includes regular view units which reflect a local or international level

includes additional material in the Self-Study Workbook and a linked cassette for the learner working at home.
