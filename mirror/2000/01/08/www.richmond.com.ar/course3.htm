GOOD TIMES
Levels 1, 2, 3 & 4
Good Times is a four level course based on fables, legends, tales of mystery and ghost stories. With its interactive focus, it provides an innovative, and effective way of teaching English to children.
  

maximum flexibility for the multi-level classroom
 an interactive story in each unit
 a variety of game-related activities
 cards and handicrafts
 story cards for presenting and creating strip cartoons
 developement of oral comprehension
