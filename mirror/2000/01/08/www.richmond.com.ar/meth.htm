RICHMOND HANDBOOKS FOR TEACHERS
A highly practical series of methodology guides,
written by teachers for teachers.

  they provide practical, realistic suggestions for the classroom,

  • photocopiable activity worksheets
  • developement tasks for the teacher and
  • brief explanations of theory

  they take into account everyday problems of teachers in foreign language classrooms,

  • diversity within the class and
  • current educational reforms and issues
