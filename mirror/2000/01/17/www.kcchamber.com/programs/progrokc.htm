GRO-KC

"A new business moves to town and out comes the welcome mat. What do you do for businesses already located here?" Meet GRO-KC, the only metropolitan economic development program for existing businesses. GRO-KC addresses the issues facing established businesses throughout the metro area, including labor and training, real estate and development, government issues and infrastructure. This includes:

Executive-to-executive visits to learn what growth issues are facing area businesses;

Job Track, a year-long research project to where and in what industries job growth is occurring;

Seal of Growth, a spotlight program to recognize growth in area businesses; and

GROWTH, a quarterly newsletter highlighting the economic and job growth among metro area businesses.
