The Greater Kansas City Chamber of Commerce's Business Resource Center is the place for information in your research and business planning. Through the Business Resource Center the Chamber seeks to provide its members a wealth of current and accurate information on a variety of subjects. The center also provides general information on Kansas City for those looking to relocate through the Chambers many informational publications.
