Q11. How can I contact my Member of Congress?

Keep in mind, that due to limited staff resources, Members of Congress place a high priority on responding to the constituents whom they represent. Also, not all Representatives and Senators have constituent access via Electronic Mail or Internet Web sites.

You can contact your Member of Congress as follows:

  1. Via Internet Web site/Electronic Mail:
    • U.S. House of Representatives - Go to the Write Your Representative Web site.
    • U.S. Senate - Go to the Senate E-Mail Web site.

    Additional Web sites providing E-Mail access to Members (and occasionally committees) are available through the Library of Congress' Legislative Branch page.

  2. Via telephone: Dial the United States Capitol switchboard at 202-224-3121 and indicate the name of the Senator or Representative whose Office you wish to speak to.

  3. Via U.S. Postal Mail::
    • U.S. House of Representatives address:

      Office of (Name)
      United States House of Representatives
      Washington, DC 20515

    • U.S. Senate address:

      Office of (Name)
      United States Senate
      Washington, DC 20510
