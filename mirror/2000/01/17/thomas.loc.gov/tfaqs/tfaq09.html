Q9. Where on the Internet can I find information about my state/local government

This information is available at non-THOMAS web sites. For example, to view U.S. state and territorial Laws visit the House of Representatives Internet Law Library. Also, under "Library of Congress Web Links" near the bottom left of the THOMAS homepage is a link you will find containing a wealth of information for State/Local Governments.
