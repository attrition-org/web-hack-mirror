2000 CISA Advanced Racing Clinic
April 15-18, 1999

Sponsor California International Sailing Association
Instructors Peter Wells, Mike Zani, Nick Adamson, JJ Isler, Skip Whyte, Morgan Larson, Kevin Hall, Jaime Malm, Andy Zinn, Carissa Harris and other qualified instructors
Location Alamitos Bay Yacht Club 7201 E. Ocean Blvd., Long Beach, CA

Program		 The program will consist of lectures and on-the-water drills including tactics, sail trim, etc., together with information on what is required for an olympic campaign in the form of money, time, travel, and personal effort.

This clinic will be held in Lasers, Laser Radials, 420's, Club FJ's, and 29ers.  Some boats may be available for charter to out-of-area applicants.  (charter fees additional)

Applicants 13 years or older and a current member of U.S. Sailing Assn. will be selected based upon their resume.  This is a very intense high level program.  Participants will be limited.

Selection is by application and acceptance only.  This years clinic will be limited to a small select number.  Make applications complete and mail early.
Entry Fee $300 per Laser or Radial.  $600 per 420, Club FJ or 29er.  ($300 per sailor).  Cost includes 3 dinners; lunches may be ordered at the club.
Applications To be considered, a check made payable to CISA must be included.
