Fast Find Search Help and Tips

  • When you enter more than one word, only ads that contain all the words you entered will be found.
  • You can enter any number of words, but:
    • words must be at least 2 characters long
    • words can contain any letter or number and the characters & - . , ' / $
  • Generally, do not use plurals ("computer" will bring up ads that contain the word "computers" but the word "computers" will not bring up ads containing "computer").
  • You don't have to type the entire word. The character string "compu" will bring up all ads containing the words "computer" and "computers."
