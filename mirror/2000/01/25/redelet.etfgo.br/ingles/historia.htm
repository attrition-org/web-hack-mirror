The Latin-American Data Communication of Technological Education Network - RedeLet, was created in 1989 and implemented in 1992 with the objective of receive and distribute informations about the technological education in Brazil and the others Latin-American countries. The RedeLET is managed by the SEMTEC - Secretaria Nacional de Educação Média e Tecnológica (Secretaryship of the National Education Ministery - MEC), abling the users to reach out informations about the Federal Technological Education Institutions - IFET and , also, becoming available the events schedules, valid and developed researches at the technological area, and the Brazilian Technological Education legislation.

The RedeLET is one project of the Multinational Program for the Work Education - PMET, of the Organization of American States - OEA.
