You could win a lovely set of the Queen's Own Cameron Highlanders Color Party made by Martin Tabony, read on to find out how...

Martin Tabony has kindly given us this set as an official launch of his new venture in the United States. Martin has teamed up with dealer and collector Mark Moriarty, who will now be acting as Martin's main agent in the United States. I'm sure this will please many American collectors because one of Martin's problems, until now, has been supplying collectors quickly enough. Martin has already said this problem will be cured now by Mark keeping a full inventory of figures in the United States, thus allowing collectors much quicker access to the figures they require.

The set of figures Martin has supplied as a prize is fantastic. Since this set was painted especially for this competition, it appears he has put in even more detail than usual. As always, Martin's figures are of an exceptional quality and I'm sure any reader would be more than delighted to be able to add this set to his or her collection.
