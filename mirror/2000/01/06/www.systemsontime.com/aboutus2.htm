Our Services:
Systems OnTime Inc. is located in Toronto and provides services in the area of Computer Systems Development, Maintenance and Consulting.  Our services include:

1.  Providing contract IT professionals to meet your project requirements.

  • Project Management
  • System Analysis & Design
  • Programming

2.  Placement of permanent IT staff.

3.  Developing and implementing complete systems solutions for small and large businesses.

4.  Maintaining and enhancing existing systems.


About Us:
Since 1996, Systems OnTime has been supplying our clients with IT resources.  When you meet someone from Systems OnTime, you can be confident that you are meeting with someone who has years of industry experience and is able to understand your requirements.   Systems OnTime is run by individuals who are enthusiastic about technology; in order to keep up to date on the latest IT news, we are involved with several user groups and associations. 

A Commitment To Our Clients:
At Systems OnTime we take pride in our ability to provide excellent, technically qualified individuals, both contract and permanent who will meet your needs.  Since all our recruiters have a technical background and were once programmers or are still involved in the technical field, we can understand your needs from a systems perspective.

We source our candidates from a database of approximately 20,000 IT professionals and this pool continues to grow.  Obviously our database contains a mix of talents - some more talented than others.  Therefore, before sending any contractor to our clients we initiate a rigorous interview process involving a meeting with the candidates, an aptitude and IT skills test, and finally, verifying their references.  We aim to provide you with systems professionals you can rely on to deliver your projects on time.  This is an uncompromising standard at Systems OnTime.
