Search: Visit this feature to search the contents of this site and find specific information you may need.

Background: HCFA's web site is divided into twelve Medicare and Medicaid-related topic areas. These topic areas range from Public Affairs Information and Publications to Statistics and Data.

In several of the topic areas, information is divided into two tracks: Consumer Information and Professional/Technical Information. This system was designed to help the user quickly locate the information he or she needs.
