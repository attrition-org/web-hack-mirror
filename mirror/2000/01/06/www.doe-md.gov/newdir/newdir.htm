New Directions, is Mound’s newsletter for stakeholders interested in the cleanup and reuse of the Mound Facility.  Our newsletter features news and information from the Environmental Restoration, Decontamination and Decommissioning, Waste Management, and other Mound facility programs. New Directions is a picture window to all Mound’s cleanup activity, including the Miami-Erie Canal cleanup action, PRS 66 Follow-up, and notice of all stakeholder meetings and special events. It is available both online at the MEMP web page and by mail.
