A note about our site...

This site is best viewed with a current version of either

 Netscape Navigator or


 Microsoft Internet Explorer.

In addition, graphics will look their best if your computer is running in 256-colour mode or better. We have attempted to ensure that this site is accessible by the majority of web browsers. However, if you are not using one of the above programs, you may notice that some buttons do not function or that certain text may appear garbled.

If you have any questions or comments, please e-mail webmaster@www.dentistry.ubc.ca.
