PADDLE THE ANCIENT CANOE ROUTE OF FIRST CANADIANS --Enjoy Beautiful Fall Colours at

“Paddle the Humber” FALL EVENT, Sunday September 20th

       
Sponsored by the Royal Bank Financial Group.
  
The Toronto and Region Conservation Authority (Gary Wilkins), in partnership with the City of Toronto (Allan Crawford), Ontario Marathon Canoe Racing Association (Ian Craig) and the Etobicoke Chamber of Commerce (Randall K. Reid) are hosting a Fall Paddle the Humber event at King's Mill Park, in the City of Toronto.

All levels of canoe enthusiasts are welcome to explore this heritage waterway between the Humber River Marshes and Bloor Street, south of the Old Mill Dam. Paddle where First Nations,  the French and English settlers once travelled.
