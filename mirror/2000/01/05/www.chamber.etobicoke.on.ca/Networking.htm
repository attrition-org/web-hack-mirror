Networking .... your opportunity to make new business contacts!
As a member of the Chamber you can take advantage of the variety of opportunities to mingle with many business leaders in your community, giving you the business exposure you need to grow.
Events sponsored by member businesses provide excellent networking opportunities to showcase one's products and services.
Social events are organized throughout the year, such as the Annual Golf Tournament, Day at the Races, Boat Cruises on Lake Ontario, Company Tours, Holiday Season Festivities, are perfect opportunities to relax, unwind and have some fun with your business peers.
The Etobicoke Chamber of Commerce  encourages community participation and supports such local intiatives as The Gatehouse located in South Etobicoke shown below.

The Gatehouse  is committed to providing children, youth, their families and adult survivors who have experienced abuse, with community response that is centered on their needs, both immediate and long-term. Call Lisa Marucci, Manager of The Gatehouse for more information at 416-255-5900.
