Why should you join the

Etobicoke Chamber of

Commerce?
"Membership doesn't cost...
IT'S AN INVESTMENT"
The Etobicoke Chamber of Commerce Members are making an investment in the future of business in Etobicoke and the Greater Toronto Area.
All levels of government must be made to listen to needs of business, and the Chamber is speaking out on your behalf. Invest in your future by joining the Chamber of Commerce.
As the voice of business in Etobicoke, the Chamber is committed to:
Making government more responsive to the needs of business.
Acting as the advocate for business in Etobicoke.
Encouraging dialogue within our business community
Fostering ecomonic prosperity and quality of life in our area
Representing your business interests
