BCSCA Conference Information

Team Aquatic Supplies
British Columbia Swim Coaches Conference

Whistler
British Columbia, Canada

 

International Conference for coaches of all levels.

Speakers & Presenters...

Bill Sweetenham - Four time Australian Olympic Coach, Five time Australian Commonwealth Games Coach, and Australian Youth Development Coach.

Peter Banks - Head Coach of the Blue Wave Swim Team located in Brandon, Florida. Was Assistant Naitonal Coach for Ireland in 1988. Coach of 1996 Olympic Gold Medalist and 1998 World Champion Brooke Bennett.

Dave Johnson - Swimming Canada's Director of High Performance Swimming. Head Coach of Canada's National Swimming Team.

Ken Radford - Swimming Canada's Director of Swimming -Technical Director

Ken Mikkelsen - Chiropractor for Canada's National Swimming Team

Peter Watson - Profiles International

 

Location....

The conference will take place at the Coast Whistler Resort, in the heart of Whistler Village. Enjoy all the guest services the Coast Whistler Resort has to offer for a special conference price of $99.00 for single, double, triple, or quadrupple occupancy. (plus taxes).

Contact 1-800-663-5644 to book your accommodation today
(after August 31 1999 rooms are subject to availability)

 

Conference Benefits....

In addition to the education sessions, several activities will be taking place over the weekend.

BCSCA AGM - Thursday, September 30 1999 at the Coast Whistler Resort. Come on out and be part of the strongest coaching association in the country

BC Swimming Awards Banquet - Saturday, October 2 1999 at the Whistler Conference Center. The BCSCA is proud to join Swim BC and the BC Swim Officials Association for the Second Annual BC Swimming Awards Banquet. Awards will include the BCSCA Coach of the Year and the Community Administrator Award sponsored by Investors Group and supported by the Coaching Association of Canada. The evening will also include the Second Annual Hall of Fame Induction Ceremony.


Conference attendees will receive a full pre- and post-conference package, ticket for the awards banquet, reduced room rate at the Coast Whistler Resort , a free golf shirt, morning breakfast of muffins, juice, and coffee, and a weekend of world leading presentations.
