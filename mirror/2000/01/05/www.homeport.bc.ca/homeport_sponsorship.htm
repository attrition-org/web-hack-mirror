If you are considering establishing a web-site but don't know where to start, we can help.  The internet is proving to be a popular means of reaching potential customers in Greater Vancouver, the Pacific NorthWest, and worldwide, but it, like other means of reaching customers, must have a clear message, and must reach its target audience.   Simply posting some pages on an unknown site will generate little or no customer traffic.  Successful "location" on the internet requires proper page design, registration with search engines, the development of links with other sites, and proximity to other related sites.
