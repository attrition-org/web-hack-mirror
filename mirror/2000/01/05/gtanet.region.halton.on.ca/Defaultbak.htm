GTA Network Vision

To develop a world class telecommunications network across the GTA which would promote the efficiency, effectiveness, and world class visibility of the GTA as a focus for government service, business, tourism, and the arts.

Strategic Objectives

  • World-wide visibility of the GTA as a global market place
  • The GTA - a Virtual Government focused on Service Delivery and Community Development
  • The GTA connected through a seamless and accessible network
