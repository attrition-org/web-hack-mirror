The Ontario College of Teachers has responsibilities ranging from professional development and accreditation to investigation and discipline. The professional misconduct regulation can be read on-line, as well as our 1998 Annual Report.

The College has a broad mandate that will impact virtually every aspect of the teaching profession in the province.

Take a moment to meet some key staff members and find out who does what. From time to time, job opportunities will become available at the College. They will be posted on this site, together with details on how to apply.

We hope you find this web site interesting and informative. If you’d like further information on any aspect of the College, we invite you to contact us at the address and telephone numbers shown on our What's New Page.
