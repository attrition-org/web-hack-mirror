The College Council is the governing body, or board of directors, for the Ontario College of Teachers. It makes decisions about the regulation of teaching in Ontario in accordance with the Ontario College of Teachers Act.

The 31-member Council and its 11 committees develop and approve College policies. Elections to the Council occur every three years. Members of the College elect 17 of the 31 members. The remaining 14 members of Council are public representatives appointed by the provincial government. Council meetings are open to the public.
