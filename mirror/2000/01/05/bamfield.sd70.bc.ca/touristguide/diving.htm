Diving:

 

        Dive the pristine waters of Barkley Sound for exciting memories. Many prolific sites are within one hour from Bamfield. The waters here are brimming with life: octopus, wharf eels, seals and sea lions, colorful exotic nudibranchs, and a wide variety of fish. For the more adventurous there are interesting wreck dives like the Vanlene and the Thiepval.
