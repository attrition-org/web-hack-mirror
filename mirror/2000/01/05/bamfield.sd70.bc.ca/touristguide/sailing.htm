Sailing:

       

        Every afternoon a perfect light breeze blows across Barkley Sound. Pack a lunch and enjoy a few hours sailing in Trevor Channel and around the Deer Group of islands (Pictured above at sunset). Soaring eagles, small deer, beachcombing black bears, frolicking seals and sea lions are often seen. If you’re in the right place at the right time you may have passed by a pod of Orcas or huge grey whales. What a wonderful way to experience beautiful Barkley Sound.
