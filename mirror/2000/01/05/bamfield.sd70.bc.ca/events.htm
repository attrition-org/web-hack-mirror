CAP Bamfield Events

        Community Access Bamfield currently offers Web Development training and resources, Year 2000 Awareness Seminars, and Kids Computer Camps.  We also provide free access (and assistance) to internet, office software, email, scanners, and printers.

CAP Site hours are 9:00am - 4:00pm Monday to Friday and 7:00pm - 9:00pm Monday - Thursday nights.

Year 2000 awareness seminars are no longer available.

        There are no kids courses or web site development classes currently scheduled, but interest can be expressed by contacting    Linda Myres, Bamfield Community School Coordinator.   Previous kids courses have included the use of internet email and the basics of web page design.  See the web pages produced by 8-12 year olds.

Notice: Individual persons and non-profit organizations resident in Bamfield, Kildonan, or Anacla will be provided with web space on the CAP server.   Business or profit ventures interested in free site hosting must inquire before receiving permission.
