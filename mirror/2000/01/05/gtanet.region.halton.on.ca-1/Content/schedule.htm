This page contains a list of scheduled committee events, meeting, and key milestones and deliverables.

In addition, we maintain the following prioritized lists of what we're working on this summer, and what we plan to do in the fall:

Summer 1996

  1. Conduct telephone survey of all 35 GTA municipalities to determine current status of GTA Internet connectivity, specifically for eMail. Itentify domain names, user ID format, mail package, Internet service provider, etc. for all connected sites. Determine connectivity plans for those municipalities who are not yet connected.
  2. Research the feasibility, implications, costs, benifits and alternatives of providing a common Internet eMail directory for the GTA (or far all Ontario Municipalities). Possible providers for such service include AMO/Municom, Bell Global Solutions, and Management Board/Go-Net.
  3. Consider and recommend any actions, if any, which might be required to enhance Internet connectivity among those GTA sites that have (or will have) their own connections is place, as well as for any GTA municipality with no immediate internal plans.
  4. Conduct telephone survey of all 35 GTA municipalities to determine current status of Web Site development, including URLs and a general summary of content, format, etc.

 

Fall 1996

  1. Research the feasibility, implications, costs, benifits, and alternatives for a common GTA Web Site, with links to all specific municipal sites within the GTA. I believe (Jim Andrew) there are may already be some activity or commitment on this front from Economic Development people. AMO/Municom also provides a directory and links to all Ontario municipal Web Sites.
  2. Consider the possibility and practical limitations associated with possible recommendations re, any common format or conttent for GTA Web Sites. I am not optimistic that a broad common format is either necessay or desireable among independent and, ultimately, competing organizations. However, there may be some appropriate recommendations of things which can be added to GTA Web Sites to enhance the overall GTA Web presence.

Milestones and Deliverables

  • Summer `96 -- Get GTANet: Internet site up an running
  • The GTA Net is fully supported and promoted by its stakeholders;
  • The GTA Net is widely recognized as a successful venture in inter-organization cooperation;
  • The GTA Net has a strong presence in the locally, provincially, nationally and internationally;
  • The GTA Net Core Team feels well informed about other related initiatives;
  • The GTA Net Core Team has the necessary input from stakeholders and consumers to affect the GTA Net's development accordingly;
  • Stakeholder and consumer expectations are aligned with the GTA Net's capabilities and direction.
