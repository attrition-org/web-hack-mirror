Welcome to the GTANet:
A Telecommunications Vision for the GTA

Introduction

Since 1992, Information Technology Directors from cities and regions in the Greater Toronto Area (GTA) have been meeting to share information and seek creative ways of saving money and improving public service by working together.

The purpose of this Web site is to allow members of the GTCC Telecommunications Steering Committee to communicate in an effective and timely manner.
