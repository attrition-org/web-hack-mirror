MEMBERSHIP INFORMATION
 

Annual Membership

Annual Membership is the most inclusive membership of the National Youth Golf Association. Annual Membership is $75.00 and includes an official NYGA Tour golf shirt, handbook, welcome package, an official NYGA bag tag, along with a one-year subscription of "In The Bag", the official newsletter of the NYGA. Annual members will receive an NYGA tour index and are eligible to participate in all invitational events, state championships, and tour championship.

Renewal Membership

Renewal Membership is only for NYGA members who have participated in a previous NYGA annual membership (membership must have been for the previous year). Renewal Membership is $60.00 and includes an official NYGA Tour golf shirt, handbook, welcome package, an official NYGA bag tag, along with a one-year subscription of "In The Bag", the official newsletter of the NYGA. Annual members will receive an NYGA tour index and are eligible to participate in all invitational events, state championships, and tour championship.

Seasonal Membership

Seasonal Memberships are to benefit NYGA members who reside in seasonal golf markets. Seasonal Membership is $45.00 and includes a National Youth Golf Association handbook, welcome package, and an official NYGA bag tag. Seasonal members will also receive an NYGA tour index and are eligible to participate in invitational events during their chosen season of enrollment.

Membership Upgrade

Membership Upgrade is for NYGA members who have participated in a seasonal membership and would like to upgrade to an annual membership (must be purchased within forty-five (45) days from completion of a seasonal membership). Membership upgrade is $30.00 and includes all of the seasonal membership benefits, including a one-year subscription of "In The Bag", the official newsletter of the NYGA, eligibility for all invitational events, state championships, and tour championship.

*Note: The Membership Upgrade does not include the official NYGA Tour golf shirt, however, this shirt may be purchased separately.
