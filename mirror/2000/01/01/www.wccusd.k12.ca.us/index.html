    Our schools will provide a secure environment that encompasses the free exchange of ideas and the fostering of creativity and critical thinking.  
    Serving the Cities of El Cerrito, Hercules, Pinole, Richmond & San Pablo & Unincorporated Areas of Contra Costa County, California, including El Sobrante, Kensington, Montalvin, North Richmond, & Tara Hills. 

    Located near the deep-water Port of Richmond on the northeast rim of San Francisco Bay, the West Contra Costa Unified School District serves over 31,000 students from kindergarten through grade 12 as well as adult education. We are the 18th largest local education agency in California. Over 70 languages are spoken within our 110 square mile jurisdiction.
