1. APPLICATIONS AVAILABLE FROM YOUR PRINCIPAL
THE ED. FUND $500 MINI-GRANTS, $1,000 SCHOOL-WIDE GRANTS and
one $2,500 SPECIAL EDUCATION GRANT
Eligible Applicants:
    • All teachers, and parents and students working with teachers, may apply for mini-grants.
    • Principals or teams of at least 3 teachers may apply for school-wide grants and the Special Education grant.
Grant Requirements: The Ed. Fund looks for:
    • innovative, exciting projects in all subject areas (for example: math, art, writing, science, health, physical fitness)
    • high student impact
    • clear teacher/student interaction
    • clear integration with curriculum
If more than one teacher from a school intends to apply for the same mini-grant project, the teachers must submit one application for the group and not request more than $500. One school may receive more than one grant, but not for replications of the same project. Application Source: Your Principal or the school secretary. If your school runs out of application forms, copy a blank form, call The Ed. Fund (233-1464), or check the district web site (http://www.wccusd.k12.ca.us). Mailing Information: All applicants must send the original and 4 copies to:

The Ed. Fund, 1807 Arlington Blvd., El Cerrito, CA 94530

 

 Deadline:

APPLICATIONS MUST BE POSTMARKED NO LATER THAN SATURDAY, OCTOBER 2, 1999.

Applications will be disqualified if they are not postmarked by the deadline and if the applicant does not include the original and 4 copies (each copy collated and stapled).

Questions:

Call Susan Wittenberg at the Ed. Fund (233-1464)
