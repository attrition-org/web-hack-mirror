Mid-Southern Savings Bank is preparing for the year 2000.  We are making certain that our computer systems are ready for the date change.  Our Board of Directors is sincerely committed to assuring that all critical steps are taken before the date change.

What does Y2K mean?

Y2K refers to the Year 2000 issue of computer programs and systems misinterpreting the "00" in the year 2000 as 1900.  This results from most programs recognizing what year it is by the last two digits of the year number.  At Mid-Southern, we are taking all actions necessary to assure that our systems are updated and ready for the next millenium.
