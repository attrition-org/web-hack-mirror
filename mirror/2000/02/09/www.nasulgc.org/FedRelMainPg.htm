The Federal Relations group, staffed by professionals with responsibilities for specific legislative areas and federal budget issues, informs NASULGC members about developments in the nation’s capital that affect higher education and seeks to advance membership interests. In addition, the group provides timely information on a wide range of complex legislative policy issues to members of Congress and their staffs.
