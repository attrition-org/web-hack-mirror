Publications

The Office of Public Affairs serves as NASULGC's primary publication's unit where its staff produces the association's monthly newsletter, Newsline, and various other publications on higher education issues. Highlights from Newsline are published electronically on this website each month. In addition, complete versions of several of NASULGC'S key publications are electronically published and abstracts of others are available on this website. Visit NASULGC's virtual library for those materials. Copies of NASULGC's publications may be obtained from the Office of Public Affairs at 202-478-6042 or e-mail: pubs@nasulgc.org
