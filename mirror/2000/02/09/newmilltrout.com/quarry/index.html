MOUSE WATER QUARRY

Mouse Waters production derives from fluvial glacial sediments laid down in eskers~in sufficient volume ~and of the quality required to match the needs of the building industry~a sector subject to the vagaries of the economy .

Despite these fluctuations ,AMP is flexible enough to meet the demand for high qualify washed concrete sand.10 mm.20 mend 40mm grade gravel's conforms BSS 882 and washed building sand to BSS 1200.Drying shrinkage conforms to the requirements of category A with compliance assured through daily on site laboratory testing

Extraction and the Environment

 

Protecting the energy of the surrounding area and its residents demands a thorough understanding of the engineering complexities of quarrying.Inbuilt noise reduction systems mean negligible intrusion on neighboring property and minimal disruption to an established rural way of life.That life style is enhanced by the nearby golf course reclaimed from a former quarry and by the presence of a listed monument~a roman camp.

All this without impacting on the finished quality of the aggregates produced!

On Site and Surrounding Safety

To ensure strict compliance with all required regulations ,and to set local minds to rest,a monthly external audit is carried out by an independent safety consultancy .On health and safety issues ,AMP practice a policy of rigid ,self imposed procedures to protect staff and eliminate avoidable risk at all stages of the extraction process.

By definition these safety measures apply equally to those living and working within the immediate vicinity o Mouse Water.
