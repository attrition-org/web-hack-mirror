When you begin swimming the sea of information that is the Internet, you may as well dive in head first. If you just dip in your toe, you'll never fathom the depths you'll  find below the surface.

Searching for law on-line explains how to use search tools that allow you to locate law that is available on the Internet. Citation of On-line Law guides you through the issues and techniques unique to the use of electronically retrieved cases. Law and Fact Development shows you the starting points for really beginning to use the various facets of the Internet.
