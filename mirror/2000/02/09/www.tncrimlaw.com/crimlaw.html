        Other CLE of interest to Tennessee Criminal Defense Attorneys

          Admissability of Scientific Evidence in Tennessee , Tennessee Bar Association 615.383.7421 ON-LINE 1 hour CLE credit

          DNA 101 - DNA 102 are online CLE courses. The instructor for the course is Dr. Nora Rudin, who co-wrote "An Introduction to Forensic DNA Analysis". Dr. Rudin explains, "we aim for the trial attorney ... the point is that we try to make the science accessible to the non-specialist/non-scientist/non-forensic science professional."

          The course will be split into two modules of 4 weeks and 3 weeks. Each week we will post a new "lesson", which students can go through at their leisure. The book is required as we will not be repeating all the information on-line. The 2nd module will include a simulated crime and data for their analysis. Cost of one course is $45 and $80 for both. More information can be found at http://www.corpus-delicti.com/dna101.html
