Craft Fair

This Craft Fair has something for everyone! With over 100 booths, you can spend the whole morning or afternoon strolling among vendors of every kind imaginable. Mendocino County’s finest crafts people are on hand for purchase of their handiwork, or to give you a helping hand and answer questions. Don’t miss this fun part of Paul Bunyan Days.
