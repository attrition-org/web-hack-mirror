Old Fashioned Dress Review

Show Starting Time : 2:00 p.m.

Location : Company Store

The Old Fashioned Dress Review takes you back to a time when folks dressed, well, different than we do now! That’s not to say that if they could see us they wouldn’t have a good laugh, but the costumes worn by the men, women and children parading in the dress review are typical of years past. From the very fancy dresses of the 1890 society ladies, to the lumberjack and cowboys work wear, they all bring back memories from your favorite movie or T.V. show! Don’t miss the colorful costumes of this years dress review, put together by chairpersons Carla Coverston and Sandy Savage. It’s a sight for sore eyes!
