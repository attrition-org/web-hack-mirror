Freeman Elementary School Mission Statement

  We believe that Freeman School should be a child-centered place of learning where bridges   
  rather than gaps are created.

  Furthermore, we believe that students, parents, and staff must work together to make the
  Freeman environment:

  • A place of security where children and adults are free from verbal and physical intimidation.
  • A place of nurturing where it is believed that every child can learn and achieve.
  • A place of risk taking where mistakes are only viewed as opportunities for growth.
  • A place of pride where children and adults respect the valuable role played by all in the learning process.
