Central's Finest

Fraser River Estate

Typical of the Central Otago landscape, it is rough rocky hillside covered in thyme, its northern aspects is what makes it suitable for grape growing.

Wines Available: Chardonnay, Cabernet Sauvignon/Merlot/Shiraz.

 

Leaning Rock Vineyard

Established on the high river terrace which forms the northeastern edge of the Alexandra-Clyde basin.

Wines Available: Pinot Noir, Chardonnay, Riesling .

 
Chard Farm 

Chard Farm was established in 1987 by Rob &Greg Hay. Initially most locals thought it was pie in the sky stuff and it was even overheard in the local pub that Chard Farm as a vineyard was a "waste of bloody good merino country". That "wasted merino country "is now producing around 100,00 bottles of wine a year .

Wines Available: Gewurtztramner, Sauvignon Blanc, Riesling, Pinot Noir, Chardonnay.

 
Gibbston Valley
Gibbston Valley is a small sophisticated winery and restaurant in the spectacular Kawarau Gorge just 25kms from Queenstown. Here the first wines planted in 1981 have thrived on the steep shist and gravel slopes where the miners dug for gold over 100 years ago.

Wines Available: Riesling, Pinot Gris, Sauvignon Blanc, Greenstone Chardonnay, Chardonnay (Oaked).

 
Olssen`s of Bannockburn

Olssen`s Garden Vineyard is in the small town of Bannockburn about 8kms from Cromwell Central Otago.

Wines Available: Gewurztraminer, Riesling, Sauvignon Blanc, Chardonnay, Pinot Noir.

 
William Hill Vineyard

William Hill vineyard lies on the western outskirts of Alexandra where in the sun soaked, sandy location and the dry summers combine to produce quality fruit.

Wines Available: Pinot Noir, Gewurztraminer, Chardonnay, Riesling.

 
Rippon Vineyard

Rippon Vineyard is located on the shores of Lake Wanaka.

Wines Available: Gewurztraminer, Chardonnay, Sauvignon Blanc, Riesling, Pinot Noir.

 
Kawarau Estate

BIO-GRO N.Z. has accorded this vineyard full organic certification. Vineyards situated overlooking Lake Hayes near Queenstown and at Lowburn, north of Cromwell are dedicated to the production of high quality wines.

Wines Available: Chardonnay, Sauvignon Blanc, Riesling, Pinot Noir.

 
Black Ridge Vineyard

The Vineyard lies at the entrance to Conroys Gully from the Earnscleugh Flats. This is a land of harsh and dramatic contrasts. The north facing slopes of the vineyard are baked by extremes of heat in summer and frozen by frosts in winter. Black Ridge is a wild and rocky place with schist rock outcrops dominating the vineyard. Where grapes cannot be planted the rocks are left to the wild thyme and the rabbits.

Wines Available: Gewurztraminer Select, Riesling, Otago Gold, Cabernet Sauvignon, Chardonnay, Gewurztraminer, Pinot Noir.

 
Dry Gully Vineyard

There is still gold 10 metres down in the river gravel under Dry Gully Vineyard and there's gold on top as well. Dry Gully won a gold medal with its first Pinot Noir. The small vineyard slopes slightly towards the river,and falls away sharply into the dredge tailings where it can be very cold and frosty.

Wines Available: Pinot Noir.

 
Alexandra Wine Company

Davishon and Crag an Oir Vineyards produce wine for the Central Otago company. Hot dry summers and cold winters bring out the best characteristics in their wines.

Wines Available: Riesling, Chardonnay.

 
Briar Vale Estate

The north facing slopes on which this vineyard is planted was originally covered in wild briar hence the name given to the vineyard and the Briar Rose featured on their label.

Wines Available: Chardonnay, Riesling.

 
Kenley Estate

All grapes for these wines are grown on hillside slopes at their home vineyard close to the town of Alexandra where the climate is continental, very high temperatures during summer and extremely cold in winter.

They are experimenting with two new grape varities for Central Otago, Viognier and Scheurebe. Look for wines from these grapes in the future.

Wines Available: Gewurztraminer/Riesling, Fume Blanc, Pinot Noir, Cabernet-Merlot.

 
Springvale Estate
Three small boutique vineyards have joined to form "Springvale Estate".

Wines Available: Gewurztraminer, Sauvignon Blanc, Chardonnay, Pinot Noir, Cabernet Sauvignon.

 
Felton Road Winery

Taking advantage of the continental climate with hot summers and north facing slopes grapevines ripen with intense aromas and flavours. The attractive vineyard, located at Bannockburn just downstream from the Kawarau Gorge , has an interesting collection of clones and rootstocks, free draining soils and carefully managed low yields, combine to produce wines of elegance and complexity.

Wines available: Dry Riesling, Riesling, Chardonnay, Pinot Noir.
