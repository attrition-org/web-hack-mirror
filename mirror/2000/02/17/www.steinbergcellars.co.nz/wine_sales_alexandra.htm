About Steinberg Cellars


Peter and Robyn Bannerman took over ownership of Steinberg Cellars in Alexandra during May 1998 and see this as an exciting time to be involved in the wine trade in Central Otago as new vineyards are producing excellent, award winning wines.

Peter is the proprietor at Steinberg and is usually about when you require assistance or a bit of light hearted banter. Robyn works part-time.


Part of the interior of our shop.
Go down the stairs at the centre and you are in the cellar.

Many new events have taken place at Steinberg Cellars over the last few months.
"Cellars Wine Club" has been set up to give club members a greater level of flexibility with their wine buying. We want to avoid the situation where people feel obliged to take their wine when the supplier says so (as is often the case) and at the price that they stipulate. We aim to tailor our selections to meet members needs. they say when they want it, how much of it they want, the mix of wines to be included and of course the dollar value of the selection that is to be put together.
New members are most welcome. We're very contactable.
