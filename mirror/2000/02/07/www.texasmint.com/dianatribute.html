A Tribute to the Princess of Hearts - Princess Diana

     Never before has pure gold complimented the most extraordinary silver coin ever minted in the United States -The Princess Diana Commemorative Coin.  This gold select process was first struck from two troy ounces of .999 fine silver.  Then selectively layered in 24K gold with extreme precision to create a three dimensional masterpeice the enhances the crowned Princess of Wales.
 
Diana the Princess of Wales who had poise, dignity, and royal charisma is distinctly shown center stage with the beautiful interior of Westminster Abbey in the background and the memorable quote of Englands Prime Minister "she was the peoples princess and that is how she will stay."

Again center stage is Princess Diana as shown with sons William and Harry which were born into the Royal Family, and were being groomed for the royal responsiblity, protocol, and duties that would put them into the spotlight of the worlds scrutiny. The exterior of the Abbey in the foreground and the Union Flag at half-mast which was a symbolic Royal Tribute the public had clamoured for days to see. It finishes off with a quote from Diana of what her father always taught her, and that was "to treat everyone as equal, I've always done so, and I'm sure that both Harry and William will follow in my footsteps." The last token and tribute to her was an arrangement of white lilies.
