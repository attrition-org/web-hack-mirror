Instructions for Issuance of a Malian Visa & Passport

           

 

Passport Application (pdf format) to be sent with 2 photos,old passport, and $45 money order.
Passport application in Microsoft format: application


Visa Application (pdf format) must be filled out in two (2) copies. 
Visa application in Microsoft format: application

                           
Download free Acrobat Reader to view visa application       


   
Return completed visa form to the Embassy, together with: 

    two (2) photographs attached, 
    a valid passport, 
    a self-addressed stamped envelope, 
    the visa fee of $20.00 in cash, cashier's check, company check or money order only (no personal check accepted), and 
    $4.00 for Certified Mail, $15.50 for Federal Express or $11.95 for Express Mail for the return of the passport. 

Photocopy of round trip ticket is required or a company cover letter for business visa.  

The health requirement is yellow fever vaccination before the departure to Mali. Cholera is recommended.  
   VISA PROCESSING REQUIRES THREE (5) WORKING DAYS  

The validity period is one (1) month from the date of entry to Mali. Requests for an extension of visa may be obtained in Bamako. Prior to entry in Mali, the visa is valid for three (3) months. 

For further information, contact the Embassy of Mali in Washington. 
  CAUTION: VISA CANNOT BE OBTAINED AT THE BORDER !!
