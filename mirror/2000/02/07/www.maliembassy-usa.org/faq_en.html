Frequently Asked Questions

          Q:Can I get a visa without sending my original passport?
          A:No. The embassy need your valid passport in order to issue a visa.

          Q:Do I need proof of my immunization before getting a visa?
          A:No. However you would need it at the port of entry.

          Q:How long does it take to grant a visa?
          A:It takes about 2-3 business days.

          Q:How long can I stay with a tourist visa?
          A:3 months. You can also get an extension of you visa in Mali.

          Q:There is no Malian embassy in my country. What can I do?
          A:You can contact the closest embassy or obtain a visa through our embassy via express mail service.

          Q:I am a Malian citizen. How can I get a new passport?
          A:Send your old passport with 2 pictures, filled out form and a money order of $45.

          Q:To whom should I write the money order?
          A:Embassy of Mali.

          Q:Can I send a copy of my plane ticket as proof?
          A:Yes. You can also send a copy of your itinerary issued by your travel agent or airline.
