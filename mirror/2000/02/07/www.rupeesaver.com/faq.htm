Why is the service free?

    So that more and more Internet users could become the members of 'rupeesaver'. More members would mean more demands. Why should manufacturers and dealers offer attractive rates to 'rupeesaver'?

    Frankly they have no other option if they really are keen in getting your business. None of the participating manufacturers and dealers will know the price that has been quoted by their competitors. 'rupeesaver' has been programmed in a way to show only those offers that are the lowest for that particular brand and the model. Yes, you have guessed it right. To beat the competition, the manufacturers and the dealers are likely to quote the bare minimum. The market forces will work to 'rupeesaver' member's advantage. And that's the essence of 'rupeesaver'.

How does 'rupeesaver' bring in authenticity to the various offers it receives?

    All participating dealers and manufacturers will have to quote their offers in writing along with their terms and conditions, if any. Our executives will collect the information periodically and update the same for your benefit.

Is 'rupeesaver' bargaining on our behalf?

    No. It is just compiling the information for you. It's in the supplier's own interest to quote their best price. However, we will always look out to better the prices than those offered by other web sites and non-'rupeesaver' dealers. In short we want 'rupeesaver' to offer rock-bottom prices to the best of our abilities.

What if the offer is declined to me by one of the dealers?

    If you bring such a happening to our notice with acceptable evidence, 'rupeesaver' will certainly take up the issue on your behalf. If need be, the dealer's name will be struck off from our computers.

What would be the conditions of sale?

    It is to be mutually agreed between you and the supplier. 'rupeesaver' in no way will be involved with the actual transaction. 'rupeesaver' is basically providing you with the information.
