Interpower Components Ltd., United Kingdom

Oskaloosa IA, USA

Interpower Components Limited was started in the UK in 1992 and is a totally owned subsidiary of Panel Components Corp. of Oskaloosa, Iowa USA, who have been supplying major international companies for over 20 years.

Panel Components Corporation was started in California in 1976 by the owner, Bob Wersen, to supply International electrical connectors, cables and power distribution components, etc. to companies wishing to distribute their products throughout the world. They are now based in Oskaloosa, Iowa (see photo above), having moved from California in 1993, into a new warehouse and manufacturing facility, employing around seventy sales and manufacturing staff. Facilities for moulding and assembling cordsets and cable assemblies are located at this plant.

Interpower Components Limited was started in Bognor Regis in West Sussex to provide a similar service for customers in the UK and Europe, due to continued expansion, is now located in Milton Keynes in Buckinghamshire. From these two sites, the company has built a substantial sales base with customers throughout Europe.
