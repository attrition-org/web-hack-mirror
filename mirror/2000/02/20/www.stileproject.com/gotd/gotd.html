Girl of the Week: Alley Baggett
Alley first appeared in Playboy's Book of Lingerie (May 1995). Suddenly her success was so astounding that she appeared in almost every one of the next lingerie books published (and obviously on many others Playboy publications, such as Book of Nudes, Girls of Summer, Sexy Swimsuits, Wet & Wild, Book of Supermodels, and many national editions of Playboy). She also posed for about 15 Playboy covers since April 1996. Alley has been on many other magazine covers and in articles, calendars, campaigns and advertisements all around the world. She also participates in charity actions by helping associations which try to save the seas, orangutans and children. How touching.
