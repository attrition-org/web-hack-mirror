TOURS
i-Kon links with a National/International Tour Operator, who is the only ASATA Tour Operator in the Free State. Quality Pre- and Post Conference tours throughout the Free State and South Africa can be arrange. 
A choice of different game reserves which are within easy reach of Bloemfontein are available. See lion, buffalo, giraffe, springbuck and many more in their natural habitat. These tours are a must for the nature lover.

BLOEMFONTEIN CITY TOUR 
Half day and day tours available. Experience the Architectural Historical, Cultural and Ecological wealth of the Free State capital. From the modern to the ancient, this tour of Bloemfontein, the city of roses, will thrill you with its sights and first-hand experiences of life in modern day Bloemfontein. Visit amongst others the Womands Memorial, the only monument in the world commemorating woman and children’s suffering in a war, the orchid house as well as Grey College, the oldest school north of the Orange River.
