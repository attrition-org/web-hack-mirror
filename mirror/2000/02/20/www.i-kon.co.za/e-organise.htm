  • Procuring list of potential delegates

    Post-conference administration - attendance register.

The way we work
i-Kon do the full organization of conferences, meetings, workshops, training sessions, seminars, strategic planning sessions, "bosberade", accommodation, etc. These services are provided free of charge to you, the client. Because we are an established business and we deal with these establishments on a daily basis, we are in the position to negotiate on your behalf for the lowest prize. 

When you give us an instruction, we get full particulars from you after which we make enquiries and then present you with three quotations for venues, accommodation, meals, refreshments and whatever other requirements you may have. Once you have made your choice, we take responsibility for all the arrangements. You and the venues are presented beforehand with all the details concerning the number of people, whether they sleep in single or double rooms, details and times of meals and refreshments, equipment that is needed, dates and times of arrivals and departures, etc.

We are aware that you have a responsibility to save as much money as possible. We assist you in this since we negotiate for the lowest prizes possible and we save you and your personnel the time and effort to do all these bookings and enquiries for yourself.
