Security Bank is a locally owned bank with a strong background in customer service. It is unusual for a bank of its size to offer such a wide variety of financial products all under one roof.
In addition to bank services, Security Mortgage Corporation, a subsidiary of Security Bank, offers financing for homebuyers in the state of South Dakota. From low mortgage rates for the first time homebuyer to flexible financing options such as ARMs and JUMBOs, Security Mortgage Corporation has the staff to make owning your dream house possible.
Security Bank’s main office is located in Madison, SD with a branch in downtown Sioux Falls, the largest city in South Dakota. Security is proud to be part of the community that was named Money Magazine's #1 city in America in 1993.
