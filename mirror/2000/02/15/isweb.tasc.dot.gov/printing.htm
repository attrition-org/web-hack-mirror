Printing Services              

We offer the complete range of printing, high-speed duplication, binding and finishing services.  We provide technical printing consultation to ensure that your publications have the best look for the best price.

Printing Services provides onsite, state-of-the-art high speed electronic copying, duplicating and offset printing.  We print everything from basic office documents and reports to multi-colored offset printing and color copying.

We offer:

  • Full Service Black and White Copying from 8 1/2 x 11 to 11 x 17
  • Full Service Digital Color Copying from 8 1/2 x 11 to 11 x 17
  • Custom Offset Printing from 8 1/2 x 11 to 17 x 22
  • Laminating up to 22 inches wide and any length
  • Reducing and Enlarging services
  • Full Service Bindery including:
  • Paper Cutting
  • In line automatic collating
  • Saddle Stitch Stapling
  • Stapling up to 3 inches thick
  • Stapling 2 stitches on the left side
  • Stapling 1 stitch in the upper left
  • Folding for paper up to 25 inches original size
  • Comb Binding from 1/4 inch to 3 inches thick
  • Punching/Drilling of paper for any number of holes
  • Photo Print enlargements up to 22 x 28
  • Transparencies Color or Black and White
