SEARCH THE CATALOG

The online catalog contains the bibliographic records for materials held by our five library locations. These materials include trade books, technical reports, serials and periodicals, various microforms, CD-ROMs, and videos. DOT employees who wish to borrow items from the collection should contact a reference librarian or circulation assistant in the library.

The catalog may be searched by title, author, subject, and a number of other different access points.  You can also browsing by these same access points. Full information and call numbers for materials can be found by selecting "Record Detail" after a search.
