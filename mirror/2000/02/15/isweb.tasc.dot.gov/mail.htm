Mail Management Services

 

We provide complete office mail services, including x-raying of all mail received, managing mail rooms, distributing interoffice mail, pick-up and delivery of postal mail.

 

We offer:

  • Twice a day, pick up and deliver postal and interoffice mail to 600 mail stops at 13 DOT locations in the Washington, DC metropolitan area.
  • Complete mail security services, including use of the latest explosive detection technology to x-ray all mail and packages prior to delivery to DOT customers, and supporting centralized courier drop off locations in all DOT headquarters buildings.
  • Savings to DOT customers in postage costs by presorting and metering all outgoing 1 and 2 ounce letters saving approximately 3 1/2 cents per letter.
  • Specialized overnight delivery and registered mail services.
  • Pack large items prior to shipment and save customers package delivery costs by obtaining volume discounts with alternate deliverers.
