Distribution Requirements

 

Using our automated, Mail Out Management System (MOMS), we determine the best method of distribution for your publications, create and manage multiple automated mail lists, and use the latest postal software to reduce your postal costs.

Our Services

~ Assist you in determining how to distribute your publications.

MOMS contains over 150,000 current addresses of DOT customers who  receive over 1 million departmental publications each year.  These  addresses are organized in over 8,400 established mailing lists, which are available for use by any DOT office.  We can also create new lists to meet the distribution needs of any of your publications.   These mailing lists and addresses are updated daily.

~ Use of the latest postal software to code and certify all addresses prior to mailing.

This reduces return mail by up to 25 percent, saving customers 32 cents postage costs for each returned item.

~ Obtain volume discounts for customers by centrally purchasing and distributing Congressional materials and other Federal agencies' publications.

We obtain discounts for you of up to 80 percent of the purchase price.
