Mail Out Management System (MOMS)

 

Our Services for the Public

You may now add your name to our DOT mailing lists so that you will receive DOT publications that are important to you. MOMS also allows you to access your mailing list data, view lists of available publications, add or remove publications, and make address changes on line.

PRIVACY STATEMENT: The data entries we receive from you will be used to generate mailing labels for sending you publications as they become available. Your personal information will not be sold, rented, or disclosed to third parties unless such action is specifically authorized by law.
