Note from the webmaster:

  • Currently the webmaster of Tyranny.org is planning a spinoff website dedicated to the cause of helping non-profit groups.  The following will be implemented into the new site.

    • A search engine to provide a means of searching the database.
    • A database interrogator to provide a way for you to input your skills, interests and talents and receieve a reply of non-profit groups that match your query.

I would like to thank you for your continuing support of Tyranny.org and hope that during these holiday seasons you find many joys in helping others.  Please tell your friends about the group, the website and most importantly the cause.  Resist but resist with a cause, because one with no cause is one with no heart.
