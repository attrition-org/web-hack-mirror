The purpose of the Ventures Program is to enable participants to engage others in creating an environment in which powerful learning is experienced by all learners systematically and consistently. The Ventures Program is intended for school leaders at all levels who are in positions to bring about significant changes in their schools or districts.

The Ventures Program consists of twelve core seminars and eight special focus seminars over three years, plus an optional year of coaching and support seminars in which the participants are expected to implement significant change efforts in their schools or districts.


Ventures is for you!
CSLA's Ventures Program is one of a kind. Known for ten years as the premier support system for California principals and school leaders, CSLA has designed this new program for senior associates and site principals to support the creation of 21st Century schools that are founded upon powerful learning for all students. It combines the restructuring research and practice on the national level with the experience from CSLA's original Foundation Program and School Leadership Team Seminars.


The Goal
Ventures will enable leaders to create powerful learning experiences for ALL students in their schools or district. Personal learning, strengthened leadership skills, a deepened knowledge base and a full range of new strategies will be integrated and applied to the participant's daily work at the site or district.


Areas of Focus:

  • Analyzing your own leadership style
  • Using systems theory and tools for change
  • Building a thinking, meaning-centered curriculum (TMCC)
  • Developing facilitation skills
  • Applying proven strategies for change
  • Working with staff to create powerful learning experiences for all students
  • Building a shared vision/shaping culture
  • Focusing on student centered results 


What Ventures can do for you!
Significant organizational change can be an awesome and sometimes overwhelming challenge for district and site level leadership. Ventures is designed to provide multi-level and multi-year support for continuous improvement and whole school change. The program is built on a constructivist foundation that provides deep and powerful learning experiences within the context of critical knowledge and key strategies that allow participants to construct personal meaning through authentic applications.

Here are just a few of the many reasons why Ventures will be a significant part of your continuous development of bold and comprehensive leadership approaches. You will:

  • have access to CSLA resources
  • focus on the future needs of your students
  • learn in a constructivist-based, interactive, and hands-on program within authentic contexts
  • develop leadership for significant school change
  • have the benefits of significant administrative and staff development experiences
  • create networking with other administrators
  • expand your knowledge base and leadership skills
  • create a professional portfolio
  • explore personal areas of need linked to your school priorities


Program Completion
Phase I:    24-30 days of seminars over a 3-year period

Phase II:   Optional. Continued support by the School Leadership Center for site implementations


Who Should Participate?

  • CSLA Senior Associates
  • Principals and Vice-Principals
