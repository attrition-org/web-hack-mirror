               Mission
                            The Madera County Office of Education staff seek opportunities
                            to deliver services that are responsive to the needs of students,
                            parents, school districts, agencies and the community.                              

                The Madera County Office of Education serves as an essential resource, service
                and support agency for the eleven school districts and a variety of government
                agencies in Madera County.  The Madera County Office of Education actively
                participates in the regional service delivery system established by the California
                County Superintendents of Schools Educational Services Association.

                The county superintendent is responsible for examining and approving school
                district budgets.  The County staff provides leadership and helps formulate new
                curriculum, staff development and training programs.  It designs business,
                personnel, and data processing systems.

                The county superintendent's office provides the following services:

                                    *   Student programs and services
                                    *   Instructional support services
                                    *   Technology services
                                    *   Fiscal services
                                    *   Interagency collaboration
                                    *   Workforce development
                                    *   Community information
                                    *   Regional program and services

                In December of 1997, the California State Board of Education approved the
                reorganization proposal for the formation of a new school district in the Madera
                Ranchos area.  The proposal was placed on the June 2, 1998, ballot.  On June
                17, 1998, the Golden Valley Unified School District, Board of Trustees were
                administered the oath of office and the first official meeting was held.

                The accomplishments of the Madera County Office of Education serve as a
                tribute to the outstanding and dedicated efforts of the staff.  This unique group
                of individuals constantly strive to provide quality service for our many publics.
                The office mission statement describes the philosophical basis of our work with
                various constituencies.

                Thank you for visiting our web page and for your interest in the Madera County
                Office of Education.
