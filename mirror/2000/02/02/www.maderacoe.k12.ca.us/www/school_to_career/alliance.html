Madera County Business & Education Alliance

What is the Alliance? The Alliance is a coalition of community representatives including business, education, parents, government, labor, civic organizations, and students who strive to bring business and education together in partnerships that will benefit "all" students throughout Madera County.

The Mission: To educate all students to meet the challenges of tomorrow's world.

The Goal: To assist all students with a career development process through support of successful partnerships.

The Purpose: To promote educational opportunities for all students to successfully transition from school to the workplace.
