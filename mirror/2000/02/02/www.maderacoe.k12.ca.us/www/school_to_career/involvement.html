How Can Employers Get Involved? Join the Madera County Business and Education Alliance.

What is the Alliance? The Alliance is a coalition of community representatives including business, education, parents, government, labor, civic organizations, and students which strive to bring business and education together in connecting activities that will benefit all of Madera County.

What is your role as a member of the Alliance? Your primary role is to use your knowledge, skills, and experience to build and improve the cooperation between all segments of your community as we work toward preparing all students for tomorrow's careers. Efforts will be toward improving communication and increasing cooperation of all those interested in education, rather than starting "another new Program." You and the other members of the Alliance will bring diverse perspectives, a knowledge of what is being done, and ideas of what is needed to prepare our youth. You may wish to view examples of how you can be involved.

The Alliance has three support chapters which meet monthly throughout Madera County:
Madera Chapter, North Fork Chapter, and Chowchilla Chapter
