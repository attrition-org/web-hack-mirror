Business Partnership

Southwest Supermarket, Circle K, Fleming Foods and Holsum Bakery have established a mini-store on campus. Students earn Wildcat Bucks for good attendance, completing assignments, promptness and good citizenship. Students write checks against their account to purchase products mutually selected by the student and their family. Students develop self-esteem, a sense of contributing to their family and learn sound financial practices.
