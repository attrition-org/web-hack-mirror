DISTRICT SPELLING BEE CHAMPIONS

The annual Murphy School District Spelling Bee was held in January in the Governing Board Room. Spelling Champions from all four schools competed in the event. They included Javier Sandoval from Garcia School, Melody Baron from Sullivan, Vicky Valenzuela from Hamilton and Jacqulyn Orona from Kuban School. Javier Sandoval won the competition. He is an eight-grader whose teacher is Mr. Andy Fraga. Javier will represent the district in the regional Spelling Bee. Congratulations Champions!
