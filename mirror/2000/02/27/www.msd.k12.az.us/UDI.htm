United Development Inc.

This is a mentoring program established by United Development, Inc. There are 20 male students involved in this program. The mentors come onto campus every Wednesday from 1:10 p.m. to 2:10 p.m. The purpose is to provide both educational and work related activities that support and encourage the young men to stay in school. They also provide real work experiences that support the student's educational goals. A Saturday program has been established dividing activities between education and work experience.
