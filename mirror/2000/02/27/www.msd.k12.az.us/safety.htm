Internet Safety for kids

1.Never ask for or tell anyone your email address
2.Never ask for or tell anyone your home address if you have one
3.Never ask for or tell anyone your last name
4.Never tell anyone where you or anyone you know lives
5.Never tell anyone your home telephone number or a friend's phone number.
6.Never tell anyone that you'll meet or talk with them in-person
7.Never tell anyone what city you live in or the name of your school
8.Don't wait! Safety first! Show your parents any message that asks for personal information such as
email address, last name, home address or phone number, or makes you feel uncomfortable in any
way. We recommend that you do not write back anybody who asks you for this information. It's not
safe.

Please let your friends know about this important information.   Enjoy surfing the internet.
