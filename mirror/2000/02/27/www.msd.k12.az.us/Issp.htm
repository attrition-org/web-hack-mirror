ISSP

WHERE: District Wide Housed at Garcia
PERSON/S IN CHARGE: Joe Baeza, Arturo F. Carrizoza
SPONSORED BY: Murphy District

In-School Supervision Program: an alternative discipline program to home suspension for students in grades 5-8. Students remain in school and complete their school work in a self-contained classroom with one-to-one supervision. Students do not participate in special area classes while in ISSP and cannot participate in any extra-curricular activities while placed in program.
