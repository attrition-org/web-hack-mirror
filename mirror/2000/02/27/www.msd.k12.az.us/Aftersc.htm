AFTER SCHOOL SPORTS PROGRAM

WHERE: District-Wide
PERSON/S IN CHARGE: Gil Enciso, Arturo F. Carrizoza
SPONSORED BY: Murphy District

A weekly sports program including practices and interdistrict/out of district games for boys and girls in grades 6-8. Boys play Flag Football, Basketball, Track, Soccer and Softball. Girls play Volleyball, Basketball, Track, Softball and participate in cheerleading. Track may include 3 - 8 grade students. For football, volleyball and Cheer, the coaches are: Garcia - Andy Fraga (football), Maria Morgia (volleyball), and no cheer sponsor yet; Hamilton - Ralph Wisterman (football), Fernando Duran (volleyball) and Molly Weeks (cheer); Sullivan - Robert Hart (football), Grace Davis (volleyball) and Erin Russell (cheer); Kuban - Eric Voss (football), Carol Mestemaker (volleyball) and no cheer sponsor yet.
