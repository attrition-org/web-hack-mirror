LOS PEQUEÑITOS - DANCE


WHERE: District-wide
PERSON/S IN CHARGE: Nora Lopez Arturo F. Carrizoza
SPONSORED BY: Murphy District

Students learn traditional Mexican dance steps. Students perform at our school festivities, church groups, State Fair, etc. It is offered weekly to students of all ages (grades K-8) and practices are held on Tuesdays and Thursdays from 4:00 to 6:00 p.m. at Sullivan School Cafeteria. Approximately 20 students participate from all four schools.
