HAMILTON FAMILY LEARN RESOURCE CENTER

WHERE: Hamilton
PERSON/S IN CHARGE: Beatriz Flores
SPONSORED BY: Project Valle Del Sol

Through a grant from the Catholic Services, several agencies formed a partnership to develop a family resource center for the family and parents at Hamilton. A room has been provided so parents can meet to learn more about parenting, special interest topics, ESL, and many other issues.
