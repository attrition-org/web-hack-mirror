ALATEEN

WHERE: Kuban
PERSON/S IN CHARGE: Carolyn McDonald
SPONSORED BY: Kuban School

This is a program for students growing up in homes affected by drugs and alcohol in the home. Beginning November 5, 1996, students in grades 5-8 will begin meeting on Thursdays from 11:00 a.m. to 12:00 noon. There are about seven participants involved.
