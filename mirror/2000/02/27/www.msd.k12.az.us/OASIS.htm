OASIS

Sandy Dominguez and Pam Morrison have been trained to work with retired citizens in helping with one-on-one tutoring and social development with our students. Retirees work with our students in grades K-3 at least two hours daily and work on different activities. They have an Author's Day, Christmas activity with children at Airport and a Love to Read Day. This year they have 44 students participating in this program.
