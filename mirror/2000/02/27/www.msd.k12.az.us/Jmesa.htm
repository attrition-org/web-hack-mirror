JUNIOR MESA


WHERE: Garcia/Hamilton
PERSON/S IN CHARGE: Martha Chambers & Gloria Rivera
SPONSORED BY: ASU

Jr. Math, Engineering and Science Achievement provides hands-on activities after school for students interested in these areas. Field trips are taken to various agencies and to the university to explore advance programs. Supplies are provided by Project Prime and Salt River Project. Hamilton School has 22 students and they meet daily from 11:50 a.m. to 12:30 p.m. Garcia School has 20 students and they meet daily from 3:30 to 4:30 p.m.
