Area Served/ Boundaries:

Murphy School District No.21 is located in the Greater Phoenix area and is one of the 13 elementary school districts which feed into the Phoenix Union High School District. The District extends from 39th Avenue to 19th Avenue and from West Van Buren to the Salt River.

DISTRICT FACTS:

  • Number of Schools: 4
  • Enrollment: 2550 students (Grades K-8)
  • Personnel/Staff: Certified= 150; Administrative =16; Classified/Support=150.
  • Racial/Ethnic Distribution: 88% Hispanic, 6% Anglo; 4% Black; 2% Other.

Landmarks/Attractions:

Murphy is a stable district located near freeways which make it accessible to the northern and eastern parts of the valley. It is conveniently located to shopping plazas, churches and the downtown business ditrict.
