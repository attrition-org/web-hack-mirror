From Murphy School District to all parents in Arizona your kids' safety is #1

As you know, Arizona has the highest drowning children rate in the nation.
Therefore please read the following:

1-Keep your eyes on your kids while they are in the pool.
2-Learn CPR, call Fire Department for special courses offered (602) 277-1500.
3-Keep fences around the pool closed at all time.
4-Don't leave your kids alone to answer the door or the phone.
5-Keep you phone close to the pool where you can see your kids.
6-Have a cell phone or cordless phone close by if possible.
7-Keep toys out of the water.
8-Kids can drown in 1 inch deep water if left alone.
9-Kids can drown in the toilet because their upper body is heavier than lower section.
10-Use sunblock for you and your kids (Minimum #30).
11-Please take care and enjoy the summer.
