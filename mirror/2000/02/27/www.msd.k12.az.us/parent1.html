(TIPS) Hiking Preparation and Tools needed

1-Have plenty of water
2-Use hat and sunglasses
3-Have a cell phone if possible
4-Use sunblock to prevent burning and skin cancer
5-Have some food and fruits
6-Enjoy

(TIPS) Camping Preparation and Tools needed

1-Have 2 flashlights
2-Plenty of water/ cooler
3-Tent and slepping bags
4-Small knife/ lighter (becareful when using)
5-Cell phone if possible
6-Food
7-Extra jackets and blankets
8-Have somebody with you and not alone
9-Enjoy
