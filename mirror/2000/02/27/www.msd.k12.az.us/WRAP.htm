The purpose of this program is to expand the traditional part-day Head Start Program to a full day program. The full day program is designed for children ages 3, 4 and 5 of low income working families or those attending job training or school. The service delivery model is based on the Head Start model. This program is held at Hamilton School during the school year and at Garcia School during the summer. The hours are from 7:30 a.m. to 6:00 p.m. with 20 students in the classroom.
