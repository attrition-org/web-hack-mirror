Murphy School District's Breakfast Program and National School Lunch Program are an integral part of the total education program, helping to provide over one-half of the recommended daily food needs of our students.  Proper nutrition sets the stage for good physical and mental development which enhances the student's ability to learn.  All our students at Murphy Schools are eligible to practice in the National School Lunch and Breakfast Programs.    To encourage the consumption of a well-balanced meal, choices within all food groups are vegetable choices.  All schools have different entree selections and vegetable choices.  Also, several varieties of fresh fruits are offered during breakfast and lunch to all students and all meals are served with milk or fruit juice.
