JAVITS GIFTED HISTORY

WHERE: Sullivan
PERSON/S IN CHARGE: Steve Wheeler
SPONSORED BY: NAU

Northern Arizona University's Center for Excellence in Education working collaboratively with Sullivan in developing special gifted programs for minority gifted youth. This program will produce programs that will discover many minority gifted students and enable them to improve their learning ability. This class is for one period a day for grades 7 and 8. There are about 20 students participating and upon completion they will receive high school credit.
