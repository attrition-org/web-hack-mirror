CAD/CAM

Computer Aided Design and Manufacturing (CAD/CAM) capabilities allow us to apply the latest techniques in computer technology to drafting, design, production and quality control.  We are continually upgrading and updating these systems to allow us to work more efficiently which translates into reduced costs to our customers.

Currently, we utilize the following software packages:
  • CAMAX SmartCAM ADVANCE 3D software, version 11 for machining
  • AutoCAD, version 14 for 2D drawings
  • ProEngineer, version 2000i for 3D modeling and drawings
  • West developed software for ASME and TUV Coded pressure vessel design calculations
