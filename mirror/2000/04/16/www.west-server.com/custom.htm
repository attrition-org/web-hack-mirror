Custom & Special Machinery Building

West has all the skills needed to manufacture and build special and custom machinery.  Under one roof we can take your design drawings, perform the fabrication and welding operations, machine and test component parts, mechanically assemble those parts, perform pneumatic, hydraulic and electrical work and then test run and inspect so the equipment will be ready for installation in your plant.

Should you have an arrangement drawing with basic data and need the design to be completed, contact us for assistance.

With a large assembly area available, 23 feet underneath the hook of our bridge cranes, engineering and skilled craft personnel at your disposal, West is ready to handle your complete machinery needs.

We are conveniently located at an Interstate 95 interchange making us easily accessible to commercial trucking.   We are within minutes of Richmond International Airport, Hanover County Airport and the Port of Richmond making international freight forwarding readily available.
