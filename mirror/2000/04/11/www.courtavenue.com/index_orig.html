We all have issues from time to time.  Maybe you've been burned by someone.  A landlord, boss or a  perhaps a neighbor?  Tell us about it!

This brand new legal forum is intended to encourage regular people and the legal professional to come together and have useful discussions about legal disputes of any nature.  Whether you're having trouble getting your rental deposit from your former landlord, or your boss won't pay you earned overtime, get your feelings out!

If you are an attorney, please feel free to post your remarks in response to the comments of others.  Who knows?  Perhaps we can resolve a few disputes without tying up the courts.
