Cyberbees Online respects its users and members and we try our best to retain your privacy on the Internet. We also structure our system and services to provide you with the safest and most private online experience here at Cyberbees Online.

Cyberbees Online abides by the following privacy policies:

We will not at any time send you any outside unsolicited emails such as commercial offers or advertisements. However, we will send you updates on your accounts and booths and send you news on the latest Cyberbees Online features. In the event that you choose not to receive such emails from this
site, we offer you the choice to unsubscribe.  However, in an extreme event, Cyberbees Online may email you about a serious issue concerning your usage of the site.

We will not, under any circumstances, distribute your email address, username or password to anyone. 

Member administration will not be disclosed to any third party members. We will not modify, delete or alter any items in your administration.

COOKIES

Cyberbees Online stores a cookie in your hard drive to provide better services in our site, and fully take advantage of what cookies are capable of providing. However, if you choose not to have a cookie stores in your hard drive, you have the choice of deleting it. By doing this, you will be limiting yourself when visiting our site.

The cookie that we store in your hard drive protects your password and privacy when using our site. The cookie also enables you to customize pages on our site.

CHANGING ACCOUNT STATUS

The choice of changing profiles is provided by our site in the event that a password has been either forgotten or intercepted. You will be required to choose a new password. For security reasons, the link to change a particular account status will be emailed to the corresponding member email that was
provided in the registration form.

If you feel that we have not met our standards concerning this, don't hesitate to contact headbee@cyberbees.com and we will, in our best efforts, correct the problem.
