WHAT iS ALL ABOUT ?
————————————————————————————————

WORSTCASE.com is meant to be a gatheringpoint
for the most friendly clans in germany playing
under the same banner.
We help each other in any way such as :
  • swapping so called ringers
  • exchanging strategies
  • building websites
  • support in search for a server
  • swapping links
  • whatever friends do for each other :-)
Whenever you meet a member of
a clan listed on WORSTCASE.com
you can be sure to have a good
portion of fun with him !
We all do this for fun, have fun,
are friendly, help newbies, ignore nazies, fight teamkiller
and value respect and honour !
