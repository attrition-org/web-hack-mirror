Ornithology Sylabus
Posted Tuesday, March 07, 2000
Welcome to Ornithology !
with Mr. Mushel
This trimester we値l be learning about the amazing world of BIRDS! We値l explore bird external anatomy, (What characteristics do birds have that enable them to fly??), internal anatomy (How does an owl digest its prey without chewing it??), identification - visual (How can I make the distinction between an immature Bald Eagle and a Turkey Vulture from a great distance??), identification - audible (What is the difference between a Wood Duck and a Mallard call??), as well as other mysterious and exciting topics related to BIRDS!!

CLASS EXPECTATIONS * Be kind
* Do your best

REQUIRED MATERIALS
* A true love of, and interest in, BIRDS!
* Notebook and writing utensil
* Binoculars (I have several extra pair)
* PMA (positive mental attitude)

GRADING SCALE 90-100% - A
80-89% - B
70-79% - C
60-69% - D
0-59% - F
GRADING CATEGORIES
Tests - Written, Auditory Identification, and Visual Identification.
Pop Quizzes
Lab Work / Field Behavior
Research Reports - 1 per month on native birds of your choice.
Class Participation & fulfillment of Class Expectations
