New and Notable:

My new web page is up and running!
Posted Tuesday, March 28, 2000
Dr.Knight's spring concert is set for May 18th at 1:30 and 7:30 in the school gymnasium! The end of the year program will include the 4th and 5th grade bands and the 4th and 5th grade choir! Mark this date on your calender! Come join us for a night of great music and fun!

5th and 4th Grade Bands are preparing of the District Elementary Band Concert to be held on April 27th at 7 o'clock at the Middle School Commons. Everyone is welcome!

Student, parents and staff at Dr. Knight are anticipating the "Green Eggs and Ham" breakfast celebration. Students took part in the "Read Across America" sensation celebrating reading and Dr. Seuss's 96th birthday. Dr.Seuss loved children and learning. His books are effective tools for teaching young children the basic skills needed to be successful readers. Most important he made reading fun!


The fifth grade band and Dr. Knight students will perform at the March 30th celebration. Dr. Seuss used rollicking read aloud rhyme that is ideally suited to choral reading. We can't forget the "Diffendoofer Song" to conclude our celebration! For more fun and activities check out his web site.
