Business Manager
Posted Tuesday, March 21, 2000
The Little Falls Community Schools has an opening for a Business Manager. Applicants must have a Minnesota administrative license or a degree in business or related field, or such alternatives to the above qualifications as the Board may find appropriate and acceptable. Responsibilities include responsibility for budget development; administers a budget control system for the District; oversees proper financial and accounting record systems; provide leadership for long-range financial planning; prepare reports or coordinated report generation as required by law and requested by the Superintendent and School Board; and such other responsibilities related to the position. Salary range is from $48,000 - $60,000 with possible exception dependent on experience and credentials. Applicants should send a letter of inquiry along with a resume and credentials to: Mary Jo Morgan, Director of Human Resources, Little Falls Community Schools, 1001 5th Avenue SE, Little Falls, MN 56345. Applications will be accepted until May 1 or until the position is filled.
