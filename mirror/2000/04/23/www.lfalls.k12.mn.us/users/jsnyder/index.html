New and Notable:

AP American History #133 Course Coverage
Posted Thursday, January 27, 2000
By the end of the second trimester on May 26, students will have covered Chapters 23-33. The general student assignment is to read the current chapter and complete the corresponding studyguide weekly.

American History #101 Extra Credit
Posted Tuesday, January 04, 2000
Students may earn up to ten points for each report submitted on a topic approved by their instructor. Reading materials are available in American History Illustrated or American Heritage magazine. See the class syllabus for further information.

American History #101
Posted Tuesday, January 04, 2000
Students should complete reading Chapter 27 by April 26, and complete the studyguide for Chapter 27. The Chapter 27 Quiz is scheduled for April 27.

American History #117
Posted Tuesday, January 04, 2000
Students should keep notes daily from class to use on Unit Four Quiz (Colonial Life) scheduled for April 28.

American History #101 extra credit work
Posted Thursday, December 16, 1999
Students may earn ten points for each of two WWII interviews completed by May 1, 2000. Interview forms and directions are available from their classroom teacher. A Korean War and Vietnam War interview are also possible.

Advanced Placement American History #132 Extra Credit
Posted Thursday, December 16, 1999
AP #133 students may earn extra points to improve their grades through viewing the Between the War video series.
