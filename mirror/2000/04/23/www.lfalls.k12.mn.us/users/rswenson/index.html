New and Notable:

DARE Graduation
Posted Wednesday, April 12, 2000
DARE graduation is set for Tuesday, April 18th. Students will join with their parents in celebrating their completion of the DARE program with Officer Rick. Congratulations to the students in the room for placing 1st in the DARE Knowledge Bowl. Some hard work and studying for the contest really paid off. Congratulations, Knowledge Bowl Champs!

That's a Fact!
Posted Wednesday, April 12, 2000
Students in one of the math groups in our room have now officially finished the textbook for fifth grade. Thirteen chapters have been taught and learned by some pretty ambitious and hardworking students. We will finish up the year by working on some advanced math skills and using materials from DYNAMATH! Congratulations on a job well done.
