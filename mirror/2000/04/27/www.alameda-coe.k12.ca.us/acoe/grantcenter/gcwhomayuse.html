Who May Use Our Services?

Educators, nonprofit personnel, parents, and community members may use the services of the Grants Resource Center. We encourage people in Alameda County as well as neighboring counties to visit the Center and explore the limitless opportunities for financial support. We recommend that people new to grantwriting enroll in one of our many workshops for a minimal fee.
