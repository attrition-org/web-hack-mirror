HELP
Guidelines for PARENTS:

Help Keep Your Children in School. If you move often:
  • Keep a copy of your child's birth certificate
  • Keep a copy of your child's school record and Immunization record
  • Ask your school if you can sign a release to have information given over the telephone to an official in your child's new school

Ask questions:

  • If you need information about health or social services, ask for it!
  • Schools can help you find the services your child needs
  • Medical, dental records, Report cards and other school records

Guidelines for SCHOOLS:

  • Provide Equal Access to a Free Appropriate Public Education
  • Provide opportunities for students to achieve high levels of academic proficiency
  • elp parents complete the paperwork that ensures school attendance
  • Make educational placements in the best interest of the student
  • Provide educational services to homeless students that are comparable to those services provided for all other students
  • Maintain school records
  • Revise policies and procedures that may act as barriers to the enrollment and education of homeless children
  • Help parents gain access to needed health and social services
