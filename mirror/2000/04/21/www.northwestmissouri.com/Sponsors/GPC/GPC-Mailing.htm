When it's got to get mailed...
Choose from our "smorgesboard" of mailing services measured in pennies per piece.  Depend on us to be your one-stop print and mail shop.

If you have printed materials to mail, we can take the hassle out of doing most, or all, of it for you. We will label your product for the U.S. Postal Service using labels you provide or those we generate for you.

We will bundle, sort and sack your product -- in a manner to secure USPS discounts for you where applicable. And we will deliver to our post office or to yours ...on time!

Mailing services include: CASS certification, carrier route & walk sequence, postal reports and required documentation, bar coding (if rigidity is met), renewal notice labels or specialty labels.

Provide us with a diskette of your addresses for processing, or e-mail your list to us, or manage your mailing list on our computer through our secure Intenet site. Regardless of your choice, you're in control of your mailing lists.
