Beef Products

Rains Natural Meats is currently using a rotation system of Angus, Shorthorn and Simmental for beef breeding. This rotation assures success in our herd on all aspects. The Angus traits assures marbling in the meat and proper fleshing. The Shorthorn helps us keep a supply of good mothers to renew our herd. Simmental breeding adds size and scale to cattle for better natural growth and muscle mass.

We believe this breeding combination helps us have the best overall beef products possible.
