From our family to yours...
Rains Natural Meats is located in the Green Hills region of Missouri, where the rolling hills and fertile bottoms of Northwest Missouri make an ideal place to raise livestock.

Rains Natural Meats is family owned, dedicated to putting meat and poultry products from our pasture to your plates. Our animals are grown under humane natural growing practices. Our animals are raised hormone and antibiotic free, the old-fashioned way. More about us...
What is natural?
Rains Natural Meats offers you wholesome meat cuts that are antibiotic and hormone free. We raise our own livestock; our animals do not receive growth producing hormones. We raise our own livestock to a standard above commercial feeders. These are the animals that also feed our families and that is why we are more selective in our breeding programs and our meat quality. There is documentation showing that hormones placed in animals can and are ingested into the human digestive system and can cause human health problems.

What can we do for you?
Rains Natural Meats prides itself on providing an antibiotic and hormone free meat that you can order the way you like it. Have you ever wanted a juicy inch-and-a-half T-bone steak? Well, at Rains Natural Meats we cut to your order. We are a USDA inspected processing plant which makes us capable of providing any special cuts or sizes of meat that you could possibly desire. Your wish is our command.
