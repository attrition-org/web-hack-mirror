elcome to the High Circles fine range of furniture.

High Circles offer a vast and comprehensive range of luxurious In-door and Out-door furniture, styled for home, office, hotel, garden and eclectic furniture in a combination of European and Asian influences.

Specialising in reproductions of Antique furniture, colonial and contemporary furniture, garden furniture, Rattan with intricately woven Leather detail to enhance the beauty of it's natural fibre and, Bamboo are all part of our outstanding line of uniquely handcrafted designs impeccably executed with expertise.

Ecologically minded plantation Teak and Mahogany Wood are the only wood we build with. Upholstered styles are all available in Leather, textured Cotton or Rattan.

Teakwood is available in two coloration's being, natural light brown or natural dark brown.

Mahogany wood is a natural rich rose coloured wood with the alternative of a darker stain to enhance the richness of Mahogany's natural rose beauty.

Rattan is highlighted in four different colour stains of natural honey, natural brown, sky blue and grass green. Leather detailing available in any choice of colour.

Bamboo is beautiful in its natural state of golden honey with a natural varnish to accentuate the warm beauty of this tropical fibre.

High Circles not only provides outstanding high quality furniture we also assist in interior and exterior design and arrangement combining the mastery of the ancient science of Feng Shui.
