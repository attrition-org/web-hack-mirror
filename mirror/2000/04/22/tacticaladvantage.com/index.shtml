Is Rogue Spear Dying? - Have you noticed a decline in people playing Rogue Spear? I have, what would cause this to happen? I have a few ideas which I am going to share with you. Do not get me wrong, Rogue Spear is a good game but it was not beta tested well, or thought through very well. Read on...
  • Serverside Disadvantage - This is when the host gets shot and his reticle jerks around and he loses control for a split second. This is very frustrating for the person hosting, and with the new addon coming from RSE, both server and client will have it. I understand RS is a realistic type of game, but sometimes you should sacrifice realism for gameplay, as in this case. I believe adding this feature to everyone will decrease the popularity of RS.
  • Death Shots - This is one of the worst implemented things in Rogue Spear. You have to be a good player to take people out with head shots, yet when people are killed in the game, they pull off amazing shots to the head on a regular basis. I have even shot people in the back, as they die they spin to the ground and when the spin points them at me, the gun fires and hits me in the head thus killing me. This happens far too often; it would be a lot better if the weapon just fired as they went down and did not aim right between your eyes.
  • Lag - There are many multiplayer games out there and all seem to have issues with lag. As much as I like RS, I get frustrated with the warping caused from lag. Lag does affect other games but I have never seen warping as bad as in RS.
  • Releasing Map Tools - Everyone knows how popular Half-Life and Quake are, and both allow users to create custom maps. This adds to the replay value of the game and lets the gaming community help the game grow; unfortunately RSE does support this belief. The current mod tools available for RS are just too limited when compared to some other tools for games like Half-Life.

    Those are the four main problems with RS in my opinion. If these were fixed, I think Rogue would be the perfect game. Like I said, these are my opinions and are based on my gaming experiences.
