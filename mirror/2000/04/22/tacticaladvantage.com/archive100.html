Wednesday, September 22, 1999
Action Figure Pulled from Market - For those of us who still like cool action figures you may have heard of The Ultimate Soldier 12" Action Figures. 21st Century Toys manufactures realistic looking action figures, some of the most detailed I have seen. In a series called the "Americas Finest" they were several figures available. The SWAT figures ( which are great, I have one ) and the villains. The villains have been pulled from the market because too many people said they looked like the two boys that opened fire at Columbine High School. Although the action figures were made a year ago, 21st Century Toys is pulling them from the market. Read a statement from the company here.

 Wonderful Day - I got up late this morning and woke to the sound of birds chirping and wind blowing ever so gently. As I opened all the windows to allow the breeze to blow through my house I realized that no matter how beautiful this day was, I was going to spend it inside at my computer. Why would I do that you ask? The answer is simple, because Rogue Spear came out today! After my shower I got on the computer and confirmed that Electronics Boutique had them in stock. I then chatted with Snicker and one of the guys from RSE for a few minutes before jumping in my low-performance sports car and speeding down the road to the mall (speeding is 45mph). I parked so my walk to the store would be as quick as possible. Entering EB I noticed they had tons of copies of Rogue Spear, I was like a kid in a candy store, or a TAC member in a adult video store, either way you look at it I was happy. The clerk gave me my copy I had prepaid and I was out the door and back home in record time. I have yet to install it, but I am going to right now, I thought I had better post this now since I may never be back. I have a big day planned, I am going to see an Egyptian exhibit at the museum, then I may go to Kosovo and visit the locals. Maybe I will see you there, lets hope so.
