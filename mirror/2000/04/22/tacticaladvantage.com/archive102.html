Rogue Spear Beta Leak - Snicker of the Rogue Spear Retreat has posted that a copy of the beta has been put on a warez sites. For those of you who do not know, a warez site allows people to download software that has been copied, of course this is illegal. So if you leaked the copy better keep an eye and ear open for Tom Clancy's goons to come looking for you. I saw the Rogue Spear Beta on a site and as I was about to click on it I heard helicopters outside....coincidence? Then strange things started happening, my door bell rang, I went to it only to find a manilla envelope with a note inside written in cut out letters, it read "Don't click that beta". I also started noticing unmarked black cars following me, and these men in black suits every where I went. Maybe it's just me being a little paranoid, but I think Snicker was right, if you get the beta from a warez site or are the one who leaked it....keep an eye out for strange happenings, and check for hidden bugs and cameras in your house and office.
- Dropzone
