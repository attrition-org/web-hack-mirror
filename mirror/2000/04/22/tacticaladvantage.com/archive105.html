Hidden and Dangerous - Talonsoft is working on a new title called "Hidden and Dangerous", I do not care for the title much but the game looks awesome. It is set in WWII and looks to up the ante in the first person perspective world of military type gaming. The environments will include factories, dams, swamps, underground caverns, fields, and rivers. The game will also support force feedback, unfortunately I use a mouse to play these type games thus my joystick is rendered useless. The game takes place in Norway, Germany, Italy,the Danube, and more. You also have the option of using vehicles you find. If you would like to see some screen shots of the game from Talonsoft then click here.

Friday, April 16, 1999     
Team Fortress Classic for Half-Life - It seems I overlooked this add-on for Half-Life many times, and I apologize for that. Team Fortress Classic is an add-on for Half-Life which in essence mimics the TF of Quake fame, only much better. Do not get this confused with Team Fortress 2 however, Valve only released this add-on to satisfy the masses until the real team Fortress 2 comes out. You can still play all the character classes (Spy, Medic, Engineer, Sniper, Scout, Heavy Weapons Man, Demolition Man, Soldier, and the Pyro) and they have all their respective skills. I believe this add-on was overlooked since everyone is waiting for the real deal to come out, but it is worth getting, so get it by clicking here.
- Dropzone
