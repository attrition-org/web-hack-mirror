    Is There A Future In S.H. RPG? - I spent this weekend looking for answers to the Superhero RPG crusade I started out on a week ago. Unfortunatly I did not get a scrap of hope for the Superhero genre. Out of all of the people that I wrote to, the only one to respond was from Mr. Brian Clair ( pulisher of the Avault ), who had the information I needed and was very helpful. It seems the two games that were scheduled for release are dead and buried, The Indestructibles by Bullfrog, and Microproses Agents of Justice ( Picture ) were abandoned do to similar ill-fated circumstances. As Mr. Clair points out, " Publishers don't feel a game based in this area would make any money - at least not enough to offset the cost of development or turn a profit and 2) Developers who don't just ignore the creation of a game in this genre based off #1 may simply feel that they couldn't do an adequate job of it." I tried Bullfrog and Microprose but I have not recieved a response. Both games looks impressive, especially Agents of Justice which you can read about here , hopefully there is a gaming company out there that will read this and pick up on the project. Thank you to the Avault for all their help.
