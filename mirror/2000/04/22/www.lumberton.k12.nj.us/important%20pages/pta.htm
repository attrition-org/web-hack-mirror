The purpose of the Lumberton Parent Teacher Association is to further the education concerns for the children of Lumberton Township by:

1.  Providing an informative connecting link between home and school,

2.  Providing an avenue of communication between parents, teachers, and administrators,

3.  Providing a vehicle for personal participation in school supportive services, and

4.  By helping facilitate the best possible education for the children.

    We are as strong and as successful in this purpose as each of you will enable us to be by your membership, support, and active participation in PTA programs.  Membership is open to all.

    PTA services include an emergency closing phone system, providing room mothers for grades K-5, holding informative activities for students and parents, and providing volunteer assistance for many programs held at each of the schools.

   The PTA, through its fund raisers, book fairs, and label collection; have made gifts to the schools of playground equipment, audio-visual equipment, team uniforms, T-shirts, books, scholarships, honors, luncheons, computer hardware, software, and many other items.

    PTA meetings are held in the Bobby's Run School at 7:30 PM on the first Tuesday of each month.  All are welcome.  We are looking forward to another full and active year.  If you have any questions please contact:
