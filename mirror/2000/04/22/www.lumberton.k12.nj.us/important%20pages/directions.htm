Directions to the Lumberton Middle School,

Bobby’s Run School, Board of Education Offices

30-32 Dimsdale Drive, Lumberton, NJ

From Ben Franklin Bridge and Points South:

Take I-676 East to I-295 North. Stay on I-295 North to Exit 40A, Mount Holly, Route 38 East. Drive on Route 38 East toward Mt. Holly for approximately five miles, passing through ten traffic lights (all very close together). After the tenth light, look for signs for Route 541 on the right. Just before the next light, turn right onto the Route 541 Bypass "jug handle". At the stop sign, make a right onto Route 541 South bypass, toward Lumberton & Medford. Stay on the Bypass through the traffic light. As you cross Route 541 the road you are on becomes Bobby’s Run Boulevard. Drive one mile and make a left onto Dimsdale Drive. (If you get to Eayrestown Road, you have just missed Dimsdale Drive.) You will see the middle school in front of you. For the Bobby’s Run School or Board of Education, drive past the middle school (it will be on your right) to the back of the complex, passing the loading dock area. You will see visitor parking just after, and to the left of, the auditorium. The entrance to the Board of Education offices is tucked in the corner at right. The entrance to the Bobby’s Run School is to the left of the Board of Ed. offices. Please check into the Main Office if you are entering either school.

From Trenton and Points North:

Take I-295 South to Exit 45A, Mount Holly. As you exit off I-295, you will spill onto Rancocas Road, crossing over I-295 and eventually the NJ Turnpike. You will drive on Rancocas Road for approximately three miles. At the second traffic light, make a right onto the Mount Holly Bypass, Route 541. Drive on the Bypass for approximately two miles to the third traffic light (you will cross Route 38 at the second light). Drive straight through the third traffic light, crossing Route 541. As you drive across Route 541 the road you are on becomes Bobby’s Run Boulevard. Drive one mile and make a left onto Dimsdale Drive. (If you get to Eayrestown Road, you have just missed Dimsdale Drive.) You will see the middle school in front of you. For the Bobby’s Run School or Board of Education, drive past the middle school (it will be on your right) to the back of the complex, passing the loading dock area. You will see visitor parking just after, and to the left of, the auditorium. The entrance to the Board of Education offices is tucked in the corner at right. The entrance to the Bobby’s Run School is to the left of the Board of Ed. offices. Please check in at the Main Office if you are entering either school.
