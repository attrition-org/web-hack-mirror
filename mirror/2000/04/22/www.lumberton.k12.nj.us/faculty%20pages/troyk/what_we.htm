THE BASIC COMPONENTS OF THE LANGUAGE ARTS PROGRAM ARE INTERWOVEN TO TEACH AND REINFORCE SKILLS IN READING, WRITING AND VERBAL EXPRESSION.

* SPELLING   WEEKLY SPELLING PROGRAM WHICH CAN BE DONE WITH PARENTAL MONITORING

* WRITING   DAILY JOURNAL WRITING AND STORY  RESPONSE LOGS
