Our Country

Our class learned about the United States. We got a new classroom map just in time too. Owney, the traveling dog, came to visit our classroom from New Mexico.  We looked in his "kennel" to see his state souvenirs.  He had a pin from almost every state. We even got to put the pin on for our state, New Jersey.
