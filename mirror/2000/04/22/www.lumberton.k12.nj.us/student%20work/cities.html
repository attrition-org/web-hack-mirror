HyperStudio City Projects

Using Encarta and the internet (especially Travelocity), our 8th grade students have created travel brochures to numerous cities worldwide.  Before you book your next vacation, please pursue our travel guides to learn more about each city.

To view our work, one can either download the HyperStudio projects and open them with HyperStudio, or use the HyperStudio netpage plug-in and view the presentations on-line.  To get the netpage plug-in, please click on the image below.
