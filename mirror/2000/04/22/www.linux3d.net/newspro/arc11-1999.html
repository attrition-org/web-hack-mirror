DCypher.Net clients are out Posted Thursday, December 30 17:08 by armin
DCypher.Net has released Linux clients and proxies for their distributed Gamma Flux project. Also available are FreeBSD clients and proxies.

Gamma Flux is a project aimed to improve safety of nuclear waste storage by calculating the gamma flux field around storage containers, it bases on raytracing with high-res 3D sample points.

Support this worthy effort and run their clients to protect the future of mankind from the follies of the 20th century.

DCypher.Net rolls out Gamma Flux project Posted Monday, December 20 11:38 by armin
DCypher.Net today launched release candidates for the Gamma Flux project that is aimed at improving storage vessels for nuclear waste. Proxies version 1.05 for Linux with Gamma Flux support are available for GLIBC 2.0 and statically linked here. Linux clients should be out by Wednesday.
