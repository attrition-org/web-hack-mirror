Gizmo3D ver 0.95 Posted Sunday, January 2 16:50 by kornel
Anders sends word that Gizmo3D is out in a new version with multipass texturing, recursive portals, recursive mirrors, refractors. The IRMA (Improved ROAM Algorithm) has been further developed for terrain rendering.

We are looking for people to help us making some neat 3D demos to show the full potential of Gizmo3D so get in touch with

anders@linux3d.net and have a look at http://www.linux3d.net/gizmo3d/ for downloads.
