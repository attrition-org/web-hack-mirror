Permedia 2 and 3 - Free implementations are being developed to support these boards. Also with support of   Elsa AG , SuSE GmbH started developing 3D accelerated drivers for Permedia and Permedia 2.

( Here is Steefen Seeger's website where you can find the latest GGI-drivers for the Permedia family of 3D graphics accelerators from 3Dlabs. )

Metro Link will include commercial support for them in their upcoming Extreme 3D system. Xi Graphics will include support for Permedia 2 in their upcoming Accelerated-X/OGL product.

3Dlabs Glint Delta + MX - Commercial support for these chips will be included in Xi Graphics upcoming Accelerated-X/OGL product. Also with support of  Elsa AG , SuSE GmbH started developing 3D accelerated drivers.

Glint GMX 1000 and 2000 - Commercial support for these chips will be include in Metro Link's upcoming Extreme 3D system.
