Daikatana with FSAA
 

Paul was kind enough to give us the links to a couple of screenshots he's done on the Daikatana demo with and without FSAA (cortesy of the 5.14 drivers). The files are in PNG format but are still around 800 kb big. They are at 1024x768x32 bit format. Paul also supplied a few bmp-files for those who can't look at PNG pics. Personally I think Daikatana is damn dark (i guess I should get the demo) which makes it hard to see the difference. However - if you zoom in you definitely see how FSAA smooths things out.
