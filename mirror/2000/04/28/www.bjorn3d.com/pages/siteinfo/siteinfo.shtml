Site Info

Björn's 3D World was started in the end of 1996 when I bought my first 3D card - a Creative 3D Blaster PCI - powered by a Rendition V1000 chip. I got instantly hooked on 3D graphics and realised that there weren't many Rendition fan-sites around. Since then the whole 3D industry has moved forward in giant leaps. In the middle of 1999 I decided to switch focus from Rendition to NVidia, a company that had proven to deliver new products in a steady stream.

During all these years I have managed to build up a good relationship with a lot of both hardware and software companies and the site has earned the respect among both normal users and people in the 3D industry.   

The aim with the web-site is to continue to give you interesting NVidia news as well as bringing you articles, reviews and previews on both NVidia products and competitive products. 

/Björn Tidal - Founder, webmaster and editor of Björn's 3D World
