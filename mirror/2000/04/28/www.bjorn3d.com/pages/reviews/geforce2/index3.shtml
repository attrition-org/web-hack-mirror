Screenshots
Updated 26 April 2000

Nvidia provided us with a lot of screenshots from a few upcoming games as well as a special Quake 3 level. Just click on each thumbnail to see the full picture. The pics are in jpg-format so some loss of quality has occured. They still are around the range of 200-500 Kb big. 

Giants

Giants is a new title from Planet Moon Studios. The game features full support for the GeForce 2 GTS T&L engine. I think these screenshots look great. They are colourfull and detailed. The huge monsters look very cool. Each pic is large (around 300-600 Kb) but worth waiting for.

Check out this big bad monster. Cool toenails and teeth! Ouch, I hate to be that guys dentist! Snow? Must be Sweden!
I guess the next sound will be .. SPLAT! FInally a game with colours! I'm so sick of brown-grey games. Check out that sky!!
Oh my. Someone is about to become a chewing-toy ... Only a mother can love that baby ..

Quake III special level

This is a special level in Q3A that has a massive amount of geometry and textures. It demonstrates the GeForce 2 GTS's ability to handle huge amounts of polygons and still proved excellent framerates. This level will either be provided with some boards or be available as a download later.
