Additional Information

A personal interview is not mandatory but desirable. If you expect to be in the Santa Fe area, you may contact the Office of Human Resources of the Santa Fe Public Schools to arrange for an appointment.

You are encouraged to attach (either in this form or send by another means) a resume, transcripts or any other pertinent information which you feel will be helpful to us in considering your application. Your application will remain active for 1 year. If you wish to remain active after 1 year, you must submit a letter stating you continued interest. Please notify us of any change of address or telephone number.
