Elementary Schools

Our district has 21 elementary schools and one Early Childhood Center. Schools range in size from 140 students to over 600 students. A comprehensive curriculum is offered which includes Language Arts, Reading, Math, Social Studies, Health, Science, Art, Music and Computer Literacy. Programs for bilingual students, remedial reading and math, as well as gifted students are offered according to the needs of the student population.
