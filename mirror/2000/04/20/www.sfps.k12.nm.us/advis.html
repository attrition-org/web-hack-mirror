Superintendent's Advisory Committees


Santa Fe Public Schools values your views about our schools. Join of of the Superintendent's Advisory Councils for the 1998-99 school year and share ideas on ways to improve our schools and assure Successs for ALL Students.

Meetings of the Advisory Councils are scheduled for one hour, at a specific time on a monthly basis.  Most meetings are held at the Educational Services Center at 610 Alta Vista. For more information about joining one the the Superintendent's Advisory Councils, call Beverly Friedman, Director, Public Information Office, 982-2631 or 954-2028.
