Objectives of APT

The objectives of the Telecommunity shall be:

  1. To Correlate Planning, Programming and Development of Telecommunication Networks;

  2. To Promote the Implementation of all Agreed Networks;

  3. To Assist in Development of National Components of Efficient Networks;

  4. To Foster Co-ordination within the Region of Technical Standards and Routing Plans;

  5. To Seek Adoption of Efficient Operating Methods in Regional Telecommunication Service.

In furtherance thereof, the Telecommunity may:

  1. Undertake, in co-ordination with the International Telecommunication Union, when pertinent, technical and other studies relating to developments in telecommunication technology of common interest to its Members and Associate Members;

  2. Encourage the exchange of information, technical experts and other specialized personnel amongst the telecommunication organizations of its Members and Associate Members;

  3. Study the feasibility of transfer of technology in the field of telecommunications amongst its Members and Associate Members;

  4. Arrange the provision of short-term technical assistance to its Members and Associate Members, when so required;

  5. Advise its Members and Associate Members in the assessment of their needs with respect to telecommunication personnel and programmes for training;

  6. Promote in co-operation with appropriate international organizations concerned with telecommunications in the region, the establishment within the region of telecommunication training institutes of a regional and multinational character;

  7. Promote and assist in the formulation and implementation of bilateral or multilateral telecommunication programmes within the region in co-operation with appropriate international or regional organizations.
