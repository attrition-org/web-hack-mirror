October 18th. Citizens Against Site 5 Meeting at Fair Oaks Ranch Country Club

Citizens Against Site 5 (CASF) will be meeting again Wednesday night, Oct. 18 at the Fair Oaks Ranch Country Club's banquet room at 7:30. You do not need to buy food or be a club member to attend.
