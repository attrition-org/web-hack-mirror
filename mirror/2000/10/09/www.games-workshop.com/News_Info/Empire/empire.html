EMPIRE ARMY BOOK RELEASED! TROOPS BEING RALLIED!

Warhammer Armies: The Empire, the first for use with 6th Edition Warhammer, is here! Fully illustrated, this tome details the characters, troops, Magic Items, and spells of the Old World's largest and most powerful realm.

This awesome book also contains a wealth of background information on the various provinces and military exploits of the Empire. A generous color section lavishly illustrates the armies of the Empire and showcases the new line of Citadel Miniatures in brilliant detail. The all important army list is most certainly included in between pages of artwork, stories, and other cool Empire information.

In addition to the Empire Book, a plethora of new models for this army are being brought out! Pick up the Empire Battalion, the Elector Count on Griffin, or the daunting Empire Army Boxed Set to destroy all those who oppose you!
