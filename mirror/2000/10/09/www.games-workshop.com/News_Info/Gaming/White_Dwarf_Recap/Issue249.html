WARHAMMER - BETTER AND BADDER THAN EVER!

Now that the Third War for Armageddon is over, what could possibly match that kind of titanic Warhammer 40,000 action?!? It would have to be something big, something huge, something completely colossal, something like...Warhammer 6th Edition!!!

That's right, Warhammer is back with a new look, a huge new rulebook and some of the best plastic miniatures we've ever produced - and that's just in the boxed game! With a fully revised and rethought set of rules, the new version of Warhammer is still the game you know and love, only better. Sticky points and often abused rules have been changed or dropped altogether and what has been created in their place is a better, more comprehensive and more fun game of incredible miniature battles set in the wartorn lands of the Old World! Whew, this is really big and it's got me so excited I don't know what to do. So, while I collect my wits, let's check out what's happening in White Dwarf #249:

With all of this great stuff what Games Workshop fanatic wouldn't want to pick up this great magazine!
