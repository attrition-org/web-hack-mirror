EMPIRE ARMY BOOK RELEASED!
TROOPS BEING RALLIED!

Warhammer Armies:The Empire, the first for use with 6th Edition Warhammer, is here! Fully illustrated, with loads of amazing miniature shots, this tome details the characters, troops, Magic Items, and spells of the Old World's largest and most powerful realm. In addition to the Empire Book, a plethora of new models for this army are being brought out! Pick up the Empire Battalion or the daunting Empire Army Boxed Set to destroy all those who oppose you!
