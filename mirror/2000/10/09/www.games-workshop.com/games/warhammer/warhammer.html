With Warhammer you become a fearless commander - painting and assembling mighty fantasy armies to do battle on the tabletop. Warhammer provides you with the core regiments of two armies, the valiant Bretonnians, and the cold-blooded Lizardmen.

In addition, you get all the rules, background, and information needed to start playing and collecting in readiness for battle!
Below is a rundown on the two armies that come in the boxed game:


There are no other men whose martial prowess can equal the Knights of Bretonnia. Bound by chivalrous vows, they fight for the poor and the innocent against marauding hordes of savage monsters.
As the commander of the Bretonnian army you will have at your disposal 12 Knights of the Realm - pledged to the service of the Lady of the Lake, upholding knightly virtues in her name. Alongside the knights you also have 24 bowmen, some of the finest archers in Bretonnia. Now you are ready to rid the world of the evil ambitions of the Lizardmen.


Lizardmen ruled the world before the coming of mankind. Now they rise again to reclaim their ancient birthright. Nothing less than conquest of the entire world will satisfy their cold and brutal hearts.
If you choose to fight with the Lizardmen you'll have 32 Skink archers. These scaly-skinned fighters are not the strongest warriors but they make up for that with sheer numbers, speed and cunning. You also have 20 Saurus warriors - brutish and savage creatures with extremely tough scaly skin. Both are driven by a hunger for world domination, guided by their bloated masters - the Slann.

WARHAMMER SUPPLEMENTS
Of course Warhammer is more than just a single game. In addition to fighting battles, players choose, collect and gradually paint their armies, building their forces to have bigger, more challenging and exciting games. Supplements can add a whole new dimension - taking the hobby as far as you want to go.

WARHAMMER MAGIC
The Warhammer Magic supplement adds all the rules you'll need to bring devastating spells and awesome magic items into your Warhammer games. With over 200 different magic items and more than 80 spells, your battlefields will never be the same!

Along with a detailed rulebook, you'll find loads of magic weaponry, armor, potions, and more. There is a Winds of Magic spell deck, and spells for every major race found in the Warhammer World. Each spell and item, from the most powerful magic sword to the lowliest chunk of warpstone, comes with full rules and its own high quality card so you can quickly outfit your army, and refer to it in the heat of a game!

WARHAMMER ARMIES
Lizardmen and Bretonnian models come in the Warhammer box, but not only are there loads of additional troops to add for those two forces, but there are tons of other armies to collect and play as well. After all, the Warhammer World is populated with a myriad of races, creatures, and monsters - all of which can be put into battle on your tabletop.
