Games Workshop makes several games (with a name like Games Workshop, we just had to make games, I mean we couldn't make mufflers now could we?) our most popular games are table-top battlegames.

Every Games Workshop boxed game comes with everything you need to get started. You get the rule books, dice, any charts and templates, plus loads and loads of Citadel Miniatures for two different forces (so you can start to duke it out right away!). You get tons of information about your forces, but also about other armies you might encounter on the fields of battle. The boxed games are really just the beginning, once you are comfortable with the rules you can start to expand your armies with more troops, special squads, and machines of war!

Just one more quick note before you start exploring - Games Workshop games allow you to explore two very different settings. One, a fantasy world inhabited by Dwarfs, Elves, rat-like Skaven, greenskinned Orcs & Goblins, and many more. The other setting is the war-torn universe of the 41st Millennium in which exist the genetically enhanced Space Marines, the all-consuming alien race known as the Tyranids, the enigmatic Eldar and many others, all embroiled in a constant battle for survival.

Warhammer
