About BRIEFME.COM, Inc.

Founded in 1999, BRIEFME.COM is an Internet and e-mail newsletter service which publishes over 80 FREE topical e-zines every week. 1100 contributing editors, all experts in their respective fields, scour the Web to find the best and most up-to-date information to review for the various newsletters. With currently over 2 million subscriptions, BRIEFME.COM is already leading the way as an e-mail publisher. BRIEFME.COM also provides subscribers the option of receiving additional opt-in e-mail marketing messages, making BRIEFME one of the top online permission marketers.

BRIEFME.COM is a privately held company based in Manhattan. Funding for BRIEFME.COM has been provided by the founders, all of whom have run profitable Internet companies prior to launching BRIEFME.COM.

With its subscriber base growing steadily each month, BRIEFME.COM looks forward to serving millions of subscribers, both in the U.S. and internationally. In addition, BRIEFME.COM has pioneered the combined use of opt-in marketing and e-newsletter sponsorship, providing marketers with a unique opportunity to communicate their brand, messages, and offers to the subscribers.
