As a contributing editor, you will receive exposure and recognition from the thousands of subscribers who look forward to reading each weekly edition of BRIEFME for your topic. If you have a website, you can also increase traffic to it through the free link that is included below each of your published BRIEFME reviews. You will receive $5 for each review (typically 75-100 words) of yours that we accept and publish; payments are dispersed on a monthly basis by check to editors within the U.S., and on a quarterly basis to editors outside the U.S.. In addition, you will have your own free web bio page at BRIEFME.COM while you are a contributing editor.

If you have an interest in one of our BRIEFME TOPICS, then please submit an application to become a BRIEFME editor. We will contact you shortly.

All it takes is a watchful eye for online news and information--perfect for those who are knowledgeable on their subject and enjoy surfing. Basically, what you do is find and briefly summarize the latest and greatest online articles and websites relating to your topic. You should write reviews for the content on the Internet that you feel your readership will want to learn about. See the editor FAQ for details.

To be evaluated as a potential editor, please fill out the form.

As an editor, you agree to be bound by the editor TERMS. Also, please be sure to read the editor FAQ.

Once you become an editor, the Editor's Guide and additional resources on the site will help you in writing your weekly submissions.
