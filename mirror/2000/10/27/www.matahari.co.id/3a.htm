Corporate Profile

Matahari is the premier retail company in Indonesia focusing on the most potential middle income segment. From its modest beginning of one small shop selling children's clothing founded by Mr. Hari Darmawan and his wife in 1958, Matahari has grown to 73 stores covering 646,436 square meters of selling space spread over 34 cities with 58 stores outside of Jakarta. Its merchandise has expanded to include housewares and daily needs.

Matahari operates its business through 3 formats. The first and most profitable is the Matahari Department Stores format. It carries a full assortment of merchandise from apparel to housewares and daily needs. It is a full price, full service concept catering to the middle income family customers in Indonesia. The second format is the Galeria store which caters to the upper segment of the market. The third format is the Mega M hypermarket . In its experimental stage, this format holds much promise and will form the core growth strategy for the company in the future. Matahari has 2 joint ventures. The most successful venture is the Matahari Timezone entertainment company that operates 40 video game outlets nationwide. In just a short 4 years, it has established itself as the dominant player in this industry. These businesses will be critical in future in combination with our retail formats to provide a complete package to our demanding consumers.

Matahari's vision is always to be the most dominant market leader in the retail industry through innovation and efficient operations, always serving our customers first.
