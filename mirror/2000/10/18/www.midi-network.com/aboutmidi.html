About MIDI-Network.com

The MIDI-Network has been providing free, high-quality MIDI files since mid-1997. Starting off with just above 30 MIDI files, the site has gradually grown to hundreds of high-quality MIDI files. From 15 visitors a month, we have now grown to 40,000 mothly unique visitors and over 200,000 page views every month. Every day, the MIDI.network gets some 30,000 hits and over 115 MB of MIDI songs are downloaded by our visitors.

MIDI-Network started of with a free site at Tripod, but soon we ran out of space and had to shift. Our next home was at Inbox.com.pk, but within six months our traffic had reached over 100 MB/day and we had to get going again. Now with its own domain name and hosted on the internet backbone, MIDI-Network.com has become a leading MIDI songs library - with the generous support of our visitors, and sponsors.

The MIDI.network is committed to giving you what you want. We have a free mailing list, MIDI-fans, that you can join and every month you'll recieve a list of the new songs we add. We also feature a Request-A-Song Service that allows you to ask us to find an MIDI you cannot find anywhere -- we'll fetch it for you in less than 48 hours, guaranteed!

We hope you enjoy your visit here, and don't forget to add this site to your bookmarks/favorites - you'll save yourself a lot of time when you're looking for quality MIDIs next time.

All suggestions and feedback is welcome at webmaster@midi-network.com.
