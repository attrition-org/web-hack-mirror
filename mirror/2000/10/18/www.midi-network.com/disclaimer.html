MIDI-Network.com Disclaimer

This disclaimer has been posted to address to concerns of music piracy, MIDI copyrights, and exchange of music online.

MIDI is a format in which music can be composed, edited, and exchanged without any voice content : these are not available from any recording companies or music retailers, but composed by music lovers and freelancer composers.

The intention of creating MIDI songs is to create media which can be quickly and easily transferred on the internet, and which can be used as background music for webpages. MIDIs do not violate copyrights of music companies because the original content is not duplicated; MIDIs are individual compositions, seperate from the original songs.

The role of MIDI-Network.com through this site is to allow people all over the world to download music they can utilise for personal use in karaoke, website development, or software development. If any MIDI composer has found any his/her copyrighted files on this site, these can be removed immediately on notification to the webmaster.

The goal of this site is to create a central place where music listeners can download MIDI songs, and where MIDI composers can upload their compositions. MIDI-Network.com only acts as an intermediary, and nearly 100% of our library consits of music files composed by a large number of individual composers.

MIDI sites are encouraged to use the MIDI files on this site, not by linking directly to them, but instead copying the MIDIs on their own servers/webspace. Any site found utilising MIDI-Network.com as a 'download site' will be in violation of this disclaimer and our usage policy, and could be penalised through their host or sponsors.
