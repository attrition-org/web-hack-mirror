MIDI-Network.com Privacy Policy

MIDI-Network.com is firmly committed to the privacy of our users, visitors, and mailing list members. MIDI-Network.com, our advertisers and partners are deeply committed to protecting the privacy of Internet users. Below is information outlining our continued support for the responsible management of consumer information.

Consumer Information
During your stay at MIDI-Network.com, we do not collect any personally identifiable information about you (such as name, address, phone number, or e-mail address). MIDI-network.com only requires visitors to fill out their name and e-mail address when they sign-up for our MIDI-fans Mailing List.

In no way are any of the e-mail addresses (of MIDI-fans members) sold or rented to any third-party.

Data Collection and Opt Out
MIDI-network.com does not utilise cookies or any other means to collect personally-identifiable information about our visitors. MIDI-network.com only utilises server log reports to track its traffic, website usage, referring sites, and most popular pages within the site for the sole purpose of maintenance of MIDI-Network.com and keeping a record of the site's usage.

At no time will MIDI-Network.com use any means to capture visitor e-mail addresses or personally-identifiable information of our visitors to sell or rent to any third-party, or for our own use.

Opt Out Procedure
If you would like to "opt out" of our MIDI-fans Mailing List, that is, to stop recieving monthly newsletters from MIDI-Network.com, kindly login to your eGroups account and remove yourself from our mailing list.

Ad Serving
MIDI-Network.com is able to provide you our free services because of the support of our advertisers. MIDI-Network.com utilises third party services to serve ads to our visitors. Our directly-placed advertising campaigns are delivered through the powerful AdCycle CGI/mySQL server, which provides us and our advertisers with targetted advertising and delivers precise reports.
