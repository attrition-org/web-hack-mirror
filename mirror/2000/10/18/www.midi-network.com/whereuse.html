Where can I use MIDIs ?

Where are MIDIs used? Everywhere!

The best thing about MIDIs is that they are perfect for storing on your PC as a collection - they take up such little disk space! And best of all, they can be used as background music for webpages!

Background music can be added to your page(s) by the following piece of HTML within the of your page :

< EMBED SRC="filename.mid" WIDTH="0" HEIGHT="0" >
where filename.mid is the name of the MIDI you want to play.

Just in case you were wondering, all those greeting card sites use MIDI files for the background music on their sites. And you can use songs downloaded from MIDI-Network.com

Apart from being used in webpages, MIDI songs are used for karaoke. You can compose a complete song on a digital keyboard and store it in MIDI format on your PC - and it will barely take any space at all! Since MIDIs do not store voice, you get crystal-clear karaoke sound from MIDIs.

MIDI files can also be embedded into Visual Basic applications, so your next VB project can bring some life into the application! You should check out sites offering Visual Basic tutorials and code snippets for finding out how to do this.
