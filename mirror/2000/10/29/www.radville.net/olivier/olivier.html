St. Olivier School, RCSSD #12, offers Kindergarten to Grade Six to 149 students in the Radville area. St. Olivier school employs 8.2 FTE staff. Our Principal is Mr. L. Weigel.

This web site is a result of our continuing effort to develop student and staff technological literacy. We intend to make this site a dynamic educational resource to aid ourselves and the global community. Look for recommended websites and student work in the grade pages represented on the menu to the left.

During the 1988-89 school year, we chose to review and revise our school's philosophy for living and learning together. The word C.A.R.E. represents:

COURTESY
APPRECIATION
RESPECT
ENTHUSIASM

St. Olivier staff and students work hard everyday to live and learn by this code of conduct. Our efforts have resulted in an academic environment which is safe, stimulating, and supportive.
