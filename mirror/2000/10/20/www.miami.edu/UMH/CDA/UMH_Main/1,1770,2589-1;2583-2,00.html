Dining and Entertainment

The University of Miami is a dynamic artistic community that includes the Lowe Art Museum, the Jerry Herman Ring Theatre, the Bill Cosford Cinema, and Gusman Concert Hall. The Food Court offers a variety of dining options to suit any taste. The Rathskeller offers an alternative option by providing home-cooked meals in a collegiate atmosphere.
