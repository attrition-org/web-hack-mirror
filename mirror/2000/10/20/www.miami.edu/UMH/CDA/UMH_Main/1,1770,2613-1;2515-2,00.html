Graduate Programs
Graduate education is an integral part of the University of Miami. The University's graduate programs include 85 master's and 55 doctoral programs coordinated through the Graduate School. These programs constitute 24 percent of all the University's students and 31 percent of all degrees it awards. Applications for admittance into graduate programs are made to the appropriate school/college or department.
