Publications
The University of Miami produces thousands of magazines, annual reports, newsletters, admissions viewbooks, guidebooks, maps, and brochures each year. These publications provide important news and information for alumni, students, prospective students, donors, parents, faculty, and staff. Many of these publications are now on-line with extensive archives. If you don’t find the specific publication you’re looking for here, perhaps it is linked from a different page within the University’s web site. Simply use the University’s search engine to help find other on-line publications. If you have questions about University publications, please call 305-284-5600.
