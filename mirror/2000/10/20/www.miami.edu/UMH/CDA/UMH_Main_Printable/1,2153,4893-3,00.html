School of Architecture
The School of Architecture’s design program combines academic and real-world experience and is set against the backdrop of South Florida’s evolving urban landscape. Faculty and students reach beyond the discipline of architecture to consult with artists, historians, social scientists, economists, engineers and members of government and community groups.
