TECHNICAL SUPPORT SERVICES

Dominant Systems offers technical support for a wide range of computer and network products, and we offer technical support programs to meet the needs of a wide range of organizations.  Our coverage area for on-site service is generally Southern Michigan and Northern Ohio, but we sell products, consult,  and do help desk support for customers all across the United States.

Our expertise is in the Hardware, Operating System, and Network layers.  We support the top products for Windows 95/98, Windows NT/2000, SCO UNIX, Linux, HP/UX and Netware networks. We have the resources to deal with your toughest technical support issues when they arise.  Together with our access to manufacturer technical support, even the largest end-user organizations will benefit from our services.   See below for more information and pricing on the services we can offer to your organization.
