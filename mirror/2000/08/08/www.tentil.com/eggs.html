3D Screensaver (works in Windows98 also)

Right click a blank spot on your desktop. Click Properties Click the Screen Saver tab From the Screen Saver drop down menu choose, 3D Text. Click the Settings button. In the text box type the word volcano. Click OK. The screen saver will now display a list of volcanoes from around the world.

Start Button fun:
Windows98:

  • Click the Start button and select Programs > Accessories.
  • Right Click Calculator and select Properties.
  • Click Change Icon... button and then select the same icon. (Double Click it) Click Ok
  • Click the Start button again. (wait for tool tip to disappear)
  • A small down arrow will appear allowing you to scroll the Shut Down... menu. Notice the new item Eject PC *smile*
  • (sometimes you have to repeat the steps a few times before the down arrow will appear)
