he beginning of our story finds our heroine, an adroit mortal Mom, living on the shores of beautiful Lake Texoma in rural Oklahoma. The picture includes two perfect examples of human offspring. Alex, a Freshman at Kingston High School is a science star and math wiz. Al is also a saxophonist for " the pride of Texomaland" Kingston High School Marching Band. Andy is ten and in the fifth grade at Kingston Elementary. She is a member of K.A.C.E. Choir, a 4.0 student and the creator of some amazing art.
And then, with a click of a mouse... the beep of the dial... the screech of the modem... the transformation takes place. Our title character becomes netizen TenTil... Web author... Beta tester... Script writer... Graphic artist... and ruler of the Palace, TenTil's Nine. No longer limited by the restraints of space, with the world literally at her fingertips, TenTil finds time to be her only enemy now.
