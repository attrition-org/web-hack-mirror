As I saw year 2000 compliance statements popping up all across the net I started thinking. Maybe I need a Y2K statement here at tentil.com. After all, as a webmaster isn't it my ethical duty to disavow any and all liability for the problems coming with the year 2000?

Naw..    When the millennium dawns and the power fails, here at tentil.com, I'm going to be more worried about my old body being Y2K compliant than my website  :-)
