About Us

Computer Safari is a division of SuperGeeks Corporation. SuperGeek's mission is to provide complete computing solutions for consumers and small to medium sized businesses.

Computer Safari's goal is to provide computer enrichment to children, preschool to grade five. Upon graduation from Computer Safari, students then enroll in Geek University.
