Send Your Child on an Adventure of Discovery and Fun!

Ice Cream Truck

Your children will get “two scoops” of mathematics in this fun-filled course as they get behind the wheel of their own Ice Cream Truck and experience real-world applications of mathematics. Students learn the unique relationship between cost, pricing and profit while having fun selling ice cream and improving their math skills.

World Traveler

Students go on an exciting adventure into geography as they explore other cultures, animals, and plants of different parts of the world. Activities include using software to visit cities and uncover the mysteries behind historic landmarks. Students will love the bold illustrations and fun animations as they discover the joys of travel.

Power Keyboarding

Tired of watching your child hunt and peck?! In this class students have fun improving their typing skills through educational exercises. Students will be grouped by age: grades 2-4 and 5-12. The material is tailored to their skill and interest levels. Don’t miss this important class. Give your children a powerful head start in this Digital Era.

Road Adventure USA

Your child will be in the driver’s seat in this geography and math based adventure. They will develop their decision-making and problem-solving abilities while managing a budget, reading street maps, and handling random roadside emergencies. Students develop invaluable planning skills as they try to arrive at their mystery destination before their time, money and luck runs out!

Incredible World Of Insects

Entomology rules in this fun class as students go on an interactive journey into the “Incredible World of Insects”. Students will learn important science concepts and build critical thinking skills while exploring a virtual insect world and interacting with the insects in their natural habitats. Exciting new feature in this course: Students use a computerized 200x microscope to study insects at a microscopic level!

Digital Microscope

Your child’s imagination will run wild in this unique course as students use the QX3 Computer Microscope. This high-tech microscope detaches from its stand, allowing high power viewing of anything from fingers to crawling insects. Students will be able to save, print and personalize images taken with the microscope. This cool program provides more fun than you ever thought possible as they explore, expand and manipulate the world around them.
