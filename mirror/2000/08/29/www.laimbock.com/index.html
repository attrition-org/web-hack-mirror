It took us almost an hour to get me to the Hilton were I was staying and I heard today that it is upposed to be a 10 minute drive....

I immediately jumped out of the bed which was stupid as I had a major hangover, showered, packed and arrived only 10 minutes late at the meeting which had not even started. So I am sitting there feeling totally, absolutely, mega wasted and then suddenly Fuan comes over and hands me his business card, whispers to look at the backside of it and goes back to his seat looking even more hung over than me.
