MartNet Communications - Company Profile
MartNet is a privately owned Internet Service Provider (ISP) located in Philadelphia, PA. We currently maintain one office and Data Center at 1241 Carpenter Street, Philadelphia, PA 19147. We also maintain a satellite office and Data Center in University City, Philadelphia.

MartNet offers a wide range of Business and Personal Internet services, all of which are competitively priced. We also offer FREE Web Site Hosting to certified non-profit groups.

Being a Regional Internet Service Provider gives us several advantages, which help us, offer better service to our customers. We offer a more personal level of support if you run into trouble using the system. We can test and implement the newest Internet technologies quickly without a lot of overhead or system downtime, and it is easy to make these features available to you. The MartNet Philosophy
We understand how difficult it can be for small companies without an army of Engineers to get connected to the Internet, and use the Internet as an effective way to communicate their ideas and reach a worldwide audience. Our goal at MartNet is to develop technical solutions that allow any individual or small business to effectively use the Internet, both as a marketing tool and as a communications medium--at a price that they can easily afford. We believe the best way to achieve this goal is through the use of standard, extensible, inexpensive hardware and software instead of proprietary, custom, expensive solutions common throughout the Internet today.

MartNet Communications maintains professional working relationships with talented local programmers, artists, graphic designers, and Internet Marketing firms, who make their talents available to our customers at a reasonable cost. Together, we provide our customers with the best possible Internet Solutions at the lowest possible cost.

Company History
MartNet Communications was founded in 1995 by Nicholas P. Schummer and K. Nikolas Weidenbacher in hopes of offering reliable, affordable, and easy-to use Internet Access. Since then, MartNet has grown considerably. We are now able to offer a full range of both personal and business Internet Services which include the following: Dial-up access, Personal Unix shell accounts, advanced World Wide Web Development and Hosting, dedicated and co-located server hosting, high speed dedicated access, and affordable E-commerce solutions for small businesses.

Back in 1995, we ran MartNet out of our house in Philadelphia in our spare time, Word spread quickly, and suddenly we outgrew the house, ran out of 'spare-time', and realized that we had grown a rock solid business. MartNet has become a full-time career for us.

Our primary business office and Data Center is in South Philadelphia, and we also maintain a satellite office and data center in University City. We have sustained working relationships with many Freelance Programmers, Graphic Designers, Web Developers, and Illustrators, all of whom make their talents available to MartNet customers at a reasonable cost.
