Proceedings of the Institute of Phonetic Sciences, Amsterdam

The Proceedings of the Institute of Phonetic Sciences, Amsterdam is an annual publication of our institute in which staf, guest researchers, and students present work done at the institute. In general, these papers contain material not (yet) published elsewhere.

  • IFA Proceedings 15 (1991)
  • IFA Proceedings 16 (1992)
  • IFA Proceedings 17 (1993)
  • IFA Proceedings 18 (1994)
  • IFA Proceedings 19 (1995)
  • IFA Proceedings 20 (1996)
  • IFA Proceedings 21 (1997)
  • IFA Proceedings 22 (1998)

From number 18 (1994) on we intend to publish the Proceedings of the Institute of Phonetic Sciences, Amsterdam in digital form (HTML with in-line graphics, Postscript files, and other formats if appropriate). How this is done is described on a separate page. In addition, some papers from older proceedings are available in digital format.

We cannot guarantee that the on-line versions of our papers are identical to the hard-copy versions. In case of doubt, consult our "paper" proceedings or send e-mail to: ifa@hum.uva.nl.
