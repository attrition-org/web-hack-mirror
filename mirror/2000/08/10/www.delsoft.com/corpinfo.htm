Background
Delsoft (India) Pvt. Ltd. is a leading software developer and provider of key technologies and services in the areas of EDA, Video Compression (MPEG), and ASIC design. Delsoft was founded in early 1995 by a group of young professionals imbued with the desire to create innovative technological solutions. Delsoft’s services are available worldwide through its strategic partner, San Jose based INTERRA INC. Delsoft specializes in providing offshore software development facility with its vast experience and well defined process in  managing large off shore projects. 
Areas of work
Delsoft has three main domains of specialization
EDA  
EDA unit of Delsoft specializes in Hardware Description Languages (HDLs) and their compilers. These languages include Verilog HDL, VHDL, EDIF LEF, and DEF among others. Compilers for these languages are available as building blocks along with language independent common data model that can be used as front ends for EDA tools. In addition to compiler, Delsoft’s EDA technology profile includes RTL synthesis and Schematic Generation.  

DVT  
Delsoft’s Digital Video Technologies concentrates on verification analysis, verification and compliance software for compressed audio & video (MPEG) digital and optical disc markets. The tools have been developed for DVB and ATSC standards for the broadcast industry and DVD video content analysis for stored media. These core technologies are being marketed worldwide in the form of following products- DProbe, MProbe and Surveyor.  

VLSI Design Services 
Delsoft specializes in providing turnkey solutions: designing VLSIs, both front-end for image processing and telecom (ASICs, memories, custom cells) and back ends, modeling complex VLSI devices such as microprocessors, peripherals, and memories. 
Front-end services comprise Verilog and VHDL-based design modeling, verification, simulation, synthesis and timing analysis using industry standard tools. Back-end expertise is in extractions and rule checking on Virtuoso, Dracula, Diva and GDT, as well as placing and routing with Silicon Ensemble and Cell3. 

Resources
Delsoft’s biggest asset is its team. Technical contribution from a team of around 70 highly qualified software and design engineers - equipped with a laboratory-like environment, Delsoft emphasizes the need to nurture and provide opportunities for creativity and excellence, which are also the hallmarks of its technological solutions.  
With a long-standing relationship with the IITs, Delsoft has consistently been staffed with some of the best resources from these institutes. Delsoft has also sponsored joint projects with the Computer Sciences Department of IIT, Kharagpur 
Locations
Delsoft has two state-of-the-art development centers: one at NOIDA, near New Delhi, the other at Salt Lake, Calcutta. The focus of the development center at Calcutta is EDA and ASIC design; while the focus of the development center at NOIDA is DVT, EDA, and ASIC design. Both the centers are equipped with state-of-the-art tools and workstations. Delsoft’s services and technologies are marketed worldwide by San Jose based Interra Inc, and its distributors in Japan, Europe, Israel, Taiwan and Korea.
