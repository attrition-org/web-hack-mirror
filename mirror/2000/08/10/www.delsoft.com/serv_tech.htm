Delsoft’s DVT offerings: 
Delsoft’s focus in the digital video arena has been on software-based verification solutions for MPEG and DVD professionals. The technologies developed at Delsoft offer comprehensive analysis, compliance testing, and trouble-shooting for organizations involved in the development of MPEG products, DVD products, and DVD content. It has successfully developed the following products in a joint development effort with Interra: 
  • Mprobe – MPEG Stream Analyzer 
  • Dprobe  - DVD Video Analyzer
  • Surveyor – DVD data Analyzer
Besides the technology offerings, Delsoft also provides offshore custom software development services in the digital audio and video domain. It has successfully executed several projects on Unix and Windows including device drivers, reference design kits, hardware debuggers, and complex GUI projects.

Delsoft’s ASIC design offerings: 
Delsoft’s Design Services concentrates on providing turnkey design services, including both front-end ASIC design and back-end tasks.  

Front-end design covers, ASIC (both Verilog and VHDL) RTL designs, RTL coding, synthesis (functional and timing), RTL and gate level simulation and timing analysis.  
Telecom chips, Mpeg encoder blocks, and image-processing applications are some of the areas in which Delsoft engineers have developed RTLs which have been successfully taken to Silicon.  

Backend Design Services offers RAM design, Cell library development, technology migration and re-targeting of ASIC and cores to improve performance and reduce product costs. Delsoft specializes in development and verification of cell libraries (macrocells, I/O's and data-path cells) including circuit design, cell layout,  design rule checking, logic to physical checking, spice simulations, timing models and timing verification.
