Regency Railings baluster elements are available FLAT (for installation on straight staircases and straight railings) or RADIUSED (for installation on curved staircases with a minimum of 28" radius and curved level railings with a minimum of 24" radius).

Regency Railings balusters were designed to meet national building codes and most local codes including the 4" sphere when assembled in accordance with the layouts in our drawings.

Regency Railings offers a variety of different hand forged ornamental designs but we are unable to produce custom ironwork, supply any handrails, apply the finish paint or provide the installation service.

Regency Railings develops all designs to meet national industry code requirements.  However, building code requirements and safety rules vary from one locality to another and it is incumbent upon the architect, builder, designer and installer to familiarize themselves and comply with these codes and regulations.
