Benefits for You

Regency Railings can provide you or your client with prefabricated hand forged baluster elements that are designed to add elegance to any project.

We reduce your cost dramatically when compared to custom ironwork by simplifying the design, manufacturing , layout and installation processes.

All of Regency Railings' designs have the full compliment of hand forged balusters needed for installation on any staircase (regardless of radius or slope) and level railings.

Regency Railings currently offers seven basic designs and most of these designs can be installed in different configurations to create many additional patterns.  Your options are limited only by your imagination and local building codes.

We provide sample installation photos and application drawings within this web site to suggest ways of combining our hand forged baluster elements to  create a truly custom looking application.  Just click on the photos or application drawings that interest you and let your imagination take over.
