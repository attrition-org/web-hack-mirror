City Government

The City Government websection introduces the City's Mayor, City Manager, City Solicitor, and Council members. The City Clerk's section provides a variety of records including by-laws, council minutes, City Council agenda, a yearly calendar of events, and election and census information. In addition, a section on the Freedom of Information and Protection of Privacy Act introduces FOIP.   City government information will be of educational interest to students and teachers particularly those dealing with grade six social studies curriculum.  What's New? provides the latest City Government news.

Purchasing and Stores provides information for doing business with the City with a list of opportunities in five areas:  Construction, Services, Equipment, Supplies/Commodities, Electric; and information on licensing, submitting bids, the process of selection, the award process, and how to get paid.
