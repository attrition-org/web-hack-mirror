General Information:  Search Centre

Welcome to the Search Centre for the City of Lethbridge website. Here, we provide for searching and also provide an explanation of the query language.  Examples are provided.

Use the form below to search for documents in this web that contain a specific word or phrase, or combinations of words.  The text search engine will display a weighted list of matching documents in order of best matches.

Each list item links to a matching document.  It is expected that all City website documents are appropriately titled to facilitate easy bookmarking and referencing. The title will be shown.

People wishing to assist the City of Lethbridge to maintain a high quality list of information about its website documents can fill out a feedback form, commenting on the specific pages.

Query Language

The text search engine allows queries to be formed from arbitrary Boolean expressions containing the keywords AND, OR, and NOT, and grouped with parentheses. For example:

seal flag
finds and lists documents containing either 'seal' or 'flag'

seal or flag
same as above

seal and flag
finds and lists documents that contain both  'seal' and 'flag'

seal not flag
finds and lists documents that contain 'seal'; 'flag' will not be in the document

(seal not flag) and Government
finds documents containing 'Government', plus 'seal' but not 'flag'

Carpent*
finds documents containing words starting with 'Carpent', such as "Carpenter" or "Carpentar".

Use the asterisk character as a wildcard when you are unsure of the correct spelling of a word.  Similarly, if the form of a word may appear in multiple ways such as "informatics" "information" "informing" "inform".
