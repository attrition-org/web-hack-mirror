Pure Nature was founded in July 1997 in Colorado as a Limited Liability Company. Mr. Joey Canyon is the majority stockholder.

The company is headquartered in Denver, Colorado. The business operates as a health, beauty and nutrition supplements company.

The Company will produce high quality vitamins, minerals, beauty products and nutritional supplements to the ever increasing health and nutrition markets (easily exceeding $7 billion a year in sales).

These Products will be endorsed and presented through all advertising mediums by top celebrities to include music, television, motion picture, sports personalities, etc. Several personalities are already under contract.
