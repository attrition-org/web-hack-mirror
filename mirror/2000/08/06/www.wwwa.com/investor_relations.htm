Customer Focus
The Company is a business-to-business service and technology company. Our focus is on corporations and global enterprises who have the competitive and economic mandate to integrate e-business technologies with Customer Relationship Management processes (e-CRM).

Our Market Opportunity & Value Proposition
We specialize in the growing market of e-CRM, the application of Web and Internet technologies to a company’s customer-related processes and communications as they interact across all customer-facing business functions and audiences: e-support; e-sales; e-marketing; other CRM e-services.

Using e-business technologies to create e-CRM systems is now mission critical to all enterprises that must meet the competitive and economic pressures of a global market and an Internet-enabled customer. We provide the software, services, and hosting to integrate and automate these e-CRM functions.

Products & Services
Web Associates offers customer value in the following service and product areas:

  1. Custom installation and management of server-based, proprietary e-CRM Web systems
  2. Integration of third-party applications and databases
  3. Customized partner and proprietary software development and integration
  4. e-CRM application hosting
  5. Web system support and maintenance services

Core Competencies

  • Strategic consultation expertise, including e-business strategic implementation issues and the application of Web technology to e-CRM systems architecture

  • Design and development expertise, including commerce and support software application development; human interface design; Web systems and information architecture; and multimedia development

  • Technology expertise, including Web and Internet infrastructure and process architectures; multi-language and multi-database integration; electronic publishing; e-Commerce, e-Support and other e-services architectures; and Web-enabled Data structures and data flow

Market Potential
"Volume of b2b eCommerce Is Poised for Exponential Growth From $187 Million in 1998 to $8.5 trillion in 2003" (Source: IDC). This is the prime driver for the demand for e-CRM systems.

Revenues in the emerging market of worldwide Internet services grew 71% in 1998 to reach $7.8 billion. Revenues are expected to grow at a CAGR of 60% and exceed $78 billion by 2003 (Source: Wall Street Journal, 7/99).
