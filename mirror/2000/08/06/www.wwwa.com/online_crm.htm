Why CRM Is Becoming "e-CRM"
CRM is a conscious, proactive approach to customer service, sales, and support communications and processes. Management of customer relationships has traditionally been expensive and time-consuming, especially when ongoing service and support is required.

Online Customer Relationship Management—customer-directed, Web-based CRM—uses the Web to integrate and simplify customer-related business processes.

Online CRM Is Better for People
Successful "Online Customer Relationship Management" empowers customers with on-demand information and service that's less expensive and far more convenient than traditional services.

Online CRM Is Better for Business
Customer-directed commerce and support allows businesses to widen and simplify distribution while increasing customer satisfaction and loyalty. Companies that successfully use e-CRM realize huge cost savings by offering support services automatically through smart Web site applications.

The primary goal of eCRM is to make services more convenient and less expensive for customers. This in turn facilitates the company's fundamental goals of increasing revenues and reducing costs through improved customer satisfaction and new effiencies created by technology.
