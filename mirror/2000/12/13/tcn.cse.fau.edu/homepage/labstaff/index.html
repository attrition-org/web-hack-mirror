Student Participation Makes Difference!

Student participation is always viewed instrumental in the successful operations of the lab. We have a volunteer program that allows students to participate on a volunteer basis all lab activities including system administration. Students with enthusiasm to learn are given access to the resources available based on their levels of participation. It may range from helping out in a single event to full system administration responsibilities. Their contributions have made it possible for the lab to provide computing services to the users in a scale that normally requires full-time employees. We credit our success largely to all the student volunteers who have participated in the establishment, daily as well as special activities of the lab. Their hardworking have made the lab an important and pleasant resource of learning for students.
