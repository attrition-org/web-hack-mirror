ICANN

Letter from Rep. Howard L. Berman,
Ranking Member of Subcommittee on Courts and Intellectual Property,
U.S. House Judiciary Committee

July 29, 1999

Mr. Michael Roberts
Interim President and Chief Executive Officer
Internet Corporation For Assigned Names and Numbers
4676 Admiralty Way, Suite 330
Marina del Rey, CA 90292

Dear Mr. Roberts:

Thank you very much for appearing before the Courts and Intellectual Property Subcommittee on July 28, 1999. I am writing to request additional information to supplement your testimony.

Please describe what mechanisms ICANN has in place to receive and act upon complaints from members of the public that ICANN-accredited registrars are not operating in accordance with their Registration Accreditation Agreements. To whom should such complaints be directed? How fast will ICANN commit to act upon them and respond to the complainant?

The hearing record remains open through August 5, 1999. However, if you will be unable to respond by this date, please contact Stacy Baird in my office at 202-225-4695, and we will try to make other arrangements to include your response in the record.

Sincerely,

Howard L. Berman
Member of Congress
