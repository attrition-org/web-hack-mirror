Proposed Amendments to ICANN Bylaws

Posted for Public Review and Comment: 8 October, 1999
Deadline for Public Comments: 22 October, 1999

A set of proposed amendments to the existing ICANN Bylaws has been posted for public review and comment. The proposed amendments include (1) amendments implementing the resolution on At Large Membership adopted by the Board in Santiago; (2) amendments implementing the ASO proposal recognized in Santiago (subject to the signing of the Memorandum of Understanding by ICANN, ARIN, APNIC, and RIPE-NCC); (3) a redefinition of the term "Initial Board" to provide that once SO-elected directors are seated the entire Board -- including the SO-elected directors -- is responsible for all future decisions of the Board; and (4) various clarifying and editorial changes.

Because they are intended to implement prior Board resolutions and to faciliate the transition to a Board of elected Directors, these amendments are appropriate for action by the Initial Board and will be posted for public review and comment for two weeks, until October 22, 1999. The Initial Board will take them up in a telephone meeting shortly thereafter.
