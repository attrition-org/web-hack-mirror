ICANN Meetings in Cairo

March 7-10, 2000

 

 

ICANN's next round of meetings will be held on March 7-10, 2000, in Cairo, Egypt. Details about the meetings are being posted on this page as the become available. Please check back often!

ICANN thanks the members of the local host committee, chaired by Tarek Kamel and sponsored by the Egyptian Ministry of Communication, the Cabinet Information and Decision Support Center (IDSC), and the Internet Society of Egypt.

AGENDA FOR ICANN PUBLIC FORUM AND BOARD MEETING
Updated 20 February, 2000

The preliminary agenda for the ICANN Public Forum on March 9 calls for review and discussion of the following:

  • At Large membership and elections
  • Considerations and policies relating to creation of new top-level domain name registries and famous names issues
  • ccTLD delegation and administration policies
  • ICANN budget for 2000-2001 fiscal year
  • Final approval of Independent Review Policy
  • Bylaws revision to allow reassignment of Original ASO Director terms

The specific schedule for the Public Forum, 9 March, is posted above.

Links to relevant documents, proposals, community feedback, and draft resolutions will be posted on this page as they become available. ICANN will be posting URLs for public comments on specific items, along with web page archives of comments received. Comments on the agenda itself should be sent to Andrew McLaughlin.
