Announcement

24 May 2000

Comments due by June 1 on ICANN Budget for FY00-01

(May 24, 2000 - Marina del Rey, California, USA) -- A proposed ICANN budget for FY00-01, beginning July 1, 2000, has been posted at <http://www.icann.org/financials/proposed-budget-04may00.htm>. Comments on the proposed budget are invited and may be made at <http://www.icann.org/mbx/budget/>. The deadline for comments that will be considered by the ICANN Board prior to its adoption of next year's budget is June 1.
