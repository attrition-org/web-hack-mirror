ICANN Announcement 01 February 1999

Public Comment Period for Singapore Board meeting agenda

February 01, 1999



In accordance with the ICANN Bylaws, background materials, reports and recommendations to the Board for consideration as part of the posted agenda for the March 2-4 meeting of the Board will be posted for public comment on or about February 8. Comments from the public on the agenda materials will be considered by the Board prior to any decisions taken in Singapore.
