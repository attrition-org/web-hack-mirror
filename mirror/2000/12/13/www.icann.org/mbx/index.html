ICANN Public Comment Forum

Welcome to the ICANN Public Comment Forum. Below is a list of topics that are currently open for comment. Your input on these issues is important to us and to the success of the ICANN policy formation process.

The ICANN Public Comment Forum is designed to provide interested Internet users with means to communicate online with each other, and with the ICANN Board of Directors and staff, about pending ICANN issues and activities. ICANN will regularly post proposals and recommendations for public review and comment. The Public Comment Forum provides an open threaded messaging mechanism in which public comments can be posted, reviewed, and responded to.

To participate, please visit the registration page and sign up.

Please note this is an experimental interface, built to facilitate thoughtful discussion of the issues currently before the ICANN community. However, the software is under development, and there will undoubtedly be some problems. If you discover any problems or bugs, or have comments on the operation of this forum, please notify us at forum@icann.org.
