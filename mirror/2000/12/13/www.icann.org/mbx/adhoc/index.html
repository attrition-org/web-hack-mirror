ICANN Public Comment Forum

Ad Hoc Group on Numbering and Addressing

This Public Comment Forum is dedicated to the work of the Ad Hoc Group on Numbering and Addressing. Click here to see the ad hoc group's charter, background, and schedule.

This forum is reserved for discussion of issues concerning IP Address policy. If your comment is not about Internet Protocol Addressing (numbers like 193.0.0.193 or ::ffff:193.0.0.193) please do not make them on this forum. Off-topic posts made here are periodically deleted. If you wish to make a post on a topic other than IP Address policy, please make it to another forum. Miscellaneous posts can be made to the Off Topic Forum.

To post a message in this forum, you must first register. You need only register once -- your username and password will work on all ICANN public comment forums.
