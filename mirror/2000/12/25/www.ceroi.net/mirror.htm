CEROI Indicators and Encyclopedia launched! (November 2000)
CEROI has developed core and expanded sets of indicators; cities may begin entering data using Publikit 1.2. The Encyclopedia explains each indicator in detail.

SoE for Vennesla, Norway  (November 2000)
Developed by students in cooperation with the municipality, this bilingual report discusses the efforts by industry to reduce waste and the effects of power lines on health.

Publikit 1.2 released  (October 2000) 
This updated version features access to the CEROI indicators database, general template improvements and the Publikit Development Kit (PDK) for creating tailor-made templates.
