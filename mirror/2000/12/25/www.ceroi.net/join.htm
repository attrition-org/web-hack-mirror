Join the CEROI Programme!

Access to environmental information can be crucial for ensuring relevant policy responses to urban environmental problems. Environmental information that is easy to access and to understand is also important for increasing public awareness and empowering urban stakeholders in a meaningful way.

Although many cities may have environmental data available, it can be fragmented and accessible only to a few people. The CEROI Programme facilitates cities in turning environmental data into usable information and in disseminating this information effectively. Member cites produce indicator-based State of the Environment (SoE) reports and publish these on the Internet.

By using the Internet, the information is not only accessible to the city residents, but also to other cities striving to manage similar environmental issues.

Benefits for member cities
 Member cities are offered the Publikit software with an integrated template for SoE reports. Publikit is a complete web-publishing tool, tailor- made for State of Environment reporting. Member cities can create urban reports with a minimum of web knowledge; once completed, the city is offered space on the CEROI server.

A CEROI Secretariat has been set up by UNEP/GRID-Arendal to serve the CEROI network. The CEROI website serves as a resource for SoE reporting, using environmental indicators and a forum where cities share experiences. The CEROI Secretariat is also working to attract funding to cities with constrained economy.
