 CEROI and Publikit at UDMS 2000
(September 2000) 
CEROI participated in the 22nd Symposium of the Urban Data Management Society in Delft, The Netherlands. An important aim of the international society is to provide a forum for discussing new approaches, considering new technologies and sharing practical experiences in the field of urban data management. CEROI and Publikit were presented as tools for improving access to urban environmental information.

 State of the Environment Report for Tbilisi released  (August 2000)
The capital of Georgia is in need of developing a city environmental action plan to address urgent problems, concludes a report prepared by GRID-Tbilisi. The report highlights challenging issues such as increases in transport-related air and noise pollution, as well as in the spread of infectious diseases due to contaminated drinking water.

 SoE Kyiv available through the CEROI gateway (July 2000)
The environmental report for the capital of Ukraine has been added to the list of CEROI reports.

 Version 1.1 of Publikit released (July 2000) Publikit version 1.1 has been released.  Major new features include support for ESRI shape files, easy creation of animations and a spell check.

 CEROI presented in Australia (May 2000)
CEROI was very well received at the first national SoE conference in Coffs Harbour, Australia 3-5 May. Representatives from various councils, goverment agencies and the private sector showed great interest in the programme and gave positive feedback. CEROI participated through presentations, workshops and an information stand.

 Welcome to the new CEROI GATEWAY 
(May 2000)
 With the launcing of the new web site, the CEROI Programme is now ready for implementation. The website serves as a gateway to urban environmental information and a meeting place for participating cities. 

 Hannover Conference  
(February 2000)
CEROI participated in the Third European Conference on Sustainable Cities and Towns, also known as the Hannover Conference 2000.
