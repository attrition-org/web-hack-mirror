CEROI Indicator Resources

The indicator resources of the CEROI Programme has two main parts:  

Comparison of urban indicator data

A limited set of core indicators are common for all CEROI cities, making it possible to compare some key values in an easy way.  These indicators will be accessible both through individual city reports and through this site. 

We are currently collecting indicator data from member cities. The data will be published here as soon as they are available.

In the meantime, please use the Indicators Encyclopedia to examine detailed information about individual indicators. Here you can also access the CEROI core indicator set. The new and revised core set has been finalised based on feedback from CEROI cities.


Indicators - what and why?

Indicators encyclopedia

The Indicator Enyclopedia offers detailed information about individual indicators such as measurements, references and examples.

The encyclopedia assists cities in selecting indicators by developing lists of proposed indicators for individual issues and themes. 

City-specific indicators for individual themes make up the main part of a city SoE report. 

DPSIR  is proposed as a framework for presenting city-specific indicators within CEROI reports.
