Members Area 

The Members area is the place where cities can find support for their State of Environment reporting. The CEROI Secretariat will continuously add resources to this site; upcoming is a training manual to guide cities from signing up as a CEROI member to publishing reports.

A database of 90 urban environmental indicators has recently been added on-line; using Publikit 1.2, cities may enter information about CEROI core indicators as well as about city-specific indicators.

Member cities are encouraged to share their experiences in developing SoE reports in the Discussion Forum. Best practices will then be added to this site.

The November edition of the CEROI Newsletter is available on-line; information on recent CEROI activites and future plans are included.
