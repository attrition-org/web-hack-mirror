Some facts you should know:

  1. Not every location can get DSL. The telephone company's (telco) central office (CO) serving your location must be equipped with DSL capability. Several suppliers such as Rhythm and Northpoint are installing equipment for this purpose.

    Click here to find out if DSL is available in your central office

  2. The telco will not have lines available for every DSL implementation requested. An order must be place to determine line availability.

  3. The distance from the CO determines the speeds available at your location.

    Click here to get an estimate of speed available for your location.

  4. Installation times vary greatly and can be very long. For this reason we strongly advise customers to keep present systems and procedures in place until the DSL line is installed and tested.

  5. Not all DSL offerings by ISPs are equal, some are considerably oversold, thereby limiting your throughput. We are not over subscribed,

  6. DSL service from MIC&S minimizes lost productivity due to employee misuse of the net by filtering out pornography and sexually explicit content. See business surfing facts

  7. MIC&S content filtering lowers risk of litigation on sexual harassment grounds involving Internet content. Be safe, not sorry.
