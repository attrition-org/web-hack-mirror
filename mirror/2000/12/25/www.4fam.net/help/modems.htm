Help:  Modems

We strongly recommend Zoom™ Industry Standard Architecture (ISA), Zoom PCI Plus, and external modems.  They have a seven year guarantee and lightening protection built in!

We can not and do not recommend any PCI modems (other than the Zoom above). The PCI modems are inexpensive software modems that generally don't work as well as the more expensive, but much higher quality, modems which use a Digital Signal Processor (DSP)
