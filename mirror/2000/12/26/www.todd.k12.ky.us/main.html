The mission of the Todd County School District is to ensure that all students are provided the opportunity to acquire the skills necessary for a lifetime of learning and to be productive, responsible citizens.

The Todd County Board of Education extends to you, our visitor, a warm welcome to our website.  We invite you to tour our site by following the links for district information and to access the websites for the four individual schools in our district.  

We believe we have many great things happening in our schools.  Be sure to take a look a our 1999 CATS scores and our School Report Cards to see for yourself.  We take pride in our students, as you will see as you tour these pages.
