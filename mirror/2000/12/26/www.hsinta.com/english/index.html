   About Hsinta

Hsinta Electronic Manufacturing Co., Ltd is an award winning manufacturer of Printed Circuit Boards located in Hsin-Chu, Taiwan. Hsinta's state of the art 85400 sq/ft manufacturing facility with fully computerized production lines manufactures Single Sided PC Boards, Double Sided PC Boards, Silver Though Hole PC Boards, and Carbon Printed PC Boards. Hsinta's highly professional team with hundreds of years of collective experience are fully committed to the principles of best quality, service and achieve the fastest-time-to-market in the Electronics Industry. Quantity support ranges from a few pieces to thousands, and rest assured that Hsinta's specialized engineers will be able to provide you with fast turnaround and accurate quotes.

Hsinta also invests in Deltar Electronics, Ltd. in the USA. With its patented new technology, Deltar manufactures Digital Counter, Digital Timer, and Temperature Controller Control Instrumentation for the Automation Industry plus Blood Pressure Monitors for a wide range of medical uses.
