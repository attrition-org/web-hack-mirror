About the Town

Jesolo has a wide variety of activities day and night. Below are some of the local attractions, most of which are within walking distance from the hotel.

The Beach!

Jesolo's main attraction is its 15km of sandy beaches, offering shallow and calm shores. Principe Palace has its own private beach area, with deck chairs and parasols free of charge. Many sports, such as parascending, water-skiing and jetskis are available.

1 minute walk away!

Shops and restaraunts

For either the day or the evening, Jesolo's promenade area offers a choice for everyone. An endless world which you explore by wandering between exciting places, shows and events, as well as bars, clubs and restaurants of all kinds that are open day and night.

From 8 o'clock in the evening to 6 o'clock in the morning, Jesolo is open only to pedestrian traffic and is therefore more suitable for both couples and families taking a long, safe evening stroll.

Aqualandia

One of the top attractions for families, Aqualandia offers the highest water slides in Italy. Altenatively you can relax by the pools or take in the adventure mini golf course.

Only ten minutes walk away from the hotel.

Go Karting

Full-size karting track with full speed karts!

Only fifteen minutes walk away from the hotel.

Play Village

Local sports centre, offering skate-boarding, tarmac football and tennis courts and also skate hire.

Only ten minutes walk away from the hotel.

Fishing

Jesolo offers both freshwater and sea fishing, giving you the perfect opportunity to catch the one to tell your friends about.

For more information visit the official Jesolo town website.
