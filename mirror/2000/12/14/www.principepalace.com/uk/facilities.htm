Facilities

The management likes to offer a warm welcome, and would like to remind all visitors that the entire staff are at your disposal. We offer many services, including:

Rooms Double, triple and four bedded rooms are available, all with shower (with limited available with a bath), WC, balcony, satellite TV, fridge, central heating and air conditioning, telephone, hair dryer and safety deposit box.
Wheelchair-friendly The hotel is easy for disabled people in wheelchairs to get around. All floors are serviced by lifts, with wide corridors and ramps throughout all areas.
Parking Private, secure parking is available free of charge within the grounds of the hotel.

Conference room

 With seating available for 60+ people, the conference room is fully equipped with business facilities such as speakers, TV projector and conference tables, also suitable for board games. Internet connection is also available. More information...
Restaurant Located on the 4th floor, accessible by stairs or lift.
Breakfast Buffet Breakfast is served in the restaurant on the 4th floor. Many hot and cold items are available every morning, including English breakfast.

Dinner

Dinner is a waiter service with a choice of menu from our award-winning chef, together with an open buffet salad and mix of regional specialities.

Vegetarian and other special dietary needs are fully catered for. Please specify any requirements when booking.
Telephone & Internet A telephone is provided in each room, allowing calls to reception and outside. Most rooms have sockets allowing for laptop computers to connect to the Internet. (Please specify at time of booking if this is a requirement.)
Television Each room has a remote controlled TV with Italian, English, German and French language satellite channels .

Reception desk

 Our experienced reception staff will be happy to assist you with any questions you may have. We offer a currency exchange facility, car hire or use of the free hotel bikes, arrangements for taxis or bus timetables, doctors and any other information you require.

Private Beach

The hotel private beach is across Mazzini square in front of the hotel. No beach pass or room key is required on entry and deck chairs with parasols are provided free of charge.

Sports

For the sporting enthusiast Jesolo offers a wide range of activities, such as the 9 hole Golf course (15km away) which can be organised from the hotel reception at reduced rates.

Other sports include go-karting, fishing and various watersports.
Coffee Bar Situated on the ground floor to the right of the main entrance, seating is available either inside or outside, where the hotel has a dominant position within the Piazza Mazzini - the centre of Lido di Jesolo. A range of snacks and soft or alcoholic drinks are available.

Pool bar

Situated on the 5th floor, the pool bar is open from 10am till 6pm. Visitors can also use the free table tennis, cards, chess, drafts, darts and other entertainment facilities from here. A wide range of snacks are also available at very reasonable prices. From 12.30pm to 2pm you will find lasagne, spagetti, hamburgers, chips, club sandwich and many other choices.

Swimming pools

There are two pools (adult and children's), situated on the 5th floor just past the Pool bar and open from 8am to 6pm and offering an excellent view over the the sea, lagoon and Jesolo. There is no charge for the sunbeds and parasols around the pool.

Bike Service

You can make use of the free hotel bikes if required - simply ask at reception for booking details. The main road of Jesolo runs for 10km with a separate cycle route, ensuring an enjoyable and safe route around town.
