PT. Asuransi Hanjin Korindo is  a joint venture general insurance company between Oriental Fire & Marine Insurance Co., Ltd in Korea and Korindo Group in Indonesia. The company has obtained a business licence to operate a non-life general insurance business since 18th November 1998, as per the busines permit decree by Minister Of Finance Of Indonesia.

In the mean time, PT. Asuransi Hanjin Korindo is adapting and implementing the fundamental culture of Korean insurance industry. Therefore the company upholds strongly one of its main philosophies i.e. fulfilling the ultimate customer needs. The company has dedicated its time to provide good service to the customers. It is our belief that the company will grow primarily based on the good customer trust and loyalty.

PT. Asuransi Hanjin Korindo is now expanding its business in terms of quality and volume with the strong support from both parent company.

Beeing in the service and the marketing business, the company shall continually increase the customer's satisfaction from time to time. During the coverage time, the company pledges to provide good quality services and to ensure its customers consistently to meet up their requirements. It is our belief that good quality service reflects a good quality company.

Future Outlook
One of the company's strategic moves is to expand its business scope from the second year of operation and to concentrate in delivering a speedy and a good quality service to customers. Apart from these, the company will strongly emphasize on the human resource improvement and professional staff development.
