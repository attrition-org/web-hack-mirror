Information about donations		 Calarcá, in the central-West Colombian, sufffered serious damages caused by the Earthquake of January 25th of 1999.   It is the main purpose of these project that wants to benefit the population of the Municipality that is 72775 people totally, giving priority to the most affected groups. This project works for a development that contributes to the equity, sostenibility and to its irreversability. 

Proyecto de: OPS/OMS Colombia, Alcaldía de Calarcá, Instituto Seccional de Salud del Quindío, Secretaría Municipal de Salud de Calarcá, Ministerio de Salud.
