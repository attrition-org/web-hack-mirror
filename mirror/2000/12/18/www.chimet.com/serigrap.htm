SERIGRAPHIC PASTES


Industrial:

  • Silver conductive pastes at different percentages (50% - 60% - 80% - 85%) for heated rear windows.
  • Ceramic enamels for wind-screens, lateral windows and heated rear windows (applications: in IR, UV, HM).

Hybrids:

  • For the microelectronic industry Ag, Ag/Pd, Ag/Pt, Au conductive pastes.

Photovoltaic:

  • -For photovoltaic applications on silicon wafer:
  • -for the front: Ag paste
  • -for the back: Ag and Ag/Al paste.
  • -For the non-reflectant: TiO2 solution and paste.

Electronic Components:

  • Ag pastes for ceramic conductors, for thermistors
    and for piezoelectric ceramics.
