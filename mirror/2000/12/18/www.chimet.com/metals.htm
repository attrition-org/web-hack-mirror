Metals Bank



CHIMET Spa's "Metal Bank" business is carried out in the Arezzo (Badia al Pino) and Vicenza Offices.
CHIMET Spa is able to fix the sale price of gold, silver, platinum, and palladium in due time thanks to its terminals that are linked up with the main world-wide stock-exchanges.

Customers have the possibility to purchase any quantity of precious metals, without the obligations fixed by the Banks.
Payment may be effected at delivery or deferred. CHIMET Spa's "Metals Bank" offers a double guarantee to its customers: the exactness of the price and the fineness of the precious metals.
