Findings



CHIMET Spa Findings Division was created and has been developed to fulfill goldsmith's needs of a wide and diversified service.

The Findings Division, together with the other divisions, allows the Company to run a complete and efficient service in the fine metals business: either the fine metal refined from waste, or the fine metal sold by our Bullion Department can be transferred to the Findings Division for further production of semi-finished items.

The wide range of products and the item's high quality enables CHIMET Spa to be an important support for the firms which work in this field.
