THE joint stock company, CHIMET Spa was established in an independent business concern in 1975. Its origins, however, are closely linked to the activities of GORI E ZUCCHI S.p.a, today known as UNO A ERRE world leader in the manufacture of gold and silverware. Invaluable experience accumulated over the previous 50 years as the refining and recovery division of a large gold manufacturing firm has been exploited and enhanced by CHIMET Spa first by taking advantage of the substantial presence of gold firms in Arezzo and later by broadening its horizons to other companies using precious metals.

Today, CHIMET Spa is the largest business operation in Italy dealing with the refining and recovery of precious metals and one of the most important in the production and distribution of precious metals and their compouns and intermediate products.

Through the use of advanced technology based on an integrated system of treatment processes, CHIMET Spa has been able to offer his clientele a competent and reliable service.
CHIMET Spa is thus a highly specialized processing and service industry put together, embodying such criteria and ability necessary to ensure the success of the activities undertaken:

QUALITY:

  • gold and silver ingots produced by CHIMET Spa are classified as Good Delivery by the London Bullion Market Association (L.B.M.A.)
  • CHIMET Spa operates according to ISO 9000 (EN 29000) in the Serigraphic Pastes sector

ENVIRONMENTAL PROTECTION:

  • CHIMET Spa has fulfilled all necessary legal requirements to carry out operations. Its plants have been installed with the latest equipment available to breakdown and purify the industrial waste generated.

SERVICES:

  • CHIMET Spa has at its disposal one of the most advanced and comprehensive laboratories for analysing and testing precious metals. Customers not only enjoy the best in freight, insurance and banking services but also have the opportunity to capitalize on those services offered by Gori e Zucchi S.p.a including the use of their private Bonded Warehouse.

CHIMET Spa is proud of the trust its clientele has bestowed upon it, a trust reflecting 20 years of continual progress and development.
