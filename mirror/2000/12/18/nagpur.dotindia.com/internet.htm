 INTERNET
  What is Internet ?

   Internet is Network of Network Linking thousands of Smaller Computer network together so that the 
   information present is one network can be shared by other network. Internet is used by million of people  
   throughout the world for  communication, research, getting information and recreation
 
 Why Use Internet ?

  In present age of information technology, internet has become a medium for accessing information on any
  topic imaginable for buying products and services of any kind as well as pleasure at the mere click of the  
  mouse key. Beside , trade , commerce and industry , the internet provides tremendous opportunity to student 
  researchers , professionals for getting information on matter relating to academic and professional topic

 What you can do on the Internet ?

 Though Internet , You can
  * Browse Though Information on any  topic on the world wide web
  * Transfer File
  * Exchange message using E-mail
  * Search the database of your internet
  * Send or Receive animation and picture Files to and from distant places
  * Communicate in real time with others connected to the internet 
 What do you need to get connected to DOT internet ?

  A Telephone connection or a leased line ,  an appropriate PC with a required software, an approved modem and
  of course an internet A/c with DOT
  Following types of connection are available :
  * TCP/IP PSTN Dial-up A/c
  * TCP/IP ISDN Dial-up A/c (64 Kbps / 128 Kbps)
  * Leased Circuit
