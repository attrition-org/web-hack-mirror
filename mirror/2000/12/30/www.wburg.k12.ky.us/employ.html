Williamsburg Independent Board of Education Non-Discrimination Policy

Any person having inquiries concerning the Williamsburg Independent School's discrimination in employment or services may contact the board designated coordinator of these programs: Mr. Paul Falin, Superintendent, Williamsburg Independent Board of Education, 1000 Main Street, Williamsburg, KY 40769 or by phone at (606)549-6047.
