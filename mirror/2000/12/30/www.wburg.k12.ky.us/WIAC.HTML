WISD ALTERNATIVE SCHOOL

The Williamsburg Alternative Center (WIAC-East) was established August 1999. This was to provide social and academic skills to a wide range of students with diverse needs.  This helps to meet graduation requirements through an individualized form of instruction, which is an alternative to the regular classroom setting. This approach is an advantage for students with Attention Deficit. Students with special needs or those students whose skills are below those in the regular classroom may be at risk of dropping out of school.
