Current Scenes at School


A group called FLOW (Future Leaders of Williamsburg) has been formed recently. The pictures below show members of FLOW giving a Powerpoint presentation to the Williamsburg City Council concerning the new waterpark that is currently under construction. FLOW members convinced the City Council that the youth of Williamsburg need to be included on planning committees such as the waterpark committee. The mayor and Council agreed and asked FLOW to select two students for membership. FLOW members are also actively supporting the establishment of a youth center for Williamsburg.
