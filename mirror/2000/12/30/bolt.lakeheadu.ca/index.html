Happy New Year

Please take a moment to catch up on the news from around the world. This weeks roundup are from The Times

Israeli soldiers shoot Lebanese man. The man was in a group of stone throwers attacked by a passing patrol

US alarm at Russian arms sale to Iran . Russia's plan to resume arms sales to Iran has triggered a diplomatic scramble to stop the collapse of a long-standing arms embargo

12 killed in Manila bomb attacks . At least 12 people have been killed and 90 others injured in a series of bomb attacks in Manila in the Philippines

Israel to close Rafah crossing point .Palestinian security services said today that they had been informed by the Israeli army that the Rafah crossing point linking Egypt to the Gaza Strip will be closed from Sunday onward

India plans cable car to world's highest war zone . India is planning a cable car to ferry men and supplies to the highest battlefield on Earth
