What Is "Trans?"

 Welcome to the official web site for TransFamily of Cleveland.

We are a support group for transgendered and transsexual people, their parents, partners, children, other family members, friends, and supportive others. We provides referrals, literature, and over-the-phone information on all transgender issues .Although our meetings are currently held in Cleveland, Ohio, the Internet has allowed us to extend a helping hand across the globe.

This site is not intended, in any way, to be a comprehensive listing of information, links and resources for transgendered and transsexual people. There is just way too much out there. The intent of this site is to offer some basic information, a point of contact for families beginning to deal with a loved one's gender idenity, on line chat sessions and email discussions for group members, links, and whatever else the webmeister's find interesting.

We're under construction, and hopefully, we always will be. We don't want this place to become static! The information here is constantly changing, so check back often!!
