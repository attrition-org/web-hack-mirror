Welcome to Arbor Hill Tree Farm



We are a family-owned tree farm located in Cottage Grove, Minnesota - just south-east of Minneapolis - Saint Paul. We specialize in Colorado Blue Spruce and are dedicated to a growing, healthy, and attractive tree.

Since we are a family-owned tree grower, we can provide a quality tree at a very competitive price. Please look over our Price List and Services (below), then contact us for more information or to place an order.

Services and Information

  1. Colorado Blue Spruce, Green Spruce, Black Hills Spruce, Fat Albert Spruce and White Pine
  2. Bare root and burlapped shade trees, Maple, Ash, Oak, and Linden. Available in 1 inch diameter and up.
  3. Tree spading at our farm or at your location.
  4. Custom potting and B&B service.
  5. Quantity discounts for landscape contractors, builders, and developers.
  6. State of Minnesota licensed tree grower.
