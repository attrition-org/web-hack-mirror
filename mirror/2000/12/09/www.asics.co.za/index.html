Conshu Limited

Conshu Limited is an investment holding and management company which, through its subsidiaries, carries on business as a manufacturer and distributor of general footwear, rubber and plastic products.

Conshu is a leading player in its field of adding value to basic leather and rubber through conversion into high quality consumer products embodying craftsmanship, originality, styling and durability.

Company Mission

  • The attainment of customer gratification and the establishment of long term partnerships
  • The generation of appropriate returns to all stakeholders
  • Being a responsible corporate citizen
  • Ensuring staff participation and training at all levels to promote excellence
