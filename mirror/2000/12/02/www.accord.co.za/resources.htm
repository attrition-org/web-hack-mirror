Work itself has been redefined, with more emphasis on continuous learning, higher order thinking, flexible work schedules and an ever-increasing emphasis on innovation. This along with other realities of the new workplace have created an unparalleled urgency for identifying, attracting, retaining, developing and intelligently deploying a workforce with the requisite talent to help companies achieve and sustain a new competitive market position.
Accord’s intelligent management of human assets has become a strategy for competitive advantage. We utilise strategic skills management as a tactical business response to the challenge of achieving competitive advantage. The human resources at Accord Technologies are draw from a diverse pool and it is this diversity coupled with creative ability, which allows us to perform exceptionally within the telecom sector. We strive to continuously identify, source, evaluate, develop, match, motivate, deploy, and reward the people for there unique contribution to the success of the many initiatives we undertake.
