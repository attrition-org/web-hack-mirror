Accord Connect focuses on the "Internet to mobile" convergence arena with the common thread of the operations in this division revolve around "connecting" people by utilising technology. The skills that reside in this division will focus on the management and operation of portals, providing solutions relating to mobile to internet convergence and the management of content. The division's joint venture with Wireless Data Services (UK) consolidates its position as market leader in mobile data support and training solutions.

The complimentary nature of managing and operating portals and mobile data support bodes well for this division. This division also houses the groups video convergence initiatives. The introduction of faster data rates in the mobile arena coupled with content management positions this division for the convergence of voice, video and data services.
