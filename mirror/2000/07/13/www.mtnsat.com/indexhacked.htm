Maritime 
Telecommunications Network
provides low cost solutions for voice, data, Internet and compressed video services to the cruise and offshore oil industry as well as the US Navy, all via satellite. MTN utilizes C-Band technology and also offers Inmarsat Services.

We are dedicated to provide our customers with state-of-the-art global communications solutions for any aspect of their network needs. We are dedicated to operational excellence, research, development and customer service in order to deliver reliable, effective and economical services.
