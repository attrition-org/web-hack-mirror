Virtually all of the world’s offshore oil fields use satellite communications to address the demanding requirements of deepwater exploration and production. For advanced systems that offer voice and data service in the world’s harshest environments, Maritime Telecommunications Network (MTN) provides the technology.

We are the leading edge in C-Band satellite service supporting WAN services such as; Frame Relay, ATM, and ISDN. Our C-Band technology offers private satellite networks delivering voice and data connectivity. C-Band and Ku-Band systems offer circuits from 64 kbps to 2.0 mbps.

For high speed data needs; mapping, remotely transmitted GPS corrections, down-hole and well logging, to routed networks for E-mail and file transfer, MTN has the experience and is ready to offer digital connectivity to all of the major offshore petroleum concessions in the world.

We also offer Inmarsat A,B, C, M and Mini-M services. Our Mini-M phones are available in portable and marine units, providing inexpensive voice, fax and data communications. Our Inmarsat B service offers high-quality voice, fax, telex and high-speed coverage. Rugged and powerful, our Inmarsat B service delivers high-speed (64 Kbps), high-volume data transfer for complete network functionality in any environment. Available in transportable, stabilized and fixed units.

Our packages include system engineering, total project management, onshore and offshore technical personnel and a 24-hour monitor and control center.

In addition, MTN offers equipment sales to offshore telecommunications providers such as Telenor Satellite Services A/S. For example, the PGS Ramform Valiant is installed with MTN equipment. Installation, service and communications links are provided by Telenor.
