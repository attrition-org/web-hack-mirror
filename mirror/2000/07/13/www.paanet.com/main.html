PAA (Programmers' Association of AOL) was started in October '98 by Brandon AKA ChiChis and Jason AKA raw with the purpose of making good AOL add-on progs.  Recently, we have changed.  Much less of our time is devoted to AOL add-ons and more to stand-alone programs and examples.  Thus, the PAA site is the best place for code examples from beginner to advanced and soon to be tutorials.
