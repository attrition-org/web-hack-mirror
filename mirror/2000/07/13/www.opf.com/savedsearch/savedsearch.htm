Welcome to the Saved Search.		  






Rather than typing in the exact same search criteria day after day to check for new properties that have just gone on the market...

You can now enter and save your search criteria here, and our computers will alert you via e-mail of any new properties meeting your criteria.

You can even save multiple searches by supplying a different password for each, and you can revise or delete your criteria at any time. To begin, click on "New Account" or "Coastal Acount" and follow the instructions provided.
