O'Conor, Piper & Flynn ERA conducts the REAL ESTATE PRINCIPLES AND PRACTICES course required for licensing by the Maryland Real Estate Commission. Classes are scheduled in Baltimore, Anne Arundel, Harford, Howard, Carroll, Frederick, Charles and St. Mary's counties and are staffed by experienced real estate instructors. Classes are now forming!

Newly Licensed Agents

Should you join O'Conor, Piper & Flynn ERA as a licensed agent, you will receive an additional six week, highly concentrated training program which introduces new associates to the necessary technical aspects of real estate, including computer training. It also initiates the development of techniques and skills necessary for listing and selling properties. Role playing simulating actual sales situations help develop the level of confidence necessary for prospecting and sustaining relationships with customers and clients.
