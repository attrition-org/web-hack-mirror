Harvard ManageMentor

SBA is pleased to partner with Harvard Business School Publishing in providing on-line learning tools for small business owners, managers and other students of enterprise. Two of the twenty Harvard ManageMentor topics available, can be accessesed AT NO CHARGE through SBA's Small Business Classroom. They are Finance Essentials and Solving Business Problems. The topics provide instant access to advice, tools and techniques for handling business finance issues and solving small business problems. A leading expert in the field has guided the development of each module to provide only the most relevant information on a particular topic in short 10 to 20 minute sessions. And, since the programs are on-line, the information is available "just-in-time" to help a businessperson deal effectively with a "live" situation. The goal of this partnership is to support, encourage and celebrate entrepreneurship.

  • Solving Business Problems
