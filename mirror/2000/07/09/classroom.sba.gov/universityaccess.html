University Access Online Courses

SBA is pleased to partner with University Access in providing online learning tools and resources for small business owners, managers, and other students of enterprise. University Access is one of the nation's leading distance learning companies, and the creator of an award-winning course entitled Introduction to Entrepreneurship: Building the Dream.

Introduction to Entrepreneurship consists of fifteen video and web-based lessons. SBA Classroom visitors have access to both the video and online portion of the first lesson, "Do You Have What it Takes?" at no charge.

Featured Lesson:
"Do You Have What it Takes?"

This lesson defines the characteristics of a successful entrepreneur and presents tools to help you decide what types of businesses best match your strengths.

To begin the available lesson (Lesson One), click here and enter the username: "sba" and password: "sba" on the left side of the University Access home page. Click on the syllabus for a brief introduction to the course, or proceed directly to the Course Index.
