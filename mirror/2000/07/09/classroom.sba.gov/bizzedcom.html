SBA creates innovative partnership with Citigroup's Bizzed.com

SBA is pleased to partner with Bizzed.com in providing new online resources for small business owners. First, Bizzed.com has created an SBA area on the bizzed.com website which provides links to a number of valuable SBA resources. Explore this resource area to discover some of the other tools and services available on this site for small business owners. Second, beginning in Late March 2000, Bizzed.com (www.bizzed.com) will present a number of online courses through the Small Business Classroom. Courses developed will address topics such as:

  • What is an IPO and Is it Right for You?
  • How Do You Get Your Business Online: For the Non-Technical Entrepreneur
  • How to Run Your Online Business: From Successful Inventory/Order Management to Customer Fulfillment.
  • How to Provide Health and Compensation (401K) Benefits To Your Employees.

This page will be updated as course development is completed and presentation schedules are set.

The cosponsorship is aimed at providing small business owners with information and training to assure them the competitive edge they need to thrive in the new Internet economy.
