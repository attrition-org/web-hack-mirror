Business ToolsSM to help your small business grow

SBA is pleased to partner with Office.com in providing on-line learning tools and information resources for small business owners, managers and other students of enterprise. The Office.com Business ToolsSM contains a variety of resources to help SBA clients start and grow their small businesses. In a series of web pages, the Office.com Business ToolsSM provide step-by-step training covering the key aspects of operating a thriving business.

Whether you need the knowledge to analyze your company's profitability, conduct a marketing or competitive analysis, or stay on top of the latest business strategies and trends, this site will help you find solutions that work for your small business.

This co-sponsorship seeks to support, encourage and celebrate entrepreneurship.
