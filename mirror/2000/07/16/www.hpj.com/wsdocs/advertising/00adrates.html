Contract Year: Advertising must be inserted within one year of the first insertion to earn the bulk rate. Cancellation of any portion of a contract nullifies all rate protection. Orders for more than one year not accepted.

Copy Acceptance: The High Plains Journal reserves the right to reject any advertising within a reasonable time after receipt of proof of text and illustration. Advertisers and advertising agencies assume liability for all content (including text, representation and illustrations) of advertisements printed, and also assume responsibility for any claims arising therefrom made against the publisher.


COMMISSION AND CASH DISCOUNTS:

Commission: 15% of the gross display space billing to recognized advertising agencies.
Cash Discount: 2% of the net allowed for payment on or before the 10th day of the following month to recognized advertising agencies.
