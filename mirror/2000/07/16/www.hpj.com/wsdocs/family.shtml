Cancer of Pancreas: The Quiet Killer

There aren't many headlines anywhere telling about the fourth leading cause of cancer death in the U.S., so if you are somewhere in the 60 to 80 age group, gobble up this information for the benefit of yourself and others about you.

Cancer of the pancreas strikes so often that over 30,000 otherwise healthy individuals in the U.S. and Canada die each year from this unfortunate disease.
