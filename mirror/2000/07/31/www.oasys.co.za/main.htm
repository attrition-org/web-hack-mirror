In-house O.I.S.P.T.A.
............or Oasys Information Systems - Pretoria if you please. Oasys is expanding to the North. Oasys Pretoria should have it's grand opening by the end of the first quarter 2000. The main idea behind Oasys PTA is to help the students to expand their skills in the computer industry and gain valuable hands-on experience. This branch will be run entirely by students ( with some help from the Head-Office ) and will target the student market in Pretoria. We also plan to open a service division at the branch in time but for now it will mainly be a carry-in service store.
