What is this all about?

When you join the IMSI Affiliate Program you can take advantage of the great opportunity to sell IMSI products online. Become an IMSI Affiliate and you will have the ability to:

How does this work?

When you sign up as an IMSI Affiliate, we begin tracking the visitors you send to our store. We'll supply the banners you use to link visitors from your site to our store. When these visitors make a purchase, you get credit for the sale.

We keep a running tab of the referral fees you earn. This information is provided by our strategic partner in the Affiliate Program, LinkShare. You can check your sales online whenever you like.
