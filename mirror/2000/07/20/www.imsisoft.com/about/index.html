Company Profile
Headquartered in Novato, IMSI produces and sells visual content, design, and graphics software over the Internet. The company also owns and operates several popular websites including ArtToday.com and newly formed HomeDesignToday.com. Read more about IMSI's company profile, our Board of Directors and the Executive Management Team.
