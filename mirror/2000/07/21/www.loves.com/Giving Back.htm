Love’s Country Stores takes pride in contributing to the communities we are in, whether it’s Oklahoma City or our store locations. We feel that we should do our part in helping each community since we are a member of each one.

Central Giving Fund
Love’s Country Stores, Inc. contributes a substantial amount to non-profit organizations each year. The United Way, Children’s Miracle Network, March of Dimes, Arthritis Foundation, and the Easter Seal Society are some of the organizations to which we contribute.

Community Support
Every Love’s location has a budget for donations to local organizations and events. Typical uses would be PTO fund-raisers, little league baseball team sponsorships, or school yearbook ads.

Adopt-a-Family Program
This is Love’s grassroots community involvement project and is done every year for Thanksgiving and Christmas. Money is raised through the year to buy food and clothing for the underprivileged in Oklahoma City. Adopt-a-Family was started by a member of the corporate office team several years ago. In 1998, over $10,570 was raised to assist 120 individuals and families during the holidays.
