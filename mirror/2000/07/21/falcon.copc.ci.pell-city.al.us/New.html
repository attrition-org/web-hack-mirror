 Entertainment
Parks - Pell City Lakeside Park is located adjacent to the Pell City Civic Center at 2801 Stemley Bridge Rd. (Hiway 34). Pell City Parks Department - 338-9713. Other Close State Parks - Oak Mountain State Park and Cheaha State Park.
Lakes - Logan Martin Lake has thousands of acres of water for fishing, boating, and other outdoor activities.
Television - All networks are available via antennae.
Coosa Cable Co., provides local cable television service.

Dining
In Town - Big Bull Restaurant, Burger King, Dairy Queen, Guadalajara, Hardees, Jacks, Kentucky Fried Chicken, McDonalds, Pell City Steakhouse, Pizza Hut, Sneaky Petes, Subway, Taco Bell.
Out of Town - 30 minutes on I-20 will take you to either Anniston (East) or Birmingham (West) where any other restaurants may be found.
Housing
Apartments - Scattered throughout the city, ranging from $185 - $425 a month.
Mobile Home - Several mobile home parks are located in and adjacent to Pell City, prices range from $250 - $400 per month.
Houses - New home construction space is plentiful and established homes are available both on and off the water in all price ranges. Rental housing is also available.
 Banks - Amsouth, Colonial Bank, Metro Bank, NBC Bank and Union State Bank all have offices in Pell City.
