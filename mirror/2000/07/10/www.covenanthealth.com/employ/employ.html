Employment Opportunities

It takes many different talents and skills to build a top-notch healthcare team - from nurses, therapists and clinical technicians to computer analysts, housekeepers and administrative staff.

Covenant Health offers CAREERS for people who want to make a difference in the health of their communities. We have six acute care hospitals, behavioral health services, rehabilitation and cancer facilities, outpatient clinics, home care and an insurance company, so you won't be "benched" by a lack of options. We actively recruit the best clinical caregivers, and we need non-clinical and professional "star players" in areas like information systems, finance, medical records, dietary, fitness and recreation, child care and many others.

We support our employee team with competitive compensation, an outstanding BENEFIT PACKAGE, flexible scheduling in many positions, an in-house training program, tuition reimbursement, and a retirement package with immediate vesting and a generous employer match.

You'll also find opportunities for community service, along with the pride that comes from working with one of the most highly regarded healthcare providers in the region. Covenant Health is a winner of multiple Tennessee Quality awards and has been named one of the top 100 healthcare systems in the country.

Join a healthcare team where every employee is an MVP - a Most Valuable Player!
