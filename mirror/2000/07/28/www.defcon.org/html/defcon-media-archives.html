DEF CON Multi Media Archives
DEF CON is creating a repository of hacking and computer security related media.
In the coming months you will see many additions to this page.  Audio and video from all over will be added, from serious speeches to funny phone pranks, to complete documentaries and videos.  If you have content that you would like archived and streamed contact us.  We can even convert and encode if necessary.
NEW 05-16-00: New audio and video encoded from found DEF CON 6 tapes, 2600 OTH section updated.
All contest is streamed using Real Networks Real Player Plus, in RTSP or PNM mode (port 554 or 7070).  If you don't have Real Player 7, you'll need it.  Note: The "PerfectPlay" option seems to mess things up.
Hacker Documentaries, Videos, and Shorts
Hacks - by Christine Bader.  Hacks is a 73 minute European documentary exploring what nature of "Hacking" is in a social context.  It can range from traditional hacking, to artist communes and environmental activism.  Well done.  Presented in QSIF NTSC size, converted from PAL.  56k to DSL / Cable encoded 2-Pass SureStream.  Photography by Ulla Barthold, Bella Halben, Gabi Schwark & Christine Bader.  Edited by Michael Thaler.  Music by Andreas Kunzmann & Jin Choi.  (c) Bader Film, 1997.
Hackers '95 - by Phon-E and R.F. Burns. Hackers '95 is a 90 minute part documentary, part spoof.  Phon-E and R.F. Burns cover the hacker related goings on of 1995.  Summer Con 95, DEF CON III, Operation Cyber Snare, Area 51, an interview with Erik Bloodaxe and more are covered.  This is what professional video hackers do with their spare time.  Presented in QSIF NTSC size.  56k to DSL / Cable encoded 2-Pass SureStream. Photography and Editing by Phone-E and R.F. Burns.
