I have tried to pick out books that form part of a common pool of knowledge of the computer undergeround. Many people ask me what they should read to try to understand what it's about. For them I have put this page together. This is not ment to be an exhaustive list, just enough to raise your curiosity.
Computer Security - There are a million books in this category, and I try and point out some of the better ones. Computer Reference - While these will help your security knowledge, these books help explain how all this stuff actually works.
Cyber Punk - These books generally deal with a dark future filled with monster mega-corporations, technology out of control, and problems our hero must deal with.  Usually not super happy fun books. Computer Underground Culture - Hey!  Books about people we know.  Watch the authors put a spin on things, and try and come to grips with the moral flexibility of the characters involved.
A Peek behind the curtains - For those of you who want to know more than how the router works.  This section explores government agencies, privacy issues, real world spooky stuff, and creepy conspiracies. Unconventional Information - How does radio monitoring work? Spy satellites? Smart drugs and wierd science?  This section deals with all that other cool stuff not directly related to computer systems.
