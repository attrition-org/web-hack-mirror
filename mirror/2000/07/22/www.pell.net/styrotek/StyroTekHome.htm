Styro-Tek, Incorporated is a fabricator of Expanded Polystyrene (EPS) foam products.
Styro-Tek EPS products are lightweight, rigid, foamed plastic and are offered in a wide range of densities and sizes in order to meet the requirements of your application or specifications.
Styro-Tek also fabricates laminated roof, wall, and facia panels for commercial and residential applications, including - sunrooms, service station canopies, store fronts and home construction.

Styro-Tek's Products include:

  • Custom Foam Shapes
  • Laminated Panels
  • Specialty Foam Products
