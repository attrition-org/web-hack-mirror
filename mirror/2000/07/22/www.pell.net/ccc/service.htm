Service Call Tips

Usual Cable Problems
* All cable channels are snowy
* No sound on any channels
If you are experiencing these problems call us immediately.
Usual TV Set Problems
* TV has sound but no picture
* TV has a picture but no sound
* TV does not light up
* TV has no color picture
* No Channels above 13 (CATV Switch)
* Sound wrong on channel (SAP Button)
* Only have channel 3 or 4 (VCR on)

If you are experiencing these problems TV repair may need to be done.

Should you have questions concerning any of the above, please call us right away. Remember, our Service Department is on-call to insure your trouble-free enjoyment of Cable TV and all of our Pay TV Services.
