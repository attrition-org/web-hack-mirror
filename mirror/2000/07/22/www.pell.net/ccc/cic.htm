Education Initiatives


1. Member of Cable in the Classroom - Cable in the Classroom provides over 500 hours of commercial free educational programming each month.

2. Free cable services - Coosa Cable Co. provides free service to the Pell City School System. Over 200 classrooms are wired to date, with more being wired as they are built.

3. Member of the Pell City Education Foundation - The foundation provides grants to teachers to aid them with educational expenses.

4. Coosa Cable Co., donates over $4,000 a year in miscellaneous donations to support various facets of education in Pell City and St. Clair County.
