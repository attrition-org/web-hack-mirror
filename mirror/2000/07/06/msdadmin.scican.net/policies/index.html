Welcome to the home page for the MSD of Martinsville's Policy Manual. The policy manual has been converted from Microsoft Word format to HTML format for this web site. Because of the conversion process there will be some formatting differences between this version and the printed version.  If you find differences between this version and the printed version, please note them and forward them to Darrel Potter at: mailto:dpotter@scican.net

The purpose for providing MSD of Martinsville Policy Manual online is two fold.

1. to encourage staff to read our policies.

2. to make the policy manual readily available to all who wish to read it.

 

Disclaimer:  This is not the official policy manual for the MSD of Martinsville.   The official policy manual is located in the MSD of Martinsville school administration office at 460 South Main Street, Martinsville, IN 46151.
