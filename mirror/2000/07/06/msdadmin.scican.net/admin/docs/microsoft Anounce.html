Work at Home Rights Back for some Office Products

For Microsoft products of Office and Office Professional full suite only,

Publisher, Project, and Front Page licensed through Open (MOLP) or Select,

education customers now have the right to use these products on either a

home or portable computer. Effective immediately, the primary user of a

computer on which a copy of the software is installed may make a second copy

on either a home or portable computer for work-related purposes.

Microsoft has decided to reinstate work at home in education only because of

the non-typical working environment of the teacher/faculty member. Most

teacher/faculty members do not have an office per se and execute most of

their work from home.

For select customers that have purchased any of the products above, a letter

will be sent to them stating that they are now entitled to work at home

privileges provided they date and sign the letter. By signing and dating

letter, the customer acknowledges and agrees to the change in their select

product use rights. Customers need to sign and date letter and then return

it to Microsoft by May 31. These letters will be sent the week of April 6.

 

For open customers that have previously purchased any of the above software,

they will not receive a letter, but will be entitled to work at home rights

immediately. These customers will be informed via newsletters and various

other communication methods.
