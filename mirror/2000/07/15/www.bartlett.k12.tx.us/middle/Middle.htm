Mission Statement

The Faculty and staff of Bartlett Middle School strive to assist students to become more responsible citizens who demonstrate respect for themselves and others.

Through a partnership with members of the community, we endeavor to offer challenging educational opportunities which will allow our students to:

*discover individual interests
*cultivate a life long love of learning
*develop skills

To cope with as well as productively contribute to our complex, dynamic and multi-cultural society.
