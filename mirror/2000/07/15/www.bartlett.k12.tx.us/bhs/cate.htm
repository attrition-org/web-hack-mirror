Bartlett Independent School District offers career and technology education programs in agri-science, home economic and business/technology education. Admission to these programs is based on state/federal criteria and the successful completion of prerequisite courses.

It is the policy of the Bartlett Independent School District not to discriminate on the basis of race, color, national origin, sex, or handicap in its vocational programs, services, or activities as required by Title VI of the Civil Rights Act of 1964, as amended; Title IX of the Education Amendments of 1972; and Section 504 of the Rehabilitation Act of 1973, as amended.

Bartlett ISD will take steps to assure that lack of English language skills will not be a barrier to admission and participation in all educational and vocational programs.

Bartlett Independent School District ofrece programas vocacionales en agri-science, home economics and business/technology educacion. La admision a estos programs se basa en criterio federal y del estado, y la conclusion con exito de los cursos requisitos.

Es norma de Bartlett ISD no discriminar por motivos de raza, color, origen nacional, sexo o impedimento, en sus programas, servicieos o actividades vocacionales, tal como lo requieren el Titulo VI de la Ley de Derechos Civiles de 1964, segun enmineda; el Titulo IX de las Enmiendas en la Educacion, de 1972, y la Seccion 504 de la Ley de Rehabilitacion de 1973, segun enmienda.

Bartlett ISD tomara las medidas necesarias para asegurar que la falta de habilidad en el uso de la lengua ingles no sea un obstaculo para la admision y participacion en todos los programas educativos y vocacionales.
