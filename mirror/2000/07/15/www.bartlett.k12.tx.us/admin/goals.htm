The BISD goal for LEADERSHIP is -

    TO: Promote effective collaboration toward achieving the district's mission on behalf of all its students

    IN A WAY THAT:

    • provides for decision-making at the most appropriate levels of the organization
    • honors effective two-way communication both internally and externally
    • pursues appropriate cooperative ventures with other educational, governmental, and business entities
    • provides the curriculum, staff, facilities, and resources necessary to facilitate student learning
    • commits the Board and staff to long-range and strategic planning which keeps student learning as the primary focus
    • recognizes the need to manage resources effectively
    • abides by all laws and policies
    • establishes systematic review and evaluation of programs and facilities
    • establishes a standard of high expectations while building a school and district climate that provides an appealing and appropriate learning environment
    • exhibits and promotes basic democratic values and processes

    SO THAT:

    • all students are successful as learners
    • the BISD accomplishes its mission
    • trust is established and fostered among the students, staff, Board, and community
    • the Bartlett Independent School District is recognized in providing outstanding educational opportunities to all its communities


  • The BISD goal for
    ESTABLISHING AN APPROPRIATE CLIMATE is -

    TO: Provide, promote, and secure a learning and working environment and culture which is warm, accepting, and friendly while establishing a high expectation level for success for all students, staff, parents, and community

    IN A WAY THAT:

    • promotes a "yes we can" attitude and a concept of "we" instead of "they"
    • expects and encourages active participation from the community
    • welcomes and celebrates the diversity of our school and community population
    • allows for and rewards innovation and risk-taking
    • emphasizes life-long learning
    • fosters instructional leadership at the campus level
    • promotes positive discipline within our school structure
    • encourages students to accept the responsibility for developing their skills and opportunities
    • maintains and promotes safe, healthy, and supportive surroundings for our students and staff
    • enhances positive staff morale
    • facilitates the recruitment and employment of quality staff
    • promotes and nurtures an attitude and a desire for continuous improvement

    SO THAT:

    • all students experience a sense of belonging and acceptance
    • employees develop a sense of district pride and ownership
    • parents and community become active participants in the educational process
    • the district is recognized as being open and responsive to the public



    The BISD goal for CURRICULUM is -

    TO: Provide challenging and equitable educational opportunities for all students

    IN A WAY THAT:

    • provides curriculum opportunities which are creative, effective, and innovative
    • increases the availability of challenging educational opportunities such as advanced placement and dual-credit classes
    • explores and promotes curriculum links with the universities through an early childhood to grade 16 approach
    • provides career and industrial/technical opportunities/classes that are consistent with the job market and utilize state-of-the art equipment and materials
    • maximizes the use of technology in the curriculum
    • promotes the development of a comprehensive K-12 program in all curriculum areas
    • provides early childhood programs that are developmentally appropriate
    • focuses on opportunities for parenting and other continuing education
    • establishes school calendars that are flexible and geared to meet the curriculum needs of students

    SO THAT:

    • all students are successful
    • every student appreciates the contributions to society by all the world's cultures
    • no student is lost as a drop-out
    • all graduates exit our schools well prepared to enter the work force and/or continue their formal education



    The BISD goal for PERSONNEL is -

    TO: Recruit, acquire, and maintain a diverse work force that is second to none in terms of qualifications, expertise, and commitment to excellence

    IN A WAY THAT:

    • focuses recruitment efforts on the best possible candidates
    • commits to the diversity of the total work force
    • establishes a comprehensive induction program for new employees
    • provides a supportive work environment
    • guarantees a competitive salary and benefit structure for all employee groups
    • ensures continuous and comprehensive professional development for all staff
    • involves the staff in the teacher preparation process

    SO THAT:

    • our students are guaranteed the best possible education
    • continuous improvement for all staff and students becomes expected
    • Bartlett ISD becomes a "RECOGNIZED" district



    The BISD goal for FACILITIES is -

    TO: Provide facilities and equipment necessary to support creative, effective, and innovative instructional programs

    IN A WAY THAT:

    • complements and accentuates the learning process
    • capitalizes on and utilizes emerging technology while maximizing resources
    • provides for a safe and comfortable learning atmosphere
    • develops a facilities master plan in collaboration with city and county planning
    • establishes a regular cycle for review and update

    SO THAT:

    • student learning is enhanced
    • students and teachers have access to modern/upgraded facilities
    • student learning occurs in safe, well-maintained facilities
    • site selection and facility planning are proactive and long-range, focusing on flexibility to meet the needs of a growing and changing district



    The BISD goal for COMMUNICATIONS is -

    TO: Achieve an open and effective internal and external communications network

    IN A WAY THAT:

    • fosters school and district pride among students, staff, and community
    • encourages and solicits communication from the public
    • promotes communication both horizontally and vertically without fear of reprisal
    • focuses on and provides for staff training in communication skills for all employees
    • maximizes the use of technology as a communication tool
    • provides for the consistent, timely release of information among the Board, staff, and the community
    • communicates and markets the accomplishments of the district to its communities and other interested entities

    SO THAT:

    • the district promotes a positive relationship with everyone in the community
    • all students, staff, and parents become ambassadors for our schools
    • the community becomes well informed about the district



    The BISD goal for COLLABORATION is -

    TO: Promote, initiate, and foster collaboration between the district and its respective communities

    IN A WAY THAT:

    • promotes collaboration among the district and universities, colleges, and other educational entities
    • encourages the cooperation and the sharing of resources among BISD, municipalities, and other governmental entities
    • promotes interactive links with the universities, governmental entities, and businesses in the use of emerging technology
    • provides for meaningful partnerships with businesses

    SO THAT:

    • community access to schools is increased
    • BISD is recognized for giving back to the community
    • a spirit of cooperation, communication, and collaboration between the BISD and each of its communities is realized



    The BISD goal for PLANNING is -

    TO: Establish a process for the periodic review and evaluation of district goals and priorities

    IN A WAY THAT:

    • ensures students as the primary focus
    • establishes a common set of goals for the district
    • integrates facility, curriculum, personnel, and resource planning
    • supports the alignment of campus action planning with district goals and resources
    • recognizes the necessity of incorporating state and federal mandates in the planning process
    • orients new members to the Board's critical issues, processes, and governance functions
    • establishes an annual Board planning retreat
    • provides for periodic workshops geared toward planning and goal setting
    • allows the Board to focus on long-term and strategic planning

    SO THAT:

    • students remain the primary beneficiary in the planning and goal setting process
    • all planning efforts address a common set of goals
    • the mission of the district is accomplished



    The BISD goal for its EVALUATION PROCESS is -

    TO: Ensure that the district is meeting its mission statement and goals

    IN A WAY THAT:

    • adequately measures the district's progress
    • ensures a process for obtaining accurate data and information for continuous improvement
    • provides an ongoing personnel evaluation process that encourages continuing improvement and excellence
    • establishes a process for the evaluation of the effectiveness and relevance of the curriculum
    • evaluates the implementation and effectiveness of technology
    • provides for the evaluation of the leadership and policy making process

    SO THAT:

    • the community realizes maximum return on its resource investment
    • BISD programs, curricula, and personnel are exemplary
