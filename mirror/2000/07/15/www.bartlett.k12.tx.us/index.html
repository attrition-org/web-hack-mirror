Bartlett Independent School District offers career and technology education programs in agri-science, home economic and business/technology education. Admission to these programs is based on state/federal criteria and the successful completion of prerequisite courses.

It is the policy of the Bartlett Independent School District not to discriminate on the basis of race, color, national origin, sex, or handicap in its vocational programs, services, or activities as required by Title VI of the Civil Rights Act of 1964, as amended; Title IX of the Education Amendments of 1972; and Section 504 of the Rehabilitation Act of 1973, as amended.

Bartlett ISD will take steps to assure that lack of English language skills will not be a barrier to admission and participation in all educational and vocational programs.
