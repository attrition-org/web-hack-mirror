Bryan Allen, Principal

   

Mr. Bryan Allen, who is starting his eighteenth year in the education profession, currently has approximately 250 students in grades PK-5.    Mr. Allen has two children, Jacob, who is a 3rd grader, and Brock, age 4.  Mr. Allen's wife works in the district as the Speech Therapist/Diagnostician. Mr. Allen is currently working on his Superintendent's certification at Southwest Texas State University in San Marcos, Texas.

Lisa Luna, Secretary

                                                  

Mrs. Lisa Luna is beginning her fourth year as secretary at Bartlett  Elementary.  She has worked for the district a total of six years.  Previously, she worked as a classroom aide.  Mrs. Luna has three children:  Josh, a 6th grader, Matt, a 4th grader and Kenny who is in Kindergarten.
