Daggett School District's Board of Education comprises five citizens who are dedicated to educational excellence. It is their responsibility to determine district policies, to approve the district budget and expenditures, and to approve the hiring of personnel. Each board member is elected to a four-year term. Ms. Reed was appointed in February 2000 to complete the term of Ms. Penny Muir, who moved from her precinct.
