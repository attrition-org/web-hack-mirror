There are a variety of search engines on the web to satisfy your needs. The few below are are rather good for what you need.

Webcrawler
This is a general based search engine which gives you specified results. 

Excite
Much like webcrawler, but gives you more specific results.

The Big Eye
This is a very interesting search engine. You can search for information, but they also post sites in general areas such as history, science, and literature

Dogpile
This engine usually combines several search engines, such as looksmart, yahoo, and others.

Ask Jeeves
This engine accepts plain english questions without any searching rules. It offers answers to a series of questions to help narrow your question. It does a good job getting different meanings from incoherent questions.

37
This sites gives you 37 search engines to search from.

DeBriefing
This site uses seven search engines. It removes duplicate sites and offers keywords to narrow your search.
