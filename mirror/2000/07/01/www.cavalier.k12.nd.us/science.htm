Cavalier High School has a wonderful department for sciences. Students taking science classes are taught Physical Sciences from Mr. Dietrich and Life Sciences from Mr. Fisher. The students get to experience labs, outdoor field trips, and group activities to help them learn more about the world of science
Physical Science

    Physical Sciences are taught by Mr. Dietrich. These classes include Physical Science, Chemistry, and Physics. In these classes you learn about physical and chemical  processes around you and the effects they have on you. These classes help students to be aware of the many changes that occur on earth and how they have impacted life. Students are taking these classes to learn why change has occurred and to discover how things are made. Students  experience labs and investigations to demonstrate physical science principles.
Life Science 

    Life Sciences are taught by Mr. Fisher. These classes include Biology, Ecology, Life Science, and Anatomy. In these classes you learn the life processes of the environment and of living organisms. These classes will help students to prepare for employment in biological science fields, to learn how the human body functions, and to gain knowledge on how we impact our environment.  Students experience labs, fieldtrips, lectures, review games, videos, and group activities in these classes. Students are taking these classes to prepare for college and future jobs, to learn how the environment works, and to learn how the human body functions.
