A variety of math classes  are offered including General Math, Algebra I & II, Geometry and Math IV.

Math classes are to help apply math concepts to everyday situations, to become a better problem solver and to practice reasoning skills for everday life.

General math helps with everyday basic problem solving. Algebra I & II are for more complex everyday problems and to teach students the basics of trig, geometry, etc. . Geometry and Math IV help to prepare you for college and the work force.

The classes you need to go to a four-year college are Algebra I & II and geometry.  For a two-year college you just need two credits of math.
