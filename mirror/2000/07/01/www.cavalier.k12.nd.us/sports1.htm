April 6, 2000
The Tornadoes start their baseball season! by Adam Furstenau
Tuesday night was the first game of the 2000 season, the Tornadoes travled to Edmore. They split the games by winning one and losing one. Blake Trenbeath earned a win in his pitching in the first game. Matt Houdek's effort fell short as the Tornadoes lost the second game. Tonight the Tornadoes host Munich in the first home game of the season. On Saturday the team travles to St. Thomas to finish out this week. This year should be fun and exciting and the fans look forward to a very promising year.

April 6, 2000
Golf Team Looks Forward to A Long Season Ahead By Jordan Watson
The Cavalier golf team's are going to be getting lots of practice this year with absolutely no snow on the ground at the end of March and early April. As long as the weather stays good the teams will be sharpining their skills for a promising season. With one boy’s golfer coming back who went to the state tournament. This year Cavalier hopes to send a team to state. Hopefully the weather will coperate.
