Cavalier is a friendly community of 1,500 people located in scenic northeastern North Dakota. Students graduate from Cavalier High School well prepared for stepping into the world.
    The mission of the Cavalier Public School is to provide opportunities leading to intellectual and cultural maturity, social and ethical growth, and global awareness. Through effective teaching and interaction, students will be free to develop attitudes, interests and values that are essential attributes for their academic and social growth.
    In a cooperative effort involving a dedicated staff, supportive parents and community, and motivated students, we provide an education that meets the academic, physical, social and emotional needs of the student.
