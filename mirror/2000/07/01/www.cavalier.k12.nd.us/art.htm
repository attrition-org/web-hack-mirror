 Mr. Anderson's art classes are the place to be. Here students can learn some basic skills and techniques in the fields of drawing, painting, and sculpting. It's a great place to kick back and relax or find a hidden talent.  

How did you first get into teaching?
When I went to school, I had excellent teachers. For me it's kind of a "follow in the footsteps" thing. It was that way in high school and college, too. I also think it's fun to be around young people.

Why art?
It's the only thing I'm good at!

Did you teach anywhere else before Cavalier?
I also taught at Larimore.

How long have you been here?
I'm on my 25th year.

Have you been impressed by our student work?
Many times. It keeps me going. I'm happy to be in such an artistic community.

How about the ITV groups?
I like teaching in the interactive television medium. It's fun. Most of the students really succeed. They always give lots of effort and I'm happy with them.

What teaching methods do you use on your students?
First of all, you need two things to succeed in my art classes: you must have the work ability to finish a project, and second, you must be honest with yourself. Just do the best you can do and NEVER quit.

What's your favorite medium ?
The first has to be watercolor, the second is pencil.
