 You may subscribe to receive e-mail notification when new Opinions are posted to the North Dakota Supreme Court website, and to be informed when Supreme Court Notices (of proposed or new rules, and the like) are posted.

There is no charge for this service.

Subscribe by e-mail to: webmaster@court.state.nd.us. If the e-mail function is active on your browser, you should be able to click on this address to start your message. (Otherwise, just use your e-mail in the regular manner.)

Put the following in the body of your email form (using your own name and e-mail address, of course):

Subscribe to E-mail Notice
Your Name
Your E-mail Address
(And, if you are a North Dakota licensed lawyer) Your N.D. Bar I.D. Number

Send the message. You will receive an e-mail confirmation when you have been successfully added.
