Barry Bruckman

Barry, a former Glen Cove resident, successfully completed the Hawaiian Ironman competition on Saturday, October 23, 1999. Barry was one of 1500 world class athletes who participated in this triathlon. The race consists of a 2.4 mile swim, 112 mile bike ride, followed by a 26.4 mile run. Barry completed the race in 12 hours and 25 minutes. Barry, the son of Stephen Bruckman and the late Susan Bruckman graduated from Glen Cove High School in 1984, where he got his start in cross-country running. He then attended the University of Virginia. Barry is a systems analyst where he lives with his wife Eileen. They are expecting their first child in December.

Class of '79

Michael Ricciardi

Attended SUNY Buffalo and graduated in 1983 with a double major, computer science and applied math, and later attended New York Institute of Technology, where he graduated in 1987 with a master's degree in computer science. Mike's interest in distributed and embedded systems eventually led him to form his own company in 1992, after his tenures with Multiwire, United Technologies, Loral Advanced Projects and CACI. Mike currently resides in Fairfax, Virginia with his wife Marie and two children, Michele and Domenic, and spends much of his time working as a director and president of Ricciardi Technologies, Inc, a Fairfax, Virginia based information technology firm that provides proffesional engineering services to fortune 100 companies around the world.

Howard Israel

Attended SUNY Albany, graduated with a double major-- Business and Computer Science. Worked for the National Computer Security Center at the National Security Agency (NSA) of the Department of Defense at Ft. Meade, MD, as a civilian employee for 5 years. In 1987 went to work at AT&T Bell Labs in various NJ locations. In 1991 he completed a Master's in Computer Science from Stevens Institute of Technology. In 1997 he was a Product Manager for AT&T WorldNet Service (a major ISP) in charge of various network operations planning and management and also in charge of their security efforts. In 1998 he started work at Lucent Technologies in charge of their Network Security Consulting practice. He has authored/co-authored several technical papers and government reports on Computer/Network Security and reviewed a major textbook ("Fundamentals of Computer Security"). He has been an active Internet user since 1982, back when there were over 200 newsgroups! He has travelled extensively in the US and throughout the world, most noteably when he lived in Taipei Taiwan for 6 months in 1993 with his family. He is married and has 2 children, currently residing in East Brunswick, NJ. "Not bad, considering I was one of 2 students ... out of 11 who didn't get exempt from the ... [Computer Programming] final exam because of crummy grades!"
