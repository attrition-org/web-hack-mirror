INETNC offers a variety of networking solutions on many platforms.

Local Area Networks
 INET offers experience in PC based LANs. Specializing in NT/95/3.11 and Apple networking, we are sure to be of service to those planning on networking their computers, upgrading the network to take advantage of the newest technologies, or simply reconfiguring or adding services to the LAN. We can also set up an Intranet to bring the ease of use of the World Wide Web to your office network. E-mail vwallace@inetnc.com for more information.

Wide Area Networks
 Although commonly used to connect LANs to the Internet, WANs can be of service to anyone trying to connect two physically remote systems. ISDN WANs are commonly used for video teleconferencing, telecommuting, and connecting office networks around town or around the country. E-mail vwallace@inetnc.com for more information on how to put this or other Internet technologies to work for you.
