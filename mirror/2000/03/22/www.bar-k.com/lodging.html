LODGING

The Lodge at the Bar-K is warm and spacious with sleeping accommodations, two baths, kitchen, dining, and a comfortable main room with fireplace. Satellite TV and a near-by shooting range provide after-hunt enjoyment.

 

The Lodge provides all meals which include pancakes, eggs, chili, barbecue, venison, steaks, pies, cookies and cheesecake. Eugene does the camp cooking while Kay is your pastry chef.
