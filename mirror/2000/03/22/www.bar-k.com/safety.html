HUNTING EQUIPMENT & SAFETY

 

Equipment

You will need a 270 or larger rifle and ammunition. You may wish to bring a camera, binoculars, a cooler for your meat to be taken home in, and liquor or beer.
 

Safety

In the Lodge, all firearms must be kept unloaded. Each guest is responsible for his own gear. Hunting will be done strictly from the hunting blinds. In the event a game animal is wounded but not killed, the hunter is to stay at the blind until one of our guides comes to help trail it.
