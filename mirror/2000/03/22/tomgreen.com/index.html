Tom has been diagnosed with cancer.

Hi everyone,

By now many people have probably heard that Tom was diagnosed with cancer last week.

For many weeks, his right testicle was painfully swollen. He looked at it and realized that this wasn't normal... this was something new. He went for exploratory surgery and his testicle (the swollen one) was removed once they determined it was cancerous.

Testicular cancer is one of the most curable forms of the disease.

Tom is having another operation today to make sure they got all of the cancer. Tom is in good spirits. In fact, he is filming the whole experience for an up-coming episode of the show. It'll be called "The Cancer Special".
