JEWETT Capabilities

JEWETT offers the capabilities and technological advantages of a large machine shop combined with the personal attention that you should expect from a family owned business. Our continued investment in the latest technology in new equipment puts us on the cutting edge. Our experienced machinists, team leaders, and project managers ensure that we can tackle even the most difficult jobs with great success.

The Way We Serve Customers

We don’t have standard products. Every job is different; every customer has different needs. Below are descriptions of some of the ways we have worked with customers to help you understand the range of our capabilities. We have:

  • Entered into long-term contracts with OEMs to provide just-in-time delivery on parts. In appropriate instances, we will dedicate one or more workcenters to the customer.
  • Run economic quantities and inventoried parts until needed by the customer.
  • Provided turnkey purchasing and machining of castings.
  • Participated in new product development by designing, building, assembling and installing machines.
  • Entered into secrecy agreements, and in some cases, provided private and secured manufacturing space for the customer.
  • Designed and built tooling and fixtures.
  • Provided reverse engineering, equipment rebuilding and refurbishing services.
  • Run low volume prototype and spare parts.

JEWETT is in business to make our customers more competitive in their markets.
