Brief History

Justin M. Fry believed he could produce a better printing ink than anyone else in 1936. He started from scratch, experimented with materials and processes and developed a superior ink for his Richmond, Virginia customers. The needs were simpler then...and the customers were closer. But the guiding principals on which the company was founded have remained the same. j.m. fry has always based its success on finding new ways to produce better inks using the most current equipment and research methods.As j.m. fry's business expanded, so did its production capabilities. Increasingly modern facilities were built throughout the Southeast. The most recent in Richmond, is the premiere printing ink production and research facility in the United States, If not the world. Using its founders success formula, j.m. fry has transformed itself from a local printers supplier to an emerging worldwide presence in the industry.

Our Mission Since 1936
j.m. fry's mission is to apply superior technical expertise to the production of high-quality printing inks that will position our customer's products in a global market. To that end, we employ the finest technology available to develop and produce the finest product, looking ahead to every possible improvement in quality and service. No matter how good the product is, we continue to make it better. We encourage innovation and update employee education and training constantly in order to stay ahead of industry trends. We become our customers business partner, educating hem fully, anticipating their needs for both products and service and responding immediately to their requests.
