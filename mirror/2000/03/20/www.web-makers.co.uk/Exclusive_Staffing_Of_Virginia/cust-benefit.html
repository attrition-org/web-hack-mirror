FOR OUR CUSTOMERS, WE PROVIDE:

QUICK RESPONSE TIME / AUTOMATED EMPLOYEE RETRIEVAL SYSTEM

EXCLUSIVE STAFFING maintains files on over 2000 qualified employees. We generally can respond within 30 - 45 minutes when filling an order for a temporary. This prompt response time is possible as a result of a unique, custom-designed Employee Information/Retrieval program. This PC-based program designed especially for Exclusive Temporaries of Virginia maintains our employee data base in readily accessible files which may be accessed in a number of ways, including by individual employee name, number, skill or combination of skills. Other pertinent data is also immediately accessible such as education, employment history, credit history, availability and transportation. Quite often, Exclusive Temporaries can confirm the availability of a specific temporary during the initial telephone conversation with a customer.

CUSTOMIZED INVOICING

Exclusive Staffing has the capability to customize invoicing to include cost center, department, division or other data to assist and facilitate internal management reporting.

SERVICE GUARANTEE

We guarantee all our employees. If an Exclusive employee is unsatisfactory we do not charge - period!
