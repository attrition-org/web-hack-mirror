About Our Beds

Most of Brass Beds of Virginia's designs for iron and brass beds are reproductions of turn of the century American styles. We feel that the classic, elegant look of these beds allows you the greatest freedom in decorating. Your new bed will become the center piece in any room setting, be it traditional or contemporary. Departing from the traditional lines of reproduction metal beds Brass Beds of Virginia has recently introduced several transitional style steel beds for customers who wants the cleanest most contemporary look.

The Making of an Heirloom

Brass Beds of Virginia believes in the old fashioned tried and true method of making metal beds. Every one of our beds is an authentic reproduction of an original. Our skilled artisans combine the highest quality materials available, and we use the most durable modern finishes to craft heirlooms of lasting beauty. They bend the scrollwork by hand to form smooth, gently curling curves. Every intersection in a bed's design is fitted with a casting, and each casting is cleaned and polished smooth before the chosen finish is applied. We know that the purchase of a hand made bed is a substantial investment, so we go to extremes to make sure you get your money's worth. Each finished bed is the product of many hours of work .

Iron Beds

Iron beds are constructed of cold roll, heavy gage steel tubing, and solid bar stock for strength and durability. An easy-to-assemble, integrated frame system is used for sturdiness and rigidity. Finishes are electrostatically applied powder coats for chip and scratch resistant beauty. Brass Beds of Virginia offers numerous hand-painted finishes to satisfy any decorating need.

Brass Beds

Brass Beds of Virginia uses heavy gauge solid brass tubing with a wall thickness of .040 inch or greater. Side posts are steel reinforced for strength and durability. We use an integrated frame system (it's actually part of the bed) to ensure sturdiness and rigidity. The natural finish of our brass beds will age to a beautiful patina or can be cleaned periodically for a bright and shiny look.

Warranty

All beds are warranted for life against defects in workmanship and materials. Coatings and finishes have a one year warranty.

Delivery

Unlike most manufacturers, Brass Beds of Virginia handcrafts each bed to order. We do not carry a finished inventory on any of our designs. Delivery traditionally runs from (6) to (8) weeks depending upon the time of year.
