Stay Tuned
Scenario: You are browsing the web and suddenly need to leave your computer for a few minutes. When you return, you find that you are no longer connected to the Internet. What happened? Simple, your Internet Service Provider has disconnected you! Why? It assumed that since
there is no activity from your side, you are not there, and don’t deserve
the promised "unlimited" time for which you pay.
Solution: Stay Tuned™! What this program does, is simulates Internet
activity to prevent you from being disconnected. It is a very comfortable
program which resides in your system tray (next to the clock and the
modem indicator).


CloakMe
A clever utility allows you to hide windows, taking them out off the screen, off the taskbar and off the ALT-TAB chain. This way you can
hide those programs that you want to keep open and running, but you don't want to be seen. Tons of comfortable features!
