Since 1962, we have helped thousands of young men improve their grades and turn their lives around; in the process, achieve the academic distinction they may never have known.

 

hoosing a school is a difficult decision. There are many attractive and excellent schools, and the differences between any of them are not always readily apparent. Your job is to look past the surface similarities to find the school that will best suit your needs and help you prepare for college and beyond.

What sets Saint Thomas More apart? We are an all-boys school which makes it easier for you to focus your energy on academics and athletics. There are under 200 students in grades 8 - 12 and postgraduates. Therefore, each student is a unique person to us, not a number in a computer database. We offer a strong athletic program in which you can reach your potential and achieve your ultimate goals. We offer a challenging curriculum supported by individual attention within a caring environment.
