Service: Boys College Preparatory Boarding School.

Focus: Students with ability, who have failed to reach their potential.

Location: Oakdale, Connecticut.   Founded: 1962

Population: Boys ages 13 - 20.   Grades: 8 - PG

Number of students: 200 Students. Geographical Range: The United States, Canada, Japan, Mexico, Spain, Germany, Korea, China, Africa, Hong Kong, Switzerland, Holland, Israel and Russia.

College Placements: St. Louis University, University of Connecticut, University of Cincinnati, University of Colorado, University of California at Berkeley, Old Dominion, University of Massachusetts at Amherst, Assumption College, Boston College, Lafayette College, Quinnipiac College, Harvard University, Brown University.
