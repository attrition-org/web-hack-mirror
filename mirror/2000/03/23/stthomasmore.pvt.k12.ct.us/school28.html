aint Thomas More School is a Catholic college preparatory boarding school for boys in grades eight through postgraduate which admits students of any religion, race, national or ethnic origin.

Saint Thomas More School educates the learner who has demonstrated evidence of intellectual ability, yet needs a structured environment to achieve academic success.

Saint Thomas More School strives to strengthen the moral, intellectual, physical, and aesthetic development of every student.

Saint Thomas More School offers a challenging curriculum supported by individual attention within a caring environment. Small classes, tutorial instruction, monitored evening study hall, and remedial programs foster skill development, content mastery and sound study habits.

Saint Thomas More School believes that strong teacher-student relationships, personal guidance, positive reinforcement, and the experience of success through achievement are necessary to build a positive self-concept.
