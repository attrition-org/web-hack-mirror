From: New York City, New Jersey, and Points South

From: Western Massachusetts and upstate New York

From: Rhode Island and New England Seaboard

From: Boston, Worcester, & Northern New England

From: Hartford, Pennsylvania, and Points West




Note: In looking at a map, remember that although our mailing address is Oakdale, this is a section of the town of Montville.


From New York City, New Jersey, and Points South:

Take New England Thruway (I-95) to Exit 70, Old Lyme, Ct. Turn left at the end of the highway ramp, to go north on Rte. 156, approximately 10 miles to the end. Turn right on Rte. 82, proceeding approximately 8 miles to Gardner Lake. While on Rte. 82, you will go past Rte. 354 (Gardner Lake and Colchester sign) on your left. Continue on Rte. 82 for about .5 mile to Catholic Church on right. Take left on Doyle Road just before church. After .5 mile, turn left, onto Cottage Road, and go .3 mile to School Entrance.


From Western Massachusetts and upstate New York:

From Massachusetts Turnpike take either I-91 or I-84 to Hartford. Take Rte. 2 East (Norwich-New London) to Exit 19. Go right on Rte. 11 approximately 7 miles to Rte. 82. Turn left onto Rte. 82 and proceed about 5 miles, going past Rte. 354 (Gardner Lake and Colchester sign) for about .5 mile to Catholic Church on right. Take left onto Doyle Road just before church. After .5 mile on Doyle, turn left onto Cottage Road, and go .3 mile to School Entrance.



From Rhode Island and New England Seaboard:

Take I-95 South to New London, Ct. Take Exit 82 for Rte. 85 heading toward Hartford/Colchester. Stay on Rte. 85 for approximately 14 miles until intersection with Rte. 82 at Salem 4 Corners. Turn right on Rte. 82 and go approximately 4 miles, past Gardner Lake and sign for Rte. 354 (Gardner Lake and Colchester sign). Continue for about .5 mile to Catholic Church on right. Take left onto Doyle Road just before church. Then take next left, onto Cottage Road and go .3 mile to School Entrance.


From Boston, Worcester, & Northern New England:

Take Massachusetts Pike to Exit 10 at Aubrun, Mass. Then take Rte.
I-395 south for approximately 60 miles to Exit 80 (Norwich, Ct.). Turn right on Rte. 82, and proceed for approximately 5 miles to Rte. 163 and take next left (approximately .8 mile) onto Doyle Road. After .2 mile, turn right onto Cottage Road, and go .3 mile to School Entrance.


From Hartford, Pennsylvania, and Points West:

Take I-84 East through Hartford, and then turn onto Rte. 2 east, heading toward Norwich. Take Rte. 2 to Exit 19. Go right on Rte. 11 approximately 7 miles to Rte 82. Turn left onto Rte. 82 and proceed about 5 miles to Gardner Lake. Continue on Rte. 82, going past Rte. 354 (Gardner Lake and Colchester sign) for about .5 mile to Catholic Church on right. Take left onto Doyle road just before church. After .5 mile on Doyle, take left onto Cottage Road, and go .3 mile to School Entrance.
