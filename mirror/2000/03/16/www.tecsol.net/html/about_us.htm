TecSol Net, Inc.  Overview

TecSol Net, Inc. is a consulting, and network design firm specializing in Intranet, Internet, and Wide Area Corporate Networks. We can meet your needs for  Web design services, Networking devices, and computer hardware. We have been providing communication services to clients for 14 years in the Chicago Area.  The combination of AT&T and TecSol Net provides an end to end communication system with complete  project management.  Our clients are typically small to mid-sized companies headquartered in the greater Chicago Metro Area.
