Pamet Systems, Inc., founded in 1987, designs and implements broad-based information technology solutions for public safety agencies.

Pamet provides customers with innovative applications for enhancing their business processes - enabling agencies to realize cost efficiencies and provide better service. Pamet's services include systems integration and automation to facilitate the acquisition, storage, processing, retrieval and communication of information for public safety agencies.

Pamet System's headquarters is in Acton, Massachusetts, about 30 minutes west of Boston.  The company's stock is publicly traded on the NASDAQ over-the-counter market (NASDAQ OTC:PAMT).

Pamet takes pride in the fact that a large part of our staff is made up of former police officers and fire fighters, so that our software is designed and built by public safety workers for public safety workers.  We understand what you do because we have been there.

The company's solutions are being used by over 120 public safety organizations, varying from small towns, to large, multi-jurisdictional agencies, to non-municipal agencies such as colleges and universities.  We invite you to visit the web sites of some of our customers.
