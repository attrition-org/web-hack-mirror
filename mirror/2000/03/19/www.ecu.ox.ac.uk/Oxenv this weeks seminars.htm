Mansfield College

Please bring your own lunch and join us.

OXTEHK was founded in 1998 in order to facilitate interdisciplinary discussions related to traditional knowledge and environmental, health and well-being concerns of indigenous, traditional and local communities. There are 5 interest "clusters": (1) ethnoecology; (2) ethnomedicine/ethnopharmacology; (3) cultural landscapes; (4) cognitive and linguistic studies; (5) philosophy and ethics.

To join OXTEHK or to find more details, please consult the Linacre College Web Page (www.linacre.ox.ac.uk) or contact

Wildlife Photography Exhibition at the Oxford Museum of Natural History

The renowned annual British Gas Wildlife Photographer of the Year Competition, organised by the Natural HIstory Museum and BBC Wildlife Magazine, has culminated in a display of over 80 winning photographs chosen from over 21,000 entries. First prize in the British Wildlife section has been awarded to Oxfordshire photographer Ron Perkins for his photograph of a roe deer in a glade.

The exhibition is currently on display at the Oxford University Museum of Natural History. It runs until Sunday 5 March and is open daily from 12 noon to 5pm. Admission is free.
