Department for Continuing Education

The Evolution of Life on Earth

A series of seven lectures to be held at the University Museum, Parks Road, Oxford Tuesday evenings at 8.00 pm starting 8 February 2000

Series Tickets at £29.50 each

How life has evolved on planet Earth is an ever interesting topic. Our understanding of this process is still being challenged in exciting ways by new fossils and new molecular data. Following the success of last year's Darwin's Astonishing Legacy, this series for the general public brings leading speakers to explain what we know today.
