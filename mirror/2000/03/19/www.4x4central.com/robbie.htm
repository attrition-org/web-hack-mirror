Robbie Gonzalez's
1995 Explorer


Robbie Gonzalez of Rock'n It Creative Designs created this Ultimate Family Vehicle. The objective was to build a quality performance vehicle: inside and out. The key was to be able to have one vehicle that can do it all: anytime, anywhere. 

The foundation would be the vehicle itself. A 1995 Ford Explorer SUV, with its power, comfort and look. To build on this foundation and enhance the versatility, performance, comfort and safety of the vehicle was the desired goal. After countless hours and several months of designing, fabrication, testing and various installations, The Ultimate Family Vehicle has become a reality. All in part to all of the dedicated contributors, whom without their efforts, this project would not be possible. RCD is extremely thankful to all of the individuals involved and is very proud to represent them. 

This SUV has captured 1st place awards at every competition entered. It has been featured in domestic and international publications. It has been a feature vehicle at SEMA since 1995, as well as a feature vehicle at CES in 1997.
