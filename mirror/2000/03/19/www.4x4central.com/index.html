Thanks for venturing "Off the Beaten Trail"! This web site was built by an Explorer enthusiast with one goal in mind, to prove the off-road worthiness of America's top selling SUV. Please take the time to view all of our Explorer features. Each one has been uniquely crafted for everything from rock crawling to surf fishing. 

Numerous technical articles will help you decide which modifications, if any are right for you and your Explorer.

You can join us on the trail for some "Serious Explorations" or just sit back and view articles from our past  "Ultimate Explorations".		 I would also like to invite you to participate in our "Ultimate Explorations" message board. This threaded message board is extremely active and there are lots of great people there ready to help you solve your most obscure Explorer related problems, whether off-road related or not. 

Now, let the Explorations begin!
