Explorer Graphics By:
Kafka Custom Graphics and Pinstriping
Steve Kafka painted these "tribal markings" using a flame paint scheme. Steve specializes in custom paint jobs for any type of vehicle. I told Steve that I wanted to freshen up the appearance of my Explorer and that I liked flame jobs, but wanted something a little different. A couple days later I met with him and he showed me sketches of nine graphic designs. Steve showed me a standard flame job, flames that were more streamlined, and these tribal markings which immediately caught my eye.
