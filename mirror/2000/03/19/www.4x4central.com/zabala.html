Zabala Brother's Explorer
Exploring Ecuador

My name is David Zabala. My families Ford Explorer is a 1992 4-door V6. We live in Quito, Ecuador, South America.  As you can see we have great places to go fourwheeling.

Suspension:
Rancho 9000 Shocks

Lift:
3 inch body lift

Winchs:
9000 lb. Ramsey Winch Front. 2000 lb. Winch which you can mount it only when you need it in the rear.

Interior:
12 CD Player from Pioneer.  Radio from Pioneer
120 watt speaker at the front and 150 watt speaker at the back, also from Pioneer.

Altimeter, inclinometer and a compass.

Warn manual hubs

Chains for mud and snow
.
We also have "El muerto", I don't know what you call it, it is useful when you're trapped in mud and you can't attach the winch cable to anything, so you dig a hole, burry "El Muerto" and attach the winch cable to it, and you can take your car out of the mud.
