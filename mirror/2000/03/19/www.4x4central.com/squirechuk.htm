John Squirechuk's
1993 Explorer XLT

(Click on photos for larger images)
Johns 1993 Explorer currently has about 120,000 km on it and has had no major trouble along the way. All of the modifications were done by John. The photos were taken near Lethbridge, Alberta and Powell River which is just up the coast from Vancouver, British Columbia. 
Engine 4.0L V6
Transmission 4 Speed Automatic
Suspension 4" Trailmaster suspension lift and shocks. 3" body lift. ProComp steering stabilizer
Tires/Wheels 35" Goodyear MT's on 15x8.5 rims
Lighting 9-Pro Comp offroad lights
Misc. Smittybuilt Surestep Nerfbars, Lund Visor, Thule Roof Rack, Swagman Bike Rack
Future Plans Lower Gears, Computer Chip, Headers/Exhaust, More Suspension Travel
