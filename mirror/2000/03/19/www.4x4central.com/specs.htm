Stats for Rick's 1993 Explorer XL
Engine 4.0 Liter V6 EFI, Pro Dyno custom exhaust, KKM True Rev Intake Kit, Hypertech Computer Chip
Transmission 5 - Speed Manual
Transfer Case BW 13-54 Manual Shift
Front Suspension Rough Country 3 1/2" Lift brackets with Superlift 5-1/2" Springs, Custom FST shock hoop, dual Rancho 9000 shocks, FST extended radius arms.
Rear Suspension Springs Relocated Over Axle, Explorer main leaf has had it's eyes cut off and a Ranger main leaf was installed on top for extra wheel travel, Rancho 9000 Shocks, FST shock mounts. Relocated swaybar.
Ramp Travel Index 803 on a 24deg ramp. A stock Explorer scores about 440.
Differentials ARB Airlockers Front and Rear 4:56 Gears, Warn manual hubs.
Axles Front Dana 35 TTB, Rear Ford 8.8"
Communications Icom 2800H VHF/UHF Transceiver, Icom 706 HF Transceiver, Garmin GPS III+
Winch Ramsey Pro 9000
Electrical Dual Optima Batteries,
Tires/Wheels BFG MT 33x12.50-15 On 15x8 Steel Wheels
Air Supply Dual ARB Compressors with 6 gallon tank, FST Air Source 5lb CO2 tank.
Skid Plates Front Differential, Transfer Case, Fuel Tank
Bumpers Front Smittybuilt, Rear Tire Carrier - Gas Can Carrier - Bumper by FST
Nerf Bars Custom FST Nerf Bars, 1/4" wall rectangular tubing.
