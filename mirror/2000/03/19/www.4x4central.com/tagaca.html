Stephen Tagaca's
1991 Ford Explorer Sport
My Explorer has 133,000 miles on it.  About 80,000 of it is in lifted condition. I'm from Hawaii. 

The mud pictures were taken on a fourwheeling trip that included about 40 trucks.  The trip was on  a trail called Mana Road on the Big Island of Hawaii. The run took place in November of 1998.  The only truck that didn't get stuck in the pit was a Ranger you see in the picture.  Chevy's even got stuck.  There are a lot of lifted trucks on this island.  Mostly Fords.  As we all say it's FORD country. 
 
 

Engine: 4.0L SOHC V-6, Borla exhaust
Transmission: 5 speed manual
Tires / Wheels: 36 inch Super Swamper Radials on Weld Road Hawk 15x12 wheels 
Front Suspension: SuperLift 5.5", SuperRunner Steering Kit, 1 inch front coil spacer, Rancho RS 5000 shocks
Rear Suspension:  Rear add-a-leaf 1 inch,  Rancho RS 5000 shocks, Total of 9.5 inches of lift
Differentials / Gearing 5:13 Gears, Trac Lock rear Differential
Body Lift: 3 inch Performance Accessories body lift
Audio: Clarion cd player, JL audio subwoofer, Precision power amplifier, MBquart mids and tweeters.
