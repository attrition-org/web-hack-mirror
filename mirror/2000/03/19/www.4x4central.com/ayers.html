Michael Ayers 1994 Explorer
Michael's Explorer is set up for a different kind of off-roading than what I'm used to. Here his Explorer is shown setup for some surf-fishing

Michael runs aired down BFG 30x9.50-15 AT's on the sand. "I have added a receiver hitch and bought a carry-all that I have modified to transport surf-fishing poles, as well as adding a rack to the front. Surf fishing has become a favorite of mine. With the Explorer, I can haul all of my stuff right to the water. I don't need to worry about crowds, not many people can get to where I go. It amazes me how many people try to drive in the soft sand with their air pressure at 40 lbs!"
