Search Web

The Galaxy database is small and appears to enter submissions in an irregular manner. It has never listed my websites after many submissions. You may add your website if you wish. Galaxy has a directory format also, but the categories are not well thought out and the hierarchical index seems haphazard at best. The directory appears not to be integrated with the searchable database so you need to look in both places. I took a look at Galaxy's What's New page and found that Cody, Wyoming was new. I then did a search for "Cody Wyoming" using "all words". Galaxy returned 7 sites, but did not include the new listing for Cody, Wyoming. If you want help, it's available. Galaxy has one of the best output formats and options of all the search engines. Its terse function returns a compact listing with just the most relevant information. Its verbose function returns comprehensive well-formated information with virtually everything it has in an easy to read and understandable presentation. For those who are interested, information is available about the producers of Galaxy, the Trade Wave Corporation. (5/5/96)
