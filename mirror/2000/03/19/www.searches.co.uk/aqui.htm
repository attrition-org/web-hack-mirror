aquí is not like other directories. It not only allows you to submit your website to it, but it also allows you, or anyone else for that matter, to submit links to the URL you submitted. It also does this immediately so everybody on the web can instantly access the information. You may also personalize the links by defining what kind of a web surfer would be interested in this link. Users of aquí register with the directory and define the type of web surfer they are. When a registered user accesses a link, aquí presents the related links in an order from top to bottom that reflects their personal preferences as registered in their profile. Webmasters may also create their own aquí bumper-sticker for their web page. This allows web surfers to add related links to the aquí link for their home page. You can try this by adding links to our aquí link by clicking on our bumper-sticker on our Special Awards and Other Recognitions page.
