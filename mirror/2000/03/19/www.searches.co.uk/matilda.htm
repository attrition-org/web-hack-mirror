International Australian  

Matilda is the search engine for AAA World Announce Archive and The World Commercial Sites Index down under in Australia. This directory is not organized as a true directory. It is a flashy searchable database of links divided into two categories: Australian only and the rest of the world. You can search in one or both parts of the database at the same time. The Directory part of this search engine has a heavy commercial slant. You can also place free classified ads in a variety of web publications throughout the world here. And interesting side-path of this site is its Missing Persons Internet Bureau.
