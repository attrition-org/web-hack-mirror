Captiva Island, Florida

Directions to South Seas Resort

Take Interstate 75 to Exit 21. Travel west on Daniels Parkway to State Road 869 (Summerlin Rd) and turn left, continuing south to the Sanibel Causeway ($3 toll). Cross the Sanibel Causeway and turn right at the 4-way stop at Periwinkle Way. Follow Periwinkle until it ends at Tarpon Bay Road. Turn right onto Tarpon Bay Road, then take a left at the next stop sign (Sanibel-Captiva Road). Follow San-Cap Road all the way to the tip of Captiva Island. Turn right at the South Seas Resort entrance sign and proceed to the guard gate.
