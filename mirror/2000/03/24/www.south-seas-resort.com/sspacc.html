Accommodations

South Seas Resort offers a variety of luxury accommodations ranging from deluxe hotel rooms to spacious one-, two- and three-bedroom villas complete with fully equipped kitchens.

These accommodations are strategically places throughout the resort's 330 acres, with views of the beach, the bay, the marina, the tennis center,  or golf course. All accommodations were built in harmony with nature - and no building is taller than the tallest tree.
