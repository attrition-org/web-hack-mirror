Far from the rigors of the everyday world is
where you'll find South Seas Resort. Here, off
the southwestern coast of Florida on Captiva Island, you'll encounter everything you've ever wanted from a world-class meeting resort. All perfectly set in a tropical paradise.

It's an idyllic atmosphere where manatees
and dolphins frolic. An island setting with 2 1/2 miles of pristine beach. A full menu of resort activities to challenge and amuse; luxurious accommodations to pamper and indulge. Delectable dining from intimate to casual.
More than 27,000 square feet of flexible
function space and a Conference Services
Staff that's experienced in pulling it all together.
