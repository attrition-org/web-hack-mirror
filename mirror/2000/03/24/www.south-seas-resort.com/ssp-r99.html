Experience a wide variety of accommodations at South Seas Resort. Our seasonal rate ranges reflect the opportunity to choose the type of accommodations that best suit your needs. We offer everything from spacious hotel rooms to one-, two- and three-bedroom accommodations. We also offer a selection of private homes, both on property and just outside the resort on Captiva Island. Apart from accommodation type, rate ranges reflect location and view. Locations are available with spectacular views of the Gulf, gardens, marina or golf course and convenient to the beach, marina, restaurants and shopping.
