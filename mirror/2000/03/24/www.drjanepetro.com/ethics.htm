As the Chairperson of
the Hospital Ethics Committee of Westchester Medical Center and Clinical Ethics Consultative Service, Dr Petro, is deeply committed to sharing medical information with the general public.

On this page she will raise and update medical issues, recommend other helpful web sites and generally discuss matters that relate to health and medical responsibility.

General Health and Well Being

http://www.apha.org/index.html
As the largest public health organization in the world, the American Public Health Association does all kinds of work on health and well-being, mainly from a disease prevention view.
They are also active in social justice issues doing research into drug addiction, poverty and infant mortality. As a standing member in their injury prevention section, Dr Petro highly recommends this organization's valuable site.

Domestic Violence
Dr Petro is a standing member of the
County Coalition on Westchester Domestic Violence and Chair of the Medical and Social Services Subcommittee of the Westchester County Commission on Domestic Violence. She recently participated in the
Commission on Domestic Violence Fatalities in New York City.

The Governor announced the findings of the Commission on Oct 7,1997.The findings report will be used to create legislation designed to reduce domestic violence fatalities in New York State.

Domestic Violence Sites and Resources

http://www.fvpf.org
This is the Family Violence Prevention Fund. It is a major organization promoting the health profession's involvement in recognizing and acting to prevent domestic violence. they provide health care workers with kits, posters, bumper stickers and excellent medical information about DV.

http://www.microserve.net/~ping/links.html
This web site list links, mostly legal, regarding domestic violence. It is a good resource site. Some of the links are dead ends.

http://www.microserve.net/~ping/art.html
This site offers clip art that can be downloaded and used for brochures, mailing, etc

http://www.feminist.org/911/crisis.html
These are domestic violence hot lines.

http://www.ofoinc.org/dvlinks.htm
These are New YOrk State hotline linkes.

http://www.ama-assn.org/public/releases/assault/orderfrm.htm
this is the AMA diagnosis and treatment guidelines link. Tells medical professionals what to look for in family violence.

http://www.acep.org/PUBLIC/fc009803.htm
This is a page from the College of Emergency Physicians and gives a doctors guide to identifying and caring for victims of domestic violence.

This link is incredible, with nearly 100 links to DV issues including a wonderful listing, 101 ways to combate DV.

This is the Physicians for Social Responsiblity site. They won the Nobel Peace Prize in the 80's for their work agains nuclear war.
