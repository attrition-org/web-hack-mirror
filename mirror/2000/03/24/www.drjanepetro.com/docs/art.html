Nose
Rhinoplasty: Changes the shape and appearance of the nose, a short procedure with excellent recovery time.

Facelift/ Rhytidectomy:
Tightens the jowls and neck, eliminates that "worn out" look. Highly recommended for those who want their face to match their energy.

  • Forehead lift: tightens the forehead and lifts the brow.
  • Facial Implants : (chin or cheek) emphasizes the cheekbone or jaw-line.


Eyelids -
Blepharaplasty): tightens the baggy skin around the eyes and eliminates that tired, puffy look.

Ears -
Ear Pinback / Otoplasty: tucks the ears in closer to the head, for a neater, smooth look.

  • Torn Earlobes: repair damage from ripping or tearing of the lobe.


Hair -
Hair Transplant: treats male pattern baldness.

Tattoo/Scar -
Removal of unsightly, unwanted tattoos. (whatever were you thinking ?)

  • Scar revision improves the appearance and feel of scars.



Skin -
Skin Resurfacing: (Laser, Peel , Dermabrasion) smooths and evens the texture of the skin.

Breast -
Enlargement: enhances the size and shape of the breast.

  • Tightening/ Mastopexy:
    Tightens the skin of the breast giving a firmer, more youthful appearance.
  • Reduction: reduces the size of the breast.
  • Reconstruction : rebuilds the breast after cancer or trauma.



Abdomen -
Abdominoplasty (Tummy Tuck): Tightens the skin and removes excess fat.

  • Mini-Abdominoplasty: removes the lower abdominal pouching.



Liposuction:- Bodysculpting
Sculpting the body by vacuuming away extra fat.

Legs - Thigh Lift: Tightens the buttocks and thighs.

  • Vein stripping : Removing unwanted varicose or spider veins.



Arms -
Arm Lift: Tightens the skin of the upper arms to avoid "chicken skin " look.

Pectorals -
Large Breasts in Men / Gynecomastia: Reduces breast size
