S. K. Group comprises of four main divisions, performing specific functions and responsibilies.

MANUFACTURING - Sky Industries Limited The manufacturing concern of the group, engaged in the manufacture of various narrow fabric items, mainly Hook & Loop tape fasterners and elastic tapes.

INDENTING & IMPORTS - S. K. Overseas The import and indenting division, representing prestigious companies worldwide, specialising in narrow fabric industries, ultra-sonic non-medical equipment and information technology products in the Indian subcontinent.

EXPORTS - S. K. Exports - S. K. Silks A Government of india recognised export house, this arm is engaged in manufacturing and international marketing of various textile and ready to wear garments.

GLOBAL TRADING - SKAY (USA) Inc. International trading division of the group, situated in the USA, functioning as a marketing platform for the Group's various products and services.
