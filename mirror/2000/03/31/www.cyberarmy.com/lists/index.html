User Contributed Lists

This is the place on the web to use and contribute to our dynamic range of web lists. Note: information from the pages below is totally from visitors to www.cyberarmy.com. It is often misleading, often just plain wrong, and sometimes useful. We have nothing to do with the following pages, we do not endorse any information on them. We don't even edit the pages (the pages are sometimes tidied up by the web scripts, that's all).

Resources

  • Proxy List - use proxies in your browser settings to surf more anonymously. If you know any publically accessable proxies, then contribute them to the list.
  • Wingate List - use wingates to telnet anonymously. Also useful for anonymous IRC.
  • SMTP Relay List - SMTP relays can be used to send anonymous email. You can often telnet to port 25 of an open SMTP relay, or use some mail software.
  • Accounts List - got a shell account or some web site account you dont want any more? Or some other sort of interesting account other people might be interested in? Post it here in case someone else is interested.
  • Address Book - frequent cyberarmy visitors might like to list their handle and contact details in our address book.
