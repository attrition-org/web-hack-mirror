Blue Springs South High School is committed to a partnership among the school, home, and community. At BSSHS, the staff believes that by working together they can provide a positive and safe environment where responsibility and appreciation for learning are encouraged. Their mission is to improve students' creative thinking skills, communication skills, technology skills, opportunities as productive citizens, physical and emotional well-being, positive self-concept, effectiveness in a global economy, respect for our earth and its inhabitants, life-long learning skills, and problem-solving and decision-making skills.
