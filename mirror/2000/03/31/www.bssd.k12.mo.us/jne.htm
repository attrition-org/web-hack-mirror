The staff of John Nowlin Elementary School believes that all students can learn and achieve mastery of basic grade-level skills regardless of their previous academic performance, family background, socioeconomic status, race, or gender. They further believe that it is the purpose at John Nowlin Elementary to educate all students to high levels of academic performance while fostering positive growth in sociavemotional behaviors and attitudes. The staff of JNE accepts the responsibility to teach all students so that they can attain their maximum educational potential.
