The Blue Springs School District has a staff of approximately 1400 employees.  Of this number, about 800 are certified personnel and 600 are support staff.  
   
   
 
       We are extremely proud of our staff.  It is one of the most experienced and well prepared in the area.  More than 70% of our certified staff hold a master's degree or above.  Our students are instructed by a staff of teachers and administrators that hold leadership positions in regional and state professional organizations.  Approximately 68% of our teachers are tenured and 64% of our building administrators have more than six years in the district.  Teacher turnover is low, approximately 11% for 95-96.  Teachers fresh out of college are also hired each year; our school district makes every effort to recruit the best teachers available.

     Our teachers are represented by professional organizations.  Many of our secretaries also belong to a professional organization.  Relationships with these groups have been quite good and continue to be strong because we all work together for the success of our students.
