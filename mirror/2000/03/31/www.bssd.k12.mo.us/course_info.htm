I. Attendance Requirement (Board of Education Policy #5.45)

Eight semesters of attendance are required of all students at Blue Springs senior high schools.

Course Requirements  
English/Language Arts 3 credits
Social Studies/History 3 1/2 credits
Mathematics 2 credits
Science 2 credits
Additional Math or Science 1 credit
Practical Arts 1 credit
Fine Arts 1 credit
Physical Education 1 credit
Health 1/2 credit
Speech/Communication 1/2 credit
Senior Seminar 1/2 credit
Electives 7 credits
                                                                               TOTAL                          23 credits

Students must complete these minimum requirements for a high school diploma as prescribed by the State of Missouri and the Blue Springs R-IV School District.  Students must also pass the U.S. Constitution Test and the Missouri Constitution Test.  Also required for graduation are eight semesters of high school attendance.  Counselors can answer specific questions regarding high school minimum graduation requirements.

Students who have completed their 23 minimum credits upon the conclusion of the seventh semester of high school may choose a modified graduation program.  Counselors can provide details about that program to students and parents.  Students should inquire about this program during the freshman or sophomore year.
