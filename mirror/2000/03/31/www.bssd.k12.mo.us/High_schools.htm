Both high schools provide complete programs with a variety of options to meet the diverse educational interests and needs of our senior high students.  Curriculum offerings at both schools remain the same with students enjoying a seven period day, instead of the traditional six, which allows them more opportunities to explore more subjects.

     Emphasis is placed both on the college-bound and vocationally-bound student.  Two required courses designed specifically to better prepare our students for the world ahead are half credit classes in Senior Seminar and Communications.  Students have choices from a wide variety of sports and extracurricular activities.
