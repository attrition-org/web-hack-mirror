The faculty and staff of James Walker Elementary, as their mission, have dedicated themselves to the education and nurturing of the children in their school environment so that all students will be able to function successfully in an ever-expanding global society. They strive to provide each child with the ability to problem-solve, to make decisions, to work cooperatively, and to realize their academic potential. At JWE the staff believes that these skills presented in a positive, caring atmosphere will enable students to compete in a multi-cultural world.
