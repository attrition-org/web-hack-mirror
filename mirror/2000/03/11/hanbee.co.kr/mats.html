Made by E.V.A. sponge, Mastiff Mats are being used in martial arts academies such as Taekwondo or Karate schools, fitness centers and kindergartens.
Started 25 years ago as a subsidiary of one of the Korea's leading shoe manufacturers to produce shoe sole, Hanbou launched mat market with the first specialized mat for martial arts.
We have supplied quality products to Kukiwon where Taekwondo's official competitions for black belts are held, major universities and many martial arts academies all over the world.
Through the Korea's largest production capacity, more than 8,000 mats are produced per month, and export to U.S. and European countries has been continuously increased in recent years.
