Windows 2000 Information:
While we believe there should be no problem running any of our software on Windows 2000, we reserve any statements of complete compatibility until the final version of Windows 2000 is completely deployed.  Nonetheless, we remain committed to continued compatibility with the Windows operating systems, and if there should be any problems with any of our software under this platform, we will make all necessary changes to accommodate it.

Macintosh Information
We do not have a Macintosh version of our software.  While some customers have reported success using Windows emulators on Macintosh computers, we cannot guarantee compatibility, and are not in a position to offer support for problems that may arise out of using our software with such emulators.

Upgrade Status:

Upgrades from AddWeb 2.x to 3.x require an upgrade purchase.  Recent purchasers of AddWeb 2.x receive a free upgrade.  The customers who fall within the free upgrade period have been notified by e-mail.

The current versions of all other software are free upgrades to all registered owners.  To upgrade from an older version, simply download the most recent version and install it over the old version.  Your registration number will be automatically accepted by the new version.
