Warrior XXI Home Page

WARRIOR XXI defines those required changes to the institutional axis to support training FORCE XXI. WARRIOR XXI has eight major initiatives that will dramatically change both institutional and self development training.. When combined with WARFIGHTER XXI and Warnet XXI, WARRIOR XXI will provide the architectural foundation for the Army training system of the future. Collectively, these three axes constitute Army Training XXI.
