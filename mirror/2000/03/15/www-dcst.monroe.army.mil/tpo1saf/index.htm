Our Mission:

Provide the Army leaders a single composable CGF that supports the requirements to meet all the Army M&S domain CGF needs that  provides a Framework and Supporting Technology permiting OneSAF components to be: Selected, configured, integrated into a Common Synthetic Environment

OneSAF Definition:

A Composable next generation CGF that can represent a full range of operations, systems, and control process (TTP) from entity up to battalion level, with variable level of fidelity that supports all M&S domain (ACR, RDA, TEMO) applications with an emphasis on human-in-the loop and no human in-the-loop.
