Game of the Week: Grand Turismo 2

This Playstation game is undoubtedly one of the best Playstation racing games around.

It comes in a neat little case with 2 discs, one which is a scratch 'n' sniff (when you scratch it is smells like burning rubber).

The gameplay on it is unbelievable, you've got over 1000 cars to choose from and almost as many tracks.

The graphics on this game truly make you think how much games have progressed over the years; from little pixelated games to these 3D games that makes it look like you actually have a Dodge Viper GTS or something similar.

Every single car on this game is made to look EXACTLY like the real thing; as well as tuning, horsepower, paint jobs and wheels, which you can see in the pictures.

Good points: The gameplay. You can have over 100 cars in your "garage" so you don't have to put up with just the one car. It's impossible to get anywhere in the game with just one car because in some of the races it may be horsepower restricted, meaning you can't go into a 200hp race with a 975hp GT One Road car, but you can go in a 975hp race with a Ford KA

Bad points: You gotta get a couple of licenses before you can go into some of the races, but luckily they aren't too hard.

Rating: 10/10
Gameplay: 10/10
Controlling: 8/10 ; Can be hard to control the cars if you don't have them setup correctly
