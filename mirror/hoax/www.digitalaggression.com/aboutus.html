How It All Started...

I would say it started with a gleam in my eye and a dream in my heart.... ahhh who am I kidding! I wanted to be part of that vast underground. Make a page that people could come to and click on lots of porn banners yet never get what they truly wanted. It first started as ArcAngel's Warez...Imagine that, what a corny title eh? But we did have a great looking site....not to shabby for my skill back then. Eventually I got Ult in on the gig and we began making more and more pages of porn and misleading links. =)

I got sick of what I was doing and was about ready to quit the whole idea. Luckily squirtgun (aka: unilynx), and I began talking about what we wanted the page to be. I was more into having good warez and great news, he had the same idea but a better approach to it. We came up with the name Digital Aggression (got a ring to it, don't you think). At first Digital Aggression was like most warez sites. Still into those misleading links and broken downloads, but we did try are hardest to keep most of our links working. We used lots of free web servers; geocities, angelfire, tripod, hotbot, and any others you can think of. They are all sellouts too. Using cheap gimmicks like banner ads, or floating scripts on pages to get more money. I know it cost to keep a server going but when the ad takes up half the page or the script messes up your HTML then I would start to worry about where your going with it.

People know us?....
Of all the things to happen to all of my websites this was probably the biggest shock. People actually knew about our site. In 2 months we where getting huge amount of hits. In 4 weeks alone on our first month we got 100,000 hits. I'm used to seeing my site getting visited once every month but having a hundred thousand was just unbelievable. All I could think was what person in their right mind would visit my site when there are tons of other better sites like New-Warez or Warez Slutzz. Sites I visit to get my programs from.

Why we changed....

I've been asked many times why we have changed from warez to links. In my opinion Digital Aggression wasn't the type of site that could keep up with the warez revolution. It's very, very hard work making a warez site and those who succeed should be applauded! I sort of think of this site as a tribute to those sites that are working their asses off and are not misleading or porn feed. We at DA hope to weed out the good warez, hacking, news, and other sites from the rest. And we also hope to give a chance to those newcomers out there to get linked while at the same time providing top quality links to users.
