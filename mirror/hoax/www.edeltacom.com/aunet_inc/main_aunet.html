Auburn Network, Inc.

 



Company Overview

Auburn Network, Inc., is a multimedia marketing company offering a wide range of services primarily related to sports marketing. The Auburn Network, a division of Auburn Network, Inc., delivers Auburn radio and television programming, as well as game publications, to thousands of listeners, viewers and readers each week.

As the exclusive producer and distributor of Auburn sports programming, the Auburn Network coordinates and distributes football, men's basketball and baseball radio broadcasts, a weekly call-in/talk radio show and weekly television shows for football and men's basketball. The network is also in charge of Auburn's pay-per-view television production, all Auburn home video projects and is heavily involved in a wide range of event marketing and licensing activities related to Auburn Athletics.

Auburn's official gameday publications for football, men's basketball and baseball are also published and distributed by the Auburn Network as well as various Olympic sport publications, posters, and schedule cards.

Additionally, Auburn Network, Inc., offers a wide range of video and audio production services, advertising services, digital audio satellite distribution and broadcast program syndication. The company also operates a commercial radio station licensed to the Opelika/Auburn market.
