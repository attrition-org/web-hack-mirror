Publications

The Auburn Network publishes and distributes the official gameday publications for Auburn's football, basketball and baseball games. The Auburn Football Illustrated has one of the largest circulations of any game program in the nation and was judged "Best in the Nation" by CoSIDA, the national association of college sports information directors, for the 1994, 1995, and 1996 seasons.

Additionally, the network produces various olympic sport gameday publications, posters, and schedule cards.
