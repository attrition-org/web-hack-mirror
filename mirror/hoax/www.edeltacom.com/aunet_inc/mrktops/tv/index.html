The Auburn Football Review

 
This extremely popular show airs on Sundays throughout football season and features head football coach Tommy Tuberville. Highlights from Auburn’s game the day before are reviewed as Coach Tuberville gives his insights and comments about the Tigers’ performance and what lies ahead. Exclusive postgame lockerroom video is a popular part of the weekly show. The half-hour show airs in all Alabama markets, selected Georgia markets and throughout the Southeast on regional cable.

The Auburn Football Preview

A half-hour magazine show, “The Auburn Football Preview” features Auburn assistant coaches who give a scouting report on the upcoming opponent and analyze the Tigers’ week of preparation. In addition, player features, interviews and a weekly visit with Tommy Tuberville are also included. The show airs on Saturdays prior to network college football throughout the fall in all Alabama markets.

The Auburn Basketball Review

Coach Cliff Ellis’ views and comments on Auburn Basketball and the SEC are featured each week on “The Auburn Basketball Review.” A look at the previous week’s basketball action, various features and fast-moving packages give viewers an inside look at Auburn Basketball. The half-hour show airs on Saturdays throughout the SEC basketball season in all Alabama markets and is also available throughout the Southeast via regional cable.
