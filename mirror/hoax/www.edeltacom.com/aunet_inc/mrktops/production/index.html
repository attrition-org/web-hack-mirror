Digital Satellite Distribution

The Auburn Network's production studio, through the Alabama Digital Satellite (ADSN), can deliver your audio production directly to over 120 radio stations throughout Alabama and neighboring states. This digital network provides instant CD-quality distribution, thus eliminating the need for multiple dubs, expensive shipping charges and, most importantly, the waste of time in getting your message on the air.
