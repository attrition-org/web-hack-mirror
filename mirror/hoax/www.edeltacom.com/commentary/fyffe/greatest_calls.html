Fyffe's Greatest Calls

To commemorate Jim Fyffe's 20th year behind the mic for the Auburn Tigers, Auburn Network On-Line is proud to present "Fyffe's Greatest Calls", where you the fan vote on Jim's best call. We've narrowed the field down to 20 of Jim's greatest moments at the mic, several of which include his trademark "Touchdown Auburn!" To hear all 20 of Fyffe's top calls, click on the clips below. To vote follow the link at the bottom of the page.

1. 1982- Bo over the top for a touchdown vs. Alabama

2. 1983- Donnie Humphrey sacks Maryland quarterback Boomer Esiason in the endzone and falls on the fumbled ball for a touchdown

3. 1983- Al Del Greco's field goal to beat Michigan, 9-7, in the 1984 Sugar Bowl.

4. 1984- Brent Fullwood touchdown to beat Florida State, 42-41, in Tallahassee.

5. 1985- Bo Jackson's 74-yard touchdown run to give Auburn a 17-14 win over Georgia Tech.

6. 1986- Lawyer Tillman reverse for a touchdown to beat Alabama, 21-17.

7. 1987- "Tillman, Tillman, Tillman!" touchdown catch vs. Georgia Tech.

8. 1989- Reggie Slack's touchdown pass to Shane Wasden to beat Florida, 10-7.

9. 1989- Win Lyle's field goal and Dennis Wallace's interception to seal the victory against Alabama, 30-20.

10. 1990- Jim Von Wyl's field goal to beat Florida State, 20-17.

11. 1990- Stan White's touchdown run to beat Indiana, 27-23, in the Peach Bowl.

12. 1993- Calvin Jackson's 95-yard interception return for a touchdown vs. Florida.

13. 1993- Scott Etheridge's 41-yard field goal to beat Florida 38-35.

14. 1993- Patrick Nix's touchdown pass to Frank Sanders vs. Alabama.

15. 1993- James Bostic's 70-yard touchdown run to seal the victory over Alabama, 22-14, to cap an undefeated season.

16. 1994- Brian Robinson's interception return for touchdown (the 4th of the day for Auburn) against LSU.

17. 1994- Patrick Nix's game-winning touchdown pass to Frank Sanders in "The Swamp" to stun top-ranked Florida, 36-33.

18. 1997- Jaret Holmes' 39-yard field goal to beat Alabama, 18-17, and give Auburn the SEC Western Division Championship.

19. 1999- Damon Duval's fake field goal for a touchdown vs. LSU.

20. 1999- Ben Leard's 78-yard touchdown pass to Ronney Daniels as Auburn upsets Georgia in Athens, 38-21.
