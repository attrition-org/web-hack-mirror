A Tiger Needs Your Help

September 13, 2000

AUBURN- A former Tiger football player needs your help. Corey Lewis, a quarterback from Chattanooga who lettered for Coach Dye in 1989, '90 and '91, had been diagnosed with Acute Leukemia and will require a bone marrow transplant.

Lewis is currently undergoing treatment in Houston, Tex. A perfect match has been found for the bone marrow transplant, but the cost of the treatment is more than $300,000. Most of this treatment will not be covered by insurance and the Lewis family needs your assistance.

The Auburn Football Letterman's Club (AFLC) is hosting its annual Clebrity Golf Classic golf outing on October 20th and all proceeds from that event will go towards the Corey Lewis Fund. If you are interested in making a donation, or playing in the Celebrity Golf Classic, send your check to:

Auburn Football Letterman's Club/Corey Lewis Fund P.O. Box 1267 Auburn, Ala. 36831

You can also contact Tom Bryan of the AFLC at 334-749-5777 or send an e-mail to golfaflc@aol.com.
