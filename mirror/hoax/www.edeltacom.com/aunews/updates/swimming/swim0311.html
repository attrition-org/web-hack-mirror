Zone B Diving Sends Nine Women, 12 Men

March 11, 2000

AUBURN- The Zone B Diving Championships concluded today as nine women and 12 men qualified for the NCAA Championships. Miami's Kyle Prandi, Tyce Routson and Imre Lengyel and Tennessee's Gabi Chereches qualified on all three boards while Jenny Keim of Miami's women's team qualified on all three boards. Miami had the most divers qualify with four men and three women.

Keim won the one-meter with a score of 427.80 and the three-meter with a score of 566.65 and finished second on the platform with a score of 446.10. Tennessee's Lizzy Flynt won the platform with a score of 458.50.

For the men, Stepan Ahrens won the three-meter board with a score of 610.20 while teammates Routson and Prandi won the one-meter (560.00) and platform (602.45) respectively.

The women's NCAA Championships will be held Mar. 16-18 at the IUPUI Natatorium in Indianapolis, Ind. The following nine women will compete at the meet:
