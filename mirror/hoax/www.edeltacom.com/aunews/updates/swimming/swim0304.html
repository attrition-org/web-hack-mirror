Auburn Men Qualify
Three More For NCAA's

March 4, 2000

AUBURN- In the final meet of the year, before NCAA's, the Auburn men's swim team provisionally qualified three more individuals for NCAA's during the James E. Martin Invitational held today in the Martin Aquatics Center.

Sophomore Brandon Roberts recorded a B-standard time in the 500-yard freestyle with a time of 4:27.93. Sophomore Eric vanGoethem provisionally qualified in the 200-yard breaststroke with 2:02.10, while Andy Haidinyak also swam a B-cut time of 1:48.67 in the 200-yard butterfly.

Auburn will not compete again until the NCAA Championships in Minneapolis, Minnesota, March 23-25. The Tigers finished their season with a record of 6-1, and 5-0 in the SEC, and also won their fourth consecutive SEC title.

Auburn will host the NCAA Zone B Diving Championships March 10-11.
