Auburn Football Practice Report

August 7, 2000

AUBURN- The Auburn newcomers went through their third and final day of preseason drills before two-a-days start on Thursday with a one-hour session on the intramural fields on Monday. Originally slated for two practices on Monday, Head Coach Tommy Tuberville and staff opted for one practice with an afternoon weight-lifting session.

"This our last practice today, we're going to cut it short," Tuberville said. "We've got a lot of guys who are sore and fatigued. They've got a long day tomorrow with academics. We're going to lift weights this afternoon and let them get their legs back tonight. All day tomorrow and Wednesday they will go through orientation and we'll turn it loose on Thursday."

Tuberville was pleased with the newcomers' three days of work, citing the efforts of the defensive linemen.

"I think we've made a lot of progress," Tuberville said. "A lot of these guys are a lot further along than what I would have imaged¯especially the defensive linemen. Now we can throw them into the mix and see what pops up."

Auburn's veterans report to campus on Tuesday. The first day of two-a-days is scheduled for Thursday, Aug. 10, with practices at 5:45 a.m. and 3:45 p.m.

All practice sessions until August 20 will be held on the intramural fields. They are open to the public.
