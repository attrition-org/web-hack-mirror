Auburn Football Practice Report

August 20, 2000

AUBURN- The Tigers returned to the practice field Sunday afternoon for a two-hour workout in full gear after taking Saturday afternoon and Sunday morning off. Head coach Tommy Tuberville was pleased with the Tigers' effort, but noted some rustiness on offense after the time off.

"We looked kind of sluggish after the day off," Tuberville said. "The offense looked like they were running in slow motion. We've got a lot of guys that have to make a tremendous amount of improvement in a week's time. But, we'll get there."

Tuberville cited the play of the defense after the practice, which concentrated mostly on team work.

"Defensively, we're playing a lot better than I would have thought at this point," Tuberville said. "We're playing some young guys and they have made some plays. We've got a lot of team work to get done in the next few days and we'll keep going against each other. We're not going to go against the scout team until we get a little bit better. Overall, the effort was good, the concentration just wasn't there today."

The Tigers will have a brief walk-thru at 9:30 a.m. and practice at 4:45 p.m. Monday before taking Tuesday off for the first day of classes.

All Auburn football practices will be held behind the Auburn Athletic Complex and are open to the public.
