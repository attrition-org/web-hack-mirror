Auburn Football Practice Report

August 6, 2000

AUBURN- The Auburn newcomers went through their second day of preseason drills with an hour and a half practice on the intramural fields Sunday. The group had one practice on Sunday after attending church as a group earlier in the day. The newcomers went through drills with their position coaches and concluded the session with conditioning. Head Coach Tommy Tuberville was encouraged by the group's efforts.

"We didn't put much new into this afternoon," Tuberville said. "We went over some things we went over yesterday. The guys held in there pretty good. We did our first conditioning and the guys are getting tired. Overall it was a good day. They went to church this morning. This is a good group and if they stay together and work together they are going to win a lot of games at Auburn."

The newcomers will practice twice on Monday with session beginning at 10 a.m. and 4:30 p.m. at the intramural fields.

Auburn's veterans report to campus on Tuesday. The first day of two-a-days is scheduled for Thursday, Aug. 10, with practices at 5:45 a.m. and 3:45 p.m.

All practice sessions until August 20 will be held on the intramural fields. They are open to the public.
