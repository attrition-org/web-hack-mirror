Auburn Football Preview To Air During Upcoming Week

August 24, 2000

AUBURN- "Auburn Football 2000," a 30-minute show previewing the upcoming Auburn football season will air on College Sports Southeast and Fox SportsNet South beginning Thursday, Aug. 24 and airing through Monday, August 28.

The show, created and produced by the Auburn Network, is hosted by Rod Bramblett and features interviews with Head Coach Tommy Tuberville along with offensive coordinator Noel Mazzone and defensive coordinator John Lovett. Auburn players James Callier, Ronney Daniels, Ben Leard, and Rob Pate will also be featured in the half-hour show.

Thursday's debut will air at 6:30 a.m. CT and again at 7:30 a.m. CT, and 6 p.m. CT on College Sports Southeast. The show will run on CSS once on Friday, Aug. 25 and Saturday, Aug. 26, four times on Sunday, Aug. 27, and twice on Monday, Aug. 28.

Fox SportsNet Southeast will air the preview show on Saturday, Aug. 26 at 4:30 p.m. CT.
