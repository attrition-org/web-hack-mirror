Auburn's Jay Heard Will Not Return To Basketball Team

August 21, 2000

AUBURN- Auburn backup guard Jay Heard will not return to the Tiger basketball program, head coach Cliff Ellis announced Monday.

The Oxford, Ala., native averaged 3.6 points and 1.1 rebounds and shot 37 percent from the field for the Tigers as a sophomore last season while appearing in 25 games.

If Heard transfers to a Div. I school, he will have to sit out the 2000-01 season under NCAA rules and will have two years of eligibility remaining.
