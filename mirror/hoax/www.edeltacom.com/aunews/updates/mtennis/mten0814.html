Auburn's Stephen Huss Earns ITA All-American Honors

August 10, 2000

AUBURN- Stephen Huss earned All-American honors by the Intercollegiate Tennis Association after posting a 13-8 record with a No. 35 ranking in singles during his senior campaign in 2000.

A native of Melbourne, Australia, Huss advanced to the quarterfinals of the NCAA Men's Tennis Championships this Spring. Huss ranks sixth all-time in singles victories with a record of 86-55 and is first all-time in doubles victories with a mark of 94-30.

The Auburn men's tennis team finished the 2000 season ranked 30th with a record of 15-10.
