Auburn Chamber Adds Six To Tiger Trail

August 18, 2000

AUBURN- The Auburn Chamber of Commerce announced the 2000 inductees to the Tiger Trail of Auburn that lines the streets of downtown Auburn. This years class features Pat Dye, Kenny Howard, Buddy McClinton, Willie Smith and Rosalind Pendergraft-Council.

These athletes will be formally inducted into the Tiger Trail of Auburn during the Induction Breakfast scheduled for Saturday, September 30, 8 a.m. at the Auburn University Hotel and Dixon Conference Center.

Auburn Athletic Director David Housel will serve as Master of Ceremonies for the event. The breakfast is open to the public, and tickets are available at the Auburn Chamber of Commerce.

The sponsors for this year’s event are BellSouth, BellSouth Mobility and BellSouth The Real Yellow Pages.

2000 Tiger Trail Inductees

Rosalind Council, 1984-87, Track and Field; Kenny Howard, 1948-80, Assistant Athletic Director; Pat Dye, 1981-92, Head Football Coach; Buddy McClinton; 1966-69, Football; Rex Frederick, 1956-59, Basketball; Willie Smith, 1975-78, Track and Field.
