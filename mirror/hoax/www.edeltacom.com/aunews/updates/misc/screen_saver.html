New Auburn Football Screen Saver Available

August 26, 2000

Sandollar Software launches a full motion video screen saver with an Auburn Football – Beat Bama Edition. See the greatest Auburn Football highlights against Alabama come to life on your computer screen.

Sandollar Software in conjunction with Enova Software has launched the first full motion video screen saver for Auburn football fans. The Beat Bama Edition is packed with over 30 full motion video highlights from 1957 – 1997 victories over Alabama.

See exciting video clips of such memorable plays as Punt Bama Punt, Bo Over the Top, Tillman on the Reverse, Nix for Six and many more. The full motion video screen saver also includes over 15 Windows wallpaper images for stunning backgrounds to your desktop.

The full motion video screen saver is fully licensed by Auburn University. To order, please contact Sandollar Software at 1-800-427-9422 or visit the web site at www.auburnscreensavers.com.
