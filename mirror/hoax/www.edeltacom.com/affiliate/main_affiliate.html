Televised Event Broadcasts

The regional cable television network, College Sports Southeast, will televise each Auburn football game this season on a tape-delay basis. Andy Burcham will handle play-by-play responsibilities while former Tiger Quarterback Stan White will provide color commentary. The network will televise each game on Sundays at noon and Wednesdays at 7 pm CT throughout the season. However, please check your local listings. The network is seen in over 675,000 homes.
