Joe Whitt
Responsibility: Defensive Coordinator/Secondary
Birthdate/Place: Epes, AL (November 1, 1939)
Family: Wife (former Sue Kelly); Children (sons Bill and Brad and daughter Kelly)
College: Alabama, 1962
Playing Experience: Alabama, 1959-61
Coaching Experience: 1993-94, Defensive coordinator, Alabama; 1990-94, Secondary, Alabama; 1986-89, Secondary, Clemson; 1984-85, Defensive coordinator, Memphis Showboats (USFL); 1980-83, Head coach, UT-Chattanooga; 1971-79, Secondary, Alabama; 1966-70; Secondary, Auburn; 1964-65, Head coach, Guntersville (AL) HS; 1963, Assistant coach, R.L Osborne (GA) HS.
Notable: As a player and coach, he has won five national championships (1961-73-78-79-92), 10 SEC championships (1961-71-72-73-74-75-77-78-79-92) and three ACC titles (1986-87-88)... Has coached a combined 23 all-conference selections at Auburn, Clemson, and Alabama... Twice (1977 and 1993) named SEC Working Coach of the Year by the Birmingham Touchdown Club... During his 15-year tenure at Alabama, his defenses recorded 28 shutouts... His 1990 Alabama secondary led the nation in pass efficiency defense... Instrumental in designing the defensive scheme in Alabama's 34-13 win over then-No. 1 Miami in the 1993 Sugar Bowl...
