Advertising Opportunities

Auburn Network On-Line has the ability to integrate marketing and advertising messages into its content. The result is a targeted and effective campaign on the most highly visited Auburn athletic site on the Internet.

The Internet is an exciting and inexpensive way for advertisers to target their message and immediately interact with potential customers. An advertiser's message on aunetwork.com serves as a gateway to further information about their product or service and also provides a link to the company's website. Ads are placed strategically within our editorial content to maximize reach.

 

Banner Advertising

$460 per month

Banner campaigns allow advertisers to increase brand awareness and visibility, while driving traffic to their own sites. The advertiser's banner will be displayed at the top of every page other than the home page (just under the header) and rotate based on a 1:20 ratio. Banner graphics can be static or animated. A four-month minimum applies to all banner advertising campaigns. All advertising is sold on a first-come, first-served basis.

 

Tile Ads

$530 per month

    

Tile advertising campaigns allow advertisers to increase brand awareness and visibility with a slightly smaller ad size than the banner, while driving traffic to their own sites. The advertiser's tile advertisement will be displayed within the column on the left of every page other than the home page and rotate based on a 1:10 ratio. The logo will be visible at all times as long as the viewer is on that page, regardless of whether the user scrolls up or down the sub page for information. A four-month minimum applies to all tile advertising campaigns. All advertising is sold on a first-come, first-served basis.

 

Sponsorships

$180 per month

Sponsorships allow advertisers to generate brand awareness and associate the advertiser's brand with targeted editorial or interactive content. Advertisers can supplement their banner campaign or tile advertising campaign by exclusively sponsoring a specific content area.

For example, a current advertiser on Auburn Network On-Line purchased title sponsorship of the "Standings and Rankings" segment of the site in addition to their tile advertising campaign. Sponsorship advertising ensures the advertiser's logo will be displayed on a static basis (not rotated with other logos) near the top of the sponsored pages. Sponsorships can only be purchased in conjunction with a banner or tile advertising campaign (minimum length of four months). A twelve-month minimum applies to all sponsorship advertising. All advertising is sold on a first-come, first-served basis.
