Tiger Talk Radio Advertising

 

Tiger Talk host Rod Bramblett

On Thursday evenings, Tiger fans know that Auburn Network radio stations are the source for information on Tiger athletics.

Tiger Talk, an hour-long, prime-time call-in/talk show, features in-season appearances with head coaches Tommy Tuberville, Cliff Ellis, Steve Renfroe and other Auburn coaches as well as various other guests and personalities.

The show, hosted by Rod Bramblett, originates from Auburn Network Control and, on select occasions, on location in front of live audiences.

A toll-free number allows callers from around the Southeast to be part of the show.

A lineup of 50 affiliates throughout the state and parts of Georgia and Tennessee provide coverage of the show.

The show airs for 30 consecutive weeks.
