Coaches' Endorsements

 

The Auburn Network handles the commercial endorsements of Head Football Coach Tommy Tuberville, Head Basketball Coach Cliff Ellis and Head Baseball Coach Steve Renfroe, three of the hottest and most successful coaches in the nation.

For selected products and companies, the Auburn Network can utilize Coach Tuberville’s, Coach Ellis’ and Coach Renfroe’s immense popularity and visibility as an official endorser or spokesperson. Each endorsement package is customized for the client. Endorsement services can include radio and television talent; the right to use his likeness in point-of-purchase displays, print advertisements or billboards; and personal appearances on behalf of the company or product.

If your company or group is in need of a top quality speaker who will make your next meeting the most successful one ever, the Auburn Network can arrange for Coach Tuberville, Coach Ellis or Coach Renfroe to be your keynote speaker.
