Jordan-Hare Radio Advertising

 

In addition to delivering the football game broadcast to thousands of listeners across the state, the Auburn Network also makes sure fans at the stadium don’t miss a single play.

Jordan-Hare Radio is a closed-circuit radio system with speakers located at each concession stand, restroom and executive suite throughout the stadium.

The broadcast, along with the network commercials and a number of stadium-only announcements, are delivered to fans throughout the concourses and other non-spectator areas of Jordan-Hare Stadium.
