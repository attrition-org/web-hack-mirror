Production Services

 

Not only can the Auburn Network deliver advertising messages to hundreds of thousands of listeners, it can also deliver a first-class production for you!

The Auburn Network’s brand new, state-of-the-art digital audio studio is equipped to produce any type of audio commercial, feature or video soundtrack you need. In fact, our Orban DSE 7000 Digital Sound Editor can utilize up to eight tracks of audio with a full line of effects to generate production of the highest quality.

Whether it’s a simple or complex production, a spot to run on our statewide network or just a single radio station, the Auburn Network can handle the job at extremely competitive rates.

The Auburn Network’s production studio, through the Alabama Digital Satellite Network (ADSN), can deliver your audio production directly to more than 120 radio stations throughout Alabama and neighboring states. This digital network provides instant CD-quality distribution, thus eliminating the need for multiple dubs, expensive shipping charges and most importantly, the waste of time getting your message on the air.
