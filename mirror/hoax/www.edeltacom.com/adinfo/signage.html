Signage

 

Advertising panels are located on the facia of each side of the stadium. The panels are integrated into interactive applause meters that react to the decibel level of the crowd. These interactive applause meters offer sponsors high visibility throughout the stadium, in addition to providing a focal point for fans during exciting moments of the game. Residual television exposure also results from facia signage. The panels are back-lit, so exposure is not only assured, but intensified during night games.

Concourse video displays with corporate sponsor panels are located on the main concourse level at Jordan-Hare Stadium. Not only are spectators viewing the video monitors to keep up with the action, but they also look at the directional signage incorporated into the displays. The information conveyed by these diplays will demand the attention of every spectator to the sponsor’s corporate message.
