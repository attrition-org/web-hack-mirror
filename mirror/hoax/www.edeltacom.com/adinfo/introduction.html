Advertising Introduction

 

Through broadcast, print, endorsement, Internet and promotional opportunities, the Auburn Network provides a comprehensive, effective and efficient way to reach your market.

The Auburn Network enables your business to take advantage of the enormous following of one of the most successful and visible athletic programs in the entire nation.

Let the Auburn Network design a complete media and marketing plan for your company and put the Auburn Tigers to work for you!

Through its radio, television, Internet and publishing efforts, the Auburn Network delivers Auburn sports programming and information to hundreds of thousands of listeners, viewers and readers. And for advertisers with a statewide or regional interest, the Auburn Network provides one of the most effective and efficient means of reaching consumers.

The Auburn Network’s radio broadcasts are aired by affiliates throughout the state of Alabama as well as parts of Georgia, Florida and Tennessee. Its extremely popular television programs, featuring Auburn coaches, are delivered throughout Alabama and the Southeast as well.

The Auburn Network is also the exclusive publisher and distributor of Auburn’s football, basketball and baseball gameday publications.

Additionally, the Auburn Network produces and distributes Auburn’s football pay-per-view telecasts; Auburn’s sports home video productions; commercial endorsements by Head Football Coach Tommy Tuberville, Head Basketball Coach Cliff Ellis and Head Baseball Coach Steve Renfroe; souvenir and apparel merchandising through direct marketing; an online computer information and entertainment service; and develops various other commercial, promotional and marketing activities involving Auburn athletics.

In the state of Alabama, nothing commands the attention of people like college sports. The Auburn Network is uniquely suited to deliver your company’s message to the large sports audience through its diverse and highly-popular broadcasts, publications and promotional opportunities.
