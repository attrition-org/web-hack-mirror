Grand National


When in Auburn, come play where the pros play. Grand National, part of the Robert Trent Jones Golf Trail, has played host to the Nike Tour Championship and the LPGA Tournament of Champions. Auburn University will also host the NCAA Men's Golf Championships at Grand National in 2000. A public, daily fee golf complex, Grand National has gained world wide acclaim as a major golf destination.
