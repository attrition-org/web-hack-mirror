For 24 years, Carl Stephens has served as the public address announcer for Auburn's home football games at Jordan-Hare Stadium and is regarded as one of the best in the country. In addition to being a familiar voice at Auburn athletic events, Stephens is also famous for playing a role in what has become a famous Auburn phrase.
