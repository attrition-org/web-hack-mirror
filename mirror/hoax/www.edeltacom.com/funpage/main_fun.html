Tiger Kids Club

The Auburn Tiger Kids Club is a junior booster organization for youth 8th grade and under. Benefits include free admission to select athletic events, an official Kids Club t-shirt, the Kids Club newsletter, your own Kids Club membership card with special discounts, and pizza parties and autograph sessions with Auburn teams.
