Mike Perniciaro

Assistant Coach
 

Mike Perniciaro was named to the Auburn staff in July, 1998. Perniciaro, 29, came to the Plains after a two-year stint with the University of Georgia softball program. While at Georgia, Perniciaro served as a hitting coach and also worked with the outfielders.

At Auburn, Perniciaro will assist with hitting and fielding responsibilities in addition to handling recruiting duties and day-to-day operations.

Prior to Georgia, Perniciaro was the assistant softball coach at Centenary College from 1995-96. At Centenary, he also served as assistant athletic director for marketing and promotions.

The St. Louis, Missouri native, played baseball for two seasons at Meramec Junior College before finishing his playing career and earning a bachelor’s degree in Excercise Physiology at the University of Missouri-St. Louis in 1993. While earning his master’s degree in Sport Administration, “Pooch” began his coaching career by serving as a graduate assistant baseball coach at Western Illinois University in 1994.
