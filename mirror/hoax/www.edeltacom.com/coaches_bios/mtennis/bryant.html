Don Bryant

Assistant Coach
 

Just two years after graduating from Auburn with a degree in accounting, Don Bryant has returned to coach the team he once played for.

A native of St. Petersberg, Fla., Bryant has spent the past two years playing in European tournaments and coaching at tennis clubs throughout Germany.

During his playing days for the Tigers, Bryant amassed a record of 17-11 in singles and 22-24 in doubles.

A few of Bryant's career highlights include reaching the semifinals in doubles at the SEC Indoor Championships in 1995, advancing to the quarterfinals of the Georgia Tech Invitational in singles and being named to the SEC Academic Honor Roll twice.

Prior to Auburn, Bryant attended Cocoa Beach High School in Florida where he earned all-conference honors in the Space Coast Conference and was ranked among the top 20 juniors in Florida.

In addition to playing tennis, Bryant also enjoys running, lifting weights and hiking.
