Matthew Mott

Assistant Coach
 

Matthew Mott enters his first year as assistant coach at Auburn after serving as head coach Karen Richter’s assistant at Central Florida for four years. Mott will be main responsibilities include serving as the recruiting coordinator and assisting Richter with all on-field coaching duties.

During his tenure at Central Florida, Mott assisted the Golden Knights to a 43-32-7 mark, three Trans Atlantic Athletic Conference titles and a berth in the NCAA Tournament last season.

Prior to his stint at UCF, Mott was head coach for four years at Hilbert College, a Division III institution in Hamburg, N.Y. In his stay at Hilbert, Mott became the school’s all-time winningest coach and set a school record for most victories in a season.

Mott is a 1999 graduate of Central Florida with a bachelor’s degree in general studies with a minor in criminal justice. He also earned an associate of science degree in 1994 from Erie Community College in Buffalo, N.Y. After graduating from Norwood Norfolk Central High School in Norwood, N.Y., in 1989, Mott spent four years in the U.S. Coast Guard, stationed in Portsmouth, Va., Petaluma, Calif., and Buffalo, N.Y.

With 11 years of coaching experience, Mott has developed a great deal of experience at numerous levels of coaching. Mott, who holds a National “C” Coaching License from the United States Soccer Federation, began his coaching while in the Coast Guard, where he assisted its select team in Portsmouth, Va. He was also an assistant coach at Churchland High School in Churchland, Va., in 1990.

Mott also has experience working with Olympic Development Programs. In 1992, he coached an under-14 boy’s select Olympic Development Program team in Orchard Park, N.Y., and in 1998-99 worked with the girls ODP in Florida as a member of the Florida Youth Soccer Association.

As a player Mott was a goalkeeper on the Norwood Norfolk Central High School team and also on the Coast Guard select team for two years.

Born in Norwood, N.Y., Mott is 29 and was married to the former Jennifer Swartz in April of 1997. Mott's wife Jennifer is currently employed by HealthSouth as a physical therapist.
