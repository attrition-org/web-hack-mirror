The Dr. Laura Schlessinger Show

A phenomenon in the world of national radio, Dr. Laura occupies a unique niche wide enough to accomodate 18 million listeners. Contradicting conventional wisdom that syndicated talk radio was a man's game, Dr. Laura's one-of-a-kind wit and wisdom swept the country in record time. In typical "break-through" fashion, Dr. Laura has revolutionized syndicated talk radio, bypassing the tried and true topics of politics, sports and relationships, to establish a lively and provocative daily forum for the discussion of morals, ethics and values in everyday behavior and circumstances.

Morals, ethics, and values... Dr. Laura Schlessinger helps millions steer a path through difficult life decisions by insisting that they do the right thing. In an "anything goes" World, Dr. Laura's message of personal responsibility and accountability has become the rallying point for millions of listeners.
