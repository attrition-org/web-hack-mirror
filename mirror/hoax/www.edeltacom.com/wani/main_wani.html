Auburn Network, Inc., owns and operates a commercial radio station in the Auburn/Opelika market. WANI 1400 AM is Auburn and Opelika's only NewsTalk and Information radio station.

The station is affiliated with NBC Radio News, CNBC Business News and ESPN Radio and is Auburn/Opelika's exclusive home for The Rush Limbaugh Show, Neal Boortz, Dr. Laura Schlessinger and other top quality talk radio programming.

The WANI NewsTalk 1400 studios are located within the Auburn Network's headquarters at 197 East University Drive in Auburn.
