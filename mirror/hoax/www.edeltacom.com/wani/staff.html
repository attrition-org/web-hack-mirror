WANI Staff
 

Mike Hubbard, President
Mail: hubbard@aunetwork.com

Mike Hubbard serves as President of Auburn Network, Inc. A 1983 broadcast journalism graduate of the University of Georgia, Hubbard joined the Auburn Athletic Department staff in January 1984 and served six years as Associate Sports Information Director prior to joining the network.

In November 1998, Hubbard was elected to the Alabama House of Representatives. Rep. Hubbard, a Republican, represents District 79 which includes portions of Auburn and Opelika.

Hubbard's wife, Dr. Susan Hubbard, is a faculty member in Auburn University's School of Human Sciences. They have one son, Clayte, born August 29, 1995.

Chad Dorsett, News Director
Mail: dorsett@aunetwork.com

As News Director for WANI, Chad Dorsett keeps Auburn and Opelika informed on local, state, and national news. The Stone Mountain, GA, native served as Assistant News Director for Root Communications prior to joining the Auburn Network staff in 1999. He also provides production assistance for numerous Auburn Network broadcasts. Dorsett is a 1997 Auburn University graduate with a degree in Mass Communications.

Steve Witten, Sales Manager
Mail: witten@aunetwork.com

As Sales Manager, Witten oversees advertising sales and day-to-day operation of WANI. Witten earned his bachelor's degree from Clemson University and a master's degree from Ohio University. His wife, Beth, is a customer service manager for McClendon Trucking in LaFayette, Alabama.

Meg Wilson, Account Executive
Mail: wilson@aunetwork.com

As account executive, Meg Wilson handles all advertising sales for WANI. Originally from Gadsden, Wilson has been in radio sales for four years. From 1995-99 Wilson served as account executive for Gadsden's WQEN, home of "The Rick and Bubba Show". Wilson moved from Gadsden to Birmingham with "The Rick and Bubba Show" before joining the Auburn Network in 1999.

Rod Bramblett, Morning Show Host
Mail: bramblet@aunetwork.com

Bramblett hosts WANI's weekday morning show, Auburn/Opelika This Morning. Bramblett is a familiar voice to WANI listeners as he serves as the lead play-by-play announcer for all Auburn Tiger baseball radio broadcasts and studio host for Auburn men's basketball broadcasts. A 1988 Auburn graduate, Bramblett grew up in Valley, AL. His wife, Paula, is a computer programmer/analyst in Auburn University's Division of Computing. They have one daughter, Shelby.

Patrick Tisdale, Engineer
Mail: tisdale@aunetwork.com

Patrick Tisdale serves as head engineer for WANI. His responsibilities include maintenance and technical development of WANI equipment. Tisdale also serves as traffic manager. Originally from Opp, AL, Tisdale is currently pursuing a degree from Auburn University in physics.
