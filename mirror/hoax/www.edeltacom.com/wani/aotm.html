Auburn/Opelika This Morning is the most informative radio show in the area. The show, hosted by Rod Bramblett, airs from 6:00-9:00 a.m. each weekday and includes news, sports, weather, local event coverage, special features, and live guests. Regular guests include Auburn Mayor Bill Ham, Opelika Mayor Barbara Patton, Lee County Sheriff Jay Jones, Rep. Mike Hubbard, Congressman Bob Riley, and representatives from local school systems, Chambers of Commerce, and Parks and Recreation Departments. Local residents know to tune to WANI NewsTalk 1400 to hear Auburn/Opelika’s movers, shakers, and decision makers on Auburn/Opelika This Morning.
Chad Dorsett is the only full-time news director in the Auburn/Opelika radio market. As head of the WANI NewsTalk 1400 news team, Chad provides listeners with ten updated local newscasts each day. Headlines can be heard following national news at the top of each hour and a full local newscast is delivered on the half hour..
