The Phone Losers of America have been annoying people on the phone for over five years now. They have put out two successful CDs with phone calls originating from all over the United States. They don't really consider themselves a "group", just a few friends who make prank calls, sometimes bringing in the help of other friends via 3-way calling.

PLA's first CD, Give Me Liberty or Give Me Steak, is 60 full minutes of hilarious prank calls which was originally released in 1994. You'll hear us call Mildred Monday, torment Radio Shack, pester Deaf Relay Service operators and much much more. You can see a listing of all the calls on this tape by clicking here or you can listen to sound clips by visiting our sound page.



The second CD - Operator Leaving Line - is an excellent sequel to the first, with many of the favorites returning for even more fun. Mildred Monday is back, bitchin' at us about her roof and siding, John Craven's wife gets in on the act, telling us a thing or two and Alex Carbon still insists he has nothing to do with AT&T. Besides that you'll hear plenty of new material along with two prank call dance mixes. You can listen to some sound clips by clicking here.
