Below are the archives of PLA activity at the Boulder News Forum. Each one is very extensive and boring as hell to read but if it's raining outside and you have absolutely nothing better to do then you'll find them mildly entertaining.

Back in 1997 the Boulder News Forum opened a discussion room dedicated to the murder of JonBenet Ramsey. Everyone's favorite old woman, Tannest, began posting silly messages there and Logic Box soon joined in which brought in a few of the old dal.net #rock people. A few weeks later RBCP joined in which brought in the help of some of the regular users on PLA's old discussion board. One thing lead to another and now it's been two years of this senselessness. Read on for fun!
