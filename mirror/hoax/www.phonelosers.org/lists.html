The Phone Losers of America currently offers two different types of lists. The first one is a discussion list in which you can participate in an ongoing conversation about phones, computers and just about anything else of interest. The other is an update list which sends you an e-mail every month or two with the latest news & updates with anything PLAish. Click the icon of your choice:

PLA Discussion List The discussion list currently has about 200 subscribers and gets anywhere from 20 to 100 posts per day. Click here to view the archives of this list to get an idea what to expect.
PLA Announcement List This is will usually get you no more than one update per month if even that. Mainly you'll receive updates about the phonelosers.org site, scheduled outages or problems, ideas, contests, input, information, etc etc etc. Click here to view the archives of this list to get an idea what to expect.
When subscribing to either of the lists you'll be asked a few questions and given a password in case you need to change, update or delete your account.
