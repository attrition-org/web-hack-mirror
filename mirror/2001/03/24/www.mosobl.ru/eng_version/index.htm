INFORMATION ABOUT THE NETWORK

Welcome to our site

"INFORMATION EXCHANGE NETWORK
FOR SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES
IN THE MOSCOW REGION"

This Network has been set up in order to develop entrepreneurship, and to provide information to entrepreneurs who are setting up or who want to start their own business.

Using modern information technology we can provide practical help to small businesses in the Moscow Region, and develop a unique business information resource in the region.

  Subscribers to the Network receive:

  • - access to information resources, distributed through the Network;
  • - access to the global Internet;
  • - an enquiry service;
  • - electronic mail and fax connections.

As a subscriber you can broadcast your business proposals, find new business partners, and make a presentation of your firm's goods and services.

We have high class and reliable information and databases, thanks to the active participation of small business support organisations, local centres in our Network in towns around the region and several specialised and industry-specific information centres.

Through our Network you will always find the information you need, obtain essential advice and pick up the latest business news.

Become a subscriber to our network, publicise your own information, and improve your entrepreneurial skills.

Do please share your opinions in our "Comments Book".

INFORMATION EXCHANGE NETWORK FOR SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISE IN THE MOSCOW REGION has been set up within the framework of regional and federal programmes for support and development of enterprise.

The objectives of the Network are to:

  • integrate Russian small and medium enterprise into the world information network;
  • create a corporate information resource for users of the Network;
  • facilitate access for small and medium enterprises and other members of the Network to a wide range of Russian and foreign information resources;
  • enable the exchange of information between members of the Network;
  • offer new services to SMEs using modern information technology;
  • advertise the Network's services in Russia and abroad;
  • provide statistics on the development of the SME sector.

The main users of the Network are small and medium enterprises in the Moscow region, for whom we provide a wide range of business information.

An important feature provided by the system is the opportunity for small and medium businesses to advertise electronically, and to offer their goods and services inside Russia and abroad. We plan to integrate commercial offers from Russian firms with Russian and world business information exchanges supported by SIORAnet, Global Trade Point Network, the UN, the EU and Tacis.

The Network has adopted software and standards used by Microsoft and the Internet. The central hub of the Network is run by Moscow Region Agency for Support of Small and Medium Enterprise.

As a first stage we plan to create 10 - 15 local centres during 1999. Cooperation between the local centres and the systems manager is the responsibility of the chief systems designer.

The information is collected from three sources: small and medium enterprise support organisations, companies, and other sources.

Access to these information resources is partly free of charge, partly on a chargeable basis.
