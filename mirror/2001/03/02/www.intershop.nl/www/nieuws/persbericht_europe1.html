1eEurope Forges Strategic Partnership with Intershop Communications
Alliance Strengthens 1eEurope's Proven Ability to Deliver Customers Leading-Edge E-Business Solutions

NEW YORK, OCTOBER 30, 2000 - 1eEurope Ltd., one of Europe's fastest growing and leading pan-European providers of e-business solutions to companies and government organizations across the Continent, today announced a strategic partnership with Intershop Communications that significantly broadens 1eEurope's ability to deliver custom e-business solutions to its business customers. The partnership, announced at Intershop's annual e-commerce summit and user conference, "Intershop Open" in New York, allows 1eEurope to configure customized solutions built on Intershop's full suite of e-business applications and software.

Intershop products and services now available to 1eEurope's integrated network of solution providers include INTERSHOP ENFINITY™, a Web-based platform enabling e-commerce through a variety of business models, and INTERSHOP 4™, a suite of software tools enabling users to develop an online e-commerce presence. The proven technology yields significant time-to-market and quality-to-market advantages, and provides a scalable platform that can support growing e-business enterprises. More than 3,000 customers around the world use Intershop technology to run their e-business operations.

"With Intershop Communications, 1eEurope has enhanced the power of its "best of breed" portfolio of products and services, tools we can use to help European businesses accelerate their adoption of e-business solutions and capitalize on e-business opportunities," said Rick Kozak, Chairman and CEO of 1eEurope. "Our pan-European presence gives us direct access to Europe's hottest e-commerce markets, and our partnership with Intershop assures our customers that we have the technology, skills and know-how to deliver customized solutions that will meet their e-business needs today and in the future."

About Intershop Communications
Intershop Communications is the world's fastest growing provider of e-commerce applications software and solutions for the enterprise. Intershop's software offers the quickest time-to-market today with the greatest flexibility for tomorrow. In addition, Intershop e-commerce solutions enable customers to "Sell Anywhere" by supporting the widest range of current and rapidly emerging e-business business models.

About 1eEurope

The company is uniquely positioned to help medium to large sized organizations and government agencies achieve their e-business objectives, enabling them to capitalize on Internet technology, productively and profitably. 1eEurope's range of services include enterprise application integration, e-business consulting, e-publishing and content management, e-procurement and e-commerce, e-marketplaces and exchanges, pervasive computing, and eCRM. 1eEurope, with headquarters in London, operates on a across Europe with companies in France, Denmark, Belgium, the UK, Germany, Spain, Sweden, Switzerland and the Netherlands.
