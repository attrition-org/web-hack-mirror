A Message from Dean Fincham:

Welcome to the University of Kansas School of Pharmacy Home page!  I am delighted you are visiting us.  The U.S. healthcare system is changing rapidly, and Pharmacy is evolving to meet the challenges that currently exist and those that are on the horizon.  KU is a terrific place to learn about many things including pharmacy.  We here in the School of Pharmacy are striving to make sure we are poised to enter the new arenas of health care delivery practice and research. If we can provide you with more information about our School, please e-mail me at: pharmacy@ukans.edu.  Thank you once again for visiting our home page.
