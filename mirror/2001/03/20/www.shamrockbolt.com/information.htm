Shamrock Bolt & Screw Co. Inc.located in Fort Smith, AR was established in 1967 as a fastener distributor. The company has since grown to 5 locations in Springdale,AR, Russellville, AR, Joplin, MO, and Springfield, MO.

The dynamic world of fasteners requires constant focus on new products as well as applications of product. Shamrock Bolt has prided itself in that we are more than a full line distributor and can assist in design and application of any threaded fastener component for industrial, construction, MRO and OEM.

Shamrock Bolt & Screw Co. Inc.currently stocks over 21,000 SKU's in our warehouse and can accommodate any quantity needed and can tailor delivery needs for inventory control systems.

Product is shipped daily on freight carrier, UPS or local delivery is available for local customers.

Shamrock Bolt & Screw Co. Inc. is a family owned corporation which strives to provide quality products and service.

We look forward to serving you.

Mike Copher, President.
