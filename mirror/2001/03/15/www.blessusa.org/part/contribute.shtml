Contribution Methods 

On-Line Credit Card or Checking Contribution 4 Contribution by mail 4 United Way Program  4 Matching Gift Program       4 Non-monetary (in-kind) Contribution 

1. On-Line Credit Card Contribution
        Contribute on-line through a credit card. You can contribute one time, or schedule recurring monthly contributions.

        Your contribution is tax deductible and you will receive a statement of your contribution(s) in February of the following year.

2. On-line Checking/Savings Account Contribution
        You can contribute one time  through your checking/savings account, or 

        You can schedule recurring monthly contributions.  If you would like to contribute the same amount monthly, automatic direct withdrawal from your checking or savings account is available if you choose this easy, check-free method. You can schedule monthly contributions on-line or by calling BLESS USA at (973) 857-4299.

        Your contribution is tax deductible and you will receive a statement of your contribution(s) in February of the following year.

3. Contribution by mail

    You can send your tax deductible contribution payable to BLESS USA and send it to:

      BLESS USA
      PO Box 384
      Cedar Grove, NJ 07009

4.  United Way Program

       Many employers deduct contributions from payroll and send it to the United Way.  The United Way supports hundreds of human service agencies, like BLESS USA.  You can choose to direct your United Way contribution at your workplace to BLESS USA.   Even if BLESS USA is not listed  as a member agency in their directory, United Way will still send your contribution to BLESS USA, since we are a nonprofit organization, tax-exempt under 501 (c) (3).  This excellent opportunity directs your workplace contribution to the less fortunate in Egypt.

        Simply indicate on your campaign designation form that you wish to designate a specific agency.  If you do not find BLESS USA listed as a member agency in the directory, write BLESS USA as the name of the agency,  and provide the contact information (PO Box 384, Cedar Grove, NJ  07009, (973) 857-4299).   

        If you have contributed $250 or more, you will receive a tax acknowledgment letter from United Way at the end of January.  Contributions less than $250, and made through payroll deductions, are reflected on the December 31st paycheck statement.  This statement substantiates the year's United Way donations in accordance with IRS regulations.  

5. Matching Gifts Program: 2 Gifts Are Better Than 1!

        Did you know that every contribution you make to BLESS USA might be matched exactly by your employer?

        If you work for one of the companies that participate in the Matching Gift Program, your employer may match your contribution (gift) dollar for dollar or more.  Please take a minute to search our list to see if your employer participates in this program.

6. Non-monetary (in-kind) Contributions

        BLESS USA volunteer servants rely heavily on modern technology, to properly and efficiently operate the organization.  

        You can help BLESS USA further its efforts with a non-monetary contribution by asking your company if they donate any items to charity non-profit organizations.   Items such as computer hardware and software greatly aid us in performing our daily tasks, facilitate our responsibilities and decrease overhead costs.  

        These donations would be tax-deductible. For more information, please contact us.
