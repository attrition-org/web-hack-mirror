How You Can Help

Prayer  4 Tell A Friend 4 Contribute 4 Membership  Volunteer as a servant 4 Christian Education

1. Prayer

    The Holy Bible reminds us: In everything by prayer and supplication, with thanksgiving, let your requests be made known to God, (Php 4:6).  Our Lord also reminds us: Without Me you can do nothing, (John 15:5).  Thus the most important way to help our brothers & sisters who are in need is to pray for their salvation and their sustenance.  Please also pray for the servants so that God may guide our thoughts and actions.  

    We have a prayer meeting every third Monday of the month from 8-9 PM (EST)... please join us in unity of prayer from wherever you are. We also have a yearly fast September 27, 28 and 29th on behalf of the service.

2. Tell A Friend

    One of the best ways to assist BLESS USA in helping our less fortunate Coptic brothers and sisters in Egypt is by Telling a Friend about our activities. The more people know, the more our brethren in need are helped.

    You don't have to write anything, just click on the link Tell a Friend and check out this page.

3. Contribute

    We have facilitated the process for people to contribute and share in the blessings of serving the Brethren of the Lord (people in need). As our Lord said: It is more blessed to give than to receive, (Acts 20:35). There are many easy ways one can contribute to their brothers & sisters.

    Your contribution is tax deductible and you will receive a statement of your contribution(s) in February of the following year.

4. Membership

    Simply fill out this application online in order to participate in any of the BLESS USA projects, or to be added to our mailing list and receive our newsletters.

5. Volunteer as a servant

    If you would like to share in the blessings of serving those in need, you can visit the BLESS USA service page or e-mail us at: serving@blessusa.org for more info!

6. Christian Education

    We wanted to spread the Bible’s message, get churches actively involved in serving the Brethren of the Lord and also create an opportunity for the youth and children to serve. Thus we devised Sunday School lesson plans & youth curricula.

    The Sunday School lesson plans, designed for different age groups, aim to teach our children the importance of various Christian virtues. We are in the process of putting this material online, so please visit this page soon.
