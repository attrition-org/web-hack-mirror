    BLESS USA is an official nonprofit charity organization of the Coptic Orthodox Church under the Bishopric of Public, Ecumenical & Social Services. Its mission is to raise the standard and quality of living for needy and disadvantaged Copts residing in Egypt, and to aid in alleviating their suffering.
