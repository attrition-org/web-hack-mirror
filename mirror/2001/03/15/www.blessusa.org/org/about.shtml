About BLESS USA

 

BLESS is an abbreviation for the BISHOPRIC OF PUBLIC, ECUMENICAL AND SOCIAL SERVICES. BLESS USA is a nonprofit charity organization of the Coptic Orthodox Church. It extends monetary aid from America to the needy of Egypt, through BLESS in Egypt. BLESS USA is located at the Coptic Orthodox Church Archdiocese of North America in Cedar Grove, New Jersey. BLESS USA is under the direct supervision and care of His Holiness Pope Shenouda III, in conjunction with H.G. Bishop Youannes of BLESS in Egypt and H.G. Bishop David at the Archdiocese of North America.

BLESS in Egypt investigates and verifies every case for aid it receives. Then His Holiness Pope Shenouda III personally meets many of these needy people every Thursday in Cairo, and every other Saturday in Alexandria.
