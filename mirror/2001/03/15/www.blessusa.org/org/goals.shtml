Mission Goals

    Extend love and compassion to our beloved brothers and sisters in Egypt.
    Promote the physical and emotional well being of disadvantaged Copts, through spiritual and monetary support.

    Raise the level of awareness about the condition of the needy in Egypt.

    Increase service to the needy Coptic community throughout Egypt by advancing the current activities of BLESS USA, as well as developing new means of relief.

    Promote awareness about the activities of BLESS USA. 

     

    Remember... One of the greatest ways to help 

    BLESS USA reach its goals is to Tell A Friend.
