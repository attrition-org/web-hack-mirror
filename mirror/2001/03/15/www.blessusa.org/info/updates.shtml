Now Offering On-Line Contribution*

BLESS USA is pleased to offer the following online services to contributors:

1. Credit card contributions - You can contribute one time or schedule monthly automatic contributions, if you would like to contribute the same amount every month.

2. Checking/savings account contributions - With this service, you can also contribute one time or schedule monthly automatic contributions from your checking/savings account. 

No more writing checks!

No more forgetting to contribute monthly!

 

*This online method of contributing to the needy in Egypt is conducted on a secure server.  BLESS USA highly values and respects the security and privacy of personal data.  Thus we utilize the Internet's strongest security technologies, including Netscape's SSL (Secure Socket Layer) and triple DES solution.   This security system keeps contributors' personal information secure by encrypting information so they cannot be seen or copied.  The secure environment is indicated on the bottom of the browser with the display of a lock or key on the status bar. The display of the secure icon indicates that encryption has been activated.  Therefore transactions are protected by the highest levels of security available to ensure security and privacy for anyone who wants to conduct transactions on the Internet.

Announcing the New Matching Gift Program

BLESS USA is excited to offer the Matching Gift Program. It is a program where your employer matches your contributions to BLESS USA. This is an easy way to double your contribution to the needy in Egypt. You can search on-line for your company to see if it participates, or you can contact BLESS USA to obtain a Matching Gift brochure.

Who is eligible for Matching Gift Program?

The following individuals may be eligible (please check with your employer):

  • Employees (full-time & part time)
  • Retired employees
  • Spouses of eligible employees
  • Board of Directors

What type of organization is BLESS USA?

BLESS USA is a nonprofit organization that qualifies under the following categories:

  • Charitable Organization
  • Religious Organization
  • Cultural Organization
  • Human Service/Social Service Organization
