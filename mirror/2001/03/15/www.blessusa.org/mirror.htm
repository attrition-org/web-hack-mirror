Sunday School Lesson Plans

  • We devised Sunday School lesson plans because we wanted to spread the Bible’s message concerning Christian living, aid the churches in becoming actively involved in serving the Brethren of the Lord (people in need) and also create an opportunity for the youth and children to serve.

United Way Program

  • Many employers deduct contributions from payroll and send it to the United Way.  The United Way supports hundreds of human service agencies, like BLESS USA.  You can choose to direct your United Way contribution at your workplace to BLESS USA.  

Announcing The Matching Gift Program

  • BLESS USA is excited to offer the Matching Gift Program. It is a program where your employer matches your contributions to BLESS USA. This is an easy way to double your contribution to people in need in Egypt. 

Now offering online contributions

  • BLESS USA is pleased to offer the following online credit card and check/savings account  contributions. You can contribute one time or schedule monthly automatic contributions, if you would like to contribute the same amount every month.
