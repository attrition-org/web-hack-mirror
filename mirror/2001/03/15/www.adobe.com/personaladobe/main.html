Edit Your Account
Use this feature to update any of your account information, such as billing and shipping addresses, phone and fax numbers, and e-mail.

You may change your password at any time or add a hint in case you forget it.

Check the status of your orders (items ordered, access purchased downloads, total cost, order date, shipping waybill number).

By selecting this option, you will delete any items in your Shopping Cart and return to the AdobeStore home page.
