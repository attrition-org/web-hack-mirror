Creative professionals are under constant pressure to deliver rich, exciting content, but design firms, freelancers, and smaller in-house teams don't always have the resources to create complex intranets or hassle with full-blown project management systems. But what if you could choose only the parts or services that you needed? And what if you could do it all online?

That's why Adobe is creating the Adobe® Studio™ Web site, the premier creative design network for Web and print professionals.

In addition to free access to an award-winning mix of tips, galleries, and online resources, Adobe Studio includes a set of subscription services that offer a new way to make the Web work for you. When you log-in to Work @ Adobe Studio, you create a virtual workspace where you can recruit and manage team members, track multiple projects, collaborate online, and share and deliver files in a secure environment that's tightly integrated with Adobe's popular publishing tools — all without additional IT or infrastructure investments.

At Adobe Studio, you'll also find cutting-edge tools that keep you competitive and an online community as passionate about design as you are. Promote your own work to a large audience of potential clients, and discover inspirational and instructional content that you can really use. From forums for sharing tips and techniques to online design critiques where participants can get real-time feedback from industry luminaries, Adobe Studio is all about what matters most — celebrating fresh ideas and unbridled thinking.
