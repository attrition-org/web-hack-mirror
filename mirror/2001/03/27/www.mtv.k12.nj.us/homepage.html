Mission Statement

The mission of the Hanover Township School District is to offer an Educational program capable of providing for the academic, social and emotional needs of all students.

About Our District

The Hanover Township School District is an elementary school district for students in kindergarten through eight grade. Additionally, the district does provide a special education program for students with special needs. This program is also designed for those students in need of early intervention. Students in grades nine through twelve form part of the Whippany Park School District.

The Hanover Township School District is comprised of Bee Meadow Elementary School (K-5 and special education), Mountview Road School (K-5), Salem Drive School (K-5) and Memorial Junior School (6-8). Bee Meadow and Salem Drive elementary schools are located in Whippany, while Mountview Road school is located in Cedar Knolls. Our middle school, Memorial Junior School is also located in Whippany.
