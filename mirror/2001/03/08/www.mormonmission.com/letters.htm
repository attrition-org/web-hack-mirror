Dear Mom and Dad,

I want to tell you how much I love you. I am about to come home from my mission to Spain and want to let you know how appreciative I am for all that you have done for me. I could have never done this without you. I am so sorry that I did not always listen to you when I should have. I love you. You have taught me so many great things in life. I thank you for all that you have given me. I want to let you know that I have a strong testimony of this church. I believe in God with all my heart and know that he has sent his son here to help us return. I know the Book of Mormon to be true. I will miss the people of Spain. I love them with all my heart and they are part of my family. I will come home soon and am ready to start my own family. I love you and look forward to coming home,

Elder Smith
