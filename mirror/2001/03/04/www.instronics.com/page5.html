Instronics maintains a number of Dealers, Representatives and System Integrators which market a number of Instronics' products into their areas of expertise. This configuration helps Instronics provide field sales support into most major industrial and commercial centres across Canada. Instronics has retained all of the current suppliers under formal distribution agreements to provide stability to end-users. Together with the manufacturers, Instronics will continue to supply and support leading-edge products and services to meet the current and future needs of the end-user.

Maintenance of all products sold by Instronics is handled out of our Pickering office and with the total back-up from our suppliers, combined with two Specialized Third Party Maintenance Organizations provide uninterrupted support to the end-users throughout Canada.
