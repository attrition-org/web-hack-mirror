End User Support
ASUS sells all of its products through major distributors, resellers and system integrators across the world. All of our products have met the highest quality and compatibility standards in the industry. If you need technical support, please contact your reseller. Otherwise, you can also visit our website at http://www.asus.com.tw for more technical reference solutions. We appreciate all feedbacks from our customers, and in return we would like to provide you with the best service possible.

How to Get Support
In order to have your technical problems solved, we prompt you to use the following methods to receive the most efficient and precise solution regarding your concern:

  1. Ask your reseller. The people who integrated and sold the system components to you. They should know best about your system configuration and the problem you are experiencing. How they serve you is also a good reference for your next purchasing.
  2. Check ASUS Technical Reference Guide. There are many technical documents and FAQs for your reference. This is the area we would like to improve the most so that you can have access to the most frequently asked questions.
  3. Check the ASUS E-Magazine. You can easily obtain the latest technical documentation, market trends, and how-to-buy information.
  4. Check the Index of archive files (HTML format). It is very likely that someone has asked the same question before.
  5. Post your question to ASUS NetQ Online Forum . We may not be able to answer all the questions posted on this newsgroup. However, we will try our best to answer questions that are common or may affect majority of the users.
  6. Newsgroups. These Newsgroups are for the purposes of sharing information on technical-oriented materials. Any articles posted not in this regard will be removed immediately without notifying the original issuer. For any non-technical information that you would like to share with others, you can try posting them onto news://news.asus.com.tw/alt.comp.periphs.mainboard.asus.
  7. Send via E-Mail. If you have any personal technical issue, you can send e-mail to ASUS technical support department. Due to the large number of daily e-mails and various reasons such as the time required for problem reproduction, we may not have the chance to reply every incoming e-mail. Please understand that we are selling through distribution channels and do not have sufficient resources to serve every possible end-users.
