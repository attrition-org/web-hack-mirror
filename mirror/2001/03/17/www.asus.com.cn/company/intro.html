ASUSTeK COMPUTER INC. (ASUS) was established on April 1st, 1989. Since then ASUS has devoted in provide Leading Edge Technology and Best Value Solutions at highest quality to all our customers. In both 1996 and 1997, we have been ranked No.1 in business performance among the top 500 manufacturers in Taiwan. In 1998, ASUS continues to rank No.1 in the Far East and No.18 Worldwide for "The World's Best-Performance Information Technology Companies" as shown in Business Week, Nov. 1998.

In 1999, ASUS's Annual Gross Sales Revenue has accumulated to more than 1,500 Million in US Dollars and is still growing rapidly. The monthly motherboard and notebook sales also continues to peak over 1,000,000 mainboards, and 20,000 notebooks in volume quantity. Currently, ASUS is capable of producing well over 100,000 notebook PCs monthly if the orders are met. Every type of product made by ASUS has been certified and complimented by global distributors and system integrators worldwide.

ASUS's strong R&D team is able to provide advanced products a step further than our competitors to assure more competitivity and flexibility in the business. All ASUS mainboards produced today support Pentium®III, AMD K6-3, Pentium®II, AMD K6-2, Pentium® Pro, and Pentium® based CPU in AGP, PCI, and ISA slot versions. ASUS also offers other additional peripherals such as CD-ROM drives, notebook computers, servers, SCSI cards, audio/video cards, and related network cards for corporate management level needs.

ASUS has been recognized by customers worldwide for consistently delivering products of highest quality and reliability. This is the reason why leading European, American, and Asian system integrators and OEM customers choose ASUS as their strategic partner.

ASUS is committed to quality and has implemented a Total Quality Program. For our design phase, our R&D department goes through an intense design review and simulation process. For our manufacturing phase, advanced SMT and Auto Test equipments are being used. All ASUS products go through rigorous tests in our production line, and dynamic aging in our burn-in room. Our quality control program includes: Incoming Quality Control, In Process Quality Control, Reliability Testing, and Environment Testing. In keeping and fulfilling the social responsibilities of an enterprise, an attempt is required to minimize any likely impacts that the plant's production activities may have towards the environment. Thus, ASUS has setup an Environmental and Occupational Health & Safety Policies to protect our living environment.
