The Bureau for Private Postsecondary and Vocational Education, California State Department of Education, has granted Pacific States University full institutional approval as a California degree - granting institution. PSU offers undergraduate and graduate programs leading to the following degrees:

  • COLLEGE OF BUSINESS

    • BACHELOR OF BUSINESS ADMINISTRATION (B.B.A.)

      B.B.A. in Accounting
      B.B.A. in Computers in Business
      B.B.A. in Marketing

    • MASTER OF BUSINESS ADMINISTRATION (M.B.A.)

      M.B.A. Concentration in Accounting
      M.B.A. Concentration in International Business
      M.B.A. Concentration in Finance
      M.B.A. Concentration in Management of Information Technology
      M.B.A. Concentration in Real Estate Management

  • COLLEGE OF COMPUTER SCIENCE & INFORMATION SYSTEMS

    • BACHELOR OF SCIENCE COMPUTER SCIENCE (B.S.)

      B.S. Concentration in Computer Science and Information Systems

    • MASTER OF SCIENCE COMPUTER SCIENCE (M.S.)

      M.S. Concentration in Computer Science
      M.S. Concentration in Information Systems

  • NON-DEGREE PROGRAMS

    • ENGLISH AS A SECOND LANGAUGE PROGRAM(ESL)
