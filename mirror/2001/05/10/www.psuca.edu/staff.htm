PSU has an excellent full-time and adjunct faculty. Its members average 20 years of teaching in higher education and extensive employment experience in their fields of specialization.

More than 95 percent of PSU's faculty members hold doctorates from such institutions as Columbia University, the University of Southern California, The University of California system, Pepperdine University, and Loyola Law School of Los Angeles. Some PSU professors also hold advanced degrees from international schools such as the London School of Economics, McGill University in Canada, the University of Basel in Switzerland, and Kon-Kuk University in Seoul, Korea.
