Research Institute Center for Asia Pacific Studies (CAPS)

The Center for Asia Pacific Studies (CAPS) is an international center for training and research related to the Asia Pacific community. Its establishment, a joint project of PSU and Kon-Kuk University in Seoul, Korea, benefits the business community and law and policy makers, and furthers the internationalization of higher education.

Plans are under way for CAPS to develop additional faculty and student exchanges between Kon-Kuk University and PSU, including the granting of joint degrees by the two institutions.
