PSU'S OBJECTIVES

Pacific States University has 3 broad objectives designed to support its professional mission based on the development of critical thinking and the imparting of values to foster life-long learning.

  • Globalization of Education. PSU educates and trains not only American students but students from all over the world and aspires to provide them with the attitudes and skills necessary to function within the diverse cultural, religious, and social parameters of the world's "Global Village."
  • Values Education. PSU inculcates in its students a philosophy of life that helps nurture an entrepreneurial spirit, genuine concern for the community and environment, and a sensitivity to the values in the American way of life.
  • Professionalism. PSU provides students with the necessary communication skills for careers in business and technology through its strong emphasis on the mastery of English , the language of international business.
