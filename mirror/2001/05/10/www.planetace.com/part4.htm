Advantages of India Inc.

Companies around the world have realized that to remain ahead of their rivals, they need to reduce costs, provide the best quality, use the latest hi-tech skills and be reliable and innovative. They are able to derive all these requirements through a single source — The software industry in India.

Gain competitive advantages by using Indian software/web services that offer high quality, cost effectiveness, time savings, state-of-the-art technologies and above all reliability.

  • Quality - is the hallmark of Indian software industry. ISO 9000 certification and SEI Level 5 and 4 maturity level are becoming common standards of the industry.

  • Reliability - Indian software companies believe in ultimate adherence to delivery schedules and customer satisfaction by using state-of-the-art technologies.

  • Cost and Time savings - Offshore software development in India with the use of high-speed data communication links (64 kbps and above) provide immense cost and time savings including 24-hour virtual offices.

  • Large Manpower Pool - 115,000 engineering graduates every year transform India into the second largest English speaking IT professionals pool in the world.

  • E-commerce and web based solutions - Indian companies offer the most cost-effective, innovative and extensive web-based solutions. They also offer varied solutions for E-commerce applications. Hotmail, Junglee are some of the examples of Indian innovativeness.

  • A virtual 12-hour time zone difference between India and USA offers cost and time savings.

  • India enjoys very strong brand equity in major markets, thanks to its growing and globally competitive software industry.
