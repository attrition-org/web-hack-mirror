IUPUI

Center for Teaching & Learning supports faculty in their quest to explore new avenues of teaching, develop new courses or redesign existing ones, and create new ways to utilize technology to enrich teaching, research, and service. Also provides consultations in teaching and advanced technology applications

Adaptive Education Services (AES) works to make campus life and learning accessible for students with disabilities

Affirmative Action Office ensures the University's compliance with all applicable federal and state nondiscrimination requirements and serves as the institution's official liaison with governmental civil rights enforcement agencies

Associate Faculty Office serves as an advocate for associate faculty and facilitates the professional development of associate faculty

Bookstores provides, in addition to textbooks and supplies, software, reference material, and giftware

Child Care Center, a licensed facility for 68 children ages 2-12; school-age children can be enrolled at the Center during the summer and vacation times

Clarian Health is the name for the consolidated health care organization that includes Methodist Hospital, Indiana University Hospital and Riley Hospital for Children

The Community Learning Network (CLN) encompasses off campus credit programs, continuing education and non-credit programs, and business outreach, education and training by delivering education training and services to learners at times and places that are convenient to learn

The Copyright Management Center assists members of the university community as they work with copyright and intellectual property rights issues in the creation of original works and in the use of existing copyrighted works for teaching, research, and service

Counseling and Psychological Services (CAPS) provides counseling services to IUPUI campus about anything affecting personal welfare or achievement

The CyberLab focuses on WWW research and development, and Web application development for teaching, learning, and distance education, offering experimental resources and services such as live broadcast over the Internet, a multimedia digital library, and media digitizing

Enrollment Center identifies links to all IUPUI Departments and course offerings, and includes online course description preparation/submission instructions

Graphic Reproductions provides electronic publishing, design, printing, duplicating, and copyright clearance services

The Indiana Higher Education Telecommunication System (IHETS) is a consortium of post-secondary institutions in Indiana that provides data, voice, and video services for education in the state

Instructional Teams, comprised of a member of the teaching faculty working with a librarian, a technologist, a course design and development specialist, a counselor, and a student mentor, work together to develop a successful learning experience for students enrolled in a course

International Affairs provides visa documentation, advises on employment procedures and policies, and offers adjustment to life in Indiana counseling

The Medical Educational Resources Program (MERP) partners with health care professionals in educational development, consultation, communications, media technology and production to enhance the learning process

The Office for Professional Development assists the IUPUI academic community in achieving the highest levels of personal attainment in teaching, research, and service

The Office of the Registrar provides faculty with information on course waitlists, course/section authorizations, audit policies, grades and change of grades, and student academic misconduct policy

The Office of Women & Commission on Women, Office for Professional Development, established to help the campus improve the workplace and learning environment for women

Parking Services operates, maintains and develops parking facilites, including locator maps

The Police, IUPUI, provides police services, operates "lost and found services," and provides safety escorts

Research and Academic Computing, provides the best possible research computing environment for Indiana University, and thus enhances the quality and quantity of research and research-oriented education at Indiana University

Student Technology Centers are staffed technology centers for student use located in several campus buildings and open to allow access to computer applications, networks, and video and voice technologies

University Writing Center strives to support faculty across disciplines in their efforts to use writing in their curricula

University Information Technology Services supports the teaching, research, and public service missions of Indiana University
