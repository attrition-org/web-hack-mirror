CENTERS FOR TEACHING & LEARNING

Centers for Teaching & Learning recognize the need to assist faculty in making the shift to a learner-focused education, one that incorporates a holistic learning environment, promotes lifelong learning, frequently is distributed wholly or in part through distance technologies, and just as often incorporates technology into the learning environment.
