General Tips 

  • Should I overclocking ?

  • A long beep sound followed by three or more short beep sounds during startup of the system indicates a display adapter failure. All other beep sounds mostly indicate problems with the memory modules/sockets.

  • The PCI architecture only allows the use of 4 IRQ's which have to be used by the PCI slots + USB controller + AGP slot. As a result, some PCI slots's IRQ lines may be linked to other devices to perform IRQ sharing. Some PCI cards (especially certain networkcards) do not support IRQ sharing. In this case you will have to more your PCI card to another slot, which has no shared IRQ.

  • Executing the DMI configuration utility  in a DOS session under Windows 95/98 may result in corruption of BIOS data ! Please use this utility in a pure DOS environment only !

Contact To Chaintech

Technical Support is one of the most important aspects of a modern day PC industry company and Chaintech is determined to providing the best service possible to its customers and endusers. Service is based on a strong relationship between Chaintech and the people that use its products. These pages will help you find the latest information on the status of your Chaintech product and/or provide links to other resources on the web or to Chaintech's e-mail service. Before you decide to consult our service department through this e-mail service please make absolutely sure to browse through these pages thoroughly as in most cases the answer(s) to your question(s) can be found here. If you have any suggestions or remarks on how we may improve these pages or our service, please feel free to mention this in an e-mail as well.  

In the left upper corner of this page you can see the various categories of support that these pages offer. Please make your selection above to jump to a particular category or just scroll through this page to view all categories. Here you can select your product's model name and see in a glimpse which resources (BIOS/driver updates, manuals, compatibility information, etc.) of this model are available on our site.

In general we advise you to update your system with the latest drivers and BIOS updates before sending us an e-mail with a problem description. New BIOS updates and drivers always contain all bug fixes and updated features of earlier versions, so if there have been several updates between your current version and the latest version you only have to install the latest version in order to make your system up-to-date.
