Since its founding 1986, Chaintech 
has won a reputation for excellent 
design, uncompromising quality 
and superlative service. In order to 
extend and enhance this reputation, 
we have implemented our Seven S’:    

                         

Staffing Chaintech emphasizes the professional development and social welfare of each and every staff member.
Satisfaction Chaintech’s mission is to meet or exceed customer expectations to provide them total satisfaction.
Solutions With advanced technology as the foundation, we strive to provide our customers with complete, high quality solutions.
Service To exceed customer expectations, Chaintech is dedicated to insuring thoughtful, forward looking service.
Superiority The pursuit of excellence at Chaintech as carried out with the goal of maintaining a superior level of product quality and service.
Strategy The underlying guiding strategy of Chaintech is the drive to integrate the concept of the 3Cs – computers, communication and customers – into a single harmonious vision.
Success Chaintech seeks to insure that all of its relationships are “win-win” for all involved. Success begets success.
