Have you ever wondered how a radio or a TV works? Or how your VCR records and plays back movies? Are you interested in radio and TV production? Do the fields of electronics and computers fascinate you?

If you answered yes (or even maybe) to any of these questions then you are in the right place. Via E-Mail,this page will allow you to ask any questions you want concerning the Broadcast Industry. Mr. TV will attempt to answer your questions on a daily basis. Of course time and space considerations may limit the scope and detail of some answers.
