NDI Solutions is a leading full-service provider of technology and professional services. Focusing primarily on the educational marketplace, we assist clients in the development and evaluation of vital technology plans and deliver integrated solutions. Our commitment is to maximize the value of our customer's technological resources.

Bringing expertise and experience, NDI Solutions enables clients to make precise information technology decisions (including hardware, software, and training) with confidence. With certified network and software specialists, professional trainers, and technicians, we offer a broad range of strategy, technology, and creative solutions. Our qualified staff has made us a leader in delivering unmatched results to hundreds of schools and businesses.

Combined with strategic relationships, we continue to offer a variety of additional technology solutions. A proud distributor of School Office and Finance Office by Specialized Data Systems, NDI Solutions is the foremost authority on SDS product implementation, utilization, and training. Both software applications have been used to manage school districts, worldwide, for over 25 years. Venturing into the realm of new media, we supply network-based, broadcast quality media applications for distance learning, content distribution, training, and video conferencing. Together with a host of hardware and software partners, NDI Solutions is excited to endorse this extraordinary line of products and associated services. By developing such strong relationships we are able to pass valuable benefits directly on to our clients.
