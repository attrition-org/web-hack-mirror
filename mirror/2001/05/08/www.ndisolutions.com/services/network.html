NDI Solutions has 15 years of experience as a solutions provider designing computer networks. In fact, NDI stands for Network, Design, and Integration. Whether you are building a new network or upgrading an existing one, we are dedicated to maximizing your technology resources. Combining experience and training, our staff offers an array of hardware services and consultation unmatched in the educational market.

  • Evaluating current needs and future expansion, we design and deliver robust, scalable solutions that meet your budgetary requirements.
  • Analyzing current infrastructure and hardware, we provide means to maximize your existing technology foundation and recommend enhancements for potential resource growth.
  • Ensuring satisfied results, we streamline the integration of new technologies to increase the services you make available to your staff and student body.
  • We provide emergency support services, limiting the impact of unforeseen technological failures that affect critical system components. Adding value to your network, we offer management services to supply our knowledge, experience, and resources limiting your need to hire an entire IT staff.
Our trained, certified, and experienced network team is dedicated to managing the design, integration, use, and support of LAN/WAN solutions. Bringing together product knowledge of industry leading companies such as Microsoft, Novell, 3-Com, and more, NDI Solutions continues to be a leading full-service technology provider.
