The Grades component supplies your school with the greatest flexibility the School Office application offers. Whether your grading system is numeric or alphabetic, generate a number of reports complete with comments. Calculate student rank by quarter, semester, or year, as well as the following:
  • Maximize efficiency with Teacher Entry
  • Create honor rolls, report cards, and transcripts
  • Record conduct grades and comments
  • Track multiple GPAs for each student
Streamline the grade reporting process with teachers entering grades directly from their desktop. Supporting data import and export allows analysis of grades any way imaginable. Linking Grades information with other School Office components expands the power and capabilities of the application to an unprecedented degree.
