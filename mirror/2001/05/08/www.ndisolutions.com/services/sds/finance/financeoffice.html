Finance Office is a multi-functional, relational database that gives you unprecedented power and flexibility in management of your financial operations and staff. From the Administration Office to each school Main Office, you can now control all accounts, grants, funds, assets, and benefits from a desktop. Use software that was designed to meet school-based accounting. Choose from a number of standard presentation-quality, including state mandated, reports quickly and easily, or create your own reports to match any criteria. With six primary components including Budget Finance, Accounts Payable, Payroll/Personnel, Fixed Assets, Management, and Reporting, Finance Office is an extraordinarily valuable management investment.
