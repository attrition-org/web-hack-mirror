Including human resources functions, Payroll tracks sick days, personal days, tardiness, and seven additional areas, allowing you to produce annual statements for each employee. Flexible enough to handle changes in tax rates, benefits, and employees options, Finance Office includes these additional features:
  • Account Structure includes 10 basic fields
  • Account Numbers with 50 digit limit
  • Generate Account Numbers automatically
  • Integration with MS Office
An exception-based system where only changes need to be entered, Payroll processes the rest automatically. With links to Accounts Payable, this component additionally provides liability and benefit payments. All of these features combine to provide powerful analysis tools for District Business Managers.
