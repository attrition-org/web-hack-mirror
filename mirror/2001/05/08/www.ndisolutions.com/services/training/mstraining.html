Software skills are becoming increasingly more important in today's technological world. Moreover, a basic understanding of the most popular commercial software applications has become nearly a necessity for success. Committed to improving client performance, NDI Solutions offers a wide assortment of HOT (Hands-On-Training) Labs for the widely-used product family from Microsoft. In addition to the labs listed, NDI Solutions will create a customized training program focusing on the skills and/or software your staff requires.
