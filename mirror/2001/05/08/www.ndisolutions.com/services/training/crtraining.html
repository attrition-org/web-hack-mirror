Crystal Reports, from Seagate, is the world's leading report software. Compatible with nearly every known database solution, an earlier version of this application drives the Advanced Reporting module in both School Office and Finance Office, by SDS. Also, subsequent stand-alone versions of Crystal Reports have been released by Seagate dramatically increasing the available features. Robust and flexible, learn how to create presentation quality reports customized specifically to your school or district needs. NDI Solutions is the source for HOT (Hands-On Training) Labs on this application, regardless of version. In addition to the labs listed, NDI Solutions will create a customized training program focusing on the skills and/or modules your staff requires.
