Announcer, from Ajax Software, is an inexpensive, easy to use software tool that broadcasts information to any networked computer throughout your school or district. Enter announcements in a single control computer and they are instantly relayed to any terminal setup to receive. You can even broadcast announcements via a closed circuit TV system.

Key Benefits
  • Silent Message Delivery
  • Messages Available All Day
  • Streamlines Bus Dismissal Safety

For a Limited time Announcer is available for a free 30 day trial. At the end of the trial, simply call NDI Solutions' Adam Smith at (800) 858-7698. Upon receipt of payment you will receive a permanent registration code. The cost of Announcer is $495.00 per building installation.
