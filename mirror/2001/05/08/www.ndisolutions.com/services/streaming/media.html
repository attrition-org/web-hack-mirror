Streamcast is a comprehensive, best-of-breed streaming media solution for broadband multimedia applications aimed at enhancing education through interschool interactivity, expanding classroom reach, content distribution, staff development, and video conferencing. Based on industry standard technology, NDI Solutions combines the expertise of real time network systems, client/server software, and digital video compression to enable existing networks to support broadcast quality media streaming. Using existing cabling infrastructure, Streamcast maximizes the power, flexibility, and performance of your network.

Streaming media delivers a range of educational benefits, from providing students instant access to multimedia educational material to extending the reach of the classroom. Distance learning applications, using video technology to allow student participation from one school in courses offered at other schools or colleges, will become an increasingly valuable alternative. Streamcast uses the desktop computer to supply greater individualization in instruction including interactivity and variety of tempo and difficulty appropriate for individual students.
