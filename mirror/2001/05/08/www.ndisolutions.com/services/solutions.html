NDI Solutions continually adds products and services to our offerings to better serve our clients. From software to bundled solutions including hardware, we endeavor to provide Total Technology Solutions to meet school's critical IT needs and streamline existing processes. We enable schools to focus on their mission, educating students.
School Office - A robust relational database application providing both powerful and flexibile student management.
Finance Office - Control accounts, grants, funds, assets, and benefits all with this multi-functional, database application.
eAssist - Our platinum support service that enables virtual meeting, demonstrations, and remote desktop control via a high-speed internet connection.
Announcer - Broadcast information throughout your school, including bus dismissals and general announcements, silently with this easy-to-use software tool.
Streamcast - Enhance the educational experience with our tailored streaming media solution that uses distance learning and video distribution applications.
LunchBox - A total food service solution that streamlines your entire operation from the cafeteria to the district office by integrating seamlessly with School Office.
