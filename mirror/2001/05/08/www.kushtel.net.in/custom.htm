               CUSTOMER CARE



TROUBLESHOOTING

1. Check that cable interconnections among your computer, modem and telephone line are proper.
2. Check that Modem is configured correctly.
3. Check that a valid Phone number has been entered before connecting.
4. If your modem is not responding then check for working of telephone.
5. If your modem receives a busy signal then the phone line may be busy. Try to connect after some time.
6. If your modem is working correctly but unable to get connected then try the back up number.
7. Though you are connected to the ISP server, but no transfer of data is taking place, it could be because of the following reasons:
a) There may be problem in telephone line. Please check whether you are getting dial tone, if not then book the complaint with the DoT or your Fixed Line Service Provider.
b) If yes, please see that your modem is connected properly and your adapter is not faulty.
8. If the problem persists please call KushTel Help Desk.
