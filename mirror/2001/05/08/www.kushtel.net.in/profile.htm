               PROFILE

The company has eminent personalities attached with it who are renowned in the telecom and networking areas. Some of them are experts in their own way. The company, by using advanced technologies like Microwave and Optical Fibre System, provides Intranet solutions.

* We are embarking upon to start the services in the cities of Delhi, Ghaziabad, Noida, Faridabad, Gurgaon,    Bulandshahar, Meerut in recent future. We are going to launch the services in cities of Agra, Aligarh, Mathura    etc shortly. We have plans to cover whole of U.P. in a few months time.
* We are also going to launch the services in the states of M.P., starting from Bhopal. This will be followed by   Jabalpur, Raipur, Gwalior etc.
* We have established our offices at Delhi, Bhopal, Jabalpur and Ghaziabad.
* We have set up our Hub at Ghaziabad. Subsequently all the other stations in the states of MP and UP will be    connected to this Hub.
* We have set up the Web Server at Ghaziabad. This will faciitate the user to get their Web page hosted at very    competitive prices.
* We are going to start E-Commerce activities shortly. This technology is going to change the way we do the    business today. In the next few years almost all the business will be carried out through Internet, as is being    presently done in USA and other developed countries.
