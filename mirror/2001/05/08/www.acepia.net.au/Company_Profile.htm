Acepia - Company Profile

With the ever-increasing demand by businesses for Internet and Email access and connectivity, Acepia   is able to supply and support these demands.

Acepia has now grown to become one of Victoria's larger providers of Internet connectivity wide-area networks, with its own dedicated Australian network and overseas links.

Acepia is a level one Internet Service Provider who maintains a 2Mb satellite link with a 256Kb land line for backup purposes. Our Internet facilities are offered throughout Melbourne, Melbourne’s suburbs, Yarra Valley and regional country areas. We have 5 sub-ISP’s connected to our system, enabling further usage throughout Victoria.

Our growing client list currently exceeds 1100 users of which approximately 60% are corporate or business clients. We service our clients with in excess of 180 modems @ 56K.  We have 45 current clients who maintain full time links routed through Acepia.

Operating with the latest Web technology in both Hardware and Software, we have three client Web Hosting machines. Acepia offers Commerce services through a high performance commerce server, supporting SQL, CGI, front page server extensions and a full range of commerce solutions for business and private.

The team at Acepia guarantee technical excellence and a professional approach to the development and maintenance of computing solutions. The personal nature maintained by both  Acepia ensures that clients always receive the high level of attention that they deserve.

Operating out of a modern two-storey office complex in Croydon,  Acepia is centrally placed to service the growing needs of our clients in Melbourne and Victoria's Eastern Regional country areas.
