Marker Boards
American Chalkboard's Markerboards

feature the AllianceWall® porcelain enamel semi-gloss surface which provides full color value to dry erase markers with negligible marker wear. This produces clear, sharp marker lines for maximum contrast and control of glare. The non-porous surface wipes clean with a dry cloth or eraser and is ideal for all magnets and magnetic impregnated teaching aids.

Available in three different colors,
our markerboards are warranted for the lifetime of the original installation that the porcelain enamel finish, under normal conditions of use, will not exhibit excessive fading of color, crazing, cracking or flaking.
