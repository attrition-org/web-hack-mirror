Events that happened in 2000 and 2001

 February 14th 2001  Rain rain go away. Well it won't, so off to Mexico we go. Karen, I, Barry and Ann head south of the border for two weeks. Two weeks? What about your job you say. Don't worry I took care of that too.

September 3rd, 2000 Warning: Party in the Delta this Labor Day weekend. It was the only place to get a big boat and an RV together for the week so we could party. We will be at the Delta Bay Club Sept 1-5 2000. So lock up your women and sheep. Just remember to bring beer and your ski boat. The pictures are Here!

August 15th, 2000 If you haven't noticed, the Bushwacker web site has had a facelift including all new graphics and animations. Cool! Good job Jeff... I also updated the destinations, cool stuff  and the vessel sections of the site.

August 10th, 2000  The Bushwacker Jokes Section gets a major overhaul.  New material, working links and some surprises.  Make sure you listen to the 9 Coronas song.  Its worth the download. Another good reason for getting DSL.

July 10th, 2000 The boat got hauled out at Nelsons. "OH my God it's so big" Like I never heard that before. That evening we celebrated Bill's Birthday with dinner and Bocce Ball. Bocce Ball you say?  No it's not some new disease.

June 26, 2000 My brother Robert, his wife Jackie, Michael and Ashley come to California for a visit and to see the family. Photos of the trip are here.

June 17th, 2000 Karen, Luke and I spend the weekend at Treasure Island. Barry is in Cabo helping Jamie, Elaine, and Harry bring Jamie's new Taswell 49 up the coast. Unfortunately some of us have to work. Well not on the weekends.

May 28th, 2000 It's that time of year when we say "hello" to summer and head for Angel Island for the start of the summer season. Follow Karen, Jeff, Barry, Ann, and I as we start the summer of 2000.

April 28th, 2000 Jeff invites us down to Laguna Seca for the weekend. He takes all of us for a ride in the car as he tears up the track. Some of us get to go three times! and LIVE...

April 15th, 2000 Jeff has some professional pictures taken of him and the car at Sears Point Raceway. Unbelievable shots a "must see". I even love editing the web site now with DSL.
Follow this link for the pictures.

March 12th, 2000 What a week! I finally spend a full week on the boat, this time I was not chased away by the rain. The self tracking DSS satellite TV antenna / Dingy davits / Outboard motor lift projects are completed. I even have an opportunity to go for a ride on "Convergence".
Follow this link for the pictures.

Feb 11th, 2000 We got HACKED!  On the same day the  RealNames website got broken into our website was hacked by a group that calls themselves '77 NET.  Gary was showing the website to a customer and had a little surprise.  Click here to see what was on the front page in place of the laughing skull. Everything is back to normal now.
