Upcoming attractions from Columbia are listed below.

HOLLOWMAN


Cast: Kevin Bacon,Elisabeth Shue,Josh Brolin,Kim Dickens, Joey Slotnick,Mary Randle,Greg Grunberg ,Rhona Mitra,William Devane

Bacon stars as Sebastian Caine, a Pentagon-funded hotshot scientist whose top-secret program has succeeded in turning lab animals invisible.Confronted with government meddling, Caine naturally jumps the gun and, in true Jekyll-Hyde fashion, makes himself the first human test subject. The trouble is, reverting to visibility is a nut the team has yet to crack, and so Caine eventually decides he'd like to stay invisible and free.


CROUCHING TIGER-HIDDEN DRAGON


Cast: Yun-Fat Chow ,Michelle Yeoh ,Ziyi Zhang ,Chen Chang ,Sihung Lung ,Pei-pei Cheng ,Fazeng Li ,Xian Gao ,Yan Hai ,Deming Wang

Crouching Tiger is a martial-arts drama, based on the novel by Wang Dulu and set in the 19th century. Chow plays legendary warrior Li Mubai, possessor of a jade sword called Green Destiny. He gives the sword to his beloved, the martial artist Yui Hsuilien (Yeoh). Someone steals Green Destiny and suspicion falls on Jade Fox (Cheng), the scheming governess of May Wong (Zhang), a curious, impressionable beauty about to be given away in loveless marriage.


CHARLIES ANGELS

Cast: Drew Barrymore, Cameron Diaz, Lucy Liu, Bill Murray, Sam Rockwell, Tim Curry, Crispin Glover Matt LeBlanc Kelly Lynch Tom Green

CHARLIE'S ANGELS revolves around three female detectives- Natalie (Cameron Diaz) the bookworm, Dylan (Drew Barrymore) the tough girl, and Alex (Lucy Liu) the class-act-as intelligent and multi-talented as they are ravishingly gorgeous and utterly disarming. What can be done when Eric Knox, a soon-to-be billionaire, is kidnapped from his own office at Knox Technologies, and the company is in jeopardy of losing a billion dollars? Under the sure hand of their suave playboy boss, notorious for his clever ways of avoiding face-to-face meetings with his employees, the Angels use feminine charm, high-tech gadgets and hand-to-hand combat to save themselves, Charlie and thousands of innocent people.

THE 6th DAY


Cast: Arnold Schwarzenegger, Tony Goldwyn, Robert Duvall, Michael Rapaport, Michael Rooker, Sarah Wynter, Wendy Crewson

Director Roger Spottiswoode (Tomorrow Never Dies, Noriega) creates a world of the very near future in which cattle, fish and even the family pet can be cloned. But cloning humans is illegal-that is until family man Adam Gibson (Arnold Schwarzenegger) comes home from work one day to find a clone has replaced him. Taken from his family and plunged into a sinister world he doesn't understand, Gibson must not only save himself from the assassins who must now destroy him to protect their secret, but uncover who and what is behind the horrible things happening to him. THE 6TH DAY is the story of Gibson's struggle to reclaim his life and his family.
