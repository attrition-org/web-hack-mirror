April Sony India factory certified as Environment friendly when it was conferred ISO 14001 Certification by DNV, Netherlands.


1998
February Sony India set up its software operations in Banglore. The two divisions - Architecture lab (Engaged in Embedded software Development for products like AIBO) and systems design division engaged in assistance with systems applications. Both these divisions are engaged in supporting the counterparts.
November Sony India factory achieved half million CTV production in its factory at Dharuhera, Haryana. To mark the occasion, company donates Rs. 2.2 mil towards education of underprivileged children through CRY- A charity organization.
December Sony India factory certified for ISO 9002
