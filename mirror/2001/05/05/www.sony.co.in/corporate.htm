Movies

Columbia Tristar Films Of India Ltd. is a part of Columbia Tristar Film Distributors International , which in turn is the international film distribution arm of Sony Pictures Entertainment. Columbia Tristar's Indian operations started in 1934 and it has since then consistently been bringing in the biggest Hollywood blockbusters to India. In 1999 , Columbia became the first Hollywood major to get FIPB approval to distribute and produce Indian films. SPE Films India ( a subsidiary of Columbia Tristar India ) opened up a world of opportunities for Indian film makers when it kickstarted its Indian operations by distributing its first venture " Pyaar Mein Kabhi Kabhi " .

Sony Pictures Entertainment’s global operations encompass motion picture production and distribution, television programming and syndication, home video acquisition and distribution, operation of studio facilities, development of new entertainment technologies and distribution of filmed entertainment in 67 countries worldwide. Sony Pictures can be found on the World Wide Web at http://www.spe.sony.com

Music

In January, 1988, Sony Corporation acquired CBS Records Group, known today as Sony Music Entertainment Inc. CBS/Sony is today known as Sony Music Entertainment (Japan) Inc. In January, 1994, in acknowledgment of its worldwide growth and success, Sony Music Entertainment reorganized into four label groups: Epic Records Group, Columbia Records Group, Relativity Entertainment Group, and Sony Classical.
