Mandy Moore

You can't get any closer to Mandy Moore than this!

What a difference a year can make... Actually, it's been even less than a year since Mandy Moore released her debut album, So Real, which reached platinum status in just three months. Now Mandy Moore returns with a new Special Edition album, I Wanna Be With You (550/Epic). I Wanna Be With You includes four additional brand-new tracks-"Every-thing My Heart Desires," "Want You Back," "Way To My Heart," and "Your Face"-plus the origi-nal and Wade Robson remix of Mandy's smash hit "Candy"; along with four of the most popular cuts from So Real.

Mandy earned a platinum debut album with So Real, and went gold with her Top Ten single "Candy
