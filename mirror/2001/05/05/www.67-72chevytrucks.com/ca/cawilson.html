C.A. Wilsons '72 Cheyenne Super 10

This 1972 Super Cheyenne belongs to C.A. Wilson from Lenoir City, TN. He is the one pictured with the trophy. He won first place in the Kingston Pike Baptist Church Fan's Choice Car Show on 6/27/98, it was also entered in the Advance Auto Parts in Loudon, TN to raise money for the Juvenile Diabetes Association, It took first place. The truck has 800 miles since it was restored about 5 years ago. Here is a zip file that that contasns all 7 pics and a info sheet they use for car shows ca.zip. I also have the info sheet converted to HTML CAsTruck.html (Some formating was lost in the HTML conversion). E-mail Andy Heisch If you have any questions or comments about this truck.
