We have the pleasure of introducing ourselves as NIIT Ltd., global IT SOLUTIONS COMPANY which today is one of the leading IT training organizations in the world. NIIT has a presence in over 30 countries with subsidiaries in India, Africa, Middle East, Asia Pacific, Europe, Japan, and USA and employee strength of over 4000 NIITians.

NIIT is ISO 9001 certified (from BVQI, UK) for Training, Software Solutions & Educational Multimedia and ISO 9002 certified for technical support on software. NIIT is not only Microsoft & Oracle course independent but is their premier training partner.
