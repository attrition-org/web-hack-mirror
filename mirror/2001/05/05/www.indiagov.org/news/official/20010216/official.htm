At the invitation of the President of India, the President of the Republic of Lithuania Mr. Valdas Adamkus is paying a State Visit to India from February 19-23, 2001.  President Adamkus is accompanied by his wife, Mrs. Alma Adamkiene.  The Lithuanian President is also accompanied by official delegation which includes the Foreign Minister of the Republic of Lithuania,  His Excellency Mr. Antanas Valionis.

The President of Lithuania and Mrs. Adamkiene will be ceremonially welcomed on February 20, 2001.  On the same day, they will call on the President of India and Smt. K.R. Narayanan.  A State Banquet will be hosted by the President of India.  The Vice President of India will meet Mr. Adamkus.  The Prime Minister of India and the Lithuanian President will hold detailed discussions on bilateral matters and on regional and international issues.  Three Agreements are expected to be signed during the visit.  The External Affairs Minister and the Leader of the Opposition will call on the President of Lithuania on February 20, 2001 and February 21, 2001 respectively.

Besides his stay in Delhi, the Lithuanian President will visit Agra and Mumbai.  Mr. Adamkus is accompanied by a large business delegation.  He will have an interactive meeting with some of the CEOs of the Confederation of Indian Industries in New Delhi and Mumbai.  He will also visit the Santa Cruz Engineering Export Processing Zone.

Relations between India and Lithuania are close and friendly.  Our cultural links extend beyond centuries and there is a perceived affinity between Sanskrit and the Lithuanian languages.

During the visit of the President of Lithuania, Lithuania will also be celebrating its National Day.
 

New Delhi
February 16, 2001.
