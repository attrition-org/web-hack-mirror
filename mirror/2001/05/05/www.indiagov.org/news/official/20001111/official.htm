Visit of the Vice Chairman of the State Peace and Development Council of Myanmar to India

At the invitation of the Vice President, Shri Krishan Kant, the Vice Chairman of the State Peace and Development Council (SPDC) of the Union of Myanmar, Gen Maung Aye will pay a State visit to India from November 14 to 21, 2000.  Gen Maung Aye is also Deputy C-in-C of the Myanmar Defence Forces and C-in-C, Myanmar Army.

Gen Maung Aye chairs the National Trade Council, National Industrial Development Committee and National Agricultural and Economic Council, which are the national level economic policy making bodies.

Gen Maung Aye, who will be accompanied by his wife and daughter, will lead a high level delegation, including Secretary – 2 of the SPDC, Lt Gen Tin Oo, the Deputy Prime Minister of Myanmar, Lt Gen Tin Hla, and six other Ministers, dealing with Foreign Affairs, Finance, Power, Commerce, Science and Technology, and Industry.
The visit will commence at Bodh Gaya.  Thereafter, the delegation will travel to Banglore for an exposure to India’s hi-tech capabilites. Gen Maung Aye is scheduled to reach Delhi on November 17, where he would be calling on the President, the Vice President and the Prime Minister.

The visiting delegation is scheduled to interact with the Indian business community at a luncheon meeting being organized by the CII.  A visit to the India International Trade Fair is planned.  An Agreement on a credit line of US $ 15 million to purchase Indian capital goods is to be signed during the visit.

The programme includes a visit ot Agra and Udaipur.
 

New Delhi.
November 10, 2000.
