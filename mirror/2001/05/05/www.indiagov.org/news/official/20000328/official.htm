The Fourth Round of the dialogue between MEA, GOI and MITI (Ministry of International Trade and Industry), Japan was held in New Delhi on 28th March 2000.

The Indian delegation was led by Mr. S.T. Devare, Secretary (Economic Relations), MEA. The Japanese delegation was led by Mr. Hisamitsu Arai, Vice-Minister for International Affairs, MITI, Japan.

The one-day dialogue covered briefings on the economic situation in India and Japan, a presentation of India’s new FDI policies, of the recent budget, etc.

The MEA-MITI dialogue highlighted the possibilities of India-Japan cooperation in the IT section covering electronic government, joint research possibilities, and IT business promotion between the two countries.

Discussions also covered multilateral trade issues, bilateral energy dialogue between India and Japan, issues relating to bilateral trade and investment, etc.

The MEA-MITI dialogue served to highlight the interest of both sides to deepen and expand economic and commercial issues. It was recognized that the time is right for both sides to encourage business expansion given the current global economic scenario and the positive outlook of both the Japanese and the Indian economies.

New Delhi
March 28, 2000
