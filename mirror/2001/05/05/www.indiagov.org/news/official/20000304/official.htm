A group of miscreants held a demonstration today in the Kapurthala District, Punjab against the Pakistan bus on its Delhi-Lahore run. The local police responded quickly to disperse the miscreants and prevent any harm to its passengers or damage to the bus except for one broken glass.

The Delhi-Lahore-Delhi bus service has become a valued transport link between India and Pakistan. It symbolizes India's constant endeavour to promote people to people contacts between the two countries. We wish the service to continue and will provide necessary security to the bus and its passengers.

New Delhi
March 4, 2000
