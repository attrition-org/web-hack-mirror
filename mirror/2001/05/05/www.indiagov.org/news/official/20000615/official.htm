As per the Order of the Supreme Court, a partially reconstituted Haj Committee, in a meeting convened by the Foreign Secretary, Shri Lalit Mansingh, re-elected Shri Tanveer Ahmad as Chairman of the Central Haj Committee. Smt. Syeda Anwara Taimur and Shri Mohd. Sohail Lokhandwala were elected as Vice Chairmen.

Shri Tanveer Ahmad was elected Chairman, for the first time in June 1999 when the Government of India reconstituted the Central Haj Committee. Smt. Syeda Anwara Taimur is a former Chief Minister of Assam and a former Member of Parliament. Shri Lokhandwala is a former MLA of the Maharashtra  Legislative Assembly.

New Delhi
June 15, 2000.
