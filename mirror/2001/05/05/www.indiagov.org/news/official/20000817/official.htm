At the invitation of President K.R. Narayanan, H.E. Mr. Maumoon Abdul Gayoom, President of the Republic of Maldives will pay a State Visit to India from August 20-23, 2000.  He will be accompanied by Madam Nasreena M.A. Gayoom.

During his visit, President Gayoom will meet President K.R. Narayanan, Vice President Krishan Kant, Prime Minister Atal Bihari Vajpayee as well as some other Union Cabinet Ministers.  He discussions with Indian leaders are likely to cover bilateral matters as well as issues of regional and international importance to the two countries.

 Besides Delhi, President Gayoom will be visiting Hyderabad to get a first-hand view of India’s hitech capabilities.

 India and Maldives enjoy close, cordial and problem-free relations. India has played an active and constructive role in the field of public health and human resource development in the Maldives.  The Joint Commission, which reviews cooperation between the two countries, met in March this year.   The present high level visit would serve to further strengthen the friendly and cooperative ties that exist between the two
countries.

 President Gayoom last visited India on a State Visit in March 1994.  In 1995, he came for the 8th SAARC Summit.  In 1998, he was in India for a brief visit in his capacity as Chairman of SAARC.
 

New Delhi
August 17, 2000
