SHRI JASJIT SINGH RANDHAWA, currently Ambassador of India to CAMBODIA, has been appointed as the next High Commissioner of India to the SEYCHELLES, in succession to Shri R.O. Wallang. 

He is expected to take up his assignment shortly. 

New Delhi
September 19, 2000

SHRI PRADEEP KUMAR KAPUR, currently Minister in Embassy of India,
Kathmandu, has been appointed as the next Ambassador of India to the
KINGDOM OF COMBODIA, in succession to Shri J.S. Randhawa.

He is expected to take up his assignment shortly.

New Delhi
September 19, 2000
