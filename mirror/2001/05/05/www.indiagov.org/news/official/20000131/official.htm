In response to press stories about the requirement for short-term visitors to provide cash bonds for visits to the UK, the Official Spokesman said: 

We are ascertaining facts about this issue. The information we have at present is through press reports as we have not been extended the courtesy of being consulted. 

It is our expectation that in consular matters any new rules or guidelines adopted by a country should be implemented across the board on a non-discriminatory basis for citizens of all countries. 

We would like to clarify that in the event that such rules and guidelines are instituted, we reserve the right to take reciprocal action.

January 31, 2000
