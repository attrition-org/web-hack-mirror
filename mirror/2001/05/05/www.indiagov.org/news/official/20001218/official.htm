Visit of the Foreign Minister of the Hellenic Republic 

Mr. George A. Papandreou, Foreign Minister of the Hellenic Republic will be visiting India on 21 & 22 December 2000 at the invitation of the External Affairs Minister. The visiting Foreign Minister  will be accompanied by his wife, the Secretary General for Foreign Affairs and senior officials. Delegation level talks will be held on 22 December, 2000. The visiting Foreign Minister will also call on the President and meet other political leaders. The visit of the Hellenic Foreign Minister will, besides allowing for a review of bilateral relations and exchange of views on regional and multilateral issues, prepare the ground for the forthcoming visit of Prime Minister  Constantine Simitis in February 2001.
 

New Delhi
December 18, 2000
