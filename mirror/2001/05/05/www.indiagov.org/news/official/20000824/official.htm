The Deputy High Commissioner in the Pakistan High Commission in New Delhi, was called to the Ministry of External Affairs today.  He was informed that Mr. Malik Muhammad Rafique, a staff member of the Pakistan High Commission in New Delhi, was found indulging in activities incompatible with his official status.  The Government of India have, accordingly, sought Mr. Rafique’s withdrawal from India by 31st  August 2000.
 

New Delhi
August 24, 2000
