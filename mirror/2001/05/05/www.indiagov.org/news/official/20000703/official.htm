Visit by Sri Lankan Minister 
for Buddha Sasana, Culture and Religious Affairs

July 4-6, 2000

Mr. Lakshman Jayakody, Minister for Buddha Sasana, Culture and Religious Affairs, Government of Sri Lanka, will be visiting New Delhi from July 4 to 6, 2000 as the Special Envoy of the Sri Lanka President, Mrs. Chandrika Bandaranaike Kumaratunga. During his visit, Mr. Jayakody is scheduled to call on the Prime Minister. He is also likely to call on the Minister of External Affairs and to meet the Minister for Tourism and Culture. 

2. The Special Envoy will be accompanied by Mr. K. Balapatabendi, Secretary to the President of Sri Lanka and Dr. Senake Bandaranayake, Sri Lanka’s Ambassador to France and former Vice Chancellor, Kelaniya University. 

New Delhi 
July 3, 2000
