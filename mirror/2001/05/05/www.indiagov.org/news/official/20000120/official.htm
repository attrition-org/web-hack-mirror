Smt.Neelam D. Sabharwal, currently Minister & Deputy Chief of Mission in the Embassy of India, Beijing has been appointed as the next Permanent Representative of India to UNESCO, Paris with the rank of Ambassador in succession to Shri Chiranjiv Singh.

She is expected to take up her assignment shortly.

Shri Binanda Kumar Gogoi, presently Minister in the Embassy of India, Helsinki, has been appointed as the next Ambassador of India to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, in succession to Shri J. S. Sapra.

He is expected to take up his assignment shortly.

New Delhi
January 20, 2000
