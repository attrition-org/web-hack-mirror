Text of the Statement made by the Spokesman on the rescheduling of External Affairs Minister Shri Jaswant Singh's visit to Saudi Arabia

At the request of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the kingdom of Saudi Arabia the official visit to the External Affairs, Shri Jaswant Singh is being rescheduled . This is owing to the involvement of the Foreign Minister of Saudi Arabia in the diplomatic efforts currently underway to diffuse the situation in the Middle East and move it towards normalcy.

A number of Foreign Ministers of neighbouring countries are already in Riyadh to prepare for a summit there on October 16, 2000 of the leaders of Syria, Saudi Arabia and Egypt . This is prior to the full fledged Arab Summit scheduled to be held in Egypt later this month. The pace of developments in the West Asian region which necessitated these summits, impacted on a visit to which both sides attach high importance and for which they had undertaken meticulous preparations.

The authorities of Saudi Arabia have expressed their deep regrets about the inconvenience that this may have caused and have expressed their keen desires to reschedule the visit in the very near future keeping in mind developments in the region and the mutual convenience of the two Foreign Ministers
