The Acting Deputy High Commissioner in the Pakistan High Commission in New Delhi was summoned to the Ministry of External Affairs this afternoon. He was informed that Mr Mohammad Khalil, Mr Rana Mohammad, Mr Mohammad Amin, staff members of the Pakistan High Commission in New Delhi had been found indulging in activities incompatible with their official status. The Government of India has, accordingly, sought withdrawal of these three officials of the Pakistan High Commission from India by 25th February, 2000

New Delhi
February 18, 2000
