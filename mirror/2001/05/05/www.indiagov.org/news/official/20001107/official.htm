National Security Council Secretariat 

1. General Cumhur Asparuk, Secretary General of the Turkish National Security Council arrived in India on 6h November on a week long official visit at the invitation of Shri Satish Chandra, Secretary, National Security Council Secretariat. 

2. General Asparuk's visit to India is reflective of the desire of the leadership in the two countries for closer relations and greater understanding. This sentiment was articulated during the State visit of His Excellency Bulent Ecevit, Prime Minister of Republic of Turkey, (March 3 1 -April 2, 2000) which has given an impetus to further development of bilateral relations between the two countries. Closer Indo-Turkish ties are only natural given the shared commitment of the two countries to the ideas of democracy, secularism and rule of law. 

3. During the discussions attention was, interalia, focussed on organisational issues as well as regional and international security issues. It was, also decided that there should be a regular exchange of views on such and other security related matters between the two National Security Council Secretariats. 

4. General Cumhur Asparuk call&d on Shri George Fernandes, Raksha Mantri; Shri Brajesh Mishra, National Security Adviser and Principal Secretary to PM; Shri K.V. Rajan, Secretary (East), MEA; and Admiral Sushil. Kumar, Chairman, Chiefs of Staff Committee. General Asparuk will also be visiting Indian Space Research Organisation in Hyderabad and Bhaba Atomic Research Centre in Mumbai. 
 

New Delhi 
7th November 2000
