At the invitation of the Vice President, Shri Krishan Kant, the Vice President of Iraq, Mr. Taha Yassin Ramadhan, will be visiting India from November 27 November – December 1, 2000.  This is the highest level visit from Iraq to India in the last 25 years.

The Iraqi Vice President will be accompanied by a 17-member delegation including the Oil Minister Dr. Amer Mohammed Rashid and Deputy Foreign Minister Nur Al Weiss.  During the visit, the Iraqi dignitary will call on the President and Vice President and will have a meeting with the Prime Minister.  External Affairs Minister, Minister of Petroleum & Natural Gas and Minister of State for Commerce & Industry will call on the Iraqi dignitary, who will also address a meeting by FICCI on 29 November and will visit Bangalore on the 30th.  In Bangalore, Iraqi Vice President will visit scientific and technical establishments and information technology centres.  The Governor of Karnataka will host a dinner in honour of the visiting dignitary.

During the visit of Vice President Taha Yassin Ramadhan, a long-term cooperation agreement between the two countries will be signed.  An MOU between the Iraqi Federation of Industry and the Confederation of Indian Industry (CII) is also expected to be finalised.
 

New Delhi
November 24, 2000
