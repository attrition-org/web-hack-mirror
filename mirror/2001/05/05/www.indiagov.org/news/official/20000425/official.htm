New Appointments

Shri Manilal Tripathy, currently High Commissioner of India to Mauritius has been appointed as the next High Commissioner of India to Bangladesh, in succession to Shri Deb Mukharji. 

He is expected to take up his assignment shortly.

New Delhi
April 25, 2000

Shri Prabhat Prakash Shukla, currently Joint Secretary in Prime Minister's Office has been appointed as the next High Commissioner of India to Singapore, in succession to Shri Prem Singh. 

He is expected to take up his assignment shortly.

New Delhi
April 25, 2000
