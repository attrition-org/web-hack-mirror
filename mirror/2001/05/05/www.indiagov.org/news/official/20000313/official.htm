The Second Session of the Indo-Iranian working Group on International issues was held at Tehran on March 11-12, 2000. The Working Group was constituted following exchange of high level visits between the two countries last year. The Indian team was led by H.E. Mr. D.P. Srivastava, Joint Secretary, Ministry of External Affairs, while the Iranian team was led by H.E. Mr. Mohd. Reza Alborzi, Director General, International Affairs Department, Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Islamic Republic of Iran.

The two sides discussed a range of political, economic and social issues. This included cooperation between the two countries in UN and NAM fora, on issues including Terrorism, Afghanistan, Security Council restructuring, human rights, dialogue among civilizations and the IPU. The discussions demonstrated a close identity of views on a number of regional and international issues.

The next round of Indo-Iranian talks on international issues would be held in New Delhi at mutually convenient dates.

New Delhi
March 13, 2000
