At the invitation of Shri Yashwant Sinha, Minister of Finance of India, the Deputy Chairman of the Government of the Russian Federation, His Excellency Dr. Viktor Khristenko will be visiting India from January 14-16, 2000.

During the visit of Dr. Khristenko, the 6th Session of the Indo-Russian Inter Governmental Commission on Trade, Economic, Scientific, Technological and Cultural Cooperation (IRIGC) will be held in New Delhi. Finance Minister and Dr. Khristenko are the co-Chairmen of the IRIGC. Foreign Secretary and the Deputy Minister of Trade of the Russian Federation are the Deputy co-Chairmen. The Executive Secretariat of the IRIGC is the Ministry of External Affairs.

Dr. Khristenko will be accompanied by a high-level delegation, including 9 Deputy Ministers. The delegation also includes top executives of Russian banks, public sector undertakings and officials.

During his visit, Dr. Khristenko will be calling on Indian dignitaries.

New Delhi,
January 12, 2000.

Dr. Saiyed Raza Hashim, presently Member, Planning Commission, has been apointed as the next Ambassador of India to Kazakhstan, in succession to Shri Rajiv Sikri. Dr. S. R. Hashim will have the rank of the Minister of State while he is Ambassador there.

He is expected to take up his assignment shortly.

January 12, 2000.
