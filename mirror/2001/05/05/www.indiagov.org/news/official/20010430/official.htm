At the invitation of the Prime Minister of Malaysia, Dr. Mahathir Mohammed, the Prime Minister of India, Shri Atal Behari Vajpayee, would be visiting Malaysia from May 13-16, 2001. During this visit, he would be meeting Prime Minister Mahathir over delegation level talks. Other senior Ministers of the Government of Malaysia would call on Shri Vajpayee.

In addition to his official engagements, the Prime Minister would also preside over business-related events and address a Malaysian think-tank.

Several agreements are expected to be signed during the visit.

The visit of the Prime Minister to Malaysia was earlier planned in February 2001 but was postponed due to the Gujarat earthquake tragedy.
