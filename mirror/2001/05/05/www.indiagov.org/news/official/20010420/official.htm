In response to a question the Spokesperson said that the recent upsurge of violence in the West Asian region is a matter of great concern for us. In this volatile situation, there is only one priority- lasting peace. We urge all to desist from provocative activity and violence, and fully respect each other’s sovereignty and territorial integrity. We fully support efforts at a  resumption of dialogue. That is the only way to restore mutual trust and confidence and move towards normalisation of the atmosphere in the region.

 India has consistently supported the Middle East Peace Process, on the conviction that all political problems must be resolved through dialogue and that violence be adjured. India continues to support the settlement of the Palestine issue within the framework of the Middle East Peace Process based on UNSC Resolutions 242 and 338, relevant agreements and understandings and the principle of “Land for Peace”.
 

New Delhi
April 20, 2001
