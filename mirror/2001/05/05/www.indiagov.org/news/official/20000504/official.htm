Visit of Mr. Francese Vendrell, Personal Representative of the UNSG and Head of UNSMA, to India 
  
Mr. Francese Vendrell, Personal Representative of the UN Secretary General and Head of UN Special Mission on Afghanistan (UNSMA) visited India on May 2-5, 2000 for consultations on the Afghanistan issue.  During the visit, he had extensive discussions with senior officials of the Ministry of External Affairs and PMO.  His programme includes call on Foreign Secretary and the External Affairs Minister. 

Apart from India, Mr. Vendrell has also visited other countries, which could contribute to peaceful, political solution of the Afghan problem. This includes Central Asian Republics, Iran, Pakistan, Russia and China. Mr. Vendrell is the highest-ranking UN official dealing with Afghanistan issue. 

India is in touch with all the principal interlocutors on Afghanistan issue bilaterally as well as at the UN and NAM fora. Mr. Vendrell’s visit is part of this regular process of consultations. 
  
New Delhi 
May 4, 2000
