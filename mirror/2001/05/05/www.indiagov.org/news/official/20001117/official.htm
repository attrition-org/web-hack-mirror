During the State Visit to India by the President of the Russian Federation, His Excellency Mr. Vladimir Putin, from October 2-5, 2000,it was decided that an Indo-Russian Joint Working Group on Afghanistan would be established.  The first meeting of the Joint Working Group is expected to take place in New Delhi on November 20-21, 2000.  The Chairman from the Indian side of the Joint Working Group is Shri Lalit Mansingh, Foreign Secretary.  The Chairman from the Russian side is His Excellency Mr. V.I. Trubnikov, First Deputy Minister for Foreign Affairs
of the Russian Federation.

New Delhi
November 17, 2000
