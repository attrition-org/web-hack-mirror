The Rt. Hon'ble Sir Anerood Jugnauth, Prime Minister of Mauritius and Lady Sarojini Devi Jugnauth will be paying a State visit to India from January 15-24, 2001.  He will be accompanied by a high level delegation.  This will be his first bilateral visit to a foreign country after assumption of the office in September 2000.

During the course of the visit, PM Jugnauth will be visiting Bangalore, Hyderabad, Chennai and Mumbai.  PM Jugnauth will be in New Delhi from January 15-17, 2001.  While in New Delhi he is expected to meet our President, Prime Minister, Minister of State for External Affairs Shri Ajit Kumar Panja, Defence Minister and Leader of the Opposition, Smt. Sonia Gandhi.  During the bilateral meetings, issues of bilateral, regional and international importance are expected to be discussed. President will be hosting a Luncheon and Prime Minister will be hosting a Banquet in honour of the visiting dignitary.

The Mauritian Prime Minister will visit BEL, HAL and Infosys at Bangalore; Hitech city and Ramoji Film Studio at Hyderabad; Tidal Park and National Institute of Ocean Technology at Chennai apart from an interactive meeting with ASSOCHAM; and he will be having interactive meetings with the Reliance Industries; Indian Oil Corporation, the All India Association of Industries and CII at Mumbai before departing for Mauritius on 24.01.2001.

The Mauritian Prime Minister's visit to India will further strengthen the existing friendly relations between India and Mauritius and also enhance existing economic and commercial relations.
 

New Delhi
January 12, 2001
