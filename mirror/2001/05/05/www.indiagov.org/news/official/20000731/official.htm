Indo-French Strategic Dialogue

New Delhi July 31, 2000

The fifth round of the Indo-French Strategic Dialogue was held in New Delhi on 31st July, 2000. The Indian side was led by Shri Brajesh Mishra, Principal Secretary to PM and National Security Adviser. He was assisted by Secretary to PM; Foreign Secretary; Secretary, National Security Council; the Indian Ambassador to France and officials from the Prime Minister’s Office, Ministry of Defence and the Ministry of External Affairs. The French side was led by Mr. Gerard Errera, Special Envoy of the French President and of the French Government. He was assisted by senior officials from the French Embassy in New Delhi and the French Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

2. The decision to institute the Strategic Dialogue was taken during Prime Minister’s visit to Paris on 29th & 30th September, 1998. Shri Brajesh Mishra and Mr. Gerard Errera were designated as the interlocutors from the two sides. Earlier meetings of the dialogue have been held in New Delhi on 28th & 29th October, 1998, in Paris on 18th &19th January, 1999, in New Delhi on 28th & 29th July 1999 and in Paris on 7th & 8th February, 2000.

3. The leader of the French delegation also called on the Prime Minister and the External Affairs Minister. Principal Secretary will be hosting a dinner in honour of the Special Envoy today.

4. The discussions were comprehensive and wide ranging. The issues which were covered included developments in the region around India, developments in the region around France and issues of regional security, including the Balkans. Multilateral issues, including the recent G-8 Summit, Security Council expansion, terrorism, disarmament & non-proliferation related issues and bilateral relations were also
discussed. The discussions demonstrated that India and France have a shared perception on many international and regional issues. Thedialogue has led to better mutual understanding and deepening and strengthening of the strategic partnership between the two countries. The next round of the Strategic Dialogue will be held in Paris.
