Spokesman's statement

The Government of India have noted with satisfaction the end of the hijacking of a Russian aircraft at Medina Airport in Saudi Arabia and the positive role of the Saudi authorities in this regard.

The Government of the India strongly condemns the hijacking of the Russian aircraft and considers it as an act of international terrorism. It calls upon the international community to make sustained efforts to combat such acts of terrorism and to ensure that individuals or groups involved therein are brought to justice.

New Delhi 
March 20, 2001
