The Regional Passport Office, Chennai has developed its own website to active collaboration with National Information Centre, Chennai.  The website can be accessed at http://www/tn.nic.in/passport. 

2. A special feature of the website is the application status enquiry.  Anyone willing to know his/her application status has to enter file number and date of birth after accessing the website and the display will inform not only deficiency, if any, but also solution to fulfil the formalities required.  To start with, status will be updated every week and our endeavour is to update the status on daily basis in the coming months.

3. On 19th July, 2000, a Passport Application Collection Centre (PACC) was inaugurated at Pondicherry by Shri Ajit Kumar Panja, Hon’ble Minister of State for External Affairs.  The PACC located at the Animal Husbandry Complex, Maraimalai Adigal Salai, Pondicherry will annually serve about 30000 applicants hailing from Karaikkal, Villupuram, Cuddalore districts besides Pondicherry.  Postal applications will not be handled by PACC and they have to be forwarded to Regional Passport
Office, Chennai.  Application for all services except applications for Tatkaal, duplicate passports in lieu of lost/damaged passports, official/diplomatic passports and adopted children will be accepted in person at PACC, Pondicherry.  Authorised Travel Agents will not be dealt in PACC and their applications will be accepted only in RPO, Chennai. No cash will be accepted at PACC Pondicherry and all applications have to accompany demand drafts drawn in favour of Regional Passport Officer, Chennai payable at Chennai.
