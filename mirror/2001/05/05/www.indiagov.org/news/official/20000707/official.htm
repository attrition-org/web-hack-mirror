Shri Ajit Kumar Panja, Hon’ble Minister of State for External Affairs, will pay an official visit to Kuwait from July 9-11 on invitation from H.E. Mr. Sulaiman Majid Al-Shaheen, Minister of State for Foreign Affairs.  He will be accompanied by senior officials from the Ministry. This visit is part of an ongoing process of further strengthening bilateral relations between India and Kuwait.  During the visit, a wide
range of regional and international issues are expected to be discussed. Strengthening of bilateral economic interaction and the welfare of the Indian community will be of special interest.

In response to invitation extended by External Affairs Minister, Shri Jaswant Singh, Secretary of the General People’s Committee for Foreign Liaison and International Co-operation of Libya H.E. Mr. Abdel Rahman Shalgam, visited India from 6-8 July 2000.  He was accompanied by a high-level delegation which included representatives of Libya’s National Oil Company, Arab Foreign Investment Company, General Department for Technical Cooperation etc.

The two Ministers had detailed discussions on global and regional issues of common interest to them as members of the Non-Aligned Movement.  They discussed ways and means to further consolidate and diversify mutually beneficial cooperation between the two countries. The discussions were marked by warmth and cordiality and enabled both sides to get a better understanding of each other’s views.

H.E. Mr. Abdel Rahman Shalgam was received by the Vice President and the Prime Minister.  He also called on Raksha Mantri and Minister of Petroleum and Natural Gas.

New Delhi
July 7, 2000
