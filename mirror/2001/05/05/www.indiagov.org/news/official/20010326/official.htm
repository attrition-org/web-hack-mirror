MS. CHITRA NARAYANAN, Ambassador of India to SWEDEN, has been concurrently accredited as Ambassador of India to the REPUBLIC OF LATVIA, with residence in Stockholm.

In response to question about articles based on Jane's Intelligence Review that Pakistan is ahead of India in nuclear capability, the Spokesperson said the following :

Let it be clearly understood that India's minimum credible deterrent, based on proven indigenous technologies and under civilian command and control, effectively addresses any threat to the country's security and territorial integrity. There need be no ambiguity or any doubt in this regard.

The entire purport of the Jane's Weekly Article is to suggest an arms race kind of a scenario in the region. This is completely misplaced. Firstly, India's nuclear programme is not country specific, and secondly, we do not subscribe to any proposition of any arms race here. Besides, speculative or hypothetical propositions offer no foundation for policy constructs.
