December 24, 2000 marked the 45th anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between India and Mongolia. On this occasion, felicitation messages were exchanged at the level of the Prime Minister and External Affairs Minister.

At the invitation of Rashtrapatiji, the President of Mongolia, His Excellency Natsagiin Bagabandi, will pay a State visit to India from January 1-5, 2001. The visit is part of the ongoing high-level exchanges between India and Mongolia. It will be the first State visit to India in the New Millennium. During his visit , the President will also visit Bangalore and Bodh Gaya.
 

New Delhi
December 26, 2000
