At the invitation of External Affairs Minister, the Minister for Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Armenia, His Excellency  Mr. Vartan Oskanian is paying an official visit to India from December 3-5, 2000. He will be accompanied  by a three member delegation .

During the visit, External Affairs Minister and Foreign Minister Oskanian will hold detailed discussions on bilateral matters and on regional and international issues of mutual concern. The Armenian Minister will also call on the Indian leadership and meet other Ministers of the Government of India.

Relations between India and Armenia are historical and exceptionally warm and friendly. The traditional historical and cultural links between the two countries have produced a reservoir of goodwill and warmth for India. There have been several high level visits from Armenia to India. Then Armenian President His Excellency Mr. Levon Ter-Petrossian was in India on a State Visit in December 1995 when an Indo-Armenian Treaty on Friendship and Cooperation was signed.

Besides Delhi, Mr. Vartan Oskanian will also be visiting Agra.

New Delhi
November 30, 2000
