H.E. Mr. Igor Ivanov, Minister of Foreign affairs of the Russian Federation, will be paying an official visit to India from May 3-5, 2001 at the invitation of the External Affairs Minister. He will be accompanied by an official delegation comprising of senior officials from the Russian Government.

2. This will be the first visit to India by a Russian Foreign Minister after a gap of five years. The the Foreign Minister of the Russian Federation, H.E. Mr. Yevgeny Primakov, visited India in March 1996. The External Affairs Minister paid an official visit to the Russian Federation in June 2000.

3. Relations between India and the Russian Federation are characterized by continuity, trust and mutual understanding. Indo-Russian bilateral cooperation is multifaceted and has been elevated to a strategic partnership. President of the Russian Federation, H.E. Mr. Vladimir Putin, Paid a State Visit to India in October 200. During this visit, seventeen bilateral documents covering several areas were signed including the Declaration on Strategic Partnership between India and the Russian Federation. This Declaration has raised our relations to a qualitatively new and higher level.

4. The Indo-Russian Inter Governmental Commission on Trade, Economic, Scientific, Technological and cultural cooperation (IRIGC) has been playing an important role in the strengthening of bilateral ties between the two countries. The 7th session of the IRIGC was held in Moscow from January 12-16, 2001. It was co-Chaired by the Finance Minister from the Indian side and by Mr. Ilya Klebanov, Deputy Prime Minister of the Russian Federation, from the Russian side. During the meeting, both sides considered ways and means of increasing Indo-Russian trade and economic cooperation, which stood at US$ 1.6 billion during 1999-2000.

5. During his official visit to India, Mr. Ivanov will have extensive discussions with the External Affairs Minister on bilateral matters and on regional and international issues of mutual interest. He is also expected to call on other high level dignitaries during his visit to New Delhi.

New Delhi
April 24, 2001
