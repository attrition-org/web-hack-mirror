SHRI MUTHU VENKATARAMAN, currently Ambassador of India to the Republic of Indonesia, has been appointed as the next Ambassador of India to JORDAN in succession to Shri H.C.S. Dhody.

He is expected to take up his assignment shortly.

New Delhi
April 18, 2001

SHRI SHYAM SARAN, currently Ambassador of India to MYANMAR, has been appointed as the next Ambassador of India to REPUBLIC OF INDONESIA in succession to Shri M. Venkataraman.

He is expected to take up his assignment shortly.

New Delhi
April 18, 2001


 
SHRI VIVEK KATJU, currently Joint Secretary in the Ministry of External Affairs, has been appointed as the next Ambassador of India to MYANMAR in succession to Shri Shyam Saran.

He is expected to take up his assignment shortly.

New Delhi
April 18, 2001

SHRI AVINASH CHANDRA GUPTA, High Commissioner of India to MOZAMBIQUE, has been concurrently accredited as High Commissioner of India to the KINGDOM OF SWAZILAND, with residence in Maputo.

He is expected to take up his assignment shortly.

New Delhi
April 18, 2001
