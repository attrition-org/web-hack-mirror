Shri Atish Sinha, presently Consul General of India in Vancouver (Canada), has been appointed as the next High Commissioner of India to Nigeria, in succession to Shri Satinder Kumar Uppal.

He is expected to take up his assignment shortly.

New Delhi,
January 7, 2000.
