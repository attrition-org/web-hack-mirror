Air attacks against Lebanese civilian infrastructure targets

We have seen reports of air-attacks against Lebanese civilian infrastructure targets. These attacks are regrettable. Civilian life and property should not be made a target of attack and restraint should be exercised by all. India calls for honouring of the April 1996 Understanding. India once again reiterates its consistent commitment to Lebanon’s unity, territorial integrity and control over its entire territory in terms of UN Security Council Resolution No. 425.

New Delhi
May 8, 2000
