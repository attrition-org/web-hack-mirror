His Excellency Dr. Theo-Ben Gurirab, President of the United Nations General Assembly and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Namibia will be paying an official visit to India from August 18 to 19, 2000.

 During his visit, he will hold discussions with the External Affairs Minister on matters relating to the UN, bilateral relations between India and Namibia, and other matters of mutual concern.  Dr. Gurirab will also be calling on the Prime Minister of India.

 Dr. Gurirab will be visiting the Training Centre for UN Peace-Keeping Operations.  He will also deliver a brief talk on UN Peace-Keeping at the United Service Institution of India.

New Delhi
August 16, 2000
