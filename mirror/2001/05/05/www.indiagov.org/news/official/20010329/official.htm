The Government of India has noted with great concern recent acts of terrorism in Stavropol Territory and the Republic of Karachayevo-Cherkessia in the North Caucasus region of the Russian Federation. These criminal acts resulted in avoidable loss of life and injury to innocent people.

The Government of India strongly condemns these acts of terrorism. They are a manifestation of the larger problem of international terrorism and extremism aimed at destabilising States and established order. The people and Government of India cannot but express themselves in full solidarity with the people and Government of the Russian Federation at the moment of concern to them. India supports the steps taken by the Government of the Russian Federation in the protection of its Constitutional order and territorial integrity.

New Delhi
March 29, 2001
