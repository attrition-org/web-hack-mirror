Shri Niranjan Natverlal Desai, Ambassador-designate to Switzerland, has been concurrently accredited as Ambassador of India to the Holy See, with residence at Berne.

New Delhi
October 05, 2000


The Government of India is deeply concerned at the recent incidents of violence in Jerusalem, West Bank, Gaza and in other parts of Palestinian National Authority (PNA) and Israel.  This has led to a most regrettable loss of lives and property.  It has also vitiated the atmosphere for the Middle East Peace Process.

Issues involved in the Middle East Peace Process are of immense complexity.  That is why the overriding need now is for restraint, avoidance of provocation and of shunning all such acts as could destabilise the process.

Moves initiated by the UN Secretary General Kofi Annan for cessation of all violence and provocative acts are timely and deserve all support.

New Delhi
October 05, 2000
