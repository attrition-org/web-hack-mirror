What is NetIP

NetIP = Network Information Protection.

Ok... so what is the difference between "computer security" and "information protection"?  Essentially, the difference is one of scope.   Computer security generally deals with the securing of computers and the information that is on them.  By contrast, information protection deals with protecting information regardless of the medium... weather it is on a computer, traversing a network, stored in a file cabinet, or thrown in a dumpster.

The NetIP Mailing List
