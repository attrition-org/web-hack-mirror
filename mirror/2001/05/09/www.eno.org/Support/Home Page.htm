Friends of ENO



English National Opera is Britain’s only full-time repertory opera company. Our award wining productions encompass the full operatic repertoire and we perform to an audience of over 350,000 each season.

We have an international reputation for artistic excellence. The singers, conductors, directors, designers and musicians who work with us are among the best in the world and we nurture the operatic stars of the future. Our aim is to present music theatre at its most compelling and dynamic. We sing in English and strive to keep seat prices as low as possible. At ENO, world class opera is made accessible to everyone.
