Enjoy Opera

A low price ticket scheme for schools

Enjoy Opera has been made possible by the generous support of Christine and Irvine Laidlaw

ENO’s mission is to present music theatre at its most compelling and dynamic, we aim to make opera both affordable and accessible.

Enjoy Opera is our low-price ticket scheme for schools. Aimed at groups of 13 – 18 year olds, we offer tickets for just £5.00* per person. Through the scheme young people have the opportunity to experience the excitement of live opera, sung in English. Seats are available for two midweek performances of each opera.

*Subject to availability. The number of adults is limited to one per seven children; groups must be ten or more people.

To register on the scheme, you can email us at box.office@eno.org or call our group bookings line on 020 7845 9218
