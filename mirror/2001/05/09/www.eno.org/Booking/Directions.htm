Car Parks

If you are travelling by car, you are entitled to a reduced rate in the following car parks on presentation of your ticket stub: Spring Gardens, Cambridge Circus, Poland Street, Rochester Row and Whitcombe Street. For further details, call the Box Office: 020 7632 8300.

Reduced rates are: £5.00 for up to 5 hours, £7.50 for up to 7 hours, £10.00 for up to 9 hours all valid between 9.00am and midnight on the day of the performance only.
