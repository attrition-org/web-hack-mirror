The ENO Studio exists as a resource unique to opera in the UK - a pioneering centre at the heart of English National Opera dedicated to the development of new opera, music theatre and innovative performance. We nurture all kinds of collaboration between composer and artist, commission work of the highest quality and provide flexible opportunities for training. The Studio is based at the ENO Works, the former Shoreditch Library in Pitfield Street, Hackney, which we share with ENO's community, education and outreach department, the Baylis Programme.
