Company Background

Since 1992 World Congress LLC has been providing cutting-edge information, analysis and strategic thinking to the foremost leaders of government, business and finance through international conferences, regional seminars and publications.

Each year our flagship event, the World Economic
Development Congress, convenes over 1200 government
ministers and business leaders from over 80 countries
across the globe in a dialogue for advancing the development of infrastructure and national economies. The Congress focuses on building partnerships and developing strategies to mobilize international investment capital, technology and expertise.

The 10th Annual World Economic Development Congress will take place in June 2001 in Kuala Lumpur. For more information about the event and other World Congress conferences please visit us at www.wedckl.com/

In May 1999, we launched the inaugural World Competitive Cities Congress as a joint initiative for urban economic development with The World Bank. On May 17-19 we organized the 2nd annual World Competitive Cities Congress in Washington. With over 500 delegates the event was again a real success. For more information about the World Competitive Cities Congress please refer to http://www.competitivecities.com/
