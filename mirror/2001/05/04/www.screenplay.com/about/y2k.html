Y2K

  

All software developed by Screenplay Systems is Year 2000 (Y2K) compliant. Our software engineering team did not design any of our current programs using the two-digit dating code system. Please be aware that there are other software titles we sell that are developed outside of Screenplay Systems. You will need to contact their respective developers for Y2K compliance verification.

All current and future versions of our software (listed below) are Y2K compliant. We are also confident that older versions of our software are Y2K compliant. To ensure absolute compliance, we strongly urge that you update any versions of our software you have that are out-dated. Simply call our customer service number at (800) 847-8679 (1-800-84-STORY). International clients should call +1 (818) 843-6557. You can also purchase updates online via our secured Web site store.

If you have any other questions or concerns regarding the Y2K problem, please feel free to contact our Y2K Team via e-mail at y2k@mail.screenplay.com. You may also send requests via fax or mail. Address your correspondence to the Y2K Task Force.

Sincerely,
