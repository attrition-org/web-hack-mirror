Benefits | Company Culture | Burbank Area

Since 1982, Screenplay Systems, Inc. ("SSI") has been the leader in its field. The incredible success of the company is attributed to the very talented group of "team members" which make up our staff. Every one of us is committed to the prosperity of SSI through excellent product quality and customer service.

Current openings at Screenplay Systems:

There are currently no positions open.


SSI provides its full time employees with a generous benefits package that includes:

  • Health Coverage -- choose among a large variety of HMOs and PPOs with a comprehensive network of physicians, and coverage available for you and your family.
  • Dental Coverage -- our Delta Dental plan offers a comprehensive plan for employees and their immediate family members.
  • Disability -- we offer a comprehensive disability and workman's compensation program.
  • Tax Deferred Savings -- our 401(k) allows you to save up to 15% of your annual income on a pre-tax basis. We also offer a very generous matching plan and vesting schedule.

 


Success Through Productivity and Harmony
SSI provides a casual and stimulating environment where everyone works together as a team to achieve the company's goals. We strongly believe that both productivity and harmony are the bases for every position here at SSI. An incredibly productive employee who is not happy will tend to "bring down" the spirited atmosphere for everybody else. Likewise, a very harmonious employee who doesn't put in his or her fair share of work won't last either. We strive to maintain a strong balance between the two.

Work Hard, But Play Harder
To reward everyone for their hard work and devotion, SSI treats its employees to all sorts of social activities, like monthly movie outings for employees and guests, company-wide birthday celebrations, a raging summer beach party, and a Winter Holiday party that takes over the entire building. "All work and no play makes Jack an irritable person to work with."

 


A Wonderful Place to Live and Work
Screenplay Systems is located in the heart of the entertainment community. Walt Disney, NBC, Warner Bros., and Universal Studios are just a few miles southwest. The "Burbank Village" and the Media City Center are one to three blocks away and contain dozens of restaurants and stores including: Starbucks, Crocodile Cafe, Market City Caffe, Bobby McGee's, Tony Roma's, Blockbuster Music, Macy's, Mervyn's, CompUSA, Barnes & Noble, Ikea Furniture, and three AMC Theaters with a total of 28 screens (including very roomy and comfortable amphitheater seating). In December 1994, Los Angeles Magazine voted Burbank as the 7th best city to live in terms of crime, education, shopping, culture, and climate.
