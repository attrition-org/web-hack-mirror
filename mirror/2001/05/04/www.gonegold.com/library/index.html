The LaPorte Library
-=Games At A Glance=-

Welcome to my games collection. I often get asked the same questions repeatedly...

How many games do you have? What do you recommend? What did you think of this game? I'm headed out to buy a game, any suggestions?

When friends come by my place and look at my library of games, they usually ask the same questions. I decided to try and allow all of you into my office, and browse my collection. Here you will not only find out what games I have, but you'll also know if I've finished the game, if I'm still playing it, or if you should just pass on it all together.

I stop by many web sites throughout the day. Few webmasters ever even refer to playing games, let alone what's in their collection. What better way to get to know what type of gamer I am then to see the games I've played, and what I've enjoyed?

The library is really meant as a tool as well. I always get a kick out of reviewers that write as if their word is etched in stone, and if they didn't like a game, no one should. Remember, I like brunettes, you may like blondes. Use the library to see if we have similar tastes. If we do, then you've found yourself a great resource for games.

NOTICE: Some CD's will have more than the center hole in them. These are games I hated so much, I gave them six rounds from my Service Revolver. It's a great way to maintain my shooting skills, and give the game a little payback.

So here are all the games I own, with a brief description. I hope you find this helpful. OH, I almost forgot, brand new games take quite awhile to make it here since I have so many other things on my "To Do" list. You'll want to check out the Gold Page that is color coded in the Price Column to let you know what I recommend.

BTW: This is just a list of my CD Games. I do have some older games that are on floppy only. Since you most likely can't buy them anymore, I didn't include them.

~Rich
