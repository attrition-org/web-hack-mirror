New Demos

As New Demos are released on the Internet, this page will be your one stop to see what's new. Some Demos are plastered all over the web, so they're not too hard to find.

One thing I hope companies will shy away from in the future are "Exclusive" Demos. Certain sites get exclusives for  the first 24 hours. It's ridiculous. No one honors that. Within a few hours of being available, it usually can be found somewhere. All it does is aggravate the gamers. There are just too many of us for one site to handle the downloads. Why make us wait in line? I understand Magazines needing exclusives. That's fine. But web sites? I really hope companies take a second look at this trend.
