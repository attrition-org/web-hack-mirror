INTRODUCING GONE GOLD'S REVIEW RATINGS!

Gone Gold is focused on New Releases and letting you know that they're in stores. Sure, we want to help you make the right decisions on purchases as well, so we have the Gold Guide for you.

Head on over to the Guide and find out what every Major gaming site PLUS is saying about the game you're considering buying.

_______________________________________

PURE GOLD - This is the "Mother Lode" of Gone Gold.  A game rated PG is an "A" level product, most highly recommended, and scores a 90/100. This title is an essential purchase, and deserves a place of honor in your gaming Library.

GOLD NUGGET - A game receiving this rating has a lot going for it, is generally a "B" level title, but lacks that certain something bringing it to an essential purchase recommendation. If you like the genre it's in, you will likely want to give it a try. 80-89% rating.

UNREFINED ORE - This "C" level title has a few good ideas but due to bugs or design, just doesn't cut it. With a big patch it might improve but generally, this one is "buyer beware!" Be sure you buy it only with a return policy or due your research at the Gold Guide. 70 - 79% Score.

FOOLS GOLD - Stay away from this product! What were they thinking?  This outdated or pale imitation of a better game shouldn't have been released. It may look superficially like the real thing, but is worthless ore on close inspection. Avoid it. 69% or below.
