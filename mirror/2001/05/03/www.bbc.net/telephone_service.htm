Listed below are some of the features we offer our customers.

Call Forwarding - Lets you temporarily forward calls to another number that you select.

Call Waiting - A short tone signals that another person is trying to call you when you are on the phone. You can put one call on hold while handling the second call, or alternate between the two.

Voice Mail - This service records your incoming calls much like an answering machine. You record a message and voice mail picks up your incoming calls when you cannot answer or when your line is busy. Voice mail can be accessed by you from any phone, anywhere.

Abbreviated Dialing or Speed Dialing - Allows you to reach eight or up to 30 frequently called numbers by dialing just one or two digits instead of the entire phone number.

Three Way Calling - Allows you to talk to two different people at the same time, to add a second person to your call, or to put one call on hold and make a second call. Can be used for local and long distance calls.

Additional Phone Lines - Computer lines, teen lines, and fax lines are available.
