Division of Refuge Planning
The Division of Refuge Planning of the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service performs detailed planning before a new national wildlife refuge is established, or the boundary of an existing refuge is significantly revised and facilitates comprehensive conservation planning on existing refuges.
