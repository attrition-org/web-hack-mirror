Hecker Brown Sherry and Johnson is a civil litigation firm whose trial lawyers are experienced in all phases of national, regional and local litigation in the federal and state courts. With offices in Philadelphia, northeastern Pennsylvania and southern New Jersey and relationships with similar firms throughout the nation, our firm is committed to providing prompt, effective, cost-efficient representation to individual and corporate clients. Our firm recognizes that management and planning, as well as early dispositive resolution, like winning at trial, are matters of utmost importance to our clients.

Our attorneys actively participate in professional and community affairs and have a strong commitment to continuing legal education. We serve as officers and chairs of national, state, and local bar associations and other professional organizations. Our attorneys also participate in a number of community and civic organizations outside the field of law.

Hecker Brown Sherry and Johnson's lawyers work individually and in concert to achieve outstanding results for our clients. They are supported by paralegals and law clerks and by computerized legal research, document management, word processing and communication facilities. Our practice areas include insurance coverage, casualty defense, commercial litigation, professional and product liability, major commercial and industrial property losses, employment law, and marine and inland marine litigation.
