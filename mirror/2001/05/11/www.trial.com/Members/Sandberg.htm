FIRM HISTORY AND INFORMATION

Sandberg, Phoenix & von Gontard, P.C. was founded in 1979 with nine attorneys. The firm has grown steadily to become one of the leading law firms in the St. Louis metropolitan area and the Midwest.

Regional Representation

The firm's main offices are located in downtown St. Louis, Missouri. The firm also maintains offices in Belleville, Illinois, in recognition of the regional nature of the St. Louis economy. All of the firm's attorneys become licensed to practice in both Missouri and Illinois.

Client Representation

Superior legal service, a cost conscious approach to the delivery of services, and client satisfaction are hallmarks of Sandberg, Phoenix & von Gontard, P.C. The firm recognizes that in a competitive economy, legal services must be delivered efficiently and economically.  The firm provides detailed billing statements to clients and works with its clients to contain costs consistent with the nature of the particular case or project. During and at the conclusion of each matter, the firm sends questionnaires to its client requesting evaluation of the service provided by the firm.

Practice Development

Sandberg, Phoenix & von Gontard, P.C. recruits outstanding students from the top law schools of the region and the country. Most of the firm's attorneys were members or editors of their law school journals, and many served as judicial clerks before joining the firm. The firm sponsors a summer intern program under which outstanding students work for the firm, usually between their second and third years of law school. The summer program both accelerates the student's understanding of the practice of law and permits the firm to identify superior individuals who will become members of the firm after graduation. Internal continuing legal education programs, attendance at professional seminars, and training with senior attorneys assure continuing professional development.

Professional Affiliations

The firm is honored to have been nominated and elected into membership of The Network of Trial Law Firms. The Network of Trial Law Firms is a national organization comprised of a select number of premier law firms from around the country with practices concentrated in civil litigation.

 

A FULL RANGE OF LEGAL SERVICES

Litigation

The firm has one of the largest litigation staffs of any law firm in the region.  Litigators engage in cases throughout the country and represent both national and international firms as well as local businesses and individuals. The litigation practice includes commercial litigation, medical malpractice defense, product liability litigation, and personal injury defense.

Product Liability

The firm is one of the leading product liability defense firms in the region. Product liability clients include automobile makers, chemical and drug manufacturers, and heavy equipment manufacturers and operators.

Health Care

The firm represents numerous hospitals and medical insurance providers in Missouri and Illinois. The firm provides a full range of services to its health care clients, including defense of medical malpractice claims and counseling on health care law matters including state and federal regulations regarding financing, reimbursement, and other issues involved in health care delivery.

Transportation

Sandberg, Phoenix & von Gontard, P.C. maintains an active admiralty practice and represents marine carriers and barge operators throughout the central United States. Attorneys provide aviation services including liability defense and regulatory representation. The firm also provides defense services to railroads and trucking companies.

Banking and Finance

The firm represents numerous financial institutions in Missouri and Illinois. Services include consumer credit, commercial lending, real estate lending, state and federal regulatory compliance, form designs, product development, bankruptcies, workouts, tax exempt financing, relationships with regulatory agencies, and general counseling in all aspects of credit extension and banking law.

Corporate

The firm's litigation, business and financial services are coordinated to provide a wide range of services to business clients. Services including corporate formation, financing, tax planning, shareholder agreements, Chapter S corporations, partnerships,  sole proprietorships and business litigation.

Bankruptcy

Lawyers in the firm's bankruptcy department are experienced in representing creditors and creditors' committees, as well as corporate and individual debtors in bankruptcy cases and workouts.
