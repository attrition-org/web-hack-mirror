Founded in 1926 in Detroit, Michigan, our firm has grown steadily through the years. Today, we practice in seven cities -- Ann Arbor, Bloomfield Hills, Detroit, Grand Rapids and Lansing in Michigan, as well as Chicago and Washington, D.C. In addition, we are affiliated with a law firm in Bologna, Italy -- a gateway for our clients to the European Union.

Our growth has been the result of many factors, foremost among them the success of our clients and their increasing need for experienced legal representation at state, national and international levels. Our 200-plus attorneys counsel clients from across the United States and abroad, ranging from Fortune 500 multinational corporations, major regional companies, financial institutions and hospitals, to start-up companies, sole proprietorships and many individuals affiliated with those companies.

To meet the extensive range of our clients' needs, we work together in interdisciplinary groups and teams. This approach helps us to respond rapidly, efficiently and cost-effectively to the most complex situations. A sophisticated interstate computer network unites all of us regardless of office location, so that each lawyer can draw on the resources of the entire firm to address a client's legal concerns. In many cases, our network is linked directly into our clients' systems for ease of information exchange.
