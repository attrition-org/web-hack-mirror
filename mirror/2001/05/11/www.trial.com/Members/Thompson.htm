About Thompson Hine

Thompson Hine lawyers serve as counselors, advisors and advocates to a full spectrum of clients ranging from major public and private corporations to financial institutions, governments, nonprofit organizations, venture capitalists and individual entrepreneurs. We have the experience and resources to handle the most challenging legal issues our clients may face.

We train our litigators to be first-chair lawyers and our transactional lawyers to be partners with our clients in the deal-making process. Our ability to respond to clients’ needs is enhanced by interaction and teamwork among our lawyers across practice areas and offices.

We attract and retain exceptional people by building and maintaining a stimulating and diverse environment. We pride ourselves on our ability to deliver extraordinary legal services through a partnership dedicated to the principles of professionalism, teamwork and collegiality.
