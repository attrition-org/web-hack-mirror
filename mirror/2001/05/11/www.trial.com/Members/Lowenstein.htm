Lowenstein Sandler is one of New Jersey's largest law firms with approximately 140 attorneys and a national reputation for excellence.  The firm's reputation rests upon the exceptional talents of its attorneys and their dedication to meeting clients' needs.  Lowenstein's large and diverse law practice provides a full range of legal services to clients engaged in virtually all areas of business and industry.

With more than 60 attorneys, Lowenstein's litigation department has experience in litigating a variety of
diversified matters, such as:

  • contract claims
  • defense of proceedings instituted by federal and state regulatory agencies
  • claims arising under zoning and other municipal ordinances
  • collection actions
  • disputes with vendors and customers
  • defense of business executives and government officials accused of white-collar crimes
  • defense of business and executives accused of civil and criminal violations of environmental laws
  • real estate tax appeals for fee owners and secured parties
  • employment disputes relating to restrictive covenants, privacy of employees, use of confidential information and trade secrets, "golden parachutes," contract terminations, discriminations, and wrongful discharge
  • disputes between members of partnerships and of closely-held corporations, including "dead-lock" proceedings
  • claims for misrepresentation, fraud and breach of warranties and covenants related to acquisitions of businesses and properties
  • claims arising from the termination of franchises or distributorships
  • copyright and trademark claims
  • antitrust and unfair competition claims
  • insurance coverage disputes, particularly in the area of environmental law and toxic torts
  • defense of product liability
  • prosecution and defense of injunction applications
