Beirne, Maynard & Parsons is devoted exclusively to civil trial and appellate practice in the state and federal courts. We successfully represent Fortune 500 clients in courts across the country and are frequently asked near trial to take over troubled litigation in Texas and other states begun by others.

Key to our successes is extensive trial experience, thorough individual case preparation and intensive strategic planning.  Regular consultation with our clients, including through video-conferencing, assures up-to-date reporting and meaningful client input as our cases move toward resolution.

To provide cost-effective representation, we use computer technologies, litigation support services and jury research. Our lawyers are supported by a firm-wide computer network linking lawyers, paralegals and other support staff inside and outside our offices. Litigation science 		techniques, including focus groups, mock jury trials and shadow juries, are frequently employed to test arguments and juror dispositions. Trial presentation technologies, including animations and computer-generated demonstrative evidence are employed where appropriate.

We serve clients in every industry, including aircraft, automobiles, chemicals, consumer and other products, construction, electrical, financial, health care, insurance, petroleum and other industries. Our practice and experience extend to all areas of civil litigation and appeal, including business, banking, consumer credit, construction, employment, environmental, products, personal injury, mass tort, real estate, securities, toxic tort, trademark and other intellectual property litigation.
