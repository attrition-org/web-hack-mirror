Kravit, Gass, Hovel & Leitner is a boutique law firm concentrating in aggravated litigation and bankruptcy. All members of the firm have backgrounds of outstanding achievement, including, as to some, election to the American College of Trial Lawyers, prior federal or state supreme court clerkships, law review or past service as an Assistant U.S. Attorney. Our firm relies on our talented staff and state-of-the-art computer support to deliver high quality service in complex litigation.

Our clients seek creative, cost-conscious approaches to dispute resolution and, as a result, we are experienced practitioners before trial and appellate courts, juries, arbitrators, hearing boards, and ADR forums. Extensive trial experience helps our firm to focus on ultimate fact-finder issues from the outset, thereby reducing expense and increasing trial effectiveness. That experience also helps us to deal with difficult trial problems, such as explaining complex ideas through visual and demonstrative evidence, and dealing with jury bias and sympathy. Skill in the courtroom translates to favorable settlements outside the courtroom. 		Our firm has special expertise in product liability and toxic tort; fraud and RICO; bad faith claims, insurance and environmental coverage issues; employment law; patent and other intellectual property; fair dealership; and commercial claims. Our substantial white collar criminal practice includes representation of persons and companies accused of, and those victimized by, fraud.
