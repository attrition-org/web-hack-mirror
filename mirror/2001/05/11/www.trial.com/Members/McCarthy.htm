About the Firm

McCarthy Tétrault is Canada's largest law firm, consisting of more than 670 lawyers located in seven Canadian cities, and in New York and London, England.

McCarthy Tétrault is a fully-integrated and full-service law firm comprising lawyers, patent and trade-mark agents and other professionals who are committed to providing the highest quality of legal services in a timely and cost-effective manner. Reflecting the duality of Canada we practise in both French and English and act in local, national and international matters in the context of the Canadian legal system, which is based on common and civil law and federal and provincial legislation. We provide legal services through relationships built on trust and confidence, while drawing on the sophisticated knowledge and experience of lawyers throughout the firm.

The firm has experience in virtually every area of legal practice, including business law and securities, litigation and class actions, Internet and e-commerce, intellectual property and technology law, labour and employment, estates and trusts, and tax. In addition, our practice is strongly developed in the areas of pension, government relations, communications, environment, health, licensing, trade, immigration, entertainment and real estate. We provide advice on legal and policy issues to private, public and non-profit sector clients and governments. We also negotiate and draft transaction documents and legislation, as well as participate in litigation. Our proven skills and experience coupled with innovation, fairness and objectivity allow us to compete with the best law firms in the world.

As part of a large full-service firm, our lawyers have access to significant legal resources and support services.

Our Litigation Practice

McCarthy Tétrault has a pre-eminent litigation practice. The strength of our Litigation Section is driven by the breadth of our national litigation practice. With more than 170 lawyers nationally practising civil and criminal litigation, we represent clients at all levels of the federal and provincial court systems, in every province, before regulatory and administrative tribunals, in commercial arbitrations and in all types of civil, commercial, constitutional and international law litigation.

Our Litigation Section has repeatedly been recognized as one of the leading litigation departments in Canada and members of the Section similarly receive ongoing recognition as pre-eminent counsel in their fields. Such accolades have been proffered in publications such as the International Financial Law Review, in the Guide to the World's Leading Litigation Lawyers, and in LEXPERT, the leading Canadian legal directory. This recognition is occasioned by the strength and depth of the McCarthy Tétrault Litigation Section. Courts throughout Canada rely upon representation by McCarthy Tétrault to mean sound legal analysis, a firm understanding of the most complex of cases, and a credible and trustworthy presentation to the court. As a result, McCarthy Tétrault litigators are accorded respect and receive attention by judges and arbitrators across the country.

We have for many years represented a national association of professionals with respect to negligence claims and professional discipline matters. As such, we have a depth of knowledge and experience representing the unique needs of professionals unrivalled in Canada. In addition, our litigation lawyers have built on this experience and have developed extensive experience in a wide range of litigious matters involving corporate law, construction, professional negligence, product liability, intellectual property, banking, high tech, securities, bankruptcy, municipal law, environmental law, consumer protection, insurance, and telecommunications.
