Firm Profile

Founded by the co-chairs of Bogle & Gates' Litigation Department, Corr Cronin offers its clients the sophistication, talent and experience of the largest law firms combined with the responsiveness and personal service of a smaller law firm. The firm’s practice is limited to complex and sophisticated litigation and trial work. Corr Cronin’s practice areas include commercial litigation, products liability, intellectual property, class actions, toxic torts, employment, construction, oil and gas, and insurance coverage.

Corr Cronin represents a wide spectrum of clients – from individuals and closely held businesses to some of the nations largest corporation.

First rate client service combined with a wide range of trial experience are the firm’s trademark.
