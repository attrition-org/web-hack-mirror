Morgenstein & Jubelirer opened on March 1, 1982 with three partners and five associates. Since then, we have grown to more than 30 lawyers, all exclusively dedicated to litigation. Our attorneys come from the country's top law schools and have distinguished themselves academically and professionally. For close to 20 years we have served clients comprised of multi-national corporations, emerging companies, and individuals, in a wide range of matters throughout the Western United States. 

Our firm is especially noted for the jury trial experience of our attorneys.  During the course of their careers, the partners in our firm have tried well over 100 cases to verdict. In addition, all of our attorneys are well-versed in the practice of alternative dispute resolution, including arbitration and mediation.

With 30 litigators and the ability to call upon contract attorneys and other professionals, we are able to staff matters varying in size and complexity with teams that ensure legal service that is both excellent and cost-effective. Without the overhead of many of our larger competitors, we are able to deliver the highest quality work product at a substantially lower cost. We offer many different forms of billing arrangements and actively work with every client to create the billing solution that best fits each engagement. 

Preferring to maintain the flexibility that allows us to optimize the staffing for each matter, our firm is not divided into formal practice groups.  Instead, our attorneys concentrate in multiple areas of litigation and counseling, allowing M&J to offer its clients not only breadth but considerable depth of legal expertise as well. The practice areas in which
we have had significant experience include alternative dispute resolution, antitrust and unfair competition, asbestos litigation, construction, employment and labor, environmental, insurance coverage, intellectual property, products liability, real estate, securities litigation, toxic torts, and federal criminal defense.
