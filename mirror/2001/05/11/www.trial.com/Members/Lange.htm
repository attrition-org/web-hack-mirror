Lange, Simpson, Robinson & Somerville was formed in 1919 when Ray Lange and James Simpson began practicing law together in Birmingham. From the very beginning, the firm has been characterized by a diversity of people and ideas.

Today Lange, Simpson, Robinson & Somerville LLP is a general civil practice law firm with a major litigation practice. The firm has offices in Anniston, Birmingham, and Huntsville, serving all of Alabama. Lange, Simpson represents businesses, individuals, financial institutions, governmental entities, and utilities, with a full range of services. Civil litigation in the State and Federal courts is a significant part of the firm's practice.		  Also, the firm has expertise in banking, corporate and commercial, labor and employment, real estate, bankruptcy, employee benefits, environmental litigation and regulation, securities, and health care law.
