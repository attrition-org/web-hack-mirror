Baker Sterchi Cowden & Rice is a trial law firm that dedicates its entire firm resources to the defense and management of civil litigation at national, regional and local levels. We try cases to win but are alert to the potential for favorable settlements and make early and continual assessments of each matter's settlement prospects in addition to advocating ADR where appropriate.

Our negotiation skills are constantly honed as comprehensive trial preparation improves our clients' positions.

Our firm pays close attention to the costs and economics of legal service and we work to maintain reasonable fees and excellent results by consulting with clients in advance on staffing and strategy issues so that the best combination of client and law firm resources can be brought to bear on our clients' litigation problems. Our commitment to litigation technology and continuing education advances our goal of excellent results with reasonable rates and minimized total costs of litigation. 		Our firm serves national, regional and local clients with national coordination and litigation management services in the areas of product liability, medical malpractice, commercial, aviation, toxic tort, environmental, construction, fidelity and surety, premises liability, architects' and engineers' malpractice, insurance coverage, employment and general litigation in all state and federal courts.
