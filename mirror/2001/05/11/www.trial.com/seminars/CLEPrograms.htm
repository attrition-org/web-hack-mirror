CLE CREDIT

The Network's CLE programs have been approved by every state bar authority to which application has been made.  Application is made by The Network based on registration and attendance at the seminar.  Typically our two-day programs have been approved for 8 to 11 credit hours, depending on content  and state requirements.  Our first one-day program was approved for between 5.5 and 7.0 credit hours, depending on the state approving the program.

For CLE credit, attendees must complete attendance forms provided at each program.  The Network handles the application process and notifies attendees when approval is received from approving state agencies.
