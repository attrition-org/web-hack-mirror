  • What ListServs Are and Why We Have Them

    A LISTSERVer is a computer system that facilitates communication among many persons (called "subscribers") who are interested in a particular topic (or, for example, practicing in a particular substantive area) and sign-up for ("subscribe" to or join) the "list."

    A LISTSERVer creates and manages e-mail communications within a virtual community whose members share an interest in the topic of the particular LISTSERVer to which those members have subscribed or joined.  For that purpose, we are using software called "LISTSERV" which is one of the leading products for creating and managing such virtual communities.

  • Our Lists are BANKRUPTCY LAW, EMPLOYMENT LAW, FINANCE LAW, FRANCHISE LAW, GENERAL, HEALTHCARE LAW, INSURANCE LAW, MARKETING and TOXIC TORT LAW

    For general matters, we have a non-topic-specific "GENERAL" list (i.e., any topic in your e-mail header is okay).

    We Plan to add more lists, depending on the interests of our member firms.
