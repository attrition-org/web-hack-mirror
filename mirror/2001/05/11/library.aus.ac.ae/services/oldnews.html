   Archive of Library News Items

The most recent library news items from our homepage appear below in reverse chronological order:

 

1 April 2001-- Tired of scanning images to a floppy disk? Well, it's now possible to save images to a CD. Just bring in your own recordable CD and ask a librarian to show you how to do it.

17 March 2001-- Just in time to complete your papers. The library website now has History and English Literature Subject Guides. Check out the great links and get an A on your next paper. LIbrary Subject Guides

17 JAN 01 -- Now OPEN 7 DAYS a WEEK.  The AUS Library is now open to serve you every day of the Spring Semester.  In addition to our regular weekday and Friday hours, the library will be open from 9 am to 5:30 pm on Thursdays. 
Full list of open hours

06-NOV-00 -- General Science Database and expanded ABI Inform are now available. General Science covers biology, chemistry, physics and more.  ABI Inform now includes over 1000 journals in the Global Edition.  Both are available through ProQuest. Try them!

04-OCT-00 -- New at the library!  JSTOR database is now available. JSTOR is a unique digital archive of over 100 core scholarly journals, with some material reaching back over 100 years. Economics, History, Humanities and other Social Sciences are covered.  Try JSTOR

20-SEPT-00 -- Whole books online!  Check out the UAE's first academic "netLibrary". Available only to AUS students, faculty and staff, follow this link to visit the AUS netLibrary.  Find out what e-books are all about.

14-SEP-00 — It's "ON RESERVE" in the library.  Your professor may have readings or assignments on reserve in the library.  It's easy to use them.  Just ask at the circulation desk near the front door, present your ID, and use the materials for up to 2 hours. 

23-AUG-00 — Announcing the first outdoor book drop in the UAE!  Books may now be returned 24 hours a day to our book drop on the walkway near the University bookstore.  Click here for details and a picture of the amazing book drop.

16-AUG-00 — Extended loans are now available to AUS faculty!  Books can now be borrowed for up to 90 days.  See our "Loan Policies" for details.

12-AUG-00 — New music has arrived!  Due to popular demand, more CDs of Arabic music have been added to the collection.  New popular, jazz and classical titiles are in stock, too.

18-JUL-00 — Faculty may now borrow journals for up to three days.  Current issues and certain titles are excluded.  Visit the library for specific information.
