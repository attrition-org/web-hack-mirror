WELCOME ABOARD!

Welcome to the USNS Rappahannock website. We're a government owned and operated  fleet oiler of Military Sealift Command, operating in the Western Pacific.  Since we are a Department of Defense  activity, please read this warning.

Our primary mission is to provide ship and aircraft fuel to Navy ships while underway. We also carry dry and refrigerated cargo, passengers, and mail for delivery to Navy ships at sea. Our crew consists of 78 Civilian Mariners, 23 U.S. Navy personnel and 4 Cadets. USNS Rappahannock was delivered to the U.S. Navy in December 1995 and has been in continuous service since then.

USNS Rappahannock is currently at Sea.

We have conducted 1053 replenishments since delivery and 105 replenishment so far this year.
