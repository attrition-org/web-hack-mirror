Color Guard 

The Color Guard team at Naval Air Reserve San Diego is here to provide service at any Retirement, Change of Command or Civil Activity.

To make reservations for the team to perform at your event, please email AK2 H. DeSousa at narcgbm@yahoo.com.  Please have all pertinent information, such as date, time and uniform requested for the event, readily available.  A memo must be drafted in order to receive support.  The memo may either be emailed or faxed to 619.545.7654.

Thank you for your interest in us and please continue to enjoy a meritorious day!
