Command Master Chief

 

CMDMC(SW) Boyd R. Briggs

Master Chief Boyd Briggs grew up in San Diego, California.  He joined the Navy in September 1978 and completed Recruit Training in San Diego.  He graduated from Personnelman "A" School at Naval Air Station Meridian, Mississippi in February 1979.

Duty assignments include USS Orleck (DD 886), Personnel Support Activity Detachment, Naval Air Station Miramar, Naval Reserve Center, La Crosse, Wisconsin, Commander, Mine Squadron Two, Naval Reserve Personnel Center, Naval and Marine Corps Reserve Readiness Center, San Diego, Commander, Destroyer Squadron One and Chief of Naval Operations (N095) Staff.

Master Chief Briggs is a graduate of the U. S. Navy Senior Enlisted Academy.  His personal awards include the Meritorious Service Medal, Navy and Marine Corps Commendation Medal (two awards) and Navy and Marine Corps Achievement Medal (3 awards).
