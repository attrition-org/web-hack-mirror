MISSION


COMTACGRU 1 0194 (CTG1-0194) is the operational and joint/interoperability training senior for the Reserve TACRON squadrons. CTG1 0194 reports to NAVAIRES San Diego for administrative purposes and to COMTACGRU 1 for operational requirements.

The mission of the Reserve group is to provide operational assistance for the Reserve squadrons and to oversee joint training. The Reserve group monitors the squadrons Long Range Training Plan's and assists, recommends and standardizes the squadron's joint operational activities. In addition, CTG1-0194 directs and facilitates the accomplishment of joint/interoperability training activities. CTG1-0194 also serves a liaison function to provide COMTACGRU 1 with a conduit for effective two way communication between active forces and reserve augment units. The Reserve group insures that all joint/interoperability training is properly documented in accordance with current directives.

CTG1-0194 is also utilized by COMTACGRU 1 and its active squadrons to supplement and assist in their operational requirements by providing technical support for TSTA 4 inspections and team training evaluations. Active requirements placed on Reserve squadrons are presented to the Reserve group to assess feasibility and capabilities.

CTG1 0194 operational training focuses upon the coordination of all air support during amphibious operations. This coordination involves all phases of an amphibious operation: planning, embarkation, rehearsal, movement and assault. The absolute necessity to maintain air superiority within the Amphibious Objective Area requires CTG1 0194 to maintain the highest levels of training and readiness in order to control all fixed and rotary wing aircraft in support of amphibious operations. In addition, CTG1 0194 will emphasize joint/interoperability training, and be operationally trained to conduct joint forces air component commander functions (JFACC).

Upon mobilization in wartime or contingency operations, CTG1 0194 will report to COMTACGRU 1 to augment his staff in the conduct of amphibious operations.
