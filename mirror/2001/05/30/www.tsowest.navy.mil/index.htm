Command Mission:

Our mission is to provide legal service to the

Department of the Navy and the commands it

comprises. We represent the United States of

America in courts-martial, and we assist

commanding officers in decision-making.

To be useful, our legal advice will be timely

and accurate. Our goal is to solve problems,

not just answer questions. We will follow-up

and follow through, until completion.
