Trial Service Office West

 

 

 

 

The Command Services Department at Trial Service Office West is dedicated to providing the best legal support to Pacific Fleet assets in the Southern California area that do not have a judge advocate assigned.  We can address matters from non-judicial punishment and courts-martial to ethics.  The department is manned by highly experienced and trained personnel who have had successful tours as trial counsel, defense counsel, staff judge avocate, and paralegal.  Don't hesitate to contact us regarding all of your official legal concerns.
