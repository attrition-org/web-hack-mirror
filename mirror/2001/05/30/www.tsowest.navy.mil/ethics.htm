JAG Ethics-Gram 98-4 of September 1998 states that commands no longer are required to provide in-person training for 450 filers every year (the annual in-person training requirement remains in place for SF-278 filers).  You may satisfy the training requirements by providing 450 filers with written materials and allowing them to review these materials during official duty time. 

Please select from one of the following documents:

Standards of Conduct and Government Ethics Training Manual (qualifies as written training materials for 450 filer review to satisfy training requirements)

OGE Form 450 and Instructions:  www.usoge.gov/ogeforms/450form.pdf   (Confidential Financial Disclosure Form -- requires Acrobat Reader)

OGE 450 Review Guide:  www.oge.gov/pubs/oge450gd.pdf (requires Acrobat Reader)

1997 Ethics Workshop Deskbook:  www.defenselink.mil/dodgc/defense_ethics/97deskbook

Post Goverment Employment Questionnare  (download, answer, and forward to TSO West with a request for services letter to obtain review and advice letter)

Twenty Basic Rules for Personnel Leaving DOD

Other written training materials are available at: www.defenselink.mil/dodgc/defense_ethics/index.html
