Medical & Dental Information

For active duty members assigned to NAVSECGRUACT Denver, military care is provided through the 821st Medical Squadron (821 MDS), a small Air Force medical clinic located on Buckley Air Force Base. This clinic is open from 0730 - 1630, Monday through Fridays. Military sick call is held from 0715 - 0830.

This clinic is not yet adequately staffed to also support family members, so, at present, they only see active duty personnel. Family members are referred to civilian medical providers contracted under the TRICARE Prime network. The managed care support contractor (MCSC) that coordinates and pays for medical care in this area (the TRICARE Central Region) is the TriWest HealthCare Alliance. TRICARE was implemented in this region on 1 April 1997.

If you have family members that accompanied you to Denver, to ensure a smooth enrollment in TRICARE here, do the following: (1) When visiting PSD, verify that your each of family member’s enrollments in DEERS is current and accurate; (2) Contact the TRICARE MCSC from your previous region via telephone to disenroll from their region; (3) Visit the TRICARE Service Center at the former Fitzsimmons Medical Center to complete enrollment of your family members in the TRICARE Central Region.

Active Duty dental care is provided to Sailors through Dentrust Dental, a contracted dental office. Your sponsor will help you drop off your dental records at Dentrust. This office is located on 6th Avenue, about a half a mile from the 6th Avenue Gate to Buckley Air National Guard Base. Family members eligible for dental care fall under the TRICARE Family Member Dental Plan (TFMDP) through United Concordia. Numerous dentists in the Metro Denver area participate in this plan and the choice of provider is left up to each service member.
