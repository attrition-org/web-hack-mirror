Buckley Child Development Center (CDC)
NSGA Denver Child Care Info (civilian providers)

This four-page document includes the FY2000 schedule of fees, as well as the application form needed to get on the waiting list for a slot.  To apply for on-base care:
 

  1. Print out the fourth page of the Buckley Child Development Center (CDC) document, titled: "DEPARTMENT OF DEFENSE CHILD DEVELOPMENT PROGRAM - REQUEST FOR CARE RECORD"
  2. Fill this form out COMPLETELY.
  3. Fax it, along with a copy of your orders, to (303) 677-6176, DSN 877-6176, ATTN: Janice Hollen.
  4. Provide your Internet/NIPRNET e-mail address and fax (commercial and unclas), as well as your sponsor's Internet/NIPRNET e-mail address, fax, and voice phone numbers on the cover sheet for confirmation.
  5. Have your sponsor check periodically check with the CDC, at (303) 677-6174, to verify the status of your application, and your position on the waiting list.
