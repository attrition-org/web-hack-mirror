Welcome Aboard!  I am looking forward to meeting you in the near future.  My most important priority is assisting you and you family in a smooth transition from your present duty station to the USS SACRAMENTO(AOE 1).  Please take the time to do one or all of the following:

 

v     Call ahead.  The Quarter Deck number is (360)476-5536 or if the ship is out of homeport, please contact Naval Station Bremerton (360)476-0126 and they will assist you until our return.

 

v     Let us know your plans—arrival time and mode of transportation.  We will assist in making arrangements to either pick you up or meet you at a designated place and time.  If you arrive at SEATAC Airport-visit the USO, located in the airport (206)433-5438, then give us a call and let us know that you are in the area.  From the airport you, take the KITSAP AIRPORTER transportation service located at the far end of the baggage claim area.  This trip is approximately 45-90 minutes depending on traffic and weather.  This service costs approximately $15.00 and will bring you Naval Station Bremerton, where the ship is homeported.  You will be reimbursed after you file your travel claim.  DO NOT TAKE A TAXI!!! 

 

v     Write us a letter or send us an e-mail!  Let us know if you need information—it will be mailed to you as soon as possible.

 

v     Your sponsor will contact you soon if they have not already.

 

v     Visit the Relocation Assistance Manager (RAM) at the Nearest Family Service Center (FSC).  The Center will provide information about the Bremerton and Washington areas, and support services that include housing, childcare, schools, spouse employment, and more.  If you are unable to obtain information from a FSC then you can obtain information as above through a Standard Installation Topic Exchange Service (SITES) printout over the Internet at http://www.dmdc.osd.mil/sites

 

v     Report in Service Uniform of the Day

 

Shipmate, we’re looking forward to meeting you and hearing from you soon!
