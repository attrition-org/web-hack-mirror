COMMAND MASTER CHIEF

(SURFACE WARFARE/ AIR WARFARE)

LINDA C. SCHWIND

 

Master Chief Linda C. Schwind joined the Navy on 29 September 1976 in Hanford, CA and completed Recruit Training in Orlando, FL.

Master Chief Schwind's shore duty assignments include Naval Air Station Alameda, CA; Fleet Area and Control Facility, San Diego, CA; Fleet Training Group Pearl Harbor, HI; U. S. Navy Joint Services Activity, Tokyo, Japan; Commander Fleet Activities Yokosuka, Japan; Puget Sound Naval Shipyard; and Naval Station Bremerton, WA. She completed Air Traffic Control "A" and "C" schools, NATTC Memphis, TN; Master at Arms School, Ft McClellan, AL; and is a graduate of the Senior Enlisted Academy, Class 91, Newport, RI. Master Chief Schwind served in USS Kiska (AE 35) and USS Abraham Lincoln (CVN 72) as Chief Master at Arms prior to reporting to her present assignment in USS Sacramento (AOE 1).

Master Chief Schwind awards include the Navy and Marine Corps Commendation Medal with two gold stars, Navy and Marine Corps Achievement Medal, Meritorious Unit Commendation with Bronze Star, Good Conduct Medal with Silver Star, National Defense Medal, Humanitarian Service Medal, Overseas Service Ribbon with two Bronze Stars and Expert Pistol Medal.
