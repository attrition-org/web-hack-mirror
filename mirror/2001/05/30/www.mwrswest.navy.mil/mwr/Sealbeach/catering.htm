Catering

Catering and Party Planning Services

Gateway Restaurant / Catering Tent
(562) 431-4956
If you're looking for a pleasant, relaxing country club setting for your next event, then you definitely want to consider the amenities the Gateway Restaurant has to offer. The restaurant, surrounded by gorgeous landscaping, has the perfect accommodations for office parties, business meetings and wedding receptions. Services range from light hor d'oeuvres to full elegant sit-down dinners. The patio area is especially set up for festive outdoor barbecues. Whether it's a meeting for 25 or a reception for 150, the Gateway has the expertise to make your event a success.



Bunker 33 Recreation Center
(562) 431-4956
You're invited to enjoy a pleasant tropical atmosphere by hosting your next event at Bunker 33. This facility can accommodate up to 150 guests in the main dining room and 70 in the outdoor patio area. There is a fully stocked bar in the main dining room with the availability of a dance floor, utility hook-ups for DJ's or bands, Karoake / CD stereo equipment, big screens and a portable bar and barbecue pits for the patio.



Barney's Beach House
Bldg#: 33 (Office)

Hours: Sat, Sun, Holidays: Sunrise to Sunset
Phone: (562) 626-7106 / DSN 873-7106
Fax: (526) 626-7272 / DSN 873-7272
Interested in a little fun in the sun? The Beach House may be the place for you with its own shaded patio, barbecue pits and a private beach perfect for all your recreational needs. Enjoy the availability of utility hookups, equipment rentals, a restroom facility with showers, a lifeguard and event coordinator. The Beach House can be reserved for weekends and holidays from 10 am-7 pm.
