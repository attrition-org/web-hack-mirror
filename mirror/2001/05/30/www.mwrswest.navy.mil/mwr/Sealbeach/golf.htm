NOTICE TO PATRONS

HIGH COSTS COMPEL MWR TO INCREASE GOLF FEES

To partially offset the rising costs of labor, goods and supplies, and utilities at the Seal Beach Navy Golf Course, greens fees will be increased in accordance with the following pricing schedule on 1 June.

While we regret the need to raise prices, we must partially defray the higher costs that are being passed along to us. Despite these increases, our market analysis shows that our golf program remains a very good bargain for our patrons when compared to costs of golf at other courses in the area, particularly for our junior active duty military personnel and those who choose to purchase our multi-play punch cards. With regard to the use of punch cards, they will continue to be offered for sale at the current price through 31 May for those persons who have six or fewer punches remaining on their current card.

As of June 1, the following prices will be charged at the Seal Beach Navy Golf Course:
