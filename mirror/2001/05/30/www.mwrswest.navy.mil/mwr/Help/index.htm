Need Help Finding What You Want? On this page you will find helpful instructions to finding your way around the MWR Web site.

On the home page and all other pages in the site you will find
two options for finding what you what:
  • 1) A"Search By Region" map of all MWR Southwest Region Bases offers you the ability to enter a region and see what it has to offer, click on the region of your choice and be taken there.

  • 2) Also offered on all pages of the site is
    "Search By Category", which is a quick way to search out what region contains certain topics you wish to know more about, click the pulldown menu and select a catagory you wish to find out more on (within the selected page will be found a listing of all regions which contain that category).
• Other navigation contained in the site is the "Home" button, located in the top blue bar, that will bring you back to the first (home) page of the site.

• Located on the left-hand side is the "Comments" button. Have any comments or questions? Send an Email to us via this page.

• At the bottom of every page are links to "Vision", "Guiding Principles" and "Privacy & Security Notice". These pages contain information related to their respective titles.
