Car Wash Now Open
NAS Lemoore's newest addition to its MWR program is a new car wash featureing the most modern facility and equipment available for personal car care.

The facility is open 24 hours, 7 days a week with five wash stations, including a RV stall, five vacuum stations, resale vending and change machines, all conveniently located next to the Auto Hobby Center.
