2. Data Collection Deadline. Most of the PAT Leaders have submitted their first data call, which included Organization Charts, Summary of Functional Area and the instruction/directives scrub.

3. Data Collection Storage.

  • The FA teams were reminded to ensure that they retain copies of their data submissions. Additionally, Ms. Christopherson recommended that the FA teams submit a hard copy of their data calls and that they also E-mail the information to Ms. Acevedo. Her E-mail address is: acevedo.yolanda@ns.cnrsw.navy.mi
    Diskette’s submitted with the data will not be returned.
  • The recently vacated Payroll Office will be used to house the Contractor and store all data submitted for the FA. Bookshelves, desks, chairs, etc., are needed to furnish this office. Ms. Christopherson asked the group if they have any of the above listed articles that can be spared to contact Ms. Acevedo at 556-7165.

4. Review of all Data Input. On 31 Jul 00, Mr. Young, Ms. Widman, Mr. Jose, and Ms. Christopherson will review the data submitted. PAT Leaders will be contacted if clarification is needed.

5. Key Processes. FA Team Leaders were asked to update their POA&M’s functional area key processes and submit the data to Ms. Acevedo by COB 2 Aug 00.

6. Overview of Support Services FA Meeting.

  • Status of Statement of Work (SOW). Ms. Christopherson stated that the SOW has been delayed pending Executive Steering Committee (ESC) approval. In addition, oral presentations from prospective contractors to assist in the Support Services SOW will be a requirement. Ms. Christopherson will represent MWR on the selection board. Initial plans are to have a contractor on board by 1 Sep 00.
  • Team Charter (PAT’s). Support Services is in the process of developing a Team Charter for the PAT’s. All Program Managers will review and approve the charter prior to distribution.
  • Support Services Website. The web site to inform interested personnel of the Support Services FA is in the process of being put on line. A FA Newsletter and all FA meeting minutes will be posted on the site. Once the web site is on line, PAT Leaders are asked distribute all FA information to any MWR employee without computer access. A question and answer page will be included in every Newsletter issue. Employees who wish to submit questions for the Subj: FUNCTIONALITY ASSESSMENT PAT TEAM LEADERS & SITE MANAGERS MEETING newsletter, will forward a draft copy of the questions to Ms. Christopherson and/or Ms. Widman for their review. Questions will then be forwarded to Ms. Damaris Wilson for publication in the Support Services FA newsletter.
  • Future Support Services Town Hall Briefing. Captain Avila will host a FA town hall meeting to inform all Support Services employees of the on-going FA. The meeting will be scheduled in late August or early September.

7. CMFO Analysts will review the PACNORWEST FA templates and use as a guide in developing worksheet templates for PAT’s to use when preparing their data collection. The templates will ensure that data is submitted in the same format. Templates will be provided for future FA data collection.

8. Ms. Widman will schedule an EXCEL training class for those PAT members who do not have a working knowledge of the program. Call or e-mail Ms. Widman if you need to attend this training.

9. PAT Leaders will be notified when the next FA meeting is scheduled. All PAT Leaders are to continue to keep Thursday afternoons open for “as needed” meetings.

10. The meeting was adjourned at 1345.
