Libraries

Facility: Library / Resource Center
Bldg#: B 1180
Base: Construction Battalion Command Port Hueneme - Ventura Complex -
Hours: Mon-Wed; Noon-8 pm / Sat-Sun; Noon- 5 PM
Phone: (805) 982-4411
The Resource Center features a well stocked selection of books, magazines and newspapers. You can relax in the study and leisure reading area and use computers for word processing and access to the Internet.
The Center participates in a library book loan program with off-base libraries in the area, which gives them a large selection of fiction and non-fiction books for adult and children.

Facility: Station Library

COMMERCIAL SPONSORSHIP OPPORTUNITIES
Commercial Sponsorship opportunities abound in MWR. The demographics of this market segment include male and female customers that are 8 weeks, 18 months, 18 years, 38 years and 58 years old. Reach these consumers in a positive light by sponsoring special events, leagues, tournaments and ongoing programs. Packages can be tailored to reach your most profitable market niche. All sponsorship dollars directly impact the Navy's quality of life programs in the San Diego area. For more information about the Navy's sponsorship program call (619) 556-7464, smith.julie@ns.cnrsw.navy.mil
