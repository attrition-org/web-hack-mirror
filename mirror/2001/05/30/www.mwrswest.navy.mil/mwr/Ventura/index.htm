Welcome to Ventura Complex

Just north of Los Angeles in Ventura County are two of the Navy's premier installations, Naval Construction Battalion Center Port Hueneme and Naval Air Station, Point Mugu. Both bases are about 65 miles northwest of downtown Los Angeles on the fertile Oxnard Plain near the towns of Oxnard, Channel Islands and Camarillo. Port Hueneme and Point Mugu are seven miles apart and offer a wide scope of recreation and entertainment options for Ventura County's Navy community. Beautiful environmental preserves, saltwater marshlands, 6.5 miles of beach front property at Point Mugu, and the restored, historical Bard Mansion at Port Hueneme are just a few of MWR's premier sites in the Southwest Region.

In addition to these Pacific gems, there is a wealth of fitness and recreation options. Choose from aerobic classes, state-of-the-art fitness centers, or league and intramural play. Swimming lessons, aquatic activities, bowling, athletic fields and courts, youth activities or golf at the 18 hole Seabee Golf Course, are also available. Duke's Place and The Point will tempt your palate for lunch and dinner and you will enjoy pay per view events and tournaments at the Sports Bar. For an outdoor approach spend a day or two on the beach at the Point Mugu Beach Cottages. If going to the city is more your idea of a great destination, visit the local discount ticket offices for money saving ideas.

Click on a tab above to find out how to celebrate Seabee Days in the Oxnard Plains and watch the finest in air acrobatics at the annual Point Mugu Air Show.
