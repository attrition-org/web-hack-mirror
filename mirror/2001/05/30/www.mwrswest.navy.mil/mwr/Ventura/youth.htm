PRIVATE KINDERGARTEN (Hueneme Site Only)

Registration for Fall 2001 going on now!!

Part-Time and Full-time hours available.
Both Millitary and DoD Civilian dependants of age elligible for Kindergarten Program.

Will your child be 5 years old by December 1, 2001? Are you thinking about Kindergarten for your child? Did you know that the Port Hueneme Youth Center has a private kindergarten with an optional before and afterschool program?

Barbara Humeston teaches the part time/full time kindergarten. The program follows the California State Curriculum, offers individualized reading instruction and a hands on approach in math to teach reasoning skills as well as basic math concepts.

If you would like to learn more about the program please contact the Youth Center at 982-4218.

TEEN CENTERS - Port Hueneme 982-2355, Point Mugu 989-7580, Camarillo 484-5414

The Youth Action Team is a group of teens ages 12-17, who come together and get involved in their community by networking teen trips, activities and programs. Membership is $20.00 per year and includes discounted admission to dances and activities. Y.A.T. members attend meetings, participate in fundraisers, and work as volunteers at community projects. Dedicate yourself to a better community on your base and join us.

Summer is coming and Y.A.T. has a lot of fun activities for you to be a part of, come check them out!

Now taking registrations for Summer Teen Sports Leagues: Volleyball, Weightlifiting, Flag Football and Basketball.

Visit your local Teen Center for schedule of upcoming events and activities.
