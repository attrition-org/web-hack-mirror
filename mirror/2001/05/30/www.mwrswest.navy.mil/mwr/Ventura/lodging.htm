Lodging & Storage

Facility: Beach Motel
Bldg#: B 774
Base: Naval Air Station Point Mugu - Ventura Complex -
Hours: 24 hours, 7 days a week
Phone: (805) 989-8407
The Beach Motel is located at Point Mugu, on the peninsula between the Mugu Lagoon and the Pacific Ocean. Their comfortable rooms and suites have microwaves and refrigerators. Guests enjoy a complimentary continental breakfast and free local newspaper, a 24-hour convenience store and outdoor recreation rental facility.



Facility: RV Park
Bldg#: B 770
Base: Naval Air Station Point Mugu - Ventura Complex -
Hours: 24 hours, 7 days a week
Phone: (805) 989-8407
The RV Park is located right on the beach with 72 full hook-up spaces complete with electricity, serer, water, cable TV and phone jacks. Each site rents for $15 per night for active duty, retired or reservists. DoD civilians pay $16.95 per night. Half the park as spaces set up for a first-come-first-served basis and reservations are available for the other sites by calling (805) 989-8407



Facility: RV Park
Base: Naval Air Station Port Hueneme - Ventura Complex -
Phone: (805) 982-6123 for reservations
The Port Hueneme PV Park has 34-service sites with picnic tables and extra parking space. Military personnel may rent a space at the RV Park for $19 per night and DOD civilians and other authorized users pay $23 per night.
