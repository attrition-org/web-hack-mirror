MWR Officers' Club welcomes you in grand style to the Bard Mansion, former residence of State Senator Thomas Bard and his wife Mollie. The Officers' Club offers an elegant environment for personal or business lunches, training, meetings, family dinners, promotion celebrations, weddings, parties and other special occasions. Lunch is served Monday through Friday from 11 am to 1:30 p.m. Enjoy dinner ordered from an extensive menu of delicious entrees on Friday and Saturday nights from 6 to 8:30 p.m. Wednesday is Cook Your Own Steak Night April through September and popular Buffet Dinners are served on Thursday from 5 to 8:30 p.m. Enjoy your favorite beverages at the popular Sundowners in the Bard Lounge every Friday from 4 to 6 p.m. Catering services are also offered.

Duke's Place is one of the most fun- filled clubs in the Navy with over two dozen television monitors and three big screen televisions on which to watch your favorite sporting events. Jersey Sports Bar has it all for the sports fan, including signed memorabilia from such favorites as Michael Jordan and Magic Johnson. Play darts, pool, bingo and dance to some of the best bands and DJ's in town. We also have Karaoke contests with your favorite tunes.

Food is offered seven days a week with weekday buffet lunch specials and an a la carte lunch menu featuring entrees at reasonable prices. This sports grill menu offers sandwiches and appetizers.

The Chief Petty Officers, their families and guests are invited to relax in the Reef CPO Club, Tuesday through Thursday from 3 to 9 pm, and on Fridays until 1 am. A professional catering staff is available for your special events. They specialize in wedding receptions and promotion parties. The beautiful large Cabaret Ballroom at Duke's will provide the perfect background for your event.

The Point Restaurant, which is newly remodeled, is conveniently located right outside the main gate of NAS Point Mugu. They are open for lunch Monday through Friday when they feature delicious hot buffet and daily sandwich specials. The Point is the ideal location for catered events including business lunches, meetings, parties, banquets, weddings and other special occasions. Their Burgundy Room is just the right place for meetings or meals for up to 24 persons. A new banquet room has been added which can accommodate 150 guests. The popular Point BBQs are served on a charming patio where 150 people can be served. Bingo, BBQs and delicious food served at reasonable prices. New Sports Bar features pay per view football (College on Saturday, NFL on Sunday).
Wednesday lunch time BBQ famous Tri-Tip, chicken, beans, corn-on-the-cob, cole slaw.
Wednesday Family Night Buffet from 5-8 pm with background music, DJ plays from 8-11 pm
Pizza, pasta, sandwiches and salads are available seven days a week. Dine in, take out or delivery (NAS only).
