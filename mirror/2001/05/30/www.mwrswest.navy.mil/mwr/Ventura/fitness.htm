Fitness and sports enthusiasts can enjoy invigorating workouts and competition. This park, located behind Needham Theater, has picnic tables, BBQ pits, a children's playground, and volleyball, horseshoes and basketball courts. There is a large picnic pavilion with refrigeration, available with reservation, at the park.

Bldg#: B 72

Hours: Winter: Mon-Fri; 11:30 am-1 PM & 4:30 PM-6 PM / Sat-Sun; Closed. Summer: Daily; 11 am-7 PM

MWR has outdoor swimming pools at Point Mugu and Port Hueneme, which are operated year-round. The Point Mugu pool is a 25 meter pool and the Port Hueneme pool is Olympic size. The Aquatics Programs at both locations offer standard Red Cross swimming lessons for people of all levels and ages, plus Water Safety instruction and SCUBA certification.
