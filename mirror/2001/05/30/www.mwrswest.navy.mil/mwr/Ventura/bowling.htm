Times and Events may vary. Please call to confirm information.

Free Bowling for Active Duty!
Active duty can get up to three games at no charge! For each game paid, active duty receive a free game for up to three games. This special is good Monday -Friday from 10 am-6 pm at CBC Lanes and 3-10- PM at Mugu Lanes and on weekends during open bowing at both centers. Free bowling is open at all active duty. Coupons are not accepted for this special.

Family Bowling Special at CBC and Mugu Lanes
Every Saturday during open bowling, adults bowl for only $1.50 per game with kids ages 17 years and under bowl for 75¢ per game. To receive this family rate, there must be parents and youths bowling together. Active duty special not valid during our Family Special.

Bowling Parties
Two hours of bowling, rental shoes. soda, balloons, stickers, invitations, plates and cups. Only $7.50 per person. Available at both CBC and Mugu Lanes.

Poor Richard Night at Point Mugu Lanes
Every Thursday, 3-10 PM Bowling 75¢; Shoe Rental 75¢; Hot Dogs 75¢; Soda 50¢; Nachos 75¢; Chili 75¢; Popcorn 75¢
