Welcome to San Diego
San Diego's military Mecca is the homeport to the Navy's Pacific Fleet. Year round recreational opportunities abound at one of the world's largest concentrations of military personnel and services. The variety of Morale, Welfare and Recreation Programs will launch quality of life adventures. Naval Station San Diego, the home of over 50 ships and a multitude of tenant commands, is the home of the world famous Club Metro and Anchors and Spurs. Naval Amphibious Base Coronado trains the elite Navy SEALS and offers beach front venues to enjoy San Diego's year round sunshine. NAS North Island welcomes the crews of carriers and air wings alike and provides programs for the unaccompanied sailor as well as married personnel with children. Naval Submarine Base Point Loma and Fleet Anti-Submarine Warfare Training Center overlooking San Diego's finest bay views and offer a complete array of services and facilities.

The most memorable events in your life, marriage, retirement, birthdays and anniversaries can be catered with five stars of service at any one of our beautiful catering venues. Choose the view that fulfills your dreams - the waves of the Pacific Ocean, the tranquil winds on the bay, or San Diego's beautiful skyline with its geometric architecture and maritime ambiance.

MWR is more than the services offered on board the five installations. It's the launching point for adventures into Southern California's destinations. Find everything you need to fill your leisure hours at the Metro San Diego MWR programs where the customer is our number one priority.
