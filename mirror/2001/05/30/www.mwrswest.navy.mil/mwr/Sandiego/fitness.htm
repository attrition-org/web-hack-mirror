Aquatics Programs
Times and Events may vary. Please call to confirm information.

Morning Lap Swim Hours Extended at NASNI's Crews Pool, Bldg. 400 at Naval Air Station North Island
A three-month trial period begins March 1 and runs through the end of May to determine if customers will utilize longer lap swim hours at the Crews Pool. The open hours will be extended for an additional 60 minutes during this period. The facility will be now open weekdays from 5:30 to 8 am for lap swimming. This expansion in operating hours will become permanent based on utilization numbers during the trial period. For more details, contact the Aquatics office at 545-2880.

Swim With The Sharks! Begins March 9
The new Naval Submarine Base Youth Swim Club, the Sharks, is a terrific way for kids to stay fit, meet new friends and develop or refine swimming techniques. This swim team is open to all interested participants rated at an American Red Cross Swim Level 4 or above. If swim level is unknown, a basic swim test will be given on site. The team meets Friday evenings from 6 to 7 pm at the SUBASE Pool and costs just $28 for eight sessions. A free, colorful team T-shirt will be given to all participants. Call 553-0934 for more details.
