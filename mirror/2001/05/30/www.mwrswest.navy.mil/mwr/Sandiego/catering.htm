Catering Facilities

New Catering Menu for San Diego Area

The MWR Catering Director is proud to announce a new consolidated catering menu shared by all seven catering locations in the San Diego area. Potential catering customers will enjoy having a choice of club locations each offering it's own unique specialty. New catering packages include the Tournament Package at the Admiral Baker Clubhouse and the Wardroom Party Package best used at one of the CPO clubs. To keep current with the electronic age, conference packages now include use of Proxima for projecting computerized presentations. All screens are controlled electronically. Eligible catering patrons include active duty, retired, reserve, or DoD personnel and their sponsored guests. The Admiral Baker Clubhouse is the only club open to the public. Please phone one of the numbers above so set up an appointment with one of our catering sales associates.
