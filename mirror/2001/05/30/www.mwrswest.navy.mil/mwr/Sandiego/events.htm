Race For The Pennant with the San Diego Padres

June 30, 2001 - 8 am

Event Hotline: (858) 792-2900

Register online with active.com
or
Click here to download application. (pdf format 77kb)

Post Registration Event: Saturday, June 16, 2001 at 11 am-1 PM in front on the Padres ticket office at Qualcomm Stadium.

Course Location: Start at Qualcomm Stadium and run through Mission Valley. Check-in located at Gate K (east Trolley access). Registration time: 6:30-7:45 am. Race time is 8 am.

Unique Awards: Overall Male & Female Winner - Winners will have the opportunity to throw out the ceremonial first pitch at an upcoming Padres game and receive autographed Padres memorabilia.
Division Winners - Autographed Padres baseball in an acrylic frame.
Team Competition for Most Participation - Corporate, Youth and Military teams.
Fastest Teams - The fastest three finishers from the Corporate, Youth and Military teams will receive an autographed baseball in an acrylic frame.

Event Highlights:

  • Finish race at Home Plate inside Qualcomm Stadium
  • Free Sparkletts Baseball Interactive Theme Park for the whole family including pitching, batting, and fun-filled games.
  • Participants receive an $8 ticket to the July 4, 2001 San Diego Padres game, T-shirt, goodie bag, post race snacks and more.
  • Awards Ceremony with unique memorabilia autographed by the San Diego Padres.

Padres Game: All entrants receive a free ticket to the July 4, 2001 game when the San Diego Padres take on the Colorado Rockies. Participants can buy additional tickets to this game for only $4 each (an $8 value) for friends and family by filling out the extra portion of the race application.
