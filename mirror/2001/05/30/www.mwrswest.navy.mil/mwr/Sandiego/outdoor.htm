Outdoor Recreation Events
Times and Events may vary. Please call to confirm information.

Please Note!
Pre-trip meetings are mandatory for trip participation.
The pre-trips are designed to cover important information (liability releases, trip itinerary, etc) and allow the group to make important decisions (departure times, possible group itinerary changes, etc). If you cannot attend the pre-trip meeting, please let the OAC Program Manager know ahead of time and an alternate meeting date will be arranged to inform you of this important information. If you miss the meeting and do not let the OAC Program Manager know ahead of time, you may not be able to attend the activity and will not receive a refund. Call 556-7493 for more details.

It's easy to get involved at the Outdoor Adventure Center (OAC). Most trips are offered to anyone over the age of 16 (minors must be accompanied by a parent). Children under 16 can participate in trips and workshops with OAC Program Manager's approval. Come into the OAC for trip registration information or call 556-7493 for information on any of the trips and workshops listed below.

Facility: Admiral Baker Campground
Base: Mission Valley, San Diego
Phone: (619) 556-5525 for reservations.


Nestled in the middle of the Admiral Baker Golf Course in Mission Valley, this campground is surrounded by green grass and tree-lined roads. The campground is centrally located just a few miles away from the major San Diego freeways, Interstate 8 and Interstate 15, and only ten minutes away from the city's Qualcomm Stadium.

The grounds are well suited for adults and children as they feature extensive playground equipment, and outdoor swimming pool, picnic areas, two volleyball courts, a basketball court, three ball fields, two tennis courts, and two horseshoe pits. The RV Park offers RV sites with water, electric and sewage hook-ups for $17 per night or $105 per week.

Laundry and showers are also available. Reservations may be made three months in advance by active duty and two months in advance for all other eligible patrons. Maximum stay at the park is two weeks, although this time may be extended for another two weeks in the park accomodates.

Pets are allowed, but must be leashed and cleaned up after at all times. An eligible rig may sponsor one guest rig. Sites are different lenghts, we appreciate your flexibility to ensure we can accommodate as many customers as possible.
