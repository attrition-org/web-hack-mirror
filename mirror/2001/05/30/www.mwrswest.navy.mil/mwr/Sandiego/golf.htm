NOTICE TO PATRONS

HIGH COSTS COMPEL MWR TO INCREASE GOLF FEES

Effective June 1, 2001, fees charged to the patrons of the San Diego Navy Golf Courses are going to be increased in accordance with the pricing schedules provided below. The charging of higher fees has become necessary due to: rising labor costs; higher wholesale costs for goods and supplies; the mandatory replacement of all gas-powered golf carts with electric carts; and, most significantly, substantially higher utilities rates that are being charged to MWR activities in the San Diego metropolitan area.

Since the beginning of the California utilities crisis, MWR has worked hard to keep prices low for our patrons by conserving energy, reducing labor, and pursuing other cost-conscious initiatives. To date, we believe we've been successful in holding our prices steady at a time when comparable programs and services in the commercial sector have been forced to raise their prices. However, with recent utilities costs now running much higher that we experienced last year and projections for additional cost increases in the future, we are no longer able to offset our program costs through efficiencies alone. Increased fees must be charged to at least partially offset the substantially higher costs that are being passed along to us. Because, by law, all Category C MWR programs are mandated to be self-sufficient, price increases are also being implemented in other San Diego area MWR programs, as well. Despite these increases, our market analysis shows that our golf program continues to provide a very good deal for our patrons, particularly our junior active duty military personnel and those who choose to purchase our multi-play punch cards.

As of June 1, the following prices will be charged at the Naval Station San Diego Golf Course:

Green Fees: E-5 and below $4.00, E-6 and above $7.00, DOD or Guest $9.00, Punch Cards Military--10 Play $55.00, Military--16 Play $88.00, 10 and 16 play cards will continue to be offered for sale through 31 May at the current rates of $35.00 and $50.00 for those patrons who have 6 or fewer punches remaining on their current cards. Rentals Rental Clubs $1.50, Pull Carts $1.50

As of June 1, the following prices will be charged at the Admiral Baker and Sea N Air Golf Courses:

Green Fees: E-5 and below $8.00, E-6 and above $17.00, E-5 and below Twilight $7.50, E-6 and above Twilight $12.00, DOD Weekday $18.00, DOD Weekend $23.00, DOD Weekday Twilight $15.00, DOD Weekend Twilight $16.00, Guest Weekday $24.00, Guest Weekend $29.00, Guest Weekday Twilight $18.00, Guest Weekend Twilight $19.00, Punch Card Military--20 Rounds $290.00, DOD--20 Rounds $340.00, 24 play cards will continue to be offered for sale through 31 May at the current rate of $288 for those patrons who have 6 or fewer punches remaining on their current cards. The Sea 'N Air Men's Golf Club has just expanded it's membership eligibility and would like to invite you to join.
If you are affiliated with the military in any capacity: active duty, retired, current or retired DoD employee or male dependant of a current club member, now is the time to join. Not only does joining give you an excuse to play golf regularly, you will also establish and maintain an official handicap, play in regular tournaments, gain a better understanding of golf's rules and promote good sportsmanship. Dues are $45 for Active Duty and $55 for retirees and civilians. Become a member of the Sea 'N Air Men's Golf Club today! For more information, contact Gene Kemp at 435-6747.

The Sea 'N Air Women's Golf Club is also looking for new members. Any woman on active duty, military retiree or female dependant over 18 is eligible to join. Dues are $25. The club plays every Thursday morning. For more information, please contact Marie at (858) 270-0585.
