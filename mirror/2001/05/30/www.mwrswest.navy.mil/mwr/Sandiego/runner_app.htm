Southwest Region Running and Triathlon Application

Navy Running and Triathlon Teams are being formed at 11 CONUS regional sites to highlight the Navy's commitment to fitness and showcase our Navy athletes as positive role models in promoting long distance running as a competitive sport and healthy exercise. These teams will provide active duty naval personnel the opportunity to compete in local, regional and state triathlons, marathons, and 5K/10K running events, as well as, All-Navy and All-Armed Forces Championships.

Male and female athletes with qualifying times from all CNRSW bases, as well as Naval Medical Center San Diego and Naval Weapons Station China Lake, are eligible for the Navy Region, Southwest Team. Each of the 11 regions are funded to support 50 athletes and will cover cost of uniforms, registration for approved races and ground transportation to regional events. The NRSW team will consist of 20 triathlon members (10 men and 10 women); 10 marathon members (5 men and 5 women); and 20 5K/10K members (10 men and 10 women).

To become a member of the Navy Region, Southwest Running Team, athletes must have competed in a sanctioned event at the local level and must meet the following minimum qualifying times:
