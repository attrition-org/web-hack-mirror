NOTICE TO PATRONS

HIGH COSTS COMPEL MWR TO INCREASE RV PARK FEES

Effective June 1, 2001, fees charged to the patrons of the Fiddler's Cove and Admiral Baker RV Parks will be increased in accordance with the schedule provided below. Although MWR has worked hard for many years to keep prices low for our patrons, the charging of higher fees has finally become necessary due to rising labor costs, higher wholesale costs for goods and supplies, and substantially higher utilities rates that are being charged to MWR activities in the San Diego metropolitan area.

Since the beginning of the California utilities crisis, MWR has worked hard to keep prices low for our patrons by conserving energy, reducing labor, and pursuing other cost-conscious initiatives. To date, we believe we've been successful in holding our prices steady at a time when comparable programs and services in the commercial sector have been forced to raise their prices, scale back their services, or go out of business. However, since December of last year, we've experienced a 400 per cent rise in monthly utilities costs, which, along with current projections for additional cost increases in the future, preclude our ability to continue to offset our program costs through efficiencies alone.

Increased fees must be charged to at least partially offset the substantially higher costs that are being passed along to us. Because law mandates all Category C MWR programs are to be self-sufficient or they must be eliminated, price increases are also being implemented in other San Diego area MWR programs, as well, including Golf, Marinas, Bowling and Food and Beverage. Despite these increases, a recent market analysis has shown that all of the programs in which we are raising our prices are still charging fees/prices that are much lower than those charged for comparable programs and services in the commercial sector.

As of June 1, the following prices will be charged at Navy RV Parks in the San Diego area:

RV Park Daily Rates: Active/Retired $19, Other $22, Portable Pump-Out $5, E-mail/per use $1, Fax /per page $1, (Send or Receive) Copier/per page 10 cents

Facility: Admiral Baker Campground
Base: Mission Valley, San Diego
Phone: (619) 556-5525 for reservations.

Reservations may be made through e-mail at:
ADMBakerPark@aol.com


Nestled in the middle of the Admiral Baker Golf Course in Mission Valley, this campground is surrounded by green grass and tree-lined roads. The campground is centrally located just a few miles away from the major San Diego freeways, Interstate 8 and Interstate 15, and only ten minutes away from the city's Qualcomm Stadium.

The grounds are well suited for adults and children as they feature extensive playground equipment, and outdoor swimming pool, picnic areas, two volleyball courts, a basketball court, three ball fields, two tennis courts, and two horseshoe pits. The RV Park offers RV sites with water, electric and sewage hook-ups for $17 per night or $105 per week.

Laundry and showers are also available. Reservations may be made three months in advance by active duty and two months in advance for all other eligible patrons. Maximum stay at the park is two weeks, although this time may be extended for another two weeks in the park accomodates.

Pets are allowed, but must be leashed and cleaned up after at all times. An eligible rig may sponsor one guest rig. Sites are different lenghts, we appreciate your flexibility to ensure we can accommodate as many customers as possible.
