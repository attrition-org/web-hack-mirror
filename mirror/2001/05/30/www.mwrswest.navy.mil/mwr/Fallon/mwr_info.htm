There are 8,000 books in our Library. We have the McNaughton collection, Naval Heritage collection, Arco collection, S.A.T., C.A.T., C.I.E.P. books and video tapes along with instructional children's videos and language learning videos. There are three computers with internet access, a fax machine, a copy machine and a paperback book exchange. Additionally, we have TRAMAN'S (contains over 70 training manuals on CD-ROM) and NRTC in PCF and NEETS.
Special Offers: children's summer reading program. During tax season, tax forms are available.

COMMERCIAL SPONSORSHIP OPPORTUNITIES
Commercial Sponsorship opportunities abound in MWR. The demographics of this market segment include male and female customers that are 8 weeks, 18 months, 18 years, 38 years and 58 years old. Reach these consumers in a positive light by sponsoring special events, leagues, tournaments and on-going programs. Packages can be tailored to reach your most profitable market niche. All sponsorship dollars directly impact the Navy's quality of life programs in the San Diego area. For more information about the Navy's sponsorship program call (619) 556-7464, smith.julie@ns.cnrsw.navy.mil
