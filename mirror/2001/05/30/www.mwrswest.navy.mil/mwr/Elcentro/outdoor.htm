Outdoor Recreation

Facility: Recreational Equipment Rental & RV Park
Bldg#: 318
Address: D Street (opposite end of MWR Admin.)
Base: Naval Air Facility - El Centro -

Check out equipment for all outdoor needs. Our RV Park is open and offers 82 spaces with full hook-ups.

Prices are subject to change. Please call to confirm all information.
