Campus Connections


If you're plugged into Wasatch, everybody benefits. Here at Campus Connections, we provide a number of ways for students, families, faculty, and friends to stay in touch.

  • This Week at Wasatch is updated daily. This page contains the weekly schedule and important announcements.
  • Coming Events lets you look into the future — you can view all the events for the rest of the year, look at just what's happening today, check out the week or month ahead, and stay up-to-date on Wasatch sports and academic events.
  • The Eye of the Tiger is our student-produced newspaper. Read the articles, the creative writings, and view the artwork of Wasatch students. It is best viewed using Internet Explorer.
  • Get your own Wasatch email account at Wasatch EMail. It's free!
  • Technical Support is there to help students and faculty who occasionally experience rare techno-hiccups. Just fill out the form and submit it. Tech Support will respond with the cure and perhaps some wise words on how to avoid the problem in the future.
  • Wasatch Window our quarterly magazine for parents, friends, and alumni of the school is now available in electronic form.

And don't forget, you can access the InterActive GradeBook or send us your comments and suggestions using the links at the bottom of every page of the Wasatch web site.
