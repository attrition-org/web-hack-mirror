U.S. Federal Aviation Administration
Office of International Aviation, AIA

The Federal Aviation Administration's Office of International Aviation is the focal point for international policy development; liaison with other US agencies, foreign governments and international organizations; and the provision of training and technical assistance to foreign aviation authorities. Its activities are carried out in support of the FAA's global leadership role to encourage the improvement of global aviation safety, security and system efficiency.
