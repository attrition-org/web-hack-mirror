Growing in LEAPS AND BOUNDS
AIBT is growing by leaps and bounds.  AIBT now has its own Distance Education office.  The first classes using distance ed started January 22, 2001, with 2 students. Our first instructor is Andy Baker.  Any faculty interested in doing online instruction should e-mail Mary Rickman-Taylor at: mtaylor@aibt.edu  AIBT is looking forward to many years of success and growth in this field.   Eager students will be successful because of dedicated teachers supported by superior administrative staff.  AIBT has those great people in abundance.  (cont. pg 2)
