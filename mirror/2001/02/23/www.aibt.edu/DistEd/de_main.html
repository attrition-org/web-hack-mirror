Our Mission

The Distance Education Office is dedicated to meeting the needs of developing and
maintaining a nontraditional learning environment, committed to preparing faculty and
staff technically and pedagogically to meet the constantly changing learning needs and
requirements of students to foster their success at functioning in a highly advanced and
expanding technological community
