ABOUT ACRO
Acro's Mission
Vector Divisions
   

Vision Statement
"We will be a world leader in servicing turbo-shaft engines, drivetrains and components"

Our Mission Statement
1. To always meet our commitments to Customers by delivering performance that leads the industry.
2. Provide a workplace where People are highly valued and have the opportunity to grow and succeed.
3. Make Continuous Improvement a way of life.
4. Deliver "Best in Class" return on investment to maximize Shareholder value.

Strategic Goals and Objectives
Customers

  • To know our customers and understand the markets we currently serve and to continually research new markets.
  • Be an industry leader in delivering value to the customer.  The customer defines value.
  • To deliver value with a burning passion to satisfy our customers.

People

  • Create an open environment where our people are respected, empowered, recognized and rewarded.
  • Provide opportunities for training and career development to all our people.
  • To constantly work to satisfy our people so that we are considered the "Employer of Choice".

Continuous Improvement

  • To embrace a culture that constantly challenges the status quo and recognizes that today's performance can always be improved.
  • Become more efficient by searching out opportunities to improve.
  • Action-oriented in implementing innovative solutions.

Maximize Shareholder Value

  • To consistently deliver above-average returns on investment and business growth.
  • Maximize returns to all stakeholders - Customers, Employees, Shareholders and Suppliers.

These objectives drive everything we do. They serve as our road map to personal and business growth.
