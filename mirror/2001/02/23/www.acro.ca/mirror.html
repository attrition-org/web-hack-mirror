Welcome!

Welcome to ACRO Aerospace Inc's home on the Internet.  We invite our customers to get to know us better.  We also encourage future employees, suppliers and investors to take a closer look at ACRO.

ACRO Aerospace is a team of dedicated professionals who provide repair and overhaul service to commercial and military customers around the world. 

ACRO stands for "Aviation Center for Repair and Overhaul". It also refers to the Greek word for "height", which is one phobia our customers do not have.

ACRO has just over 400 highly trained employees who collectively have the expertise to overhaul and repair most types of helicopters .

ACRO's customers include the Canadian Department of National Defense, Canadian and United States Government-owned aircraft and a host of other domestic and international operators.
