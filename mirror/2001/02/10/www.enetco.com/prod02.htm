High-Speed Digital and Analog Communications Over a Single Line

Developed by IBM, the WaveRunner 7845 Network Terminator Extended (NT-1 Plus) is a programmable, standalone communication link that enables a personal computer or workstation, equipped for Basic Rate (BRI) ISDN and standard analog telephone lines, to share a single digital phone line into the home or office.

Affordable and easy to install, the NT-1 Plus provides access to ISDN's expanded bandwidth. In addition, it offers a variety of ISDN voice telephone services --- services that typically cost the consumer extra dollars every month --- using standard analog telephone equipment.

The NT-1 Plus takes advantage of high bandwidth and speed --- providing faster response times and file transfer rates, up to 128 Kbps via two ISDN B channels. Basic Rate Interface (BRI) services are available from your local telephone company. 

Both B channels can be used -- for the cost of one ISDN service connection -- and the NT-1 Plus gives you the choice of how to use them. The NT-1 Plus enables one BRI B channel to connect to a terminal adapter (for 64 Kbps bandwidth) and converts the other channel to a standard analog interface for connecting to all existing analog telephones and fax machines. It can also operate as a standard NT1, passing both B channels and the D channel to your terminal equipment for full 128 Kbps digital bandwidth.

A 120VAC  power adapter is included, and backup batteries can be added to enable use during a power outage.
