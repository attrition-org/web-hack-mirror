The Best of Both Worlds... in One Little Card

The WaveRunner all-in-one PC Card (PCMCIA) communications adapter delivers the Internet in both digital and traditional dial-up capabilities, including analog modem speeds to 28.8 Kbps and ISDN up to 128 Kbps... right to your notebook.

You can now enjoy the benefits and speed of ISDN and the convenience of analog modem connections at home, in the office, or on the road, almost anywhere in the world. This is the perfect device for business travelers who visit parts of the world that have invested heavily in ISDN infrastructure like Asia and Europe.
