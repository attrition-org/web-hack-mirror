The WaveRunner PC Board

The WaveRunner ISDN TA connects networks of PCs over ISDN for smooth end-to-end communications at speeds up to 128 Kbps, functionally three times faster than today's dial-up analog modems. 

Based on IBM's advanced MWAVE Digital Signal Processor (DSP) technology, this high-speed basic rate interface (BRI) board enables use of digital phone lines with other ISDN devices. Available in both ISA and Micro Channel configurations, it's ready to go.
