Company Profile               

We are located in Fort Collins at the northern end of Colorado's Front Range, a 1-hour drive from Denver. In addition to being an recreational playground with unmatched natural beauty, our region is well-known for its abundance of high-tech industrial, educational, and human resources. Our regional economy is strong and diverse, with a number of large international businesses choosing to locate their home offices in the local area. Fort Collins is also host to Colorado State University, one of the finest educational centers in the western United States and home of the CSU Rams championship football, basketball, and volleyball teams.
 

eNetco International was founded in 1998 and acquired shortly thereafter by WaveRunner Technologies Corporation, a privately-held Michigan Corporation.

The primary purpose of eNetco International is to facilitate an exchange of products and technologies between small U.S. manufacturers and Japanese reselling partners. eNetco sells the WaveRunner family of high-speed ISDN adapters, developed and manufactured by IBM,  to resellers and end-users. eNetco also provides project-based consulting and translation services. Some of eNetco's major customers include the IBM Corporation; NTT Electronics Corporation (NEL); AVM of America, Inc.; BI Incorporated; and eSoft Corporation.
