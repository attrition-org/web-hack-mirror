NOTE

 

(a)                For plan I to plan IV if monthly data transfer exceeds from the specified limit customer will be charged  for incremental monthly data transfer.  Customer will have the option to shift to higher plan by paying the difference of annual charges.

 

(b)                Annual charges include a standard 4U-19(7"high x 19" wide x 18"depth) rack space that can accommodate single server of standard dimensions, customer desirous of colocating multiple machines will need to take additional space in multiple of 4U-19 standard at Rs.1.0 lakh per annum each.

 

(c)                Customer can take a rack space in multiples of 4U-19 standard or equivalent volume.

 

(d)                All above charges will be payable on advance quarterly installments.

 

(e)                Customer will be given an Ethernet segment or port, additional components will be based d on space and cumulative traffic.

 

(f)                 Customer paying in USD, The exchange rate will be applicable as per closing rate on last date of the previous month of the bill.

 

(g)                A server occupying space more than 4U-19 standard will be charged on prorata basis, other network devices such as routers, hubs, load balancers, storage, monitor etc will be charged as per 4U-19 standard on prorata basis cummulatively.
