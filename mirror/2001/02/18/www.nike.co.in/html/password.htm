ICQ Users , Please take extra precautions to safeguard your account !

  Have you changed your password ?
  Letting your password know is as good as signing a blank cheque ! 

  1. The moment you learned the Log In and Log Out procedure , you ought to learn next best step of managing or changing your own Password.
  2. You can change the password in two ways.
  3. TCP/IP account user should Telnet to your own account. After Loging in , Select the option to Change Password. Enter the Old password, Type P to pick up your own choice, Now enter the new Password , Re-enter for verification & Wow ! It's done. Sounds simple. Don't worry, if you can not manage it for the first time, ;-) . Just give a call to your Customer support agent.
  4. Change Your password by following the simple web based options provided by your local node.
  5. Click your respective city to reach the password change Page.
    Mumbai.
    Pune.
  6. Change your password frequently.
  7. You may not be able to reuse passwords. So do not try to revert back to old passwords.
  8. Looking for more tips on Password .. Click here  !

  Have you lost your password ?

  1. Inform Helpdesk and see if you can request to lock the account. Keep your customer code handy.
  2. Prepare an Application form , with a request to reset your password , addressed   to respective Customer Relations officer.
  3. Sign. The signature should be the same as per the original application form when you subscribed  the account.
  4. Enclose a cheque of Rs 100/- , payable to "Videsh Sanchar Nigam Limited".
  5. Submit personally to the office and collect the new password.
