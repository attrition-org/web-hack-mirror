Customers/ISPs should provide the following information at the time of submission of application form for connectivity to enable access their IP address requirements:

 1.                 Network Topology

2.                 POP Design

3.                 IP Addressing (unnumbered IP address should be used in a pool, only PPP dial up protocol is supported. SLIP protocol will not be supported).

4.                 IP Address requirement projections (preset requirement, after three months, after six months and after one year).

 

The allocation of IP address will be subject to the following terms and conditions:

 

1.                  IP address allocation will be as per policy of APNIC (Asia Pacific Network Information Center, Australia) for IP address allocation.

2.                  VSNL encourages ISPs to get the IP addresses directly from APNIC by becoming member of APNIC.

3.                  Routing policy for ISPs will be as per details provided in ISP booklet.

4.                  Customer /ISPs should give following undertaking, i.e. :                      

a)      Customer /ISPs understands that IP addresses allocated via VSNL/through VSNL are non portable.

b)      Customer/ISP understands that he cannot in turn allocate IP addresses to other smaller ISPs or customers.

c)      Customer /ISPs understands that IP addresses to be allocated by VSNL are subject to availability of IP addresses with VSNL.

Customer/ISPs understands that they need to return IP addresses allocated by VSNL after expiry of contract with VSNL.

i ) Charges of 5 Km =Rs .55820/-
ii ) Charges of 10 Km=Rs .88056/-
iii) Difference ( ii )-( i )=Rs. 32236/-
iv) Per Km Rate ( iii ) / 5 =Rs. 6447.20/-
v) Rate for 7 Km [(i)+2km*(iv)]=Rs. 55820+2*Rs6447.20
