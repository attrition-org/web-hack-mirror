Eligibity for getting VSNL student Internet A/c are:
1. The student must be of X1th standard and above and below age of 30 years.
2. He /She should provide bonafide certificate on the college letter head showing mentioning that he/she is a full time regular student for the current academic year.
3. Student must submit Xerox of its i-card and must show the original i-card for verification.
4. The payment of RS.1050/- by D.D/PAYORDER/BANKER'S CHEQUE in favour of VSNL.
5. Student must submit the filled up application with the above documents personally in any working days between 10 a.m to 5 p.m (i.e. MONDAY to FRIDAY)  at Lokmanya Videsh Sanchar Bhavan , Prabhadevi,   for Mumbai and at respective Customer Relations section in other cities.
