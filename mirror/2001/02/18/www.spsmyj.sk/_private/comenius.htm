LIFE IN OUR SCHOOL

The Secondary Technical School in Myjava is four-year boarding school for students aged 14 – 18 years.
The condition of entering this school is the written entrance examination in Slovak and mathematics.
Having become students of STS Myjava we could chose our specialisation from this four following possibilities:.
1. ENGENEERING INDUSTRY - graphic systems
  - technical management

- machine building
 
 
 
 
 
2. ELECTRICAL ENGENEERING - electroenergetics
                                                             - informatics and telecommunications
 
3. TECHNICAL AND INFORMATICS SERVICES

4. MECHATRONICS

We learn both compulsory and elective subjects.

             

 
GENERAL SUBJECTS include:
Slovak language, two foreign languages /English, French,German,Spanish/, civics, history, ethic or religion education, mathematics, physics, chemistry, ecology, geography.

PHYSICAL TRAINING

SPECIAL SUBJECTS in accordance with special orientation:
technical drawing, mechanics, informatics, engeneering, electronics, economy, legal study, practice, graphic systems, telecommunication and computer network, automation technics.
The academic year begins after the summer holiday the 2 nd September / the 1 st September is Constitution Day/ and it is divided into two terms:
September – January
February – June
with the interval between them formed by mid-term holiday.Apart from this holiday we have:
two days´ autumn holiday in November
two weeks´ winter holiday in December
one week´s spring holiday in February
two months´ Summer holiday
Classes in our school begin at 8.10 a.m. and usually end at 15.20 p.m.. One course lasts 45 minutes, break takes 5 or 10 minutes and the “big” one takes 15 minutes /at 10.05/.
The average number of students in a class is 30, but it varies.

Out-of-school activities are very important at our school: we are sport – loving boys and girls, volleyball about all. Most of us takes dancing lessons, Internet lessons. Elder classmates publish school newspaper called STROJAR / The Machine Builder / and we have also our own radiobroadcasting at school. We are lovers of travelling – we have visited France, Italy, Germany, Greece.

There si so much to speak about : memorable moments of our Immatriculation parties, when the first class students are accepted to the student “guild” , of our “ green ribbon parties “ - more jolly part of the closing fourth class, etc.

The study at STS finish with the school leaving examination that is taken in this subjects:

  1. SLOVAK LANGUAGE AND LITERATURE - examination is composed from written part / composition on a topic given by school / and discourse.
  2. PRACTICAL part of examination
  3. THEORETIC part of examination from special subjects
  4. ELECTIVE subject - most often it is foreign language, mathematics or informatics
  5. VOLUNTARY subject - any other subject

Having finish study on Secondary Technical School, our school-leaves are acquainted with work in factories, offices or qualified for university studies / Technical University, Economical and others Universities/.
