Agri-Trade

Agri-Trade Business Network

OTK's Agri-trade business provides a comprehensive service to clients regarding the marketing of agricultural and irrigation supplies, mechanisation equipment and insurance services in Mpumalanga and the Eastern Free State. The retail network consisting of 50 stores, as well as a team of skilled marketers, offer clients supplies such as fuel, fertilizer, plant pesticides, stock feeds, animal health products, fencing materials, packaging materials, tyres, batteries, building materials, hardware, irrigation supplies, tractors, implements and spare parts. Direct deliveries to farms, form an integral part of this service.

OTK provides a broking service which includes short term and crop insurance. Trading under the name OTK Insurance Brokers they also offer credit life insurance, medical schemes and funeral schemes.
Insurance broking offices are strategically situated and offer the best available products from reputable underwriters. A further unique feature on offer includes the financing of premiums to qualifying clients.
OTK Insurance Brokers provide all types of insurance; personal, commercial and agricultural insurance.

Attractive financing packages, which take the client's unique needs into account, are available on request.
