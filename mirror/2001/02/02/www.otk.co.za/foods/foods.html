Food Products

OTK's mills - two in Gauteng and four in Mpumalanga - produce high quality food products. The product range includes super, special and sifted maize meal, samp, mealie-rice, grain sorghum meal and other products. The products are marketed directly from mills, via food depots and from other OTK centres in Mpumalanga, Gauteng, Northern Province and KwaZulu-Natal.

Animal Feeds

OTK's four stock feed factories and feather meal plant manufacture stock feeds for poultry, pigs, dairy cows, sheep, cattle, dogs and other animals. These products are distributed directly from factories and via a network of OTK centres and other agents. The division has a technical section and a well-equipped laboratory to advise clients.

Broilers

Earlybird Farm is a joint venture and it is the second largest broiler producer in South Africa. Activities include breeding farms (consisting of rearing, laying and hatching facilities) and several broiler farms. The company also makes extensive use of contract growers to raise broilers. The operations are located in Gauteng and Mpumalanga. Broilers are slaughtered at the abattoirs in Standerton and Olifantsfontein. There is also a processing plant in Standerton where value-added products are prepared. The company's most important brands are Goldi and Festive.

Eggs

OTK is involved in three facets of the egg industry, namely the breeding of day-old laying hen chicks, the raising of the chickens until they are ready to lay and commercial egg production.
A new investment in Eggbert Eggs and Arendsnes allows OTK entry into commercial egg production and marketing. The activities of this company are in Kwazulu-Natal and Gauteng.
