Grain Marketing

OTK acts as an agent between sellers and buyers in the free market trading environment of agricultural products, especially yellow maize, white maize, grain sorghum, wheat, sunflowers, soya beans, dried beans and peanuts.



Grain Storage

OTK has a good infrastructure of silo capacity which is strategically situated so that producers and buyers can utilise it to their maximum advantage. The 45 grain silos are located in such a way that they satisfy most of the needs of both producers and buyers.


Grain Finance

OTK provides financing to buyers of grain.
