ECO-O.K. Coffee Activist's Kit

The kit includes information about coffee farming in Latin America, how the shift to sun coffee is effecting migratory birds and what you can do to help educate other coffee drinkers.

Two ways to obtain a copy of the ECO-O.K. Coffee Activist's Kit:

1. Download it in Adobe Portable Document Format (PDF).

Join the Coffee-Hot-Line

  We would like to invite you to join us for an email discussion of migratory songbirds and coffee farming. To join, click on the "Coffee-Hot-Line" above, and in the body of the message write "subscribe coffee-hot-line". You will be added within 24 hours. This is an ideal forum for you to share your thoughts and ask questions.




Order More Information
 

If you would like more information about how you can help promote well-managed agriculture that benefits communities and wildlife, please contact us at can@ra.org. We would be happy to answer any questions you might have.
