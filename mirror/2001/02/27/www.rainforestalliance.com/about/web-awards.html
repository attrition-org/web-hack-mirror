Web Site Awards

The Rainforest Alliance Web site has received the ASAE (the American Society of Association Executives) Gold Circle Award for best Web site. It was selected as the best of 63 sites and recognized for its excellence in nonprofit communications.


This site was included in the Children's Television Workshop's Best of the Web list. The Kids & Teachers section of the site was highlighted as being a place for imaginative play and exploration.


Britannica.com has selected the Rainforest Alliance Web site as one of the best on the Internet based on quality, accuracy of content, presentation and usability.


This site was awarded the Clean Planet Award by The PlanetPals. The Clean Planet Award is given to sites that are of high quality and that promote concern for the environment by encouraging recycling and the study of ecology and earth sciences through school project ideas and valuable Web site content.


The Rainforest Alliance's Kids & Teachers page has been selected as an especially peaceful, tasteful and amazing site to visit by Nana Ellen. She has awarded this site her Grandmother-tested seal of approval and has recognized the contribution that the Rainforest Alliance has made to a kid safe web experience.


The Rainforest Allaince site received the "Excellent Site Award" from Topmarks Education in England for being one of the best educational sites on the Internet. Conceived by educator Sue Spolton, Topmarks is a free Web site that aims to make it easier for students, teachers and parents to use the Internet effectively for learning.
