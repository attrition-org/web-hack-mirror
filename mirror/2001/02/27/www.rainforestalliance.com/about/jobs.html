Position Title: Associate Director of Development/Major Gifts Manager
Reports to: Director of Development

Position Summary

The Associate Director of Development/Major Gifts Manager is responsible for overseeing all aspects of individual major donor fundraising ($1,000+ gifts) for the organization including identification, research, cultivation, solicitation and stewardship of prospects/donors. The Associate Director supervises a Development Assistant, manages the development department in the Development Director's absence and works closely with the Executive Director.

Primary Responsibilities:

  • Identify individual major donor prospects with the capacity to give $1,000 or more, with emphasis on prospects with the capacity to give $25,000 or more. Contact/review sources for prospect identification such as publications, Board members, other volunteers, events and trips, etc.

  • Coordinate research of major donors/prospects utilizing varying resources to gain information about donors/prospects such as libraries, face-to face meetings, the Internet, publications, contacts, volunteers, etc.

  • Recruit prospects and donors to individual/corporate Canopy Associate program. Use program as recognition, cultivation and fundraising vehicle. Organize events and trips for Canopy donors and others as a way to cultivate their involvement and interest in RA. Cultivate non-canopy donors/prospects through additional means.

  • Develop solicitation strategies and coordinate solicitations with DOD, ED and other appropriate staff/volunteers. Solicit donors in most appropriate way (face-to-face, in writing, electronically).

  • Supervise administrative staff and processes associated with maintaining data/hard files for individual major donors. Ensure all gifts are acknowledged appropriately and in a timely manner.

  • Develop, implement and administer RA planned giving program.

  • Develop relationships with Board members and coordinate annual Board giving efforts with Development Committee Chair, ED and DOD.

  • Supervise and motivate Development Assistant. Provide guidance, support, and opportunities for growth.

  • Contribute to overall planning and growth of development department, support team efforts, and manage the department when the Development Director is unavailable.

  • Other responsibilities as assigned by the Director of Development.
Qualifications:

  • Strong leadership and interpersonal skills.

  • 10 years work experience, minimum 5 years major gifts experience.

  • Demonstrated experience identifying, cultivating, and soliciting at least $500,000/year from major donors.

  • Strong planning and organizational skills.

  • Articulate and persuasive communicator, both oral and written.

  • Proficiency with computers - word processing (Word) and spreadsheets (Excel).

  • Background in conservation or international development and understanding of related issues a plus.
Compensation:

Salary commensurate with experience. Competitive benefits include 4 weeks vacation, summer schedule, health and dental coverage, and 403b retirement plan. Excellent working environment.

To apply:

Send resume, cover letter, and salary history to Personnel, Rainforest Alliance, 65 Bleecker Street, New York, NY, 10012. Fax: 212-677-2187.

The Rainforest Alliance is an equal opportunity employer.
