Help conserve rainforests by purchasing SmartWood certified products. More than sixty criteria are used to ensure that the wood used in the manufacturing of these products comes from forest under ecologically sound management.

Purchase farm products carrying the ECO-O.K.® label and help support responsible agriculture and environmental sustainability.  Buying from certified farms sends a message of thanks to producers for their conservation efforts.

Order books about the rainforest and general conservation issues from Amazon.com.  An added bonus: the Rainforest Alliance earns a percentage of the sales!

Yolanda Kondonassis, one of America's foremost harpists, has just released a new compact disc of music by composer Alan Hovhaness, which includes the world premiere recording of his piece, Spirit of Trees. Kondonassis will donate a portion of her royalty from the album to the Rainforest Alliance. For more information on Yolanda and to hear some of her music, visit her Web site at www.ykharp.com.

Within the Nikolai Allowance, our Kids Can Give area allows children to donate to many worthy causes, including the Rainforest Alliance.

Printed on recycled paper with soy ink, Rainforest Alliance checks and address labels are a great way to show your support of tropical forest conservation. Spread the word about the importance of conserving these diverse ecosystems with every check you write and know that 10% of every order goes directly to important Rainforest Alliance programs.

Whales & Friends Catalog offers hundreds of nature and animal gifts online. You'll find items like clothing, bath accessories, toys, jewelry, home decor, books, and lots more. You can feel good about buying from Whales & Friends -- a portion of the proceeds will benefit the Rainforest Alliance Fund.

CharityWave.com, a national online charitable giving program now lists the Rainforest Alliance as one of it's participating charities! Do all your online shopping at the Rainforest Alliance HomeGalleria.com online shopping village. When you purchase your favorite stuffed animals from Fuzzy Friends, you support conservation programs of the Rainforest Alliance. For each sale of a Fuzzy Friends animal, 20% of the pre-tax amount of your order will be donated to the Rainforest Alliance.

Purchase the Rainforest for Children Video Series, Library Video Company's award-winning educational videos, online and 5% of the sale will be donated to the Rainforest Alliance. Filmed on location in the rainforests of Costa Rica, People of the Rainforest, Animals of the Rainforest and Plants of the Rainforest are appropriate for children grades 3 - 6.

Order your magazines online and 10% of each subscription price will be donated to the Rainforest Alliance!  DrMag.com features hundreds of publications, ranging from mainstream magazines to specialized journals covering topics like the environment, business and trade.

Purchase individually crafted, 100% recycled paper, stationery, wedding invitations and gifts from Twisted Limb Paperworks and 15% of the price will go to support the Rainforest Alliance.  Be sure to mention the Rainforest Alliance in the "Questions and Comments" box on Twisted Limb's order form.

Purchase a CD or cassette recording of the sounds of the rainforest. Natural Sound produces and distributes Rainforest Dawn and Rainforest Dusk, which capture real sounds of wildlife and nature in the rainforest. $1.50 from each purchase will be donated to the Rainforest Alliance.
