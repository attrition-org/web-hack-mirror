Expert Sources

Journalists -- Many Rainforest Alliance staff are world-renowned experts in their fields. Just click on the topics that interest you and you can read the biography of the individual best-suited to answer your inquiry or provide expert commentary. We would be happy to work with you as you develop stories and are available to comment and and provide insight on a variety of topics, including:

· Eco-labeling (Chris Wille, Richard Donovan)
· Rainforest flora and fauna (Diane Jukofsky)
· Sustainable development (Dan Katz, Chris Wille, Tensie Whelan)
· Forest management/certification (Richard Donovan)
· Agricultural certification (Chris Wille)
· Nonprofit management (Tensie Whelan, Dan Katz, Karin Kreider)
· Business and the environment (Dan Katz, Chris Wille, Tensie Whelan)
· Environmental reporting (Diane Jukofsky, Tensie Whelan, Sofia Perez)
· Environmental issues in the Neotropics (Diane Jukofsky, Chris Wille)
· Non-timber forest products (Richard Donovan)
· Cut flowers and ferns industry (Ronald Sanabria)
· Ecotourism (Ronald Sanabria)
