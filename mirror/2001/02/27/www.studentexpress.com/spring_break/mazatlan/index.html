The Pearl of the Pacific

The NUMBER ONE destination of college students on the West coast. Located directly across from the tip of the BAJA, Mazatlan is the most incredible SPRING BREAK a college student could dream of. Known as the "Pearl of the Pacific," this truly is the place for sun, nightlife and fun! Enjoy the incredible sunsets as you have never seen one before. You will be charmed by the Mexican heritage and the friendly people of this wonderful laid back resort. Then get ready for the night life. No matter what you think, Mazatlan will not take second to any Spring Break destination in the world for nightlife. There is a huge bash every night at different clubs. You will be amazed at the variety of the clubs and most are open until dawn. Party all night and sleep all day on the sun drenched beaches and enjoy the most spectacular Spring Break one can only dream of. The English translation of "Mazatlan" actually means "the party." Experience for yourself the views, clubs, beaches and bars of Mazatlan!
