The Pearl of the Pacific

From Sunrise...

"Maz" is the NUMBER ONE destination of  students on the West coast. Located directly across from the tip of the BAJA, Mazatlan is the most incredible SPRING BREAK a student could dream of. Known as the "Pearl of the Pacific," this truly is the place for sun, nightlife and fun! Enjoy the incredible splendor of sunsets as you have never seen them before. You will be charmed by the Mexican heritage and the friendly people of this wonderful laid back resort. 

To Sunset...

In Mazatlan you can enjoy the splendor of orange- violet-yellow sunsets splashed across the endless sky while relaxing on the longest uninterrupted beach in Mexico. After the sun dies, night clubs and discos rule the scene. There are a huge bashes every night at different clubs. You will be amazed at the variety of the clubs and most, like Valentino's, Bora Bora and El Caracol stay open until dawn.  All the clubs have the latest hi-tech sound systems and more lights and gadgets than you have ever seen. Other fun places to go include Harley's, Mundo Bananas, El Toro Bravo (with the famous mechanical bull), and Carlito's.  Party all night and sleep all day on the sun drenched beaches and enjoy the most spectacular Spring Break imaginable.

And In between...

After Dark...

During the day hangout in front of Joe's Oyster Bar, located on a fabulous sandy beach.  As for dining, Mazatlan has all the variety of any major city. You can eat inexpensively or elaborately, depending on your taste and budget. Some dining ideas include, Earnie Tomato's, The Shrimp Bucket and Jungle Juice.  The English translation of "Mazatlan" actually means "the party." Experience for yourself the views, clubs, beaches and bars of Mazatlan!
