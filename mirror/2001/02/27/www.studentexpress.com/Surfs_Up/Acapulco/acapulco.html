Acapulco. Where the Difference is Night and Day...

Acapulco is Mexico's largest, most dazzling seaside resort. It offers more variety, more excitement, more fun, more Mexican flavor and more for your money than any other resort in the country.

Known as the 24-hour paradise, Acapulco never rests. You can have breakfast at noon and dine at midnight. Where people show their best moves on the tennis court byday and on the dance floor by night. Acapulco has a special magic that is difficult tocapture, but visitors and residents alike are fascinated by its incredible beauty and the hypnotic charm of its very own brand of enchantment.

A wide variety of exciting water sports and world class hotels with beautiful swimming pools are just some of the reasons that Acapulco by day is as much fun as Acapulco by night.
