An island surrounded by the warm aqua-blue waters of the sunny Caribbean, Cancun is home to world-famous clubs and discos, such as Senor Frogs, Dady Rock, La Boom and Fat Tuesday, just to name a few. Restaurants range from the inexpensive McDonald's and Subway to the very exquisite like the Argentine Restaurant, with many fine establishments in-between, including Pat Obrien's, Outback and La Parilla.

The city of Cancun and it's resort environs are relatively young, however, the great Mayan Civilization occupied this peninsula centuries before. Cancun is only a short tour bus ride from many rich historical treasures of this ancient Mayan culture, such as Tulum, and Xcaret.
