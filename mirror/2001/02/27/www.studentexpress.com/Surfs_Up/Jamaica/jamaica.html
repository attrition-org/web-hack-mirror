Come to Jamaica and feel all right!

Thatched roofs, evening campfires on the beach, Reggae music and that special feeling of "Irie" mon, that's Jamaica!  Spring Break is an explosion of activities around the clock!

Daily beach parties, nightly reggae concerts, and thousands of students from every part of the planet mean a Spring Break you will never forget!

Dine on a "patty" and a Red Stripe, slide the rocks into the seas at Pickled Parrot, or ride bareback through the surf on seven mile beach.

Take a catamaran cruise, or play under the many waterfalls. Jamaica is a land of vast tropical beauty, a laid-back atmosphere and a partying spirit.
