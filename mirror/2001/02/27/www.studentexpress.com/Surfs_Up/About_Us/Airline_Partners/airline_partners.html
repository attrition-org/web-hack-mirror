BREAK with the BEST!

Over the fourteen years of operation of Student Express we have seen smaller charter airlines fail to meet reasonable criteria of timely service. We, therefore, have taken the proactive course. In order to ensure the best possible reliability, safety and comfort for our customers, we have developed business relationships with two outstanding airlines for our charter services. No travel system can guarantee perfect performance, however, in 1999 TWA had 100% on-time service and Allegro had 96% on-time service for Student Express travelers.

In addition to being safe and reliable, both of these carriers have clean and well maintained fleets of aircraft, something much appreciated by anyone who has traveled on a charter plane that smelled like an old movie theater.

If a tour operator is known by the company he keeps, we are glad to be in such good company.
