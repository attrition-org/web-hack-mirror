Student Express & Surfs-Up Vacations

Surfs-Up Vacations is one of the nation's largest young adult travel companies, specializing in Spring, Graduation and Winter Break vacations.  Surfs-Up is an affiliate of the multi million-dollar travel company Vacation Travel, Inc. which began student programs 14 years ago.  Our knowledge, professionalism, experience and integrity are well known throughout the group travel industry. Since 1986 we have provided travel arrangements for over 200,000 student trips.
