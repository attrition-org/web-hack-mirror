TOUR PARTICIPANT AGREEMENT

The Tour Participant Agreement (TPA) outlines the agreement between Student Express, Inc (SEI) and you, the tour participant, regarding the terms and conditions under which we are providing tour service and you are receiving that service.

Each individual planning to travel on a Student Express tour, must sign a Tour Participant Agreement (TPA).  If an individual planning to travel with us is under the age of 18 years, that person's TPA must be signed by a parent or legal guardian.

Final confirmation for travel and travel documents will not be provided for all persons on a booking if there are any persons on that booking for whom we (SEI) have not received a signed TPA (2 pages).

The TPA for Spring Break Travelers may be downloaded from this site, reviewed on your computer screen and printed-out on your printer.  The document is on two pages which are downloaded (both are required).  We must receive both pages of a TPA  in our office(signed where indicated) in order for us to consider that the TPA  is complete.  If for any reason the downloaded version which you print from your printer is not completely legible, please call us 1-800-787-3787 to receive a fax copy or a copy through the mail. The character size on the printed copy is small. You may review the document fully on your computer screen using the acrobat reader (discussed below) prior to printing them. The acrobat reader allows you to increase the size of the document type for easy reading.

Participants should understand that the agreement, as available on this site (or via fax, etc.) from SEI constitutes the entire agreement.  Anyone attempting to travel under an altered agreement may be subject to fines and other legal remedies under fraud statutes.
