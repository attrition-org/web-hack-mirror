 Javasaw is a jigsaw puzzle with selectable levels of difficulty (6 levels) and 4 images to choose from (more to come soon!).

To play the puzzle:

  1. Read the instructions.
  2. Play the jigsaw at your preferred resolution:
The low resolution version (for 640 x 480)
The high resolution version (for 800 x 600)

If you are not sure which one to use, choose low resolution.
