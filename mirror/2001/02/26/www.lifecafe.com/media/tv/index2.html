You have two options: with sound or without sound. To hear the streaming audio, you must have a JAVA compatible browser like Netscape. A 28.8 modem will do just fine, but with all things, speed helps. If you choose without sound, you'll be able to read the transcript, and view pictures. TIP: If you are having problem with hearing the sound, try resizing your window.

10/9/99 - The sound pages were originally created about 2 years ago using Emblaze technology. It seems that the sound works better with Netscape than with Internet Explorer. It takes a few minutes for the sound to download to your computer. Sometimes pressing the "Reload" button on your browser helps.
