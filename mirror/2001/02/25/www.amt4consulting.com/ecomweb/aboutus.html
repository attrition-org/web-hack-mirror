As a AMT TRADE & CONSULTING CORPORATION our first aim is help for our clients with their today's necessity; "WEB WORLD" Almost every company must by now have realized that having an Internet presence is vital to their continued business success.

Visitors accessing your web site should be able to find information efficiently and quickly.

This can be achieved with well-organized categories and an intuitive graphical layout. The completed site design should be effective and successful.

Their experience with the web site actually starts before the first image or words even appear. Rapid download times ensure that people will successfully complete the voyage to the first page.

Once the main page has successfully downloaded to the site visitor's computer, people visiting the page should instantly feel that the site is credible.

Within the first twenty seconds of the visit, site visitors should have a solid understanding of what the site will offer and know how to navigate to their location of interest within the site.

All of this is accomplished with graphical layouts representing the tone, image and feeling of your real-life business. The site may not be designed to entertain visitors, but it should be intuitive and aesthetically pleasing.
