About the Connected Learning Community Web Templates
Microsoft believes that the single most important use of technology is to improve education. This belief is demonstrated through our continuing efforts to help build a global "Connected Learning Community" in which all students, educators and parents have access to technology and the tools and skills to support learning today and for a lifetime. The Connected Learning Community Web Templates are one of many ways we are helping schools get connected, communicate, and collaborate.
