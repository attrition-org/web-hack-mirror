REGISTRATION INFORMATION

These items are needed when registering in Center Point ISD:
  • Birth Certificate
  • Social Security Card
  • Immunization Record
  • Proof of residence in Center Point ISD
  • Previous Report Card
  • Transcripts (grades 9-12 only)
  • Complete address of previous school
  • Proof of income (Headstart only)
