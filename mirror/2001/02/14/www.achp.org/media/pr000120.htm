Statement of Daniel Wolfson of ACHP Regarding President Clinton's Proposal to Cover the Uninsured

Washington, D.C. - "Because access to quality care for the uninsured is one of ACHP's guiding values and principles, we are pleased that President Clinton has expressed a willingness to address the significant problems faced by those without health insurance. "ACHP member health plans believe bringing this issue to the forefront allows for the type of discussions in Washington and elsewhere so vital to finding answers for this national dilemma. "We look forward to working with the Administration, Members of Congress and advocates for the uninsured to help secure the best possible solution for America."

Daniel B. Wolfson
President and CEO
Alliance of Community Health Plans
Washington, DC
