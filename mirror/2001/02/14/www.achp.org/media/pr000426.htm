BIQS Featured in Managed Care Magazine

Washington, D.C. - ACHPs pioneering Benchmarks in Quality and Safety (BIQS) initiative is detailed in a feature article in the April 2000 edition of Managed Care Magazine.

In the article, "Not-for-Profit Group of Plans Goes After Medicines Holy Grail," Managed Care Magazine, managing Editor Michael Dalzell details the genesis, framework and challenges associated with creating and implementing a new outcomes measurement program.

ACHP president and CEO Daniel Wolfson, HealthPartner's chief medical officer George Isham, MD, Washington Business Group on Health's director Claire Sharda and the American College of Physicians-American Society of Internal Medicine's director of scientific policy Christel Mottur-Pilson are among those interviewed for this informative report on BIQS.
