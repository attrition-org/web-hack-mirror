Welcome to the Alliance Of Community Health Plans' Press Release Page

The Media Center provides breaking news and press releases describing the activities and achievements of the Alliance. In addition, the Alliance stands ready to assist journalists seeking information about health care delivery systems in general and managed care in particular. With its national database, the Alliance is well positioned to put you in touch with medical experts from around the country.
