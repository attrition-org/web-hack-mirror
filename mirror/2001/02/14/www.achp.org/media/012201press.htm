ALLIANCE OF COMMUNITY HEALTH PLANS SELECTS THE MWW GROUP AS COMMUNICATIONS COUNSEL

Public Affairs Practice to Provide Media Relations for Organization Aimed at Improving Quality Safety and Affordability of Health Care

EAST RUTHERFORD, N.J., January 22, 2001 - The MWW Group, (www.mwwpr.com) one of the nation's 20 largest public relations firms, today announced that it has been selected by the Alliance of Community Health Plans (ACHP) to support its media relations activities. ACHP is an organization that aims to improve medical practice for individuals and communities.

MWW will develop and implement a communications program to inform industry peers, opinion makers, the press and the public about ACHP's pioneering activities. The program will further position ACHP and its member plans as leaders and innovators.

The Alliance of Community Health Plans is an organization of 22 leading not-for profit and provider-based health plans serving more than 8 million Americans in 22 states and the District of Columbia. ACHP is affiliated with more than 100,000 doctors throughout the country.

"ACHP and its member plans have long been advocates for the improvement of health care services and standards in this country," said Michael W. Kempner,

president and CEO of The MWW Group. "MWW will develop and implement a program that demonstrates the effectiveness of ACHP and its member plans to make measurable improvements in the delivery of health care services."
