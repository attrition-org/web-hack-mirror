Introduction

ACHP plans distinguish and differentiate themselves to consumers and communities through a united focus on quality measurement and improvement. Alliance members have both the means and the motivation to focus on quality because they contribute to and facilitate care beyond simple insurance transactions. Under this framework, plans promote and improve the health of individuals and communities by encouraging and facilitating scientifically based medicine and health care practices.

What follows is a description of the values and principles that reflect the structure and operations of these physician-driven organizations.
