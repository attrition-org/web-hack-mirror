Originators
HEDIS® (Healthplan Employer Data and Information Set) was originated with ACHP health plans. This evolving set of standardized performance measures is used to evaluate the quality of health plans in areas important to purchasers of health insurance. HEDIS is a registered trademark of the National Committee for Quality Assurance.
Services
Shared Improvement Projects- Plans participate in the development of evidence- based interventions, quality markers, and outcomes to maximize improvement in clinical processes.

Benchmarking and Effective Practice Dissemination- Plans regularly compare performance on quality dimensions and spotlight clinical practices that are employed to obtain high performance.

TEMINEX: Technology Management Information Exchange- Providing evidence-based medical technology assessments in partnership with HAYES, Inc.

ECP: Effective Clinical Practice- 30,000 subscriber journal published bimonthly in collaboration with the American College of Physicians-American Society of Internal Medicine.

ACHPICL: Alliance Of Community Health Plans Insurance Company Ltd. (Reinsurance)- Provides stop-loss coverage to participating plans. Also provides reinsurance coverage for plans' Point-of-Service products and for self-insured providers using a member plan as a provider network and TPA.
