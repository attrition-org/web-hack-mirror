Effective Clinical Practice

This publication is a collaborative effort of The Alliance of Community Health Plans and The American College of Physicians- American Society of Internal Medicine (ACP-ASIM). More than 30,000 subscribers read the journal's peer-reviewed articles that emphasize systems of care and innovations that can improve clinical practice and patient outcomes.

Published bimonthly, Effective Clinical Practice focuses on providing evidence-based solutions to questions about health status, patient satisfaction, provider satisfaction and cost utilization. The journal is designed for practicing physicians, health care executives and other professionals involved with health care delivery systems.
