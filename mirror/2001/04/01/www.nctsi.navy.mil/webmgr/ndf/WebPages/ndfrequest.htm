US Navy JTIDS Network Design Facility

Navy Requesters

Navy requesters will submit requests to NCTSI and inform their chain of command.  Requesters will submit a completed Network Design Request Form which is found below or in the JNL 200 QRG.  If the request is service urgent or if rapid turnaround is required, NCTSI should be notified by Contacting us.


You can also fax a copy of the form to us at the fax number listed below or email us the information required in the form.

US Joint Service/Staff Requesters

NCTSI is responsible for coordinating with the other service NDFs and the JNDL in collecting or responding to Joint network design requirements. The Joint Network Design Guide defines the procedures for collecting network requirements from Joint commands. The JTIDS Network Design Library (JNDL) component of Forces Command (FORSCOM) serves as a library for current Joint networks.  When requirements for a new Joint network are received, service NDFs and the JNDL coordinate in assigning the requirement to a lead service NDF.  NCTSI coordinates with the JNDL for each Joint requirement assigned to the Navy NDF and provides the required network design support.

Allied Requesters

Allied network design requirements may be reported through the JNDL FORSCOM.  When Allied requirements are assigned to the Navy NDF, NCTSI will provide the required network design support.  In addition, NCTSI will coordinate procedures for managing as well as collecting these Allied requirements.  These procedures will be integrated with standard Navy and Joint procedures for managing requirements to the extent practical.
