Directions to NCTSI
 From South San Diego:
  1. Exit on I 8 W (Beaches)
  2. At the end of Interstate 8 make a left at the light onto Nimitz Blvd
  3. Take Famosa Blvd/Catalina Blvd exit and turn right at the light to Catalina Blvd
  4. Continue for about 2.5 miles then turn right on Electron Drive to Fleet Combat Training Center
  5. You may obtain specific directions, visitor parking information and a map at the bldg to your right
 From Los Angeles, CA:
  1. From I 5 exit on Sea World Drive
  2. Turn left at traffic light and follow road for about 3 miles
  3. Make a left at the light onto Nimitz Blvd
  4. Take Famosa Blvd/Catalina Blvd exit and turn right at the light to Catalina Blvd
  5. Continue for about 2.5 miles then turn right on Electron Drive to Fleet Combat Training Center
  6. You may obtain specific directions, visitor parking information and a map at the bldg. to your right
 From the SD International Airport:
  1. Exit on Harbor Drive, make a right (about 2 miles to Rosecrans St.(Hwy 209))
  2. Turn left on Rosecrans
  3. Turn right on Canon St
  4. At the end of Canon turn left on Catalina Blvd
  5. follow steps 4 from the "From Los Angeles, CA" steps above
