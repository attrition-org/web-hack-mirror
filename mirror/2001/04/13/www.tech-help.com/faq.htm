What does it cost?
It is does not cost anything to ask a question or request technical help on
www.tech-help.com. You pay for the solution at a price you choose from the posted solutions from our Experts.

Our WOLRD-WIDE team of "TRUE" Experts assembled from top specialists from around the globe will review your question and provide Technical Solutions from one-page recommendations that can be e-mailed to complete on-site turn-key projects.

After asking your technical question, we will contact you via email with a password which you can then access your solution and cost. Expert solution prices may vary so you will be able to choose your own solution cost. All prices quoted on this site are in US Dollars.

Payments for simple one-page recommendations may be by credit card.

Turn-key projects are taken on a per case basis with progress payments as arranged individually.

How do I sign up?

It's easy. Click on Submit Question and fill out the form, we need this information so we can contact you with your solution.

All the fields have to be filled out in order for us to correctly process
the registration. Once the information is filled out press the "Submit" button. We will submit your question to our panel of experts and we will contact you with a solution.
