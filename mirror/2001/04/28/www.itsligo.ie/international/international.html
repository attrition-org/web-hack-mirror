The Institute has formal articulation agreements with a large number of colleges both nationally and internationally, facilitating movement of students between partner colleges. There is a strong cosmopolitan, multicultural feel to the institute and students here rub shoulders with other students from France, Germany, Spain, Britian, Portugal, Belgium, Hungary, Holland, Italy, Greece, Denmark, Sweden, Norway, Finland and Japan. 

It all adds to the exciting, lively atmosphere you'll find in Sligo.
