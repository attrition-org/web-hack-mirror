Welcome to the Institute of Technology, Sligo.

Situated in the heart of Yeats' Country, in the North-West of Europe, the Institute is one of Irelands' most progressive third level colleges. With student numbers reaching close on 3,000 the college offers courses in the fields of Science, Engineering, Business and Humanities.

The Campus boasts an impressive array of modern buildings and facilities with the planned completion of the new Engineering block set for winter 2000. The official opening of the library and administration buildings by An Taoiseach, Mr. Bertie Ahern TD was held on the 4th of February 2000 and details can be found here. Photos of the official opening of the languages and marketing building by the Minister for Education and Science, Dr. Michael Woods, T.D. are located here.
