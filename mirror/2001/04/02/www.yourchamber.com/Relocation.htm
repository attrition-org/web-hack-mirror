Clackamas County offers a wide venue of attractions and entertainment, as well as a number of lifestyle choices which make it an excellent opportunity for relocation of home or business, or a week or week-end stop. Community events, attractions and activities, from the End of the Oregon Trail Interpretive Center to fishing derbies, and from Milwaukie Daze Festival to Gladstone’s Chautauqua Festival make this Northwestern Oregon county the perfect place for family or individual enjoyment.

Living choices range from single family to row houses, or duplex to multi-story apartments. Whether your taste runs to highly modern, restored Victorian or classic bungalow, or if you’re looking for a county or city setting, you’ll find it in Clackamas County.

Information in this section will make your choices easier, and offer you a glimpse of Clackamas County, your next stop.
