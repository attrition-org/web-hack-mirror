Your Chamber's Mission

"To provide responsible leadership and innovative resources for business and the community"

The North Clackamas Chamber of Commerce is an association of businesses, organizations and individuals, working to provide innovative leadership to ensure a successful business climate and promote the quality of life in Clackamas County.  In this capacity, the Chamber functions as the advocate for business; a leading economic development organization, and a clearing house for information about the area.  The Chamber provides its members with a direct link to all major business, economic, governmental and community decision making in Clackamas County.

The North Clackamas Chamber of Commerce is the largest, most dynamic resource and action-oriented, non-profit structure in the area, and is totally dedicated to the success of business, economic, political and community development and quality-of-life issues.
