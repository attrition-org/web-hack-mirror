Conference Facilities and Meeting Spaces:

The Tunxis Room can accommodate up up to 75 people in a theatre type setting. The seating arrangement is especially suited for up to 50 people for classroom and seminar purposes.

Our Hillstead Room seats up to 20 for meeting purposes. There is also a full panoply of audio-visual equipment available, including direct satellite transmission and other hi-tech conferencing facilities.

Other equally appointed rooms are also available for smaller groups of people.
