Guests at the Farmington Inn are welcomed into a warm and comfortable lobby bedecked with flowers, antiques and local original art work.  The Guest rooms are beautifully appointed, featuring white Italian Carrera marble bathrooms, matching Nightingale pattern drapes and bedspreads, cable TV and amenities for business travelers.

The elegant suites offer terrycloth bathrobes, handsome armoires housing television sets with built-in VCRs and small refrigerators. The bathrooms are oversized and equipped with hair dryers, exquisite toiletries, irons and full ironing boards.

Guests awaken to a sumptuous continental breakfast of steaming flavored coffees, fresh fruit and juices, cereal and freshly baked muffins, croissants and pastries, served in the enchanting Victoria's Cafe.

The Inn's staff is happy to recommend exceptional dining spots, such as an Italian bistro next door, two award winning restaurants on the Farmington River banks, and others specializing in cuisines from around the world.
