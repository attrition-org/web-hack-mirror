About Top Secret Recipes on the Web

If you’ve ever tried to make a brand-name clone recipe gathered from elsewhere on the Web, you’ve likely had that sinking feeling that comes from wasting time and ingredients. That’s because every other site on the 'Net simply gathers clone recipes indiscriminately (they like to call 'em "copycat recipes") from various unqualified sources without any quality-control. Rarely is anyone testing the recipes or researching the products; and they just pass the garbage off on you. 

Top Secret Recipes on the Web is the world’s only Web site that brings you original custom recipes that have been created from scratch in the test kitchen by the guy who has devoted the last decade to kitchen cloning. With over 1 million Top Secret Recipes books in print, Todd can often spend days at a time and dozens of attempts creating the perfect clone of a famous brand-name product. He brings those recipes to you here every week, free of charge, and without misrepresentation. When you cook using one of these recipes, you can rest-assured that it is time well-spent. 

Top Secret Recipes on the Web contains scores of original clone recipes, insider secrets and food lore, plus a message board community that can instantly help you with any cooking or food question you may have…no matter how quirky. From word-of-mouth alone, devoted fans have made this one of the fastest-growing specialty cooking sites on the Internet, with over 7 million impressions recorded each month!  

If you want the best, most trusted clone recipes on the Web since 1996, you’ve come to the right place. And we’re very glad that you can join us.
