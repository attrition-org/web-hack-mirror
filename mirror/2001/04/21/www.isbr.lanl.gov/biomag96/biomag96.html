Tenth International Conference on Biomagnetism

Biomag96

February 16-21, 1996 Santa Fe, New Mexico, USA


"Sunset, El Rancho", Oil by Jean Morgan George, 1994

 

The Tenth International Conference on Biomagnetism (Biomag96) was held at the Eldorado and Santa Fe Hilton Hotels in Santa Fe, New Mexico, USA, from February 16-21, 1996. The conference covered all areas of research concerned with the acquisition, analysis, interpretation and application of magnetic fields produced by biological tissue.
