Unique combination of Erbium YAG and CO2 in one device to enable you to meet all your dental practice's needs. The Er:YAG laser, operating at 2.94 microns, is designed primarily for hard tissue procedures on teeth and bone. The CO2 laser, at 10.6 microns, is effective for a wide variety of soft tissue applications.
 

Hard Tissue procedures:

  • Cavity and crown preparation
  • Caries removal
  • Enamel etching
  • Curretage

Soft Tissue procedures:

  • Gingevectomy
  • Gingivoplasty
  • Frenectomy
  • Implant exposure and maintenance
  • Root canal disinfection
  • Laser troughing
  • Crown lengthening
  • Operative dentistry
  • Periodontal procedures
  • Preprosthetic surgery
  • Guided tissue regeneration
