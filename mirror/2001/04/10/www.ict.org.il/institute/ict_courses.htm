Strategic Counter-Terrorism Course

Now open to individuals!

The International Policy Institute for Counter-Terrorism is now offering the intensive seminar “Strategic Counter-Terrorism” to individuals. The seminar will be given in English at the Interdisciplinary Center, Herzliya Israel. Dates of future courses will be posted on the site.

For information on topics covered, see attached course description.

The seminar consists of 10 lectures over a 5-day period. Lectures will be delivered by ICT's researchers and educational staff. Guest lectures will be given by Israeli leaders in the field of security and international policy. In addition the program will include a sightseeing excursion to Israel's historical sites, as well as an additional one-day introductory tour of the Israeli security industry.

Costs
The cost of the seminar is $1,500 (U.S.). Tuition includes:

  • All Lectures & workshops
  • Travel to and from the Interdisciplinary Center
  • Travel & Sightseeing expenses at historical sites, including entry fees
