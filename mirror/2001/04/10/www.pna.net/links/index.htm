WELCOME TO THE
RENOVATED OFFICIAL PNA WEBSITE

Welcome on your visit to the Land and People of Palestine through the authorized, official site of the Palestinian National Authority. 

You will find here the genuine data, documentation, news, opinions, official statements and political information related to Palestine:  the land, the people, the issues, the problems and the hopes;  the texts and the images, the figures and the charts which reflect the efforts of the PNA, of the PLO, and of the Palestinian people as a whole, to construct, with the assistance and support of the international community, a democratic society and a viable economy in a sovereign, independent Palestinian State, with Holy Jerusalem as its capital.

Meet Palestine on this site.

This reconstructed website opens on the eve of historical negotiations: for the first time in the modern history of the Arab-Israeli confrontation, the fundamental issues of the Palestinian-Israeli conflict - Jerusalem, Settlements, Borders, Refugees, Water, etc… - will be put on the agenda.

We intend to offer our readers and guests fresh documentation and data, regularly updated, on the state of the peace process, on the progress of negotiations, on the obstacles to overcome and on the Palestinian proposals.

We will publish the documents which reflect the place of Palestine in the world: our international relations, the efforts and results of cooperation in all fields, and the involvement of the world community in the search for peace, stability and prosperity for all in our region.

We will also follow-up developments in our area, with particular attention to the political debate taking place among our Israeli neighbors, and hopefully partners in peace. We will analyze and comment them, and open our columns to independent views.

Your comments and suggestions, criticism and encouragement are all equally welcome.
