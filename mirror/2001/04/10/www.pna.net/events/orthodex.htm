The official spokesman of the Orthodox Church:

National struggle is not terror

Jerusalem 6/2 WAFA- The official spokesman of the Orthodox Church in Jerusalem and The Holy Land, Archimandrite, Dr. Atalla Hanna, says that the Palestinian legitimate National struggle against the occupation, can’t be called terrorism by all means, the Palestinian people seek peace and justice.

Dr. Hanna was briefing a French Human Rights delegation visiting the Orthodox Patriarchiat yesterday in Jerusalem, about the suffering of the Palestinian people under the Israeli occupation and aggression.

He explained that, any people who seeks freedom and independence, offers sacrifices.
