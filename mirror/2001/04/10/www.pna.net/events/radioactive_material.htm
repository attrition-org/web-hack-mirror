Israel used radioactive materials against the Palestinians

Gaza Mar. 24th Wafa,- Dr. Youssif Abu Safeya, the Palestinian environment Minister, declared, yesterday, that the International Company which produces radioactive products, for peace purposes, assured him that in fact the company, supplied, recently, to Israel, radioactive devices.

Dr. Safeya says that the Israeli armed forces are using these devices, against the Palestinian people.

The company sources said, that, these devices which are called (CH-53) and (SR.90), generate radioactivity which can penetrate 1mm thick aluminum, and harms people exposed to it.

Dr. Abu Safeya says that these devises which were supplied, with top secrecy to Israel.
