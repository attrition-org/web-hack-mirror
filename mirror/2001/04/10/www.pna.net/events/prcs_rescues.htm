Medical Ethics


At 11:20 am on Feb 1,

Palestine Red Crescent Society Emergency Medical
Station in Jenin (North West Bank) responded to rescue an injured Israeli civilian who had apparently been shot and wounded earlier on the outskirts of the town. The PRCS paramedics worked to stabilize the victim’s condition. The 25 year old male was shot 4 times and was bleeding heavily. He was transported to Jenin Public Hospital. After additional medical care, he was later transported by the PRCS ambulance crew to an awaiting Israeli military ambulance.
