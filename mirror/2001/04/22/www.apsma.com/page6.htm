• To advance the administration or management of health care in academic, hospital and private pediatric surgical entities.

  • To promote the concept of professional management in pediatric surgical administration.

  • To enhance the continuing educational process for pediatric surgical administrators through sponsorship of meetings, seminars, written communications, etc.

  • To collect and disseminate information concerning present and proposed national, provincial, state and local legislation and other data pertinent to the professional administrator of a pediatric surgery entity.

  • To interact in liaison with other professional associations when such interaction advances these stated purposes.

  • To promote cooperation, understanding and fellowship within the membership of this organization.
