The current school library was opened in 1992 and is fully automated with F.I.L.M.S. (Functional Integrated Library Management Systems).

All classes use the library for borrowing , skills and research.

Mrs Bell the library officer enjoys reading stories to all classes.

The library is a place to enjoy different activities during lunchtime. Theses incllude games, colouring in and watching a video on wet days. Many children just enjoy using the library to read at lunchtime.

This year the whole school has been networked to the library and a lot of use is made of the internet during the day, both in the library and in the classrooms.
