招聘启事

   为满足成人外语教育发展的需要，我院招聘专职英语教师、合同制英语教师。专职英语教师2001年共录用1名；合同制英语教师常年招聘，名额不限。

二、合同制英语教师 要求及待遇如下：
1、应聘条件：有丰富的高校，尤其是成人高校英语课堂教学经验；身体健康，每周能完成16-20学时的教学工作量；英语专业本科以上学历；讲师以上职称。
2、待遇：
①条件优秀、教学效果好的教师，酬金从优发放。
②若京外人员应聘，我院可为其提供住宿。

English Language Teaching 

Job in Predeparture Training Department

      We are a department offering intensive language courses to those who are about to go to English speaking countries for further education or training. The students are all adults, highly motivated, from various background. Each year we employ six to eight native speakers of English to teach speaking and writing. We are now looking for teachers of English for the 2001-2002 academic year, which begins on Sept. 1, 2001. We offer the following to someone who has a bachelor degree with a certificate in TEFL or TESOL:

--A monthly salary of RMB 2,200 - 2,800 yuan;
--A two-room apartment on campus (furnished, air-conditioned, 
   kitchen & bathroom, phone, TV, etc.);
--Free medical care (cost for spectacles, false teeth and doctors' 
   home visit, etc. are not included);
--Single international flight fare upon completion of the contract.

      If an applicant has an MA TEFL/TESOL/TESL degree plus a few years teaching experience overseas, he/she will be offered return international flight fare and a salary of RMB 3,300-3,600 yuan. The terms for accommodation and medical care are the same as those for people with a bachelor degree.

      The workload is the same for the two status: 14 - 16 classroom hours per week. If interested, please contact::
