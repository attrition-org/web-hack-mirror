  Welcome to CAD Design Software’s world of Electronic Design Automation layout tools. CAD Design Software produces the most innovative EDA layout software available for leading-edge technologies in the RF/Microwave PCB, Hybrid/MCM, and IC packaging industries. CAD Design Software utilizes very advanced 2D and 3D graphics capabilities and includes manufacturing yield simulation and a very high level of design automation. Internal high speed calculations and links to major simulation tools vastly improve manufacturability.

CAD Design Software tools were developed by designers looking to solve design problems , not software engineers writing work-arounds. From concept to completion (schematic to Gerber / GDSII), CAD Design Software design suites leads the industry with the most advanced design and time-to-market solutions available at any price .

Stop using old work-arounds and start using new solutions to your design problems. Let us help you work smarter, not harder.
