What is a Hosting Plan?

Hosting is a term used to describe where your Web site files and images reside; which most often is on a computer server. You may have noticed that we may at times all it "webspace". You rent webspace on our servers and we agree to maintain and host your Web site files and images on our server in return.

TheWebsiteDistrict.com  specializes in virtual servers as well as dedicated servers which means that you can find a home for your web site on our web servers and establish a presence on the internet regardless of your needs. We help you register your own domain name, which will become your unique address on the World Wide Web. Having your own domain name does not reveal that you are renting web space, and, since e-mail can be addressed to your domain (e.g.. WWW.YOURNAME.COM ) you will not have to announce a new e-mail address if you change internet access providers. And, with a domain name you can transfer your web presence at any time and never lose your identity since you can take your www.yourname.com with you. 

The TheWebsiteDistrict.com Virtual Server Solution allows you to start small and scale upward to whatever server and bandwidth combination fits your needs. Our customers range from small businesses to large corporate clients.

TheWebsiteDistrict.com server architecture is the most robust and flexible in the industry. Customers use their TheWebsiteDistrict.com/ TheWebsiteDistrict.com server solutions for ecommerce, on-line auctions, chat forums, virtual postcards, polls and surveys, live audio and video, secure conferencing, market research, banner advertisements, shopping carts, e-mail (virtual post offices), classified ads, databases, search engines, on-line stores and malls and much more.

What is a domain name?

A domain name is a unique Internet address that can be used for web hosting,e-mail, etc. It allows you to send and receive information at your customized e-mail address. It is your companys identity on the World Wide Web (WWW). If your organization is Your Company, Inc., you could register the domain name of yourcompany.com and your e-mail address could be  you@yourcompany.com. For more information see Naming Your Site

Does TheWebsiteDistrict.com handle domain registration?
Yes. TheWebsiteDistrict.com will register new domain names with ICANN for you. However, due to changes in ICANN registration policy, domain name registration must be prepaid. ($70.00 for two years) 

We do not charge for registration and we register it in YOUR name, if you establish a TheWebsiteDistrict.com or Sitebuilder's account with us. There is a $20.00 registration charge for all others. 

If you have an existing domain we will fill out and send you a modify domain name template to forward to hostmaster@internic.net. When ICANN announces the completion of the transfer, there is a 72 hour propagation period where visitors could go to either site. At the end of the 72 hours you should contact the previous host to have them delete the old domain from their records and update their DNS record. If you use this same provider for your Internet access you will not be able to access your domain site at through our affiliate server base until they update their DNS. During the propagation period persons accessing your site will be going to both sites until all are eventually accessing the new site hosted by TheWebsiteDistrict.com. The process should be seamless.

 
How long does it take before my domain name is active?

Once the ICANN announces that your domain name has been registered (or transferred, if applicable), it usually takes about 72-hours before it is active. All internet providers must update their records (DNS tables) to reflect new site locations. This is called propagation.
