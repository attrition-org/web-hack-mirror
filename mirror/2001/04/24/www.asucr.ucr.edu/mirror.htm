Welcome to the ASUCR Web page. ASUCR stands for the Associated Students of the University of California, Riverside, which is the student government here at UCR. In addition to serving the students in the form of advocacy, representation and participation in current issues, ASUCR also funds most on-campus clubs, coordinates and assigns students to campus committees, provides carious free workshops, implements student senate elections and homecoming, and operates two student owned and operated businesses, Ditto Note Taking Service and the Exchange.
