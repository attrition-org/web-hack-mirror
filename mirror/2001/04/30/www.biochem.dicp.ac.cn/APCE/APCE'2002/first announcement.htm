4th International Symposium on Microscale Separations and Analysis (APCE2002)

Shanghai, China             October 11-14, 2002

  4th International Symposium on Microscale Separations and Analysis (APCE'2002) will be held in Shanghai on October 11-14, 2002.

  This symposium, following previous three highly successful meetings in Singapore, Dalian and Hong Kong, will cover many different aspects of the latest developments in capillary electrophoresis, chip-CE, other CE-based and chip-based analysis, and related microscale techniques through plenary, keynote, invited and contributed lectures and poster by prominent leaders and scientists from Asia, North American, Australian, Europe and elsewhere.
