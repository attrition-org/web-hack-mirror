The United States is a democracy. This means that United States citizens have a very important power: the power to vote. When citizens vote for president, governor, Congressman, or mayor, they expect that person to work hard to make their community a better place to live in. The person who wins, or is elected, is called a public servant because they serve the public. Public servants make many important decisions for us. They can change the lives of many people in their community. If you were a public servant and could make a law that would help others, what law would you make? Remember that the law you make should help as many people as possible. Write the law and sign your name on it.
