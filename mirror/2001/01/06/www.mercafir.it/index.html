For Valentina



Never think that I don't care
When you are feeling down.
No matter, night or day--
For you, I'll be around.
Nothing could ever be so bad
To make me stop loving you.
If it's something you've said or done
To your own heart you must be true.
I know life isn't always fair,
the choices we're forced to make.
But if you ever need someone,
I'll help you decide which paths to take.
There's never a favor too big
That you could ask of me.
Asking is all you need to do,
and I'll do my best to make it be.
You are my life, my everything,
so if something is wrong with you,
we'll do our best together to solve it,
for it's my problem, too.
