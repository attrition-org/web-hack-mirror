COMPANY PROFILE



Guardian Security Company has provided security guard services and has been doing business with the government, both federal and state, as well as on the commercial level since 1992. Our corporate staff includes military veterans familiar with military procedures and active/retired police officers familiar with security and law enforcement at all levels. We contribute our high retention rate with our clients due to our belief that we not only want our people to look professional, but also to perform professionally. Guardian Security Company is fully staffed and maitains a corporate office in Steelville, Missouri, with branch offices in Kansas City, Missouri and New Century, Kansas.
