Indoexpress has since its establishment been registered as a member of the International Air Transport Association (IATA), the Associated World Logistics Network Inc. (AWLN) and the FIATA (Federation Internationale des Associatenes de Transitaires et Assimilies/The International Federation of Freight Forwader Association). 

Our scope of activities cover air and sea cargo, customs, distribution and logistics support as well as relocation services. Entering the third millenium Indoexpress has provided a virtual e-commerce service through the Internet. You may visit us at: http://www.indoexpress.co.id.

Indoexpress   is a subsidiary of the H.M. Sampoerna Group, one of the largest and prominent cigarette manufacturer in Indonesia, established since 1913. H.M. Sampoerna currently employs more than 22,000 workers.
