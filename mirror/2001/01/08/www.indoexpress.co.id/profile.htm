FOREWORD

Indoexpress  was founded in 1994 as a courier service company. We are in the business of transporting goods and documents over land, air and sea. Our activities also include customs clearance, the distribution and logistics support, relocation services, and even virtual e-commerce service through the net.

We firmly believe the field of delivery service is an “on time” service. We, therefore, apply an absolutely carefully assessed work system to show that we are in complete control of the business.

In an effort to improve the quality of our service to our customers, we constantly perform various innovations to our products, technology and the quality of human resources. Examples of these innovations are cost efficient and guaranteed packages; in-house operational application and on-line tracking & tracing system at all our branches; zone pricing system  and periodic education & training for all our employees. All these innovations are in line with our vision to become a prominent Total Transportation Company in Indonesia. As a proof and acknowledgement to such commitment, we passed the ISO 9002 Certification in December 1999.

Our vision manifests itself in the form of a friendly, sincere and professional service at an affordable price; sophisticated technology; and various products that meet customers’ needs. It is this commitment and vision that make Indoexpress  stand out among courier service companies in Indonesia.
