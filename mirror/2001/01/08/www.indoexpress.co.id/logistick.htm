Indoexpress’ Logistics Services offers the right solution for companies who choose to outsource the management and distribution of their goods. We will carry out the distribution and management of your products, be it heavy goods, components, manufactured products or medical equipments. You may rest assured and concentrate on your operational activities while we attend to your distribution process.

Our Logistics Services utilizes a modern warehouse management system. Our department of Information Systems monitors and records all movements of goods through our warehouse. With information technology behind us we are able to connect to all over the world, thus enabling us to handle problems related to warehousing, permits, cargo shipments, telemarketing, pickpack, and inventory control in a timely manner.
