Patient & Visitor Information

Going to the hospital can be a stressful experience, and unfamiliar surroundings, parking garages and hallways only add to the confusion. But with the information available on the Sharp eCare Web site, the stress can be taken out of your next visit. We’ve included information on visiting hours, parking, maps, directions and virtual tours of patient rooms and hospital facilities. So take a few minutes to acquaint yourself with Sharp’s visitor information, and enjoy your visit.
