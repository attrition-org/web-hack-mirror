Sharp HealthCare Locations

Sharp HealthCare includes four acute care hospitals and three specialty hospitals, including the region’s only freestanding women’s hospital. In addition, Sharp HealthCare treats thousands of San Diegans through its three affiliated medical groups: Sharp Community Medical Group, Sharp Mission Park Medical Group, and Sharp Rees-Stealy Medical Group. Sharp medical groups have offices located throughout San Diego County and provide primary and specialty care as well as six urgent care centers.

Sharp HealthCare also includes four home health and hospice agencies, skilled nursing facilities and a full range of other services and community education programs.
