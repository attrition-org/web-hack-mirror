Sharp Health Plan

Sharp Health Plan is the largest locally based health plan in San Diego. We are a not-for-profit health plan that has been serving San Diego since 1992. As a subsidiary of Sharp HealthCare, we are backed by the largest provider of comprehensive healthcare services in San Diego.

Sharp Health Plan offers a variety of coverage options that combine affordability and flexibility in choice, while ensuring high quality health care and customer service. Sharp Health Plan provides health insurance for companies throughout San Diego, whether a small business with just a few employees or a large company with several thousand. Sharp Health Plan is also the plan of choice for many San Diegans who receive health coverage through one of several State-sponsored programs, such as AIM (Access for Infants and Mothers), Healthy Families, and Healthy San Diego (Medi-Cal).

Our Mission

It is our mission to improve the health of those we serve with a commitment to excellence in all that we do.

Our goal is to ensure that our members receive quality care and services that set community standards, exceed patients’ expectations, and are provided in a caring, convenient, cost-effective and accessible manner.
