The Audio Health Information Library provides accurate and timely information contained within electronic sound files. Each sound file is also accompanied by a text file. Choose a category from the list to the right.

You will need a player installed on your browser that is capable of playing .wav sound files. Most web browsers come pre-configured with a sound playback device. If you are unable to play a sound file, download the WinAmp player.

The Audio Health Information Library is not intended to provide medical advice on personal health matters or to replace a physician's consultation or diagnosis. If you have questions regarding your health, please contact your primary care physician.
