Sharp Foundations

Sharp HealthCare is supported by three non-profit, philanthropic foundations. Contributions to the Sharp Foundations are vital to helping Sharp HealthCare meet the health needs of residents throughout San Diego County.

The Sharp Coronado Hospital Foundation supports the programs, services, and equipment needs of Sharp Coronado Hospital.

The Grossmont Hospital Foundation supports health education, patient care, clinical research and major capital projects affiliated with Grossmont Hospital.

The Sharp HealthCare Foundation supports the charitable mission of Sharp Memorial Hospital, Sharp Mary Birch Hospital for Women, Sharp Mesa Vista Hospital, Sharp Vista Pacifica, and Sharp Chula Vista Medical Center.
