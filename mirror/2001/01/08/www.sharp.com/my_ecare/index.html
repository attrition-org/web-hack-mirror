My eCare

My eCare is a place where you can personalize how you access health information from Sharp eCare. Our philosophy is to help keep you healthy by providing information about healthy lifestyles, preventive measures, health screenings, special services and advanced treatments.

My eCare Health e-Newsletters

Through My eCare, you can subscribe to Sharp’s free health e-newsletters. These e-newsletters are designed especially for our patients, families and friends – and anyone who is interested in good health. They are delivered directly to your email in box. From the e-newsletter, you can link to Sharp eCare to learn more, forward them to a friend, or even cancel your subscription at any time.

There are four e-newsletters to choose from:

  1. Men's Health
  2. Breast Cancer
  3. Heart Care
  4. Women's Health

Sharp e-newsletters are monthly clinical updates with the latest medical science announcements on breaking clinical research news for each topic. You simply choose the topic you are interested in, and Sharp will send you an e-mail each month with a brief overview of the latest clinical information available in the on-line newsletter posted to the Sharp eCare site. The newsletters are updated on a regular basis and contain timely information on each topic. 

Our goal is to deliver targeted information -- the specific information you are interested in -- directly to your desktop.
