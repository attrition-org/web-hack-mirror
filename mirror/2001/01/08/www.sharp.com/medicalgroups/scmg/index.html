Sharp Community Medical Group

Sharp Community Medical Group (SCMG) is an association of private practice primary care physicians and specialists who practice in their own private offices located conveniently throughout San Diego county.

The Sharp Community Medical Group network includes over 200 primary care physicians and over 500 specialists who currently provide care to more than 120,000 patients. The physicians within the group are held to very high quality standards and are reviewed by their medical expert colleagues. You can expect to receive convenient, comprehensive and pleasant medical care offered by physicians who are committed to excellence in your health care experience.

Mission and Goals of Sharp Community Medical Group

Sharp Community Medical Group physicians are dedicated to providing professional and effective health care in a safe and welcome environment. The mission of the physicians of Sharp Community Medical Group is to provide you access to quality health care with physicians in private practice so you can properly use the benefits of your Managed Care Health Plan.

Our goal is to offer quality care and services that exceed patients’ expectations in a caring, convenient, affordable and accessible manner

Sharp HealthCare, a not-for-profit health care system, relies on philanthropy to help fund its patient and community services. The Sharp Foundations gratefully accept tax-deductible contributions.
