Sharp HealthCare Medical Groups

If all health plans and their benefits are starting to sound the same, just remember this: The most important part of any health care plan is the personal physician you select. That’s why at Sharp HealthCare, we offer a large selection of physicians and hospitals in San Diego and southern Riverside Counties.

More than 1,200 physicians are members of Sharp HealthCare-affiliated medical groups. Within each of the medical groups, you’ll find physicians who practice in a variety of settings, from multi-specialty groups to private practice offices. That means you can choose a doctor and style of care you are comfortable with, at a facility that is convenient for you.
