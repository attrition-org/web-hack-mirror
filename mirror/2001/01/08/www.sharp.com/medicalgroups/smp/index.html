Sharp Mission Park Medical Group

Sharp Mission Park is a highly respected medical group founded in 1961 in North San Diego County. Today, we have 10 medical sites extending to Coronado and Imperial Beach with 65 primary care physicians and over 400 employees. Our primary care network of pediatricians, family practice and internal medicine physicians is enhanced by 350 community specialists who represent virtually every field of medicine.

At Sharp Mission Park, we are committed to providing our patients with quality, convenient, patient-oriented medical services in an atmosphere of health and well-being. We recognize that the physician-patient relationship is the most important aspect of your care. Your Primary Care Physician (PCP) is the doctor who will provide and coordinate all your health care needs. Our team approach to delivering your medical care is designed for you.

We accept most HMO (health maintenance organization), PPO (preferred provider organization), EPO (exclusive provider organization), POS (point of service), indemnity, fee-for-service, Medicare and workers’ compensation plans.

We have three networks of primary care physicians, specialists and hospitals from which to choose. These networks are community-based for your convenience. Patients in Coronado and Imperial Beach who need hospitalization are admitted to Sharp Coronado Hospital and Health Center. Patients in the Oceanside, Vista, Carlsbad, San Marcos and Encinitas areas are admitted to hospitals in their local communities.
