Roger R. Rapp was named Deputy Under Secretary for Operations of the National Cemetery Administration on March 26, 2000. Mr. Rapp's title prior to being named Deputy Under Secretary for Operations was Director, Field Operations, a position he held since 1987.

As Deputy Under Secretary, Mr. Rapp is in charge of operations and construction at 119 cemeteries in the National Cemetery Administration.

Mr. Rapp is also responsible for the State Cemetery Grants Program, which provides federal grants to aid states in the establishment, expansion and improvement of State veterans cemeteries.

Mr. Rapp began his career with VA in 1972, holding a number of positions in VA's Veterans Health Administration, including field assignments in medical centers in Dayton, Ohio, and Washington, D.C. Prior to 1987, he served as director of the National Cemetery Area Office in Philadelphia, Pa.

Mr. Rapp has a bachelor’s and a master’s degree from the State University of New York at Albany.
