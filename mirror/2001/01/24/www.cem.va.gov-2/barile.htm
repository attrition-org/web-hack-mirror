Vincent L. Barile was named Deputy Under Secretary for Management of the National Cemetery Administration (NCA) on March 26, 2000. Mr. Barile's title prior to being named Deputy Under Secretary for Management was Director, Operations Support, a position he held since 1990.

The Deputy Under Secretary for Management coordinates the staff functions of Budget and Planning, Information Systems, Communications Management, Human Resources, Administration and Contracting, and Memorial Programs.

Mr. Barile is the former director of NCA Budget and Planning, where he was responsible for the formulation and execution of the annual budget, all planning activities, and other activities including emergency preparedness, energy conservation and safety programs. He had held that position since 1984 when he joined NCA.

Mr. Barile began his career with the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) as a veterans benefits counselor at the Washington Regional Office in 1975. In 1976, he transferred to the Controller’s Budget Service in VA’s central office as a budget analyst. From 1979 to 1984, he served as a budget analyst in VHA where he supervised budget formulation and operations.

Mr. Barile was born and raised in Brooklyn, N.Y. He received his bachelor of business administration degree from the City College of New York. He also completed graduate work in Business Administration at Bryant College in Smithfield, R.I., while on active duty. He retired from the Naval Reserve with the rank of commander. As a naval officer, Mr. Barile served as a department head aboard several combat support ships stationed in the Mediterranean and the Far East.
