Getting Started

The first step is to look through our personal profiles and read the information each lady submitted. Start by writing down the ID numbers of those you like most. Don't hesitate to make several selections. The more you choose the better your chances are for success. Most of our clients start with 10-20 addresses. You should not be concerned about age or race as a factor. It is quite common for a lady to correspond with a man who is 10-25 years older or younger. All of the ladies presented are currently available.

Once you complete the selection and have your list ready, fill out our Order Form, select your payment method and submit it on-line. We have several methods of payment for you to choose from. It is possible to purchase the addresses on-line using your credit card (VISA, MasterCard, Discover or American Express).

You may also complete, print, and fax the Order Form to (850) 906-9536 or mail it with your check, money order, cashiers check or cash to the address below. After receipt of your order and payment, it will be immediately processed. Remember that personal checks take longer because they must clear the bank before we can begin. Credit cards, cashier checks, money orders or cash are the quickest way to fulfill your order. The addresses will be forwarded to you within 24 hours. Personal checks will take approximately 10 days processing time.
