How to Order and Prices

Each address costs $3.00, plus shipping and handling. (U.S Dollars only).

Upon receipt of your request via our email service, the names and addresses of the ladies will be forwarded to you within 24 hours. There is no charge for sending the requested information to you in this manner. However, you may elect to utilize regular U.S. mail. There is an additional $ 5.00 shipping and handling charge for this service. Second day priority mail is available,and your request should arrive within two days. There is an additional $ 10.00 shipping and handling charge for this service.

Fax service is available for an additional $ 7.00 shipping and handling charge.

Florida residents must add 7.50% sales tax to each order.
