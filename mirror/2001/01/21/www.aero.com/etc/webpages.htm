Considering going high tech with the Internet for sales and marketing communications? We are not an Internet Service Provider - our goal is to provide access to the spirit of aviation! Aero.com offers your business, association or school the opportunity to make your information available worldwide, 24 hours a day by providing presence on the Internet using the latest technology - the World Wide Web.

Whether your customer's interest is in flying off for the weekend, jumping out of a Skyhawk, inflating an Aerostar at 6 a.m. or learning the theory of aerodynamics - we offer you a unique opportunity to reach your market. Our focus on aviation makes Aero.com a point of research to users on the Internet. This focus greatly increases traffic through your site and the possibility that your pages will reach your clientele, aviation professionals and enthusiasts. Aero.com's concept is actively marketed in the publishing industry and on the Internet. Links to and from other aviation servers are also actively maintained.

Web Page Access
 Aero.com features an area entitled "Aviation Yellow Pages" where on-site Web pages are displayed. Links will be provided from "Aviation Yellow Pages" and "Other Resources" page to provide easy access to your information.

Programming/Rendering
 If you are an ace at HTML you may provide the source code and images yourself... or leave the details to us. Basic HTML authoring is simple and we will help you learn it free of charge with a commitment for space on our server. Image rendering is a little more complex: though we are not equipped to instruct others we can do the rendering for you by special arrangement at a nominal cost.

Features
 The Web has many unique and powerful features, i.e. forms processing, interactive pages, text search, and point-of-sale applications. We will work with you to create a Web application suited to your functional and financial needs - whether it be informative or sales- oriented.

Feedback
 Aero.com provides you with on-line monthly reports which include general and detailed analysis of the month's traffic on the server. These reports provide you with insightful ideas and valuable marketing information.
