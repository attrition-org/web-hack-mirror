Sunrise Aviation
Santa Ana, CA

The Aerobatic Division at Sunrise is the largest and most active in the western United States; we have trained more than a thousand pilots in aerobatic flight. We take pride in serving the widest possible range of aviation interests: recreation, business, and career. In addition to one-on-one training, we offer classroom ground schools at every level, along with a wide range of advanced seminars on electrical systems, aviation weather, airspace, Loran/GPS, and instrument refresher. All FAA Knowledge Tests ("computer tests") can be taken on our premises. For more information please visit our web site!
