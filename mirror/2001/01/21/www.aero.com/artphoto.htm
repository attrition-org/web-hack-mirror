Artists & Galleries

This section introduces you to the many aviators using a different kind of oil. We have collected contact info and some profiles on many artists and the galleries/resellers which proudly represent them. We have also seen work done by many of them at various air shows and suggest keeping aviation a little closer to home with their masterpieces.

If you are a "starving artist" maybe we can help you! Please email us your name, address and phone number and, if you wish, a profile of yourself. See the above Artists page for examples. We are happy to add you to the collection!

 

Photography Archive

Aero.com is pleased to provide space to photographers and artists (novice and weathered) for displaying a selection of their work for everyone to enjoy. These images include everything from "oldies" at the Smithsonian Air & Space Museum to a balloon rally to ultralights.

If you wish to submit your own creations please email paul@aero.com for instructions. If your images are already in electronic format they should be 72 dpi and no larger than 50K in size. If you have slides or actual prints please advise Paul of this so arrangements can be made. Please do not submit anything which is copyright material. We want to keep everything legal and make sure the artists receive their due rewards and hopefully a lot of "exposure"! :-)

Thinking of putting your business on the web or advertising to a focused market? Then contact us at info@aero.com
