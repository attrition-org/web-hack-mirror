A NEW MILLENNIUM OF LEADERS: MARCH 4-5
We are pleased to announce that the 28th Annual WASC Advisors Conference will be held at the Eau Claire Holiday Inn on March 4-5, 2001. 

Imagine, it’s the 28th Anniversary of this informative, motivating and enthusiastic two-day In-service program for the people who create opportunities for and help to develop student leaders in the state of Wisconsin!  Please know that the planning committee has created another terrific program that will offer ideas, new programs, support, encouragement and networking opportunities for everyone that will carry out the theme:  “A New Millennium of Leaders” (more)
 
 
NHS CONFERENCE FAST APPROACHING!
The 23rd Wisconsin Honor Society Conference will be held on Monday, February 19, 2001 at the Madison Sheraton Hotel, 706 John Nolan Drive, just off the Beltline (12-18)  by the Dane County Expo Center and Coliseum.  

This is the second  year that the conference has been  structured for one day only in an attempt to keep the costs lower. However, a block of rooms has been reserved Sunday night for those who wish to arrive on Sunday night rather than driving in on Monday morning. Obviously, distances from Madison enter into that decision. (more)
 
 
2001: A LEADERSHIP ODYSSEY!
Pulaski High School Student Council has been hard at work planning exciting and educational activities for your conference. This year’s conference will be held in Green Lake on May 6th and 7th, 2001: mark your calendars! It will be an experience of a lifetime that you won’t want to miss. The two keynote speakers for this year’s conference have been selected, and their combination of personal experience, wit, and leadership wisdom promises two very inspiring days. (more)
