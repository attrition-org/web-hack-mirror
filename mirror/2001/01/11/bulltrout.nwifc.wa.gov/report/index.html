Dear Reviewer:

This DRAFT Pilot Basin Assessment Report of the Salmon and Steelhead Habitat Inventory and Assessment Project (SSHIAP) highlights the results of a habitat assessment for four watersheds representing four of the six SSHIAP assessment areas. These watersheds were selected as pilots based upon the availability of stream and watershed-level information, and were used to develop specific methodologies for assessing habitat quantity and quality. The pilot assessments present a wide range of different techniques and approaches which may or may not be applicable to other watersheds. As work progresses, some or all of the approaches developed in the pilot analyses will be incorporated into subsequent assessments as needed.

The report is prefaced by an introduction, a description of methods, and an update on project status, followed by four sections describing the results of habitat assessments for the Nooksack, Dungeness, Big Quilcene and Skookumchuck watersheds. As you read through each of these sections, please comment on content, style, and organization, and note any content that you feel should be included or deleted. This is an interim report and subject to change in format and content before final publication. Please pay particular attention to assessment products such as graphs and charts and comment as to appropriateness, meaningfulness, etc. and note if there are other graphics that might display the data or results of the assessment more clearly.

This DRAFT version is missing most map products and photographs that we hope to include in a final document and comments concerning types, content and quantity of these graphics you'd like to see would be helpful. Are there other ways to display this information that we are missing? Is there other pertinent information that should be included in the assessment? If you have ideas concerning data management (storage, retrieval, accessibility, updating) please include them.

Please direct your comments either by mail or electronically to Randy McIntosh at the Northwest Indian Fisheries Commission no later than May 1, 1997. Once your comments have been received, they will be reviewed by the SSHIAP team for inclusion into subsequent analyses and reports. Publication of the final report is anticipated by October 1997. Should you have further questions, feel free to contact either Randy McIntosh at (360)438-1181 x 369 (rmcintos@nwifc.wa.gov), or Dave Somers at (206)489-0781 (dsomers@nwifc.wa.gov). Thank you in advance for your help.
