This home-page is intended to provide information about the Salmon and Steelhead Habitat Inventory and Assessment Project (SSHIAP). SSHIAP is being conducted by the Northwest Indian Fisheries Commission (NWIFC) in cooperation with the Washington State Department of Fish and Wildlife and many other state and federal agencies, timber companies and other private groups. We intend to update this page often in order to provide current information regarding the status of the project and to provide access to information developed through SSHIAP.
