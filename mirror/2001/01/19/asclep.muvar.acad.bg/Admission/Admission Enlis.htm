ADMISSION TERMS FOR FOREIGN STUDENTS The teaching in all Bulgarian schools is carried out in the official language of the country therefore proficiency in Bulgarian is of paramount importance for the mastering of medical knowledge. The Varna University of Medicine organises a one-year preparatory course in VARNA UNIVERSITY OF MEDICINEApplication and Guide to Education for Foreign Medical StudentsAcademic Year 2000/2001 Bulgarian language prior to the beginning of the medical education which lasts from October 1st to June 30th. This preparatory course provides the necessary knowledge and skills in written and oral Bulgarian language, including the specialised medical terminology in fundamental disciplines (biology, chemistry, physics, anatomy, Latin, etc). The application forms should be submitted at the Department of Students' Affairs at the Varna University of Medicine from 1st of July till 30th of September. The applicants have to present a diploma for completed secondary education translated into Bulgarian and legalised. The diploma results in biology, physics and chemistry are taken into consideration for admission. The preparatory course finishes with examinations in the disciplines studied. Students who have successfully passed these examinations obtain a certificate. Thereafter they can continue as first-year medical students at the University. Foreign citizens who are already proficient in Bulgarian or have previously studied Bulgarian have to successfully pass an entrance test in all subjects studied during the preparatory course. Students who do not perform sufficiently well at the entrance examinations can take up short-term intensive course for further improve ment in the respective subjects.

The annual tuition fees for foreign citizens can be paid in one or two instalments:
* 3 900 US $ for medical students; download Application form
* 2 500 US $ for health management students; download Application form
* 2 900 US $ for the preparatory course of Bulgarian; download Application form
* 4 000 US $ for full-time postgraduate specialisations; download Application form
